# Marketing Copy — Zolai Platform

## Mission statement (use verbatim or adapt)

> Ensure the Zolai language thrives in the AI era — allowing the Zomi people to learn, work, and interact with cutting-edge technology entirely in their native tongue.

## Audience segments & messaging

| Audience | Core message |
|---|---|
| Zolai Learners | "Learn Tedim Zolai with an AI tutor that adapts to your level" |
| Zomi Community | "Your language, preserved and accessible forever" |
| Researchers | "The most comprehensive Tedim Zolai linguistic dataset available" |
| AI/ML Contributors | "Fine-tune and contribute to the first Zolai language model" |

## Key stats (use in copy)

- 24,891 ZO↔EN dictionary entries
- 68,817 parallel corpus sentences
- ~2M raw Zolai sentences
- 66 books of the Tedim Bible corpus
- CEFR A1–C2 adaptive tutor

## Feature headlines

- Dictionary: "24,891 words. Every synonym, antonym, and example sentence."
- AI Tutor: "A Socratic tutor that speaks your language — literally."
- Bible Corpus: "The complete Tedim Bible, verse-by-verse with KJV alignment."
- Grammar Wiki: "Phonology, morphology, dialect — documented for the first time."
- Training Dashboard: "Watch the model learn Zolai in real time."

## Email subject line formulas

- Announcement: `[New] Feature name — what it does for you`
- Newsletter: `This week in Zolai: [hook]`
- Onboarding: `Welcome to Zolai AI — start here`

## Social media tone

- Twitter/X: concise, mission-forward, use Zolai words with translations
- Community posts: warm, celebratory, community-first
- Always include: what it is + why it matters for the Zomi people
