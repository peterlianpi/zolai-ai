================================================================================
9 SOP (CONTEXT EXPANSION)
================================================================================

Each entry gets enriched with:

1. POS (Part of Speech)
   └─ verb, noun, adjective, adverb, preposition, etc.

2. Synonyms
   └─ Words with similar meaning

3. Antonyms
   └─ Words with opposite meaning

4. Domains
   └─ religion, daily, education, culture

5. Register
   └─ formal, informal, archaic, slang

6. Examples
   └─ 2-3 example sentences

7. Related Words
   └─ Semantically linked entries

8. Etymology
   └─ Source or origin

9. Frequency
   └─ Count in corpus
