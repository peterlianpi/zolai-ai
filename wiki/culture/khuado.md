# Cultural Traditions: Khuado (Zolai Tedim)

Based on the analysis of traditional "Nunnop Khuado" texts, Zolai Tedim culture is rich in agricultural and community festivals.

## Khuado Festival
The Khuado festival is the premier annual celebration for the Tedim people, marking the harvest and the transition between agricultural seasons.

### Key Aspects
- **Community Thanksgiving:** Offerings and thanks for the crops.
- **Lineage Celebration:** Historical and genealogical songs/chants (*khanggui*).
- **Unity:** Reinforcement of social bonds through shared feasts and music.

### Terminology
- **Khuado Pawi:** Khuado Festival.
- **Nunnop:** Traditional dance/celebration.
- **Khuado La:** Khuado songs.

## Other Traditions
- **Minvoina:** Naming ceremonies.
- **Tengmaw:** Traditional marriage customs.
- **Hnat:** Traditional agricultural labor exchange.
