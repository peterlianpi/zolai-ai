## Verses

### v1
**EN:** In my distress I cried unto the

**TDB77:** Ka cihmawh ciangin Topa tungah ka kiko a, amah in hong dawng hi.

**ZVS:** Ka cihmawh ciangin { Topa' } tungah ka kiko a, amah in hong dawng hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Deliver my soul, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, mizuaute leh mikhemte kiang panin kei hong honkhia in.”

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, mizuaute leh mikhemte' kiang panin kei hong honkhia in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue?

**TDB77:** Mikhem theite aw, note’ tungah bang hong kipia ding a, note’ tungah bang hong kihih lai ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Mikhem theite aw, note' tungah bang hong kipia ding a, note' tungah bang hong kihih ding ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper.

**TDB77:** Galhangpa’ thaltang hiamte leh sesing hol am!

**ZVS:** Galhangpa' thal hiamte leh sesing hol-am hi ding hi.

### v5
**EN:** Woe is me, that I sojourn in Meshech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar!

**TDB77:** Note lakah ka om khak pen, Meshek gam sunga teen’ khak sangin haksa zaw a, Kedar mite’ lak-a teen’ khak sangin siahuai zaw hi.

**ZVS:** Note lakah ka omkhak pen Meshek gamsungah ten'khak sangin haksazaw a, Kedar mite lakah ten'khak sangin siahuaizaw hi.

### v6
**EN:** My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace.

**TDB77:** Gal kilemna a deih lo mite lakah sauvei ka teeng lua khin ta hi.

**ZVS:** Gal kilemna a deihlo mite lakah sauvei ka teng luakha hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war.

**TDB77:** Kei in gal kilemna a ngaihsun mi ka hi hi; ahih hangin kilem nading thu ka gen ciangin amaute in hong do nuam semsem-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kei pen gal kilemna a ngaihsun mi ka hi hi. Ahih hangin kilemna ding thu ka gen ciangin amaute in galkido ding utna tengin nei-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
