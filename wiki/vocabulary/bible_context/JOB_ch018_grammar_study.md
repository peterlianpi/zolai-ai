## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Shuah gammi Bildad in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** How long will it be ere ye make an end of words? mark, and afterwards we will speak.

**TDB77:** “Job aw, khawl hun nei thei lo ding na hi hiam? Hong khawl phot lecin, ko zong thu kong gen thei ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, khawl hun nei theilo ding na hi hiam? Hong khawl phot lecin, thu kong gen thei ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Wherefore are we counted as beasts, and reputed vile in your sight?

**TDB77:** Bang hangin bawng bang lelin kote hong ngaihsun a, mihaipi-in kote hong seh na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** He teareth himself in his anger: shall the earth be forsaken for thee? and shall the rock be removed out of his place?

**TDB77:** Na hehna tawh nangmah a kigimsak bek na hi hi. Nangma hangin leitung kinusia ding a, mualte kikhin khia ding sa na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Na hehna tawh nangmah a kigimsak bek na hi hi. Nangma hangin leitung kinusia ding a, mualte a kinkhia dingin ngaihsun na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Yea, the light of the wicked shall be put out, and the spark of his fire shall not shine.

**TDB77:** “Migilopa’ meivak kisumit ding a, cikmah hunin a kuangkik ngei nawn kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** “Migilopa' meivak kisumit ding a, cikmah hunin kuangkik nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** The light shall be dark in his tabernacle, and his candle shall be put out with him.

**TDB77:** Ama buk sungah khuamial ding a, a meivak mit ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ama buk sungah a meivak mit ding a, ama inn sungah khua a mial ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v7
**EN:** The steps of his strength shall be straitened, and his own counsel shall cast him down.

**TDB77:** A khe kip ngei a, ahi zongin tu-in a khe bai ta hi. Amah in a ngaihsut khialhna puklawh hi.

**ZVS:** A khe kip ngei a, ahi zongin tu-in a khe bai ta hi. Amah in a ngaihsut khialhna a puklawh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** For he is cast into a net by his own feet, and he walketh upon a snare.

**TDB77:** Thangsiahna ngen sungah bungbu tuk a, gum sungah amah kia hi.

**ZVS:** Thangsiahna ngen sungah amah bungbu tuk a, gum sungah amah kia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** The gin shall take him by the heel, and the robber shall prevail against him.

**TDB77:** Thang-ah a khetul-in awk a, thangkhau in amah gakcip hi.

**ZVS:** Thangah a khetulin awk a, thangkhau in amah gakcip hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** The snare is laid for him in the ground, and a trap for him in the way.

**TDB77:** Amah siahna-in lei lakah khauzak kitung a, a pai nading lampi-ah thang kisiah hi.

**ZVS:** Amah siahna-in leilakah khauzak kitung a, a paina ding lampi-ah thang kisiah hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Terrors shall make him afraid on every side, and shall drive him to his feet.

**TDB77:** A ciangciang-ah lauhuaina in amah pang a, a kalsuanna khempeuh ah sulzuih hi.

**ZVS:** Lauhuaina in amah pang a, a kalsuanna khempeuh a sulzuih hi.

### v12
**EN:** His strength shall be hungerbitten, and destruction shall be ready at his side.

**TDB77:** Siatna in amah ngak a, siatna in su dingin amah ngak gige hi.

**ZVS:** A hauh ngei hangin tu-in gilkial ta a, siatna in a su dingin amah ngak gige hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v13
**EN:** It shall devour the strength of his skin: even the firstborn of death shall devour his strength.

**TDB77:** A kihtakhuai natna in a pum khempeuh tuam a, a khut leh a khete muatsak ta hi.

**ZVS:** A kihtakhuai natna in a pum khempeuh tuam a, a khut leh a khete muatsakmang khinta hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** His confidence shall be rooted out of his tabernacle, and it shall bring him to the king of terrors.

**TDB77:** Lungmuang tak-a a omna inn panin kikaikhia a, Sihna Kumpipa kiangah kitunpih hi.

**ZVS:** Lungmuang takin a omna a inn panin amah kikaikhia a, Sihna Kumpipa kiangah amah kitun'pih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** It shall dwell in his tabernacle, because it is none of his: brimstone shall be scattered upon his habitation.

**TDB77:** Ama sihna hangin a inn zatui tawh a kisian’suah khit ciangin a ut peuhpeuh tua inn-ah teeng thei pah ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin zatui tawh ama inn a kisian'suah khit ciangin tua inn-ah a ut peuhpeuh teng thei pah ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** His roots shall be dried up beneath, and above shall his branch be cut off.

**TDB77:** Lei sunga ama zungte keu in, a tunga ama hiangte a si ta hi.

**ZVS:** Lei sunga ama zungte keu-in, a tunga a hiangte a si ta hi.

### v17
**EN:** His remembrance shall perish from the earth, and he shall have no name in the street.

**TDB77:** Inn leh gam-ah a minthan’na bei ta a, kuamah in amah phawk kha nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** Inn leh gamah ama minthan'na beita a, kuamah in amah phawkkha nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v18
**EN:** He shall be driven from light into darkness, and chased out of the world.

**TDB77:** A nungtate’ gam panin amah kihawl khia ding a, khuavakna mun panin khuamialna-ah kihawl khia ding hi.

**ZVS:** A nungtate' gam panin amah kihawlkhia ding a, khuavakna mun panin khuamialna-ah kihawlkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** He shall neither have son nor nephew among his people, nor any remaining in his dwellings.

**TDB77:** Amah in suan-le-khak nei lo a, a teen’nasa mun-ah ama gui zom ding om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in suanlekhak neilo a, ama ten'nasa munah ama gui a zom ding omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v20
**EN:** They that come after him shall be astonished at his day, as they that went before were affrighted.

**TDB77:** Nisuahna pan nitumna dong ama thuakna a za peuhmah kigaih-in launa tawh liing-te hi.

**ZVS:** Nisuahna pan nitumna dong ama thuakna a za peuhmah kigaihin a ling-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v21
**EN:** Surely such are the dwellings of the wicked, and this is the place of him that knoweth not God.

**TDB77:** Migilote, Pasian a ngaihsun lote’ thu tawpna in hih bang mah ahi hi,” a ci hi.
