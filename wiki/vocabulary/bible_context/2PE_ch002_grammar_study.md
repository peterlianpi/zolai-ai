**TDB77:** Ahih hangin mite lakah kamsang zuau hong piang mah bangin a kikhenthang thei up khialhna ngeina, kheemna tawh guangin, amau pumpi-te a lei Topa nangawn nialin, amau tungah a manlangin kisiatna a tungsak thu zuau hilh siate in note lakah zong a om ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mite lakah kamsang manlo a omngei mah bangin note lakah sia manlo hong om ding hi. Siatna a piangsak thei thuhilhna a manlo hong lutpih simin, amaute mahmah a tankhia Topa nangawn nial ding-te hi. Tua ahih manin thakhat thu-in siatna thuaklawh ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Hih sia manlote in a huaiham manun note kiang panin sum a ngahtheihna dingun phuahtawm thu hong gen ding-te hi. Ahi zongin sawtpeka kipanin amaute' thu a Khen dingpa a kigingsa-in om niloh a, amaute a Susia dingpa in en gige hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh vantung mite nangawn in khialhna a neih ciangin Pasian in khahkhong loin hell khuk sungah khia a, Thukhen Ni hun dong ngakin khuamial sungah kol bulhsa-in a omsak hi.
