## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Unto thee will I cry, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, awng-in nang kong sam hi; ka suangpi aw, ka tungah bengngong kei in, tua ci-in kei tungah hong dai dide lecin, Leihawm sunga paisukte tawh a kibang ka suak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, awngin nang kong sam hi. Ka belh suangpi aw, keima thu-ah hong bilngong kei in. Tua bangin kei tungah hong dai dide lecin, Leihawm sunga a paisukte tawh a kibang ka suak ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Hear the voice of my supplications, when I cry unto thee, when I lift up my hands toward thy holy oracle.

**TDB77:** Siangthopen mun lamah ka khutte laamto a, nang kiangah huhna kong nget mah bangin, kong thumna aw hong za in.

**ZVS:** Siangtho pen mun lamah ka khutte hong laamto a, nangma kiangah huhna kong nget mah bangin kong thumna aw hong za in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Draw me not away with the wicked, and with the workers of iniquity, which speak peace to their neighbours, but mischief is in their hearts.

**TDB77:** Amau lungsim sungah siatna om hi napi a vengte tawh kilemna thu a gen khem, siatna bawlte, migilote tawh kei hong lakhia khawm kei in.

**ZVS:** Amau' lungsim sungah siatna a om hinapi a vengte tawh kilemna thu a gen khem, siatna bawlte, migilote tawh kei hong lakhiakhawm kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Give them according to their deeds, and according to the wickedness of their endeavours: give them after the work of their hands; render to them their desert.

**TDB77:** Amau sepna leh amau gamtat siatnate bangin amaute thukkik in; amau khut sepna tawh kizui-in thuk in la, amau ngah dinga kilawm thaman pia in.

**ZVS:** Amau' sepna leh a gamtat siatnate uh bangin amaute thukkik in. Amau' sepna tawh a kizui-in amaute tungah thuk in la, amau' ngah ding a kilawm thaman pia in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Because they regard not the works of the

**TDB77:** Amaute in Topa’ nasepna, a khutte’ nasepna thupi ngaihsut lo ahih manin, amah in amaute susia-in a bawlkik nawn kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in { Topa' } nasepnate thupi ngaihsutlo ahih manun, amah in amaute susia-in a donkik nawnkei ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | causal: because

### v6
**EN:** Blessed be the

**TDB77:** Ka thuumna aw hong za ahih manin Topa in thupha ngah ta hen!

**ZVS:** Ka thumna aw hong za ahih manin { Topa } in thupha ngah ta hen.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v7
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ka hatna leh ka lum hi a, ka lungsim in amah muang hi; tua ahih manin kei hong kihuh a, ka lungsim nuamin diang a, ka lasakna tawh ama tungah lungdamna ka pia hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } ka hatna leh ka lum hi a, amah ka muang hi. Tua ahih manin kei hong kihuh a, lungdamin ka diang a, ka lasakna tawh ama tungah lungdamna ka ko hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ama mite’ thahatna hi a, amah in a sathau nilhpa’ kihotkhiatna buk ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } ama mite' hatna hi a, amah pen a sathau nilhpa' bukna leh a honkhiapa ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Save thy people, and bless thine inheritance: feed them also, and lift them up for ever.

**TDB77:** Na mite hon in la, na luahte thupha pia in; amau cingpa hi in la, a tawntungin amaute huh in.

**ZVS:** Na mite hon in la, na luahte thupha pia in. Amau a cingpa hi in la, a tawntungin amaute huh in.
