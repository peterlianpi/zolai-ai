## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa maang hi; liatna tawh kumpipuan kisilhsak hi; Topa, kumpipuan kisilhsak a, thahatna tawh kidim hi. Hi hi, leitung kikip sak a, kiling ngei lo ding hi;

**ZVS:** { Topa } in uk a, vangliatna kumpi puan kisilhsak hi. { Topa, } kumpi puan kisilhsak a, thahatna tawh amah kidim hi. Leitung kikipsak a, kiling ngeilo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Thy throne is established of old: thou art from everlasting.

**TDB77:** taanglai pek panin na tokhom a kipsa hi a, nang in a tawntung-a om na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Tanglai pek panin na tokhom a kipsa hi a, nang pen a tawntunga om na hi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** The floods have lifted up, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, gunte hong khangto-in, gunte in a aw uh hong sangsak a, gunte, nakpi takin hong ging hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, gunte hong khangin, a aw uh hong sangsak a, nakpi takin hong ging hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** A sangna-a om Topa vanglian hi, tui tampi gingte sangin vanglian zaw hi, tuipi hualte sangin vanglian zaw hi.

**ZVS:** A sangna munah a om { Topa } vanglian hi. Tui tampi gingte sangin vanglian zaw a, tuipi hualte sangin vanglian zaw hi.

### v5
**EN:** Thy testimonies are very sure: holiness becometh thine house, O

**TDB77:** Na thupiaknate in a hi taktak ahi hi; Topa aw, siangthona in a tawntungin na inn tawh a kilawm hi.

**ZVS:** Na thupiaknate kip mahmah hi. { Topa } aw, siangthona pen a tawntungin na inn tawh a kilawm ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
