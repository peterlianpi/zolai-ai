## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Unto thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangma tungah ka lungsim kong laamto hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangma tungah thu ka hong ngen hi.

### v2
**EN:** O my God, I trust in thee: let me not be ashamed, let not mine enemies triumph over me.

**TDB77:** Ka Pasian aw, nang kong muang hi, hong zahhuaisak kei in; ka galte ka tungah gualzo-in lungdam sak kei in.

**ZVS:** Ka Pasian aw, nangmah ka hong muang hi. Hong zahhuaisak kei in. Keima tungah ka galte gualzo-in lungdamsak kei in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** Yea, let none that wait on thee be ashamed: let them be ashamed which transgress without cause.

**TDB77:** Hi hi, nang hong ngakte kuamah zahhuaisak kei in; thu khual lo-a zuau theite zahhuaisak in.

**ZVS:** Nangmah a hong ngakte kuamah zahhuaisak kei in. Thukhual loin a zuau theite zahhuaisak in.

### v4
**EN:** Shew me thy ways, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na zia na tong thei-in hong bawl in la, na lampite hong hilh in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na zia na tong a thei-in hong bawl in la, na lampite hong hilh in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** Lead me in thy truth, and teach me: for thou art the God of my salvation; on thee do I wait all the day.

**TDB77:** Na thuman sungah kei hong paipih in la hong hilh in, bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in ka kihotkhiatna Pasian na hi hi; sunthapai-in nang kong ngak hi.

**ZVS:** Na thuman sungah kei hong paipih in la, lam hong hilh in. Bang hang hiam cih leh kei a hong hon Pasian na hi a, nang kong muang tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Remember, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, taang pek-a kipan a omsa ahih manin, na hehpihna leh na itna kip phawkkik in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, tanglai peka kipan a omsa ahih manin, na hehpihna leh na itna kip hong phawkkik in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Remember not the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions: according to thy mercy remember thou me for thy goodness' sake, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka khangno lai-a ka mawhna leh ka khialhnate ciamteh kei in la, na hoihna na ngaihsutna leh na itna kip tawh kizui-in kei hong phawk in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka khangno lai-a ka mawhna leh ka khialhnate ciamteh kei in la, na hoihna na ngaihsutna leh na itna kip tawh kizui-in kei hong phawk in!

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Good and upright is the

**TDB77:** Topa hoihin thutang hi; tua ahih manin mimawhte lam hilh hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } hoihin thutang hi. Tua ahih manin mimawhte lam a hilh hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v9
**EN:** The meek will he guide in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way.

**TDB77:** Amah in lungsim kiniamkhiat mite a man-ah paipih-in a zia a tong hilh hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in lungsim kiniamkhiatte a manah paipihin a zia a tong a hilh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** All the paths of the

**TDB77:** Ama thuciamna leh a thu a kipsakna a zuite’ adingin Topa’ lampite in itna kip leh cihtakna hi.

**ZVS:** Ama thuciamna leh a thupiak a zuite-a' dingin { Topa' } lampi pen itna kip leh cihtakna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** For thy name's sake, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka mawhna lian ahih manin, nangma min hangin keima mawhna hong maisak in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka mawhna lian ahih manin, nangma min phatna dingin keima mawhna hong maisak in.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v12
**EN:** What man is he that feareth the

**TDB77:** Topa a zahtak mi kua ahi hiam? A lampi zuih ding Pasian’ hilhna a ngah mipa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } a zahtak mi kua ahi hiam? Ama lampi zuih ding Pasian' hilhna a ngah mi ahi hi.

### v13
**EN:** His soul shall dwell at ease; and his seed shall inherit the earth.

**TDB77:** Amah nuamsa tawntung ding a, a tate in leitang nei ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** The secret of the

**TDB77:** Topa tawh kilawmtatna in amah a zahtakte’ ading hi a, a thuciamna amau tungah kitheithang sak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } tawh kilawmtatna pen amah a zahtakte-a' ding hi a, a thuciamna amau tungah kitheithangsak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** Mine eyes are ever toward the

**TDB77:** Ka mitte in Topa’ lamen tawntung hi, bang hang hiam cih leh amah in ngenthang panin ka khete sutkhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } lam ka en tawntung hi; bang hang hiam cih leh amah in ngenthang panin ka khete hong sutkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v16
**EN:** Turn thee unto me, and have mercy upon me; for I am desolate and afflicted.

**TDB77:** Keima lamah nang hong kihei in la hong hehpih in; bang hang hiam cih leh kei guak hi-in gimna ka thuak hi.

**ZVS:** Kei lamah nang hong kihei-in, kei hong hehpih in; bang hang hiam cih leh kei guakbek hi-in gimna ka thuak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** The troubles of my heart are enlarged: O bring thou me out of my distresses.

**TDB77:** Ka lungsim natnate hong damsak in la, ka gimnate panin hong paikhiatpih in.

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim natnate khempeuh hong damsak in la, ka gimnate khempeuh panin hong paikhiatpih in.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v18
**EN:** Look upon mine affliction and my pain; and forgive all my sins.

**TDB77:** Ka gimna leh ka thuaknate ngaihsun in la, ka mawhna khempeuh hong maisak in.

**ZVS:** Ka gimna leh ka thuaknate hong ngaihsun in la, ka mawhna khempeuh hong maisak in.

### v19
**EN:** Consider mine enemies; for they are many; and they hate me with cruel hatred.

**TDB77:** Ka galte bangzah takin a tamna, leh keimah hong mudah luatnate ngaihsun in.

**ZVS:** Ka galte bangzah takin a tamna leh kei hong muhdah luatnate hong ngaihsun in.

### v20
**EN:** O keep my soul, and deliver me: let me not be ashamed; for I put my trust in thee.

**TDB77:** Kei hong cing in la, hong honkhia in; nangmah hong bel ka hih manin kei hong maizumsak kei in.

**ZVS:** Kei hong cing in la, hong honkhia in. Nangmah hong bel ka hih manin kei hong maizumsak kei in.

### v21
**EN:** Let integrity and uprightness preserve me; for I wait on thee.

**TDB77:** Nang hong ngak ka hih manin, kicin’na leh thutan’na in kei hong kem ta hen.

**ZVS:** Nang hong muang ka hih manin, kicin'na leh thutan'na in kei hong kem ta hen.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix
