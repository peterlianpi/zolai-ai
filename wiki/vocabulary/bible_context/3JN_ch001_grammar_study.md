# 3 John Chapter 1 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 14 verses

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |

| negation: kei | 5 |
| plural: uh marker | 3 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The elder unto the wellbeloved Gaius, whom I love in the truth.

**TDB77:** Upa in a man takin ka it Gaias kong zasak hi.

**ZVS:** A khangham bel keimah Johan in Lungsim takpi tawh ka hong it ka lawm Gaias tungah lai ka hong khak hi.

### v2
**EN:** Beloved, I wish above all things that thou mayest prosper and be in health, even as thy soul prospereth.

**TDB77:** Ka it mi aw, na lungsim a nopsak mah bangin nangmah in na khempeuhah nopsakna leh cidamna na ngah nading thu ka ngen hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lawm it Gaias aw, nang na ci damin na vai khempeuh zong na ma pai ding hi, ci-in ka lamen hi. Kha thu tawh kisai lamsangah zong bangmah lungkhamna omlo ding hi, ci-in ka thei hi.

### v3
**EN:** For I rejoiced greatly, when the brethren came and testified of the truth that is in thee, even as thou walkest in the truth.

**TDB77:** Nang in thuman thutak na zuih mah bangin na neihsa thuman thutak ah na cihtakna thu ute naute in hong pai a, teci hong pan’ uh ciangin ka nuam mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** Nang a mantakin a gamta na hih takpi mah bangin, i Khristian mipih hih lai-a hong pai peuhpeuh in a mantakin na nuntakna thu hong gen uh ahih manin ka nuam mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v4
**EN:** I have no greater joy than to hear that my children walk in truth.

**TDB77:** Ka tate in thuman thutak lam a zuih thu ka zak cianga ka nuamna sangin nuam zawkna a om kei hi.

**ZVS:** Tate bangin ka hong neih mite a mantakin a gamtatna uh ka zakna hangin ka lungdamna sangin adang lungdamna lianzaw bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v5
**EN:** Beloved, thou doest faithfully whatsoever thou doest to the brethren, and to strangers;

**TDB77:** Ka it mi aw, nangmah in ute naute tungah ahi zongin, zinte tungah ahi zongin na bawlna khempeuh, dikna tawh kipawlin a bawl na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka hong it lawm Gaias aw, na theih ngeiloh hinapi-in i Khristian mipihte lungsim takpi tawh na na huh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Which have borne witness of thy charity before the church: whom if thou bring forward on their journey after a godly sort, thou shalt do well:

**TDB77:** Tua mite in na itna thu pawlpi mai-ah teci a pang uh hi. Nangmah in Pasian’ deihna tawh kituakin tua mite amau lampi ah na khak leh, a pha a bawl na hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute na limbawlna thu, hih lai-a pawlpite kiangah hong gen uh hi. I biak Pasian' deihna tawh kituakin a khualzinna uh nuamtakin a zoptheihna dingun na huh in.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Because that for his name's sake they went forth, taking nothing of the Gentiles.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih leh, amaute in Topa’ min hangin Gentailte lakah huhna la loin a pai uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute pen Pasian' nasem dingin a khualzin khia uh hi a, thu umlo mite tung panin bangmah huhna ngahlo uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** We therefore ought to receive such, that we might be fellowhelpers to the truth.

**TDB77:** Tua hi a, eite in thuman thutak a huh mi i suah nadingin tua bang mite zin i do ding a kilawm hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Pasian thuman nasepna-ah nasem khawm mite ihih theihna dingin ei Khristian-te in hihbang mite i huh ding ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** I wrote unto the church: but Diotrephes, who loveth to have the preeminence among them, receiveth us not.

**TDB77:** Pawlpi kiangah vaikhakna lai gelhin ka pia zo hi. Ahih hangin a uk a lian a suak nuam Diotrefes in ka thu a mang kei hi.

**ZVS:** Na pawlpite kiangah lai ka gelh ngei hi. Ahi zongin na pawlpi sungah milian pen a ut Diotrefes in ka thugen hong mang nuamlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Wherefore, if I come, I will remember his deeds which he doeth, prating against us with malicious words: and not content therewith, neither doth he himself receive the brethren, and forbiddeth them that would, and casteth them out of the church.

**TDB77:** Tua a hih ciangin ka hong pai ciangin amah in a bawlna thu ka phawksak kik ding hi. Amah in en’na, siatsak nopna kam tawh kote hong kosia tham loin, ute naute ama innah a tungsak nuam kei hi. A tungsak nuam mite zong khamin, pawlpi sung pan a hawlkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin kong pai ciangin kote hong gensiatna, zuauthu a genna, leh a gamtatna khempeuh ka pholak ding hi. Tua bang mi gensiatna ciang bek hiloin, i Khristian mipihte zin nangawn tungsak nuamlo a, zin a tungsak nuamte nangawn khamin, pawlpi panin hawlkhiat ding hanciam lai hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Beloved, follow not that which is evil, but that which is good. He that doeth good is of God: but he that doeth evil hath not seen God.

**TDB77:** Ka it mi aw, a sia lam zui kei in, a hoih lam zui in. A hoih a gamta mi in Pasian tawh kipawl hi. A hoih lo a a gamta mi in Pasian a mu nai kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ka hong it lawm Gaias aw, mi hoihlote' gamtatna zui kei in la, mihoihte' gamtatna zui in. A gamtat hoih mite pen Pasian tawh a kipawl mi hi a, a gamtat hoihlo mite pen Pasian a thei nailo ahi uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Demetrius hath good report of all men, and of the truth itself: yea, and we also bear record; and ye know that our record is true.

**TDB77:** Mi khempeuh in Demetrias ading teci a hi uh hi. Thuman thutak in zong ama teci a hi hi. Ahi hi, kote in zong tua mi lamah teci ka pang uh hi. Kote teci pan’na thu in a man hi ci-in na thei hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khempeuh in Demetrias phatkim uh a, a manin ama gamtatna mahmah in hih thu a manna hong kilangsak hi. Kote in zong ama hoihna ka thei uh a, tua ama hoihna ka gennate uh a thuman ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** I had many things to write, but I will not with ink and pen write unto thee:

**TDB77:** Ka gelh ding thu tampi a om hangin laikung leh laitui tawh gelh ding ka deih kei hi.

**ZVS:** Gen ding tampi ka neih hangin lai-in gelhkhiat ding kong sawm kei zaw hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v14
**EN:** But I trust I shall shortly see thee, and we shall speak face to face. Peace be to thee. Our friends salute thee. Greet the friends by name.

**TDB77:** A sawt loin nang tawh kimu-in, khat leh khat kihopihin i kikup khop ding ka lamen hi.

**ZVS:** Nang tawh kimu baih thei dingin kilamen ka hih manin, tuahun ciangin maitang kimu-in i kiho ding hi. Nangma tungah nopsakna thupha hong tung ta hen. Hih lai-a om i lawmte in hong hopih uh hi. Tua lai-a om i lawmte khempeuh a milipin na hopihsak in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix
