# Psalms Chapter 3 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 8 verses

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 2 |
| quotative: said/saying | 1 |
| plural: uh marker | 1 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 1 |

## Verses

### v2
**EN:** Many there be which say of my soul, There is no help for him in God. Selah.

**TDB77:** “Pasian in amah huh lo ding,” mi tampi in hong ci uh hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in amah huhlo ding, mi tampi in hong ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** But thou, O

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin, Topa aw, nangmah in ka kidalna lum, ka minthan’na leh ka lu hong phongpa na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin { Topa } aw, nang pen ka kidalna lum, ka minthan'na leh ka lu hong phongpa na hi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** I cried unto the

**TDB77:** Nakpi in Topa ka gal sap a, amah in a mual siangtho panin hong dawng hi.

**ZVS:** Nakpi takin { Topa } ka galsap a, amah in a mual siangtho panin kei a hong dawng hi.

### v5
**EN:** I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the

**TDB77:** Lum-in ka ihmu a, Topa in hong kem ahih manin ka thokik hi.

**ZVS:** Lumin ka ihmu a, { Topa } in hong kem ahih manin ka thokik hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because

### v6
**EN:** I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set themselves against me round about.

**TDB77:** Keimah hong galbawl, hong umcih mi tulsawm tampite ka kihta kei hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah a hong galbawl keimah a hong umcih mi tul sawm tampite ka kihta kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Arise, O

**TDB77:** Tho in, Topa! Kei hong hon in, ka Pasian! Bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in ka galte khempeuh’ paang na satgawp a, migilote’ ha na su-tan hi.

**ZVS:** Tho in, { Topa}! Kei hong hon in, ka Pasian! Bang hang hiam cih leh ka galte khempeuh' paang nangmah in na beenggawp a, migilote' ha na sutan hi.

### v8
**EN:** Salvation belongeth unto the

**TDB77:** Hotkhiatna in Topa, Nangma hang ahi hi; na mite tungah na thupha hong tung ta hen!

**ZVS:** Gualzawhna pen { Topa } nangma hang ahi hi. Na mite tungah na thupha hong tung ta hen!
