| Pattern | Count |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my prayer.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka kikona aw hong za in la, ka thungetna hong ngai in,

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, ka kikona aw hong za in la, ka thungetna hong ngai in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

**TDB77:** ka lungsim a neu ciangin, leimong panin nang kong sam hi. Kei sanga a sangzaw suangpi tungah nangmah in hong paipih in;

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim a neu ciangin, leimong panin nang kong sam hi. Keimah sangin a sangzaw suangpi tungah hong paipih in.

### v3
**EN:** For thou hast been a shelter for me, and a strong tower from the enemy.

**TDB77:** bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in keima bukna na hi a, galte’ langah tausang muan’huai na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nang, keima bukna na hi a, galte' langah tausang muan'huai na hi hi.

### v4
**EN:** I will abide in thy tabernacle for ever: I will trust in the covert of thy wings. Selah.

**TDB77:** Na buk sungah hong omsak tawntung ta in! Nangma khate’ hutna sungah lungmuanga om dingin!

**ZVS:** Na buk sungah hong omsak tawntung in la, nangma khate' hutna sungah lungmuangin hong omsak in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** For thou, O God, hast heard my vows: thou hast given me the heritage of those that fear thy name.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian aw, ka thuciamnate nangmah in na za khin a, nangmah hong zahtakte’ luahna keimah nong pia khin ta hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian aw, ka thuciamnate khempeuh nangmah in na za khin a, nangmah a hong zahtak mite' ngah dingte keimah nong pia khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v6
**EN:** Thou wilt prolong the king's life: and his years as many generations.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa’ khan’ to sak in; khang tawntungin amah nungta sak in.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa khan'tosak in la, khang tawntungin nungtasak in.

### v7
**EN:** He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare mercy and truth, which may preserve him.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ mai-ah amah om ngitnget hen la, ama itna kip leh a thumanna in amah kem ta hen.

**ZVS:** Pasian' mai-ah amah om ngitnget hen la, ama itna kip leh a thumanna in amah kem ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** So will I sing praise unto thy name for ever, that I may daily perform my vows.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin ni simin ka thuciamnate bangin ta-in, na min phatna ka sasa ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin nisimin ka thuciamnate bangin gamta-in, na min phatna ka sasa ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will
