## Verses

### v1
**EN:** As snow in summer, and as rain in harvest, so honour is not seemly for a fool.

**TDB77:** Tuk lai tak-a vukkia, leh an lak hun-a guahzu bangin, pahtawina in mihaite’ adingin a kilawmna om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Tuk laitaka vuk kia leh anlak huna guahzu bangin mihaite-a' dingin pahtawina a kilawmna omlo hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2

**TDB77:** Thu om lopi-in hong kisamsiatna in bangmah hong cih thei lo a, khawlna mun mel a nei lo a lengsuk a lengto Phialphiah tawh kibang lel hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
