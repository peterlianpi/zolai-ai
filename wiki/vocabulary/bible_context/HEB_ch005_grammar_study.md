## Verses

### v1
**EN:** For every high priest taken from among men is ordained for men in things pertaining to God, that he may offer both gifts and sacrifices for sins:

**TDB77:** Mi lakah a kiteelkhia siampi ukpa in mawhna taang ding biaknate leh piaknate mite ading a pia dingin Pasian tawh kipawl nate-ah a kikoih hi.

**ZVS:** Siampi lianpen ahi peuhpeuh mipa pen a mite' mawhna hangin Pasian' tungah biakna leh letsong a piaksak dingin ama mipihte lak panin kiteelin nasep a kiguan' ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Who can have compassion on the ignorant, and on them that are out of the way; for that he himself also is compassed with infirmity.

**TDB77:** Amah in thanem ahih ciangin a pil lo mite, a mawh mite a thuakpih dingin a siam hi.

**ZVS:** Amah zong lam tampi-ah a ginalo thei mi mah ahih manin thu theilo mite leh a khial mite tungah lungnem takin na sem thei hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** And by reason hereof he ought, as for the people, so also for himself, to offer for sins.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin mite mawhna leh ama mawhna taang dingin biakna a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Amah zong miginalo mi mah ahih manin mite' mawhna hangin biakna a pia hi bek loin ama mawhna mahmah hangin zong biakna a pia ahi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** And no man taketh this honour unto himself, but he that is called of God, as was Aaron.

**TDB77:** Pasian in Aaron a sap bangin a sap mite longal, kuamah in tua za a lian a la thei kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bangin pahtawina a nei siampi lianpen nasep pen a semdingte amau utthu tawh a kisehtawm mawk hilo hi; siampi lianpen a sem dingin amahmah a kisehtawm hilo a, Aaron a kisap mah bangin Pasian in amah a seh ahi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v5
**EN:** So also Christ glorified not himself to be made an high priest; but he that said unto him, Thou art my Son, to day have I begotten thee.

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin Khazih in zong siampi ukpa za a ngah nadingin ama pumpi a pahtawi kei hi. “Nang in ka Tapa na hi hi; Tuni-in nang kong piangsak hi,” ci-in a thu a piapa in Khazih a pahtawi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua mah bangin Khrih zong, amahmah in siampi lianpen nasep a semtawm mawk hiloin Pasian in ama kiangah, “Nang, ka Tapa na hi hi. Tuni-in na Pa ka suak hi,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | negation: kei

### v6
**EN:** As he saith also in another place, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchisedec.

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin Lai Siangtho mun khatah a gen-inah: “Nang in Melkhizedek’ pawl a tawntung Siampipa na hi hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Mun dang khatah zong, “Melkhizedek siampi gui zui-in a tawntungin siampi na hi ding hi,” na ci lai hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v7
**EN:** Who in the days of his flesh, when he had offered up prayers and supplications with strong crying and tears unto him that was able to save him from death, and was heard in that he feared;

**TDB77:** Tua Khazih in mi pumpi bangin a om lai takin, sihna pan a honkhia zopa kiangah kikona, khitui luan’na tawh thungenin thuum a, Pasian kihtakna lungsim hangin thungaihna ngahin,

**ZVS:** Leitungah mihingin Jesuh a omlai-in sihna panin amah a honkhia theiding Pasian tungah thu ngenin khitui naptui tawh thumin na kiko hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Though he were a Son, yet learned he obedience by the things which he suffered;

**TDB77:** Ta taktak ahi napi-in gimna tawlna a thuakna tawh thuman’na a sin hi.

**ZVS:** Amah, Pasian' Tapa hinapi-in a thuakna tawh thumang ahih theihna ding a na kisin hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v9
**EN:** And being made perfect, he became the author of eternal salvation unto all them that obey him;

**TDB77:** Tua bangin a kicin’na a tun’ khit ciangin a thumang khempeuhte ading gup tawntungna a bawl mi suak a,

**ZVS:** Tua bangin a cingtaak mi a suahkhit ciangin ama thu a mang mite khempeuh-a' dingin tawntung hotkhiatna a kipatna hong suak a,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Called of God an high priest after the order of Melchisedec.

**TDB77:** Melkhizedek’ pawl siampi lian hi ci-in Pasian in a min a phuak hi.

**ZVS:** Melkhizedek gui zui-in siampi lianpen dingin Pasian in a bawl hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** Of whom we have many things to say, and hard to be uttered, seeing ye are dull of hearing.

**TDB77:** Tua Melkhizedek thu kote gen ding tampi a om hi. Ahih hangin note bil pil lo na hih uh ciangin a gen nading a haksa hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thu tawh kisai-in i gen ding tampi a om hangin note in thu thei hak mahmah na hih manun i gentel theihna ding haksa hi.

### v12
**EN:** For when for the time ye ought to be teachers, ye have need that one teach you again which be the first principles of the oracles of God; and are become such as have need of milk, and not of strong meat.

**TDB77:** Note in a sawt vei zo-in thuhilhte a suak khin ding na kilawm napi-un, Pasian thupiakna lakah a kisim masa, a bulpi thute, midangte kiangah khatvei na sin kik nading uh om ahih ciangin zan hak an sam loin, nawizu nete bek na hi uh hi!

**ZVS:** Tu-in note pen midang a hilh dingin sia na suahtheih ding uh hun hikhin napi-in note mahmah pen Pasian' thu a bulpite hilhding na kisam lai uh hi. Antak nethei nailoin nawi bek a nethei lai na hi uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** For every one that useth milk is unskilful in the word of righteousness: for he is a babe.

**TDB77:** Nawi bek a ne mi in naupang bang ahih ciangin dikna tawh kipawl thu a siam kei hi.

**ZVS:** Nawi bek a ne laite pen naungek ahih manin a man leh a manlo thu bangmah phawkthei nailo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** But strong meat belongeth to them that are of full age, even those who by reason of use have their senses exercised to discern both good and evil.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin an tak in a sia a pha khen theihna pilna a nei, zakna muhnate zangin zongsang a, amau leh amau a kiseksa, a picing mite’ ading ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin antak i cih pen a sia leh a pha a khenthei, a lim leh a limlo a khentheita, a khangham khinsa mite-a' ding ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
