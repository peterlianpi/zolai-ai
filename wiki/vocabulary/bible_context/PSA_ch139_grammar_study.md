## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangmah in kei nong entel-a nong thei khin hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangmah in kei na hong entel a, nong thei khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v2
**EN:** Thou knowest my downsitting and mine uprising, thou understandest my thought afar off.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in ka heksukna, ka hektohna khempeuh na thei hi; nangmah in ka ngaihsutnate gamlapi pek panin na thei hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in ka heksukna, ka hektohnate na thei hi. Nangmah in ka ngaihsutnate gamlapi pek panin na thei hi.

### v3
**EN:** Thou compassest my path and my lying down, and art acquainted with all my ways.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in ka sep laitak ahi a, ka khawl laitak ahi zongin nong mu a, ka gamtatnate khempeuh na thei hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in ka sep laitak ahi a, ka khawl laitak ahi zongin na hong mu a, ka gamtatna khempeuh a vekpi-in na thei hi.

### v4
**EN:** For there is not a word in my tongue, but, lo, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kam khat bek nangawn ka paukhiat ma-in, nangmah in ka gen ding teng a vekpi in na thei khin hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kam khat bek nangawn ka paukhiat ma-in, nangmah in ka gen dingteng a vekpi-in na thei khin hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | completed: already done

### v5

**ZVS:** Nangmah in ka nung leh ka mai-ah nong dal a, na thahatna tawh kei na hong hu hi.

### v6

**TDB77:** Tua bangin kei nong theihna pen kei adingin lamdang lua mahmah a, thuthuk lua ahih manin kei’ theihtel zawh ding hi lo hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | purpose: in order to | future: will | negation: lo | causal: because

### v7

**ZVS:** Nangmah a pelh dingin koi-ah pai ding a, nangma omna panin koi lai-ah taikhia ding ka hi hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v8

**TDB77:** Vantungah kahto leng tua lai-ah nang na om a, Leinuai misite gamah lupna bawl ta leng zong tua lai-ah nang na om veve ding hi.

**ZVS:** tua lai nangawnah nangma khut in kei hong paipihin, na khut taklam in kei hong len ding hi.

### v11

**ZVS:** tua nangawnah, nangma-a' dingin khuamial pen miallo a, zan khuamial pen sun nitaang tawh kibang hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh nang-a' dingin khuamial pen khuavak mah bang lel hi.
