## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Come down, and sit in the dust, O virgin daughter of Babylon, sit on the ground: there is no throne, O daughter of the Chaldeans: for thou shalt no more be called tender and delicate.

**TDB77:** Nungak a bang Babilon aw, hong paisuk in la, leivui lakah tu in; nungak a bang Khaldea aw, tokhom om lohna lei lakah hong tusuk in! Bang hang hiam cih leh nang pen, a moi milmel, a ngeek dikdek, hong kici nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nungak a bang Babylon aw, hong paisuk in la, leivui lakah tu in. Nungak a bang Khaldea aw, tokhom omlohna leilakah hong tusuk in! Bang hang hiam cih leh nang pen a moi milmel, a ngeek dikdek, hong kici nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Take the millstones, and grind meal: uncover thy locks, make bare the leg, uncover the thigh, pass over the rivers.

**TDB77:** An gawina suangte la in la, an gawi in; na kikhuhna puan lakhia in la, na nik na puan suah-in gunte kantan in.

**ZVS:** An gawina suangte hei in la, an gawi in. Na kikhuhna puan lakhia in la, na nik na puan suahin gunte kantan in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Thy nakedness shall be uncovered, yea, thy shame shall be seen: I will take vengeance, and I will not meet thee as a man.

**TDB77:** Na selsim teng kilang ding a, na maizumna teng kimu ding hi. Keimah in kong thukkik ding a, kuamah ka zah kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na selsim teng kilang ding a, na zumhuaina teng kimu ding hi. Keimah in kong thukkik ding a, kuamah ka zah kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** As for our redeemer, the

**TDB77:** Eite hong Tanpa— Vanglianpen Topa cihna min a neipa— in Israelte’ Siangtho Pa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Israel-te' Siangtho Pa in eite hong suaktasak a, ama min pen Vanglian { Topa } ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** Sit thou silent, and get thee into darkness, O daughter of the Chaldeans: for thou shalt no more be called, The lady of kingdoms.

**TDB77:** Babilon aw, gamdai takin tu in la, khuamialna sungah pai in; bang hang hiam cih leh nang pen, kumpi gamte’ kumpinu hong kici nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Babylon aw, gamdai takin tu in la, khuamialna sungah pai in; bang hang hiam cih leh nang pen kumpi gamte' tonu, hong kici nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** I was wroth with my people, I have polluted mine inheritance, and given them into thine hand: thou didst shew them no mercy; upon the ancient hast thou very heavily laid thy yoke.

**TDB77:** Ka mite’ tungah ka heh a, keima luah gamh ka ninsak hi; nangma khut sungah amaute kong pia a, nang in amaute na hehpih kei hi; a khangham mite tungah na hakkol na giksak mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** Ka mite tungah ka heh a, ka luah gamh ka ninsak hi. Nangma khutsungah amaute kong pia a, nangmah in amaute na hehpih bawl kei hi. A khangham mite nangawn na ngong bawl mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | negation: kei

### v7
**EN:** And thou saidst, I shall be a lady for ever: so that thou didst not lay these things to thy heart, neither didst remember the latter end of it.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in, “Kei pen, tonu ka hi tawntung ding hi,” ci na hih manin hih thute na lungsim ah koih loin, bang thu piang thei cih zong na ngaihsun kha kei hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in, “Tonu ka hi tawntung ding hi,” ci na hih manin hih thute na lungsimah koihloin, a thumongte zong na phawkkha kei hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | causal: because

### v8
**EN:** Therefore hear now this, thou that art given to pleasures, that dwellest carelessly, that sayest in thine heart, I am, and none else beside me; I shall not sit as a widow, neither shall I know the loss of children:

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin tu-in, a nuam bek a ut, lungmuang tak-a a tu hit-hiat, na lungsim sungah, “Keimah hi’ng, kei sim loh midang om kei’ meigongin om kei ning, ta-khuai cih zong sin kei ning,” a ci khuapi aw, hih thu za in:

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin tu-in, a nuam bek a ut, lungmuang takin a tu hithiat, na lungsimah, “Keimah hi'ng, kei simloh midang om kei; meigongin om ngei kei ning, ta-khuai cih zong sin kei ning,” a ci khuapi aw, hih thu za in:

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v9
**EN:** But these two things shall come to thee in a moment in one day, the loss of children, and widowhood: they shall come upon thee in their perfection for the multitude of thy sorceries, and for the great abundance of thine enchantments.

**TDB77:** Aisan siamnate leh mibum siamna vang lianpi na neih hangin ta khuaisuahna leh meigon’na, hih thu nihte thakhat thu-in ni khat sungin a kicing takin nang tungah hong tung veve ding hi.

**ZVS:** Aisan siamnate leh mibum siamna vang lianpi na neih hangin ta khuaisuahna leh meigon'na, hih thu nihte thakhat thu-in ni khat sungin a kicing takin nangma tungah hong tung veve ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** For thou hast trusted in thy wickedness: thou hast said, None seeth me. Thy wisdom and thy knowledge, it hath perverted thee; and thou hast said in thine heart, I am, and none else beside me.

**TDB77:** Na gitlohna ah nang na lung muang a, “Kuamah in kei hong mu lo hi,” na ci hi; na pilna lehna theihnate in lam hong pialsak a, na lungsim sungah, “Keimah hi’ng, kei sim loh midang om kei,” na ci hi.

**ZVS:** Na gitlohna-ah nang na lungmuang a, “Kuamah in kei hong mu kei hi,” na ci hi. Na pilna leh na theihnate in lam hong pialsak a, na lungsim sungah, “Keimah hi'ng, kei simloh midang om kei,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | negation: lo

### v11
**EN:** Therefore shall evil come upon thee; thou shalt not know from whence it riseth: and mischief shall fall upon thee; thou shalt not be able to put it off: and desolation shall come upon thee suddenly, which thou shalt not know.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin nangmah in na beisak theih loh tuahsiatna na tungah hong tung ding hi; nangmah in maisakna na ngah theih loh gimna nang tungah hong tung ding hi; nang in a thu na theih hetloh siatna na tungah thakhatin hong tung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin nangmah in na beisak theihloh lungkhamna na tungah hong tung ding a, nangmah in maisakna na ngah theihloh gimna nang tungah hong tung ding hi. A thu na theih hetloh siatna na tungah hong tungvat ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v12
**EN:** Stand now with thine enchantments, and with the multitude of thy sorceries, wherein thou hast laboured from thy youth; if so be thou shalt be able to profit, if so be thou mayest prevail.

**TDB77:** Na neu lai-a kipan na sep ahi na mibumna leh na aisannate ah kip takin om in; phattuamna na ngah kha ding a, mite na lausak zo kha ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na neulai-a kipan na sep na mibumna leh na aisannate-ah kiptakin om in. Phattuamna na ngah khading a, mite na lausakzo khading hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Thou art wearied in the multitude of thy counsels. Let now the astrologers, the stargazers, the monthly prognosticators, stand up, and save thee from these things that shall come upon thee.

**TDB77:** Nang tungah hong kilak thu tampite in nang hong gimsak hi; vantungte a seh-in khenin, aksite en a, khadet simin nang tunga thu piang ding hong genkhol mite ding sak in la, nangmah kihonsak in.

**ZVS:** Nang tungah a hong kilak thu tampite hangin na gim hi. Vantungte a sehin khenin, aksite en a, khadet simin nang tunga thupiang ding a hong genkhol mite tu-in dingsak in la, nangmah kihonsak in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** Behold, they shall be as stubble; the fire shall burn them; they shall not deliver themselves from the power of the flame: there shall not be a coal to warm at, nor fire to sit before it.

**TDB77:** En in, amaute pen buhkung tawh kibang uh a, mei in amaute kangtum hi; meikuang’ vangliatna panin amau leh amau kihontawm thei lo uh hi. Hih pen khua lum nading mei-am hi lo a, awi ding mei zong hi lo hi.

**ZVS:** En in, amaute pen buhkung tawh kibang uh a, mei in amaute kangtum hi. Meikuang' vangliatna panin amau leh amau kihontawm theilo uh hi. Hih pen khua lumna ding mei-am hilo a, awi ding mei zong hilo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** Thus shall they be unto thee with whom thou hast laboured, even thy merchants, from thy youth: they shall wander every one to his quarter; none shall save thee.

**TDB77:** Nang tawh na a semkhawm, na neu lai-a kipan nang tawh na a semkhawmte tungah hih thute hong tung ding hi; amaute pen amau’ tum ciatah zauvak uh hi; nang hong hon ding kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Nang tawh na a semkhawm, na neu lai-a kipan nang tawh na a semkhawmte tungah hih thute hong tung ding hi. Amaute pen amau' tum ciatah zauvak uh a, nang a hong hon ding kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
