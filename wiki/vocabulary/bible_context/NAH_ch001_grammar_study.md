## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The burden of Nineveh. The book of the vision of Nahum the Elkoshite.

**TDB77:** Hih in Nineveh khua tawh kisai thugenna hi a, Elkosh khuami Nahum’ mangmuhna a kiciamtehna lai ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih pen Nineveh khua tawh kisai thu genna hi a, Elkosh khuami Nahum' maangmuhna a kiciamtehna lai ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** God is jealous, and the

**TDB77:** Topa Pasian in amah a demte koihkhong lo a, amah a langpangte daantatin, a galte thukkik hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } Pasian in amah a demte koihkhonglo a, amah a langpangte gim pia-in a galte thukkik hi.

### v3
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa a lungduai mahmah a, a vang zong lian a, ahih hangin mimawhte khahkhong ngei lo hi. A paina peuhpeuh ah huihpi nungin, pingpei kipei a, ama paina hanga leivui leeng in meiipite ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } lungduai mahmah a, a vang zong lian mahmah a, ahih hangin mimawhte khahkhong ngeilo hi. A paina peuhpeuhah huihpi nungin pingpei kipei a, ama paina hanga leivui leeng meiipi kai tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** He rebuketh the sea, and maketh it dry, and drieth up all the rivers: Bashan languisheth, and Carmel, and the flower of Lebanon languisheth.

**TDB77:** Amah in tuipi a taii ciangin tuipi kangcip pah a, gunpite zong kangcip sak hi. Bashan gam keu gawpin, Karmel Mual kausan a, Lebanon Mual-a pakpalhte vul hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in tuipi a tai ciangin tuipi kangcip pah a, gunpite zong kangcipsak hi. Bashan gam keugawpin Karmel Mual kausan a, Lebanon Mual-a om pakpalhte a vul hi.

### v5
**EN:** The mountains quake at him, and the hills melt, and the earth is burned at his presence, yea, the world, and all that dwell therein.

**TDB77:** Ama mai-ah mualliante liing a, Mualneute tuisuak hi. Ama mai-ah leitang kicim a, leitung leh a tunga om khempeuh liing-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ama mai-ah mual liante ling a, mual neute a tuisuak hi. Ama mai-ah leitang kicim a, leitung leh a tunga om khempeuh a ling-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Who can stand before his indignation? and who can abide in the fierceness of his anger? his fury is poured out like fire, and the rocks are thrown down by him.

**TDB77:** Ama hehna kua in nang zo ding a, a lunghamna kua in thuak zo ding ahi hiam? A hehna meikuang bangin hong khahkhia a, ama mai-ah suangtumpi kitamzan hi.

**ZVS:** Ama hehna kua in nangzo ding a, ama lunghamna kua in thuakzo ding ahi hiam? A hehna, meikuang bangin hong khahkhia a, ama mai-ah suangtumpite kitamzan hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa hoih hi; lungkham hunin a mite huh a, amah a bel mite lim takin kem hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } hoih hi; lungkham hunin a mite huh a, amah a beel mite limtakin a kem hi.

**Grammar:** adjective: hoih (good)

### v8
**EN:** But with an overrunning flood he will make an utter end of the place thereof, and darkness shall pursue his enemies.

**TDB77:** Tuikhang in a suksiat bangin a langte susiagawp a, a galte, khuamial sungah nawhkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Tuikhang in a suksiat bangin a langte susiagawp a, a galte khempeuh khuamial sungah a nawtkhia hi.

### v9
**EN:** What do ye imagine against the

**TDB77:** Bang hangin Topa sawm ngam na hi hiam? Amah in nang hong susia ding hi. A su peuhpeuh a dingkik zo om ngei lo hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hangin { Topa } sawm ngam na hi hiam? Amah in nang hong susia ding hi. Amah a su peuhpeuh a dingkikzo om ngeilo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo

### v10
**EN:** For while they be folden together as thorns, and while they are drunken as drunkards, they shall be devoured as stubble fully dry.

**TDB77:** Amaute, linglom bangin om-te a, zu leh sa tawh kidiahin a om lai takun ham bangin kihaltum ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute, linglom bangin om-te a, zu leh sa tawh kidiahin a om laitakun ham bangin kihaltum ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** There is one come out of thee, that imagineth evil against the

**TDB77:** Nineveh khuapi aw, gitlohna tawh thu vaihawmin, Topa a sawm, nang’ sungah mi khat om hilo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Nineveh khuapi aw, gitlohna tawh thu vaihawmin, { Topa } a sawm mite, nang' sungah om hilo ahi hiam?

### v12
**EN:** Thus saith the

**TDB77:** Topa in ama mite tungah hih bangin thu gen a, “Assiria mite thahat-in a tam mahmah hangin, amaute kisusia-in maimang ding hi. No ka mite na hih-te leh, khat vei kong gimsak khit hangin, kong gimsak kik nawn kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in ama mite tungah hih bangin thu gen a, “Assiria mite thahatin a tam mahmah hangin, amaute kisusia-in maimang ding-te hi. No ka mite na hih-te leh, khatvei lai-in kong gimsak khit hangin tu-in note kong gimsakkik nawn kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** For now will I break his yoke from off thee, and will burst thy bonds in sunder.

**TDB77:** Note tungah Assiria mite’ aana neihna ka beisak ta ding a, note hong khihna sikkhaute ka sutat ding hi,” ci hi.

**ZVS:** Note' tungah Assiria mite' aana neihna ka beisak ding a, note hong khihna sikkhaute ka sutat ding hi,” ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will

### v14
**EN:** And the

**TDB77:** Topa in Assiria mite tawh kisai-in hih bangin thu pia hi: “Note na cimang ding-te a, note’ pasiante’ biakinn sunga om milimte ka susia ding hi. Note siahuai lua na hih manun, na hankhuk ding-te a bawlbawl ka hi ta hi,” ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in Assiria mite tawh kisai-in hih bangin thu pia hi: “Note na cimang ding-te a, na pasian-te-te biakinn sunga om milimte ka susia ding hi. Note siahuai lua na hih manun na hankhuk ding-te a bawlbawl ka hi ta hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** Behold upon the mountains the feet of him that bringeth good tidings, that publisheth peace! O Judah, keep thy solemn feasts, perform thy vows: for the wicked shall no more pass through thee; he is utterly cut off.

**TDB77:** En un, mual tungah kamtaipa hong tai a, gualzawhna thupha hong gen ding ahi hi. Judah mite aw, pawi bawl un la, Pasian’ tunga na ciam bangun gamta un. Bang hang hiam cih leh mi gilote kisusiacip khin a, na gam-te hong simkik ngei nawn lo ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** En un, mualtungah kamtaipa hong tai a, gualzawhna ding thupha hong gen ding ahi hi. Judah mite aw, pawi bawl un la, Pasian' tungah na ciam bangun gamta un. Bang hang hiam cih leh migilote kisusiacip khin a, na gam-te hong simkik nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
