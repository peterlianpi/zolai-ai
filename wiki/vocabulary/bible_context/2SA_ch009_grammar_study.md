### v1
**EN:** And David said, Is there yet any that is left of the house of Saul, that I may shew him kindness for Jonathan's sake?

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin David in, “Jonathan hangin ama tungah migitna ka lah theih nading Saul’ innkuan sungah mi khatpeuh a om lai hiam?” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore

### v2

**ZVS:** Saul' innkuan sungah Ziba min nei nasempa khat om a, amaute in David kiangah amah sam uh hi. Kumpipa in ama kiangah, “Ziba na hi hiam?” a ci hi. Amah in, “Na nasempa kei pen Ziba ka hi hi,” a ci hi.
