## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Except the

**TDB77:** Topa in inn lam kei leh, a lam mite’ nasepna in a mawkna ahi hi. Topa in khuapi hu kei leh, khuapi cingte’ cin’na in a mawkna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in inn lampih kei leh innlamte' sepna a mawkna ahi hi. { Topa } in khuapi hutpih kei leh khuapi cingte' cin'na a mawkna ahi hi.

### v2
**EN:** It is vain for you to rise up early, to sit up late, to eat the bread of sorrows: for so he giveth his beloved sleep.

**TDB77:** Zingsangin tho baih-in, gimpi-a nangma’ sepsa an na nek hakna in a mawkna ahi hi; bang hang hiam cih leh amah in ama itte tungah a ihmut sung nangawnun a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Zingsangin tho baihin, gimpi-a nangma sepsa an na nek hakna zong a mawkna mah ahi hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in ama itte tungah a ihmut sung nangawnun a pia hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** Lo, children are an heritage of the

**TDB77:** En un, tapate pen Topa’ tung pan-a letsong hi a, tate pen ama hong piak thupha ahi hi.

**ZVS:** En un, tate pen { Topa' } tung pana letsong hi a, tapate pen ama hong piak thupha ahi hi.

### v4
**EN:** As arrows are in the hand of a mighty man; so are children of the youth.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in a khangno lai-in a neih tapate pen galhangpa’ khut sunga om thaltangte tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat in a khangno lai-in a neih tate pen galhangpa' khutsunga a om a thaltangte tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Happy is the man that hath his quiver full of them: they shall not be ashamed, but they shall speak with the enemies in the gate.

**TDB77:** A thallawng dimin tua bang thaltang a neipa in mi lungdam ahi hi. Amah guallel-in zahhuai ngeilo ding hi.

**ZVS:** A thallawng dimin tua bang thaltang a neipa mi lungdam ahi hi. Amah guallel ngeilo ding a, zahhuai ngeilo ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will
