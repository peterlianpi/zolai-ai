## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And the lot of the children of Joseph fell from Jordan by Jericho, unto the water of Jericho on the east, to the wilderness that goeth up from Jericho throughout mount Beth–el,

**TDB77:** Josef suan-le-khakte’ tanh in Jerikho tuite’ nisuahna lam, Jerikho kiang Jordan panin sehnel gamah pai a, Jerikho panin mualtung gamte tawnin Beth-el ah paito hi;

**ZVS:** Josef' suanlekhakte' tanh gamgi pen Jerikho luite' nisuahna lam, Jerikho kiang Jordan panin sehnel gamah pai a, Jerikho panin mualtung gamte tawnin Bethel-ah paito hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** And goeth out from Beth–el to Luz, and passeth along unto the borders of Archi to Ataroth,

**TDB77:** tua ciangin Beth-el panin Luz-ah pai-in, Arkhi mite gam Ataroth nawk hi;

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Bethel panin Luz-ah pai-in, Arkhi mite gam Ataroth nawk hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** And goeth down westward to the coast of Japhleti, unto the coast of Beth–horon the nether, and to Gezer: and the goings out thereof are at the sea.

**TDB77:** tua ciangin nitumna lam Jaflet mite gam lam Bethhoron a niam lam gam ciang dongah paisuk hi, tua ciangin Gezer-ah pai-in tuipi-ah tawp hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin nitumna lam Jaflet mite' gam lam Bethhoron a niam lam gam ciangdongah paisuk hi. Tua ciangin Gezer-ah pai-in tuipi-ah tawp hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** So the children of Joseph, Manasseh and Ephraim, took their inheritance.

**TDB77:** Josef suan Manasseh, leh Efraim mite in amau’ gamhluah ngah-te hi.

**ZVS:** Josef' suan Manasseh, leh Efraim mite in amau' gamhluah dingin hih gamte ngah-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** And the border of the children of Ephraim according to their families was thus: even the border of their inheritance on the east side was Ataroth–addar, unto Beth–horon the upper;

**TDB77:** Amau' behbehin Efraim mite’ gam in anuai-a bang ahi hi: nisuahna lamah amau’ gamh gamgi in Ataroth-addar, Bethhoron tungnung ciang ahi hi;

**ZVS:** Amau' behbehin Efraim mite' gam pen a nuai-a bang ahi hi: Nisuahna lamah amau' gamgi pen Atarothaddar pan Bethhoron tungnung ciang ahi hi.

### v6
**EN:** And the border went out toward the sea to Michmethah on the north side; and the border went about eastward unto Taanath–shiloh, and passed by it on the east to Janohah;

**TDB77:** tua panin tuipi ciangah gamgi pai hi; leilu lamah Mikmethath ahi hi; tua ciangin nisuahna lam gamgi Ta-anathshiloh lamah kual pai-in, nisuahna lam a gal khatah pai-in Janoah tung hi;

**ZVS:** Tua panin tuipi ciangah a gamgi pai a, leilu lamah Mikmethath ahi hi. Tua ciangin nisuahna lam a gamgi Taanathshiloh lamah kual pai-in, nisuahna lam galah pai-in Janoah a tung hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** And it went down from Janohah to Ataroth, and to Naarath, and came to Jericho, and went out at Jordan.

**TDB77:** tua ciangin Janoah panin Ataroth leh Na-arah-ah paisuk a, Jerikho ban-in Jordan-ah tawp hi.

**ZVS:** Janoah panin Ataroth leh Naarah-ah paisuk a, Jerikho banin Jordan-ah tawp hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** The border went out from Tappuah westward unto the river Kanah; and the goings out thereof were at the sea. This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Ephraim by their families.

**TDB77:** Tappuah panin nitumna lam Kanah lui-ah gamgi pai-in, tuipi-ah tawp hi. Tua in amau’ behbehin Efraim mite’ gamhluah hi a,

**ZVS:** Tappuah panin nitumna lam Kanah lui-ah gamgi pai-in tuipi-ah tawp hi. Hih pen amau' behbehin Efraim minamte' gamhluah hi a,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v9
**EN:** And the separate cities for the children of Ephraim were among the inheritance of the children of Manasseh, all the cities with their villages.

**TDB77:** Manasseh mite’ gamluah sung panin Efraim mite’ adingin a kikhen khia khuapite, a khua neute tawh ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Manasseh mite' gamhluah sung panin Efraim mite-a' dingin a kikhenkhia khuapite, a khuaneute tawh ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** And they drave not out the Canaanites that dwelt in Gezer: but the Canaanites dwell among the Ephraimites unto this day, and serve under tribute.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin amaute in Gezer sunga teeng Kanaan mite hawl khia lo-te hi: tua ahih ciangin tu ni dongin Kanaan mite in Efraim mite lakah teeng a, ahih hangin aana nasep a sem dingin sal suak-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin amaute in Gezer sunga teng Kanaan mite hawlkhialo-te hi. Tua ahih ciangin tuni dongin Kanaan mite pen Efraim mite lakah teng a, ahih hangin aana nasep a sem dingin sal a suak-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to | negation: lo | plural: uh marker
