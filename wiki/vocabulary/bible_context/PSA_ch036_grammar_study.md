## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |

| plural: uh marker | 4 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The transgression of the wicked saith within my heart, that there is no fear of God before his eyes.

**TDB77:** Thu palsatna in migilopa’ lungsim tawngah thu gen a, ama mai-ah Pasian zahtakna om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Mawhna in migilopa' lungsim tawngah thu gen a, amah in Pasian zahtakna nei hetlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v2
**EN:** For he flattereth himself in his own eyes, until his iniquity be found to be hateful.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ama mawhna kidawk khia lo, muhdahna thuak lo ahih manin amah kimawk phat tawm hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ama mawhna kidawkkhia lo a, muhdahna thuaklo ahih manin amah kimawkphat tawm hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit: he hath left off to be wise, and to do good.

**TDB77:** Ama kammalte in siatna leh khemna vive hi a, pil gamtatna leh hoih gamtatna bei khin ta hi.

**ZVS:** Ama kammalte pen siatna leh khemna vive hi a, pil gamtatna leh hoih gamtatna bei khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v4
**EN:** He deviseth mischief upon his bed; he setteth himself in a way that is not good; he abhorreth not evil.

**TDB77:** A lupna tungah siat gamtat ding sawm a, hoih lohna-ah amah kibulhtawm hi; amah in siatna nolh lo hi.

**ZVS:** A lupna tungah lumin siat gamtat ding a ngaihsun hi. Hoihlohna lamah amah kibulhtawm a, amah in siatna nolhlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Thy mercy, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na itna kip in van ban a, na cihtakna in meii ban hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na itna kip in vantung ban a, na cihtakna in vanpi dong banto hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Thy righteousness is like the great mountains; thy judgments are a great deep: O

**TDB77:** Na thumanna in Pasian’ mualte tawh kibang a, na thukhennate in tuipi thuk tawh kibang hi; mihing leh ganhing na hon hi, Topa aw.

**ZVS:** Na thumanna pen Pasian' mualte tawh kibang a, na thukhennate pen tuipi thuk tawh kibang hi. Mihing leh ganhing na hon hi, { Topa } aw.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** How excellent is thy lovingkindness, O God! therefore the children of men put their trust under the shadow of thy wings.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, na itna kip bangzahta in manpha! Mihing tate na kha lim-ah bu-te hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, na itna kip manpha mahmah hi. Mihing tate na kha liimah bu-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house; and thou shalt make them drink of the river of thy pleasures.

**TDB77:** Na inn sunga na tampite tawh amaute nuamsa-te a, na lungkimna gun panin amaute dawn ding na pia hi.

**ZVS:** Na inn sunga na tampite tawh amaute nuamsa-te a, na hoihna gun panin amaute na dawnsak hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** For with thee is the fountain of life: in thy light shall we see light.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nuntakna tuinak nang tawh om a, na khuavak sungah kote in khuavak ka mu-te hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nuntakna tuinak nangmah tawh om a, na khuavak sungah kote in khuavak ka mu-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** O continue thy lovingkindness unto them that know thee; and thy righteousness to the upright in heart.

**TDB77:** Nang hong theite tungah na itna kip hong zom in la, lungsim thutangte tungah na hotkhiatna hong zom in!

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Let not the foot of pride come against me, and let not the hand of the wicked remove me.

**TDB77:** Kihihsakna khe kei hong sik khasak kei in la, kei pen migilote, hong hawlkhiasak kei in.

**ZVS:** A kihisak mite kei hong sukhasak kei in la, migilote, hong hawlkhiasak kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** There are the workers of iniquity fallen: they are cast down, and shall not be able to rise.

**TDB77:** Hia lai-ah siatna bawlte cimawh-in kun-te a, amaute kisuncip sukin, tho zo lo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Hua lai-ah siatna bawlte a cimawhin kun-te a, amaute kisuncip sukin, thozo nawnlo-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
