## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O God, thou art my God; early will I seek thee: my soul thirsteth for thee, my flesh longeth for thee in a dry and thirsty land, where no water is;

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangmah in keima Pasian na hi hi, nangmah kong zong hi, ka lungsim khempeuh tawh nang kong deih hi; tui om lohna gamkeu bangin ka pumpi in nang hong deih hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, keima Pasian na hi hi. Nangmah kong zong a, ka lungsim khempeuh tawh nang kong deih hi. Tui omlohna gamkeu bangin ka pumpi bup in nang hong deih hi.

### v2
**EN:** To see thy power and thy glory, so as I have seen thee in the sanctuary.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin na vangliatna leh na minthan’nate mu dingin biakinn sungah nangmah kong en hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin biakinn sungah nangmahmah leh na vangliatna na minthan'nate kei hong musak in.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** Because thy lovingkindness is better than life, my lips shall praise thee.

**TDB77:** Na itna kip in nuntakna sangin hoihzaw ahih manin, nangmah ka hong phat ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nuntakna sangin nangma itna kip hoihzaw ahih manin nangmah ka hong phat ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Thus will I bless thee while I live: I will lift up my hands in thy name.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ka nuntak lai teng nangmah kong phat ding a, ka khutte nang lamah lamto-in thu kong ngen ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin ka nuntak laiteng nang kong phat ding a, ka khutte hong laam to-in thu kong ngen ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v6
**EN:** When I remember thee upon my bed, and meditate on thee in the night watches.

**TDB77:** Ka lupna tungah nangmah hong ngaihsunsunin, zanthapai-in kong lungngaihngaih ciangin guhngek leh sathau ne bangin ka lung taii mahmah a, lungdam mahmahna tawh nangmah phatna la ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v7
**EN:** Because thou hast been my help, therefore in the shadow of thy wings will I rejoice.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in kei hong huhpa na hi a, nangma hutna sungah lungdamin la ka sa hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh kei a hong huhpa na hi a, nangma hutna sungah lungdamin la ka sa hi.

### v8
**EN:** My soul followeth hard after thee: thy right hand upholdeth me.

**TDB77:** Nangmah ka hong bel hi; nangma taklam khut in kei hong len hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah ka hong bel a, nangma taklam khut in keimah a hong len hi.

### v9
**EN:** But those that seek my soul, to destroy it, shall go into the lower parts of the earth.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin kei thah dingin a hong zongte pen lei thukpi sungah paisuk ding hi;

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin thah dingin kei a hong zongte, lei thukpi sungah paisuk ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v10
**EN:** They shall fall by the sword: they shall be a portion for foxes.

**TDB77:** Amaute pen gal sungah si ding-te a, ngiate’ an suak ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Galsungah amaute si ding-te a, ngiate' an suak ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** But the king shall rejoice in God; every one that sweareth by him shall glory: but the mouth of them that speak lies shall be stopped.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Pasian sungah kumpipa lungdam ding hi; amah tawh thuciam khempeuhte zong minthang ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh zuauthu gente kamte kihubing ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Pasian' hangin kumpipa lungdam ding a, Pasian tawh thuciamna a nei khempeuhte zong minthang ding-te hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh zuauthu a gen kam khempeuh kihubing ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
