## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And the fifth angel sounded, and I saw a star fall from heaven unto the earth: and to him was given the key of the bottomless pit.

**TDB77:** Ngana vantungmi in peengkul a tum ciangin vantung pan leitungah hong kia aksi khat ka mu a, tua mi kiangah kuam thukpi hon’na tawh a pia hi.
