## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And in that day thou shalt say, O

**TDB77:** Ni khat ni ciangin mite in, “Topa aw, kei’ tungah na heh hangin na hehna dam a, nangmah in kei hong hehnem na hih manin nang kong phat ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ni khat ni ciangin mite in, “{ Topa } aw, kei tungah na heh hangin na hehna damta a, kei hong hehnem na hih manin nang tungah lungdamna kong ko ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v2
**EN:** Behold, God is my salvation; I will trust, and not be afraid: for the

**TDB77:** “En in, Pasian in keima kihotkhiatna ahi hi; amah ka muang ding a, ka lau kei ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh Topa Pasian in thahatna leh vangliatna hong pia a, amah in keimah hong honkhiapa ahi hi,” a cih hun-te hong tung ding hi.

**ZVS:** En in, Pasian pen kei a hong honkhiapa ahi hi. Amah ka muang ding a, ka lau kei ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa { Pasian } in thahatna leh vangliatna hong pia a, amah pen kei a hong honkhiapa ahi hi,” a cih hun-te hong tung ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Therefore with joy shall ye draw water out of the wells of salvation.

**TDB77:** Tui siangtho in a dangtakte a lungdamsak mah bangin, Pasian’ hotkhiatna tuikhuk in a mite lungdamsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tui siangtho in a dangtakte a lungdamsak mah bangin, Pasian' hotkhiatna in a mite lungdamsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v4
**EN:** And in that day shall ye say, Praise the

**TDB77:** Ni khat ni ciangin mite in, “Topa’ tungah lungdamna ko un la, a min lo-in, ama hong huh ding ngen un; minamte khempeuh lakah ama septe kitheisak un la, ama min kiliansak hi, ci-in gen un.

**ZVS:** Ni khat ni ciangin mite in, “{ Topa}' tungah lungdam ko un la, ama hong huh ding ngen un. Minam khempeuh lakah ama septe kitheisak un la, ama min kiliansak hi, ci-in gen un.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Sing unto the

**TDB77:** “Amah in minthang takin sem khin ahih manin Topa’ tungah phatna la sa un; leitung khempeuh ah hih thu kitheisak un.

**ZVS:** Amah in minthang takin sem khin ahih manin { Topa}' tungah phatna la sa un la, leitung khempeuhah hih thu kitheisak un.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Cry out and shout, thou inhabitant of Zion: for great is the Holy One of Israel in the midst of thee.

**TDB77:** Zion-a teeng mite aw, nomau’ lakah Israelte’ Siangtho Pa lianin om ahih manin awng un la, lungdamin la sa un,” a cih hun-te hong tung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Zion-a teng mite aw, nomau' lakah Israel-te' Siangtho Pa lianin om ahih manin lungdamin awng un la, lungdamin la sa un,” a cih hun-te hong tung ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because
