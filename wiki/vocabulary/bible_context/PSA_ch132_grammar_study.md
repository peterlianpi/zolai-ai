## Verses

### v5
**EN:** Until I find out a place for the

**TDB77:** { Topa } aw, David na ngaihsutna-in a gim thuaknate khempeuh leh amah in, “Jakob' Vanglianpa' tenna ding, { Topa}-a' ding mun ka muh matengin ka inn sungah ka lum kei ding a, ka lupna ka zuan kei ding hi. Ka ihmu kei ding a, ka mitkhute ka kikhupsak kei ding hi,” ci-in Jakob' Vanglianpa' tungah hong thuciamna leh { Topa' } tungah hong kiciamnate phawk in.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Lo, we heard of it at Ephratah: we found it in the fields of the wood.

**TDB77:** En un, Jaar gam sungah ka mu uh hi; cihna thuthak Ephrathah-ah kiza hi.

**ZVS:** Jaar gamsungah Thuciamna Singkuang ka mu uh hi, cihna thuthak Bethlehem-ah kiza hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** We will go into his tabernacles: we will worship at his footstool.

**TDB77:** “Ama omna mun-ah va pai ni; ama khe-ngakna-ah va bia ni!”

**ZVS:** “Ama omna munah va pai ni; ama khengakna-ah va bia ni,” ka ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** hortative: let us

### v8

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, tho in la na vangliatna lim Thuciamna Singkuang tawh na omna munah pai in.

### v9

**TDB77:** Nangma siampite in diktatna puan silh uh hen la, na misiangthote lungdamin awng ta uh hen.

**ZVS:** Na mite na hotkhiatna thu, na siampite in taangko hen la, na mi siangthote lungdamin awng ta uh hen.

### v10

**TDB77:** Na nasempa David tungah thu na ciam zo hi; na sathau nilhpa nial kei in.

**ZVS:** Na nasempa David tungah thu na ciamzo hi; na sathau nilhpa nial kei in.

### v11
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in a pelh loh ding kiciamna taktak khat David tungah a ciam a, “Nang sung pan-a na tate khat na kumpi tokhom tungah ka koih ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in a pelhloh ding kiciamna taktak khat David tungah kiciam a, “Nang' sung pana na tate khat na kumpi tokhom tungah ka koih ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** If thy children will keep my covenant and my testimony that I shall teach them, their children shall also sit upon thy throne for evermore.

**TDB77:** Na tate in, amau tunga ka hilh ding ka thuciamna leh ka teci pan’nate a zuih uh leh, amau’ tate zong na kumpi tokhom tungah tu paisuak ding uh hi,” na ci hi.

**ZVS:** Na tate in, amau' tungah ka hilh ding ka thuciamna leh ka teci pan'nate a zuih nak uh leh, amau' tate zong na kumpi tokhom tungah tu paisuak ding uh hi,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa in Zion teel khin zo a, amah in tua mun pen ama teen’ nadingin a deih hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } in Zion teel khinzo a, amah in tua mun pen ama ten'na dingin a deih hi.

### v14
