## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa, ka Pasian aw, sun-in huhna kong ngen a, zan-in nangma mai-ah kong kiko hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } ka Pasian aw, sunin huhna kong ngen a, zan hun sungin nangma mai-ah kong kiko hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Let my prayer come before thee: incline thine ear unto my cry;

**TDB77:** Ka thungetna hong za in la, huh ding kong ngetna hong ngai in.

**ZVS:** Ka thungetna hong za in la, nong huh ding ka hong ngetna hong ngai in.

### v3
**EN:** For my soul is full of troubles: and my life draweth nigh unto the grave.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh lungkhamna tampi ka tungah hong tung a, ka si dektak hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh lungkhamna tampi thuakin, ka si dektak hi.

### v4
**EN:** I am counted with them that go down into the pit: I am as a man that hath no strength:

**TDB77:** Kei pen, a si dektak mi tawh ka kibang a, ka thahatna khempeuh bei khinta hi.

**ZVS:** Kei pen a sidek mi tawh ka kibang ta a, ka thahatna khempeuh beimang khinta hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Free among the dead, like the slain that lie in the grave, whom thou rememberest no more: and they are cut off from thy hand.

**TDB77:** Misite lakah a kinusia, a kithatlum, han sunga a lum, nangma huh zawh ahi nawn lo, na phawk nawn lohte tawh ka kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Misite lakah a kinusia, a kithatlum, han sunga lum, na huh zawh ahi nawnlo, na phawk nawnlohte ka bang ta hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Thou hast laid me in the lowest pit, in darkness, in the deeps.

**TDB77:** Kei pen, mial-a a thukna mun, Leihawm thukna sungah nong koih khin hi.

**ZVS:** Kei pen, mialin a thukna mun, Leihawm thuk sungah nong koih khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v7
**EN:** Thy wrath lieth hard upon me, and thou hast afflicted me with all thy waves. Selah.

**TDB77:** Nangma hehna keima tungah gik mahmah a, na tuihualte khempeuh tawh kei nong vukcip hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma hehna keima tungah gik mahmah a, na tuihualte khempeuh tawh kei na hong vukcip hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v8
**EN:** Thou hast put away mine acquaintance far from me; thou hast made me an abomination unto them: I am shut up, and I cannot come forth.

**TDB77:** Ka kikholhpihte in kei hong pelh dingin nong bawl khin a, amaute tungah a cikziat huai khatin kei nong bawl hi. Ka suahtak khiat theih loh nadingin kei hong kikhumcip hi;

**ZVS:** Ka kikholhpih mite in kei a hong pelh dingin na hong bawl a, amaute tungah a cikzethuai khatin kei na hong bawl hi. Ka suahtak theihlohna dingin kei hong kikhumcip a,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Mine eye mourneth by reason of affliction:

**TDB77:** thuakna hangin ka mit mial hi. Topa aw, ni simin nangma tungah kong ngen hi; thu ngenin ka khutte nangma lamah kong zan hi.

**ZVS:** ka gim thuakna hangin ka mit a mial ta hi. { Topa } aw, nisimin nangma tungah kong ngen a, thu ngenin ka khutte nangma lamah kong zan hi.

### v10
**EN:** Wilt thou shew wonders to the dead? shall the dead arise and praise thee? Selah.

**TDB77:** A sisate’ adingin nangmah in nalamdang bawl na hi hiam? Nangmah phat dingin mi site hong tho ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** A sisate-a' dingin na lamdang a bawl na hi hiam? Nangmah a phat dingin mi a si khinsate hong tho ding ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v11
**EN:** Shall thy lovingkindness be declared in the grave? or thy faithfulness in destruction?

**TDB77:** Han sungah na itna kip kigen a, siatna mun-ah na thumanna kigen ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Han sungah na itna kip kigen ding a, siatna munah nangma thumannate kigen ding ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Shall thy wonders be known in the dark? and thy righteousness in the land of forgetfulness?

**TDB77:** Khuamialna sungah nangma nalamdangte kithei a, mangngilhna gam sungah na hotkhiat huhna kithei ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Khuamialna sungah na na lamdangte kithei a, mangngilhna gamsungah na hotkhiat huhna kithei ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** But unto thee have I cried, O

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Topa aw, nangma tungah huhna kong ngen a, zingsang simin nangma mai-ah thu kong ngen hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin { Topa } aw, na tungah huhna kong ngen a, zingsang simin na mai-ah thu ka hong ngen hi.

### v15
**EN:** I am afflicted and ready to die from my youth up: while I suffer thy terrors I am distracted.

**TDB77:** Ka neu-a kipan gimna thuakin ka si dektak a, nangma dante ka thuak hi; ka cih nading mel ka thei kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ka neu tunga kipan gim thuakin ka si dektak hi. Nong gimpiakte ka thuak a, ka cihna ding mel ka thei nawn kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** Thy fierce wrath goeth over me; thy terrors have cut me off.

**TDB77:** Nangma hehna in kei hong nawk khin a, a lauhuai nangma hong dona in kei hong susia hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma hehna in keimah hong nawkgawp khin a, a lauhuai mahmah nangma hong sukgawpna in kei hong susia ta hi.

### v17
**EN:** They came round about me daily like water; they compassed me about together.

**TDB77:** Amaute in tuikhang bangin sun tawntung kei hong umcih-te a, kei hong tuamcip-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in tuikhang bangin sun tawntungin kei hong umcih-te a, kei hong tuamcip-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v18
**EN:** Lover and friend hast thou put far from me, and mine acquaintance into darkness.

**TDB77:** Hong itte leh ka lawmte keimah nong pelhsak khin a, khuamial bek, keima kikholhpih ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong itte leh ka lawmte keimah nong pelhsak khin a, khuamial bekbek, keima kikholhpih ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
