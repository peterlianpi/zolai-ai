## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O sing unto the

**TDB77:** Amah in nalamdangte bawl khin ahih manin Topa tungah la thak khat sa un! Ama khut taklam leh ama khut siangthote in amah gualzosak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in na lamdangte bawl ahih manin { Topa' } tungah la thak sa un. Ama khut taklam leh ama khut siangthote in amah gualzosak hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v2
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ama gupkhiatna kitheisak khin zo a, amah in minamte muhna-ah ama thumanna lang sak khin hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in ama gualzawhna kitheisak khinzo a, amah in minamte' muhna-ah ama thumanna langsak khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** He hath remembered his mercy and his truth toward the house of Israel: all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

**TDB77:** Amah thutakin, it tawntungna nei ahih manin Israelte' tunga a thuciamna tung sak hi. Leitung mi khempeuh in i Pasian’ gupkhiatna mu khin uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amah thutakin, it tawntungna nei ahih manin Israel-te tungah a thuciamna a tungsak hi. Leitung mi khempeuh in eite' Pasian' gualzawhna mu khinta uh hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | causal: because | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Make a joyful noise unto the

**TDB77:** Leitung mi khempeuh aw, Topa tungah lungdamin la sa un la, lungdamin awng khia un la phatna la sa un!

**ZVS:** Leitung mi khempeuh aw, { Topa' } tungah lungdamin la sa-in lungdamin awngkhia un la, phatna la sa un.

### v5
**EN:** Sing unto the

**TDB77:** Laiza lasakna tawh Topa tungah phatna late sa un la, laiza lasakna leh langaih tawh phat un!

**ZVS:** Laia lasakna tawh { Topa' } tungah phatna late sa un la, laia lasakna leh la ngaih tawh phat un.

### v6
**EN:** With trumpets and sound of cornet make a joyful noise before the

**TDB77:** Pengkulte leh sa-ki aw tawh Topa, Kumpipa mai-ah lungdamna ging sak un!

**ZVS:** Peengkulte leh saki aw tawh { Topa, } Kumpipa' mai-ah lungdamna gingsak un.

### v7
**EN:** Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.

**TDB77:** Tuipi leh a sunga omte khempeuh aw, awng un la, leitung leh a tunga teeng khempeuh aw, la sa un.

**ZVS:** Tuipi leh a sunga omte khempeuh aw, awng un la, leitung leh a tunga teng khempeuhte aw, la sa un.

### v9
**EN:** Before the

**TDB77:** Gunte aw, na khut uh beng un. Mualte aw, { Topa' } mai-ah lungdamin la sa un. Bang hang hiam cih leh leitung a uk dingin amah hong pai ta a, thumanna tawh leitung mite' thu ahi ding bangin hong khen ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will
