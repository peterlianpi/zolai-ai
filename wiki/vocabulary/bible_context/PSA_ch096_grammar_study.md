# Psalms Chapter 96 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 12 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 4 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |
| future: will | 2 |
| quotative: said/saying | 1 |
| causal: because | 1 |
| purpose: in order to | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O sing unto the

**TDB77:** Leitung mi khempeuh aw, Topa tungah la thak khat sa un; Topa tungah la sa un!

**ZVS:** Leitung mi khempeuh aw, { Topa } tungah la thak khat sa un. { Topa } tungah la sa un.

### v2
**EN:** Sing unto the

**TDB77:** Topa tungah la sa un la, ama min phat un; ni khat khit ni khat ama hong hotkhiatna thu gen tawntung un.

**ZVS:** { Topa } tungah la sa un la, ama min phat un. Ni khat khit ni khat ama hong hotkhiatna thu gen tawntung un.

### v3
**EN:** Declare his glory among the heathen, his wonders among all people.

**TDB77:** Ama minthan’na, minamte lakah gen un la, ama nalamdang septe, mi khempeuh lakah pulak un!

**ZVS:** Ama minthan'na minamte lakah gen un la, ama na lamdang septe mi khempeuh lakah pulak un.

### v4
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa lian a, nakpi tak-a phat ding ahi hi; pasian dangte khempeuh sangin amah zahtak zawk ding ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } lian a, nakpi takin phat ding ahi hi; pasian dangte khempeuh sangin amah zahtak zawk ding ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** For all the gods of the nations are idols: but the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh minam dangte’ pasiante khempeuh in milim hi lel a, ahih hangin Topa ahih leh vantungte a bawlpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh minam dangte' pasian khempeuh milim hi lel a, ahih hangin { Topa } ahih leh vantungte a bawlpa ahi hi.

### v6
**EN:** Honour and majesty are before him: strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.

**TDB77:** Pahtawina leh liatnate ama mai-ah om a, hatna leh hoihnate ama omna mun siangtho-ah a om hi.

**ZVS:** Pahtawina leh liatnate ama mai-ah om a, hatna leh hoihnate ama omna mun siangtho-ah om hi.

### v7
**EN:** Give unto the

**TDB77:** Minam kimte aw, Topa in minthan’na leh hatna nei hi, ci-in mang un, Topa in nei hi, ci-in mang un.

**ZVS:** Mi namkimte aw, { Topa } in minthan'na leh hatna nei hi, ci-in mang un. { Topa } in nei hi, ci-in mang un.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Give unto the

**TDB77:** Ama min in a ngah dinga kilawm minthan’na, Topa tungah pia un la, biak piakna hong keng-in ama huang sungah hong pai un!

**ZVS:** Ama min in a ngah dingin a kilawm minthan'na { Topa' } tungah pia un la, biakpiakna hong kengin ama huangsungah hong pai un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** O worship the

**TDB77:** Puan siangtho silhin Topa bia un. Leitung mi khempeuh aw, ama mai-ah ling un!

**ZVS:** Puan siangtho silhin { Topa } bia un. Leitung mi khempeuh aw, ama mai-ah ling un.

### v10
**EN:** Say among the heathen that the

**TDB77:** Minamte lakah, “Topa in uk hi! Hi hi, leitung kikipsak a, liing ngei lo ding hi; amah in mihingte’ thu a manin khen ding hi,” ci un.

**ZVS:** Minam khempeuh lakah thu gen kawikawi un la, “{ Topa } in uk hi. Leitung kikipsak a, kiling ngeilo ding hi. Amah in mihingte' thu a manin khen ding hi,” ci un.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof.

**TDB77:** Vantungmite kipak hen la, leitung lungdam hen; tuipi leh a sunga omte khempeuh awng ta uh hen;

**ZVS:** Vantung mite kipak hen la, leitung mite lungdam hen. Tuipi leh a sunga om khempeuh awng ta uh hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Before the

**TDB77:** Lo leh a sunga om khempeuh nuamin diang uh hen. Gamlaka sing om khempeuh in { Topa' } mai-ah lungdamin la sa hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh amah hong pai ta hi. Leitung mite' thu a khen dingin hong pai ta hi. Amah in thumanna tawh leitung ukin mite' thu ahi bangin khen ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will
