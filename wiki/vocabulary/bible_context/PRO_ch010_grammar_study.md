### v1
**EN:** The proverbs of Solomon. A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.

**TDB77:** Solomon’ paunakte in: Tapil in a pa lungdamsak a, tahai in a nu dahsak hi.

**ZVS:** Hihte pen Solomon' paunakte ahi hi: Ta pil in a pa lungdamsak a, ta hai in a nu dahsak hi.

### v2
**EN:** Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but righteousness delivereth from death.

**TDB77:** Thuman lo-a ngahna-ah phattuamna om lo a, thumaanna in sih nading pan hong hon hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman loin ngahna-ah phattuamna omlo a, thumanna in sihna ding pan hong hon hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo
