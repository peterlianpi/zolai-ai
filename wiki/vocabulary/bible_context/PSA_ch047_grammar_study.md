## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God with the voice of triumph.

**TDB77:** Mi khempeuh aw, na khut-te beeng un! Lungdamna la nakpi-in sa-in Pasian phat un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa, a Sang Penpa, dipkuat-huai-a, leitung khempeuhah kumpi lian khat ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa, } a Sangpenpa, dipkuathuai a, leitung khempeuh a uk kumpi lian khat ahi hi.

### v3
**EN:** He shall subdue the people under us, and the nations under our feet.

**TDB77:** Mi khempeuh eima nuai-ah, minamte i khe nuai-ah amah in hong zawhsak hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khempeuh eima nuai-ah, minamte i khenuai-ah amah in hong zawhsak hi.

### v4
**EN:** He shall choose our inheritance for us, the excellency of Jacob whom he loved. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ama itpa Jakob’ angtan’pih ahi, eima luah ding amah in hong teelsak hi.

**ZVS:** Ama it Israel-te' angtan'pih eima luah ding leitang amah in hong teelsak hi.

### v5
**EN:** God is gone up with a shout, the

**TDB77:** Aw ging tawh Pasian paito a, pengkul aw tawh Topa paito hi.

**ZVS:** Awging tawh Pasian paito a, peengkul aw tawh { Topa } paito hi.

### v6
**EN:** Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises unto our King, sing praises.

**TDB77:** Pasian tungah phatna la sa un, phatna la sa un! I Kumpipa tungah phatna la sa un, phatna la sa un!

### v7
**EN:** For God is the King of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian in leitung khempeuhte’ Kumpi ahi hi; lasakna tawh amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian pen leitung khempeuhte' kumpi ahi hi. La sakna tawh amah phat un!

### v8
**EN:** God reigneth over the heathen: God sitteth upon the throne of his holiness.

**TDB77:** Minamte tungah Pasian maang hi; a tokhom siangtho tungah Pasian tu hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in minamte uk a, a tokhom siangtho tungah Pasian tu hi.

### v9
**EN:** The princes of the people are gathered together, even the people of the God of Abraham: for the shields of the earth belong unto God: he is greatly exalted.

**TDB77:** Uliante in Abraham’ Pasian’ mite tawh kikhawm-te hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh leitung’ lumte in Pasian aa ahi hi; amah sangpi-in kithangsak hi!

**ZVS:** Uliante leh Abraham' Pasian' mite kipawl khawm-te hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh leitung mite' lumte khempeuh Pasian-a' suak khinta a, amah a sangpi-in kithangsak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
