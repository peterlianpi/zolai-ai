## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un! Ke’n, Topa ka phat ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. Ke'n, { Topa } ka phat ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v2
**EN:** While I live will I praise the

**TDB77:** Ka hin’ lai teng Topa ka phat ding hi; ka nuntak lai teng ka Pasian tungah phatnate ka sa ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka hin' laiteng { Topa } ka phat ding a, ka nuntak laiteng ka Pasian' tungah phatnate ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3
**EN:** Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help.

**TDB77:** Huhna hong pia thei lo uliante ahi a, mihing’ tapa ahi zongin muang kei un.

**ZVS:** Huhna a hong pia theilo uliante ahi a, mi mawkmawkte ahi zongin muang kei un.

### v4
**EN:** His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth; in that very day his thoughts perish.

**TDB77:** A husan’na a bei ciangin amah lei-ah ciahkik a, tua ni mahin ama ngaihsutnate bei ta hi.

**ZVS:** A husan'na a bei ciangin lei-ah ciahkik-te a, tua ni mahmahin a ngaihsutnate-te beita hi.

### v5
**EN:** Happy is he that hath the God of Jacob for his help, whose hope is in the

**TDB77:** Jakob’ Pasian pen ama huhpa hi-in, ama Topa Pasian, a muangpa in mi lungdam ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Jakob' Pasian pen amah huhpa hi-in, ama { Topa } Pasian a muang mipa mi lungdam ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** Which made heaven, and earth, the sea, and all that therein is: which keepeth truth for ever:

**TDB77:** Amah in van leh lei, tuipi leh tuate sunga omte khempeuh bawl a, a tawntungin thuman hi;

**ZVS:** Amah in van leh lei, tuipi leh tuate sungah a om khempeuh bawl a, a tawntungin thuman hi.

### v7
**EN:** Which executeth judgment for the oppressed: which giveth food to the hungry. The

**TDB77:** a kinengniam mite’ adingin thumanna semkhia a, gilkialte an a vaak hi. Topa in thongkiate khahkhia hi;

**ZVS:** A kinengniam mite-a' dingin thumanna semkhia a, gilkialte an a vak hi. { Topa } in thongkiate a khahkhia hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in mittawte, khua musak hi. Topa in, niamsakna a thuak mite sangto sak a, Topa in thuman mite a it hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in mittawte khua a musak hi. { Topa } in niamsakna a thuak mite sangtosak a, { Topa } in thuman mite a it hi.

### v9
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in peemtate kem a, meigong numei leh pa nei lote a huh hi; ahih hangin amah in migilote’ vaihawmna suksiatsak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in peemtate kem a, meigong numeite leh pa neilote a huh hi. Ahih hangin amah in migilote' vaihawmna a suksiatsak hi.

### v10
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Zion aw, nangma Pasian Topa in khang tawntungin uk ding hi. Topa phat un!

**ZVS:** Zion aw, nangma Pasian { Topa } in khang tawntungin uk ding hi. { Topa } phat un.

**Grammar:** future: will
