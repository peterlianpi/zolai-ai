### v1
**EN:** And I saw another mighty angel come down from heaven, clothed with a cloud: and a rainbow was upon his head, and his face was as it were the sun, and his feet as pillars of fire:

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin adang a thahat vantungmi khat in vantung meii silhin vantung pan hong kumsuk ka mu hi. Ama lutang tungah sakhituihup a om hi, a maitang ni bang ahi hi, a khete meikuang bang ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin a thahat vantung mi khat, meiipi in tuamin, a lu tungah sakhituihup khimin, a maitang nitaang tawh kibangin, a khete meikuang khuampi tawh a kibang khat, vantung panin a hong kumsuk ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v9

**TDB77:** Tua vantungmi kiangah pai-in tua laibu neu ka va nget ciangin vantungmi in, “La in la, ne in. Na gil sungah a kha ding hi, na kam sungah ahih leh, khuaizu bangin a khum ding hi,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin vantung mi kiangah va pai-in tua laizial neu a hong piakna dingin ka va gen leh amah in, “La in la, ne in. Na gil sung a tun' ciangin thuk mahmah ding a, ahi zongin na kam sungah khuaizu bangin khum mahmah ding hi,” hong ci hi.
