## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I exhort therefore, that, first of all, supplications, prayers, intercessions, and giving of thanks, be made for all men;

**TDB77:** A masa penin kong gennop thu in: Mi khempeuh-a' dingin Pasian tungah ngetna, thungetna, thumna leh lungdam kohna na neih ding-te ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** For kings, and for all that are in authority; that we may lead a quiet and peaceable life in all godliness and honesty.

**TDB77:** Eite nuamtakin omthei-in, biakna lungsim nei-in a kilawm takin i nuntak theihna dingin kumpite leh uliante-a' dingin thu na ngetsak un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour;

**TDB77:** Tua bang hihna in hihna pha hi a, eite hong Honpa Pasian’ deihna tawh kituak ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bang gamtatnate pen eite hong Hon Pasian' muhna-ah a hoih a kilawm thu ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.

**TDB77:** Tua Pasian in mi khempeuh in gupna ngahin, thuman thutak a theih nading a deih hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khempeuh in hotkhiatna ngahin, thuman a theihkim ding Pasian in deih hi.

### v5
**EN:** For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus;

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, Pasian khat bek om a, Pasian leh mite kikal ah mimah ahi hong kikham Khazih Zeisu khat bek a om hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian khat bek om a, Pasian leh mite a hong kilemsak kik thei Khrih Jesuh khat bek om hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Who gave himself a ransom for all, to be testified in due time.

**TDB77:** Tua Zeisu in mi khempeuh tankhia dinginah ama pumpi a pia hi. Amah a hun a tun’ ciangin teci pang dingin a om hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khempeuh a tankhia dingin tua Jesuh hong kipiatawm a, a hun lian laitakin a hong kipiakna pen Pasian in mi khempeuh a hotkhiat nopna thu a kilatna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v7
**EN:** Whereunto I am ordained a preacher, and an apostle, (I speak the truth in Christ, and lie not;) a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and verity.

**TDB77:** Keimah in tua teci pang mi za-ah ahi zongin, sawltak za-ah ahi zongin, upna thu leh thuman thutak thu Gentailte kiangah a hilh za-ah ahi zongin hong koih hi, ka cih ciangin thuzuau ka gen kei hi, thuman thutak ka gen hi.

**ZVS:** Hih ka gen thu pen zuau a hilo, thu taktak a gen ka hi a, Gentail mite lakah upna thu leh hih thuman a gen dingin sawltak na semin thu a hilh dingin kei a hong kisawl ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** I will therefore that men pray every where, lifting up holy hands, without wrath and doubting.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin mun khempeuh ah pasalte in hehna, kinialna om loin a khut siangtho lamto-in thu a nget ding-te ka deih hi.

**ZVS:** Mun khempeuhah a om pasal khempeuh in kuamah tawh kitotna leh lungsim kinopmawhna nei hetloin, lungsim siangtho tawh a khut-te lamin thu a nget ding-te ka deih hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** In like manner also, that women adorn themselves in modest apparel, with shamefacedness and sobriety; not with broided hair, or gold, or pearls, or costly array;

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin numeite zong a kilawm a kituak bekin kizemin kipuahin, sam khawng a uanglua-in bawlloin, a khengvalin kizepna a tuamtuamte zangloin,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** But (which becometh women professing godliness) with good works.

**TDB77:** biakna lungsim neite-a' dingin a kilawm ahih mah bangin gamtat hoih a neihding-te ka deih hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Let the woman learn in silence with all subjection.

**TDB77:** Numei in na khempeuh ah ukna mangin pau loin om hen.

**ZVS:** Numeite in gamdai takin kiniamkhiatna tawh thu ngai-in a sin ding-te ahi hi.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be

### v12
**EN:** But I suffer not a woman to teach, nor to usurp authority over the man, but to be in silence.

**TDB77:** Numei in thuhilh ding ahi zongin, pasal tungah a uk ding ahi zongin thu ka pia kei hi; amah in pau loin a om ding ka deih hi.

**ZVS:** Numeite in pasalte tungah aana nei-in thu a hilh ding ka phalkei hi. Gamdai takin a om ding-te ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v13
**EN:** For Adam was first formed, then Eve.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, Adam a piangsak masa a, a nung ciangin Evua a piangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh a masa-in Adam kipiangsak a, tua ciangin Eve kipiangsak pan hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v14
**EN:** And Adam was not deceived, but the woman being deceived was in the transgression.

**TDB77:** Tua tham loin, Adam in kheemna a thuak kei hi, numei in kheemna thuak a, a khial hi.

**ZVS:** Adam in khemna thuak masalo a, numei in thuakin a khial masa a suak hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v15
**EN:** Notwithstanding she shall be saved in childbearing, if they continue in faith and charity and holiness with sobriety.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin numeite in khuaval lohna, upna, itna leh siangthona-ah kipin a om-te leh, nausuahna-ah gupna a ngah ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin numei in a kilawm a kituakin upna, itna leh siangthona thu-ah kiptakin a om nak leh nausuah gimna sihpihlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
