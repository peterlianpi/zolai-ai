# Psalms Chapter 97 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 12 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| plural: uh marker | 4 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 3 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in uk hi; leitung lungdam hen la, tuikulh neu tampite zong lungdam ta uh hen!

**ZVS:** { Topa } in uk hi. Leitung lungdam hen la, tuikulh neu tampite zong lungdam ta uh hen.

### v2
**EN:** Clouds and darkness are round about him: righteousness and judgment are the habitation of his throne.

**TDB77:** Meiipite leh khuamial sahpite ama kiim-ah om a, thumanna leh thutan’nate in ama tokhom’ bulpi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Meiipite leh khuamial sahpite ama kiimah om a, thumanna leh thutan'nate ama tokhom bulpi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** A fire goeth before him, and burneth up his enemies round about.

**TDB77:** Ama mai-ah mei pai a, a kimkot-a om ama langpangte kangtum hi.

**ZVS:** Ama mai-ah mei pai a, ama kiima om a galte a kangtum hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** His lightnings enlightened the world: the earth saw, and trembled.

**TDB77:** Ama khuaphialepte in leitung taang sak a, leitung in mu-in a liing hi.

**ZVS:** Ama khuaphialepte in leitung taangsak a, leitung in mu-in a ling hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** The hills melted like wax at the presence of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ mai, leitung mi khempeuh Topa’ mai-ah mualliante in khuainun bangin tui suak uh hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } mai, leitung mi khempeuh' { Topa' } mai-ah mual liante khuainun bangin a tuisuak uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** The heavens declare his righteousness, and all the people see his glory.

**TDB77:** Vantungte in ama thumanna genkhia a, mi khempeuhte in ama minthan’na en uh hi.

**ZVS:** Vantungte in ama thumanna genkhia uh a, mi khempeuh in ama minthan'na mu uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Confounded be all they that serve graven images, that boast themselves of idols: worship him, all ye gods.

**TDB77:** Bangmah man lo milimte a kisak theihpih, milim a bia mi khempeuhte maizumin kikoih a, ama mai-ah pasian khempeuh kun uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bangmah a manlo milimte a kisaktheihpih, milim a bia mi khempeuhte maizumin kikoih a, ama mai-ah pasian khempeuh kun uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** Zion heard, and was glad; and the daughters of Judah rejoiced because of thy judgments, O

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, Zion in za-in nuam a, nangma thukhennate hangin Judah khuapite lungdam uh hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, Zion in za-in nuam a, na thukhennate hangin Judah khuapite lungdam uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** For thou,

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa aw, leitung khempeuh a ukpa na hi a, pasian dang khempeuh sangin nakpi takin na lianzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } aw, leitung khempeuh a ukpa na hi a, pasian dang khempeuh sangin nakpi takin na lianzaw hi.

### v10
**EN:** Ye that love the

**TDB77:** Topa in gitlohna a mudahte it a, amah in a mi siangthote’ nuntakna kem hi; amah in migilote khut sung panin amaute honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in gitlohna a mudahte it a, amah in a mi siangthote' nuntakna siatna ding pan a kem hi. Amah in migilote' khutsung panin amaute a honkhia hi.

### v11
**EN:** Light is sown for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart.

**TDB77:** Thuman mite’ tungah khuavak hong taang a, lungsim thutang mite’ tungah lungdamna hong tung hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mite' tungah khuavak hong taang a, lungsim thutang mite tungah lungdamna hong tung hi.

### v12
**EN:** Rejoice in the

**TDB77:** Thuman mite aw, Topa sungah lungdam un la, ama min siangtho phat un!

**ZVS:** Thuman mite aw, { Topa' } nasepnate hangin lungdam un la, ama min siangtho phat un.
