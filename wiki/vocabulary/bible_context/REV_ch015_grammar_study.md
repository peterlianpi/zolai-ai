## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And I saw another sign in heaven, great and marvellous, seven angels having the seven last plagues; for in them is filled up the wrath of God.

**TDB77:** Tua nung ciangin a lian mahmah nalamdang khat vantungah ka mu hi. Gimna nam sagih a nei vantungmi sagihte ahi-te hi. Tua gimnate in a nunung bel gimna ahi-te hi. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, Pasian’ hehna tua gimna tawh a kicing hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin vanah na lamdang lian mahmah khat ka mu hi. Gimna nam sagih a hong keng vantung mi sagih om-te a, Pasian' hehna a lah tawpna-te ahih manin a nunung pente ahi-te hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** And I saw as it were a sea of glass mingled with fire: and them that had gotten the victory over the beast, and over his image, and over his mark, and over the number of his name, stand on the sea of glass, having the harps of God.

**TDB77:** Tua nung ciangin mei tawh kihel, limlang tuipi banga om ahi zongin, sahang tung, ama milim leh ama min nambat gimna pan suakta-in a zo mite in, Pasian’ gawsemte tawi-in tua limlang tuipi kiangah ding-in a om-te ahi zongin ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin mei tawh a kihel limlang peekpi kiphah tuipi tawh a kibang ka mu hi. Ganhing, a lim, leh a min nambat tawh a kipiasa a zote, Pasian' lasakna harpte tawi-in tuipi tawh a kibang gei-ah a ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** And they sing the song of Moses the servant of God, and the song of the Lamb, saying, Great and marvellous are thy works, Lord God Almighty; just and true are thy ways, thou King of saints.

**TDB77:** Tua mite in Pasian’ sila Moses’ la leh Tuuno’ la sa-in: “Vanglianpen Topa Pasian aw, Na bawlnate lianin, a lamdang mahmah hi! Mipawl khempeuhte’ Kumpipa aw, Na gamtatnate in a dikin, a man hi!

**ZVS:** Amaute in Pasian' nasem Moses la leh Tuuno la sa-te a, “A Vanglian Topa Pasian aw, na nasepnate lamdangin lian mahmah hi. Khang tawntunga Kumpipa aw, na nasepnate-ah thumanin na thutang hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** Who shall not fear thee, O Lord, and glorify thy name? for thou only art holy: for all nations shall come and worship before thee; for thy judgments are made manifest.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nang min kua in kihta lo, phat loin a om ding hiam? Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, Nang bek na siangtho a, Nangma dikna gamtatnate in a kilang zo ahih ciangin Mipawl khempeuh in na mai-ah hong thuumin, hong bia ding-te hi,” ci-in a phat-te hi.

**ZVS:** Topa aw, nang, kua in hong kihtalo ding a, na liatna hong phatlo ding ahi hiam? Nang bek na siangtho hi. Thumanin na sepnate mi khempeuh in mu-te ahih manin minam khempeuh nang kiangah hong pai-in, na mai-ah hong bia ding-te hi,” a ci-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** And after that I looked, and, behold, the temple of the tabernacle of the testimony in heaven was opened:

**TDB77:** Tua thute khit ciangin vantungah teci tawh kipawl buk ahi, biakinnpi a kihong ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Tua khit ciangin ka khuadak leh a sungah Pasian' Ompihna Mun a om vantung biakinn a kihong ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** And the seven angels came out of the temple, having the seven plagues, clothed in pure and white linen, and having their breasts girded with golden girdles.

**TDB77:** Gimna pawl sagih a nei vantungmi sagihte in a siangtho, a taang suangmanpha silhin, a awm-ah kham awmgak gakin tua biakinnpi pan a pusuah khit-te ciangin,

**ZVS:** Tua biakinn sung panin vantung mi sagih, gimna sagih tawh hong pusuak-te a, puanpak silhin, kham awmgak tawh a awm-te a gak-te hi.

### v7
**EN:** And one of the four beasts gave unto the seven angels seven golden vials full of the wrath of God, who liveth for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** ganhing lite khat in vantungmi sagihte kiangah, a tawntung a nungta Pasian’ hehna tawh a dim kham hai sagih a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Ganhing lite sung panin khat in a nungta tawntung Pasian' hehna a dim hai sagih, vantung mi sagihte kiangah a pia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** And the temple was filled with smoke from the glory of God, and from his power; and no man was able to enter into the temple, till the seven plagues of the seven angels were fulfilled.

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin biakinnpi in Pasian’ vangliatna hangin meikhu tawh dimin, tua vantungmi sagihte’ gimna sagih a bei matengin biakinnpi sungah kuamah a lut thei kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua biakinn sungah Pasian' minthan'na leh a vangliatna khu kidim ahih manin, vantung mi sagihte in hong paipih gimna sagih a ven' matengin biakinn sungah kuamah lut theilo-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei
