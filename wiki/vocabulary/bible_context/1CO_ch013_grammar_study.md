## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Though I speak with the tongues of men and of angels, and have not charity, I am become as sounding brass, or a tinkling cymbal.

**TDB77:** Keimah in leitungmite’ kam ahi zongin, vantungmite’ kam ahi zongin ka gen theih hangin itna ka neih kei leh, a ging thei dak, a ging thei daltuah tawh kibang ka hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah in mihingte pau a tuamtuamte leh vantung mite' pau nangawn ka pautheih hangin itna ka neih kei leh a bil-aphuai zam leh daltuah kitum tawh a kibang lel ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** And though I have the gift of prophecy, and understand all mysteries, and all knowledge; and though I have all faith, so that I could remove mountains, and have not charity, I am nothing.

**TDB77:** Kamsangte’ pilna nei-in, thuthuk khempeuh, pilna khempeuh thei siam a, mual kinsak zawh nading upna apawl apawl ka neih hangin itna ka neih kei leh, a mawkna teitei ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Thuhilh siamna nei-in, thusim khempeuh thei-in, na khempeuh thei-in, mualte kinsak zawhna ding upna ka neih hangin itna ka neih kei leh ka kiman'na bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** And though I bestow all my goods to feed the poor, and though I give my body to be burned, and have not charity, it profiteth me nothing.

**TDB77:** Mizawngte vaak dingin ka sum khempeuh ka hawmsak tham loin, ka pumpi meikhul dingin ka piak hangin itna ka neih kei leh, ka phattuamna om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ka neih khempeuh midangte tungah pia-in, Pasian' nasepna hangin kihaltumna ka thuak ahi zongin itna ka neih kei leh ka phattuamna bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo

### v4
**EN:** Charity suffereth long, and is kind; charity envieth not; charity vaunteth not itself, is not puffed up,

**TDB77:** Itna in a lungduai thei hi, a hehpih thei hi. Itna in ki-elna a om kei hi. Itna in a kisathei kei hi, a kiphasak thei kei hi,

**ZVS:** Itna neite a lungduai-in migi hi. Itna neite in hazatna lungsim neiloin, zong kisathei lo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Doth not behave itself unseemly, seeketh not her own, is not easily provoked, thinketh no evil;

**TDB77:** a kituak loin a gamta kei hi, ama phat nading a zong kei hi, a heh baih thei kei hi, gitlohna a ciamteh kei hi,

**ZVS:** Itna neite a kilawm loin gamtalo, heh pakpaklo, mite' mawhna ciamtehlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Rejoiceth not in iniquity, but rejoiceth in the truth;

**TDB77:** gitlohna thu-ah a lungdam thei kei hi, thuman thutak ah a lungdam thei hi,

**ZVS:** Thuman lote-ah lungdam ngeilo uh a, thumante bekah lungdam uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v7
**EN:** Beareth all things, believeth all things, hopeth all things, endureth all things.

**TDB77:** na khempeuh a thuak thei hi, na khempeuh a um thei hi, na khempeuh a thuak keeikaai hi.

**ZVS:** Itna neite lungkia cih om ngeilo hi; a upna, a lametna, lungduai takin a ngaknate uh a mawkna cih om ngeilo hi.

### v8
**EN:** Charity never faileth: but whether there be prophecies, they shall fail; whether there be tongues, they shall cease; whether there be knowledge, it shall vanish away.

**TDB77:** Itna in a mangthang kei ding hi. Kamsangte’ thu a om leh a mangthang ding hi. Pautuam gen theihna a om leh, a bei ding hi. Lungpilna a om leh, a mangthang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Itna pen a tawntungin a kip ahi hi. Thuhilh siamna a om tei hangin tawlkhat sung bek kimangin, pau lamdang pautheih siamnate a om hangin bei ding a, thutheih siamna a om hangin maimang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v9
**EN:** For we know in part, and we prophesy in part.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, eite in kicing loin i thei hi, kamsangte’ pilna tawh kicing loin i hilh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh eite' neih thutheih siamnate leh i thuhilh siamnate a kicing hilo hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** But when that which is perfect is come, then that which is in part shall be done away.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin kicin’na a tun’ ciangin a kicing lote a mangthang ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin a kicing thu hong tun' ciangin a kicingtaaklo thuteng maimang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** When I was a child, I spake as a child, I understood as a child, I thought as a child: but when I became a man, I put away childish things.

**TDB77:** Naupang ka hih lai takin naupang bangin thu ka gen hi, naupang bangin ka ngaihsun hi, naupang bangin ka hanciam hi. Ahih hangin ka khan’ khit ciangin naupang nate ka paaikhia hi.

**ZVS:** Naupang ka hih hun lai-in naupang mah bangin kampau-in, thu ngaihsunin, ka gamta hi. Ahi zongin tu-in khangham ka hih manin naupang vaite ka zang nawn kei hi.

### v12
**EN:** For now we see through a glass, darkly; but then face to face: now I know in part; but then shall I know even as also I am known.

**TDB77:** Tu lai takin kimu tel loin limlang sungah i en hi. Tu nung ciangin i maitang a tel takin i mu ding hi. Tu-in a kicing loin ka thei hi. Tu nung ciangin Pasian in kei hong theih bangin ka thei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Dal limlang sunga et bangin tu laitakin a kitel phaloin i mu milmial a, ahi zongin mailamah maitang kituahin i mu ding hi. Tu laitakin i theihnate kicinglo a, ahi zongin mailamah Pasian in eite hong theih mah bangin a kicing takin amah i thei ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** And now abideth faith, hope, charity, these three; but the greatest of these is charity.

**TDB77:** Tu-in upna, lawpna, itna hih thute thum a om uh hi. A thumte sungpan itna thu a lianbel hi.

**ZVS:** Tu laitakin a omlai thu thumte pen upna, lametna, leh itna hi a, tua thumte sung panin itna mah a thu lianpen ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker
