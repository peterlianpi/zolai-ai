**EN:** What, my son? and what, the son of my womb? and what, the son of my vows?

**TDB77:** Ka it mahmah ka ta aw, thu ngenngen-a kong neih tapa na hi hi. Bang hong gen ding ka hi hiam?

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v3

**TDB77:** Na thatang teng numei tungah beisak kei in la, na sum khempeuh numei tungah zang kei in, numei in kumpipa siasak thei hi.

**ZVS:** Na thatang teng numei tungah beisak kei in la, na sum khempeuh numei tungah zang kei in. Numei in kumpipa siasak thei hi.

### v4
**EN:** It is not for kings, O Lemuel, it is not for kings to drink wine; nor for princes strong drink:

**TDB77:** Lemuel aw, ka thugen hong ngai in. Kumpite in zu nek huai lo a, mi ukte in khamtheihzu a duh ding hi lo hi.

**ZVS:** Lemuel aw, ka thugen hong ngai in. Kumpite in zu nek huailo a, mi ukte in khamtheihzu a duh ding hilo hi.

**ZVS:** Zu ne le uh, thukham mangngilh uh a, thukham hei-in mizawngte gim pia khathei uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker
