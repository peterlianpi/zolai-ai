## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then Job answered and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Job in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** I have heard many such things: miserable comforters are ye all.

**TDB77:** “Tua bang thute ka za ngei khin hi; hehnepna in gim nading bek ahi hi.

**ZVS:** “Tua bang thute ka zaza khin a, no' hehnepna pen gimna ding bek ahi hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v3
**EN:** Shall vain words have an end? or what emboldeneth thee that thou answerest?

**TDB77:** Khawl hun nei loin gengen lai ding na hi-te hiam? Na gen ding-te omom lai ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Khawl hun neiloin gengen lai ding na hi-te hiam? Na gen ding-te omom lai ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** I also could speak as ye do: if your soul were in my soul's stead, I could heap up words against you, and shake mine head at you.

**TDB77:** Note leh kei kikheek thei hi lehang, note’ gen khempeuh kei zong ka gen thei ding hi. Note ka lu hong pei sanin, thu tampi tak mah kong gen thei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Note leh keimah a kikhek thei hi lehang, note' gen khempeuh kei zong ka gen thei ding hi. Note ka lu hong peisanin thu tampi tak mah ka hong gen thei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** But I would strengthen you with my mouth, and the moving of my lips should assuage your grief.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin, keimah in note hong tua cih loin, thu hoih tawh note hong hehnemin, note hehnep nading thu ka gen ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin keimah in note hong tua cihloin, thuhoih mahmah tawh note hong hehnemin, note hehnepna ding thu ka gen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** Though I speak, my grief is not assuaged: and though I forbear, what am I eased?

**TDB77:** “Thu gen leng ka natna nuamtuam lo a, pau kei ta leng zong, phatuam tuan lo hi.

**ZVS:** Thu gengen ta leng ka natna nuamtuamlo a, pau kei ta leng zong, phatuam tuanlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v7
**EN:** But now he hath made me weary: thou hast made desolate all my company.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, kei nong paatsak ngiingei a, ka innkuan teng na si sak khin hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, kei nong paatsak ngiingei a, ka innkuan khempeuh na sisakmang khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v8
**EN:** And thou hast filled me with wrinkles, which is a witness against me: and my leanness rising up in me beareth witness to my face.

**TDB77:** Kei nong mancip a, ka gal na suak hi. Ka vun guak leh ka guh guak bek-in ka om a, keima mawhna a kilatna-in mite in ngaihsun-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kei na hong mancip a, keima gal na suak hi. Ka vun guak leh ka guh guak bekin ka om a, keima mawhna a kilatna-in mite in a ngaihsun-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** He teareth me in his wrath, who hateth me: he gnasheth upon me with his teeth; mine enemy sharpeneth his eyes upon me.

**TDB77:** Pasian in a hehna tawh hong balnen ngiingei a, a ha hong gawi sanin hong heh et hi.

**ZVS:** “Pasian in a hehna tawh hong balnen ngiingei a, a ha hong gawisanin hong heh et hi.

### v10
**EN:** They have gaped upon me with their mouth; they have smitten me upon the cheek reproachfully; they have gathered themselves together against me.

**TDB77:** Mite in kei hong zahko-te a, ka paang a beng dingin hong kikhawmtuah-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11

**TDB77:** Pasian in migilote khut sungah kei hong pia hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in migilote' khutsungah kei hong pia hi.

### v12
**EN:** I was at ease, but he hath broken me asunder: he hath also taken me by my neck, and shaken me to pieces, and set me up for his mark.

**TDB77:** Kei nuamtakin a om kim lai, Pasian in ka gawl-in hong manin, hong balgawpin hong sugawp hi. Pasian in a muitum dingin kei hong suang a,

**ZVS:** Kei nuamtakin a om kimlai, Pasian in ka gawlin hong manin, hong balgawpin hong sugawp hi. Pasian in a muitum dingin kei hong suang a,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

**TDB77:** Amah in kei hong liamsak thuahthuah a, galkap a hehpa bangin hong hih gawp hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in hong hehpih hetloin kei hong liamsak thuahthuah a, galkap a hehpa bangin kei hong hihgawp hi.

### v15

**TDB77:** Kei thuum-in puanhampi ka silh a, guallel-in leivui lakah ka om hi.

**ZVS:** “Kei thumin puanhampi ka silh a, guallelin leivui lakah ka om hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v16

**TDB77:** Ka kah kahna-in ka mai khempeuh sangawp a, ka mitkhute bawkin hong liahcip hi.

**ZVS:** Ka kahkahna-in ka mai khempeuh san gawp a, ka mitkhute bawkgawpin khua ka mu thei kei hi.

### v17
**EN:** Not for any injustice in mine hands: also my prayer is pure.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin ngongtatna themkhat zong ka zang kei a, Pasian’ tungah ka thungetna in lungsim takpi tawh ahi hi.
