## Verses

### v1
**EN:** How amiable are thy tabernacles, O

**TDB77:** Vanglianpen Topa aw, na teen’na mun bangzah takin etlawm ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Vanglian { Topa } aw, nangma ten'na mun bangzah takin etlawm ahi hiam?

### v2
**EN:** My soul longeth, yea, even fainteth for the courts of the

**TDB77:** Ke’n ka lunggulh mahmah hi, hi taktak hi, Topa’ huang sung ka lunggulh mahmah a, ka lungsim leh ka pumpi in a nungta Pasian tungah lungdamin la a sa hi.

**ZVS:** Ke'n ka lunggulh mahmah hi. { Topa' } huangsung ka lunggulh mahmah a, ka lungsim leh ka pumpi a nungta Pasian' tungah lungdam mahmahin la a sa hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v3
**EN:** Yea, the sparrow hath found an house, and the swallow a nest for herself, where she may lay her young, even thine altars, O

**TDB77:** Mi honpite Topa, ka kumpi leh ka Pasian aw, na biakna tau-ah, kawlgit nangawn in a inn ding mu a, phialphiah in ama om nading, a note a koihna bu a mu hi.

**ZVS:** Vanglian { Topa, } ka kumpi leh ka Pasian aw, na biakna tau-ah, kawlgit nangawn in a inn ding mu a, phialphiah in a omna ding, a note a koihna ding bu a mu hi.

### v4
**EN:** Blessed are they that dwell in thy house: they will be still praising thee. Selah.

**TDB77:** Nangma inn sungah teeng a, nangmah phatna a sa ngitnget mite in thupha ngah mi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma inn sungah tengin, nangmah phatna a sa ngitnget mite thupha ngah mi ahi uh hi.

### v5
**EN:** Blessed is the man whose strength is in thee; in whose heart are the ways of them.

**TDB77:** Nangma sungah amau thahatna om a, amau lungsim sungah Zion zuatna lampi a om mite in thupha ngah mi ahi uh hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma tung panin thahatna ngahin Zion zuatna lampi a tawn nuam mite thupha ngah mi ahi uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Who passing through the valley of Baca make it a well; the rain also filleth the pools.

**TDB77:** Amaute in Kuam keuna mun kantanin a pai laitakin, tua kuam pen ciktui tampi tawh kidim a, guahzu masate in zong tua mun pen bualtuite tawh kidim sak hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in Kuamkeu mun kantanin a pai laitakun tua kuam pen ciktui tampi tawh kidim a, guahzu masate in zong tua mun pen bualtuite tawh a kidimsak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** They go from strength to strength, every one of them in Zion appeareth before God.

**TDB77:** Amaute a tha uh hat semsem hi; Zion-ah pasiante’ Pasian kimu ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute a paikawmun a tha uh hat semsem a, pasian-te' Pasian Zion-ah mu ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Mi honte Topa Pasian aw, ka thungetna hong za in; hong ngai in, Jakob Pasian aw!

**ZVS:** Vanglian { Topa } Pasian aw, ka thungetna hong za in la, ka thungetna hong ngai in, Jakob Pasian!

### v9
**EN:** Behold, O God our shield, and look upon the face of thine anointed.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka lum uh en in; na sathau nilhpa’ maitang en in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, kote a hong hu dingpa hong etsak in la, na sathau nilhpa hong kem in.

### v10
**EN:** For a day in thy courts is better than a thousand. I had rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God, than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mun dangah ni tulkhat om sangin nangma huang sungah ni khat om hoih zaw hi. Gitlohna inn sungah ka teen’ sangin ka Pasian’ inn kongcingpa ka hi nuamzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mun dangah ni tul khat om sangin nangma huang sungah ni khat om hoihzaw hi. Gitlohna inn sungah ka ten' sangin ka Pasian' inn kongcingpa ka hi nuamzaw hi.

### v11
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa Pasian in eite hong hupa leh a minthang i Kumpi hi a, maipha leh pahtawina hong pia hi. Thuman-a gamtate tungah Topa in na hoih bangmah siit lo hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } Pasian pen eite a hong hupa a minthang i kumpi hi a, maipha leh pahtawina a hong piapa ahi hi. Thumanin a gamta mite tungah { Topa } in na hoih bangmah sitlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v12
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Mi honte Topa aw, nangmah hong muang mi in thupha ngah mi ahi hi!

**ZVS:** Vanglian { Topa } aw, nangmah a hong muang mite, thupha ngah mi ahi uh hi.
