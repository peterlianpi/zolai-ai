## Verses

### v2
**EN:** Thou hast forgiven the iniquity of thy people, thou hast covered all their sin. Selah.

**TDB77:** Na mite’ mawhna na maisak a, amau khialhna khempeuh na maisak hi.

**ZVS:** Na mite' mawhna na maisak a, amau' khialhna khempeuh na maisak hi.

### v3
**EN:** Thou hast taken away all thy wrath: thou hast turned thyself from the fierceness of thine anger.

**TDB77:** Na hehna khempeuh na damsak a, na sin so-na na daisak hi.

**ZVS:** Na hehna khempeuh na damsak a, na sinsona na daisak hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v4
**EN:** Turn us, O God of our salvation, and cause thine anger toward us to cease.

**TDB77:** Kote’ kihotkhiatna Pasian aw, kote ka ngei uh hong bangsak kik in la, kote tunga na lungkim lohna beisak ta in!

**ZVS:** Kote hong honkhia Pasian aw, kote ka ngei uh hong bangsakkik in la, kote tunga na lungkimlohna hong beisak ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Wilt thou be angry with us for ever? wilt thou draw out thine anger to all generations?

**TDB77:** Kote tungah hong heh paisuak ding na hi hiam? Khang tawntungin heh paisuak ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Kote' tungah heh paisuak ding na hi hiam? Khang tawntungin heh paisuak ding na hi hiam?

### v6
**EN:** Wilt thou not revive us again: that thy people may rejoice in thee?

**TDB77:** Nangmah kong phat theih nadingun ko na mite hong nungtasak kik lo ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Kote in nangmah kong phat theihna dingun ko na mite hong nungtasakkik loding na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Shew us thy mercy, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kote tungah na itna kip hong lak in la, na hotkhiatna kote hong pia in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kote tungah na itna kip hong lak in la, hong huhin hong hon in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** I will hear what God the

**TDB77:** Topa Pasian’ thu gen ding hong zasak dih un, bang hang hiam cih leh amah in ama lamah a lungsim uh tawh a kiheite, ama mi siangthote, a mite tungah kilemna thu gen ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } Pasian' thugen ding hong zasak dih un. Bang hang hiam cih leh ama lamah lungsim takpi tawh a kihei ama mi siangtho, ama mite' tungah kilemna thu a gen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Surely his salvation is nigh them that fear him; that glory may dwell in our land.

**TDB77:** I gam sungah minthan’na a om theih nadingin amah a zahtakte’ adingin ama hotkhiatna hong om takpi hi.

**ZVS:** I gam sungah minthan'na a om theihna dingin amah a zahtakte-a' dingin ama hong hotkhiatna hong om takpi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v10
**EN:** Mercy and truth are met together; righteousness and peace have kissed each other.

**TDB77:** Itna kip leh thutakna kigawm ding a, thumanna leh kilemna kihual ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Itna kip leh thutak kigawm ding a, thuman leh kilemna kihual ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** Truth shall spring out of the earth; and righteousness shall look down from heaven.

**TDB77:** Leilak panin thutakna hong po khiato ding a, thumanna in vantung panin ensuk ding hi.

**ZVS:** Leilak panin thutak hong pokhia ding a, vantung panin thuman in hong ensuk ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v12
**EN:** Yea, the

**TDB77:** Hi hi, Topa in a hoih nate hong pia ding a, i leitang in hauhna piangsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in a hoih nate eite tungah hong pia ding a, i leitang in hauhna piangsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | future: will

### v13
**EN:** Righteousness shall go before him; and shall set us in the way of his steps.

**TDB77:** Thumanna in Topa’ mai-ah mapai ding a, ama lampi bawlkhol ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } mai-ah thuman na ma pai ding a, ama lampi a bawlkhol ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
