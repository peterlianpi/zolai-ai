### v1
**EN:** This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;

**TDB77:** Hih in Adam’ suan-le-khakte’ tangthute ahi hi. Pasian in mi a piansakin, amah tawh a kisunin a bawl hi.

**ZVS:** Hih pen Adam' suanlekhakte' laibu ahi hi. Pasian in mi a pian'sakin, amah in Pasian tawh a kisunin a bawl hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.

**TDB77:** Amah in pasal leh numei piangsak hi, amaute a kipian’ sak khit ciangin amah in amaute Mi ci a, thupha a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in numei leh pasal a piangsak hi. Amaute a kipiansakin amah in amaute, “Mi,” ci a, amaute thupha a pia hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
