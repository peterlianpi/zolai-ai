## Verses

### v1
**EN:** When the

**TDB77:** Topa in Zion-ah eite hong ciahpih kik ciangin, i mang tawh kibang liang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in Zion-ah eite hong ciahpih kik ciangin i mang tawh kibang liang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Then was our mouth filled with laughter, and our tongue with singing: then said they among the heathen, The

**TDB77:** Lungdamin i nui a, lungdam lua-in la i sa hi. Tua ciangin minam dangte kiho-in, “Topa in amau adingin na lianpite bawl khin ta hi,” kici-te hi.

**ZVS:** Lungdamin i nui a, lungdam lua-in la i sa hi. Tua ciangin minam dangte kiho-in, “{ Topa } in amau-a' dingin na lianpite bawl khinta hi,” kici-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in eite’ adingin na lianpite bawl khin ta a, eite i kipak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in eite-a' dingin na lianpite bawl khinta a, eite i kipak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Turn again our captivity, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, lei keuna mun-ah guah in tui a puak bangin kote’ nopsaknate a ngei hong bangsak kik ta in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, lei keuna munah guah in tui a puak bangin kote' nopsaknate a ngei hong bangsakkik ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** They that sow in tears shall reap in joy.

**TDB77:** Khitui tawh khai a tuhte, lungdam-a kikona tawh an lasak ta in!

**ZVS:** Khitui tawh khai a tuhte lungdamin kikona tawh an la sak ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing precious seed, shall doubtless come again with rejoicing, bringing his sheaves with him.

**TDB77:** Kap kawm-in kuankhia-in a tuh ding khaici a pua mi in lungdam-a kikona tawh buhlomte pua-in innah hong ciah ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kap kawmin kuankhia-in a tuh ding khaici a pua mi lungdamin kikona tawh buhlomte pua-in hong ciah ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
