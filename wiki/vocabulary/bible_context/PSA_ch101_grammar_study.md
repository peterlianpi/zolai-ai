## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I will sing of mercy and judgment: unto thee, O

**TDB77:** Keima la in nangma itna leh thutan’na hi a, nang kong sak ding hi. Topa aw, kong sak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Keima la pen thumanna leh thutan'na la ahi hi. Nang ka hong sak ding hi; { Topa } aw, kong sak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v2
**EN:** I will behave myself wisely in a perfect way. O when wilt thou come unto me? I will walk within my house with a perfect heart.

**TDB77:** Paubaan’na a om lo thu bek ka kin ding hi. Cik ciangin kei kiangah nang hong pai ding na hi hiam? Lungsim hoihna tawh ka inn sungah ka gamta ding hi;

**ZVS:** Paubaan'na a omlo thu bek ka kin ding hi. Cik ciangin kei kiangah hong pai ding na hi hiam? Lungsim hoihna tawh ka inn sungah ka gamta ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** I will set no wicked thing before mine eyes: I hate the work of them that turn aside; it shall not cleave to me.

**TDB77:** a sia na peuhmah ka deih kei ding hi. Lam khial mite’ nasepna ka mudah hi; tuate tawh ka kisukha kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** A sia na peuhmah ka deih kei ding hi. Lamkhial mite' nasepna ka mudah a, tuate tawh ka kisukha kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** A froward heart shall depart from me: I will not know a wicked person.

**TDB77:** Lungsim kawina ka deih het kei a, siatna, ngaihsut-in zong ka nei kha kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Lungsim kawina ka deih hetkei a, siatna, ngaihsutin zong ka neikha kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei

### v5
**EN:** Whoso privily slandereth his neighbour, him will I cut off: him that hath an high look and a proud heart will not I suffer.

**TDB77:** A vengte a gensia sim mite ka susia ding a, kiphatsakna leh kihihsakna lungsim a nei mi ka thuak kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** A vengte a gensia sim mite ka susia ding a, kiphatsakna, kihihsakna lungsim a nei mi ka thuak kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Mine eyes shall be upon the faithful of the land, that they may dwell with me: he that walketh in a perfect way, he shall serve me.

**TDB77:** Leitungah a thuman mite kei tawh a omkhop theih nadingun maipha ka pia ding a, paubaan’na om lo a, a gamta mi in keima na hong sem ding hi.

**ZVS:** Leitungah a thuman mite kei tawh a om theihna dingin maipha ka pia ding a, paubaan'na omloin a gamta mi in keima na hong sem ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo

### v7
**EN:** He that worketh deceit shall not dwell within my house: he that telleth lies shall not tarry in my sight.

**TDB77:** Khemna a zang mi peuhmah keima inn ah teeng lo ding a, zuauthu a gen mi peuhmah keima omna-ah om thei nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Khemna a zang mi peuhmah keima inn-ah tenglo ding a, zuauthu a gen mi peuhmah keima omna-ah om thei nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** I will early destroy all the wicked of the land; that I may cut off all wicked doers from the city of the

**TDB77:** Zingsang simin, leitunga migilote khempeuh ka susia ding a, Topa’ khuapi panin, siatna bawlte khempeuh ka khenkhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Zingsang simin leitunga migilote khempeuh ka susia ding a, { Topa' } khuapi panin siatna bawlte khempeuh ka khenkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
