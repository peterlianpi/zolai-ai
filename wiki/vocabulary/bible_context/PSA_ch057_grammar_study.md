## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: for my soul trusteth in thee: yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I make my refuge, until these calamities be overpast.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangmah hong bel ka hih manin kei hong hehpih in, kei hong hehpih in; siatna huihpi a ven’ mateng na kha limah kong bu ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong hehpih in Pasian aw, nang hong bel ka hih manin kei hong hehpih in. Siatna huihpi a ven' mateng na kha liimah kong bu ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v2
**EN:** I will cry unto God most high; unto God that performeth all things for me.

**TDB77:** A Sang Penpa Pasian’ tung, kei’ Pasian’ tung, kei’ adingin ama deihna a tungsak Pasian’ tungah ka ko hi.

**ZVS:** A Sangpen Pasian' tungah, keima deihna a hong tungsak Pasian' tungah ka ko hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** He shall send from heaven, and save me from the reproach of him that would swallow me up. Selah. God shall send forth his mercy and his truth.

**TDB77:** Amah in vantung panin hong dawngin keimah hong hon ding a, amah in kei hong tuancilte maizumin koih ding hi. Pasian in a itna kip leh a thumanna hong lak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in vantung panin keimah hong dawngin hong honkhia ding a, amah in kei hong tuancilte maizumin a koih ding hi. Pasian in ama itna kip leh a thumanna hong lak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** My soul is among lions: and I lie even among them that are set on fire, even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows, and their tongue a sharp sword.

**TDB77:** Mihing tate, huaihamna tawh a netum humpinelkaite laizang ah ka om hithiat hi; amau hate in teipi leh thaltangte hi a, amau leite in namsau hiamte ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Huaihamna tawh mihing a netum, humpinelkai tawh a kibang mite' lakah ka om hithiat hi. Amau' hate, namsau hiam tawh kibang hi.

### v5
**EN:** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens; let thy glory be above all the earth.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, vantung’ tungah sangto lai in! Leitung khempeuhah na minthan’na omsak in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, vantungah na liatna kilangsak in. Leitung khempeuhah na minthan'na omsak in.

### v6
**EN:** They have prepared a net for my steps; my soul is bowed down: they have digged a pit before me, into the midst whereof they are fallen themselves. Selah.

**TDB77:** Amaute in ka khe, thang hong siah-te hi; lungkhamin hong koih-te hi. Ka pai nading ah gum khat to-te a, ahih hangin tua sungah amaute mah kia-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in keima khe, thang hong siah-te a, lungkhamin hong koih-te hi. Ka paina ding munah gum khat to-te a, ahih hangin tua gum sungah amaute mah kia-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed: I will sing and give praise.

**TDB77:** Ka lung muang hi, Pasian aw, ka lung muang hi! La ka sa ding a, nakpi takin ka sa ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungmuang hi, Pasian aw, ka lungmuang hi. La ka sa ding a, nakpi takin ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** Awake up, my glory; awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake early.

**TDB77:** Ka tho ding hi! Harp lasakna leh laiza lasakna aw, tho un! Zingsang khuavak hong phong ning!

**ZVS:** Ka tho ding hi. Harp lasakna leh laia lasakna aw, tho ta un. Zingsang khuavak hong phong ning.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** I will praise thee, O Lord, among the people: I will sing unto thee among the nations.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, mite lakah nang tung lungdamna kong ko ding a, minamte lakah nangmah phatna ka sa ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, mite lakah lungdam kong ko ding a, minamte lakah nangmah phatna ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v10
**EN:** For thy mercy is great unto the heavens, and thy truth unto the clouds.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh na itna kip in vantung ban a, na thumanna in meiipi banto hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: let thy glory be above all the earth.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, vantungte’ tungah sangto lai in! Leitung khempeuhah na minthan’na om sak in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, vantungah na liatna kilangsak in. Leitung khempeuhah na minthan'na omsak in.
