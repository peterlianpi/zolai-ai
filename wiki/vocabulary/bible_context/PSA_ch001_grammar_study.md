## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.

**TDB77:** Mi nuamsa i cih in, migilote’ thu a zui lo mi, mimawhte’ lampi-a ding lo mi, mi a thusim lote’ phual-a tu lo mi;

**ZVS:** Mi nuamsa i cihte pen migilote' thu a zuilo mi, mimawhte' gamtat bangin a gamtalo mi, mi a thusim lote tawh a kipawllo mi ahi hi.

### v2
**EN:** But his delight is in the law of the

**TDB77:** ama nopsakna in Topa’ thu sung bek mah hi a, sun leh zanin ama thu a ngaihsunsun mi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ama nopsakna pen { Topa } thu sung bek mah hi a, sun leh zanin ama thu a ngaihsunsun mi ahi hi.

### v3
**EN:** And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season; his leaf also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.

**TDB77:** Amah in luitui luang gei-a kisuan, ama gah hunin gah a, a teh a vul lo singkung tawh kibang hi. Ahihna khempeuh ah a ma pai hi.

**ZVS:** Amah pen lui tuiluang gei-a a kisuan ama gah hunin gah a, a teh a vul ngeilo singkung tawh kibang hi. Ama hihna khempeuhah a ma pai mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** The ungodly are not so: but are like the chaff which the wind driveth away.

**TDB77:** Migilote ahih leh tua bang hi lo a, huih letkhiat ansi tawh kibang-te hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote ahih leh tua bang hilo-te a, huih in a letkhiat anvai tawh kibang lel-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin migilote in thukhenna nang zo lo ding a, mimawhte in midikte’ pawl sungah kihel thei lo ding hi;

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin migilote in thukhenna thuak zolo ding-te a, mimawhte pen midikte' pawl sungah kihel theilo ding-te hi;

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** bang hang hiam cih leh Topa in midikte’ lampi kem a, migilote’ lampi ahi leh siamang ding hi.

**ZVS:** bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } in midikte lam lakin hu tawntung a, migilote ahih leh sia maimang ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
