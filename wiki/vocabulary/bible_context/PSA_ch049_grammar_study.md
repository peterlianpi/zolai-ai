## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Hear this, all ye people; give ear, all ye inhabitants of the world:

**TDB77:** Hih thu za un, mi khempeuh aw! Ngai un, leitung a teeng khempeuh,

**ZVS:** Hih thu za un, mi khempeuh aw! Ngai un, leitunga a teng khempeuh,

### v2
**EN:** Both low and high, rich and poor, together.

**TDB77:** milian mineu, mihau mizawngte aw!

### v3
**EN:** My mouth shall speak of wisdom; and the meditation of my heart shall be of understanding.

**TDB77:** Ka kam in pilna thu gen ding hi; ka lungsim’ ngaihsutna in thutelna hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka kam in pilna thu gen ding a, ka lungsim ngaihsutna kitelsiang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v4
**EN:** I will incline mine ear to a parable: I will open my dark saying upon the harp.

**TDB77:** Paunak thu theih hak khat ka ngai ding a, laiza lasakna tawh a khiatna ka gentel ding hi.

**ZVS:** Paunak thu theihhak khat ka ngaihsun ding a, laia lasakna tawh a khiatna ka gentel ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v5
**EN:** Wherefore should I fear in the days of evil, when the iniquity of my heels shall compass me about?

**TDB77:** Haksatna hun, kei hong bawlsiate’ gitlohna in kei hong nehcip ciangin bang hangin lau ding ka hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Haksat hun, hong bawlsiate' gitlohna in hong nen'cip ciangin bang hangin lau ding ka hi hiam?

### v6
**EN:** They that trust in their wealth, and boast themselves in the multitude of their riches;

**TDB77:** Mite in a hauhna-te muangin, tampi a neihna-te a kisaktheihpih hangun,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** None of them can by any means redeem his brother, nor give to God a ransom for him:

**TDB77:** amah a kitankhia tawm taktak thei, a nuntakna’ man ding Pasian’ tungah a pia thei mi kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** amah leh amah a kitankhia tawmzo taktak, a nuntakna man ding Pasian' tungah a piazo mi kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** That he should still live for ever, and not see corruption.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh a nuntakna tatna ding a man tam mahmah a, Leihawmpi mu ngeilo zah ding a hing tawntung dingin kitankhiazo ngeilo hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** For he seeth that wise men die, likewise the fool and the brutish person perish, and leave their wealth to others.

**TDB77:** Hi hi, amah in a pilte nangawn a si mu ding a, a haite leh a teilote a kibangin sia-in, amau neihsate midangte’ tungah nusiat-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in a pilte nangawn a si mu ding a, a haite leh a teilote a kibangin sia-in, amau' neihsa khempeuh midangte' tungah nusiat-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** Their inward thought is, that their houses shall continue for ever, and their dwelling places to all generations; they call their lands after their own names.

**TDB77:** Amaute in leitangte amau min a puaksak hangun, hante pen khang tawntunga amau omna, amau inn hi ngitnget hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in leitangte amau' min a puaksak-te hangin hankhuk pen khang tawntung amau' omna hi ngitnget ding hi.

### v12
**EN:** Nevertheless man being in honour abideth not: he is like the beasts that perish.

**TDB77:** Ama hauhna-ah mi kip suak thei lo a, amah in a bei thei ganhingte tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Ama hauhna-ah mi kipsuak theilo a, a sithei ganhingte tawh a kibang lel ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** This their way is their folly: yet their posterity approve their sayings. Selah.

**TDB77:** Hih in a kihai muan’te’ ngah hi a, amau ngahsa tawh a lungkimte’ thumong ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih pen a kihai muan'te' ngah hi a, amau' ngahsa tawh a lungkimte' thumong ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** Like sheep they are laid in the grave; death shall feed on them; and the upright shall have dominion over them in the morning; and their beauty shall consume in the grave from their dwelling.

**TDB77:** Tua bangin amaute Leinuai-a dingin kiseh hi; Sihna in amau cingpa hi ding hi; Midikte in zingsang ciangin uk ding-te hi, hankhuk-ah amaute tuaksuk suak-te a, a meel-te sia ding hi; Leinuai in amau’ inn hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bangin amaute Leinuai-a dingin kiseh hi. Sihna pen amau a cingpa hi ding hi. Hankhuk sungah amaute tuaksuksuak ding-te a, a meel-te sia ding a, Leinuai, amau' inn hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** But God will redeem my soul from the power of the grave: for he shall receive me. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Pasian in Leinuai’ vangliatna panin kei hong tankhia ding hi, bang hang hiam cih leh amah in kei hong sang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin Pasian in Leinuai' vangliatna panin kei hong honkhia ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh amah in kei hong sang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v16
**EN:** Be not thou afraid when one is made rich, when the glory of his house is increased;

**TDB77:** Mi khatpeuh a hauh ciang ahi zongin, a innkuan minthan’na a khan’ ciang ahi zongin lau kei in.

**ZVS:** Mi khatpeuh a hauh ciang ahi a, a innkuan minthan'na a khan' ciang ahi zongin lau-in lunghihmawh dingin ngaihsun tuan kei in.

### v17
**EN:** For when he dieth he shall carry nothing away: his glory shall not descend after him.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh a sih ciangin bangmah keng lo ding a, a minthan’na in amah zuisuk lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh a sih ciangin bangmah kenglo ding a, a minthan'na in amah zuisuklo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v18
**EN:** Though while he lived he blessed his soul: and men will praise thee, when thou doest well to thyself.

**TDB77:** A hin’ lai-in amah a lungdamin kingaihsunin, mi khat in ama ading na a limsep ciangin phatna a ngah hangin,

**ZVS:** A hin' lai-in amah a lungdamin kingaihsunin, mi khat in ama-a' ding na a lim sep ciangin phatna a ngah hangin,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** He shall go to the generation of his fathers; they shall never see light.

**TDB77:** khuavak mu ngei nawn lo dingin a sisa a pu a pate kiang mah-ah amah pai veve ding hi.

**ZVS:** khuavak a mu nawnlo dingin a si khinsa a pu a pate kiang mahah amah pai veve ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20
**EN:** Man that is in honour, and understandeth not, is like the beasts that perish.

**TDB77:** Ama hauhna in mihing suaksak thei lo a, amah in a si thei ganhingte tawh a kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Ama hauhna in mi hing paisuaksak theilo a, a sithei ganhingte tawh a kibang lel ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
