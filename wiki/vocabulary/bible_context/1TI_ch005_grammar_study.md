## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Rebuke not an elder, but entreat him as a father; and the younger men as brethren;

**TDB77:** Khangham upate na taai kei in, pa bangin na thuum in. Tangvalte na sangampa bangin ahi zongin,

**ZVS:** Nang sangin a uzaw mite taiibawl kei in la, na pa bangin ngaihsunin hanthawn in. Nang sangin a nauzawte na nau bangin bawl in.

### v2
**EN:** The elder women as mothers; the younger as sisters, with all purity.

**TDB77:** nupite nute bangin ahi zongin, nungakte na khempeuhah a siangtho tawh ahi zongin thu na hilh in.

**ZVS:** Nang sangin a uzaw numeite na nu bangin bawl in la, nang sangin a nauzaw numeite, lungsim siangtho tak tawh na nau numei bangin bawl in.

### v3
**EN:** Honour widows that are widows indeed.

**TDB77:** Meigong taktak ahi numeite na zahtak in.

**ZVS:** Meigong taktak ahi numeite zahtakbawl in.

### v4
**EN:** But if any widow have children or nephews, let them learn first to shew piety at home, and to requite their parents: for that is good and acceptable before God.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin ta leh tu a nei meigongnu ahih leh, a tute a tate in a masa-in amau innkuanpihte zahtakin, a pa, a pite’ hehpihna thukkik nading sin uh hen. Bang hang hiam cih leh, tua bang hihna in Pasian mai-ah a deihhuai ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Meigong numei khatpeuh in tate ahi a, tute ahi zongin a neih leh innkuan sungah biakna tawh kisai-in tavuan a neihna uh hilh in la, a nu a pa, a pu a pite a huhna dingun gen in. Hih bang gamtatna pen Pasian' muhna-ah a hoih thu ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** Now she that is a widow indeed, and desolate, trusteth in God, and continueth in supplications and prayers night and day.

**TDB77:** Meigongnu taktak hi a, khat guak a om lai numei in Pasian muang a, sun tawh zan tawh thungenin a om tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** A kem a khoi ding a omlo meigong taktak ahi numeite in Pasian bek mah muan' leh suan'in nei-in, a sun a zanin Pasian' huhding deihin thu ngen tawntung uh hi.

### v6
**EN:** But she that liveth in pleasure is dead while she liveth.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin a mawktatna-ah, nopsa-a a om meigongnu ahih leh, a nuntak hangin si-in a om hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin a pumpi uh deihna zui-in a gamta meigong numeite pen a pumpi uh a nuntak hangin kha thu-ah a sikhin ahi uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** And these things give in charge, that they may be blameless.

**TDB77:** Tua mite in mawhsakna tawh a kipelh nadingun hih thute vaikhak in.

**ZVS:** Amaute paubaan'na a omlohna dingin hih ka hong thupiaksate amaute tungah gensawn in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** But if any provide not for his own, and specially for those of his own house, he hath denied the faith, and is worse than an infidel.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in ama mite, ahi takpi gen leeng ama innkuanpihte, a kep kei leh, thu-upna nial a, a um lo mite sangin a gilo zaw hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khatpeuh in a beh a phung, a diakdiakin a innkuansung mite a vak nop kei leh a upna a nusia hita a, thu umlo mite sangin siazaw lai hi.

### v9
**EN:** Let not a widow be taken into the number under threescore years old, having been the wife of one man,

**TDB77:** Meigongnu in kum sawmguk a phak kei leh, a min na khum kei in. Amah in pasal khat a nei ngei,

**ZVS:** Meigong numei khat peuhpeuh a khan' kum sawmguk a cingsate, leh pasal khatvei bek a nei ngeite bek, meigong sazian sungah koih in.

### v10
**EN:** Well reported of for good works; if she have brought up children, if she have lodged strangers, if she have washed the saints' feet, if she have relieved the afflicted, if she have diligently followed every good work.

**TDB77:** na pha sem a, maipha a ngah, a tate a khoi thei, zin a do thei, mi siangthote’ khe a sil thei, gimna thuakte a hehnem thei, nasepna a pha khempeuh a sem thei numei ahih leh, tua numei a min na khum ta in.

**ZVS:** Tua banah a gamtat hoihna a kitheite, a tate hoihtakin a khan'suahsate, a inn-ah zin leh leengla do a siam mite, kiniamkhiatin Pasian' mite' khe a silsakte, a lungkham mite a huhte, leh thu khempeuhah a hoih gamtat mite hi lai ding uh hi.

### v11
**EN:** But the younger widows refuse: for when they have begun to wax wanton against Christ, they will marry;

**TDB77:** A khangno lai meigongnute ahih uh leh, a min uh khum kei in. Bang hang hiam cih leh, tua numeite in nopsakna lungsim nei-in, Khazih tawh kilem loin pasal nei nuam uh a,

**ZVS:** A khangno lai meigong numeite, sazian sungah hel kei in. A pumpi uh deihna zui-in pasal a neihkik nop uh ciangin Khrih taisan uh ahih manin,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** Having damnation, because they have cast off their first faith.

**TDB77:** a masa thuciamna siasak uh ahih ciangin thukhenna a thuak uh hi.

**ZVS:** a thuciam masak uh palsat uh ahih manin a mawh a suak uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** And withal they learn to be idle, wandering about from house to house; and not only idle, but tattlers also and busybodies, speaking things which they ought not.

**TDB77:** Tua tham loin, inn khat pan inn khatah vak kawikawi-in, thadah tham loin, kam tamin, a gen tuak lo nate genin, amau tawh kikum lo thu-ah a kihel thei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bek zong hiloin, bangmah semloin inn khat pan inn khatah vak kawikawi-in a hun uh mawk beisak uh a, a vakmawk uh tawh maisak loin mi gensia kawikawi-in midangte va nawngkaisak uh a, a genlohpi ding teng uh genin va nei uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** I will therefore that the younger women marry, bear children, guide the house, give none occasion to the adversary to speak reproachfully.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin a khangno meigongnute in pasal a neih nading, ta zong a neih nading, a inn sung a uk nading, i galte in hong gensiat theih loh nading ahih nadingun ka deih hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin a khangno lai meigong numeite in pasal nei-in, ta nei-in, amau innkuansung ukin, i langte' hong gensiatna ding a omlohna ding ka deihzaw hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v15
**EN:** For some are already turned aside after Satan.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, a kimkhat in tu lai nangawnin Satan nung a zui uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh meigong numei pawlkhat, Satan' nung a zui dingin kipan khinta uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v16
**EN:** If any man or woman that believeth have widows, let them relieve them, and let not the church be charged; that it may relieve them that are widows indeed.

**TDB77:** Thu-um numei khatpeuh in a inn sungah meigongte a neih leh, pawlpi tungah van suan loin amahmah in vaak hen. Tua bang ahih leh, meigongnu taktak ahi-te pawlpi in a vaak thei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Thu um innkuansungah meigong numei a nei a om leh pawlpite in a kisam meigong taktakte a huhtheihna dingin pawlpi tungah tavuan hong guan' kei hen la, amau' meigong numeite na huh ta uh hen.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v17
**EN:** Let the elders that rule well be counted worthy of double honour, especially they who labour in the word and doctrine.

**TDB77:** Pawlpi a pha takpi-a ukte, a man takpi ci leeng Pasian’ thu gen a, thuhilhin a hahkatte in, a zah nih zahtakna ngah ding a kilawm na ciamteh in.

**ZVS:** Pawlpi sungah ukna leh makaihna siamin, thuhilh leh thugen hahkat tawh a thuah upate pen thaman a zah nih piakdingin kilawm hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** For the scripture saith, Thou shalt not muzzle the ox that treadeth out the corn. And,

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh: “Buh a cil bawng’ kam na hencip kei in,” leh “Nasemte in thaman ngah ding a kilawm hi,” ci-in Lai Siangtho in a gen hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Lai Siangtho in, “Buh a cil bawngtal muk na tuam kei in,” a cih banah, “Nasempa in a thaman a ngahding a kilawm ahi hi,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** Against an elder receive not an accusation, but before two or three witnesses.

**TDB77:** Teci nih bang thum bang a om kei leh, pawlpi ukpa tungah mawhsakna kam na ngai kei in.

**ZVS:** Upa khat peuhpeuh a mawhsak mi nih leh thum a om kei buang leh thu-in na sim kei in.

### v20
**EN:** Them that sin rebuke before all, that others also may fear.

**TDB77:** Midangte in zong a lau theih nadingun, a khial mite, mi khempeuh mai-ah na taai in.

**ZVS:** A khial takpi upa a om leh upa dangte a lautheihna dingin a lawmte mai-ah tua a khialpa taii-in thuhilh in.

### v21
**EN:** I charge thee before God, and the Lord Jesus Christ, and the elect angels, that thou observe these things without preferring one before another, doing nothing by partiality.

**TDB77:** Thu ma khen loin, gup tuam neihna lungsim tawh bangmah bawl loin, hih thute na zuih nadingin Pasian’ mai, Khazih Zeisu’ mai leh a kiteel vantungmite mai-ah pha takpi-in thu kong pia hi.

**ZVS:** Hih ka hong gen thute na zatna-ah kuamah guptuam paihtuam nei hetloin hoihtakin hong mangin na zatna dingin Pasian, Khrih Jesuh, leh a siangtho vantung mite' muhna-ah limtakin a hong gen ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v22
**EN:** Lay hands suddenly on no man, neither be partaker of other men's sins: keep thyself pure.

**TDB77:** Thu ngaihsun masa loin kuama tungah a manlangin khutngakna nei kei in. Midangte mawhna tawh na kikum kei in, nangma nuntakna siangtho sak in.

**ZVS:** Topa' na a sem dingin kipsakna-in khut nga-in thungetna pen kuama tungah zang mengmeng kei in. Midang a khialte tawh khialkhawm kha kei in la, siangtho takin na omtheihna dingin limtakin kikem in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v23
**EN:** Drink no longer water, but use a little wine for thy stomach's sake and thine often infirmities.

**TDB77:** Na gilpi ading leh na cinatna ading tu-a kipan tui bek dawn loin leenggahzu tawm na zang in.

**ZVS:** Na gilsung natna leh na nat mun zelzelna hangin tui bek zang nawnloin leenggahzu tawmtawm na zang in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v24
**EN:** Some men's sins are open beforehand, going before to judgment; and some men they follow after.

**TDB77:** Mi kimkhatte a gitlohna uh kilangin thu kikhen masa thei hi. Mi kimkhat a gitlohna un, a nung uh hong zui hi.

**ZVS:** Mi pawlkhatte' khialhnate kimu pahpahin mawhsakna thuakpah uh a, pawlkhatte' khialhna pen damdamin hong kinung theih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v25
**EN:** Likewise also the good works of some are manifest beforehand; and they that are otherwise cannot be hid.

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin a kilang gamtatna hoih a om thei hi, a kilanglo gamtatna hoih a kiseel zo kei hi.

**ZVS:** Tua mah bangin gamtat hoihnate zong kilangin, kimu-in, a kithei pah kei zongin ki-imcip suak theilo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix
