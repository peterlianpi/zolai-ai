## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Through desire a man, having separated himself, seeketh and intermeddleth with all wisdom.

**TDB77:** Midang tawh a kipawl thei phalote pen amah bek thupi a kisate hi a, midangte’ cih khempeuh maan a sak khatbeek om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Midangte tawh a kipawlthei phalote amah bek thupi a kisate hi a, midangte' cih khempeuh man a sak khat beek omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
