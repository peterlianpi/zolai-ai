## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Blessed is he that considereth the poor: the

**TDB77:** Mi gentheite a khual mi in thupha ngah ahi hi. Topa in haksatna hunah amah honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Mi genthei a khualte thupha ngah ahi hi. { Topa } in haksatna hunah amaute honkhia ding hi.

### v2
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in amah hu-in hingsak hi; leitungah amah thupha ngah mipa kici hi; ama galte’ deihna-ah amah na piakhia kei hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in amaute hu-in hingsak ding a, leitungah amaute thupha ngah mi kici ding hi. Amau' galte' khutsungah amaute pialo ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** ci na-a a lupna tungah Topa in amah na kem a, a ci nat ciangin ama gitlohna khempeuh na damsak hi.

**ZVS:** A ci uh a nat ciangin { Topa } in amaute kem ding a, a cinatna khempeuh uh damsakkik ding hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v4
**EN:** I said,

**TDB77:** Ke’n ahih leh, “Topa aw, kei hong hehpih in; keimah hong damsak in, bang hang hiam cih leh nang tungah kong khial zo hi!” ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** Ke'n ahih leh, “{ Topa } aw, kei hong hehpih in la, kei hong damsak in; bang hang hiam cih leh nang tungah kong khialzo hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | quotative: said/saying

### v5
**EN:** Mine enemies speak evil of me, When shall he die, and his name perish?

**TDB77:** Ka galte in elna tawh keima thu gen uh a: “Cik ciangin amah a si tam, cik ciangin ama min a mang tam?” ci uh hi.

**ZVS:** Ka galte in elna tawh keima thu gen uh a, “Cik ciangin amah a si tam, cik ciangin a min a mang tam,” ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** And if he come to see me, he speaketh vanity: his heart gathereth iniquity to itself; when he goeth abroad, he telleth it.

**TDB77:** Keimah en dingin mi hong pai ciangin, kam mawkna pau uh hi, a lung sungah siatna kaikhawm uh a, a paikhiat uh ciangin genthang uh hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah a en dingin mi hong pai ciangin, kam mawkna a pau uh hi. A lungsim uhah siatna kaikhawm uh a, a paikhiat uh ciangin a genthang uh hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** All that hate me whisper together against me: against me do they devise my hurt.

**TDB77:** Kei hong mudah khempeuh in keima thu kihuauhuau uh a, kei’ adingin a sia thei pen gelgel uh hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong mudah khempeuh in keima thu kihuauhuau uh a, kei-a' dingin a sia thei pen ding a geelgeel uh hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** An evil disease, say they, cleaveth fast unto him: and now that he lieth he shall rise up no more.

**TDB77:** Amaute in, “Thusia khat ama tungah baang zo hi; a lupna panin thokik nawn lo ding hi,” ci uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in, “Amah nak gim mahmah hi. A lupna panin thokikzo nawnlo ding hi,” ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted, which did eat of my bread, hath lifted up his heel against me.

**TDB77:** Ka muan’, ka lawm itpa, keima an a nepa nangawn in, a khetul tawh keimah hong peek hi.

**ZVS:** Ka muan', ka lawm itpa, keima an a nepa nangawn in a khetul tawh kei hong peek vial hi.

### v10
**EN:** But thou, O

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin, Topa aw, amaute ka thukkik theih nadingin kei hong hehpih in la, hong phong to in!

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin, { Topa } aw, amaute na thukkik theihna dingin kei hong hehpih in la, hong damsak in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v11
**EN:** By this I know that thou favourest me, because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.

**TDB77:** Ka galte in keimah hong zawh lohna thu-ah, keima tungah nong lungkimna ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Ka galte in keimah hong zolo ding a, keima tungah nong lungkimna ka thei ding hi.

### v12
**EN:** And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity, and settest me before thy face for ever.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin keima kicin’na hangin nong huh a, nangma omna-ah kei nong koih den hi.

**ZVS:** Keima hoihna hangin na hong huh ding a, nangma omna-ah nong omsak tawntung ding hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Blessed be the

**TDB77:** Israel Pasian, Topa in khang tawntungin thupha ngah ta hen! Amen, Amen.

**ZVS:** Israel Pasian, { Topa } in khang tawntungin thupha ngah ta hen. Amen, Amen!
