## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Remember now thy Creator in the days of thy youth, while the evil days come not, nor the years draw nigh, when thou shalt say, I have no pleasure in them;

**TDB77:** Na lungkham hun ding hong tun’ ma, “Nuntakna nuamsa kei ing,” na cih ma hun, na khangno lai takin nang hong Piangsakpa phawk in.

**ZVS:** Na lungkham hun ding a hong tun' ma, “Nuntak nuamsa kei ing,” na cih ma hun, na khangno lai-in nang hong Piangsakpa phawk in.

### v2
**EN:** While the sun, or the light, or the moon, or the stars, be not darkened, nor the clouds return after the rain:

**TDB77:** Lungkham hun i cih in: Nang adingin nitaang, khataang, aksi taangte a dialh hun, meiipi kai a then’ nawn loh hun,

**ZVS:** Lungkham hun i cih in: Nang-a' dingin nitaang, khataang, aksi taangte a dialh hun, meiipi kai a then' nawnloh hun,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** In the day when the keepers of the house shall tremble, and the strong men shall bow themselves, and the grinders cease because they are few, and those that look out of the windows be darkened,

**TDB77:** na muan’ na suan’ a thahat na khut, na baan, na khete, a thanem hun, an hai nadingin na hatang tawm lua ta-in, na mitmangin khua na lim muh theih nawn loh hun,

**ZVS:** na muan' na suan' a thahat na khut, na baan, na khete, a thanem hun, an haina dingin na hatang tawmlua ta-in na mit maangin, limtakin khua na muh theih nawnloh hun,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** And the doors shall be shut in the streets, when the sound of the grinding is low, and he shall rise up at the voice of the bird, and all the daughters of musick shall be brought low;

**TDB77:** khuasung gamlumnate na bil in za thei nawn loin, ansukna gingte na zak theih phat nawn loh hun, la kisate za thei nawn loin, vasa ham nangawn in hong phawn’ zawh nawn loh hun,

**ZVS:** khuasung gamlumnate na bil in za thei nawnloin, ansukna gingte na zak theih phat nawnloh hun, la kisate za thei nawnloin vasa ham nangawn in hong phawn'zawh nawnloh hun,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Also when they shall be afraid of that which is high, and fears shall be in the way, and the almond tree shall flourish, and the grasshopper shall be a burden, and desire shall fail: because man goeth to his long home, and the mourners go about the streets:

**TDB77:** keen sangte-ah pai ngam nawn loin, lam pai ding a haksat hun, na samte hong kang-in, na khangno thahatna teng bei ta-in, na tha a kiam hun ding ahi hi. Eite in i tawntung inn ding a zuan ding i hi a, khuasung lampi-ah kahna awging kiza ding hi.

**ZVS:** keen sangte-ah pai ngam nawnloin, lam pai ding a haksat hun, na samte hong kang-in, na khangno thahatna beita-in na tha a kiam hun ding ahi hi. Eite in a tawntung inn ding a zuan ding ihi a, khuasung lampi-ah kahna awging kiza ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Or ever the silver cord be loosed, or the golden bowl be broken, or the pitcher be broken at the fountain, or the wheel broken at the cistern.

**TDB77:** I ngun meivak khaina khau kitatin, i kham meivak kia-in kitam ding a, i tuikhaina khau kitatin, i tuikhaina beel kitam ding hi.

**ZVS:** I ngun meivak khaina a khau kitatin, i kham meivak kia-in kitam ding a, i tuikhaina khau kitatin, i tuikhaina beel kitam ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Then shall the dust return to the earth as it was: and the spirit shall return unto God who gave it.

**TDB77:** I pumpi, lei suak kik ding a, i nuntakna a hong pia Pasian’ kiangah, i kha ciahkik ta ding hi.

**ZVS:** I pumpi, lei suakkik ding a, i nuntakna a hong pia Pasian' kiangah i kha ciahkik ta ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Vanity of vanities, saith the preacher; all is vanity.

**TDB77:** Thugen Siampa in, “A kiman’na a om lo, a mawknapi, na khempeuh a mawknapi ahi hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Thugen Siampa in, “A kiman'na a omlo, a mawknapi, na khempeuh a mawknapi ahi hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** And moreover, because the preacher was wise, he still taught the people knowledge; yea, he gave good heed, and sought out, and set in order many proverbs.

**TDB77:** Thugen Siampa, mipil ahih ban-ah ama theih teng, mite tungah hilh hi. Amah in paunakte, lim takin sin a, aman ahihna thu hoih takin entel hi.

**ZVS:** Thugen Siampa, mipil ahih banah, amah in ama theihteng mite tungah a hilh hi. Amah in paunakte limtakin sin a, a man ahihna thu hoihtakin entel hi.

### v10
**EN:** The preacher sought to find out acceptable words: and that which was written was upright, even words of truth.

**TDB77:** Thugen Siampa in a zaknophuai kammal khawm a, ama gelh kammalte, aman taktak ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Thugen Siampa in a zaknophuai kammal khawm a, ama gelh kammalte a man taktak ahi hi.

### v11
**EN:** The words of the wise are as goads, and as nails fastened by the masters of assemblies, which are given from one shepherd.

**TDB77:** Mipilte’ thugente in, tuucingte in tuu hawlna-in a zat ciangkhut hiam tawh kibang hi. Tua thute pen, eite khempeuh tuucin’ banga hong Cingpa Pasian’ piak ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mipilte' thugente pen tuucingte in tuu hawlna-in a zat ciangkhut hiam tawh kibang hi. Tua thute pen eite khempeuh tuucin' bangin a hong Cing Pasian' piak ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** And further, by these, my son, be admonished: of making many books there is no end; and much study is a weariness of the flesh.

**TDB77:** Ka ta aw, na phawk ding thudang khat om hi. Lai gelh-a a bu-a bawlna in bei hun nei thei lo a, lai tam sin luatna in pumpi gim-sak hi.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, na phawk ding thudang khat omlai hi. Lai gelh-in a bu-a bawlna in bei hun nei theilo a, lai tam sin luatna in pumpi gimsak hi.

### v13
**EN:** Let us hear the conclusion of the whole matter: Fear God, and keep his commandments: for this is the whole duty of man.

**TDB77:** Hih thute khempeuh banah, gen ding thu khat bek om hi. Mihing hong kipian’sakna, a thu bulpipen in: Pasian zahtakna, leh a thupiaknate man’na ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thute khempeuh banah gen ding thu khat bek om hi. Mihing hong kipian'sakna a thu bulpi pen in: Pasian zahtakna leh a thupiakte man'na ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** For God shall bring every work into judgment, with every secret thing, whether it be good, or whether it be evil.

**TDB77:** Apha hita leh asia hita leh, a simtham-a i hih simte nangawn, i hihna khempeuh ah, Pasian in thu hong khen ding hi.

**ZVS:** A pha hita leh a sia hita leh a simthamin i hih simte nangawn i hihna khempeuhah Pasian in thu hong khen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
