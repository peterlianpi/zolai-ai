## Verses

### v1
**EN:** God, who at sundry times and in divers manners spake in time past unto the fathers by the prophets,

**TDB77:** A beisa hunah Pasian in kamsangte zangin i pupite tungah a lam tuamtuam tawh a khen khenin thu na gen ngeizo hi.

### v2
**EN:** Hath in these last days spoken unto us by his Son, whom he hath appointed heir of all things, by whom also he made the worlds;

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin tuhun nunung ciangin leitung vantung na khempeuh a bawlin a zat, a tawpna-ah na khempeuh a luah dingin a seh a Tapa zangin eite tungah thu hong gen hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** Who being the brightness of his glory, and the express image of his person, and upholding all things by the word of his power, when he had by himself purged our sins, sat down on the right hand of the Majesty on high;

**TDB77:** Tua a Ta in Pasian’ vangliatna meel leh pianlim hi a, ama vangliatna tawh na khempeuh kipin, eite mawhnate amahmah in a sian’sak khit ciangin, a sang mun vangliatna ziat lamah tu-in a om hi.

**ZVS:** Tua Tapa in Pasian' minthan'na vang hong taangkhiasak a, Pasian' lim taktak mah hong pua-in, ama kampauna tawh leitung vantung buppi pai-in a kip hi. Mihingte a mawhna panun a sian'sak khit ciangin Vanglianpipa Pasian' taklam, vantungah a tu hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Being made so much better than the angels, as he hath by inheritance obtained a more excellent name than they.

**TDB77:** Vantungmite a ngah uh min sangin a phazaw min a ngah mah bangin vantungmite sangin a lianzaw mi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Vantung mite' ngah minthan'na sangin Tapa tungah Pasian in a piak min a liatzawk mah bangin vantung mite sangin Tapa lianzaw thampek hi.

### v5
**EN:** For unto which of the angels said he at any time, Thou art my Son, this day have I begotten thee? And again, I will be to him a Father, and he shall be to me a Son?

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh: “Nang in ka Tapa na hi hi, Tuni-in na Pa ka suak zo hi,” ci-in ahi zongin, “Keimah in ama Pa ka hi ding hi, Amah in zong ka Tapa ahi ding hi,” ci-in ahi zongin a kua vantungmi kiangah khatvei beek thupia ngei ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian in vantung mi khat peuhpeuh tungah, “Ka Tapa na hi hi. Tu ni-in na Pa ka suak hi,” ci ngeilo hi. Tua mah bangin, “Kei, ama Pa ka hi ding a, amah, ka Tapa ahi ding hi,” ci-in zong kam pau ngeilo hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** And again, when he bringeth in the firstbegotten into the world, he saith, And let all the angels of God worship him.

**TDB77:** Lam khat panin a Tapa upa leitungah a hong sawl ciangin: “Pasian’ vantungmi khempeuh in bokin Amah a bia uh hi,” ci-in a gen hi.

**ZVS:** Tua banah, Pasian in leitungah a Tapa neihsun hong paisak ding ciangin, “Pasian' vantung mi khempeuh in amah bia ding uh hi,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** And of the angels he saith, Who maketh his angels spirits, and his ministers a flame of fire.

**TDB77:** Lam khat panin: “Ama vantungmite in huih nung bang ahi zongin, A nasemte meikuang bang ahi zongin a piangsak hi,” ci-in vantungmite ngaihsunin a gen hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin vantung mite' thu a gen ciangin, “Pasian in a vantung mite huih bangin bawl a, a nasemte meikuang bangin bawl hi,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** But unto the Son he saith, Thy throne, O God, is for ever and ever: a sceptre of righteousness is the sceptre of thy kingdom.

**TDB77:** A Tapa thu ahih leh: “Pasian aw, na tokhom in A om tawntung tokhom ahi hi, Dikna ahi ukna Ciang in Na gam ukna ciang ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Tapa' thu a gen ciangin Pasian in, “Pasian aw, na kumpi tokhom khang tawntungin kip ding a, thuman thutang tawh na gamsung na uk ding hi.

### v9
**EN:** Thou hast loved righteousness, and hated iniquity; therefore God, even thy God, hath anointed thee with the oil of gladness above thy fellows.

**TDB77:** Nang in dikna thu na deih a, Gitlohna thu na mudah hi; Tua ahih ciangin nangma Pasian ahi Pasian in, Nangma lawmte khempeuh sangin Nang hong pahtawi zaw-in, Lungdamna sathau tawh hong nilh zo hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman na deih a, khialhna na mudah hi. Tua ahih manin nangma Pasian in nangmah hong teelin, na lawmte tungah a piak sangin a lianzaw, a lungdamhuai pahtawina nang hong pia hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** And, Thou, Lord, in the beginning hast laid the foundation of the earth; and the heavens are the works of thine hands:

**TDB77:** Khatvei: “Topa aw, nang in a kipat cilin Leitung bul na piangsak hi; Vantung zong na khut tawh Na bawl na ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua banah, “Topa ahi nangmah in a kipat cilin leitung na piangsak a, na khut tawh vantungte na bawl hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** They shall perish; but thou remainest; and they all shall wax old as doth a garment;

**TDB77:** Tuate a kisiat ding uh hangin, Nang na om tawntung ding hi; Tuate puan bangin a tul ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua nate khempeuh mangthang ding a, nang na hih leh na om paisuak ding hi. Tua nate puan bangin luisuak thei ding a,

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** And as a vesture shalt thou fold them up, and they shall be changed: but thou art the same, and thy years shall not fail.

**TDB77:** Amaute puan bangin na zial ding hi, Amaute puan bangin a kikheel ding uh hi. Nang na hih leh, na kikheel ngei kei ding hi, Na nuntakna a bei ngei kei ding hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** puan khep bangin na khep ding a, tua nate khempeuh kikheelkheel ding uh hi. Ahi zongin nang na kibang tawntung ding a, teek hun cih na nei ngei kei ding hi,” na ci lai hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** But to which of the angels said he at any time, Sit on my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool?

**TDB77:** Tua tham loin: “Na galte khempeuh na khe nuai-ah Ka koih matengin, Ka ziat lamah na tu in,” ci a, kua vantungmi khatvei beek thupia ngei ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Pasian in ama vantung mite kiangah, “Na galte na khenuai-ah ka hong sikcipsak matengin ka taklamah hong tu in,” ci ngeilo hi.

### v14
**EN:** Are they not all ministering spirits, sent forth to minister for them who shall be heirs of salvation?

**TDB77:** Vantungmite khempeuh in gupkhiatna luah ding mite kem dingun, sawltak nasem khate hilo ahi uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Vantung mite pen Pasian' na a sem, a kimu theilo khate hi uh a, hotkhiatna a ngahding mite a huh dingin Pasian in a hong sawl ahi hi.
