## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Dare any of you, having a matter against another, go to law before the unjust, and not before the saints?

**TDB77:** Khat leh khat thu na neih-te ciangin misiangthote khensak loin Lawkite a khensak ngam ding note lakah khat beek na om-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Note khatlekhat thu na kineih-te ciangin Pasian' mi siangthote khensakzaw loin lawki thukhente' mai na kitun' ngam-te hi.

### v2
**EN:** Do ye not know that the saints shall judge the world? and if the world shall be judged by you, are ye unworthy to judge the smallest matters?

**TDB77:** Misiangthote in leitung mite thu a khen ding-te thei lo na hi-te hiam? Note in leitung mite thukhen takpi ding na hih-te leh, thuneute a khen dingin kilawm lo na hi-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian' mite in leitung mite' thu a khensakzaw ding ahihlam theilo na hi-te hiam? Note pen leitung mite' thu a khensak ding na hih-te ciangin tua bang thu neute khen theilo lawmlawm na hi-te hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Know ye not that we shall judge angels? how much more things that pertain to this life?

**TDB77:** Eite in vantungmite thu i khen ding thei lo na hi-te hiam? Tua ahih ciangin leitung thu a khen dingin kilawm zaw hilo na hi-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Eite pen vantung mite' thu nangawn a khensak ding ihih lamtak theilo na hi-te hiam? Tua ahih manin hih leitunga no' thu neuneute leltak na khentheih zawk kan ding-te ahi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin leitung thu na khen ding-te leh, pawlpi sungah mi ginalote thukhen ding za-ah bang hangin na koih-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Tua bang hinapi-in hih bang thu themthem hong pian' ciangin pawlpi sungah bangmahin a kisimlo mite' mai kitun'ding na hi-te hiam?

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | plural: uh marker

### v5

**TDB77:** Tua bang ka cih inah, note zum nadingin kong ci hi. A ute a naute thu khensak thei ding note lakah lungpil nei mi khat beek om lo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Note-a' dingin zumhuai mahmah hi. Note khatlekhat na kithu-neihnate-te a khenthei ding note lakah mipil khat beek om buang ding hi.

### v6

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7

**TDB77:** Tua bangin note in khat leh khat na kitot-te ciangin na guallelhna-te a kilang mahmah hi. Thuum loin bawlsiatna bang hangin thuak zaw lo na hi-te hiam? Bang hangin sum zatna thuak zaw lo na hi-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Note khatlekhat zum na kitut mahmah-te zong a gina hetlo na hihna-te hong kilangsak hi. Bang hangin thuakzaw mailo a, sum ngamzaw mailo na hi-te hiam?

### v8

### v9

**TDB77:** Mi diklote in Pasian’ Gam a luah theih loh ding thu thei lo na hi-te hiam? Na kikheem kei un: Numei tawh a mawhte, milim a biate, mi’ zi tawh a mawhte, pasal leh pasal a khialte, tua thute ah thuneite;

**ZVS:** Migilote, Pasian' Ukna sungah a lut hetlohna ding thu na thei-te hi. Note kihaibawl kei un. Numei tawh a paktatte, milim biate, mi zi ahih kei leh mi pasal tawh a mawhte, pasal leh pasal a khialte,

**ZVS:** Note pawlkhat, tua bangin na gamta-te hi. Ahi zongin note pen i Topa Jesuh Khrih leh i Pasian' Kha Siangtho hangin mawhna panin a hong kisiangsak khinsa, Pasian' tungah a kipiasa, Pasian tawh a kipawlsa mite na hi-te hi.

**TDB77:** Keimah in na khempeuh ka bawl theih hangin na khempeuh hih ding a kilawm kei hi. Na khempeuh ka hih theih hangin tuate khat’ sila-in ka kai kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** “An pen i gilpi-a' ding hi a, gilpi zong an-a' ding mah hilo ahi hiam?” a ci zong omkha ding hi. Ahi zongin Pasian in tuate gel a beisak hun hong tung ding hi. Pumpi pen paktatna-in zatding hilo a, Topa' na sepna-in zatding hizaw hi. Topa in pumpi-a' ding na hong sem hi.
