## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Teman khuami Elifaz in thu gen a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Teman khuami Elifaz in thu hong gen a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** If we assay to commune with thee, wilt thou be grieved? but who can withhold himself from speaking?

**TDB77:** “Mi khatpeuh in thu hong genpih leh na heh ding hiam? Kua in a kam ip zo nawn ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Mi khatpeuh in thu hong genpih leh na heh ding hiam? Kua in a kam ipzo nawn ding ahi hiam?

### v3
**EN:** Behold, thou hast instructed many, and thou hast strengthened the weak hands.

**TDB77:** Lunghiang mite na hanthotna, khut kha, ban khate na khauhsakna,

**ZVS:** Lunghiang mite na hanthotna, khut kha ban kha mite na thakhauhsakna,

### v4
**EN:** Thy words have upholden him that was falling, and thou hast strengthened the feeble knees.

**TDB77:** a puk mite na thugenna tawh na kipsakna, khuk zaw mite na hatsak ngeite ngaihsun in.

**ZVS:** a puk mite na thugenna tawh na kipsakna, khukzaw mite na hatsak ngeite ngaihsun in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** But now it is come upon thee, and thou faintest; it toucheth thee, and thou art troubled.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin nang tungah tuate hong tung a, na thuak zo kei hi; tuate in nang hong su a, na lung niam hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin nang tungah tuate hong tung a, na thuakzo kei hi. Tuate in nang hong su a, na lungniam hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v6
**EN:** Is not this thy fear, thy confidence, thy hope, and the uprightness of thy ways?

**TDB77:** Na biakna in hong hehnem zo lo ahi hiam? A paubaang lo, na nuntakna in lametna hong pia zo lo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Na biakna in hong hehnem zolo ahi hiam? A paubaanglo, na nuntakna in lametna hong pia zolo ahi hiam?

### v7
**EN:** Remember, I pray thee, who ever perished, being innocent? or where were the righteous cut off?

**TDB77:** Tu-in ngaihsun in, midik kimlai kua sia ngei ahi hiam? A thutang mi a kisuksiatna koi-ah na mu ngei hiam?

**ZVS:** Tu-in ngaihsun in: Midik kimlai kua sia ngei ahi hiam? A thutang mi a kisuksiatna koi-ah mu ngei na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Even as I have seen, they that plow iniquity, and sow wickedness, reap the same.

**TDB77:** Mawhna bawlin buaina ahih mi in a gamtatna bangin a gah a atna ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Mawhna bawlin buaina a piangsak mi in a gamtatna bangin a gah a atna ka thei hi.

### v9
**EN:** By the blast of God they perish, and by the breath of his nostrils are they consumed.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ husan’na tawh tua bang mite sia a, ama hehna in tua bang mite kangtum hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' husan'na tawh tua bang mite sia a, ama hehna in tua bang mite kangtum hi.

### v10
**EN:** The roaring of the lion, and the voice of the fierce lion, and the teeth of the young lions, are broken.

**TDB77:** Humpinelkai bangin migilote hawkna dai ta a, Pasian in amau hate suktan sakin gamdai ta-te hi.

**ZVS:** Humpinelkai hawk bangin migilote' hawkna dai ta a, Pasian in amaute' ha suktansak khin ahih manin gamdai ta-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** The old lion perisheth for lack of prey, and the stout lion's whelps are scattered abroad.

**TDB77:** An nek ding mu loin a thahat humpinelkai bangin si-te a, a tate-te kithehthang hi.

**ZVS:** Annek ding a mulo a thahat humpinelkai bangin amaute simang khin-te a, a tate-te kithehthang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12

**TDB77:** Tu-in kei kiangah thu khat hong tung khian a, ka bil in tua thu ginghiau a za hi.

**ZVS:** “Tu-in kei kiangah thu khat hong tung khian a, ka bil in tua thu ginghiau a za hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** In thoughts from the visions of the night, when deep sleep falleth on men,

**TDB77:** Mi a ihsip hun zankim laitak, zan mangmat hun lai takin,

**ZVS:** Mi a ihsip hun zankim laitak, zan mangmat hun laitakin

### v14
**EN:** Fear came upon me, and trembling, which made all my bones to shake.

**TDB77:** launa leh liin’na kei tungah hong tung a, ka pumpi liin’na in kei hong lausak hi.

**ZVS:** launa leh lin'na kei tungah hong tung a, ka pumpi lin'na in kei hong lausak hi.

### v15

**TDB77:** Huih in ka mai hong mut a, ka khuasikmul hong suaksak hi.

### v16
**EN:** It stood still, but I could not discern the form thereof: an image was before mine eyes, there was silence, and I heard a voice, saying,

**TDB77:** A meel ka khen theih loh lim khat hong ding a, ka mai-ah mihing lim khat hong om a, tua ciangin aw-uun duadua khat ka za a,

**ZVS:** A meel ka khen theihloh lim khat hong ding a, keima mai-ah mihing lim khat hong om a, tua ciangin aw un duadua khat ka za a,

### v17
**EN:** Shall mortal man be more just than God? shall a man be more pure than his maker?

**TDB77:** ‘Pasian mai-ah mi khatpeuh paubaang loin om zo ngei ding ahi hiam? Amah a Bawlpa’ mai-ah sitbaang loin om zo ngei ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** ‘Pasian' mai-ah mi khatpeuh paubaang loin omzo ngei ding ahi hiam? Amah a Bawlpa' mai-ah sitbaang loin omzo ngei ding ahi hiam?

### v18
**EN:** Behold, he put no trust in his servants; and his angels he charged with folly:

**TDB77:** Pasian in ama nasemte mahmah muang zo loin, a sawltakte a mawhna mu ahih leh,

**ZVS:** Pasian in ama nasemte mahmah muangzo loin, ama sawltakte' mawhna mu ahih leh,

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v19
**EN:** How much less in them that dwell in houses of clay, whose foundation is in the dust, which are crushed before the moth?

**TDB77:** inn umna kawm dingin buan-a kizatna inn sunga teeng mite, a inn bulpi leivui ahi, vasa bu bangin a kimeekcip thei,

**ZVS:** inn umna kawmin buan a kizatna inn sunga a teng mite, a inn bulpi leivui ahi,

### v20
**EN:** They are destroyed from morning to evening: they perish for ever without any regarding it.

**TDB77:** zing kal nitak kal-a a kisusia thei sungah a omte, bangzah ta-in kuama don lohin sia cipin,

**ZVS:** zing kal nitak kalin, a kisusia thei sungah a omte, bangzah ta-in kuama don lohin si pakin,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v21
