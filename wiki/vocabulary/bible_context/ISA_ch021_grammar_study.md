### v1

**TDB77:** Babilon gam tawh kisai thu: Pingpeite leitaw lam panin hong kipat mah bangin, a lauhuai gam panin siatna hong pai hi.

### v2
**EN:** A grievous vision is declared unto me; the treacherous dealer dealeth treacherously, and the spoiler spoileth. Go up, O Elam: besiege, O Media; all the sighing thereof have I made to cease.

**TDB77:** A kihtak huai mangmuhna khat kei tungah hong kigen hi; A lehheekpa in lehheekin a sut thei mipa in sut hi, a susia thei mipa in susia hi. Elam galkapte aw, kuan to un, Media galkapte aw, umcih un; Babilon’ pian’sak ahi, khuisatnate khempeuh Pasian in beisak ta hi.

**ZVS:** A kihtakhuai maangmuhna khat kei tungah hong kigen hi. A sutzo mipa in sut a, a susiazo mipa in susia hi. Elam galkapte aw, kuanto un. Media galkapte aw, umcih un. Babylon' pian'sak khuisatnate khempeuh Pasian in beisak ta hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ka pumpi na hi; nausuak numei in na a sak bangin na ka sa hi; khuaza thei lo zah dongin a kuncip-in ka om a, ka muh nate hangin lamdang sa in ka om hi. khua mu thei lo zah dongin lungkia-in ka om hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin ka kawng na a, nausuak numei in na a sak bangin na ka sa hi. Khua a za theilo zah dongin a kuncipin ka om a, khua a mu theilo zah dongin lungkia-in ka om hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)
