## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un! Vantung panin Topa phat un la, a sangna panin amah phat un!

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. Vantung panin { Topa } phat un la, a sangna panin amah phat un.

### v2
**EN:** Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts.

**TDB77:** Ama vantungmi khempeuh aw, amah phat un, ama mihonpite khempeuh aw, amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Ama vantung mi khempeuh aw, amah phat un. Ama mihonte khempeuh aw, amah phat un.

### v3
**EN:** Praise ye him, sun and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light.

**TDB77:** Ni leh kha aw, amah phat un, a taang aksi khempeuh aw, amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Ni leh kha aw, amah phat un. A taang aksi khempeuh aw, amah phat un.

### v4
**EN:** Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that be above the heavens.

**TDB77:** Van sangpente leh vante tunga om tuite aw, amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Van sangpente leh vantunga om tuite aw, amah phat un.

### v5
**EN:** Let them praise the name of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ min amaute phatsak un! Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in thu pia a, amaute a piang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } min amaute phatsak un. Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in thu pia a, amaute a piang hi.

### v6
**EN:** He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass.

**TDB77:** Amaute a tawntung a om dingin amah in kipsak hi; a palsat loh ding uh thupiakna amah in a bawl hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute a tawntunga om dingin amah in kipsak a, a palsatloh ding uh thupiakna amah in a bawl hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** Fire, and hail; snow, and vapour; stormy wind fulfilling his word:

**TDB77:** mei leh gial, tuikhal leh vuuk, ama thupiakna a mang huihpi!

**ZVS:** mei leh gial, tuikhal leh vuk, ama thupiakna a mang huihpi!

### v9
**EN:** Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars:

**TDB77:** Mualliante leh mualneute khempeuh, gah a nei singkungte leh taamtaak singkung khempeuh!

**ZVS:** Amah phat un, no mual liante leh mual neute khempeuh, gah a nei singkungte leh taamtak singkung khempeuh!

### v10
**EN:** Beasts, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl:

**TDB77:** Gamsate leh innsate khempeuh, a bokvak ganhingte leh a leeng vasate!

**ZVS:** Gamsate leh innsate khempeuh, a bokvak ganhingte leh a leeng vasate khempeuh!

### v11
**EN:** Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth:

**TDB77:** Leitung kumpite leh mihing khempeuhte, leitung uliante leh thukhente khempeuh!

**ZVS:** No leitung kumpite leh mihing khempeuh, leitung uliante leh thukhente khempeuh!

### v12
**EN:** Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children:

**TDB77:** Nungak leh tangvalte, teeksiate leh naupangte!

**ZVS:** Nungakte leh tangvalte, teeksiate leh naupangte aw, amah phat un.

### v13
**EN:** Let them praise the name of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ min phatsak un, bang hang hiam cih leh ama min bek lian a, ama minthan’na in leitung leh vantung sangin sangzaw lai hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } min phat un. Bang hang hiam cih leh amah bek a minlian hi a, ama minthan'na pen leitung leh vantung sangin sangzaw lai hi.

**TDB77:** Amah in ama mite thahat sak a, ama misiangthote khempeuh, ama mai-a om Israelte khempeuh in amah phat uh hi. Topa phat un!

**ZVS:** Amah in ama mite thahatsak a, ama mi siangthote khempeuh, ama mai-ah a om Israel-te khempeuh in amah phat uh hi. { Topa } phat un.
