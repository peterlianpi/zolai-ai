## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The fool hath said in his heart, There is no God. Corrupt are they, and have done abominable iniquity: there is none that doeth good.

**TDB77:** “Mihai mi in a lungsim sungah, Pasian om lo hi,” ci-in ngaihsun hi. Amaute sia uh a, a kihhuai gamta uh hi; a hoih-a gamta kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Mihai in a lungsim sungah, “Pasian omlo hi,” ci-in ngaihsun hi. Amaute sia uh hi. A kihhuai-in gamta uh a, a hoihin a gamta kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** God looked down from heaven upon the children of men, to see if there were any that did understand, that did seek God.

**TDB77:** A pil, Pasian a zong mi a om uh hiam cih a theih nadingin Pasian in vantung panin mihing’ tate en suk hi.

**ZVS:** A pil, Pasian a zong mi a om uh hiam, cih a theihna dingin Pasian in vantung panin mihingte hong ensuk hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** Every one of them is gone back: they are altogether become filthy; there is none that doeth good, no, not one.

**TDB77:** Amaute a vekpi-un puk khin uh a, a kibangin kisia kim uh hi; a hoih gamtat khat beek om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute khempeuh a vekpi-un pukkhin uh hi. A kibangin kisia kim uh a, a hoihin a gamta khat beek omlo hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Have the workers of iniquity no knowledge? who eat up my people as they eat bread: they have not called upon God.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ tungah thu a ngen lo, an nek bang-a ka mite a netum, siatna a bawlte in thu teltheihna nei lo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian tungah thu a ngenlo, an nek bangin ka mite a netum, siatna a bawlte in thu theihtelna neilo ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** There were they in great fear, where no fear was: for God hath scattered the bones of him that encampeth against thee: thou hast put them to shame, because God hath despised them.

**TDB77:** Cik mah-a tua bang launa om ngei lo, launa lianpi sungah amaute om uh hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian in migilote’ guhte thehthang ding hi; Pasian in amaute nial ahih manin, amaute maizumin kikoih ding hi.

**ZVS:** Cikmahin tua bang launa a om ngeilo, launa lianpi sungah amaute om uh hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian in migilote' guhte thehthang ding hi. Pasian in amaute nial ahih manin, amaute maizumin kikoih ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Oh that the salvation of Israel were come out of Zion! When God bringeth back the captivity of his people, Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.

**TDB77:** Zion panin Israelte’ adingin hotkhiatna hong tung ta leh maw! Pasian in ama mite’ nopsakna a piak kik ciangin, Jakob nuamin Israel kipak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Zion panin Israel-te-a' ding hotkhiatna hong tun'na ding thu ka ngenngen hi. Pasian in ama mite tungah nopsakna a piakkik ciangin, ama mi Israel-te kipak mahmah ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
