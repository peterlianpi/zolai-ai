## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Woe to them that are at ease in Zion, and trust in the mountain of Samaria, which are named chief of the nations, to whom the house of Israel came!

**TDB77:** Israel minamte in muan-a hong neih, minam laka ulian ahi, Zion-ah nuntak nuama a om hithiat, Samaria-ah lungmuang taka a om note’ adingin, zumhuai mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** Israel minamte in muan'in a hong neih, i minam lakah ulian ahi, Zion-ah nuamtakin a om hithiat, Samaria-ah lungmuang takin a om note-a' dingin zumhuai mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | purpose: in order to

### v2
**EN:** Pass ye unto Calneh, and see; and from thence go ye to Hamath the great: then go down to Gath of the Philistines: be they better than these kingdoms? or their border greater than your border?

**TDB77:** Kalneh khua-ah pai un la va en un. Tua ciangin Hamath khuapi lian ah paisuak un la, Filistiate’ khuapi Gath dong va paisuk un. Judah leh Israel kumpi gamte sangin hoih zaw a, amau’ gam in note’ gam sangin lianzaw ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Kalneh khua-ah pai un la limtakin va en un. Tua ciangin Hamath khuapi lian-ah va paisuak un la, Filistia-te' khuapi Gath khua dong va paisuk un. Judah leh Israel kumpi gamte sangin hoihzaw tuan a, amaute' gam pen note' gam sangin lianzaw tuan ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore
