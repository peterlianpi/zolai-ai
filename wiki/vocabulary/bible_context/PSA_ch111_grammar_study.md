## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un, lungsim thutangte a omna, mihon kikhopna sungah ka lungsim khempeuh tawh Topa tungah lungdamna ka ko ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. Lungsim thutangte a omna, mihon kikhopna sungah ka lungsim khempeuh tawh { Topa' } tungah lungdamna ka ko ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** The works of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ nasepnate lian a, hoih a sa mi khempeuh in tuate kankan-te hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } nasepna lian a, hoih a sa mi khempeuh in tuate kankan-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** His work is honourable and glorious: and his righteousness endureth for ever.

**TDB77:** Ama nasepna in pahtawina leh minliatna tawh kidim a, ama thumanna in kip tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Ama nasepnate, pahtawina leh minliatna tawh kidim a, ama thumanna kip tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered: the

**TDB77:** Amah in phawk tawntung dingin a lamdang ama sepnate hong bawl a, Topa dikin hehpihna a nei hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in phawk tawntung dingin a lamdang nasepnate hong bawl a, { Topa } dikin hehpihna nei hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** He hath given meat unto them that fear him: he will ever be mindful of his covenant.

**TDB77:** Amah in amah zahtakte an pia a, ama thuciamna hong phawk tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in amah a zahtakte an pia a, a thuciamna a phawk tawntung hi.

### v6
**EN:** He hath shewed his people the power of his works, that he may give them the heritage of the heathen.

**TDB77:** Amah in ama mite tungah minamte’ gamh a piakna tawh ama vangliatna hong lak khin hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in ama mite tungah minamte' gamh a piakna tawh ama vangliatna hong lak khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v7
**EN:** The works of his hands are verity and judgment; all his commandments are sure.

**TDB77:** Ama nasepnate khempeuh ah amah thumanin citak a, ama thupiaknate muan’taak mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** Ama nasepnate khempeuhah amah thumanin citak a, a thupiaknate muan'taak mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v8
**EN:** They stand fast for ever and ever, and are done in truth and uprightness.

**TDB77:** A tawntungin tuate kip ngitnget a, thuman leh thutang tawh zuih ding ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** He sent redemption unto his people: he hath commanded his covenant for ever: holy and reverend is his name.

**TDB77:** Amah in a mite hong honkhia a, amah in a tawntunga dingin thupiakna a bawl khin hi. Ama min, siangtho-in kihtak-huai mahmah hi!

**ZVS:** Amah in ama mite honkhia a, a tawntunga kimang dingin thupiakna a bawl khin hi. Ama min siangtho-in vanglian mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** The fear of the

**TDB77:** Topa zahtakna in pilna kipatna ahi hi; ama thupiak a zuite khempeuh tungah thukhen siamna a pia hi. Amah phat tawntung ding ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } zahtakna pen pilna kipatna ahi hi. Ama thupiak a zuite khempeuh tungah thukhen siamna a pia hi. Amah phat tawntung ding ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
