### v1

**TDB77:** A manpha paknamtui sangin minpha hoihzaw a, i suah ni sangin i sih ni hoihzaw hi.

### v2
**EN:** It is better to go to the house of mourning, than to go to the house of feasting: for that is the end of all men; and the living will lay it to his heart.

**TDB77:** Pawi simna sangin si galna hoihzaw hi; sih ding bek a ngak ahihna mihing khempeuh in a phawk ding kilawm hi.

**ZVS:** Pawisimna sangin sigalna hoihzaw hi. Sih ding bek a ngak ahihna mihing khempeuh in a phawk ding-te kilawm hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Sorrow is better than laughter: for by the sadness of the countenance the heart is made better.

**TDB77:** Nuihna sangin dahna hoihzaw hi; na mai hong gumsak zah in na thu theihna hong khangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Nuihna sangin dahna hoihzaw hi; na mai hong gumsak zahin na thu theihna hong khangsak hi.

### v4
**EN:** The heart of the wise is in the house of mourning; but the heart of fools is in the house of mirth.

**TDB77:** Mipilte, dahna lakah om tual a, mihaite, nopna lakah om tual hi.

**ZVS:** Mipilte dahna lakah omtual a, mihaite nopna lakah omtual hi.

### v5

**TDB77:** Mihaite in pahtawina la hong sak sangin, mipilte’ hong taina hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite in pahtawina la hong sak sangin mipilte' hong taina hoihzaw hi.

### v6

**TDB77:** Mihaite’ nuihna in meikuang sunga selkeu kikhul a puak zaizai tawh kibang a, khiatna nei lo, a mawknapi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite' nuihna pen meikuang sunga selkeu kikhul a puak zaizai tawh kibang a, khiatna neilo, a mawknapi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7

**ZVS:** Mipil khat in midangte a khem ciangin mihai bangin a gamta ahi hi. Sumgolh lakna in a lapa' gamtat hoihna siasak hi.

### v8

**ZVS:** Thu khatpeuh a kipatna sangin a tawpna hoihzaw a, kiphatsakna sangin lungduaina hoihzaw hi.

### v14

**ZVS:** Nopna na ngah laitakin lungdam in; haksatna na tuah ciangin hih thu ngaihsun in: Lungdamna leh lungkhamna hong piapa pen Pasian hi a, mailamah bang piang ding cih kuamah in theilo hi.

**ZVS:** Ka nuntakna a mawknapi hi a, ahi zongin ka nuntak sungin thu khempeuh ka thei hi. Mi khat hoih napi-in si a, mi khat siahuai napi-in a khan' sau hi.
