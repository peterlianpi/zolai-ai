## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My breath is corrupt, my days are extinct, the graves are ready for me.

**TDB77:** Ka bei ding naikuan ta hi. Ka hu ka sang zo nawn kei a, kei adingin hankhuk cih loh bangmah dang om nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka bei ding hun nai kuan ta a, hu ka sangzo nawn kei hi. Kei-a' dingin hankhuk cihloh bangmah dang om nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo

### v2
**EN:** Are there not mockers with me? and doth not mine eye continue in their provocation?

**TDB77:** Ka heina khempeuh ah mite in hong zahko a, amau’ hong phinna in hong lungzingsak hi.

**ZVS:** Ka heina khempeuhah mite in hong zahko-te a, amau' hong phinna in hong lungzingsak hi.

### v3
**EN:** Lay down now, put me in a surety with thee; who is he that will strike hands with me?

**TDB77:** “Pasian aw, kei hong hu in. Ka thugente hong pan’pih ding kuamah dang om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, kei hong hu in. Ka thugente hong pan'pih ding kuamah dang omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v4
**EN:** For thou hast hid their heart from understanding: therefore shalt thou not exalt them.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in amaute thuman a mu lo dingin bawl na hih manin amaute na gualzosak kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in amaute thuman a mulo dingin bawl na hih manin amaute a gualzo-in na bawl kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | negation: kei | causal: because

### v5
**EN:** He that speaketh flattery to his friends, even the eyes of his children shall fail.

**TDB77:** Paunak in, sum ngah nop manin mi khat in a lawmte a lehhek leh, a tate in tua gitlohna thaman thuak hi, kici hi.

**ZVS:** Paunak-ah, sum ngahnop manin mi khat in a lawmte a heek leh, ama tate in tua gitlohna thaman thuak hi, kici hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** He hath made me also a byword of the people; and aforetime I was as a tabret.

**TDB77:** “Tu-in amaute in kei tungah hih paunak hong bulh-te a, mite in hih thu za-in kei hong cilpih-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in amaute in kei tungah hih paunak hong bulh-te a, mite in hih thu za-in kei hong cilphih-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Mine eye also is dim by reason of sorrow, and all my members are as a shadow.

**TDB77:** Ka dahna hangin ka mit mial a, ka khut ka khete ciangkang bang hi.

**ZVS:** Ka dahna hangin ka mit mial a, ka khut ka khete ciangkang bang ta hi.

### v8
**EN:** Upright men shall be astonished at this, and the innocent shall stir up himself against the hypocrite.

**TDB77:** Mi hoih-a kineite in lamdang hong sa-te a, migilo-in kei hong seh-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mihoihin a kineite in lamdang hong sa-te a, migilo-in kei hong seh-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** The righteous also shall hold on his way, and he that hath clean hands shall be stronger and stronger.

**TDB77:** Mite’ zahtak-a kinei mite in, amau’ ngaihsutna man sa semsem-te hi. Thuman sem mite in thahat semsem ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mite' zahtaka a kinei mite in amau' ngaihsutna-te man mahmah sa semsem-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** But as for you all, do ye return, and come now: for I cannot find one wise man among you.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin amaute khempeuh hong pai-in ka mai-ah hong ding le uh, amaute lakah mipil khat zong ka mu kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin amaute khempeuh hong pai-in ka mai-ah hong ding le-te amaute' lakah mipil khat zong ka mu kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei

### v11
**EN:** My days are past, my purposes are broken off, even the thoughts of my heart.

**TDB77:** Ka hun bei ta hi; ka geelna teng maimang a, ka lametna teng bei ta hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka hun beita hi. Ka geelna teng maimang a, ka lametna teng beita hi.

### v12
**EN:** They change the night into day: the light is short because of darkness.

**TDB77:** Ka lawmte in, zan khuamial pen sun khuavak, ci-te hi; khuavak hong nai ta hi, ci-te a, ahi zongin khuamial sung mah ah kei ka om lai veve hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lawmte in, khuamial kimlai, sun khuavak, ci-te hi. Khuavak hong nai ta hi, ci-in gen-te a, ahi zongin khuamial sung mahah kei ka omlai veve hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** If I wait, the grave is mine house: I have made my bed in the darkness.

**TDB77:** Kei’ lamet nading misite’ gam bek om lai a, tua khuamial sungah lumin ka ihmu ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei' lamet ding misite' gam bek omlai a, tua khuamial sungah lumin ka ihmu ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v14
**EN:** I have said to corruption, Thou art my father: to the worm, Thou art my mother, and my sister.

**TDB77:** Hankhuk pen ka pa hi, ka ci ding a, kei hong ne thante pen ka nu leh sanggam numeite, ka ci ding hi.

**ZVS:** Hankhuk sung pen ka pa hi, ka ci ding a, kei hong ne thante pen ka nu leh ka sanggam numeite, ka ci ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v15
**EN:** And where is now my hope? as for my hope, who shall see it?

**TDB77:** Kei’ lamet ding tua lai-ah bang om ahi hiam? Ka lamet ding tua lai-ah kua in nong muhsak-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Kei' lamet ding tua lai-ah bang om ahi hiam? Tua lai-ah ka lamet ding bang nong muhsak-te hiam?

### v16
**EN:** They shall go down to the bars of the pit, when our rest together is in the dust.

**TDB77:** Misite’ gam Leinuai-ah ka pai ciangin nopsakna in kei hong zui lo ding hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Misite' gam Leinuai-ah ka pai ciangin nopsakna in kei hong zuilo ding hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will
