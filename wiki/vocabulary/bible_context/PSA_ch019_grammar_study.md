# Psalms Chapter 19 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 14 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 6 |
| negation: lo | 2 |
| purpose: in order to | 1 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 1 |
| sequence: then/therefore | 1 |
| future: will | 1 |
| negation: kei | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork.

**TDB77:** Vantung in Pasian’ minthan’na gen a, vanpi in ama nasepna taangko hi.

**ZVS:** Vantung in Pasian' minthan'na gen a, vanpi in ama nasepna taangko hi.

### v2
**EN:** Day unto day uttereth speech, and night unto night sheweth knowledge.

**TDB77:** Ni simin thu gengen a, zan simin theihtelna pulaklak hi.

**ZVS:** Nisimin thu gengen a, zan simin theihtelna pulaklak hi.

### v3
**EN:** There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard.

**TDB77:** Pauna leh kammal om lo a, amau aw kiza lo hi;

**ZVS:** Pauna leh kammal omlo a, amau' aw kizalo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Their line is gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world. In them hath he set a tabernacle for the sun,

**TDB77:** ahih hangin a aw uh leitung khempeuh ah kizel a, a kammal uh leimong dongah tung hi. Ama sungah ni’ adingin omna khat bawl khin a,

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin a aw uh leitung khempeuhah kizel a, a kammal uh leimong dongah a tung hi. Amau' sungah ni-a' ding omna khat bawl khin a,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber, and rejoiceth as a strong man to run a race.

**TDB77:** ama inn sung pan-a hong pusuak zi pi tangval tawh ahi zongin, lungtaii tak-a tai dinga kipan mi thahat khat tawh ahi zongin kibang hi.

**ZVS:** a innsung pan a hong pusuak zi pi tangval tawh ahi zongin, lungtaii takin tai ding a kipan mi thahat khat tawh ahi zongin a kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** His going forth is from the end of the heaven, and his circuit unto the ends of it: and there is nothing hid from the heat thereof.

**TDB77:** Ama suahna vanmong khat pan hi a, mong khat dongin pai a, nisa bangin ama satna a liah bangmah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ama suahna van mong pan hi a, mong khat dongin pai a, ama satna panin a kiliah thei bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v7
**EN:** The law of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ thu kicing a, nuntakna thaksuak sak hi; Topa’ teci pan’na pen hi takpi a, mipil lote pil sak hi;

**ZVS:** { Topa' } thu kicing a, nuntakna a thaksuaksak hi. { Topa' } teci pan'na hi takpi a, mi pillote pilsak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** The statutes of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ thuhilhte man a, lungsim nuam sak hi; Topa’ thupiakna siang a, mitte vaaksak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } thuhilhte man a, lungsim nuamsak hi. { Topa' } thupiakna siang a, mitte vaksak hi.

### v9
**EN:** The fear of the

**TDB77:** Topa a kizahtakna in siang a, kip tawntung hi; Topa’ ngeina kipte thuman a, a vekpi-in kituak takin man kim hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } a kizahtakna siang a, kip tawntung hi. { Topa' } ngeina kipte thuman a, a vekin kituakin man kim hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** More to be desired are they than gold, yea, than much fine gold: sweeter also than honey and the honeycomb.

**TDB77:** Kham sangin ahi zongin, kham siangte nangawn sangin ahi zongin tuate deihhuai zaw a, khuaizu leh khuailuzu tak sangin zong khumzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Kham sangin ahi zongin kham hoihpen sang ahi zongin tuate deihhuaizaw a, khuaizu leh khuailuzu taakte sangin zong khumzaw lai hi.

### v11
**EN:** Moreover by them is thy servant warned: and in keeping of them there is great reward.

**TDB77:** Tham loin tuate hangin na nasempa in thu phawkna ngah a, tuate zuihna-ah noptuam lian om hi.

**ZVS:** Tua thamloin tuate' hangin a thanempa in thuphawkna ngah a, tuate zuihna-ah noptuamna lian a om hi.

### v12
**EN:** Who can understand his errors? cleanse thou me from secret faults.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin kua in ama khialhnate kimukhia thei hiam? Theih loh man-a khialnate panin hong siangsak in.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin kua in ama khialhnate mukhia theitawm ahi hiam? Theihloh manin khialhna panin kei hong siangsak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins; let them not have dominion over me: then shall I be upright, and I shall be innocent from the great transgression.

**TDB77:** Mawh kaltangnate panin zong na nasempa hong kem in la, tuate keima tungah hong uk sak kei in! Tua ciangin kawk ka baang kei ding a, mawhna lian panin ka siang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mawh kaltangnate panin zong na nasempa hong kem in la, tuate keima tungah hong uksak kei in! Tua ciangin kei ka kawkbaang kei ding a, mawhna liante panin ka siang ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | negation: kei

### v14
**EN:** Let the words of my mouth, and the meditation of my heart, be acceptable in thy sight, O

**TDB77:** Topa, ka suangpi leh keimah hong tankhiapa aw, ka kammal leh ka lungsim ngaihsutnate nangma muhna-ah san’huai suak sak ta in!

**ZVS:** { Topa, } ka belh suangpi leh keimah hong tankhiapa aw, ka kammal leh ka lungsim ngaihsutnate nangma muhna-ah a san'huai suaksak ta in!
