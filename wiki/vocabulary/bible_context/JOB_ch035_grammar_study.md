## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Elihu spake moreover, and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Elihu mah in,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** Thinkest thou this to be right, that thou saidst, My righteousness is more than God's?

**TDB77:** “Job aw, Pasian’ muhna-ah ka paubaang kei hi, na cih ding kilawm lo hi.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, Pasian' muhna-ah ka paubaang kei hi, na cih ding kilawmlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For thou saidst, What advantage will it be unto thee? and, What profit shall I have, if I be cleansed from my sin?

**TDB77:** Pasian kiangah, ka khialh hangin nang bang phamawh sa na hi hiam? Khialhna ka bawl loh manin bangmah phattuamna ka ngah kei hi, na cih ding kilawm lo hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian kiangah, ka khialh hangin bang phamawh sa na hi hiam? Khialhna ka gamtatloh manin bangmah phattuamna ka ngah kei hi, na cih ding kilawmlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | negation: lo | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** I will answer thee, and thy companions with thee.

**TDB77:** Nang leh na lawmte tungah, thu kong gen ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nang leh na lawmte tungah thu ka hong gen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v5
**EN:** Look unto the heavens, and see; and behold the clouds which are higher than thou.

**TDB77:** Vantung en to dih in, Meiite bangzah takin sang ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Vantung ento dih un. Meiite bangzah takin sang omto ahi hiam?

### v6
**EN:** If thou sinnest, what doest thou against him? or if thy transgressions be multiplied, what doest thou unto him?

**TDB77:** Mawhna na bawl manin, Pasian bangmah sukha lo hi. Tampi tak khial ta lecin amah sukha tuan ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Mawhna na gamtat manin, Pasian bangmah sukhalo hi. Tampi tak khial talecin zong amah sukha tuan ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because

### v7
**EN:** If thou be righteous, what givest thou him? or what receiveth he of thine hand?

**TDB77:** Na thumanna hangin Pasian in phattuamna ngah ahi hiam? Pasian in nang kiang panin bangmah deih lo hi.

**ZVS:** Na thumanna hangin Pasian in phattuamna ngah ahi hiam? Pasian in nang kiang panin bangmah deihlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Thy wickedness may hurt a man as thou art; and thy righteousness may profit the son of man.

**TDB77:** Na khialhna hangin na mipihte bek in thuak a, na gamtat hoihna hangin na mipihte bek in phattuamna ngah hi.

### v9
**EN:** By reason of the multitude of oppressions they make the oppressed to cry: they cry out by reason of the arm of the mighty.

**TDB77:** “Mite in gimna a thuak uh ciang tau uh a, amaute a hon dingin midang khatpeuh sam uh hi.

**ZVS:** “Mite in gim a thuak ciangin tau uh a, amaute a honkhia dingin midang khatpeuh a sam uh hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** But none saith, Where is God my maker, who giveth songs in the night;

**TDB77:** Lungziin’ pen hun lai takin thahatna a pia, amau a Piangsakpa Pasian’ lam nga lo uh hi.

**ZVS:** Lungzin' hun laitakin thahatna a pia, amaute a Piangsak Pasian' lam ngalo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth, and maketh us wiser than the fowls of heaven?

**TDB77:** Eite mipil-in a hong bawl, ganhingte leh vasate sangin a pil zaw-in a hong bawl Pasian’ lam nga lo uh hi.

**ZVS:** Eite mipilin a hong bawl, ganhingte leh vasate sangin a pilzaw-in a hong bawl Pasian' lam ngalo uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** There they cry, but none giveth answer, because of the pride of evil men.

**TDB77:** Amaute migilo mi kihisakte ahih manin, huh ding a nget hangun Pasian in pia lo hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute migilo mi kihisakte ahih manun, huh ding a nget hangun Pasian in pialo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Surely God will not hear vanity, neither will the Almighty regard it.

**TDB77:** Ngetnget, gengen a phattuamna bangmah om lo hi. Vanglian Pasian in na mu lo, na ngai lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ngetnget, gengen, a phattuamna bangmah omlo hi. Vanglian Pasian in na mulo, na ngailo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v14
**EN:** Although thou sayest thou shalt not see him, yet judgment is before him; therefore trust thou in him.

**TDB77:** Job aw, nang in, Pasian ka mu thei kei a, ahi zongin ka thu ama khut sungah om a, lungduai takin ka ngak niloh hi, na ci hi.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, nangmah in, ‘Pasian ka mu thei kei a, ahi zongin ka thu ama khutsungah om a, lungduai takin ka ngak niloh hi,’ na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v15
**EN:** But now, because it is not so, he hath visited in his anger; yet he knoweth it not in great extremity:

**TDB77:** Pasian in kuamah daan pia lo a, khialhna zong thupi ngaihsut lo hi, ci- in na ngaihsun hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in kuamah gim pialo a, khialhna zong thupi ngaihsutlo hi, ci-in na ngaihsun hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v16
**EN:** Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vain; he multiplieth words without knowledge.

**TDB77:** Tua banga kam na paupau a mawknapi hi a, na gen mel zong a thei lo na hihna kilang hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua banga kam na paupau a mawknapi hi a, na gen mel zong a thei hetlo na hihna thu a hong kilangsak na hi hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | reciprocal: ki- prefix
