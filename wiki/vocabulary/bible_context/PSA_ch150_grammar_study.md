## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un! Ama biakinn sungah Pasian phat un la, ama vangliatna mun vantungah amah phat un!

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. Ama biakinn sungah Pasian phat un la, ama vangliatna mun vantungah amah phat un.

### v2
**EN:** Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness.

**TDB77:** A vanglian ama sepnate hangin amah phat un la, ama liat mahmahna tawh kituakin amah phat un!

**ZVS:** A vanglian ama sepnate hangin amah phat un la, a liat mahmahna tawh kituakin amah phat un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp.

**TDB77:** Peengkul aw tawh amah phat un la, githa lasakna leh harp lasakna tawh amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Peengkul aw tawh amah phat un la, githa lasakna leh harp lasakna tawh amah phat un.

### v4
**EN:** Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.

**TDB77:** Khuang leh laamna tawh amah phat un la, khausai lasaknate leh tamngai tawh amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Khuang leh lamna tawh amah phat un la, khausai lasaknate leh tamngai tawh amah phat un.

### v5
**EN:** Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals.

**TDB77:** Daltuahte tawh amah phat un la, nakpi-a ging daltuahte tawh amah phat un!

**ZVS:** Daltuahte tawh amah phat un la, nakpi takin a ging daltuahte tawh amah phat un.

### v6
**EN:** Let every thing that hath breath praise the

**TDB77:** Husang theite khempeuh in Topa phat ta un! Topa phat ta un!

**ZVS:** Husang thei khempeuh in { Topa } phat un. { Topa } phat un.
