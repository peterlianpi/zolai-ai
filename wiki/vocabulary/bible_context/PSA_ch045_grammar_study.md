## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My heart is inditing a good matter: I speak of the things which I have made touching the king: my tongue is the pen of a ready writer.

**TDB77:** Thubul hoih khat tawh keima lungsim hong dimlet-in, ka late kumpipa tungah ka sak a, ka leii in lai theipa’ laikung tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Thubul hoih khat tawh ka lungsim hong dimletin, kumpipa la ka phuah a, ka leii pen laitheipa' laikung tawh hong kibang hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Thou art fairer than the children of men: grace is poured into thy lips: therefore God hath blessed thee for ever.

**TDB77:** Mihing’ tate lakah nangmah na hoihpen a, thugen siamin na kam khum zungzung hi; tua ahih ciangin Pasian in nang thupha hong pia tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Mihingte lakah nangmah na hoihpen a, thugen na siam a, na kam khum zungzung hi. Tua ahih manin Pasian in nang thupha hong pia tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v3
**EN:** Gird thy sword upon thy thigh, O most mighty, with thy glory and thy majesty.

**TDB77:** Vanglianpa aw, na minthan’na leh na liatna tawh, na namsau kuah in.

**ZVS:** Vanglian kumpipa aw, na minthan'na leh na liatna tawh na namsau kuah in.

### v4
**EN:** And in thy majesty ride prosperously because of truth and meekness and righteousness; and thy right hand shall teach thee terrible things.

**TDB77:** Thumanna a om nading leh thuman hu’ dingin na minliatna tawh gualzo-in hong pusuak in la, dipkuat-huai hih nate na taklam khut nangmah kihilhsak in!

**ZVS:** Thumanna a omna ding leh thuman a hu dingin na minliatna tawh gualzo-in hong pusuak in la, dipkuathuai hihnate na taklam khut nangmah kihilhsak in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Thine arrows are sharp in the heart of the king's enemies; whereby the people fall under thee.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa’ galte’ lungtang sungah na thaltang hiamte lut a, mite na nuai-ah puk-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa' galte' lungtangah na thaltang hiamte lut a, mite na nuai-ah puk-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Thy throne, O God, is for ever and ever: the sceptre of thy kingdom is a right sceptre.

**TDB77:** Na kumpi tokhom siangtho kip tawntung hi. Na kumpi ciangkhut in thumanna ciangkhut hi a,

**ZVS:** Na kumpi tokhom siangtho kip tawntung hi. Na kumpi ciangkhut thumanna ciangkhut hi a,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Thou lovest righteousness, and hatest wickedness: therefore God, thy God, hath anointed thee with the oil of gladness above thy fellows.

**TDB77:** thumanna deihin gitlohna na mudah hi. Tua ahih ciangin Pasian ahi, nangma Pasian in, na gualpihte tungah kikhaito-in lungdamna sathau tawh nang hong nilh zo a,

**ZVS:** thuman deihin gitlohna na mudah hi. Tua ahih ciangin Pasian ahi, nangma Pasian in na khan'gualte tungah a kikhai to-in lungdamna sathau nang hong nilhzo a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** All thy garments smell of myrrh, and aloes, and cassia, out of the ivory palaces, whereby they have made thee glad.

**TDB77:** na puantualpi mirrah paknamtui, alo paknamtui leh thalsing namtuite a nam hi. Saiha tawh kibawl innpite panin khausai lasaknate in nang hong lungdamsak-in,

**ZVS:** na puantualpite myrrh paknamtui, aloes paknamtui, leh thalsing namtuite a nam hi. Saiha tawh kibawl innpite panin khausai lasaknate in nang hong lungdamsak a,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Kings' daughters were among thy honourable women: upon thy right hand did stand the queen in gold of Ophir.

**TDB77:** kumpite tanute, numei uliante lakah om a, na taklam ah Ofir gam khamte tawh kizemin kumpinu a ding hi.

**ZVS:** kumpite' tanute, numei uliante lakah om a, na taklamah Ofir gam khamte tawh kizemin kumpinu a ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Hearken, O daughter, and consider, and incline thine ear; forget also thine own people, and thy father's house;

**TDB77:** Tanu aw, ngai in, ngaihsun in la, na bil hong ngat in; nangma mite leh na behte mangngilh in;

**ZVS:** Tanu aw, ngai dih in; ngaihsun in la, na bil hong ngat in. Na mite leh na behte mangngilh in.

### v11
**EN:** So shall the king greatly desire thy beauty: for he is thy Lord; and worship thou him.

**TDB77:** kumpipa in na mel hoihna deih ding hi. Na topa ahih ciangin ama mai-ah kun in;

**ZVS:** Na melhoihna hangin kumpipa in hong deih ding hi. Na topa ahih ciangin ama mai-ah kun in.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v12
**EN:** And the daughter of Tyre shall be there with a gift; even the rich among the people shall entreat thy favour.

**TDB77:** Mi haupen ahi Taia mite in na manpha namkim letsong tawh nang hong maivil ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** She shall be brought unto the king in raiment of needlework: the virgins her companions that follow her shall be brought unto thee.

**TDB77:** mel tuamtuam a nei puantualpite silhin mihon kigual deudaute lakah a kithuahpih nungakte leh amah a khate tawh kumpipa kiangah kipaipih hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpi tanu a inndei sungah kham puantualpi tawh kizem a, mel tuamtuam a nei puantualpite silh a, mihon kigual deudaute lakah a kithuahpih nungakte leh amah a khate tawh kumpipa kiangah kipaipih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** With gladness and rejoicing shall they be brought: they shall enter into the king's palace.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa innah a tun’ ciangun lungdamna leh nuamna tawh amaute kitumpih hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa' inn-ah a tun' ciangun lungdamna leh nuamna tawh amaute kitumpih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** Instead of thy fathers shall be thy children, whom thou mayest make princes in all the earth.

**TDB77:** Na pate’ tang dingin na tapate hi ding a, nangmah in leitung khempeuh ah amaute, ulian na suaksak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na pate' tangding na tapate hong hi ding a, nangmah in leitung khempeuhah amaute, ulian na suaksak ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v17
**EN:** I will make thy name to be remembered in all generations: therefore shall the people praise thee for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** Keimah in khang tawntungin nangma min ka kiphawksak ding hi; tua ahih ciangin mite in nang hong phat ngitnget ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah in khang tawntungin na min ka kiphawksak ding hi. Tua ahih ciangin mite in nang hong phat den ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
