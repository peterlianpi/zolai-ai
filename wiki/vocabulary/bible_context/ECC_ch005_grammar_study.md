## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Keep thy foot when thou goest to the house of God, and be more ready to hear, than to give the sacrifice of fools: for they consider not that they do evil.

**TDB77:** Biakinn-ah na pai ciangin lim takin kingaihsun in. Aman leh aman lo a khen thei lo mihaite’ biakpiaknate tawh a kibang piakna sangin thungai dingin biakinn-a paina hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Biakinn-ah na pai ciangin limtakin kingaihsun in. A man leh a manlo a khen theilo mihaite' biakpiaknate tawh a kibang piakna sangin thu ngai dingin biakinn-a paina hoihzaw hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Be not rash with thy mouth, and let not thine heart be hasty to utter any thing before God: for God is in heaven, and thou upon earth: therefore let thy words be few.

**TDB77:** Thu ngaihsun masa-in pau in la, Pasian’ tungah thu mawkciam pak kei in. Vantungah Pasian om a, leitungah nang om na hih manin na pau dinga kilawm zah val pau kei in.

**ZVS:** Thu ngaihsun masa-in pau in la, Pasian' tungah thu mawk ciampak kei in. Vantungah Pasian om a, leitungah nang om na hih manin na pau ding-a kilawm zahval pau kei in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For a dream cometh through the multitude of business; and a fool's voice is known by multitude of words.

**TDB77:** Na lunghimawh semsem leh na mang sia semsem ding a, tam pau semsem lecin na haina kilang semsem ding hi.

**ZVS:** Lunghimawh semsem lecin na mang sia semsem ding a, tam pau semsem lecin na haina kilang semsem ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** When thou vowest a vow unto God, defer not to pay it; for he hath no pleasure in fools: pay that which thou hast vowed.

**TDB77:** Pasian tungah thu na ciam ciangin manlangin tung sak pah in. Amah in mihaite dik sa lo hi. Na thuciamna bangin tung sak in.

**ZVS:** Pasian' tungah thu na ciam ciangin manlangin tungsak pah in. Amah in mihaite diksalo hi. Na thuciamna bangin tungsak in.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** Better is it that thou shouldest not vow, than that thou shouldest vow and not pay.

**TDB77:** Na thuciamna bangin na tunsak loh ding sangin thuciamna neih vet loh ding hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Na thuciamna bangin na tun'sak kei ding leh thu ciam vet kei zaw in.

### v6
**EN:** Suffer not thy mouth to cause thy flesh to sin; neither say thou before the angel, that it was an error: wherefore should God be angry at thy voice, and destroy the work of thine hands?

**TDB77:** Pasian’ siampipa’ mai-ah ka khial kha hi ci-in na gen ding kisam kha ding ahih manin na kampauna hangin khial kha kei in. Tua bangin gamta kha lecin nangma tungah Pasian heh ding a, na sepsa khempeuh hong suksiatsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Khialkha ing, ci-in Pasian' siampipa' mai-ah na gen ding kisam khading ahih manin na kampauna hangin khialkha kei in. Tua bangin gamta khial lecin nangma tungah Pasian heh ding a, na sepsa khempeuh hong suksiatsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** For in the multitude of dreams and many words there are also divers vanities: but fear thou God.

**TDB77:** Mang tam man leeng a mawkna mah tam a, kam tam pau leeng zong a kituak mahin a mawkna thu mah kibeh laplap hi; bangbang ahi zongin Pasian zahtak lai veve in.

**ZVS:** Mang tam man leeng a mawkna mah tam a, kam tam pau leeng zong a kituak mahin a mawkna thu mah kibehlap hi; bangbang ahi zongin Pasian zahtak lai veve in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** If thou seest the oppression of the poor, and violent perverting of judgment and justice in a province, marvel not at the matter: for he that is higher than the highest regardeth; and there be higher than they.

**TDB77:** Kumpite in mizawngte gim ngah sakin, thuman thutangin na a kisep kei leh, lamdang sa kei in. Uliante pen amau’ tunga uliante in en-te a, tuate tungah a en sawn a lianzaw khat om lai hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpite in mizawngte gim ngahsakin, thuman thutangin na a kisep kei leh lamdang sa kei in. Uliante pen amau' tunga uliante in en-te a, tuate' tungah a ensawn a lianzaw khat omlai hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** Moreover the profit of the earth is for all: the king himself is served by the field.

**TDB77:** Gam khat peuhpeuh in a kumpipa in lokhawhna lamsang a lunghimawh kumpi kisam hi.

**ZVS:** Gam khat peuhpeuh in lo khawhna lamsang a lunghimawh kumpi khat kisam hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** He that loveth silver shall not be satisfied with silver; nor he that loveth abundance with increase: this is also vanity.

**TDB77:** Sumdeihna lungsim na neih leh lungkim hun cih na nei ngei kei ding hi; na hauh nop hangin na deih khempeuh na ngah ngei kei ding hi; hih in a mawknapi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Sum deihna lungsim na neih leh lungkim hun cih na thei ngei kei ding hi. Na hauhnop hangin na deih khempeuh na ngah ngei kei ding hi. Hih pen a mawknapi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v11
**EN:** When goods increase, they are increased that eat them: and what good is there to the owners thereof, saving the beholding of them with their eyes?

**TDB77:** Hau semsem lecin na vak ding mi tam semsem a, hauzaw ing, ci-a na theih cih bek simloh bangmah phattuamna na ngah kei hi.

**ZVS:** Hau semsem lecin na vak ding mi tam semsem a, a hau zaw hi'ng, ci-a na theih bek cih simloh bangmah phattuamna na ngah kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v12
**EN:** The sleep of a labouring man is sweet, whether he eat little or much: but the abundance of the rich will not suffer him to sleep.

**TDB77:** Na a sem mipa in a nek ding nei taleh nei takei leh lungnuam takin ihmu thei a, a haute ahih leh lungnuam takin ihmu thei lo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Na a sem mipa pen a nek ding nei ta leh nei ta kei leh lungnuam takin ihmu thei a, a haute ahih leh lungnuam takin ihmu theilo-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** There is a sore evil which I have seen under the sun, namely, riches kept for the owners thereof to their hurt.

**TDB77:** A hoih het lo na khat hih leitungah ka mu hi: mite, amau’ lunggim nadingin hau-te hi.

**ZVS:** A hoih hetlo na khat hih leitungah ka mu hi: Mite amau' lunggimna dingin hau-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** But those riches perish by evil travail: and he begetteth a son, and there is nothing in his hand.

**TDB77:** Na a sep khialh manin a neihsa teng-te suplawh-te a, a tu a tate’ luah ding bangmah om nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** Na a sep khialh manun a neihsa teng-te suplawh-te a, a tu a tate' luah ding bangmah om nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because | plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** As he came forth of his mother's womb, naked shall he return to go as he came, and shall take nothing of his labour, which he may carry away in his hand.

**TDB77:** I nute’ sung panin bangmah nei loin tangguakin i suah mah bangin tangguak mahin i ciahkik hi. I sepnate, i ciahpih kik theih bangmah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** I nute' sung panin bangmah neiloin guaktangin i suah mah bangin guaktang mahin i ciahkik hi. I sepsate i ciahpihkik theih bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** And this also is a sore evil, that in all points as he came, so shall he go: and what profit hath he that hath laboured for the wind?

**TDB77:** A dahhuai mahmah thu in: i suah hun lai-a mah bang tektekin i ciahkik ding hi; a mawknapi dinga i sepna-ah bang phattuamna ngah i hi hiam?

**ZVS:** A dahhuai mahmah thu in: I suah hun lai-a mah bang teekteek-in i ciahkik ding hi. A mawknapi ding-a i sepna-ah bang phattuamna ngah ihi hiam?

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will

### v17
**EN:** All his days also he eateth in darkness, and he hath much sorrow and wrath with his sickness.

**TDB77:** I nuntak sung teng lungzin’na, dahna, lunghimawhna, natna satna leh hehna bek tawh hun bei lel hi.

**ZVS:** I nuntak sungteng lungzin'na, dahna, lunghihmawhna, natna satna, leh hehna bek tawh hun bei lel hi.

### v18
**EN:** Behold that which I have seen: it is good and comely for one to eat and to drink, and to enjoy the good of all his labour that he taketh under the sun all the days of his life, which God giveth him: for it is his portion.

**TDB77:** Keimah in ka phawk khiat thu in: Pasian in tomkha nuntak hih leitungah hong piak sungin, i sep thaman ne-in dawnin, nuamsa-in i om ding a hoihpen ahi hi. Hih in mihingte’ thumong ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah in ka phawkkhiat thu in: Pasian in tomkha nuntak hih leitungah hong piak sungin, i sep thaman ne-in dawnin i om ding a hoihpen ahi hi. Hih pen mihingte' thumong ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v19
**EN:** Every man also to whom God hath given riches and wealth, and hath given him power to eat thereof, and to take his portion, and to rejoice in his labour; this is the gift of God.

**TDB77:** Pasian in mi khat neih leh lam guan’in, a hauhna nuam takin a nek sak leh, amah lungdamin a sepna thaman ne-in a om ding ahi hi. Tua nopsakna in Pasian’ kiang pan ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in mi khat neihlelam guan'in, a hauhna nuamtakin a neksak leh, amah lungdamin a sepna thaman ne-in a om ding hi. Tua nopsakna pen Pasian' kiang pan ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20

**ZVS:** Pasian in lungdamin omsak ahih manin a nuntak tomlam phawkin nei khalo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because
