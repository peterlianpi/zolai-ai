## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Deliver me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, migilote kiang panin kei hong honkhia in la, mi ngongtatte kiang panin kei hong kem in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, migilote kiang panin kei hong honkhia in la, mi ngongtatte kiang panin kei hong kem in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Which imagine mischiefs in their heart; continually are they gathered together for war.

**TDB77:** Amaute in a lung sung uh ah thusia geelgeel uh a, gal vive phuang ngitnget uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in a lung sung uhah thusia geelgeel uh a, gal phuang ngitnget uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent; adders' poison is under their lips. Selah.

**TDB77:** Amaute’ leii in gulpi leii bang a, a thugente uh gulgu bang hi.

**ZVS:** A leii uh gulpi leii bang a, a thugente uh gulgu bang hi.

### v4
**EN:** Keep me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, migilote khut sung panin kei hong cing in la, kei, sukpuk ding a hong sawm mi ngongtatte’ kiang panin kei hong kem in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, migilote' khutsung panin kei hong cing in la, kei, sukpaih ding a hong sawm mi ngongtatte kiang panin kei hong kem in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** The proud have hid a snare for me, and cords; they have spread a net by the wayside; they have set gins for me. Selah.

**TDB77:** Mi kiphasakte in kei hong siahna-in tehlop koihsim uh a, amaute in khau tawh ngenthang zak uh a, lam gei-ah thangzak tawh kei hong siah uh hi.

**ZVS:** Mi kiphasakte in keimah hong siahna-in tehlop tung sim uh a, amaute in ngenthang zakin lam gei-ah thangzak tawh kei hong siah uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** I said unto the

**TDB77:** Topa’ tungah hih bangin ka ci hi: Nang in keima Pasian na hi hi; keima thuumna awte hong ngai in, Topa!

**ZVS:** { Topa' } tungah hih bangin ka ci hi: Nang pen keima Pasian na hi hi; ka thumna awte hong ngai in, { Topa}!

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v7
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa, keima Topa, a thahat kei hong honkhiapa aw, gal kidona sungah keimah nong hu hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } keima Topa, a thahat hong honkhiapa aw, galkidona sungah keimah nong hu hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Grant not, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, migilopa’ deihnate tangtungsak kei in la, ama thusia sawmna a maban sot sak kei in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, migilopa' deihnate tangtungsak kei in la, ama thusia sawmna a maban sotsak kei in!

### v9
**EN:** As for the head of those that compass me about, let the mischief of their own lips cover them.

**TDB77:** Kei hong umcih mite, gualzosak kei in la, kei hong sawmnate uh amau’ lu tung mah tusak in!

**ZVS:** Kei hong umcih mite gualzosak kei in la, kei hong sawmnate uh amau' lu tung mah tusak in!

### v10
**EN:** Let burning coals fall upon them: let them be cast into the fire; into deep pits, that they rise not up again.

**TDB77:** Amaute tungah mei-am kuang zusak in! A thokik zo nawn lo dingin dum sungah amaute kia suk sak in!

**ZVS:** Amaute tungah mei-am kuang zusak in. A thokikzo nawnlo dingin dum sungah amaute kiasak in!

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Let not an evil speaker be established in the earth: evil shall hunt the violent man to overthrow him.

**TDB77:** Mi gensiapa leitungah kip sak kei in la, mi ngongtatpa pen thusia in sulzuih mengmeng ta hen!

**ZVS:** Mi gensiapa leitungah mavangsak kei in la, mi ngongtatpa pen thusia in sulzuih ta hen!

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** I know that the

**TDB77:** Topa in a thuak mite’ thu a panpihna, mi cimawhte’ adingin thu a manin a khenna ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in gimna a thuak mite' thu a pan'pihna, mi cimawhte-a' dingin thu a manin a khenna ka thei hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | causal: because

### v13
**EN:** Surely the righteous shall give thanks unto thy name: the upright shall dwell in thy presence.

**TDB77:** Thuman mite in nangma min hong phat pelmawh ding uh a, lungsim thutangte pen nangma omna-ah teeng ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mite in nangma min hong phat pelmawh ding uh a, lungsim thutangte pen nangma omna-ah teng ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker
