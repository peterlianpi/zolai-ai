## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na heh laitak hong tai kei in la, lungso sa-in hong thuhilh kei in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na heh laitak hong taii kei in la, lungso sa-in hong thuhilh kei in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** Have mercy upon me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, thanem ka hih manin kei tungah dikna hong zang in; Topa aw, ka guh ka tang na ahih manin hong dam sak in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, thanem ka hih manin kei tungah dikna hong zang in. { Topa } aw, ka guh ka taang na mahmah ahih manin hong damsak in.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v3
**EN:** My soul is also sore vexed: but thou, O

**TDB77:** Ka hin’ gim mahmah hi. Topa— bang tanvei hong sawt lai ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Ka hin'gim mahmah hi. { Topa } aw, bang tanvei nang hong zekai lai ding na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v4
**EN:** Return, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, hong kihei in la kei hong gum in; itna kip neipa na hih manin keimah hong hon in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, hong kihei in la, kei hong hon in. Itna kip a neipa na hih manin keimah hong hon in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** For in death there is no remembrance of thee: in the grave who shall give thee thanks?

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh sihna-ah nangmah phawk theihna om lo ahih manin, Leinuai panin kua in nang hong phat thei ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh misite omna gam sungah nangmah phawk theihna ding bangmah omlo ahih manin, Leinuai panin kua in nang hong phat thei ding ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because

### v6
**EN:** I am weary with my groaning; all the night make I my bed to swim; I water my couch with my tears.

**TDB77:** Ka tautauna-in ka gim hi; zan sialin ka khitui in ka lupphah tum a, ka kahkahna-in ka lupna kawt dikdek hi.

**ZVS:** Ka tautauna-in ka gim hi. Zan sialin ka khitui in ka lupna tum a, ka kahkahna-in ka lupna kawt dikdek hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** Mine eye is consumed because of grief; it waxeth old because of all mine enemies.

**TDB77:** Dahna hangin ka mit mial a, ka galte’ hangin ka mitzung zaw semsem hi.

**ZVS:** Dahna hangin ka mit mial a, ka galte' hong bawlna hangin ka mitzung zaw semsem hi.

### v8
**EN:** Depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity; for the

**TDB77:** Siatna bawlte khempeuh aw, ka kiang panin kihemkhia un; bang hang hiam cih leh Topa in ka kahna aw a za zo hi.

**ZVS:** Siatna a bawlte khempeuh aw, ka kiang panin kihemkhia un; bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } in ka kahna aw a za hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ka thumna za zo a, Topa in ka thungetna hong sang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in keima thumna hong za khinzo a, { Topa } in ka thungetna hong sang ta hi.

### v10
**EN:** Let all mine enemies be ashamed and sore vexed: let them return and be ashamed suddenly.

**TDB77:** Ka galte khempeuh zahhuai in genthei mahmah ding-te hi; amaute in nungkik-in, maizumna thuak pah ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ka galte khempeuh zahhuai mahmahin genthei ding-te hi. Amaute nungkikin maizumna thuak pah ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker
