## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Having therefore these promises, dearly beloved, let us cleanse ourselves from all filthiness of the flesh and spirit, perfecting holiness in the fear of God.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ka itte aw, tua thuciamna eite tawh kikum ahih ciangin i pumpi kihhuainate leh lungsim kihhuainate khempeuh panin kisiangthosakin, Pasian kihta-in, a siangtho gamtatna tawh dimin om ni.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin ka hong it lawmte aw, pumpi leh lungsim a ninsak theite khempeuh panin kisian'suahin, Pasian zahtakna tawh nungta-in siangtho sinsenin om ni.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | hortative: let us | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2

**TDB77:** Kote ading na lungsim uh na za sak un. Kote in kuamah tungah ka khial kei uh hi. Kuamah ka bawlsia kei uh hi. Kuamah kheemin a sum ka la kei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Kuama tungah kote hong khiallo, kuamah hong siasaklo, noptaksa-in kuama tungah hong gamtalo ka hih manun na lungsim uh hong pia un.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v3

**TDB77:** Note hong mawhsak nadingin hih bang ka ci kei hi. No tawh nuntak khawm nadingin ahi zongin, sih khawm nadingin ahi zongin ka deih dongin note ka lungtang sungah kong it hi ci-in tuma-in ka hong ci zo hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bang ka hong cih ciangin note mawhsak nopna tawh thu a hong gen ka hi kei hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh ka hong gensa mah bangin note hong it mahmah ka hih manun note na hin' uh leh hingkhawm, na sih uh leh zong a sikhawm ngam ka hi uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | negation: kei

### v4

### v5

**ZVS:** Masedonia gam ka tun'khit uh ciang nangawnin tawldam cih om thei tuanlo a, a koimah peuhah kitotna leh lungkhamna om kawikawi ahih manin lungsim patauhna hi tawntung hi.

### v7

**ZVS:** Ama hong tun'na hang bek zong hiloin ama lungsim a thahatna dingin note' gamtatna zong a hong zasak hi. Note in kei na hong muhnopna thu uh, note na dahna thu uh leh kei na hong hutnopna thu uh hong gen ahih manin ka lung a dam semsem hi.

### v8

### v9
**EN:** Now I rejoice, not that ye were made sorry, but that ye sorrowed to repentance: for ye were made sorry after a godly manner, that ye might receive damage by us in nothing.

**TDB77:** No hong dahsak ahih maninah a nuam ka hi kei hi, hong dahsaka hong kisiksak ahih manin ka lung a dam hi. Kote hangin note noptuamna uh a neukha beek a kisiat loh nadingin Pasian’ deihna tawh kituakin ka hong dahsak hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh tu-in ka lung a dam hi. Note ka hong dahsak khakna hangin a lungdam hiloin note na dahna hangun na ngaihsutna uh kheel thei na hih manun a nuam ka hi hi. Tua na dahna uh Pasian in a hoihlamin hong zang ahih manin note-a' dingin siatna a hong piangsak ka hi kei zaw uh hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: kei | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

**ZVS:** Tua na dahna uh hotkhiatna ngahtheihna dingin na lungsim uh na kheelna dingun Pasian in hong zang ahih manin dahna ding bangmah omlo hi. Ahi zongin hih leitung dahna lelte in ahih leh sihna piangsak hi.

### v11

In all things ye have approved yourselves to be clear in this matter.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ deihna tawh kituak note dahna in note sungah bang ci bang phawkna, bang ci bang mawh kiphelna, bang ci bang hehna, bang ci bang launa, bang ci bang lunggulhna, bang ci bang deihna lungsim, bang ci bang thuman tawh daan piak thu-a bawlphatna ci-in en ta un.
