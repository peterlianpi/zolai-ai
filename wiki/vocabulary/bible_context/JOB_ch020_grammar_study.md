## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then answered Zophar the Naamathite, and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Na-amah gammi Zofar in dawng a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Naamah gammi Zofar in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** Therefore do my thoughts cause me to answer, and for this I make haste.

**TDB77:** “Job aw, ka heh nong suaksak a, kei ka ki-iip zo nawn kei hi.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, ka heh nong suaksak a, ka ki-ipzo nawn kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** I have heard the check of my reproach, and the spirit of my understanding causeth me to answer.

**TDB77:** Na thugente khempeuh kei nong simmawhna kam hi a, ahi zongin kong dawn’ nading ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Na thugente khempeuh kei nong simmawhna kampau vive hi a, ahi zongin kong dawn'na ding ka thei hi.

### v4
**EN:** Knowest thou not this of old, since man was placed upon earth,

**TDB77:** Tanglai pek-a kipan, leitungah mihing a om cil-a kipanin,

**ZVS:** Tanglai peka kipan, leitung mi a om cila kipanin,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** That the triumphing of the wicked is short, and the joy of the hypocrite but for a moment?

**TDB77:** migilote’ nopsak hun a tomna thu, Pasian a thudon lote’ lungdam hun a tomna thu thei lo na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** migilote' nopsak hun a tom mahmahna thu, Pasian a donlote' lungdam hun a tom mahmahna thu theilo na hi hiam?

### v6
**EN:** Though his excellency mount up to the heavens, and his head reach unto the clouds;

**TDB77:** Migilopa lianin vantung a banto zongin, lian mahmah in a lutang in meii lak a banto zongin,

**ZVS:** Migilopa lian mahmahin vantung a banto zongin, lian mahmahin a lutang in meii lak a banto zongin,

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v7
**EN:** Yet he shall perish for ever like his own dung: they which have seen him shall say, Where is he?

**ZVS:** leivui them bangin maimang veve lel ding hi. Amah a thei ngei mite in, ‘Amah koi-ah omta ahi hiam?’ ci-in lamdangsa ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v8

**ZVS:** Amah pen zana mangmat bang, mumaang bangin maimang ding a, cikmah hunin kimukik ngei nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9

**TDB77:** Ama teen’na ngei mun panin amah mangthang ding a,

**ZVS:** Ama ten'na ngei mun panin amah maimang ding a,

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11

### v12

### v13
**EN:** Though he spare it, and forsake it not; but keep it still within his mouth:
