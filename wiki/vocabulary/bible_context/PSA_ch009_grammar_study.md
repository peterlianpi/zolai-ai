## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I will praise thee, O

**TDB77:** Ka lungsim khempeuh tawh Topa tungah lungdamna kong pia ding hi; na bawlna a lamdangte khempeuh ka gen ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim khempeuh tawh { Topa } tungah lungdamna kong ko ding a, na bawl a lamdangte khempeuh ka gen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v2
**EN:** I will be glad and rejoice in thee: I will sing praise to thy name, O thou most High.

**TDB77:** Lungdamin nangma sungah nuamin ka diang ding a, min phatna la ka sa ding hi, Sangpen Pa aw.

**ZVS:** Nangma hangin nuamin ka diang ding a, na min phatna ka sa ding hi, Sangpenpa aw.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3
**EN:** When mine enemies are turned back, they shall fall and perish at thy presence.

**TDB77:** Ka galte a nungkik ciangin, nangma mai-ah amaute pukin kisia uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** For thou hast maintained my right and my cause; thou satest in the throne judging right.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, keima thutan’na nong kipsak a, tokhom tungah tu-in thuman na khen hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh keima thutan'na na hong kipsak a, tokhom tungah tu-in thu a manin na khen hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Thou hast rebuked the heathen, thou hast destroyed the wicked, thou hast put out their name for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** Minamte na tai a, migilote na susia hi; a tawntunga mangthang dingin amau min na phiat hi.

**ZVS:** Minamte na taii a, migilote na susia hi. A maimang dingin amau' min na phiat hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v6
**EN:** O thou enemy, destructions are come to a perpetual end: and thou hast destroyed cities; their memorial is perished with them.

**TDB77:** Siat denna sungah galte beimang hi; a khuate uh na zunglot hi; amau kiphawk kha nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** Siat denna sungah galte beimang hi. A khuate uh na zunglot a, amaute kiphawkkha nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v8

### v10
