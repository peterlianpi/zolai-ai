## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Babilon kumpipa Nebukhadnezzar in Jerusalem khua panin Jehoiakim’ tapa Judah kumpipa Jekoniah leh Judah kumpi uliante, khutsiamte leh siksek siamte, Babilon khua-ah sal dingin a pai khiatpih khit ciangin Topa in hih mang hong musak hi. Topa’ biakinnpi mai-ah theigah bawm nih dim ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Babylon Kumpi Nebukhadnezzar in Jerusalem khua panin Jehoiakim' tapa Judah kumpipa Jehoiakhin leh Judah kumpi uliante, khutsiamte leh sikseek siamte, Babylon khua-ah sal dingin a paikhiatpih khit ciangin { Topa } in hih maang hong musak hi. { Topa' } biakinn mai-ah theigah bawm nih dim ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v2
**EN:** One basket had very good figs, even like the figs that are first ripe: and the other basket had very naughty figs, which could not be eaten, they were so bad.

**TDB77:** Bawm khat sunga theigahte, a min masate tawh kibangin limci mahmah hi. Ahih hangin a bawm khat pen sunga theigahte limci het loin nek theih ding bang hi dek lo hi.

**ZVS:** Bawm khat sunga theigahte, a min masate tawh kibangin limci mahmah hi. Ahih hangin a bawm khat pen sunga theigahte limci hetloin nektheih ding bang hi deklo hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Then said the

**TDB77:** Topa in hong hopih a, “Jeremiah aw, bang na mu hiam?” ci-in hong dong hi. Kei zong, “Theigah ka mu hi. A limcite limci mahmah a, a limci lote limci lo lua ahih manin nek theih ding bang hi lo hi,” ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in hong hopih a, “Jeremiah aw, bang mu na hi hiam?” ci-in hong dong hi. Kei zong, “Theigah ka mu hi. A limcite limci mahmah a, a limcilote limcilo lua ahih manin nektheih ding bang hilo hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | causal: because
