## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| future: will | 7 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 5 |
| plural: uh marker | 4 |
| negation: lo | 4 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My son, attend unto my wisdom, and bow thine ear to my understanding:

**TDB77:** Ka ta aw, ka pilna leh ka thumuhnate, lim takin ngai-in hong za in.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, ka pilna leh ka thumuhnate, limtakin ngai-in hong za in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** That thou mayest regard discretion, and that thy lips may keep knowledge.

**TDB77:** Tua hi leh, a kilawmin na gamtat nading zia na thei ding a, na kampaunate in na pil lam tak hong kilangsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi leh a kilawmin na gamtatzia ding na thei ding a, na kampaunate in na pil lamtak hong kilangsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For the lips of a strange woman drop as an honeycomb, and her mouth is smoother than oil:

**TDB77:** Numei paktatte’ kampau, khuaizu bangin khum mahmahin, a thugente uh a neel dakdak hangin,

**ZVS:** Numei paktatte' kampau khuaizu bangin khum mahmahin a thugente uh a neel dakdak hangin,

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** But her end is bitter as wormwood, sharp as a twoedged sword.

**TDB77:** a tawpna-ah lungkhamna leh natna sim loh, nangma tungah bangmah dang hong guan lo hi.

**ZVS:** a tawpna-ah lungkhamna leh natna simloh nangma tungah bangmah dang hong guanlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** Her feet go down to death; her steps take hold on hell.

**TDB77:** Amah in sihna gamah hong paipih ding a, ama tot lampi in sihna mun tun’na lampi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in sihna gamah nang hong paipih ding a, ama tot lampi pen sihna mun tun'na lampi ahi hi.

### v6
**EN:** Lest thou shouldest ponder the path of life, her ways are moveable, that thou canst not know them.

**TDB77:** Nuntakna lampi-ah amah om lo a, nang na phawk loh hangin amah lam pialin a vakvai ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Nuntakna lampi-ah amah omlo a, nang na phawkloh hangin amah lam pialin a vakvai ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v7
**EN:** Hear me now therefore, O ye children, and depart not from the words of my mouth.

**TDB77:** Tate aw, tu-in ka thugente hong ngai un la, ka thugente mangngilh kei un.

**ZVS:** Tate aw, tu-in ka thugente limtakin hong ngai un la, ka thugente mangngilh kei un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** Remove thy way far from her, and come not nigh the door of her house:

**TDB77:** Tua bang numei tawh kineh kha kei un la, a inn gei nangnawn-ah pai kha kei un.

**ZVS:** Tua bang numei tawh kinehkha kei un la, a inn gei nangawnah paikha kei un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Lest thou give thine honour unto others, and thy years unto the cruel:

**TDB77:** Tua bang na hih kei uh leh, na min hoihna teng uh midang tungah tung ding a, hehpihna a nei lo mite’ khutlum tawh na khan’sih ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang na hih kei uh leh na min hoihna teng uh midang tungah tung ding a, hehpihna a neilo mite' khutlum tawh na khan'sih ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** Lest strangers be filled with thy wealth; and thy labours be in the house of a stranger;

**TDB77:** Khualmite in na neih khempeuh uh la ding a, na sepsa khempeuh uh midangte in ngah ding hi.

**ZVS:** Khualmite in na neihsa teng uh la ding a, na sepsa khempeuh uh midangte in ngah ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** And thou mourn at the last, when thy flesh and thy body are consumed,

**TDB77:** Na sih kuan uh ciangin thuumin na tau ding uh a, gawng-in na guh guak bekun na om ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Na sih kuan uh ciangin thumin na tau ding uh a, gawngin na guhguak bekun na om ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** And say, How have I hated instruction, and my heart despised reproof;

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin, “Bang hangin thu thei thei hoh lo a, hong kihilhna mang hoh lo ka hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin, “Bang hangin thu thei hoh lo a, hong kihilhna mang hoh lo ka hi hiam?

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** And have not obeyed the voice of my teachers, nor inclined mine ear to them that instructed me!

**TDB77:** Kei hong hilh siate’ thu ka mang nuam kei a, amau’ hong hilhnate ka don kei kha hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong hilh siate' thu ka mang nuam kei a, amau' hong hilhnate ka don keikha hi.

### v14
**EN:** I was almost in all evil in the midst of the congregation and assembly.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih manin tu-in ka cimawh vat a, mi mai-ah ka min a sia hi,” na ci ciat ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin tu-in ka cimawh vat a, mi' mai-ah ka min a sia hi,” na ci ciat ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | causal: because | plural: uh marker

### v15

**TDB77:** Na zi’ tungah citak in la, amah bek itin nei in.

**ZVS:** Na zi' tungah citak in la, amah bek itin nei in.

### v16

**TDB77:** Pasal dang tawh va vak zau-in kongzing lakah va mawh kha ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasal dang tawh va vakzau-in kongzing lakah va mawhkha ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v17
**EN:** Let them be only thine own, and not strangers' with thee.

**TDB77:** Na zi in nangma ading bek hi a, midang tawh na neihkhawm ding hi lo hi.

**ZVS:** Na zi pen nangma-a' ding bek hi a, midangte tawh na neihkhawm ding hilo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo

### v18
**EN:** Let thy fountain be blessed: and rejoice with the wife of thy youth.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin na zi tawh lungkim in la, na teen’pih numei tawh lungdam in.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin na zi tawh lungkim in la, na ten'pih numei tawh lungdam in.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v19
**EN:** Let her be as the loving hind and pleasant roe; let her breasts satisfy thee at all times; and be thou ravished always with her love.

**TDB77:** A it huai a ngaihbangin ngaihsun tawntung in la, ama ngaihbaan’na tawh lungdamin, ama hong itna tawh lungkim in.

**ZVS:** A ithuai a ngaihbaangin ngaihsun tawntung in la, ama ngaihbaan'na tawh lungdamin, ama hong itna tawh lungkim in.

**EN:** And why wilt thou, my son, be ravished with a strange woman, and embrace the bosom of a stranger?

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, bang hangin numei dang lunggulh ding a, numei dangte' ngaihbaan'na iplah ding na hi hiam?

### v21

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian in na gamtatna khempeuh hong mu a, na paina khempeuh hong en kawikawi hi.

**TDB77:** Migilopa’ mawhna in thangzak bang hi a, ama mawhna thang sung mahah amah awk hi.

**ZVS:** Migilopa' mawhna thangzak bang hi a, ama mawhna thang sung mahah amah a awk hi.
