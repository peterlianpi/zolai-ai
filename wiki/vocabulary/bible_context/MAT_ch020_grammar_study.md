## Verses

### v17
**EN:** And Jesus going up to Jerusalem took the twelve disciples apart in the way, and said unto them,

**TDB77:** Zeisu Jerusalem khuapi-ah a pai lai takin, a nungzui Sawm-le-nihte gam daihna-ah sam a,

**ZVS:** Jerusalem khua manawhin Jesuh a paitoh laitakin, a paikawmun sawmlenihte tawh tuamtonin thu genpih a,

### v20
**EN:** Then came to him the mother of Zebedee's children with her sons, worshipping him, and desiring a certain thing of him.

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin Zebedi’ tapate, a nu leh a tate tawh Zeisu kiangah paikhawm-te a, thu khat a ngen-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Zebedi tate James leh Johan' nu, a tate gel tawh Jesuh kiangah hong pai-in a mai-ah khukdin'in hong thum hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v21
**EN:** And he said unto her,

**TDB77:** Zeisu in, “Bang deih na hi-te hiam?” ci-in a dot ciangin, “Topa na Gam sungah ka tapate, na taklam khat, na veilam khat a tut theih nadingun thu hong pia in,” ci-in a ngen-te hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh in tua nupi kiangah, “Bang deih na hi hiam?” ci-in a dot ciangin amah in, “Kumpi-in na om ciangin hih ka tate khat na taklamah, khat na veilamah a tutna dingin thu pia lecin ka ut hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v22
**EN:** But Jesus answered and said,

**TDB77:** Zeisu in, “Note in na hong ngetna-te na thei kei-te hi. Ka dawn ding hai dawn zo ding na hi-te hiam?” a cih ciangin, “Ka dawn zo ding-te hi,” a ci-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Jesuh in, “Na nget-te na thei kei-te hi. Ka thuakding gimna note in thuakzo ding na hi-te hiam?” ci-in a dot ciangin amaute in, “Ka thuakzo ding-te hi,” a ci-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v23
**EN:** And he saith unto them,

**TDB77:** Zeisu in, “Ka dawn ding hai na dawn takpi ding-te hi. Ahih hangin, ka taklam leh veilam ah tut nading, ke’n thu ka hong pia thei kei hi. Ka Pa in ngah dinga a bawlsate in a ngah ding-te hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh in amaute kiangah, “Keima thuak mah bangin note in na thuak takpi ding-te a, ahi zongin ka taklam leh ka veilama tutding pen kei' hong piaktheih hilo hi. Ka Pa in a sehsa mite-a' ding bek ahi hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | negation: kei | plural: uh marker
