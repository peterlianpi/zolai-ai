# Psalms Chapter 2 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 11 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| future: will | 4 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 3 |
| plural: uh marker | 3 |
| quotative: said/saying | 3 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 3 |
| sequence: then/therefore | 2 |
| completed: already done | 1 |
| purpose: in order to | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Why do the heathen rage, and the people imagine a vain thing?

**TDB77:** Bang thu hangin minamte in siat nading ah kithukhat sim a, mite in a mawk nading sawmsim uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang thu hangin minam dangte siatna dingah kithukhat sim uh a, mite in a mawkna dingpi-in sawmsim uh ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** The kings of the earth set themselves, and the rulers take counsel together, against the

**TDB77:** Leitung kumpite leh gam ukte in, Topa leh ama sathau nilhpa langpangin vaihawm uh a,

**ZVS:** Leitung kumpite leh gam ukte in { Topa } leh ama sathau nilhpa langpangin vaihawm uh a,

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Let us break their bands asunder, and cast away their cords from us.

**TDB77:** “Amau’ hong hennate su-tat khia-in, amau khaute ei kiang panin paikhia ni,” ci uh hi.

**ZVS:** “Amau' hong henna khaute sutat khia-in, amau' khaute ei kiang panin paiikhia ni,” ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** He that sitteth in the heavens shall laugh: the Lord shall have them in derision.

**TDB77:** Vantunga tu-pa in nuihsan a, tua Topa in amaute zahko hi.

**ZVS:** Vantunga a tupa in amaute na nuihsan a, tua { Topa } in amaute a zahko hi.

### v5
**EN:** Then shall he speak unto them in his wrath, and vex them in his sore displeasure.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin hehna tawh amau kiangah thu gen ding a, heh luatna tawh amaute linglawng sakin,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin hehna tawh amau kiangah thu gen ding a, hehluat mahmahna tawh amaute linglawng sakin,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Yet have I set my king upon my holy hill of Zion.

**TDB77:** “Ka mual siangtho Zion tungah ka Kumpi ka tusak khin hi,” ci hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka mual siangtho Zion tungah ka kumpi ka tusak khin hi,” ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | completed: already done

### v7
**EN:** I will declare the decree: the

**TDB77:** Topa’ thukhenna kong gen ding hi; Amah in kei’ tungah, “Nang in ka tapa na hi a, tuni in nang kong piang sak khin zo hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } thukhenna tu-in ka hong gen ding hi: Amah in keima tungah, “Nang pen ka tapa na hi a, tuni-in na Pa ka suak hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** Ask of me, and I shall give thee the heathen for thine inheritance, and the uttermost parts of the earth for thy possession.

**TDB77:** Keima tungah hong ngen in; nangma luah dingin minamte ka bawl ding a, leimong dongin nangma neihsa ka hisak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Keima tungah hong ngen in; nangma luah dingin minamte ka bawl ding a, leimong dongin na neihsa ka hisak ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v9
**EN:** Thou shalt break them with a rod of iron; thou shalt dash them in pieces like a potter's vessel.

**TDB77:** Siktawn khat tawh amaute na satkham ding a, beelseekpa’ beel den’khap bangin amaute lei-ah na dengzan ding hi,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Sik tawn khat tawh amaute na satkham ding a, beelsekpa in beel a den'khap bangin leilakah amaute na dengzan ding hi,” hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will

### v10
**EN:** Be wise now therefore, O ye kings: be instructed, ye judges of the earth.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin kumpite aw, tu-in pil un; leitung gam ukte aw, thu phawk un.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin kumpite aw, tu-in pil ta un; leitung gam a ukte aw, tu-in thu phawk ta un.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v12
**EN:** Kiss the Son, lest he be angry, and ye perish from the way, when his wrath is kindled but a little. Blessed are all they that put their trust in him.

**TDB77:** Launa tawh { Topa' } na sem un la, lin'na tawh a khe nam un; tua hi kei leh amah sinso-in lamkalah na siamang ding uh hi; bang hang hiam cih leh amah heh pakpak hi. Amah a bel khempeuh mi nuamsate ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker
