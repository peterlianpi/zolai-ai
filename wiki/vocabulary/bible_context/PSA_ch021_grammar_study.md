## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The king shall joy in thy strength, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangma thahatna sungah kumpipa lungdam a, gawlzawhna na piakna sungah amah naknuam mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na hatna hangin kumpipa lungdam a, na huhna hangin amah nak nuam mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v2
**EN:** Thou hast given him his heart's desire, and hast not withholden the request of his lips. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ama lungsim’ deihna nang in na piakhin a, ama hong ngetna na khaktan kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ama lungsim deihna teng nangmah in na pia khin a, ama hong ngette na khaktan kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** For thou preventest him with the blessings of goodness: thou settest a crown of pure gold on his head.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh thupha hoihte tawh amah na dawntuah a, ama lu tungah kham siang lukhu na khusak hi.

### v4
**EN:** He asked life of thee, and thou gavest it him, even length of days for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** Nangma tung panin amah in nuntakna hong ngen a, ama tungah sau veipi nuntakna na pia hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma tung panin amah in nuntakna hong ngen a, ama tungah nuntak sau na pia hi.

### v5
**EN:** His glory is great in thy salvation: honour and majesty hast thou laid upon him.

**TDB77:** Nangma huhna hangin ama minthan’na lian a, hoihna leh minliatna ama tungah na pia hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma huhna hangin ama minthan'na lian a, hoihna leh minliatna ama tungah na pia hi.

### v6
**EN:** For thou hast made him most blessed for ever: thou hast made him exceeding glad with thy countenance.

**TDB77:** Hi hi, nangmah in a tawntungin thupha ngah pen dingin amah na bawl a, nangma ompihna lungdamna tawh amah na kipaksak hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in a tawntungin thupha ngah pen dingin amah na bawl a, nangma ompihna hangin lungdamna tawh amah na kipaksak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** For the king trusteth in the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh kumpipa in Topa muang a, Sangpen Pa’ itna kip hangin amah a kiling kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh kumpipa in { Topa } muang a, Sangpenpa' itna kip hangin amah a kiling kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Thine hand shall find out all thine enemies: thy right hand shall find out those that hate thee.

**TDB77:** Na khut in na galte khempeuh su ding a, na khut taklam in nang hong mudahte sugawp ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na khut in na galte khempeuh sugawp ding a, na khut taklam in nang hong mudahte sugawp ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** Thou shalt make them as a fiery oven in the time of thine anger: the

**TDB77:** Na kilat ciangin meiphual kuang bangin amaute na bawl ding hi. Topa in hehna tawh amaute nawmvalh ding a, mei in amaute kangtum ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na kilat ciangin meiphual kuang bangin amaute na hih ding hi. { Topa } in hehna tawh amaute nawmvalh ding a, meikuang in amaute kangtum ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Their fruit shalt thou destroy from the earth, and their seed from among the children of men.

**TDB77:** Leitung panin a suan a khakte, mi’ tate lak panin amau’ tate na susia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Leitung panin a suan a khakte, mi' tate' lak panin amau' tate na susia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** For they intended evil against thee: they imagined a mischievous device, which they are not able to perform.

**TDB77:** Na langah siat nading vaihawm a, gitlohna a bawl-te leh, tangtung lo ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma langah siatna ding vaihawmin, gitlohna a bawl-te hangin tangtunglo ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** Therefore shalt thou make them turn their back, when thou shalt make ready thine arrows upon thy strings against the face of them.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute na taisak ding hi; na thalte tawh a maitang-te na ngim ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute na taisak ding a, na thal tawh a maitang-te na ngim ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v13
**EN:** Be thou exalted,

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na hatna hangin nang hong kipahtawi ta hen! Nangma vangliatna la sa-in kong phat ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na hatna hangin nang hong kipahtawi ta hen! Nangma vangliatna la sa-in ka hong phat ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
