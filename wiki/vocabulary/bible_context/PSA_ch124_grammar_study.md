## Verses

### v1
**EN:** If it had not been the

**TDB77:** Ei lam-ah Topa, hong pang lo hi leh, Israelte in tu-in ci ta-te hen—

**ZVS:** Israel mite aw, { Topa } in hong pan'pihlo hi leh i tuah ding thu thei un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo

### v2
**EN:** If it had not been the

**TDB77:** Mihingte ei lang pangin hong thawh ciangin ei lamah Topa hong pang lo hi leh,

**ZVS:** Eite langpangin mite hong kipat ciangin eite' lamah { Topa } hong panglo hi leh,

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v3
**EN:** Then they had swallowed us up quick, when their wrath was kindled against us:

**TDB77:** ei tunga amau hehna a kisosuah ciangin amaute in eite a hingin hong nawmvalh khin ding-te hi;

**ZVS:** eite' tungah a hehna-te a kisosuah ciangin amaute in eite a hingin hong nawmvalh khin ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Then the waters had overwhelmed us, the stream had gone over our soul:

**TDB77:** tuikhang in eite hong taikhia in, tuihualte in hong tummang khin ding a,

**ZVS:** Tuikhang in eite hong tai-in, tuihual in hong tum khin ding a,

### v5
**EN:** Then the proud waters had gone over our soul.

**TDB77:** tuipi husiate in hong tummang khin ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** Blessed be the

**TDB77:** Amau’ suksiat dingin eite a hong piakhia lo Topa in thupha ngah ta hen!

**ZVS:** Amaute' suksiat dingin eite a hong piakhialo { Topa } in thupha ngah ta hen.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v7
**EN:** Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: the snare is broken, and we are escaped.

**TDB77:** Thangsiahte thangzak panin vasa a suahtak bangin eite i suakta hi; thangkhau kibottat a, eite i suakta khin ta hi.

**ZVS:** Thangsiahte' thangzak panin vasa a suahtak bangin eite i suakta hi. Thangkhau kibottat a, eite i suakta khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Our help is in the name of the

**TDB77:** Van leh lei a bawl Topa’ tung panin eite hong kihuhna hong pai hi.

**ZVS:** Van leh lei a bawl { Topa' } tung panin eite huhna hong pai hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
