**EN:** Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity!

### v2
**EN:** It is like the precious ointment upon the head, that ran down upon the beard, even Aaron's beard: that went down to the skirts of his garments;

**TDB77:** Tua in, a puanpi ngawng tunga Aaron’ khamul kaisuk zui-a a taaksuk, a lu tunga sathau manpha tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Tua pen a puan ngawng tungah Aaron' khamul kaisuk zui-in a lu tung panin a taaksuk, sathau manpha tawh kibang hi.

### v3

**ZVS:** Tua pen Zion mualte tungah a kia, Hermon mual pana hong piang daitui tawh kibang hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } in nuntak tawntungna thupha tua munah a pia hi.
