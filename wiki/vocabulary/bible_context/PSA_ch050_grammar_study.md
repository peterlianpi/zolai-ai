## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The mighty God, even the

**TDB77:** Vanglianpa, Topa ahi Pasian, hong pau a, nisuahna leh nitumna kikal leitung khempeuh a khawm hi.

**ZVS:** Vanglian, { Topa } Pasian hong paukhia a, nisuahna leh nitumna kikal leitung mi khempeuh a khawm hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty, God hath shined.

**TDB77:** Hoih kicin’na, Zion panin, Pasian hong taangkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Hoih kicin'na Zion panin Pasian hong taangkhia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Our God shall come, and shall not keep silence: a fire shall devour before him, and it shall be very tempestuous round about him.

**TDB77:** Pasian hong pai hi, amah dai om lo hi, ama mai-ah mei husia om a, a kimkotah huihpi om hi.

**ZVS:** Eite' Pasian hong pai a, amah dai ommawk lo hi. Ama mai-ah mei husia om a, a kimkotah huihpi om hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** He shall call to the heavens from above, and to the earth, that he may judge his people.

**TDB77:** A mite thu a khen theih nadingin tunglam vantung leh leitung a sam hi.

**ZVS:** A mite tungah thu a khen theihna dingin tunglam vantung leh leitung a sam hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** Gather my saints together unto me; those that have made a covenant with me by sacrifice.

**TDB77:** “Kei tawh biak piakna-in thuciamna a bawlte, ka micitakte ka kiangah hong khawm un!”

**ZVS:** “Biakpiakna tawh, kei tungah thuciamna a bawlte, ka mi citakte ka kiangah hong khawm un,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** And the heavens shall declare his righteousness: for God is judge himself. Selah.

**TDB77:** Vantung in ama thumanna gen hi, bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian mahmah thukhenpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Vantung in ama thumanna gen hi; bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian mahmah thukhenpa ahi hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v7
**EN:** Hear, O my people, and I will speak; O Israel, and I will testify against thee: I am God, even thy God.

**TDB77:** “Ka mite aw, ngai un, thu kong gen ding hi, Israelte aw, note’ lang khatah teci ka pang ding hi. Kei in Pasian ka hi a, nomau’ Pasian ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka mite aw, ngai un; thu ka hong gen ding hi. Israel-te aw, note' langkhatah teci ka pang ding hi. Kei pen Pasian ka hi a, nomau' Pasian ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** I will not reprove thee for thy sacrifices or thy burnt offerings, to have been continually before me.

**TDB77:** Na biak piakna uh ah note kong mawhsak kei hi; na meihal biaknate uh ka mai-ah om ngitnget hi.

**ZVS:** Na biakpiakna uhah note kong mawhsak kei hi. Na meihal biaknate uh ka mai-ah om ngitnget hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v9
**EN:** I will take no bullock out of thy house, nor he goats out of thy folds.

**TDB77:** Na inn pan uh-a bawngtal hita leh, na ganbuk pan uh-a keeltal hita leh kong sang kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin na inn pan uha bawngtal hita leh na ganbuk pan uha keeltal hita leh ka hong sang kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v10
**EN:** For every beast of the forest is mine, and the cattle upon a thousand hills.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh gamlak ganhingte khempeuh, mual tul tampite’ tunga ganhingte keima-a’ ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh gamlak ganhingte khempeuh, mual tul tampite tunga a om ganhingte keima-a' ahi hi.

### v11
**EN:** I know all the fowls of the mountains: and the wild beasts of the field are mine.

**TDB77:** Huih lak vasate khempeuh ka thei a, gam lak-a gamta khempeuh keima aa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Huih lak vasate khempeuh keimah in ka thei a, gamlaka gamta khempeuh keima-a' ahi hi.

### v12
**EN:** If I were hungry, I would not tell thee: for the world is mine, and the fulness thereof.

**TDB77:** Ka gil kial taleh zong, no kiangah kong gen kei ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh leitung leh a sunga om khempeuh keima aa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka gil kial ta leh zong note kiang panin an kong ngen kei ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh leitung leh a sunga om khempeuh keima-a' ahi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Will I eat the flesh of bulls, or drink the blood of goats?

**TDB77:** Bawngtalte’ sa keimah in ne ding ka hi hiam, keelte’ si dawn ding ka hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bawngtalte' sa ne ding a, keelte' sisan dawn ding ka hi hiam?

### v14
**EN:** Offer unto God thanksgiving; and pay thy vows unto the most High:

**TDB77:** Lungdam biakpiakna Pasian’ tungah pia un la, Sang Penpa tungah na thuciamnate uh bawl un;

**ZVS:** Lungdam biakpiakna Pasian tungah pia un la, Sangpenpa tungah na thuciamnate uh bawl un.

### v15
**EN:** And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

**TDB77:** haksat hun ciangin kei tungah hong ko un; note kong honkhia ding a, note in kei nong thangsak ding uh hi.”

**ZVS:** Haksat hun ciangin keima tungah hong ko un. Note kong honkhia ding a, note in keima min nong thangsak ding uh hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v16
**EN:** But unto the wicked God saith, What hast thou to do to declare my statutes, or that thou shouldest take my covenant in thy mouth?

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin migilote tungah Pasian in hih bangin ci hi: “Ka thukhamte lawh nading, ka thuciamna na muk uh tawh gen nading note in bang thu nei na hi uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin migilote tungah Pasian in thu gen a, “Ka thukhamte lawhna ding ka thuciamna na muk uh tawh genna ding thu nei na hi uh hiam?

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v17
**EN:** Seeing thou hatest instruction, and castest my words behind thee.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh note in hilhna na mudah uh a, ka thute na nunglam uhah na pai uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh hilh ding na ut kei uh a, ka thute na nunglam uhah na paii uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v18
**EN:** When thou sawest a thief, then thou consentedst with him, and hast been partaker with adulterers.

**TDB77:** Guta khat mu le uhcin ama lawm, na hi pah uh a, numei-pasal mawhna a bawlte tawh na kikhawl uh hi.

**ZVS:** Guta khat mu le uh cin ama lawm na hi pah uh a, a paktat mite tawh na kikhawl uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v19
**EN:** Thou givest thy mouth to evil, and thy tongue frameth deceit.

**TDB77:** Na kam uh, siatna na uksak uh a, na leii un kheemna khang sak hi.

**ZVS:** “Na kam uh, siatna na uksak uh a, na leii un khemna a khangsak hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v20
**EN:** Thou sittest and speakest against thy brother; thou slanderest thine own mother's son.

**TDB77:** Na tu uh a, na sanggamte langpangin thu na gen uh hi; amaute’ mawhna na zong uh hi.

**ZVS:** Na sanggamte uh langpangin thu na gen uh a, amaute' mawhna na zong uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v21
**EN:** These things hast thou done, and I kept silence; thou thoughtest that I was altogether such an one as thyself: but I will reprove thee, and set them in order before thine eyes.

**TDB77:** Hihte khempeuh na gamta khin uh a, bangmah ka pau kei hi; kei pen no tawh a kibangin nong ngaihsun uh hi. Ahih hangin tu in note kong tai a, na mawhna uh na mai uh-ah ka koih hi.

**ZVS:** Hihte khempeuh na gamta khin uh a, bangmah ka pau kei hi. Kei pen no tawh a kibangin nong ngaihsunkha uh hi. Ahih hangin tu-in note ka hong taii a, na mawhna uh na mai uhah ka koih hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v22
**EN:** Now consider this, ye that forget God, lest I tear you in pieces, and there be none to deliver.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin, Pasian a mangngilh mite aw, hih thu ciamteh un, tua hi kei leh note kong balgawp ding hi, hong honkhia ding kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** “Tua ahih ciangin Pasian a mangngilh mite aw, hih thu ciamteh un. Tua hi kei leh note kong balgawp ding hi. Note hong honkhia ding kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | negation: lo

**TDB77:** Biakpiak nadingin lungdam piakna hong paipih mi in kei hong pahtawi ahi hi; Ka thu a mang mite khempeuh ka honkhia ding hi,”

**ZVS:** Biakpiakna dingin lungdam piakna hong paipih mi in kei a hong pahtawi ahi hi. Ka thu a mang mite khempeuh ka honkhia ding hi,” a ci hi.
