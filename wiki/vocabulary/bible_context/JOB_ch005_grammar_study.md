## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Call now, if there be any that will answer thee; and to which of the saints wilt thou turn?

**TDB77:** Tu-in na thu ko in; nang hong huh ding om ahi hiam? A siangthote kua pen’ lam zuan ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Tu-in na thu ko in: Nang hong huh ding om ahi hiam? A siangthote kua pen' lam zuan ding na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** For wrath killeth the foolish man, and envy slayeth the silly one.

**TDB77:** Mihaipa in ama hehna sih lawh a, muhdahna in sihna piangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaipa in a hehna sihlawh a, muhdahna in sihna piangsak hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v3

**TDB77:** Mihaipa in lim takin zung kha ngei napi, ama inn thakhatin kizunglot ka mu ngei hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaipa in limtakin zung kha ngei napi, ama inn thakhatin a kizunglot ka mu ngei hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** His children are far from safety, and they are crushed in the gate, neither is there any to deliver them.

**TDB77:** A tapate kuama suahtaksak zawh ding hi nawn lo hi; kulh kongpi gei-ah amaute kisugawp a, amaute a honkhia ding kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** A tapate suahtaksak zawh ding hi nawnlo hi. Kulhkongpi gei-ah amaute kisugawp a, amaute a honkhia ding kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5

**ZVS:** Midangte in a neihsate liahtumsak-te a, amah sangin a hatzawpa in a um a beel sunga a omte nangawn laksak a, a neih a lam duhgawhin a deihgawh hi.

### v6

### v9
