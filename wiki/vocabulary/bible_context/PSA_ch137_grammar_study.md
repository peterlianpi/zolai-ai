### v1
**EN:** By the rivers of Babylon, there we sat down, yea, we wept, when we remembered Zion.

**TDB77:** Zion khuapi i ngaihsut khak ciangin Babilon luite gei-ah eite tusukin i kapkap hi.

**ZVS:** Zion khuapi i ngaihsut khak ciangin Babylon luite gei-ah eite tusukin i kapkap hi.

**TDB77:** Tua lai-a gialhek kungte tungah i laiza lasaknate i khai hi.

### v3
**EN:** For there they that carried us away captive required of us a song; and they that wasted us required of us mirth, saying, Sing us one of the songs of Zion.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ei hong man mite in la i sak ding hong deih a, ei hong bawlsiate in i gualnop ding hong deihin, “Zion late khat hong sak un!” hong ci uh hi.

**ZVS:** bang hang hiam cih leh ei hong man mite in la i sak ding hong deih a, ei hong bawlsiate in i gualnop ding hong deihin, “Zion late khat hong sak un,” hong ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** If I forget thee, O Jerusalem, let my right hand forget her cunning.

**TDB77:** Jerusalem khuapi aw, nang kong mangngilh taak leh ka taklam khut bui ta hen!

**ZVS:** Jerusalem khuapi aw, nang kong mangngilh taak leh ka taklam khut buisum ta hen!

### v6
**EN:** If I do not remember thee, let my tongue cleave to the roof of my mouth; if I prefer not Jerusalem above my chief joy.

**TDB77:** Nang kong phawk kei a, kei’ lungdamna a lianpen sangin Jerusalem a sangzaw in kong koih kei taak leh ka dang tawngah ka leii toncip ta hen!

**ZVS:** Nang kong phawk kei a, kei' lungdamna a lian pen sangin Jerusalem a sangzaw-in kong koih kei taak leh ka dang tawngah ka leii toncip ta hen!

### v7
**EN:** Remember, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, Jerusalem a siat ni-a Edom mite in, “Sumai un, sumai un! A bulpi dongin sumai un!” a cih vialna uh phawk in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, Jerusalem a siat hun lai-in Edom mite in, “Sumai un, sumai un! A bulpi dongin sumai un!” a cih vialna uh phawk in.

### v8
**EN:** O daughter of Babylon, who art to be destroyed; happy shall he be, that rewardeth thee as thou hast served us.

**TDB77:** Mi a susia, Babilon aw, kote tunga nong bawl bangin nang tungah hong thukpa in lungdamna ngah ta hen!

**ZVS:** Misusia Babylon aw, kote' tungah nong bawl bangin nang tungah hong thukpa in lungdamna ngah ta hen!

### v9
**EN:** Happy shall he be, that taketh and dasheth thy little ones against the stones.

**TDB77:** Na taneute hong laksakin suangtumpi den’na-a hong neihsakpa in lungdamna ngah ta hen!

**ZVS:** Na taneute hong laksakin suangtumpi den'na-in a hong neihsakpa in lungdamna ngah ta hen!
