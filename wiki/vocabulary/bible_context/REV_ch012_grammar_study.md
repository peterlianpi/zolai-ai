**EN:** And there appeared a great wonder in heaven; a woman clothed with the sun, and the moon under her feet, and upon her head a crown of twelve stars:

**TDB77:** Vantungah lamdang lian khat a kilang hi. Numei khat in ni silhin, kha in ama khe nuai-ah omin, aksi sawm leh nih a tuang kumpilukhu a lu tangah a khu hi.
