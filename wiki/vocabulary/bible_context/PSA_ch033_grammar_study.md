# Psalms Chapter 33 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 21 verses

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 6 |
| purpose: in order to | 3 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |
| negation: lo | 2 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Rejoice in the

**TDB77:** No thuman mite aw, Topa tungah lungdam un! Pahtawina in thutangte tawh kilawm kituak hi.

**ZVS:** No thuman mite aw, { Topa } tungah lungdam un! Pahtawina pen thutangte tawh kilawm kituak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Praise the

**TDB77:** Laiza khausai lasakna tawh Topa phat un la, harp zang sawm khausai lasakna tawh ama tungah la ngaih sa un!

**ZVS:** Laia khausai lasakna tawh { Topa } phat un la, harp zang sawm khausai lasakna tawh ama tungah la ngaih sa un.

### v3
**EN:** Sing unto him a new song; play skilfully with a loud noise.

**TDB77:** Ama tungah la thak sa un la, a gingin hoih takin khausai tum un.

**ZVS:** Ama tungah la thak sa un la, a gingpha mahmahin hoihtakin khausai tum un.

### v4
**EN:** For the word of the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa’ thu tang a, ama hihna khempeuh thuman tawh sem hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } thutang a, ama hihna khempeuh thuman tawh a sem hi.

### v5
**EN:** He loveth righteousness and judgment: the earth is full of the goodness of the

**TDB77:** Amah in thumanna leh thutan’na deih a, leitung in Topa’ itna kip tawh kidim hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in thuman leh thutang deih a, leitungah { Topa' } itna kip a kidim hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** By the word of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ kampauna tawh vantung piang a, ama kam pan-a husan’na tawh vantung nate a piang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } kampauna tawh vantung piang a, a kam pana husan'na tawh vantung nate a piang hi.

### v7
**EN:** He gathereth the waters of the sea together as an heap: he layeth up the depth in storehouses.

**TDB77:** Amah in tuipi tuite thawlkhat sunga thun bangin khawm a, tui thukpite tuibem-ah koih hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in tuipi tuite thawl khat sunga thun bangin mun khatah gawm a, tuithukpite tuibeem-ah a koih hi.

### v8
**EN:** Let all the earth fear the

**TDB77:** Topa pen leitung khempeuh in zahtak uh hen la, leitung a teengte khempeuh in amah lamdang sakna tawh ding hen!

**ZVS:** Leitung khempeuh in { Topa } zahtak uh hen la, leitunga tengte khempeuh, amah lamdangsakna tawh ding ta uh hen!

### v9
**EN:** For he spake, and it was done; he commanded, and it stood fast.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah pau a, hong piang hi; thu pia a, hong om hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah pau a, leitung hong piang hi; amah in thu pia a, na khempeuh hong om hi.

### v10
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in minamte’ thu ngaihsutna a mawkna suah a, mite’ geelna khaktan hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in minamte' thungaihsutna a mawkna suahin, mite' geelna a khaktan hi.

### v11
**EN:** The counsel of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ thu ngaihsutna kip ngitnget a, a lungsim ngaihsutna khang tawntungin kip hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } thungaihsutna kip ngitnget a, ama lungsim ngaihsutna khang tawntungin a kip hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Blessed is the nation whose God is the

**TDB77:** Topa pen Pasian-a a nei minam, ama luah dingin teelna a ngah minam in thupha ngah ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } Pasian-a a nei minam, ama luah dingin teelna a ngah minamte thupha ngah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v13
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in vantung panin hong ensuk a, mi’tapate khempeuh a mu hi;

**ZVS:** { Topa } in vantung panin hong ensuk a, mihing khempeuh a mu hi.

### v14
**EN:** From the place of his habitation he looketh upon all the inhabitants of the earth.

**TDB77:** a tutna mun panin amah hong dakkhia-in, leitung mi khempeuh a en hi;

**ZVS:** A tutna mun panin amah hong dakkhia-in, leitung mite a en hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v15
**EN:** He fashioneth their hearts alike; he considereth all their works.

**TDB77:** amau lungsim khempeuh a bawlpa in, amau gamtatna khempeuh en gige hi.

**ZVS:** Amau' lungsim a bawlpa in amaute' gamtatnate en gige hi.

### v16
**EN:** There is no king saved by the multitude of an host: a mighty man is not delivered by much strength.

**TDB77:** A galkap tampi in kumpi khat suakta sak zo lo a, galkap hangpa ama hatna tawh kihonkhia zo lo hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpi khat a galkap tampite in suaktasak zolo a, galkap hangpa ama hatna tawh kihonkhia zolo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** An horse is a vain thing for safety: neither shall he deliver any by his great strength.

**TDB77:** Sakol galkap in gualzawh nadingin a mawkna lametna hi a, ama hatna tawh hon zo lo hi.

**ZVS:** Sakol galkapte pen gualzawhna dingin a mawkna lametna hi a, amau' hatna tawh hon zolo hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo

### v19
**EN:** To deliver their soul from death, and to keep them alive in famine.

**TDB77:** Amah a zahtakte, a itna kip lametna a neite, sihna panin honkhia-in, kialpi hun sungin amaute a hin'kep theihna dingin { Topa } in amaute en tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20
**EN:** Our soul waiteth for the

**TDB77:** Topa i ngak hi; amah in eimah huhpa leh i lum ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } i ngak hi; amah pen eite hong huhpa leh i lum ahi hi.

### v21
**EN:** For our heart shall rejoice in him, because we have trusted in his holy name.

**TDB77:** Hi hi, ama min siangtho muang i hih manin ama sungah i lungdam hi.

**ZVS:** Ama min siangtho eite in muang ihih manin ama sungah i lungdam hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v22
**EN:** Let thy mercy, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nang kong lamet uh mah bangin na itna kip ko tungah hong omsak in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nang kong lamet uh mah bangin kote' tungah na itna kip hong omsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
