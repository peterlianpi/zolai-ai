## Verses

### v1
**EN:** So I returned, and considered all the oppressions that are done under the sun: and behold the tears of such as were oppressed, and they had no comforter; and on the side of their oppressors there was power; but they had no comforter.

**TDB77:** Leitungah thuman lo-a a kigamtatnate khempeuh ka ngaihsun kik leuleu hi. Nenniamna a thuak mite kap uh a, ahi zongin kuamah in amaute hehnem lo hi. A nengniam mite thahat lua uh ahih manin kuamah in thuk ngam lo hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin leitungah thuman loin a kigamtatnate khempeuh ka ngaihsun kik leuleu hi. Nenniamna a thuak mite kap uh a, ahi zongin kuamah in amaute hehnemlo uh hi. A nengniam mite thahat lua uh ahih manin kuamah in thuk ngamlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** Wherefore I praised the dead which are already dead more than the living which are yet alive.

**TDB77:** A hing lai mite sangin a si khinsa mite hampha ka sa zaw hi.

**ZVS:** A hinglai mite sangin a si khinsa mite hamphazaw ka sa hi.

### v3
**EN:** Yea, better is he than both they, which hath not yet been, who hath not seen the evil work that is done under the sun.

**TDB77:** Mihing-a hong piang nai lo, hih leitung gitlohna teng a mu nai lote hampha zawlai sawnsawn hi.

**ZVS:** Mihinga hong piang nailo, hih leitung gitlohna teng a mu nailote hamphazaw lai sawnsawn uh hi.

### v4
**EN:** Again, I considered all travail, and every right work, that for this a man is envied of his neighbour. This is also vanity and vexation of spirit.

**TDB77:** Mi khat leh khat, hauh nop man-a kidemna hangin nakpi-a na kisem ahihna ka mu hi. Hih zong a mawknapi hi a, huihpi nung delh mah tawh kibang bek hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khatlekhat, hauhnop mana kidemna hangin nakpi taka na kisem ahihna ka mu hi. Hih zong a mawknapi hi a, huihpi nungdelh mah tawh kibang bek hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** The fool foldeth his hands together, and eateth his own flesh.

**TDB77:** Mihaite in na sem loin a khut zepin om uh a, a tawpna-ah gil kialin sih lawh uh hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite in na sem loin a khut zepin om uh a, a tawpna-ah gilkialin sihlawh uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Better is an handful with quietness, than both the hands full with travail and vexation of spirit.

**TDB77:** Nakpi-in na sem a, a mawknapi ahi ding khut nih dim sangin, lungsim nuam-a khut khat dim neihna hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Nakpi takin na sem a, a mawknapi ahi ding, khut nih dim sangin lungsim nuamin khut khat dim neihna hoihzaw hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** Then I returned, and I saw vanity under the sun.

**TDB77:** Nuntakna sungah a mawknapi ahi thudang khat ka mu leuleu hi.

### v8
**EN:** There is one alone, and there is not a second; yea, he hath neither child nor brother: yet is there no end of all his labour; neither is his eye satisfied with riches; neither saith he, For whom do I labour, and bereave my soul of good? This is also vanity, yea, it is a sore travail.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in a tapa ahi a, sanggam ahi zongin nei lo napi-in, a tawntungin nasemin a neihsa tawh lungkim hun nei thei lo hi. Kua adingin gimpi-in na sem a, a ut utin ta hoh lo ahi hiam? Hih in zong a mawknapi mah, a dahhuai nuntakna khat ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat in tapa ahi a, sanggam ahi zongin neilo napi-in, a tawntungin na semin a neihsa tawh lungkim hun nei theilo hi. Kua-a' dingin gimpi-in na sem a, a ututin ta hoh lo ahi hiam? Hih zong a mawknapi mah, a dahhuai nuntakna khat ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | negation: lo

### v9
**EN:** Two are better than one; because they have a good reward for their labour.

**TDB77:** Mi khat sangin mi nih hoihzaw hi; amau’ sepna thaman ngah uh hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat sangin mi nih hoihzaw hi; amau' sepna thaman a tamzaw ngah uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** For if they fall, the one will lift up his fellow: but woe to him that is alone when he falleth; for he hath not another to help him up.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh khat cimawh leh a khat in huh a, mi khat guak pen cimawh zenzen leh a huh ding om lo ahih manin a dahhuai pha mahmah ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh khat cimawh leh a khat in huh a, mi khat guak pen cimawh zenzen leh a huh ding omlo ahih manin a dahhuai pha mahmah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | negation: lo | causal: because

### v11
**EN:** Again, if two lie together, then they have heat: but how can one be warm alone?

**TDB77:** Khua a dam ciangin mi nih lumkhawm leh khua lum hi; khat guak bangci khua lum thei ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Khua a dam ciangin mi nih lumkhawm leh khua lum hi; khat guak bangci khualum theih ding ahi hiam?

### v12
**EN:** And if one prevail against him, two shall withstand him; and a threefold cord is not quickly broken.

**TDB77:** Mi khat bek hi leh zawh nop kisa a, nih pha leh kizo zo lo hi. Zang thum-a kiheek khauhual bawhsat hak hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat bek hi leh zawhnop kisa a, nih pha leh kizo zolo hi. Zang thum-a kiheek khauhual bawhsat hak hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Better is a poor and a wise child than an old and foolish king, who will no more be admonished.

**TDB77:** Mi’ thugen a sang thei nawn lo, teek leh hai a thuah kumpipa sangin, zawng napi-in a pil tangval khat hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Mi' thugen a sang thei nawnlo, teek leh hai a thuah kumpipa sangin zawng napi-in a pil tangval khat hoihzaw hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v14
**EN:** For out of prison he cometh to reign; whereas also he that is born in his kingdom becometh poor.

**TDB77:** Mi khat pen thong panin suakta-in kumpi suak thei a, zawng napi-in ama gam sungah kumpi suak thei hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat pen thong panin suakta-in kumpi suak thei a, zawng napi-in ama gamsungah kumpi suak thei hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v15
**EN:** I considered all the living which walk under the sun, with the second child that shall stand up in his stead.

**TDB77:** Hih leitungah a teeng mi khempeuh’ thu ka ngaihsun a, tua mite lak panin kumpipa’ za a luah ding khangno pawlkhat a omna ka phawk hi.

**ZVS:** Hih leitungah mi khempeuh' thu ka ngaihsun a, tua mite lak panin kumpipa' za a luah ding khangno mi khat a omlam ka phawk hi.

### v16
**EN:** There is no end of all the people, even of all that have been before them: they also that come after shall not rejoice in him. Surely this also is vanity and vexation of spirit.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa’ uk mite sim zawh lohin tam a, ahi zongin amah a sih ciangin a nasepna hangin a lungdam khat beek om lo hi. Hih in a mawkna taktak, huihpi nung delh bang bek ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa' uk ding mite simzawh lohin tam a, ahi zongin amah a sih ciangin ama nasepna hangin a lungdam khat beek omlo hi. Hih pen a mawkna taktak, huihpi nungdelh bang bek ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo
