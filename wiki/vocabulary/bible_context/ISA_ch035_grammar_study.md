## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The wilderness and the solitary place shall be glad for them; and the desert shall rejoice, and blossom as the rose.

**TDB77:** Gamlak leh lei keuna gam lungdam ding a, sehnel gam lungdamin palhkhia ding hi; unok pak bangin

**ZVS:** Gamlak leh lei keuna gam lungdam ding a, sehnel gam lungdamin palhkhia ding hi. Unok pak bangin

**Grammar:** future: will

### v2
**EN:** It shall blossom abundantly, and rejoice even with joy and singing: the glory of Lebanon shall be given unto it, the excellency of Carmel and Sharon, they shall see the glory of the

**TDB77:** tampi takin palhkhia ding a, lung nopna leh lasakna tawh lungdam ding hi. Lebanon mual a minthan’na, Karmel mual leh Sharon mual, a min liatna ama tungah kipia ding hi. Amaute in Topa’ minthan’na, i Pasian’ liatna mu ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** tampi takin palhkhia ding a, lungnopna leh lasakna tawh lungdam ding hi. Lebanon mual a minthan'na, Karmel mual leh Sharon mual a minliatna tua leitang in ngah ding hi. Mi khempeuh in { Topa}' minthan'na, i Pasian' liatna mu ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Strengthen ye the weak hands, and confirm the feeble knees.

**TDB77:** A zaw khutte khauhsak un la, a no khukte kipsak un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Say to them that are of a fearful heart, Be strong, fear not: behold, your God will come with vengeance, even God with a recompence; he will come and save you.

**TDB77:** Lungsim lauthawng mite kiangah, “Thakhauh un, lau kei un! En un, na galte uh tungah phu hong laksak dingin Pasian hong pai ding a, amah in note hong honkhia ding hi,” ci un.

**ZVS:** Lungsim patau-in, a lauthawng mite kiangah, “Thakhauh un, lau kei un! En un, na galte uh tungah phu hong laksak dingin Pasian hong pai ding a, amah in note hong honkhia ding hi,” ci un.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin mittawte’ mit kihong ding a, bilngongte’ bil kihong ding hi;

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin mittawte' mit kihong ding a, bilngongte' bil kihong ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Then shall the lame man leap as an hart, and the tongue of the dumb sing: for in the wilderness shall waters break out, and streams in the desert.

**TDB77:** tua ciangin khebai mipa pen sakhi bangin diangin lam ding a, a pau thei lo mipa in lungdamin la sa ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh gamlakah tui hong phulkhia ding a, sehnel gam sungah lui tui hong luang ding hi;

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin khebai mipa diangin lam ding a, a pau theilo mipa in lungdamin la sa ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh gamlakah tui hong phulkhia ding a, sehnel gamsungah luitui hong luang ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will

### v7
**EN:** And the parched ground shall become a pool, and the thirsty land springs of water: in the habitation of dragons, where each lay, shall be grass with reeds and rushes.

**TDB77:** a sa mahmah sehnelte bualtui hong suak ding a, a keugaw leitang pen ciktui tawh hong kidim ding hi; ngiate’ omna ngei leitang pen nawngpi suakin, lopa, pumpeng leh mau po ding hi.

**ZVS:** A sa mahmah sehnelte bualtui hong suak ding a, a keugaw leitang pen ciktui tawh hong kidim ding hi. Ngiate' omna ngei leitang pen nawngpi suakin, lopa, pumpeeng, leh mau, a po ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** And an highway shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way of holiness; the unclean shall not pass over it; but it shall be for those: the wayfaring men, though fools, shall not err therein.

**TDB77:** Tua lai-ah lam lianpi khat om ding a, Lampi Siangtho kici ding hi; a siang lo mite in tua tung tawn ngei lo ding a, mi haite in tua lam a zui mite khialsak nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua lai-ah lam lianpi khat om ding a, Lampi Siangtho kici ding hi. A sianglo mite in tua tung tawn ngeilo ding a, mihaite in tua lam a zui mite khialsak nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** No lion shall be there, nor any ravenous beast shall go up thereon, it shall not be found there; but the redeemed shall walk there:

**TDB77:** Tua lai-ah humpinelkai om lo ding a, sasialne ganhing khat peuhpeuh hong pai lo ding hi; tua lai-ah ganhing lauhuaite kimu lo ding hi. Topa’ tatkhiatsa mite in tua lampi tawnin hong ciah ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua lai-ah humpinelkai omlo ding a, sasialne ganhing khat peuhpeuh hong pailo ding hi. Tua lai-ah ganhing lauhuaite kimu lo ding hi. { Topa}' hotkhiatsa mite in tua lampi tawnin hong ciah ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10

**TDB77:** Topa’ tatkhiatsa mite hong ciah kikin, lungdam tawntungna mai pua-in lasa kawmin Jerusalem hong tung ding uh hi; amaute in lungdamna leh kipahna ngah ding uh a, dahna leh khuisatna om nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa}' hotkhiatsa mite hong ciahkikin, lungdam tawntungna mai pua-in la sa kawmin Jerusalem-ah hong tung ding uh hi. Amaute in lungdamna leh kipakna ngah ding uh a, dahna leh khuisatna om nawnlo ding hi.
