## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Have mercy upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude of thy tender mercies blot out my transgressions.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangma itna kip om bangin kei hong hehpih in la, na hehpihna lian om bangin ka thu palsatnate hong phiatkhiat sak in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nangma itna kip om bangin kei hong hehpih in la, na hehpihna lian om bangin ka thu palsatnate hong phiatkhiatsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Wash me thoroughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin.

**TDB77:** Ka khialhna panin kei hong silsiang kiuhkeuh in la, ka mawhna panin hong siangsak in!

**ZVS:** Ka khialhna panin hong silsiang kiuhkeuh in la, ka mawhna panin kei hong siangsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my sin is ever before me.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ka thu palsatnate ka thei a, ka mawhna ka mai-ah om ngitnget hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ka thupalsatnate ka thei a, ka mawhnate khempeuh ka phawk ngitnget hi.

### v4
**EN:** Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.

**TDB77:** Nangma tung mahmah-ah khialin, na muhna-ah a sia ka gamta khin hi, tua ahih ciangin na thugenna-ah nang na tang a, na thukhenna ah paubaan’na om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma tungah khialin, nangma muhna-ah a sia ka gamta khin hi. Tua ahih ciangin na thugenna-ah nang na thutang a, thu na hong khenna-ah pauna ding bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | intensifier: very/greatly | completed: already done | negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me.

**TDB77:** En in, a khialsa mahin kei ka suakkhia a, ka nu in hong paai cil-a kipan a mawhsa ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** A khialsa mahin kei ka suak a, ka nu in a gil sungah hong paii tunga kipan a mawhsa ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts: and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom.

**TDB77:** En in, nang in nuntakna sungah thumanna na deih hi; tua ahih ciangin ka lungsim tawngah ciimna hong hilh in.

**ZVS:** Nang in nuntakna sungah thumanna na deih hi. Tua ahih ciangin ka lungsim tawngah ciimna hong hilh in.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v7
**EN:** Purge me with hyssop, and I shall be clean: wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.

**TDB77:** Puknah tawh hong nul in, kei ka siang ding hi; hong sil in, vuk sangin ka ngozaw ding hi.

**ZVS:** Puknah tawh hong nul in; kei ka siang ding hi. Hong sil in; vuk sangin ka ngozaw ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** Make me to hear joy and gladness; that the bones which thou hast broken may rejoice.

**TDB77:** Lungdamna leh kipahna hong pia in la, na sukgawpsa mipa lungdamsak in.

**ZVS:** Lungdamna leh kipahna kei tungah hong pia in la, na hong sukgawp mipa hong lungdamsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Hide thy face from my sins, and blot out all mine iniquities.

**TDB77:** Ka mawhna na mai panin seel in la, ka khialhna khempeuh hong phiatkhiat sak in.

**ZVS:** Ka mawhna khempeuh na mai panin seel in la, ka khialhna khempeuh hong phiatkhiatsak in.

### v10
**EN:** Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka sungah lungsim siangtho hong piangsak in la, a thak, a man, ngaihsutna ka sungah hong guan in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, keima sungah lungsim siangtho hong piangsak in la, a thak a man ngaihsutna ka sungah hong guan' in.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v11
**EN:** Cast me not away from thy presence; and take not thy holy spirit from me.

**TDB77:** Na omna panin kei hong paikhia kei in la, kei kiang panin na Kha Siangtho la kei in.

**ZVS:** Nangma omna panin kei hong paiikhia kei in la, keima kiang panin na kha siangtho lakhia kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Restore unto me the joy of thy salvation; and uphold me with thy free spirit.

**TDB77:** Na hotkhiatna hangin lungdamna kei hong ngahsak kik in la, utna lungsim tawh kei hong huh in.

**ZVS:** Nong hotkhiatna hangin hong lungdamsak kik in la, utna lungsim tawh keimah hong huh ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; and sinners shall be converted unto thee.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin thu palsatte na zia ka hilh ding a, mimawhte nangma lamah hong kihei kik ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin thupalsatte na zia na tong ka hilh ding a, nangma lamah mimawhte hong kiheikik ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou God of my salvation: and my tongue shall sing aloud of thy righteousness.

**TDB77:** Pasian, kei hong hon Pasian aw, sih nading daan panin kei hong honkhia in; nong hotkhiatna thu lungdam takin ka sa ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei a hong hon Pasian aw, sihna ding panin kei hong honkhia in. Nong hotkhiatna thu lungdam takin ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v15
**EN:** O Lord, open thou my lips; and my mouth shall shew forth thy praise.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka pau theih nadingin hong huh in; nangmah ka hong phat ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka pau theihna dingin kei hong huh in; nangmah ka hong phat ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v16
**EN:** For thou desirest not sacrifice; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt offering.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ganhing tawh biakna na deih kei hi, meihal biakna nang hong pia hi ta-leng zong, nang na lungdam kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ganhing tawh biakna na deih kei hi. Meihal biakna hong pia hita leng zong, na lungdam kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v17
**EN:** The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit: a broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ san’ theih biakna in lungsim kitamzan hi; Pasian aw, a kitamzan, a kisik lungsim na simmawh kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' san' theih biakna pen lungsim kitamzan ahi hi. Pasian aw, a kitamzan, a kisik lungsim na simmawh kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18

**TDB77:** Na lungkimna pha tawh Zion tungah a pha bawl in; Jerusalem kulhpite ciang kik in,

**ZVS:** Na lungkimna pha tawh Zion tungah a pha bawl in la, Jerusalem kulhpite ciangkik ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** Then shalt thou be pleased with the sacrifices of righteousness, with burnt offering and whole burnt offering: then shall they offer bullocks upon thine altar.

**TDB77:** tua ciangin, biakpiakna man, meihal biakna leh meihal biakpiakna bup-ah na lungkim ding hi; tua ciangin na biakna tau tungah bawngtalte hong kipia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin biakpiakna man, meihal biakna leh meihal biakpiakna bupah na lungkim ding a, na biakna tau tungah bawngtalte hong kipia ding hi.
