# Psalms Chapter 143 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 12 verses

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |

| purpose: in order to | 3 |
| causal: because | 3 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Hear my prayer, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka thungetna hong za in la, ka thuumnate hong ngai in! Na thumanna leh na thutakna tawh kizui-in kei hong dawng in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka thungetna hong za in la, kong thumnate hong ngai in. Na thumanna leh na thutakna tawh kizui-in kei hong dawng in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** And enter not into judgment with thy servant: for in thy sight shall no man living be justified.

**TDB77:** Na nasempa, thu hong sit kei in; bang hang hiam cih leh nangma mai-ah mihing a dik kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Na nasempa, thu hong sit kei in. Bang hang hiam cih leh nangma mai-ah mihing a dikzo kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v3
**EN:** For the enemy hath persecuted my soul; he hath smitten my life down to the ground; he hath made me to dwell in darkness, as those that have been long dead.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh gal in kei hong bawlsia a, hong zogawp hi; sau veipi-a a site bangin a mial sungah hong tusak hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh gal in kei hong bawlsia-in hong zogawp hi. Sauveipi lai-a a site bangin a mial sungah hong tusak hi.

### v4
**EN:** Therefore is my spirit overwhelmed within me; my heart within me is desolate.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ka cih nading ka thei nawn kei a, ka lungtang-ah si teeng lo hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin cihna ding ka thei nawn kei a, ka lungtangah si teng nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** I remember the days of old; I meditate on all thy works; I muse on the work of thy hands.

**TDB77:** Hunluite ka phawk a, nangma sepsate khempeuh ka ngaihsunsun hi; na khut in a sepsa nate ka lungngaingai hi.

**ZVS:** Hun luite ka phawk a, na sepsa khempeuh ka ngaihsunsun hi. Na khut in a sepsa nate ka lungngaingai hi.

### v6
**EN:** I stretch forth my hands unto thee: my soul thirsteth after thee, as a thirsty land. Selah.

**TDB77:** Thu ngenin nang lamah ka khut kong zan a, leikeu in tui a duh bangin nang kong duh hi.

**ZVS:** Thu ngenin nang lamah ka khutte ka hong zan a, lei keu in tui a duh bangin nang ka hong duh hi.

### v7
**EN:** Hear me speedily, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kei hong dawng mengmeng in! Kei ka cimawh ta hi! Leihawm sung misite kiang-a paisukte tawh ka kibat loh nadingin kei kiang panin na maitang seel kei in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kei hong dawng mengmeng in. Kei ka cimawh ta hi. Leihawm sung misite kiangah a paisukte tawh ka kibat lohna dingin kei kiang panin na maitang seel kei in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Cause me to hear thy lovingkindness in the morning; for in thee do I trust: cause me to know the way wherein I should walk; for I lift up my soul unto thee.

**TDB77:** Nangmah hong muang ka hih manin na itna kip zingsangin kei hong zasak in. Nangma tungah thu hong ngen ka hih manin, ka pai nading lampi hong hilh in.

**ZVS:** Nang hong muang ka hih manin zingsang simin na itna kip kei hong phawksak in. Nangma tungah thu hong ngen ka hih manin ka paina ding lampi hong hilh ta in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Deliver me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka langte’ kiang panin kei hong honkhia in! Nangmah beel dingin kong tai hi!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka langte' kiang panin kei hong honkhia in. Nangmah a bel dingin ka hong tai hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Teach me to do thy will; for thou art my God: thy spirit is good; lead me into the land of uprightness.

**TDB77:** Nang in keima Pasian na hih manin nangma deih banga gamta dingin kei hong hilh in! Kei hong hehpih in la, lamphei-ah hong kha in.

**ZVS:** Keima Pasian na hih manin nangma deih bangin a gamta dingin kei hong hilh in. Kei hong hehpih in la, lam phei-ah hong kha in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | causal: because

### v11
**EN:** Quicken me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangma min na ngaihsutna-in keima nuntakna hong kem in! Nangma thumanna tawh kituakin haksatna panin kei hong paikhiatpih in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangma min na ngaihsutna-in keima nuntakna hong kem in. Nangma thumanna tawh kituakin haksatna panin hong paikhiatpih in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** And of thy mercy cut off mine enemies, and destroy all them that afflict my soul: for I am thy servant.

**TDB77:** Kei pen nangma nasem ka hih manin na itna kip tawh kituakin ka galte khenkhia in la, ka langte khempeuh susia in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix
