# Psalms Chapter 52 — Grammar Study

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 3 |
| future: will | 3 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Why boastest thou thyself in mischief, O mighty man? the goodness of God endureth continually.

**TDB77:** Mi thahatpa aw, bang hangin miphate suksiatna-ah kiphasak na hi hiam? Sunthapai-in nang in suksiatna na ngaihsun hi.

**ZVS:** Mi thahatpa aw, bang hangin miphate suksiatna-ah kisial na hi hiam? Sunthapai-in, suksiatna na ngaihsunsun hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Thy tongue deviseth mischiefs; like a sharp razor, working deceitfully.

**TDB77:** Zuau nasempa aw, na leii in mulkiatna temhiam khat tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Zuau nasempa aw, na leii a hiam pha mahmah mulkiatna tem tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Thou lovest evil more than good; and lying rather than to speak righteousness. Selah.

**TDB77:** Nang in hoihna sangin siatna na deihzaw a, thuman genna sangin zuauna na deihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Hoihna sangin siatna na deihzaw a, thuman genna sangin zuauna na deihzaw hi.

### v4
**EN:** Thou lovest all devouring words, O thou deceitful tongue.

**TDB77:** Mi khem theipa aw, a siasak thei kammal khempeuh na deih hi.

**ZVS:** Mi a khem theipa aw, a siasak thei kammal khempeuh deih belin na nei hi.

### v5
**EN:** God shall likewise destroy thee for ever, he shall take thee away, and pluck thee out of thy dwelling place, and root thee out of the land of the living. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Pasian in nang hong susia paisuak ding hi; amah in nang hong bawhin na buk panin hong kaikhia ding hi; a hingte’ leitang panin nang hong zunglot ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Pasian in nang hong susia paisuak ding hi. Amah in nang hong bawhin na buk sung panin hong kaikhia ding a, a hingte' leitang panin hong zunglot ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** The righteous also shall see, and fear, and shall laugh at him:

**TDB77:** Thuman mite in mu-in, lau ding-te a, amah nuihsan-in,

**ZVS:** Thuman mite in nang hong mu-in, lau mahmah ding-te a, nang hong nuihsanin,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Lo, this is the man that made not God his strength; but trusted in the abundance of his riches, and strengthened himself in his wickedness.

**TDB77:** “Pasian a beel lo, ama hauhnate a muang, ama hauhna a beelpa en un!” ci ding hi.

**ZVS:** “Pasian a bello, ama hauhnate a muang, ama hauhna a belpa en un,” hong ci ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** But I am like a green olive tree in the house of God: I trust in the mercy of God for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin kei ka hih leh Pasian’ inn sunga oliv kung hing tawh ka kibang hi. Pasian’ itna kip ka muang ngitnget hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin kei ka hih leh Pasian' innsunga a om oliv kung a hing tawh ka kibang hi. Pasian' itna kip ka muang ngitnget hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** I will praise thee for ever, because thou hast done it: and I will wait on thy name; for it is good before thy saints.

**TDB77:** Hih bang bawl khin na hih manin, na tungah ka lungdam ngitnget ding hi. Thuhoih ahih manin, miphate’ omna-ah na min ka taangko ding hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bangin keimah hong bawlkhin na hih manin nangma tungah ka lungdam ngitnget ding hi. Thuhoih ahih manin miphate omna-ah nangma min ka taangko ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because
