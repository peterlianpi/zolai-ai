## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.

**TDB77:** Keimah in mual tung lam ka ento hi. Keimah huhna koi lai pan hong pai ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Keimah in mualtung lam ka ento hi. Keimah huhna koi lai pan hong pai ding ahi hiam?

### v2
**EN:** My help cometh from the

**TDB77:** Van leh lei a bawl Topa kiang panin keima huhna hong pai hi.

**ZVS:** Van leh lei a bawl { Topa' } kiang panin keimah huhna hong pai hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not slumber.

**TDB77:** Amah in ka khe taalsak lo ding a, kei hong kempa lusu lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in ka khe taalsaklo ding a, kei hong kempa lusulo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v4
**EN:** Behold, he that keepeth Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep.

**TDB77:** En un, Israel a kempa pen lusu lo a, zong ihmu lo hi.

**ZVS:** En un, Israel a kempa lusulo a, zong ihmulo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in nangmah hong cing ding a, nangmah hong hu dingin na kiangah hong om hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in nang hong cing ding a, nangmah a hong hu dingin na kiangah hong om hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** The sun shall not smite thee by day, nor the moon by night.

**TDB77:** Sun-in ni in nang hong gimsak lo ding a, zan-in kha in nang hong sukha lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Sunin ni in nang hong gimsaklo ding a, zanin kha in zong nang hong sukhalo ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v7
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in, siatna khempeuh panin nang hong hu ding a, amah in a muanhuai-in nang hong kem ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in siatna khempeuh panin nang hong hu ding a, amah in a muan'huai-in nang hong kem ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v8
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in, tu-a kipan a tawntungin na pusuahna na sungtumna-ah nang hong kem ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in tu-a kipan a tawntungin na vaksuk na vaktohna-ah nang hong kem ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
