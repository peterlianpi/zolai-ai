## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Who is as the wise man? and who knoweth the interpretation of a thing? a man's wisdom maketh his face to shine, and the boldness of his face shall be changed.

**TDB77:** Mipilte bek in na akhiatna taktak theithei uh hi. Pilna in mipilte maitaisak a, a hehmai bei sak hi.

**ZVS:** Mipilte bek in na a khiatna taktak theithei uh hi. Pilna in mipilte maitaisak a, a hehmai a beisak hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** I counsel thee to keep the king's commandment, and that in regard of the oath of God.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa hong sawl bangin gamta in la, Pasian’ tungah thu mawkciam pak kei in.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa' hong sawl bangin gamta in la, Pasian' tungah thu mawk ciampak kei in.

### v3
**EN:** Be not hasty to go out of his sight: stand not in an evil thing; for he doeth whatsoever pleaseth him.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa in a ut peuhpeuh hih thei ahih manin, ama kiang panin paikhia in la, ama lungkim lohna hih teitei kei in.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa in a ut peuhpeuh hih thei ahih manin ama kiang panin paikhia in; ama lungkimlohna hih teitei kei in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Where the word of a king is, there is power: and who may say unto him, What doest thou?

**TDB77:** Kumpipa in aana neihna tawh gamta hi; ama gen bangin gamta in; kuamah in amah nial zo lo hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa aana neihna tawh gamta hi. Ama gen bangin gamta in; kuamah in amah nial zolo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v5
**EN:** Whoso keepeth the commandment shall feel no evil thing: and a wise man's heart discerneth both time and judgment.

**TDB77:** Ama thu a mangte in haksakna tuak kha lo ding hi; mipilte in a gamtat nading zia leh a hun hoih thei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Ama thu a mangte in haksatna tuakkha lo ding hi. Mipilte in a gamtatna zia leh a hun hoih thei uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Because to every purpose there is time and judgment, therefore the misery of man is great upon him.

**TDB77:** Na khatpeuh i hih ding ciangin a kilawm hun, leh a ngeina man om a, ahi zongin eite in bangmah i thei kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** For he knoweth not that which shall be: for who can tell him when it shall be?

**TDB77:** A hong piang ding thu i thei kei a, a hong gen thei zong kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** A hong piang ding thu i thei kei a, a hong gen thei zong kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v8
**EN:** There is no man that hath power over the spirit to retain the spirit; neither hath he power in the day of death: and there is no discharge in that war; neither shall wickedness deliver those that are given to it.

**TDB77:** A sih ding a pelh thei, a sih ding hun a phiat thei kuamah om lo hi. Gal a om ciangin gal launa a pelh thei, siatna sungah a lut ciangin a peng thei kuamah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** A sih ding a pelhthei, a sih ding hun a phiatthei kuamah omlo hi. Gal a om ciangin gallauna a pelhthei, siatna sungah a lut ciangin a pengthei kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v9
**EN:** All this have I seen, and applied my heart unto every work that is done under the sun: there is a time wherein one man ruleth over another to his own hurt.

**TDB77:** Leitunga gamtatnate ka ngaihsut ciangin, hih thute ka mukhia hi. Mi khat in midang tungah vangliatna nei-in, a uk sung mite a gimsak gawp hun om hi.

**ZVS:** Leitunga gamtatnate ka ngaihsut ciangin hih thute ka mukhia hi. Mi khat in midang tungah vangliatna nei-in, a uk sung mite a gimsak gawp hun a om hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** And so I saw the wicked buried, who had come and gone from the place of the holy, and they were forgotten in the city where they had so done: this is also vanity.

**TDB77:** Migilote si-in kivui a, a siat gamtatna khua sunga mite mah in han panin a ciah ciangun amah a phat vevena uh ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote si-in kivui a, a siat gamtatna khuasunga om mite mah in han panin a ciah ciangun amah a phat vevena uh ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Because sentence against an evil work is not executed speedily, therefore the heart of the sons of men is fully set in them to do evil.

**TDB77:** A mawhte kidaantat pahpah lo ahih manin, mihingte in khialh ding haksa sa lo uh hi.

**ZVS:** A mawhte gim kipia pahpahlo ahih manin mihingte in khialhding haksa salo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** Though a sinner do evil an hundred times, and his days be prolonged, yet surely I know that it shall be well with them that fear God, which fear before him:

**TDB77:** Mawhnei khat in zavei dong siatna bawl napi-in, a khan’ sau veve hi. Ahi zongin, “Pasian a zahtakte adingin, thu khempeuh nuam ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mawhnei khat in zavei dong siatna bawl napi-in a khan' sau veve hi. Ahi zongin “Pasian a zahtakte-a' dingin thu khempeuh nuam ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | future: will

### v13
**EN:** But it shall not be well with the wicked, neither shall he prolong his days, which are as a shadow; because he feareth not before God.

**TDB77:** Migilote’ adingin, thu nuam lo ding a, amaute in Pasian zahtak lo uh ahih manin, a nuntak hun uh nilim bangin bei pak ding a, khan’sih ding uh hi,” kici hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote-a' dingin thu nuamlo ding a, amaute in Pasian zahtaklo uh ahih manin a nuntak hun uh nilim bangin beipak ding a, khan'sih ding uh hi,” kici hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** There is a vanity which is done upon the earth; that there be just men, unto whom it happeneth according to the work of the wicked; again, there be wicked men, to whom it happeneth according to the work of the righteous: I said that this also is vanity.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin hih thugenna a mawknapi ahi hi. Leitunga thupiangte ngaihsun dih in: khatveivei midikte’ in migilote’ daantuah ding teng tuak zawsop a, migilote in midikte’ thaman ngah ding teng ngah zawsop uh hi. Hih thu in a hai vai, a mawkna taktak ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin hih thugenna a mawknapi ahi hi. Leitunga thupiangte ngaihsun dih in: Khat veivei midikte in migilote' gimthuak dingteng thuak zawsop a, migilote in midikte' thaman ngah dingteng ngah zawsop uh hi. Hih thu pen a haivai, a mawkna taktak ahi hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** Then I commended mirth, because a man hath no better thing under the sun, than to eat, and to drink, and to be merry: for that shall abide with him of his labour the days of his life, which God giveth him under the sun.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin mihing in hih leitungah nuam a sak theih ding adang om lo ahih manin, an ne tui dawnin nuamsa-in a om ding bek ahi hi. Pasian in hih leitungah nuntak a piak sung teng, a sep thaman a ngah theih ding hih bek om hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin mihing in hih leitungah nuam a sak theih ding adang omlo ahih manin an ne tui dawnin nuamsa-in a om ding bek ahi hi. Pasian in hih leitungah nuntak a piak sungteng a sep thaman a ngah theih ding hih bek om hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | causal: because

### v16

### v17
**EN:** Then I beheld all the work of God, that a man cannot find out the work that is done under the sun: because though a man labour to seek it out, yet he shall not find it; yea further; though a wise man think to know it, yet shall he not be able to find it.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ nasepna i theih zawh lohna ka phawk hi. Mihingte in bangzahin i hahkat hangin kithei khia zo lo hi. Mipilte in thei a kisak hangun, bangmah thei lo uh hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' nasepna i theihzawh lohna ka phawk hi. Mihingte in bangzahin i hahkat hangin kitheikhia zolo hi. Mipilte in thei a kisak hangun bangmah theilo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
