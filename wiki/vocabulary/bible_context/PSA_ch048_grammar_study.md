## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Great is the

**TDB77:** Topa lian hi, i Pasian khuapi sungah a mual siangtho sungah nakpi taka phat ding ahi hi!

**ZVS:** { Topa } lian a, i Pasian' khuapi sungah nakpi takin phat ding ahi hi.

### v2
**EN:** Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth, is mount Zion, on the sides of the north, the city of the great King.

**TDB77:** Kumpi lianpa’ khuapi, lei lulam-a om ama mual siangtho hoih-a a sang mahmah Zaphon in Zion Mual hi a, leitung khempeuh’ lungdamna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpi lianpa' khuapi, leilu lama a om ama mual siangtho hoihin a sang mahmah Zion mual pen leitung khempeuh' lungdamna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v3
**EN:** God is known in her palaces for a refuge.

**TDB77:** A muan’huaina tua mual-ah Pasian in amah a hu’ taktakpa ahihna a lak khin hi.

**ZVS:** A muan'huai tua mualah amah a hu taktakpa ahihna Pasian in a lakkhin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v4
**EN:** For, lo, the kings were assembled, they passed by together.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin, en un, kumpite kikaikhawm in, hong paikhawm-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin, kumpite kikaikhawmin, a sim dingin hong kuankhawm-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** They saw it, and so they marvelled; they were troubled, and hasted away.

**TDB77:** Amaute in tua a muh phetun lamdang sa a, amaute dipkua vatin tai-te hi;

**ZVS:** Amaute in Zion a muh phetun lamdangsa mahmah-te a, dipkua vatin tai-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Fear took hold upon them there, and pain, as of a woman in travail.

**TDB77:** numei nausuak na a sa bangin amaute liinggawp-te hi.

**ZVS:** Numei nausuak na a sa bangin amaute linggawp-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish with an east wind.

**TDB77:** Nisuahna huihpi tawh Tarshis kici teembaw liante nang na susia hi.

**ZVS:** Nisuahna huihpi tawh Tarshish kici teembaw liante nang na susia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the

**TDB77:** Ka zaksa bangun, mi honte’ Topa’ khuapi sung, i Pasian’ khuapi sungah, Pasian in a phuh kip ngitngetsa ka mu-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ka zaksa bangun Vanglian { Topa' } khuapi, i Pasian' khuapi, Pasian in a phuhkip ngitngetna ka mu khin-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God, in the midst of thy temple.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, na biakinn sungah nangma itna kip ka ngaihsun-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** According to thy name, O God, so is thy praise unto the ends of the earth: thy right hand is full of righteousness.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, na min in leimong dong a zelh bangin, nangmah hong kiphatna in zong a zel hi. Na taklam khut in gualzawhna tawh a kidim hi;

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, na min in leimong dong a zelh bangin, nangmah hong kiphatna in zong a zel hi. Na taklam khut gualzawhna tawh a kidim hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Let mount Zion rejoice, let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of thy judgments.

**TDB77:** Zion Mual lungdam sak in! Na thukhenna hangin Judah’ mite lungdam sak in!

**ZVS:** Zion Mual lungdamsak in. Na thukhenna hangin Judah mite lungdamsak in.

### v14
**EN:** For this God is our God for ever and ever: he will be our guide even unto death.

**TDB77:** Hih pen eima Pasian hi a, a tawntungin eima Pasian hi, ci-in khangkikte tungah na gen theihna dingun Zion kiimvelin pai-in, a tausang phazah sim un la, a kulh phungmai a hoih mahmahte ngaihsunin, a phualpi muan'huaite va nawk kim un. Amah pen a tawntungin lam hong lak dingpa ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix
