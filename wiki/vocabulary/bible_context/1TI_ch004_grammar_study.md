# 1 Timothy Chapter 4 — Grammar Study

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|

| future: will | 3 |
| purpose: in order to | 3 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |
| quotative: said/saying | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Now the Spirit speaketh expressly, that in the latter times some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to seducing spirits, and doctrines of devils;

**TDB77:** Kha Siangtho in a tel taka a genkholh inah, a tawpna hun ciangin,

**ZVS:** Nidang ciangin a upna panun mi pawlkhat pialin, zuauthu gente leh dawi thu a kigente a zuihna ding uh Kha Siangtho in teltakin a gen hi.

### v2
**EN:** Speaking lies in hypocrisy; having their conscience seared with a hot iron;

**TDB77:** Tua bang zuau thuhilhnate pen zuau genin mi a khem, sik emsa tawh tatgawp bangin a lungsim un a hoih leh a hoihlo a khenthei nawnlote' kam panin hong gingkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3
**EN:** Forbidding to marry, and commanding to abstain from meats, which God hath created to be received with thanksgiving of them which believe and know the truth.

**TDB77:** Tua bang mite in zi neih leh pasal neih ding, leh an pawlkhat nek ding hoihlo hi, ci uh hi. Ahi zongin thu umin, thuman thutak a thei mite in lungdamna ko-in a nek dingin Pasian in tua ante a bawl ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** For every creature of God is good, and nothing to be refused, if it be received with thanksgiving:

**TDB77:** Pasian’ bawlsa khempeuh a pha hi a, hehpihna phatin a kisan’ leh, bangmah tan’ ding a om kei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' bawlsa na khempeuh a hoih hi a, nialloin, lungdamna pulakin san' ding ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** For it is sanctified by the word of God and prayer.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, Pasian’ thu tawh ahi zongin, thungetna tawh ahi zongin a kisiangthosaksa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' thu leh thungetna hang tawh tuate a hoih a suak ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** If thou put the brethren in remembrance of these things, thou shalt be a good minister of Jesus Christ, nourished up in the words of faith and of good doctrine, whereunto thou hast attained.

**TDB77:** Hih nate nang in ute naute kiangah na hilh leh, upna thu leh tu dongin nang na zuih thuhilhna a hoih tawh kivakin, Khazih Zeisu’ nasem hoihpa na hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nang in na zuihsa upna thu leh thuhilhna man na zuih kawmin hih ka hong thugente, i sanggamte kiangah na gen nak leh Khrih Jesuh' nasem a hoih khat na hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** But refuse profane and old wives' fables, and exercise thyself rather unto godliness.

**TDB77:** Numei teekte in a gen theih uh ahi mawkna tangthute paaikhia in. Pasian biakna lamah nuamsa a, na om nadingin na kiseksak in.

**ZVS:** Pasian' thu tawh a kituaklo, a haihuai tangthu phuahtawmte ngaihsutin na nei vet kei in. Pasian' deihna tawh a kituakin na nuntak zawhna dingin na kisin in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** For bodily exercise profiteth little: but godliness is profitable unto all things, having promise of the life that now is, and of that which is to come.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, pumpi ading kiseksakna in phattuamna tawmkha a pia thei hi; Pasian biakna lamah nopsakna in tu laitak nuntakna leh tu nung ciangin a piang ding nuntakna tawh kipawl thu kiciamna ngah a, na khempeuhah phattuamna hong pia thei hi,

**ZVS:** Pumpi kisekin kisinna manpha hi. Ahi zongin kha thu-ah kisekin kisinna pen tuhun laitak leh a hong tungding hunah nuntakna hong piathei ahih manin manphazaw lai hi,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** This is a faithful saying and worthy of all acceptation.

**TDB77:** a cihna kam in thuman thutak hi a, a vekpi-in san’ ding a kilawm kam zong ahi hi.

**ZVS:** ci-a kigen pen a man taktak thu hi a, umin sin dingin a kilawm thu ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** For therefore we both labour and suffer reproach, because we trust in the living God, who is the Saviour of all men, specially of those that believe.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin mi khempeuh Gumpa ahi, a um mite a man takpi a gum, a nungta Pasian, eite in muangin i um a, hahkatin semin, simmawhna zong i thuak hi.

**ZVS:** A diakdiakin ei thu umte in mi khempeuh a Honkhia, a nungta Pasian muangin lamen ihih manin nakpi takin hanciamin na a sem ihi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v11
**EN:** These things command and teach.

**TDB77:** Hih thute vaikhak in la, hilh in.

**ZVS:** Ka hong gensa thute na hilhsawn in.

### v12
**EN:** Let no man despise thy youth; but be thou an example of the believers, in word, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in faith, in purity.

**TDB77:** Khangno lai na hih hanginah kuamah kisimmawhsak kei in. Thugenna, gamtat ngaihsutna, midang itna, upna, siangthona sung pan a um mite ading limpha na suak in.

**ZVS:** Khangno lai na hihna thu hangin mite' simmawh dingin na om kei in la, kampauna, gamtatna, itna, upna leh siangthona-ah thu um mite' etteh dingin na om in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Till I come, give attendance to reading, to exhortation, to doctrine.

**TDB77:** Ka hong pai matengin Lai Siangtho simin, mi hansuahna leh thuhilhnate hahkatin om in.

**ZVS:** Kei ka hong pai matengin mipi mai-ah Lai Siangtho simna, a khiatna genna, leh hanthotnate-ah tavuan na la in.

### v14
**EN:** Neglect not the gift that is in thee, which was given thee by prophecy, with the laying on of the hands of the presbytery.

**TDB77:** Genkholhna tawh, pawlpi upate’ khutngakna tawh nang hong kipia thupha letsong zang loin om kei in.

**ZVS:** Kamsangte' thugennate leh upate in na tungah a khut uh nga-in thu hong ngetsakna ahi nangma hihtheih siamnate na valkhongsak kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** Meditate upon these things; give thyself wholly to them; that thy profiting may appear to all.

**TDB77:** Na khan’tohna mi khempeuh mai-ah a kilat nadingin hih nate deihpha in la, hahkat in.

**ZVS:** Mi khempeuh in na khan'tohna hong muhtheihna dingin hih thute limtakin ngaihsun in la, hahkatin na sem in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** Take heed unto thyself, and unto the doctrine; continue in them: for in doing this thou shalt both save thyself, and them that hear thee.

**TDB77:** Nangmah ahi zongin, na thuhilhna ahi zongin, kikeem in la, na om in. Hih nate-ah na kip tawntung in. Tua bang na hih leh, nangmah ahi zongin, na thu a ngaite ahi zongin gupna na ngahsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na gamtatna leh na thuhilhnate-ah hoihtakin kidawmin om lecin nangmah-a kipan na thugen a za mite-a' dingin phattuamna omding ahih manin na nasepna-ah hoihtakin om in.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
