## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My soul is weary of my life; I will leave my complaint upon myself; I will speak in the bitterness of my soul.

**TDB77:** “Ka nuntakna ka cimtak ta hi; ka dahna thu ka ut utin ka gen ding a, ka lungkham manin ka paupau ding hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka nuntakna ka cimtak mahmah ta hi. Ka dahna thu ka ut utin ka gen ding a, ka lungkham manin ka paupau ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v2
**EN:** I will say unto God, Do not condemn me; shew me wherefore thou contendest with me.

**TDB77:** Pasian kiangah hih bangin ka ci ding hi: Kei hong mawhsak kei in; kei nong langpan’na thu hong gen in.

**ZVS:** Pasian kiangah hih bangin ka ci ding hi: Kei hong mawhsak kei in. Kei nong langpan'na thu hong gen in.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Is it good unto thee that thou shouldest oppress, that thou shouldest despise the work of thine hands, and shine upon the counsel of the wicked?

**TDB77:** Na khut tawh na sepsate simmawh bawlin, migilote’ vaihawmna pan’pihin, hih bangin nong netniamna-ah bang phattuam ngah na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Na khut tawh na sepsate simmawh bawlin, migilote' vai pan'pihin, hih bangin nong netniamna-ah bang phattuamna ngah na hi hiam?

### v4
**EN:** Hast thou eyes of flesh? or seest thou as man seeth?

**TDB77:** Mihingte bang lelin gamta na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Mihing bang lelin gamta na hi hiam?

### v5
**EN:** Are thy days as the days of man? are thy years as man's days,

**TDB77:** Na nuntakna kote’ aa tan bang lelin ngaihsun na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Na nuntak hun tan kote' nuntak tan bang lelin ngaihsun na hi hiam?

### v6
**EN:** That thou enquirest after mine iniquity, and searchest after my sin?

**TDB77:** Bang hangin ka mawhna khempeuh sulzuih-in, ka khialhna khempeuh kantel na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang hangin ka mawhna hong sulzuihin, ka khialhna khempeuh hong kantel na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** Thou knowest that I am not wicked; and there is none that can deliver out of thine hand.

**TDB77:** Ka mawh lohna na thei a, nang kiang panin kei hong hon ding a om lohna na theihsa mah ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka mawhlohna na thei a, nang kiang panin kei hong hon ding a omlohna na theihsa mah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Thine hands have made me and fashioned me together round about; yet thou dost destroy me.

**TDB77:** Nangma khut in kei hong lomin nong piangsak a, tu-in nangma khut mah in kei hong leh suksiat kik hi.

**ZVS:** “Nangma khut tawh kei hong lomin na hong piangsak a, tu-in nangma khut mah in kei hong lehsuksiat hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Remember, I beseech thee, that thou hast made me as the clay; and wilt thou bring me into dust again?

**TDB77:** Leivui panin kei nong bawlna phawkkik in; leivui mah-in hong dengvui kik ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Leivui pana kei nong bawlna hong phawkkik in. Leivui mahin hong dengvui kik takpi ding na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Hast thou not poured me out as milk, and curdled me like cheese?

**TDB77:** Kei ka pian’ nadingin ka pa tungah thatang na guan’ a, ka nu’ sungah kei nong khangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Kei ka pian'na dingin ka pa' tungah tha na guan' a, ka nu' sungah kei nong khangsak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v11
**EN:** Thou hast clothed me with skin and flesh, and hast fenced me with bones and sinews.

**TDB77:** Guh leh thaguite tawh ka pumpi ding nong bawl a, sazang leh vun tawh tua guhte nong tuamsak hi.

### v12
**EN:** Thou hast granted me life and favour, and thy visitation hath preserved my spirit.

**TDB77:** Kei tungah nuntakna hong pia-in nong it tawntung a, nangma hong kepna hangin ka nungta hi.

**ZVS:** Kei, nuntakna hong pia-in, nong it tawntung a, nangma hong kepna hangin kei ka nungta hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v13
**EN:** And these things hast thou hid in thine heart: I know that this is with thee.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin a beisa hun sung khempeuh-ah kei adingin thusim khat na na neihna tu-in ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin a beisa hun sung khempeuhah kei-a' ding thusim khat na neihna tu-in ka theita hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v14
**EN:** If I sin, then thou markest me, and thou wilt not acquit me from mine iniquity.

**TDB77:** Ka khialh khakna hong ensim niloh na hih manin ka mawhna nong maisak nuam kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ka khialhkhaknate hong ensim niloh na hih manin ka mawhnate hong maisak nuamlo na hi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | causal: because

### v15
**EN:** If I be wicked, woe unto me; and if I be righteous, yet will I not lift up my head. I am full of confusion; therefore see thou mine affliction;

**TDB77:** Ka khialh phetin gimna nong pia a, ka gamtat hoih ciangin nong pakta tuan kei hi. Keimah in mindaina leh maizumna tawh ka kidim hi.

**ZVS:** Ka khialh phetin gim nong pia pah a, ka gamtat hoih ciangin zong nong pakta tuan kei hi. Mindaina leh maizumna tawh kei ka kidim hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** For it increaseth. Thou huntest me as a fierce lion: and again thou shewest thyself marvellous upon me.

**TDB77:** Mavan’na khatpeuh neisun leng zong, humpinelkai bangin ka siat nading nong zong a, kei’ gim theih nadingin nalamdang nangawn na bawl hi.

**ZVS:** Mavan'na khatpeuh neisun leng zong, humpinelkai bangin ka siatna ding na zong a, kei' gimtheihna dingin na lamdang tuamtuam nangawn na bawl liang hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v17
**EN:** Thou renewest thy witnesses against me, and increasest thine indignation upon me; changes and war are against me.

**TDB77:** Kei a hong langpang dingin teci na nei tawntung a, kei tungah na hehna khang semsemin, kei nong suk nading thu thak na mu tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Kei a hong langpang dingin teci na nei tawntung a, keima tungah na hehna hong khang semsem a, kei nong sukna ding thuthak na mu tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v18
**EN:** Wherefore then hast thou brought me forth out of the womb? Oh that I had given up the ghost, and no eye had seen me!

**TDB77:** “Pasian aw, bang hangin ka nu’ sung pan hong piangsak peuhmah na hi hiam? Kuama’ hong muh ma-in a si hi zaw leng!

**ZVS:** “Pasian aw, bang hangin ka nu' sung panin kei hong piangsak peuhmah na hi hiam? Kuama hong muh ma-in a si hizaw hoh leng!

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v19
**EN:** I should have been as though I had not been; I should have been carried from the womb to the grave.

**TDB77:** Ka nu’ sung pan hankhuk zuat pah ding pen, mihing-a pian’ loh zah mahin a hoih ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka nu' sung pan hankhuk zuat pah ding pen mihinga pian'loh zah mahin a hoih ahi hi.

### v20
**EN:** Are not my days few? cease then, and let me alone, that I may take comfort a little,

**TDB77:** Ka nuntakna bei dektak ta hilo ahi hiam? Hong hici om sak hoh in! Ka hun neih lai sun teng nuamtak hong zangsak in.

**ZVS:** Keima nuntakna bei dektak ta hilo ahi hiam? Hong hici omsak hoh in! Ka hun neih laisun teng nuam takin hong zangsak in.

### v21
**EN:** Before I go whence I shall not return, even to the land of darkness and the shadow of death;

**TDB77:** Kei a pai baih ta ding ka hi a, kong ciahkik ngei kei ding hi, khuamial, lungzin’na gam,

**ZVS:** Kei a pai baihta ding ka hi a, a hong ciahkik ngei ding ka hi kei hi — khuamial, lungzin'na gam,

### v22
**EN:** A land of darkness, as darkness itself; and of the shadow of death, without any order, and where the light is as darkness.

**TDB77:** khuavak nangawn khuamial ahihna, khuamial gam, lungzin’na gam, lungkhamna gam a zuan ta ding ka hi hi,” a ci hi.
