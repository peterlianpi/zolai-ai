## Verses

### v1
**EN:** These things have I spoken unto you, that ye should not be offended.

**TDB77:** “Note na kipuk loh nadingun hih thu kong genkhol hi.

**ZVS:** “Note na pukkhak lohna dingun hih thute a hong gen ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** Then said some of his disciples among themselves, What is this that he saith unto us,

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin, a nungzuite kimkhat in, “Tawlkhat khit ciangin nong mu kei ding-te hi. Tawlkhat khit ciangin na hong mu kik ding-te hi,’ leh ‘Pa kiangah ka pai man-inah, a cihna in bang thu ahi hiam?” ci-in khat leh khat a kici-te hi.

**ZVS:** a nungzuite pawlkhat kidong-te a, “Tawlkhat khit ciangin kei na hong mu nawn kei ding-te a, tawlkhat khit ciangin na hong mukik ding-te hi, a cihna leh Pa kiangah a ciahkik ding ka hi hi, a cihnate bang a cihnopna ahi hiam?” a kici-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v18
**EN:** They said therefore, What is this that he saith,

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin, “Tawlkhat khit ciangin,’ a cihna in bang ahi hiam? Bang a cihnopna hiam ka thei kei-te hi,” ci-in a gen-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tawlkhat a cihcih pen bang a cihnopna ahi hiam, i thei kei hi, a kici-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v19
**EN:** Now Jesus knew that they were desirous to ask him, and said unto them,

**TDB77:** Amaute in a dot nop-te Zeisu in a theih ciangin, “Tawlkhat khit ciangin note in kei nong mu nawn kei ding-te hi. Tawlkhat khit ciangin, note in kei nong mu kik ding-te hi ka cihna thu note na kidong tek-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Jesuh in amaute' theihnop lamtak phawk ahih manin amaute kiangah, “Note khatlekhat na kidot uh, ‘Tawlkhat khit ciangin na hong mu nawn kei ding-te a, tawlkhat khit ciangin na hong mukik ding-te hi,’ ka cihnopna a kidong na hi-te hiam?

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v29
**EN:** His disciples said unto him, Lo, now speakest thou plainly, and speakest no proverb.

**TDB77:** A nungzuite in, “Tu-in thugentehna zang loin a tel takpi-in nong gen hi!

### v30
**EN:** Now are we sure that thou knowest all things, and needest not that any man should ask thee: by this we believe that thou camest forth from God.

**TDB77:** Na khempeuh na thei khin a, kuamah in nang hong dot nading thu a om kei hi ci-in tu-in ka thei-te hi. Tua ahih ciangin nangmah in Pasian’ kiang pan hong pai na hi ci-in ka um-te hi.” a ci-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in na khempeuh na theihkhitna thu ka phawk-te a, ka theihnop-te thute kote in ka hong dot ding-te zong kisam nawnlo ahih manin Pa kiang panin a hong pai na hihna thu ka hong um-te hi,” a ci-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
