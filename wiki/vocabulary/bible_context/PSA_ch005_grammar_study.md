## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Give ear to my words, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kong paunate hong ngai in la, kong thumnate hong thudon in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kong paunate hong ngai in la, ka hong thumnate hong thudon in.

### v2
**EN:** Hearken unto the voice of my cry, my King, and my God: for unto thee will I pray.

**TDB77:** Ka Kumpi leh ka Pasian aw, nangma tungah hong thum ka hih manin ka kikona aw hong ngai in.

**ZVS:** Ka kumpi leh ka Pasian aw, nangma tungah hong thum ka hih manin ka kikona aw hong ngai in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** My voice shalt thou hear in the morning, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, zingsangin ka aw nong za hi; zingsangin nangma adingin biakna bawlin kong ngak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, zingsang simin ka aw nong za a, zingsang simin nangma-a' dingin biakna bawlin kong ngak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** For thou art not a God that hath pleasure in wickedness: neither shall evil dwell with thee.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh gitlohna a deih Pasian na hi kei a, siatna in tawlkhat sung zong nang tawh a omkhawm thei kei hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh gitlohna lamsang a deih Pasian na hi kei a, siatna pen tawlkhat zong nang tawh a omkhawm thei ahi kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v5
**EN:** The foolish shall not stand in thy sight: thou hatest all workers of iniquity.

**TDB77:** A kisatheite na mit tawh kituak lo hi; siatna bawlte khempeuh na mudah hi.

**ZVS:** A kisathei mite nangma mit tawh kituaklo a, siatna a bawlte khempeuh nangmah in na mudah hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Thou shalt destroy them that speak leasing: the

**TDB77:** Zuauthu a gen mite na susia hi; Topa in, mi thah nopna lungsim leh mi kheem nopna lungsim neite mudah hi.

**ZVS:** Zuauthu a gen mite nangmah in na susia hi. { Topa } in mi thahnopna lungsim leh mi khemnopna lungsim a neite a mudah hi.

### v7
**EN:** But as for me, I will come into thy house in the multitude of thy mercy: and in thy fear will I worship toward thy holy temple.

**TDB77:** Kei ahih leh, a kicing na itna kip suangin na inn sungah ka tum ding a, nangmah zahtakna tawh a siangtho na biakinn lam nga-in ka bia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei ahih leh a kicing itna kip suangin na inn sungah ka tum ding a, nangmah zahtakna tawh kunin a siangtho na biakinn lam nga-in kong bia ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Lead me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka galte’ hangin na thumanna tawh hong makaih in la, ka mai-ah na lampi tangsak in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, gal tampi nei ka hih manin na thumanna tawh kei hong makaih in la, keima mai-ah na lampi tangsak in.

### v9
**EN:** For there is no faithfulness in their mouth; their inward part is very wickedness; their throat is an open sepulchre; they flatter with their tongue.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amau sungah thuman om lo hi; amau lungsim ah siatna bek ngaihsun uh a, amau gawlgui in a kihongsa hankhuk tawh kibang hi. A leii uh tawh mawk phat uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amau sungah thuman omlo hi. Amau lungsimah siatna bek ngaihsun uh a, amau' gawlgui pen a kihongsa hankhuk tawh kibang hi. A kam uh tawh mawk phat uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** Destroy thou them, O God; let them fall by their own counsels; cast them out in the multitude of their transgressions; for they have rebelled against thee.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, a mawhna uh amau thuak sak in la, amau thu mah tawh amau puksak in; amaute in nangmah hong lehdo uh ahih manin amau mawhna tampite hangin amau paaikhia in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, a mawhna uh amau mah thuaksak in la, amau' thu mah tawh amaute puksak in. Amaute in nangmah hong lehdo uh ahih manin amau' mawhna tampite hangin amaute paiikhia in.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v11
**EN:** But let all those that put their trust in thee rejoice: let them ever shout for joy, because thou defendest them: let them also that love thy name be joyful in thee.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin nang hong belte khempeuh lungdam sak in la, nuamna la sasak tawntung in; na min a deihte, nangma sungah gualzo-a a lungdam theih nadingin amaute na hu hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin nang hong belte khempeuh lungdamsak in la, nuamna la sasak tawntung in. Nangma min a deihte nangma hangin gualzo-in a lungdam theihna dingun amaute na hu hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v12
**EN:** For thou,

**TDB77:** Topa aw, thuman mi thupha na pia hi; lum tawh dal bangin deihsakna tawh na dal hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, thuman mi thupha na pia a, lum tawh dal bangin deihsakna tawh na dal hi.
