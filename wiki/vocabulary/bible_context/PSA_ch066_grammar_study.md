## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Make a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands:

**TDB77:** Leitung mi khempeuh aw, Pasian tungah lungdamna aw gingpha sak un;

**ZVS:** Leitung mi khempeuh aw, Pasian' tungah lungdamna aw ging phasak un.

### v2
**EN:** Sing forth the honour of his name: make his praise glorious.

**TDB77:** ama minthan’na sa un la, ama tungah minthang phatna pia un!

**ZVS:** Ama minthan'na sa un la, ama tungah minthan'na pia un.

### v3
**EN:** Say unto God, How terrible art thou in thy works! through the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves unto thee.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ tungah hih bang ci un, “Nangma bawlnate bangzah takin dipkuat huai! Na vanglian mahmah ahih manin na mai-ah na galte in nang hong mai-et-te hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' tungah thu gen un la, “Nangma bawlnate bangzahta-in dipkuathuai! Na vang lian mahmah ahih manin na mai-ah na galte in nang hong mai-et-te hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | causal: because | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** All the earth shall worship thee, and shall sing unto thee; they shall sing to thy name. Selah.

**TDB77:** Leitungmi khempeuh in nangmah hong bia-te hi; amaute in nangmah phatna hong sak-te a, nangma phatna hong sak-te hi.”

**ZVS:** Leitung mi khempeuh in nangmah hong bia-te hi. Amaute in nangmah phatna la hong sak-te a, nangma minphatna la hong sak-te hi,” ci-in gen un.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Come and see the works of God: he is terrible in his doing toward the children of men.

**TDB77:** Hong pai un la Pasian’ sepsate mu un; mite’ lak-a a bawlnate ah amah dipkuat-huai hi.

**ZVS:** Hong pai un la, Pasian' sepsate mu un. Mite' laka a bawlnate-ah amah dipkuathuai hi.

### v6
**EN:** He turned the sea into dry land: they went through the flood on foot: there did we rejoice in him.

**TDB77:** Amah in tuipi pen leikeu suaksak a, i pu i pate in khe tawh tuipi kantan-te hi. Tua ama sepna hangin i lung a dam hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in tuipi tui pen leikeu suaksak a, i pu i pate in a khe-te tawh tua tuipi na kantan-te hi. Tua bangin ama nasepna hangin eite i lungdam hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** He ruleth by his power for ever; his eyes behold the nations: let not the rebellious exalt themselves. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ama hatna tawh a uk ngitngetpa, a mit tawh minamte a encik ngitngetpa, mi lehdote in langpan’ kei-te hen.

**ZVS:** Ama thahatna tawh a uk ngitngetpa, ama mit tawh minamte a encik ngitngetpa, mi lehdote in langpan' kei-te hen.

### v9
**EN:** Which holdeth our soul in life, and suffereth not our feet to be moved.

**TDB77:** Mite aw, a hingte laka a hong kempa, i khe a hong taalsak lopa, eite' Pasian phat un. Amah phatna aw kizasak tawntung un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** For thou, O God, hast proved us: thou hast tried us, as silver is tried.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian aw, nangmah in kote nong sin zo hi; mei tawh ngun a kisin bangin kote nong sin khin hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian aw, kote na hong sin khin a, mei tawh ngun a kisin bangin kote na hong sin khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Thou broughtest us into the net; thou laidst affliction upon our loins.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in thong sungah kote nong tung sak a, nangmah in ka nungzang uh-ah vangikpi nong suan hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in ngen sungah kote na hong tungsak a, ka nungzang uhah vangikpi na hong suan hi.

### v12
**EN:** Thou hast caused men to ride over our heads; we went through fire and through water: but thou broughtest us out into a wealthy place.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in ka galte-te nong tuancilsak a, tui leh mei lauhuainate ka thuak-te hi; ahih hangin nangmah in a tanzauna munah nong paipih hi.

**ZVS:** Ka galte-te nong tuancilsak a, tui leh mei lauhuainate ka thuak-te hi. Ahih hangin nangmah in a tanzau munah kote nong paipih hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** Which my lips have uttered, and my mouth hath spoken, when I was in trouble.

**TDB77:** Meihal biaknate tawh nangma inn sungah kei ka hong pai ding hi. Ka lungkham laitakin ka muk pana a gingkhia, ka kama ka thuciamte nangma tungah ka hong pia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v15
**EN:** I will offer unto thee burnt sacrifices of fatlings, with the incense of rams; I will offer bullocks with goats. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ganhing thaute tawh meihal biaknate, tuutal-a biakna khute tawh biakna kong pia ding hi; bawngtalte leh keelte tawh nangmah biak piaknate ka bawl ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ganhing thaute tawh meihal biaknate, tuutal-a biakna khute tawh biakna kong pia ding a, bawngtalte leh keelte tawh nangmah biakpiakna ka bawl ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v16
**EN:** Come and hear, all ye that fear God, and I will declare what he hath done for my soul.

**TDB77:** Pasian zahtak note khempeuh aw, hong pai un la, za un, kei’ adinga ama bawlsate kong gen ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian a zahtakte khempeuh aw, hong pai un la, hong za un. Kei-a' dinga ama bawlsate ka hong gen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v17
**EN:** I cried unto him with my mouth, and he was extolled with my tongue.

**TDB77:** Ama tungah nakpi-in ka kiko a, ka leii tawh amah kiphat hi.

**ZVS:** Ama tungah huhna ngenin nakpi takin ka kiko a, lasakna tawh amah ka phat hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** If I regard iniquity in my heart, the Lord will not hear me:

**TDB77:** Ka lung sungah mawhna kem leng, Topa in ka thu hong ngai lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lung sungah mawhna kem leng, Topa in keima thu hong ngailo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v19
**EN:** But verily God hath heard me; he hath attended to the voice of my prayer.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Pasian in ka thu hong ngai khin taktak hi; amah in ka thungetna aw hong don zo hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin Pasian in, keima thu hong ngai khin taktak a, keima thungetna aw, hong don khinzo hi.

### v20
**EN:** Blessed be God, which hath not turned away my prayer, nor his mercy from me.

**TDB77:** Amah in keima thungetna hong nolh loin, kei kiang panin a itna kip lakhia lo ahih manin Pasian in thupha ngah ta hen!

**ZVS:** Amah in keima thungetna hong nolhloin, keima kiang panin a itna kip lakhialo ahih manin Pasian in thupha ngah ta hen!

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix
