## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Why standest thou afar off, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, bang hanga gamla din’ na hi hiam? Haksat hunin bang hanga nang bu khong na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, bang hangin gamla din' na hi hiam? Haksat hunin bang hangin nang bucip khong na hi hiam?

### v2
**EN:** The wicked in his pride doth persecute the poor: let them be taken in the devices that they have imagined.

**TDB77:** Migilote in kiphatsakna tawh mizawngte uuk delh-te hi; amau ngaihsut vaihawmna mah-ah amaute awk sak in.

**ZVS:** Migilote in kiphatsakna tawh mizawngte uuk delh-te hi. Amau ngaihsut vaihawmna mahah amaute awksak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** For the wicked boasteth of his heart's desire, and blesseth the covetous, whom the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh migilo in a lungsim deihna kiphatsak pih a, ngah nading a huaihampa in, Topa phuisam-in taisan hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh migilo in a lungsim deihna kisaktheihpih a, ngahna dingah a huaihampa in { Topa } phuisamin a taisan hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** The wicked, through the pride of his countenance, will not seek after God: God is not in all his thoughts.

**TDB77:** Migilo in kiphatsakna maisuah tawh amah zong lo a, ama ngaihsutna khempeuh ah, “Pasian om kei,” ci hi.

**ZVS:** Migilo in kiphatsakna lungsim nei a, { Topa } zonglo hi. Ama ngaihsutna khempeuhah, “Pasian om kei,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** His ways are always grievous; thy judgments are far above out of his sight: as for all his enemies, he puffeth at them.

**TDB77:** Ahih nate a ma pai ngitnget hi; amah kiphasak a, na thukhennate ama muhna’ bat zawh lohin sang hi; a galte khempeuh ahih leh amah in nehpiksan lel hi.

**ZVS:** A hihna khempeuhah a ma pai ngitnget hi. Na thukhennate khempeuh ama muhna in a batzawh lohin sang mahmah a, a galte khempeuh ahih leh amah in nehpiksan lel hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** He hath said in his heart, I shall not be moved: for I shall never be in adversity.

**TDB77:** A lungsim sungah, “Kiling ngei kei ning; khang tawntungin kamsiatna tuak kei ning,” ci-in amah in ngaihsun hi.

**ZVS:** A lungsim sungah, “Kiling ngei kei ning; khang tawntungin kamsiatna tuak kei ning,” ci-in a ngaihsun hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** His mouth is full of cursing and deceit and fraud: under his tongue is mischief and vanity.

**TDB77:** Ama kampau in hamsia leh khemna leh netniamna tawh kidim a, siatna leh manlohna thugenin nei hi.

**ZVS:** Ama kampaute pen hamsia, khemna, leh netniamna tawh kidim a, siatna leh manlohna bekbek thugenin a nei tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** He sitteth in the lurking places of the villages: in the secret places doth he murder the innocent: his eyes are privily set against the poor.

**TDB77:** Khua neute sungah mi bukin tu a, munbute-ah mawh nei lote that hi. Ama mit in mi tuahsiate encik sim a,

**ZVS:** Khuaneute sungah mi a buksimin tu a, munbu kawmah mawhnei lote a that hi. Ama mit in mi tuahsiate ensim a,

### v9
**EN:** He lieth in wait secretly as a lion in his den: he lieth in wait to catch the poor: he doth catch the poor, when he draweth him into his net.

**TDB77:** humpinelkai in a kua pan a buk bangin amah in buk sim-in, mizawngte man dingin buk a, mizawngte a ngenthang sungah kai-in man hi.

**ZVS:** humpinelkai in a kuahawm sung pan a buk bangin amah in buksimin, mizawngte a man dingin buk a, a ngenthang sungah mizawngte kai-in a man hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v10
**EN:** He croucheth, and humbleth himself, that the poor may fall by his strong ones.

**TDB77:** A thatang hat thu tawh mi tuahsia mi, sucip-in, hoi-in puk suksak hi.

**ZVS:** A thatanghat thu tawh mi tuahsia mi sucipin a puksuk sak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** He hath said in his heart, God hath forgotten: he hideth his face; he will never see it.

**TDB77:** Ama lungsim sungah, “Hih pen, Pasian in mangngilh hi, a mai seel hi, en lo ding hi,” ci-in ngaihsun hi.

**ZVS:** Ama lungsim sungah, “Hih thute pen Pasian in mangngilh hi; a mai seel hi, enlo ding hi,” ci-in a ngaihsun hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v12
**EN:** Arise, O

**TDB77:** Tho in, Topa! Pasian aw, na khut lamto in la, a thuakte mangngilh kei in.

**ZVS:** Tho in, { Topa}! Pasian aw, na tha hong san in la, a thuakte mangngilh kei in.

### v13
**EN:** Wherefore doth the wicked contemn God? he hath said in his heart, Thou wilt not require it.

**TDB77:** Bang hangin migilo in Pasian simmawh a, a lungsim sungah “Nangmah in thu dongtel lo ding,” ci ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang hangin migilo in Pasian simmawh a, a lungsim sungah, “Hong thei kei na'n teh,” ci ahi hiam?

### v14
**EN:** Thou hast seen it; for thou beholdest mischief and spite, to requite it with thy hand: the poor committeth himself unto thee; thou art the helper of the fatherless.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in na mu hi; hi takpi hi, na khut sungah na koih theih nadingin haksatna leh gimna na ciamteh hi; tuahsia mi in nangma tungah ama pumpi hong ap hi; pa nei lote a huhpa na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in na mu hi. Na huh theihna dingin haksatna leh gimna a thuakte na ciamteh hi. A tuahsia mi in nangma tungah ama pumpi hong ap a, pa neilote a huhpa na hi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v15
**EN:** Break thou the arm of the wicked and the evil man: seek out his wickedness till thou find none.

**TDB77:** Migilo leh siatna bawlte’ ban kuaitan in la, a gitlohnate a bei matengin zong in.

**ZVS:** Giloin siatna a bawlte' khut kuaitan gawp in la, siatna a bawlzo nawnlo dingin gim pia in.

### v16
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in a tawntungin Kumpi hi a, ama leitang panin minamte beimang ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } a tawntungin kumpi hi a, ama leitang panin minam dangte beimang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v18
**EN:** To judge the fatherless and the oppressed, that the man of the earth may no more oppress.

**TDB77:** Pa nei lo leh nen’niamna a thuakte tungah na thutang a, leitung mite in launa tuak kha nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pa neilote leh netniamna a thuakte tungah nang na thutang a, leitung mite in launa tuakkha nawnlo ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo
