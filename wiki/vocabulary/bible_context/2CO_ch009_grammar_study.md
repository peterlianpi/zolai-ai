## Verses

### v1
**EN:** For as touching the ministering to the saints, it is superfluous for me to write to you:

**TDB77:** Misiangthote’ vakna thu note kiangah kong gelh hangin na phattuamna-te om tuam lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Judea gama om Pasian mite huhna ding tawh kisai-in lai kong gelh ding kisam nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** For I know the forwardness of your mind, for which I boast of you to them of Macedonia, that Achaia was ready a year ago; and your zeal hath provoked very many.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, note in huh nopna lungsim na neih-te ka thei hi. Tua huh nopna lungsim hangin ka kisathei hi. “Akhaia gam mite in nikum pekin a khol zo-te hi,” ci-in Makedonia gam mite kiangah ka gen thei hi. Note’ thanopna lungsim in mi tampi a hansuah zo hi.

**ZVS:** Note' huhnopna thu ka theih khitsa hi a, Masedonia gama pawlpite kiangah zong note ka hong kisialhpih khitsa ahi hi. “Akhaia gama pawlpite in ni kum-a kipanin huhdingin a kithawisa-te ahi hi,” ci-in ka na gen hi. Tua banga note' lawpna in amaute tampi tak thanuamsak hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Yet have I sent the brethren, lest our boasting of you should be in vain in this behalf; that, as I said, ye may be ready:

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin note hanga kote’ kisaktheihna in a mawkna suak loin, tua kam mah bangin a kigingkholsa-in na om nading-te ka u ka naute kong sawl hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin note ka hong kisialhpihna-te a mawkna a suahkhak lohna dingin na huhna ding-te na kiging khol un, ka hong cih mah bangin na kigin'kholh theihna dingun ka sanggamte-te ka hong ma paisak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Lest haply if they of Macedonia come with me, and find you unprepared, we (that we say not, ye) should be ashamed in this same confident boasting.

**TDB77:** Tua bang a hih kei leh, Makedonia mi kimkhat in kei tawh hong pai khawmin note a kholsa-a na om loh-te hong mu le-uh note’ zum ding gen loh, lau lo-a kong muan’na sa uhah kote in ka maizum ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang hi kei leh kei ka hong pai ciangin Masedonia gama ka lawmte in hong zuikha zenzen hen la, note a kiging lopi-in a om hong tuakkha leh, note ka hong muan' gen mahmahsa ahih manin note' maizum ding genloh, kote bangzah takin maizum ding ka hi-te hiam?

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Therefore I thought it necessary to exhort the brethren, that they would go before unto you, and make up beforehand your bounty, whereof ye had notice before, that the same might be ready, as a matter of bounty, and not as of covetousness.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin nidang a, kiciamsa note’ sumpi piakna, zawhthawh thu-a piak sum bang hi loin, hehpihna bangin khol kholh nadingin ka u ka naute in no kiangah hong pai masa-in, tua sumpi na kiginkholh nadingun hong hansuah loin om leng, kilawm ka sa kei hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin piakdingin na kamciamsa-te huhna sumte a baihsa-in a omtheihna dingin hih ka sanggamte a hong mapai ding kisam ka sa hi. Tua hi leh kei ka hong tun' ciangin a baihsa-in na om ding a, note in piakloh phamawh cihna lungsim tawh hiloin utna lungsim tawh a pia na hihna-te hong kilang ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** But this I say, He which soweth sparingly shall reap also sparingly; and he which soweth bountifully shall reap also bountifully.

**TDB77:** Khaici tawmkha a vawhte in tawmkha a at ding hi, citpiakna tawh a tampi a vawhte in a tampi a at ding hi ci-in na ciamteh un.

**ZVS:** Note in na phawk ding-te thu panin: Khaici tawm a vawhte in tawm mah la ding a, tampi a vawhte in tampi mah la ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v7
**EN:** Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver.

**TDB77:** Mi khempeuh in amau lungsim sung uhah a hanciam bangin pia-te hen. Hazatna lungsim tawh ahi zongin, khia loin om thei lo ding cihna lungsim tawh ahi zongin kipia lo ding hi. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, piak nopna lungsim tawh a pia mi bek Pasian in a it hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in piaknopna lungsim tawh a pia mite bek deih ahih manin, a kuamah peuh in na lungsim sung uhah na ngaihsutsa zah ciat-te na pia un la, pia nuamlopipi leh sawlzawh thu bek tawh na pia kei un.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** And God is able to make all grace abound toward you; that ye, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good work:

**TDB77:** Note in na khempeuhah na zat khop ding-te sum a pawlpawl na neih niloh-te a, gamtatpha apawlpawl tawh na dim mahmah nadingun thupha apawlpawl a kicin’ nading Pasian in a hih thei hi.

**ZVS:** Note in na kisap khempeuh-te na neihtheihna ding-te leh midangte huhna ding zong na neihthuah theihna dingun Pasian in hong pia thei hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** (As it is written, He hath dispersed abroad; he hath given to the poor: his righteousness remaineth for ever.

**TDB77:** Lai Siangtho in: “Amah in a hawmthang zo hi, mi zawngte a thupha a hawm zo hi; Ama dikna in a kip tawntung hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Lai Siangtho in, “Amah in citna tawh mizawngte pia zuahzuah a, ama migitna kip tawntung hi,” na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Now he that ministereth seed to the sower both minister bread for your food, and multiply your seed sown, and increase the fruits of your righteousness;)

**TDB77:** Khaici vawhte’ khaici ahi zongin, nek theih an ahi zongin a piapa in note vawh ding khaici hong pia a, a khangsak ding a, note dikna’ gahte a tamsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin vawhding khaici leh nekding an hong pia Pasian in note' cit piakna hangin nopsakna lian tampi na ngahtheihna dingun note tungah a kisam khaici hong guan'in a pungsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** Being enriched in every thing to all bountifulness, which causeth through us thanksgiving to God.

**TDB77:** Tua bangin note in hehpihna lungsim tawh apawl apawl hawmsak a, na piak nadingun na khempeuh na hauh-te ciangin kote tungah Pasian’ hehpihna phatna ding a om ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kote tung tawnin note' huhna sumte a ngahna-te hangin mi tampi in Pasian a phattheihna dingin note na cit gamtatzawh tawntungna dingun Pasian in hong hausak tawntung ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v12
**EN:** For the administration of this service not only supplieth the want of the saints, but is abundant also by many thanksgivings unto God;

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, hih nasepna in Pasian’ mite lak-a, mizawngte zawn’na a huh tham loin, Pasian’ hehpihna phat nadingin na tampi tawh a dim hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh note' huhna sumte in Pasian' mi a cimawhte' dong a hu bek hiloin, Pasian' tungah lungdam kohna ding zong a piangsak ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v13
**EN:** Whiles by the experiment of this ministration they glorify God for your professed subjection unto the gospel of Christ, and for your liberal distribution unto them, and unto all men;

**TDB77:** Note in Khazih’ Lungdamna Thu deihna-ah a kilanga na zuih-te ahi zongin, tua misiangthote-a kipan mi khempeuh hehpihna lungsim tawh hawmin na piakna-te ahi zongin, amau in a ngaihsut-te ciangin tua kivakna lim teci paulamin Pasian’ hehpihna a phat thei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Note in na up-te Jesuh hangin lungdamna thu-ah na cihtakna-te leh amaute leh midang khempeuh tungah na cit piakna hangun mi tampite in Pasian' min phat ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14

**ZVS:** Note tungah Pasian in hehpihna thupha a val a kanin hong piakna hangin amaute in note hong munuam mahmahin thu zong hong ngetsak ding-te hi.
