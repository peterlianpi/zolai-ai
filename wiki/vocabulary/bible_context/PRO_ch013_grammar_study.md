## Verses

### v1
**EN:** A wise son heareth his father's instruction: but a scorner heareth not rebuke.

**TDB77:** Ta pil in a pa’ hilhna mang a, a kihisak mi in akhial min pua nuam lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ta pil in a pa' hilhna mang a, a kihisak mi in a khial min pua nuamlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth: but the soul of the transgressors shall eat violence.

**TDB77:** Mihoihte in a kamsiamna bangun thaman sang a, mizuaute in siattat ding bek ut-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mihoihte in a kam siamna bangun thaman sang a, mizuaute pen siattat ding bek ut-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** He that keepeth his mouth keepeth his life: but he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.

**TDB77:** Pau a ki-ip zo mi in a nuntakna a hu hi a, a mawk paupau mi in amah a kisiasak tawm ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pau a ki-ipzo mi in a nuntakna a hu hi a, a mawk paupau mi pen amah a kisiasak tawm ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing: but the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.

**TDB77:** Thadah mi in a deih ngah zo lo a, nasep hahkat mi in a deih khempeuh ngah zo hi.

**ZVS:** Thadah mi in a deih ngahzo lo a, nasep hahkat mi in a deih khempeuh ngahzo hi.

### v5
**EN:** A righteous man hateth lying: but a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.

**TDB77:** Thuman mi in zuauthu deih lo a, a gilo mi in gensiatna leh mindai nading bek pau hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mi in zuauthu deihlo a, a gilo mi in gensiatna leh mindaina ding bek a pau hi.

### v6
**EN:** Righteousness keepeth him that is upright in the way: but wickedness overthroweth the sinner.

**TDB77:** Diktatna in midikte hu a, gitlohna in mawhneite’ pukna hi.

**ZVS:** Thumanna in midikte hu a, gitlohna pen mawhneite' pukna ahi hi.

### v7
**EN:** There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing: there is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great riches.

**TDB77:** Mi pawlkhatte, zawng napi-in kihau sak-te a, mi pawlkhatte, hau napi-in kizawngsak-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mi pawlkhatte, zawng napi-in kihausak-te a, mi pawlkhatte, hau napi-in kizawngsak-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** The ransom of a man's life are his riches: but the poor heareth not rebuke.

**TDB77:** Mihaupa’ sum hauhna in a nuntakna tankhia zo a, mizawngpa in kitat nading bangmah nei lo hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaupa' sum hauhna in a nuntakna tankhiazo a, mizawngpa in a kitatna ding bangmah neilo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** The light of the righteous rejoiceth: but the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.

**TDB77:** Thuman mite, khuavak bangin taang lela a, migilote meivak bangin mit pak hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mite khuavak bangin taang lela a, migilote meivak bangin mitpak hi.

### v10
**EN:** Only by pride cometh contention: but with the well advised is wisdom.

**TDB77:** Hilh ding ut lo a, kihihsakna in kitotna bek piangsak thei a, hong kithuhilh ding utna in a hoihzaw ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hilh ding utlo, kihihsakna in kitotna bek piangsak thei a, hong kithuhilh ding utna pen a hoihzaw ahi hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth by labour shall increase.

**TDB77:** Ol tak-a hauh pakna in kiam kik pah a, haksapi-a hauh tohna in khang semsem hi.

**ZVS:** Ol takin hauhpakna oltak mahin kiamkik pah a, haksapi-in hauhtohna khangto semsem hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Hope deferred maketh the heart sick: but when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.

**TDB77:** Lamet tun’lohna in lungsim cinasak a, deihna tangtun’na in lungdamna hi.

**ZVS:** Lamet tun'lohna in lungsim cinasak a, deihna tangtun'na pen lungdamna ahi hi.

### v13
**EN:** Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed: but he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.

**TDB77:** Thu kihilh ding ut lohna in siat ding utna hi a, thu kihilhna zuihna in lungnopna hi.

**ZVS:** Thu kihilh ding utlohna pen siat ding utna hi a, thu kihilhna zuihna pen lungnopna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** The law of the wise is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death.

**TDB77:** Mipilte’ thuhilhna in nuntakna tuinak bang hi a, sihna thang tawh kipelhna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mipilte' thuhilhna pen nuntakna tuinak bang hi a, sihna thang tawh kipelhna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** Good understanding giveth favour: but the way of transgressors is hard.

**TDB77:** Thu ngaihsut theihna in maipha ngahna hi a, zuautatna in lungkhamna bek piangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Thungaihsut theihna maipha ngahna hi a, zuautatna in lungkhamna bek piangsak hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v16
**EN:** Every prudent man dealeth with knowledge: but a fool layeth open his folly.

**TDB77:** Thu ngaihsun theite, pilna tawh gamta a, mihaite in a haina tangkoko hi.

**ZVS:** Thungaihsun theite pilna tawh gamta a, mihaite in a haina taangko hi.

### v17
**EN:** A wicked messenger falleth into mischief: but a faithful ambassador is health.

**TDB77:** Kamtai ginalo in mi lungkhamsak a, a citak thu puapa in mi lungdam kik sak hi.

**ZVS:** Kamtai ginalo in mi lungkhamsak a, a citak thupuapa in mi lungdamsak kik hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth instruction: but he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.

**TDB77:** Thu kihilhna zui lo mi in zawn’na leh mindaina ngah a, thu kihilhna a zui mi in minphatna leh pahtawina ngah hi.

**ZVS:** Thukihilhna a zuilo mi in zawn'na leh mindaina ngah a, thukihilhna a zui mi in minphatna leh pahtawina a ngah hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul: but it is abomination to fools to depart from evil.

**TDB77:** Deihna ngahna in lungsim nuam sak a, siat gamtatna panin mihaite pialkhia nuam lo hi.

**ZVS:** Deihna ngahna in lungsim nuamsak a, siat gamtatna panin mihaite pialkhia nuamlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v20
**EN:** He that walketh with wise men shall be wise: but a companion of fools shall be destroyed.

**TDB77:** Mipil tawh kikhawl lecin na pil ding a, mihai tawh kikhawl lecin gimna na thuak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v21
**EN:** Evil pursueth sinners: but to the righteous good shall be repayed.

**TDB77:** Tuahsiatna in mawhneite sulzuih kawikawi a, midikte tuahpha kawikawi hi.

### v22
**EN:** A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children's children: and the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.

**TDB77:** Midikte in a tu a tate’ luah ding gamh nei a, mawhneite neihsate ahih leh midikte’ ading hi lel hi.

**ZVS:** Midikte in a tu a tate' luah ding gamh nei-te a, mawhneite' neihsate ahih leh midikte-a' ding hi lel hi.

### v23
**EN:** Much food is in the tillage of the poor: but there is that is destroyed for want of judgment.

**TDB77:** Lo thak panin mizawngte’ khamkhop ding an piang kha ding a, ahi zongin thuman lohna hangin tua nangawn maimang thei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Lo thak panin mizawngte' khamkhop ding an piangkha ding a, ahi zongin thumanlohna hangin tua nangawn maimang thei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v24
**EN:** He that spareth his rod hateth his son: but he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.

**TDB77:** A ta a tai nuam lote in a mudah hi a, a ta a itte in lim takin thuhilhin tai hi.

**ZVS:** A ta a tai nuamlote a ta mudah hi a, a ta a itte in limtakin thuhilhin a tai hi.

### v25
**EN:** The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul: but the belly of the wicked shall want.

**TDB77:** Thuman mi in a kham ding nei-in a gil vah a, a gilo mi in nektawm ding nei loin a gil kial hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mi in a kham ding nei-in gilvah a, a gilo mi in nektawm ding neiloin a gil a kial hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix
