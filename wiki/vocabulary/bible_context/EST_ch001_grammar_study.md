## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Now it came to pass in the days of Ahasuerus, (this is Ahasuerus which reigned, from India even unto Ethiopia, over an hundred and seven and twenty provinces:)

**TDB77:** Hih lai-a kigen thu piangte pen India gam panin Ethiopia gam dong, gamneu za khat sawmnih-le-sagih a uk Ahasu-eras’ hun lai-a piang thu ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih lai-a kigelh thupiangte pen India gam panin Ethiopia gam dong, gamneu za khat sawmnihsagih a uk Ahasueras' hun lai-a piang thu ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** That in those days, when the king Ahasuerus sat on the throne of his kingdom, which was in Shushan the palace,

**TDB77:** Hih hun lai takin kumpi khua ahi Susa khuapi sunga a kumpi tokhom tungah amah tu hi.

**ZVS:** Hih hun laitakin kumpi khua ahi Susa khuapi sunga kumpi tokhom tungah amah tu hi.

### v3
**EN:** In the third year of his reign, he made a feast unto all his princes and his servants; the power of Persia and Media, the nobles and princes of the provinces, being before him:

**TDB77:** Ama uk a kum thumna kumin ama nuai-a a uliante khempeuh leh a nasemte’ adingin pawi khat bawksak hi. Ama khut nuai-a om Persia leh Media galkapmangte leh ama nuai-a uliante leh a ma nuai-ah gam a uk sawnte a om lai takin,

**ZVS:** Ama uk a kum thumna kumin ama nuai-a a uliante khempeuh leh a nasemte-a' dingin pawi khat bawlsak hi. Ama khutnuai-a om Persia leh Media galkapmangte leh ama nuai-a uliante leh ama nuai-ah gam a uksawnte a om laitakin,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4

**TDB77:** a kumpi gam hauhna leh ama thupi ziazuana ni tampi sung, ni za leh ni sawmgiat sung tak lak hi.

**ZVS:** a kumpi gam hauhna leh ama thupi ziazuana ni tampi sung, ni za leh ni sawmgiat sung tak a lak hi.

### v5
**EN:** And when these days were expired, the king made a feast unto all the people that were present in Shushan the palace, both unto great and small, seven days, in the court of the garden of the king's palace;

**TDB77:** Hih pawi hun a bei khit ciangin kumpi khua Susa khuapi sunga om mi lian mi neu mi khempeuh adingin kumpipa in pawi khat bawl hi; tua pawi pen kumpi inn huang sungah kibawl a, ni sagih kibawl hi.

**ZVS:** Hih pawi hun a bei khit ciangin kumpi khua Susa khuapi sunga om milian mineu mi khempeuh-a' dingin kumpipa in pawi khat a bawl hi. Tua pawi pen kumpi inn huang sungah kibawl a, ni sagih sung kibawl hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

**TDB77:** Tua huang sungah suangkang khuamte kiphut a, khau malneu leh khau dupsan tawh ngun kualte ah kikhihin a kikhai dalna puan pakte kikhungin, suangmanpha a san, a dum, a kang, leh a vom, a nam tuamtuamte kizangin a kizeem suangphah kiphah tungah kham leh ngun kibawl lupnate kikoih hi.

**ZVS:** Tua huang sungah suangkang khuamte kiphut a, khau malneu leh khau dupsan tawh ngun kualte-ah kikhihin a kikhai dalna puanpakte kikhungin, suangmanpha a san, a dum, a kang, leh a vom, a nam tuamtuamte kizangin a kizem suangphah kiphah tungah kham leh ngun kibawl lupnate kikoih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7

**ZVS:** Kham hai nam tuamtuamte tawh zu kihawm a, kumpipa' hauhna tawh kituakin deihdeihin zu kizang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8

**TDB77:** Tua lai-ah zu nekna zia pen kihah piak cih om lo hi; bang hang hiam cih leh mi khempeuh in amau’ duh zahzah bek a neksak nading-te kumpipa in ama nuai-a uliante thupia hi.

**ZVS:** Tua lai-ah zu nekna zia pen kihah piak cih omlo hi; bang hang hiam cih leh mi khempeuh in amau' duh zahzah bek a neksakna ding-te kumpipa in a nuai-a a uliante thu pia hi.

### v11

**TDB77:** A ni sagih ni, zunekna hangin a lung a nop ciangin kumpipa in ama na a sem innsungmang sagihte Mehuman, Biztha, Harbona, Bigtha, Abagtha, Zethar, leh Karkas-te sam a, mite leh uliante mai-ah Kumpinu Vashti a melhoihna a lahtheihna dingin a kumpi lukhu khusa-in ama kiangah a paipihna dingun a sawl hi; bang hang hiam cih leh tuanu a mel a hoih takpi mah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Kumpinu Vashti in innsung mangte’ thu puak kumpipa’ sapna nial hi. Hih a nialna thu ah kumpipa heh mahmah a, a hehna hangin a sin sung so hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin Kumpinu Vashti in innsungmangte' thupuak kumpipa' sapna a nial hi. Hih a nialna thu-ah kumpipa heh mahmah a, a hehna hangin a sin so hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v13
**EN:** Then the king said to the wise men, which knew the times, (for so was the king's manner toward all that knew law and judgment:

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin kumpipa in upadi siam a mi pilte thu dong hi; bang hang hiam cih leh upadi leh biakna lam thu theite khempeuh thu dotpen kumpipa’ zat ngei khat ahi hi;

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin kumpipa in upadi siam a mipilte thu dong hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh upadi leh biakna lam thutheite khempeuh thu dot pen kumpipa' zat ngei khat ahi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v14

**TDB77:** ama gei nai pen ah a tu, hun omzia a thei, Persia leh Media gam ulian sagihte ahi Karshena, Shethar, Admatha, Tarshish, Meres, Marsena, leh Memukante hi-te a, kumpipa kiangah a hawh thei, kumpi gam sungah ulian pente ahi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ama gei nai penah a tu mite pen Persia leh Media gam ulian sagihte ahi Karshena, Shethar, Admatha, Tarshish, Meres, Marsena, leh Memukan-te hi-te a, kumpipa kiangah a hawh thei, kumpi gamsungah ulian pente ahi-te hi.

### v15

**ZVS:** “Innsungmangte' tung tawnin keimah kumpi Ahasueras' thupiakna bangin Kumpinu Vashti gamtalo ahih manin Kumpinu Vashti tungah upadi tawh kizui-in bangci kihih ding ahi hiam?” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin kumpipa leh uliante' zakin Memukan in dawng a, “Kumpinu Vashti khial hi; kumpipa tung bek hiloin Kumpipa Ahasueras' ukna gamsung khempeuha om uliante leh mi khempeuh tungah zong a khial ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpinu' gamtatna thu numei dangte khempeuh in thei ding-te a, hih thu hangin numeite in a pasalte-te simmawh ding-te a, amaute in, ‘Kumpipa Ahasueras in ama kiangah a pai dingin Kumpinu Vashti thu pia napi-in pailo hi,’ ci ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Hih banga kumpinu' gamtatna thu a za Persia leh Media gam numei liante in kumpipa nuai-a uliante tungah tuni mahmahin hih thu gen ding-te a, kisimmawhna leh kizahtak lohna tampi piangsak ding hi.

### v19

**ZVS:** Kumpipa in hoih na sak leh, kumpi thupiakna khat paisak in la, Kumpipa Ahasueras' mai-ah Vashti a kilakkik nawnlohna dingin a kikhelkik ngei nawnlo ding Persia mite leh Media mite' upadi sungah gelhsak in. Tua ciangin amah sangin a kilawmzaw numei khat ama za luahsak in.
