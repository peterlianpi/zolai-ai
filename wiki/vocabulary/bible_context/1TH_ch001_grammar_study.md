## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Paul, and Silvanus, and Timothy, unto the church of the Thessalonians which is in God the Father and in the Lord Jesus Christ: Grace be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.

**TDB77:** Paul, Silvanas leh Timoti-te in Pa Pasian leh Topa Zeisu Khazih sungah kip-a a om Thesalonika pawlpi kong zasak-te hi: Hehpihna thupha leh nopna in nomau tungah om ta hen.

**ZVS:** Keimah Paul, leh Silas, leh Timoti in Pa Pasian leh Topa Jesuh Khrih sungah a om Thesalonika khua-a ompawlpi mite tungah lai ka hong khak-te hi. Pasian in note tungah dikna leh nopsakna thupha hong tungsak ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** We give thanks to God always for you all, making mention of you in our prayers;

**TDB77:** Note khempeuh hangin Pasian tungah lungdamna ko tawntungin, ka thungetna uhah note ka hong phawk tawntung-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Remembering without ceasing your work of faith, and labour of love, and patience of hope in our Lord Jesus Christ, in the sight of God and our Father;

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh i Pa Pasian kiangah thu ka nget simun note' upna hangin na nuntakziate uh, itna na neih manun nakpi takin na nasepnate-te leh i Topa Jesuh Khrih hong paikik ding lametna na neih manun kiptakin na omnate-te ka phawk tawntung-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Knowing, brethren beloved, your election of God.

**TDB77:** Sanggamte aw, Pasian in note hong itin ama mi dingin a hong teel khitna thu ka thei-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** For our gospel came not unto you in word only, but also in power, and in the Holy Ghost, and in much assurance; as ye know what manner of men we were among you for your sake.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, kote in note tawh ka omkhawm lai takun, note adingin bang ci mi ka hih-te na theih-te mah bangin, kote’ hilh Lungdamna Thu in note sungah kampau bek hi loin vangliatna tawh ahi zongin, up khialhna om loin lungkim taktakna tawh a dim thu ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh note kiangah Lungdamna Thu ka hong hilh lai-un kam bek tawh a hong gen hiloin, Kha Siangtho' vangliatna leh Lungdamna Thu a man taktak hi, ci-in theihna tawh a hong pai ka hi-te hi. Note kiangah ka om laitakun note' phattuamna dingin ka sep ka bawlna khempeuh-te na thei-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v6
**EN:** And ye became followers of us, and of the Lord, having received the word in much affliction, with joy of the Holy Ghost:

**TDB77:** Note in gimna lian na thuak-te hangin Kha Siangtho tawh akipawl lungdamna tawh, Pasian thu sangin kote tawh Topa ngeina bangin a gamta mite na hi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kote leh Topa' gamtat bangin note na gamta-te a, note in gimna nakpi takin thuaklawh napi-in Kha Siangtho' huhna tawh lungdam takin thu umin na om-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** So that ye were ensamples to all that believe in Macedonia and Achaia.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin Makedonia gam leh Akhaia gamah thu-um mite khempeuh ading note in limpha a lak mi na hi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Masedonia gam leh Akhaia gamsung khempeuhah thu um mi khempeuhte-a' dingin etteh hoih na suak-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** For from you sounded out the word of the Lord not only in Macedonia and Achaia, but also in every place your faith to God-ward is spread abroad; so that we need not to speak any thing.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, note kiang panin Topa thu in Makedonia gam leh Akhaia gamah kizeel tham loin, note in Topa’ thu na upna-te mun khempeuh a tung hi. Tua ahih ciangin kote in bangmah ka gen ding-te a om kei hi.

**ZVS:** Note' gamtatna ziate' hangin Masedonia leh Akhaia gam sungteng bekah Topa' thu a kithei hiloin, note in Pasian na upna thu-te mun khempeuhah kiza ahih manin kote' gen ding bangmah kisam nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** For they themselves shew of us what manner of entering in we had unto you, and how ye turned to God from idols to serve the living and true God;

**TDB77:** Note kiangah ka hong pai lai-un kote na hong limbawlna thu uh, leh a nungta a man Pasian' thu zui-in a bia ding,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.

**TDB77:** leh a hong tungding Pasian' hehna panin eite a hong honkhia, sihna panin Pasian in a thawhsakkik a Tapa Jesuh, van panin a hong paiding ngak dingin milim paisanin Pasian' lamah na kiheina-te thute mi khempeuh in gen belin a nei-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
