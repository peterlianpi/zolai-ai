| Pattern | Count |

| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 6 |
| plural: uh marker | 4 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Hear, ye children, the instruction of a father, and attend to know understanding.

**TDB77:** Tate aw, na pa uh’ hong hilhnate ngai un la, thu na teltheih nadingun lim takin ngaihsun un.

**ZVS:** Tate aw, na pa uh' hong hilhnate ngai un la, thu na teltheihna dingun limtakin ngaihsun un.

### v2
**EN:** For I give you good doctrine, forsake ye not my law.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh kong hilh thute thuhoih ahi hi; kong hilhte khempeuh lim takin, ciamteh un.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh kong hilh thute thuhoih ahi hi. Kong hilhte khempeuh limtakin ciamteh un.

### v3
**EN:** For I was my father's son, tender and only beloved in the sight of my mother.

**TDB77:** Kei zong ka nu leh ka pa’ tapa neihsun, naupangno khat ka hih lai-in,

**ZVS:** Kei zong tapa khat neihsun ka hi a, naupangno khat ka hih lai-in

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** He taught me also, and said unto me, Let thine heart retain my words: keep my commandments, and live.

**TDB77:** ka pa in thu hong hilh a, “Ka thugente ciamteh in la mangngilh ngei kei in. Kong gen bangin gamta in, tua hi leh na nungta ding hi.

**ZVS:** ka pa in thu hong hilh a, “Ka thugente ciamteh in la, mangngilh kei in. Kong gen bangin gamta in; tua hi leh na nungta ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v5
**EN:** Get wisdom, get understanding: forget it not; neither decline from the words of my mouth.

**TDB77:** Pilna leh thu ngaihsut theihna na neih nading hahkat in la, kong gen thute mangngilh kei in la, hoih takin, ngaihsun in.

**ZVS:** Pilna leh thu ngaihsut theihna na neihna ding hahkat in. Kong gen thute mangngilh kei in la, hoihtakin ngaihsun in.

### v6
**EN:** Forsake her not, and she shall preserve thee: love her, and she shall keep thee.

**TDB77:** Pilna nusia kei in, tua hi leh siatna panin nang hong hu ding hi, pilna deih in, tua hi leh lungmuang takin na om nadingin hong kem ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pilna nusia kei in; tua hi leh siatna panin nang hong hu ding hi. Pilna deih in; tua hi leh lungmuang takin na omna dingin hong kem ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v7
**EN:** Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding.

**TDB77:** Pilna na ngah nading a thupi masapen hi a, na neih khempeuh na bei lawh zongin na zon’ ding ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pilna na ngahna ding a thupi masa pen hi a, na neih khempeuh na beilawh zongin na zon' ding ahi hi.

### v8
**EN:** Exalt her, and she shall promote thee: she shall bring thee to honour, when thou dost embrace her.

**TDB77:** Pilna deih in, mi lianin nang hong bawl ding hi; limtak pom in, pahtawina hong ngah sak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pilna deih in; mi lianin hong bawl ding hi. Limtakin pom in; pahtawina hong ngahsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** She shall give to thine head an ornament of grace: a crown of glory shall she deliver to thee.

**TDB77:** Pilna in pahtawina leh minthan’na, nang tungah hong tung sak ding hi,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Pilna in pahtawina leh minthan'na nangma tungah hong tungsak ding hi,”hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will

### v10
**EN:** Hear, O my son, and receive my sayings; and the years of thy life shall be many.

**TDB77:** Ka ta aw, ka thugente lim takin zui in, tua hi leh na khan a sau ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, ka thugente limtakin zui in. Tua hi leh na khan' sau ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** I have taught thee in the way of wisdom; I have led thee in right paths.

**TDB77:** Na pil nading lampi kong hilh zo a, na gamtat nading zia kong gen zo hi.

**ZVS:** Na pilna ding lampi kong hilhzo a, na gamtatna ding zia kong genzo hi.

### v12
**EN:** When thou goest, thy steps shall not be straitened; and when thou runnest, thou shalt not stumble.

**TDB77:** Hoih takin gamta lecin bangmah in hong nawngkaisak lo ding a, na tai hangin zong na puk kha kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Hoihtakin gamta lecin nang hong nawngkaisakzo ding bangmah omlo ding a, na tai hangin zong na pukkha kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v13
**EN:** Take fast hold of instruction; let her not go: keep her; for she is thy life.

**TDB77:** Hong kihilhnate lencip in la khahsuah kha kei in; na nuntak nading ahih manin lim takin zui in.

**ZVS:** Hong kihilhnate lencip in la, khahsuah kei in. Na nuntakna ding ahih manin limtakin zui in.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** Enter not into the path of the wicked, and go not in the way of evil men.

**TDB77:** Migilote tawh kikhawl kei in la, amau’ gamtatnate zui kei in.

**ZVS:** Migilote tawh kikhawl kei in la, amau' gamtatnate zui kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away.

**TDB77:** Amau’ gamtat bangin gamta peuhmah ke’n! Gitlohna peel in! Pai san in la, na maban sem in.

**ZVS:** Amau' gamtat bangin gamta peuhmah ke'n! Gitlohna peel in! Paisan in la, na maban sem in.

### v16
**EN:** For they sleep not, except they have done mischief; and their sleep is taken away, unless they cause some to fall.

**TDB77:** Migilote in asia khatpeuh a sep mateng lum thei lo uh a, mi khatpeuh a suk zawh mateng ihmu lo uh hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote in a sia khatpeuh a sep mateng uh lum theilo uh a, mi khatpeuh a sukzawh mateng uh ihmu theilo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | plural: uh marker

### v17
**EN:** For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.

**TDB77:** Gitlohna, annek bangin nuamsa uh a, ngongtatna, tuidawn bangin ngaihsun uh hi.

**ZVS:** Gitloh gamtatna, annek bangin nuamsa uh a, ngongtatna, tui dawn bangin a ngaihsun uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v18
**EN:** But the path of the just is as the shining light, that shineth more and more unto the perfect day.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin thuman mite’ zuih lampi ahih leh, sun dong a taang pha semsem zing khuavak tung tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin thuman mite' zuih lampi ahih leh sun dong a taang pha semsem zingsang khuavak tung tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** The way of the wicked is as darkness: they know not at what they stumble.

**TDB77:** Migilote’ lampi ahih leh zan khuamial bangin mial a, amaute a tuksak, a suihkhak nate nangawn mu thei lo uh hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote' lampi ahih leh zan khuamial bangin mial a, amaute a tuksak, a suihkhakte nangawn mu theilo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | plural: uh marker

### v20

**TDB77:** Ka ta aw, ka thugente don in la ka kammalte ngai in.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, ka thugente don in la, ka kammalte ngai in.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh tua thu a zuite in, nuntakna leh cidamna-in nei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh tua thu a zuite in nuntakna leh cidamna-in a nei uh hi.

### v23

**ZVS:** Na lungsim ngaihsutnate nuntakna nak hi a, na neihsa a manpha pen bangin na kep ding ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Kisuan'na nei hetloin om liuliau in la, maizumin kuncip kei in.
