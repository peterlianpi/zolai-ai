## Verses

### v1
**EN:** There is an evil which I have seen under the sun, and it is common among men:

**TDB77:** Hih leitunga mihingte tungah a man het lo-a hong kibawlna ka phawk hi.

**ZVS:** Hih leitung-a mihingte tungah a man hetloin hong kibawlna ka phawk hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** A man to whom God hath given riches, wealth, and honour, so that he wanteth nothing for his soul of all that he desireth, yet God giveth him not power to eat thereof, but a stranger eateth it: this is vanity, and it is an evil disease.

**TDB77:** Pasian in mi khatpeuh tungah neih leh lam, leh hauhna leh pahtawina pia-in, a deih khempeuh neisak napi-in, tua nate nuam takin zang sak loin, midang khat in nopsak lawh laizang hi. Hih in a mawknapi, a thuman het lo khat ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in mi khatpeuh tungah neihlelam, hauhna, leh pahtawina pia-in, a deih khempeuh neisak napi-in, tua nate nuamtakin zangsak loin, midang khat in nopsaklawh laizang hi. Hih pen a mawknapi, a thuman hetlo khat ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** If a man beget an hundred children, and live many years, so that the days of his years be many, and his soul be not filled with good, and also that he have no burial; I say, that an untimely birth is better than he.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in ta zakhat nei-in, kum saupi zong nungta thei kha ding hi; bangzahin sauveipi a nungta ahi zongin leitungah nopsakna ngah loin, lim tak-a vuina a ngah kei leh, a sisa-a suak naungek hoihzaw lai hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat in ta za khat nei-in kum saupi zong nungta thei khading hi. Bangzahin sauveipi a nungta ahi zongin leitungah nopsakna ngahloin, limtaka vuina a ngah kei leh, a sisa-a suak naungek hoihzaw lai hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v4
**EN:** For he cometh in with vanity, and departeth in darkness, and his name shall be covered with darkness.

**TDB77:** A sisa-a suak naungek a kiman’na om lo hi; khuamial sungah suakin, khuamial sung mah-ah mangthangin kiphawk nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** A sisa-a suak naungek a kiman'na omlo hi. Khuamial sungah suakin, khuamial sung mahah mangthangin kiphawk nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Moreover he hath not seen the sun, nor known any thing: this hath more rest than the other.

**TDB77:** Sun khuavak mu kha loin, nuntakna bangci hiam, cih a theih khak loh hangin tawlngakna a ngah hi buang hi.

**ZVS:** Sun khuavak mukha loin, nuntakna bangci hiam, cih a theihkhakloh hangin tawlngakna a ngah hi buang hi.

### v6
**EN:** Yea, though he live a thousand years twice told, yet hath he seen no good: do not all go to one place?

**TDB77:** Nuntakna-ah nopsakna a ngah lo mipa kum tulnih a hing zongin, a tawpna-ah a sisa-a suak naungek mah tawh a tun’na mun kibang lel hi.

**ZVS:** Nuntakna-ah nopsakna a ngahlo mipa kum tul nih a hing zongin, a tawpna-ah a sisa-a suak naungek mah tawh a tun'na mun kibang lel hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** All the labour of man is for his mouth, and yet the appetite is not filled.

**TDB77:** Mihingte’ nasepna in a nek tawm ding bek a ngaihsutna hi napi-in, a kham khop ngah zo tuan lo hi.

**ZVS:** Mihingte' nasepna pen a nektawm ding bek a ngaihsutna hilo napi-in, a khamkhop ngahzo tuanlo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo

### v8
**EN:** For what hath the wise more than the fool? what hath the poor, that knoweth to walk before the living?

**TDB77:** Tua ahih manin mihaipa sangin mipilpa a hamphat zawkna bang om tuan ahi hiam? Mihaipa in nuntak nading zia a theih hangin bang phatuam tuan ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin mihaipa sangin mipilpa a hamphat zawkna bang om tuan ahi hiam? Mihaipa in nuntakna ding zia a theih hangin bang phatuam tuan ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v9
**EN:** Better is the sight of the eyes than the wandering of the desire: this is also vanity and vexation of spirit.

**TDB77:** Na dang dang deih thuah ngitngetna sangin i neihsa teng tawh lungkimna hoihzaw hi. Hih zong a mawknapi, huihpi nung delh mah tawh a kibang bek ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Na dangdang deihthuah ngitngetna sangin i neihsa teng tawh lungkimna hoihzaw hi. Hih zong a mawknapi, huihpi nungdelh mah tawh a kibang bek ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** That which hath been is named already, and it is known that it is man: neither may he contend with him that is mightier than he.

**TDB77:** A piang thu khempeuh, a tuaci ding mahin tangtawng-a kisehsa hi a, mi khat in amah sangin a thahatzaw khat a nial zawh lohna thu i vekpi-in i thei hi.

**ZVS:** A piang thu khempeuh, a tuaci mahin tangtawnga kisehsa hi a, mi khat in amah sangin a thahatzaw mi khat a nial zawhlohna thu i vekpi-in i thei hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Seeing there be many things that increase vanity, what is man the better?

**TDB77:** Sauvei kinial lehang a mawkna hi semsem a, a phattuamna bang om hiam?

**ZVS:** Sauvei kinial lehang a mawkna hi semsem a, a phattuamna omlo hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** For who knoweth what is good for man in this life, all the days of his vain life which he spendeth as a shadow? for who can tell a man what shall be after him under the sun?

**TDB77:** Nilim bangin a pai hanhan, a mawknapi hih nuntak tom sungah mihing’ ading a hoihpen kua in thei ding ahi hiam? Ama sih khit ciangin leitungah bang piang ding cih kua in gen thei ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Nilim bangin a pai hanhan, a mawknapi hih nuntak tom sungah mihing-a' ding a hoihpen kua in thei ding ahi hiam? Ama sih khit ciangin leitungah bang piang ding cih kua in gen thei ahi hiam?
