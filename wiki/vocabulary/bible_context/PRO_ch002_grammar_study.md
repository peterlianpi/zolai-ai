## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My son, if thou wilt receive my words, and hide my commandments with thee;

**TDB77:** Ka ta aw, kong hilhte thei in la, kong gente mangngilh ngei kei in.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, kong hilhte thei in la, kong gente mangngilh kei in.

### v2
**EN:** So that thou incline thine ear unto wisdom, and apply thine heart to understanding;

**TDB77:** Pilna thute limtakin ngai in la, na theihtel zawh nadingin hanciam in.

**ZVS:** Pilna thute limtakin ngai in la, na theihtel zawhna dingin hanciam in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** Yea, if thou criest after knowledge, and liftest up thy voice for understanding;

**TDB77:** Hi hi, thu theih nading hanciam takpi in la, thumuh nading hahkat takpi in.

**ZVS:** Thu theihna ding hanciam takpi in la, thu muhna ding hahkat takpi in.

### v4
**EN:** If thou seekest her as silver, and searchest for her as for hid treasures;

**TDB77:** Ngun, ahih kei leh a kiseel sum manpha zon’ bangin, hahkatin pilna zong in.

**ZVS:** Ngun, ahih kei leh a kiseel sum manpha zon' bangin, hahkatin pilna zong in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Then shalt thou understand the fear of the

**TDB77:** Tua hi leh, Topa zahtakna thu na thei ding a, Pasian na theitel ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi leh, { Topa } zahtakna thu na thei ding a, Pasian na theitel ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh pilna a piapa in Topa hi a, amah in pilna leh siamna a hilhpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh pilna a piapa pen { Topa } hi a, amah pen pilna leh siamna a hilhpa ahi hi.

### v7
**EN:** He layeth up sound wisdom for the righteous: he is a buckler to them that walk uprightly.

**TDB77:** Pasian in thuman mite pilna guan a, midiktatte a hu hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in thuman mite pilna guan' a, midikte a hu hi.

### v8
**EN:** He keepeth the paths of judgment, and preserveth the way of his saints.

**TDB77:** Amah in thutang mite cing a, amah a itte kem hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in thutang mite cing a, amah a itte a kem hi.

### v9
**EN:** Then shalt thou understand righteousness, and judgment, and equity; yea, every good path.

**TDB77:** Tua hi leh thuman leh thutang, ahoih leh a kilawm na thei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi leh thuman leh thutang a hoih leh a kilawm na thei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** When wisdom entereth into thine heart, and knowledge is pleasant unto thy soul;

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin mipil na suak ding a, na pilna hangin nuam na sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will

### v11
**EN:** Discretion shall preserve thee, understanding shall keep thee:

**TDB77:** Na thu ngaihsut theihna leh na thutelna in, nangmah hong hu-in,

**ZVS:** Na thungaihsut theihna leh na thutelna in nangmah hong hu-in,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v12
**EN:** To deliver thee from the way of the evil man, from the man that speaketh froward things;

**TDB77:** a sia-in na gamtat khak ding hong kem ding a, kampauna tawh buaina a piangsak thei mi,

**ZVS:** a sia-in na gamtatkhak ding hong kem ding a, kampauna tawh buaina a piangsak thei mi,

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v13
**EN:** Who leave the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of darkness;

**TDB77:** nuntakna manin a nungta lo, mawhna khuamial sungah a gamta,

**ZVS:** nuntakna manin a nungtalo, mawhna khuamial sungah a gamta,

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v14
**EN:** Who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the frowardness of the wicked;

**TDB77:** a sia-in gamtat ding nuam a sa, asia-a gamtatna-ah a lungkim mahmah,

**ZVS:** a sia-in gamtat ding nuam a sa, a sia-a gamtatna-ah a lungkim mahmah,

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v15
**EN:** Whose ways are crooked, and they froward in their paths:

**TDB77:** mi lungsim kawi, muan’ theih loh ding mite panin nang hong kem ding hi.

**ZVS:** mi lungsim kawi, muan' theihloh ding mite panin nang hong kem ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v17
**EN:** Which forsaketh the guide of her youth, and forgetteth the covenant of her God.

**TDB77:** A pasal tungah citak loin a pasal a nusia, Pasian' mai-ah a kiciamna a mangngilh, kam khum mahmah tawh nangmah zol ding a hanciam, numei paktatte panin hong cing ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** For her house inclineth unto death, and her paths unto the dead.

**TDB77:** Tua nu’ inn-ah pai lecin sihna lampi zui na hi a, misite’ gam a zuan na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua nu' inn-ah pai lecin sihna lampi a zui na hi a, misite gam a zuan na hi hi.

### v19
**EN:** None that go unto her return again, neither take they hold of the paths of life.

**TDB77:** Tua bang numei tawh mawhna a bawl mite, nuntakna nuam nei kik cih om ngei lo hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang numei tawh mawhna a bawl mite in nuntak nuam neikik ngeilo uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20
**EN:** That thou mayest walk in the way of good men, and keep the paths of the righteous.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin mihoihte’ gamtat bangin na gamta ding a, thuman mite’ gamtatna na zui ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin mihoihte' gamtat bangin na gamta ding a, thuman mite' gamtatna na zui ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will

### v21
**EN:** For the upright shall dwell in the land, and the perfect shall remain in it.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi thuman, mi ginate, hih i leitungah teengin om ding uh a,

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi thuman, miginate, hih i leitungah tengin om ding uh a,

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v22
**EN:** But the wicked shall be cut off from the earth, and the transgressors shall be rooted out of it.

**TDB77:** ahi zongin leitung panin migilote kilawn khia-in, singkung zung bawh bangin mawhneite kibotkhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** ahi zongin Pasian in leitung panin migilote lawnkhia-in, singkung zungbawh bangin mawhneite botkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
