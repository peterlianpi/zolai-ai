### v1
**EN:** For this Melchisedec, king of Salem, priest of the most high God, who met Abraham returning from the slaughter of the kings, and blessed him;

**TDB77:** Kumpite a thahna panin a ciahkik Abraham dawn a, thupha a piak khit ciangin,

**ZVS:** Abraham in a galsimna-ah kumpite thatin gualzo-in a hong ciah ciangin A Lianpen Pasian' siampi ahih banah Jerusalem khua kumpi zong ahi Melkhizedek in Abraham na dawnin thupha a pia hi.

### v2
**EN:** To whom also Abraham gave a tenth part of all; first being by interpretation King of righteousness, and after that also King of Salem, which is, King of peace;

**TDB77:** Abraham in a ngah sum khempeuh seh sawm suah seh khat khenin a piak, Salem Kumpipa ahi, Sang Bel Pasian’ Siampi Melkhizedek in, a min bulpi a khiatna pen dikna’ Kumpipa, tua tham loin, nopsakna’ Kumpipa a cihnopna, Salem Kumpipa hi a,

**ZVS:** Abraham in a na ngah khempeuh sung panin Melkhizedek tungah sawmah khat a pia hi. Melkhizedek min a khiatna in, “Thuman Kumpi,” a cihna hi phot a, tua banah Jerusalem khua kumpi zong hi lai ahih manin ama min pen, “Kilemna Kumpi,” cihna zong ahi hi.

### v3
**EN:** Without father, without mother, without descent, having neither beginning of days, nor end of life; but made like unto the Son of God; abideth a priest continually.

**TDB77:** pa om lo, nu om lo, a suan a khak om lo, a nuntakna kipatna ahi zongin, a beina ahi zongin nei loin, Pasian’ Tapa tawh kibangin siampi-in a tawntungin a om hi.

**ZVS:** Melkhizedek' pa leh a nu-a kipanin a suahna thu, a sihna thu, leh a khang tangthu peuhmah a kiciamtehna omlo ahih manin Pasian' Tapa tawh a kibang hi a, siampi mah hi tawntung paisuak hi.

### v4

**ZVS:** Levi suanlekhak sung panin siampi a sem mite pen Abraham' suanlekhak mah hinapi-in, amau mipih Israel-te tung panin sawmah khat a lakna dingun Thukham a na kibawl hi.
