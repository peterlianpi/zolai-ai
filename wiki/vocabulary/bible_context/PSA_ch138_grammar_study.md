## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I will praise thee with my whole heart: before the gods will I sing praise unto thee.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka lungsim khempeuh tawh nang tungah lungdamna kong ko a, pasiante’ mai-ah nangmah phatna ka sa hi;

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka lungsim khempeuh tawh nangma tungah lungdamna kong ko a, pasian-te' mai-ah nangmah phatna ka sa hi.

### v2
**EN:** I will worship toward thy holy temple, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.

**TDB77:** nangma biakinn siangtho lamah ka kun a, na itna kip leh na thumanna hangin na min kong phat hi; bang hang hiam cih leh na khempeuh sangin na min leh na thu a liat zawkna na lak hi.

**ZVS:** Na biakinn siangtho lamah kei ka kun a, na itna kip leh na thumanna hangin na min kong phat hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh na dang khempeuh sangin na min leh na thu a liatzawkna na lak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** In the day when I cried thou answeredst me, and strengthenedst me with strength in my soul.

**TDB77:** Nang kong sap ciangin kei nong dawng a, na thahatna tawh kei nong thakhauhsak hi.

### v4
**EN:** All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, leitung kumpi khempeuh in na thuciamnate za khin-te ahih manin nang hong phat ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, leitung khempeuh in na thuciamnate za khin-te ahih manin nang hong phat ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | future: will | causal: because | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ minthan’na lian ahih manin amaute in Topa’ nasepna ziate la-in sa ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } minthan'na lian mahmah ahih manin amaute in { Topa' } nasepna ziate la-in sa ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | causal: because | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Though the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa lian napi-in amah in a neute don hi; ahih hangin a kiphasakte ahih leh amah in gamlapi panin mu thei hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } lian mahmah napi-in amah in a neute don a, a kiphasakte ahih leh amah in gamlapi panin mu thei veve hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me: thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.

**TDB77:** Haksatna tuamtuamte ka thuak ciangin kei nong kem hi; a heh ka galte, kei’ tangin nong dosak a, na thahatna tawh kei nong honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Haksatna tuamtuamte ka thuak ciangin kei nong kem hi. A heh ka galte, kei' tangin nong dosak a, na thahatna tawh kei na hong honkhia hi.

### v8
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in kei adinga a ngaihsutna tung sak ding hi; Topa aw, na itna kip kho tawntung hi. Na sepsa na maban hong tun in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in kei-a' dingin a ngaihsutte hong tungsak ding hi. { Topa } aw, na itna kip tawntung hi. Na sepsa na maban hong tun' ta in.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
