## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The word that Jeremiah the prophet spake unto Baruch the son of Neriah, when he had written these words in a book at the mouth of Jeremiah, in the fourth year of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah, saying,

**TDB77:** Josiah’ tapa Jehoiakim, Judah gamah kumpi a suah zawh kum li a cin’ ciangin Neriah’ tapa Baruk in kamsangpa Jeremiah’ gen bangbang laibu khat sungah a gelh hi. Tua ciangin Jeremiah in Baruk kiangah:

**ZVS:** Josiah' tapa Jehoiakim, Judah gamah kumpi a suahzawh kum li a cin' ciangin Neriah' tapa Baruk in laibu khat sungah keima gen bangbang a gelh hi. Tua ahih ciangin Baruk kiangah,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2

**TDB77:** “Baruk aw, Topa, Israel mite’ Pasian in nang hong hopih hi.

**ZVS:** “Baruk aw, { Topa, } Israel mite' Pasian in nang hong hopih hi.

### v3
**EN:** Thou didst say, Woe is me now! for the

**TDB77:** Nangmah in, ‘Gimna ka thuak hi! Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa in ka dahna leh ka natna hong behlap hi. Ka tautauna-in ka gim khin zo a, ka tawlnga thei kei hi,’ na ci hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in, ‘Gimna thuak ing! Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } in ka dahna leh ka natna hong behlap hi. Ka tautauna-in ka gim khinzo a, ka tawlnga thei kei hi,’ na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei

### v4
**EN:** Thus shalt thou say unto him, The

**TDB77:** Topa in nang’ tungah hih bang a gen dingin hong sawl hi: En in, keimah in ka lam sate ka phel kik a, ka phuh sate ka bot khia kik hi. Keimah in leitung buppi tua bangin ka hih ding hi.

**ZVS:** “{ Topa } in nang tungah hih bang a gen dingin hong sawl hi: En in, keimah in ka lamsate ka phelkik a, ka phuhsate ka botkhia kik hi. Keimah in leitung buppi tua bangin ka hih ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** And seekest thou great things for thyself? seek them not: for, behold, I will bring evil upon all flesh, saith the

**TDB77:** Tua panin nang a peng thei tuam dingin kingaihsun na hi hiam? Tua bangin kingaihsun kei in; bang hang hiam cih leh, en in, Topa in kong cih-inah, mi khempeuh’ tungah gimna ka tung sak ding hi. Ahih hangin nang pen na paina mun khempeuh ah kong suakta sak liailiai ding hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua sung panin nang a pengthei tuam dingin kingaihsun na hi hiam? Tua bangin kingaihsun kei in; bang hang hiam cih leh en in, { Topa } in kong cihin-ah, mi khempeuh tungah gimna ka tungsak ding hi. Ahih hangin nang pen na paina mun khempeuhah kong suaktasak liailiai ding hi, ci hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
