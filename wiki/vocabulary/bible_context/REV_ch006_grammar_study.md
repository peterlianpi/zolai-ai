## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And I saw when the Lamb opened one of the seals, and I heard, as it were the noise of thunder, one of the four beasts saying, Come and see.

**TDB77:** Tuuno in ciamtehna sagih laka khat a hon’ ka mu hi. Tua lai takin ganhing lite-ah khat in vanging aw bang aw tawh, “Hong pai in,” hong cih ka za a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Tuuno in tua ciamtehna sagih sung panin khat a hon'khiat ka mu hi. Tua laitakin ganhing lite sung panin khat in vanging bangin nakpi takin, “Hong pai in,” a cihna ka za hi.

### v2
**EN:** And I saw, and behold a white horse: and he that sat on him had a bow; and a crown was given unto him: and he went forth conquering, and to conquer.

**TDB77:** ka et ciangin sakol kang khat ka mu hi! Tua sakol tung tuang mi in thal a tawi hi. Tua mi kumpilukhu khat a kipia hi. Amah in zong zoin ahi zongin, zawh nadingin ahi zongin a pusuak hi.

**ZVS:** Ka khuadak leh sakol kang khat hong pai a, a tungah a tuangpa in thal khat a tawi hi. Ama tungah lukhu khat kipia a, a gualzopa hihna tawh a gualzo dingin a kuankhia hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3

**TDB77:** A san ahi sakol dang khat hong pusuak hi. Tua sakol tung tuang mi in leitung pan nopsakna lakkhiat theihna thu leh leitung mi khat leh khat kithahsak theihna thu a pia hi. Amah namsau saupi khat zong a kipia hi.

**ZVS:** Sakol a ausan khat hong pai a, mite khatlekhat a kithah theihna dingun a sakol tung tuangpa in leitungah galkidona a pian'sak theihna ding vangliatna kipia a, ama tungah namsau lian mahmah khat zong kipia lai hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5

**TDB77:** Tuuno in thumna ciamtehna a hon’ ciangin thumna ganhing in, “Hong pai in,” hong cih ka za a, ka et ciangin sakol vom khat ka mu hi! Tua sakol tung tuang mi in a khut sungah tawikhaina a tawi hi.

**ZVS:** Ciamtehna a thumna a hon' ciangin ganhing a thumna in, “Hong pai in,” a cihna ka za hi. Sakol vom khat hong pai ka mu a, a tungah a tuangpa in a khutah tawitehna a tawi hi.

### v6
**EN:** And I heard a voice in the midst of the four beasts say, A measure of wheat for a penny, and three measures of barley for a penny; and see thou hurt not the oil and the wine.

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin, “Mangbuh pawta khatah hamu, tang pawta thumah hamu man hen. Sathau leh leenggahzute ahih leh, su kha kei in,” ci-in ganhing lite laizang pan mah bangin aw khat ka za hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ganhing lite lak panin awging bang khat ka za a, “Mangbuh lawhta khat in ni khat tha man a, mangbuhham lawhta thum in ni khat tha mah man hi. Ahi zongin oliv huan leh leenggui huan susia kei in,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8

**ZVS:** Ka khuadak leh sakol puang khat ka mu a, a tungah a tuangpa' min pen Sihna hi a, a nungah Hell in a zui hi. Amaute in namsau, kialpi, pulnatna, leh gamsate tawh leitung mi sehlisuah sehkhat a thahtheihna dingun thu a kipia hi.
