## Verses

### v2
**EN:** Let my prayer be set forth before thee as incense; and the lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrifice.

**TDB77:** Ka thungetna pen nangma mai-ah paknamtui, ka khutte ka laptohna pen nitak biak piakna bangsak in!

**ZVS:** Ka thungetna pen na mai-ah paknamtui bangsak in la, ka khutte ka laptohna pen nitak biakpiakna bangsak in.

### v3
**EN:** Set a watch, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, keima kam a cing ding khat koih in la, ka mukte’ kongkhak a vil ding khat koih in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, keima kam a cing ding khat koih in la, ka mukte' kongkhak a vil ding khat koih in.

### v4
**EN:** Incline not my heart to any evil thing, to practise wicked works with men that work iniquity: and let me not eat of their dainties.

**TDB77:** Mawhna bawl nopna lungsim hong nei sak kei in la, a gilo mite tawh hong kikhawlsak kei in. Amau anlimte hong duhgawh sak kei ta in.

**ZVS:** Mawh bawlnopna lungsim hong neisak kei in la, a gilo mite tawh hong kikhawlsak kei in. Amau' anlimte hong duhgawh sak kei ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Let the righteous smite me; it shall be a kindness: and let him reprove me; it shall be an excellent oil, which shall not break my head: for yet my prayer also shall be in their calamities.

**TDB77:** Mi phapa kei hong vuasak in la, ahih hangin migilopa ka lu sathau nilh sak ngei kei in; bang hang hiam cih leh amau’ gitloh gamtatnate lang pangin thu ka ngenngen hi.

**ZVS:** Miphapa kei hong vuasak in la, ahih hangin migilote ka lu sathau nilhsak kei in; bang hang hiam cih leh amau' gitloh gamtatnate nakpi takin langpangin thu ka ngenngen hi.

### v6
**EN:** When their judges are overthrown in stony places, they shall hear my words; for they are sweet.

**TDB77:** Amaute, thu kisitin mawhsakna a thuak uh ciangin, Topa’ thute man hi, ci-in a thei ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute, thu kisitin mawhsakna a thuak uh ciangin, { Topa' } thute man hi, ci-in a thei ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Our bones are scattered at the grave's mouth, as when one cutteth and cleaveth wood upon the earth.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in suangpi khat sukhamin leitungah a thehthang gawp bangin Leinuai’ kongvang ah amau guhte kithehthang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat in suangpi khat sukhamin leitungah a thehthang gawp bangin Leinuai' kongvangah amau' guhte kithehthang hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** But mine eyes are unto thee, O

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Topa Pasian aw, nang ka hong muang tawntung a, nangmah ka hong beel hi; kei hong nusia kei in!

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin { Topa } Pasian aw, nang ka hong muang tawntung a, nangmah ka hong bel hi. Tua ahih manin kei hong nusia kei in.

### v9
**EN:** Keep me from the snares which they have laid for me, and the gins of the workers of iniquity.

**TDB77:** Amaute in keimah hong siahna uh tehlop leh siatna bawlte’ thangzak panin kei hong kem in!

**ZVS:** Amaute in kei hong siahna uh tehlop leh siatna bawlte' thangzak panin kei hong kem in.

### v10
**EN:** Let the wicked fall into their own nets, whilst that I withal escape.

**TDB77:** Kei ka suahtak ciangin amau’ ngenthang sung mah-ah migilote awk sak in.

**ZVS:** Kei ka suahtak khit ciangin amau' ngenthang sung mahah migilote awksak in.
