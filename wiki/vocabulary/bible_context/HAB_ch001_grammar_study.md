### v2

**TDB77:** Topa aw, bang tan vei huhna hong ngen ding a, hong za lo niloh ding na hi hiam? Gimna thuak ung, ci-in bang tan vei hong kiko ding a, hong hon loin om niloh ding na hi hiam?
