## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, according to the promise of life which is in Christ Jesus,

**TDB77:** Khazih Zeisu sungah a om nuntakna hong pia ning ci-in a thuciamna mah bangin, Pasian’ deihna hangin Khazih Zeisu sawltak keimah Paul in:

**ZVS:** Khrih Jesuh tawh i kipawlna hangin hong kipia ding nuntakna thu a gen dingin Pasian' deihna tawh Khrih Jesuh' sawltak nasep a sem dingin a hong kisawl keimah Paul in

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** To Timothy, my dearly beloved son: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.

**TDB77:** Ka it ka tapa Timoti kong zasak hi. Pa Pasian leh i Topa Khazih Zeisu kiangpan hehpihna thupha, hehpihna leh nopna nang tungah om ta hen.

**ZVS:** Ka it ka ta Timoti tungah lai ka hong khak hi. Pa Pasian leh i Topa Khrih Jesuh in dikna, hehpihna, leh nopsaknathupha nang tungah hong tungsak ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** I thank God, whom I serve from my forefathers with pure conscience, that without ceasing I have remembrance of thee in my prayers night and day;

**TDB77:** A sun a zanin ka thungetna-ah nang ka hong phawk tawntung a, ka pu ka pate' sep mah bangin kisuan'na lungsim om hetloin a na ka sep Pasian tungah ka lungdamna ka ko hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Greatly desiring to see thee, being mindful of thy tears, that I may be filled with joy;

**TDB77:** Na khitui luan'nate phawk ka hih manin ka lung a damtheihna dingin nang tawh kimu nuamin a sun a zanin ka ngaihsun tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** When I call to remembrance the unfeigned faith that is in thee, which dwelt first in thy grandmother Lois, and thy mother Eunice; and I am persuaded that in thee also.

**TDB77:** Na pi Lois leh na nu Eunis in a neih upna bangte pawl mah, lungsim takpi tawh na upna thu ka phawk a, tu-in zong nang sungah tua bang upna om hi, ci-in ka ngaihsun hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** Wherefore I put thee in remembrance that thou stir up the gift of God, which is in thee by the putting on of my hands.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin nangma tungah ka khut ngakna tawh, nang, Pasian in hong piak letsong thupha na kuan’sak det nadingin kei in ka hong phawksak hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin nang tungah ka khut nga-in thu ka hong ngetsak lai-in nangma tungah Pasian in hong piak siamna na sosuahna dingin ka hong phawksak hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to

### v7
**EN:** For God hath not given us the spirit of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, Pasian in eite launa lungsim hong pia ahi kei hi; vangliatna, itna leh ei-le-ei ki-uk zawhna Kha hong pia zo hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian in eite tungah meidawina lungsim hong guan'lo a, thahatna, ki-itna, pumpi ki-ukzawhna lungsim hong guan' hizaw hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v8
**EN:** Be not thou therefore ashamed of the testimony of our Lord, nor of me his prisoner: but be thou partaker of the afflictions of the gospel according to the power of God;

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin Topa teci pan’na ahi zongin, tua Topa’ thongkiapa ahi keimah ah ahi zongin zum kei in. Pasian hong piak a vangliatna bangin Lungdamna Thu tawh kizomin gimna na thuak in.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin i Topa' thu genna ding thu-ah ahi a, ama thu hangin thong a kia keima thu-ah ahi zongin maizum het kei in. Pasian in nangma tungah thahatna hong pia ahih mah bangin Lungdamna Thu hangin thuakna-ah zong a thuaknuam hizaw in.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Who hath saved us, and called us with an holy calling, not according to our works, but according to his own purpose and grace, which was given us in Christ Jesus before the world began,

**TDB77:** Tua Pasian in eite gamtatna hang hi lo, tang lai a hun hong tun’ ma-in, Khazih Zeisu sungah eite ading ama hanciamna leh a hong piak hehpihna a ngaihsut kholhsa bangin eite hong gumkhia a, a siangtho sapna tawh hong sam hi.

**ZVS:** Eite' sepzawhna hang hiloin ama ngimnasa bang leh a dikna hangin Pasian in eite hong honkhia-in ama mi dingin a hong sam hi. Pasian in leitung a pian'ma pekin Khrih Jesuh tung tawnin tua dikna a hong piak hi a,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** But is now made manifest by the appearing of our Saviour Jesus Christ, who hath abolished death, and hath brought life and immortality to light through the gospel:

**TDB77:** Tua hehpihna thupha in eite hong Gumpa Topa Zeisu Khazih hong kilatna hangin tu-in a kilang zo hi. Tua Topa in sihna mangthang sak a, nuntakna leh a kisia thei lo nuntakna, Lungdamna Thu hangin hong langsak hi.

**ZVS:** tuhun ciangin eite hong Honpa Khrih Jesuh tung tawnin a dikna hong langsak ta hi. Tua Jesuh in sihna beisak a, lungdamna thu hangin sih nawnlohna thu a hong kilangsak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Whereunto I am appointed a preacher, and an apostle, and a teacher of the Gentiles.

**TDB77:** Tua thu hilh ding za-ah ahi zongin, sawltak za-ah ahi zongin, laihilh za-ah ahi zongin kei hong koih zo hi.

**ZVS:** Hih a thak Lungdamna Thu hilhin a taangko ding leh sawltak na a sem dingin kei a hong kikoih ahi hi.

### v12
**EN:** For the which cause I also suffer these things: nevertheless I am not ashamed: for I know whom I have believed, and am persuaded that he is able to keep that which I have committed unto him against that day.

**TDB77:** Tua thu hangah hih gimnate zong ka thuak hi. Ahih hangin ka zum kei hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh, ka uppa ka thei a, ama kiangah ka piak nate zong tua Ni dongin a kem zo ding hi ci-in ka um hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin hih bangteng a thuak ka hi hi. Ahi zongin ka uppa ka thei a, ama tungah ka apte tua Ni dong hong kepsakzo ding hi, ci-in thei ka hih manin ka maizum het kei hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

**TDB77:** Keima kiangah na zaksa, na ngahsa a pha hilhnate, Khazih Zeisu sungah a om upna leh itna tawh na lenkip in.

**ZVS:** Keima tung panin na zaksa a man thute zui in la, Khrih Jesuh tawh i kipawlna hangin i ngah upna leh itna thu-ah kiptakin om in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

**ZVS:** Onesiforas in tamveipi kei hong lungnuamsak ahih manin Pasian in a innkuan bupun hehpihna thupha pia ta hen. Ka thongkiatna hangin amah maizumlo a,

**ZVS:** Topa in tua Ni ciangin ama tungah hehpihna thupha pia ta hen. Efesa khua-ah ka omlai-in nakpi takin kei a hong huhnate khempeuh na theihsa ahi hi.
