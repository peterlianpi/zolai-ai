## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Make haste, O God, to deliver me; make haste to help me, O

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, keimah hong honkhia nuam in! Topa aw, keimah huh dingin hong manlang in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, keimah hong honkhia nuam in. { Topa } aw, keimah a huh dingin hong manlang in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v2
**EN:** Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt.

**TDB77:** Kei, thah hong sawmte maizumin kibuaigawpsak in! Keima siat nading a deihte nungkiksak in la, mindaisak in!

**ZVS:** Kei, thah hong sawmte maizumin kibuaigawp sak in. Keima siatna ding a deihte nungkiksak in la, mindaisak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Let them be turned back for a reward of their shame that say, Aha, aha.

**TDB77:** “Ha, ha!” a cite, a maizumna uh tawh maikhingsak in!

**ZVS:** “Ha, ha!” a cite a maizumna uh tawh maikhingsak in.

### v4
**EN:** Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: and let such as love thy salvation say continually, Let God be magnified.

**TDB77:** Nangmah hong zongte khempeuh lungdamin nangma sungah kipak ta hen! Na hotkhiatna a deihte in, “Pasian in lian hi!” ci ci ta hen.

**ZVS:** Nang hong zongte khempeuh lungdam mahmahin na min hangin kipak ta hen. Na hotkhiat ding a utte in, “Pasian lian hi,” cici ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** But I am poor and needy: make haste unto me, O God: thou art my help and my deliverer; O

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin keimah zawng-in ka cimawh hi, Pasian aw, hong manlang in! Nangmah in keimah huhpa leh hong honkhiatpa na hi hi; Topa aw, hong zekai kei in!

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin kei zawngin ka cimawh hi. Pasian aw, hong manlang in. Nang pen keimah huhpa leh hong honkhiapa na hi hi. { Topa } aw, hong zekai kei in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)
