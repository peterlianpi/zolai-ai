## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I said, I will take heed to my ways, that I sin not with my tongue: I will keep my mouth with a bridle, while the wicked is before me.

**TDB77:** “Ka leii tawh ka khialh loh nadingin ka gamtatna ka kikem ding a, ka omna-ah migilote a om lai teng ka kam ka kham ding hi,” ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka leii tawh ka khialhlohna dingin ka gamtatna ka kikem ding a, ka omna-ah migilote a omlaiteng ka kam ka kham ding hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** I was dumb with silence, I held my peace, even from good; and my sorrow was stirred.

**TDB77:** Pau loin ka dai om a, ka dai omna a mawkna hi; ka thuakna khang semsem a,

**ZVS:** Pauloin ka dai om a, ka dai omna a mawkna suak a, ka thuakna khang semsem hi.

### v3
**EN:** My heart was hot within me, while I was musing the fire burned: then spake I with my tongue,

**TDB77:** ka sungah ka lung so hi. Ka lungngaih ciangin ka lung so semsem hi; tua ciangin ka pau khia a:

**ZVS:** Ka sungah ka lung so a, ka lungngaih ciangin ka lung so semsem hi. Tua ciangin ka paukhia a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v5
**EN:** Behold, thou hast made my days as an handbreadth; and mine age is as nothing before thee: verily every man at his best state is altogether vanity. Selah.

**TDB77:** En in, ka nuntakna hun tuk khat tan bang bekin na bawl a, ka nuntakna hun nang’ muhna-ah bangmah gen tham lo hi. Mihing khempeuh in husan bang bek hi takpi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka nuntakna hun khutme tan bang bekin na bawl a, ka nuntakna hun nangma muhna-ah bangmah genthamlo hi. Mihing khempeuh, husan' bang bek hi takpi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v6
**EN:** Surely every man walketh in a vain shew: surely they are disquieted in vain: he heapeth up riches, and knoweth not who shall gather them.

**TDB77:** Ni liim bangin mihing pai kawikawi takpi hi. A mawkna-in amau lungkham takpi uh a, kua in ngah ding cih thei lopi-in mite in nate khol hi.

**ZVS:** Niliim bangin mihing pai kawikawi takpi hi. A mawkna-in amau lungkham takpi uh a, kua' ngah ding cih theilopi-in mite in na a khol uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** And now, Lord, what wait I for? my hope is in thee.

**TDB77:** “Tu-in Topa aw, ke’n bang ngak lai ka hi hiam? Ka lametna nang sungah om hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in Topa aw, ke'n, bang ngaklai ding ka hi hiam? Keima lametna nangma sung bekah om hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** Deliver me from all my transgressions: make me not the reproach of the foolish.

**TDB77:** Ka thu palsatna khempeuh panin kei hong honkhia in. Mihaite’ nuihsatin kei hong bawl kei in!

**ZVS:** Ka thupalsatna khempeuh panin kei hong honkhia in la, mite' nuihsatin kei hong bawl kei in.

### v9
**EN:** I was dumb, I opened not my mouth; because thou didst it.

**TDB77:** Hih banga bawl nangmah na hih manin pau loin ka om a, ka kam ka ka kei hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bangin a hong bawl nangmah na hih manin pauloin ka om a, ka kam ka ka kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | causal: because

### v10
**EN:** Remove thy stroke away from me: I am consumed by the blow of thine hand.

**TDB77:** Nong vuakna ma ka tung panin lakhia in; na khut tawh nong vuakna-in ka tha bei ta hi.

**ZVS:** Nong vuakna ma ka tung panin lakhia in. Na hong vuakna hangin ka tha beita hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** When thou with rebukes dost correct man for iniquity, thou makest his beauty to consume away like a moth: surely every man is vanity. Selah.

**TDB77:** Mawhna hangin mi satin na tai ciangin, nget in a nektum bangin ama na manpha na beisak hi; mi khempeuh in husan’ khat bang bek hi takpi hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat a mawhna hangin tai-in na sat ciangin, nget in a nektum bangin ama na manpha na beisak hi. Mihing khempeuh husan' khat bang bek hi takpi hi.

### v12
**EN:** Hear my prayer, O

**TDB77:** “Topa aw, ka thungetna hong ngai in la, ka kikona hong za in; ka khitui luan’na hong don loin om mawk kei in; Bang hang hiam cih leh ka pu ka pate khempeuh bangin keimah in na zin, na peemta ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka thungetna hong ngai in la, ka kikona hong za in. Ka khitui luan'na hong thudon in. Bang hang hiam cih leh ka pu ka pate khempeuh bangin kei zong na zin, na peemta ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** O spare me, that I may recover strength, before I go hence, and be no more.

**TDB77:** Ka man’than’ hun matengin ka lungnop theih nadingin kei hong en kei in!”

**ZVS:** Ka man'than' hun matengin ka lungnop theihna dingin kei hong en in!

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to
