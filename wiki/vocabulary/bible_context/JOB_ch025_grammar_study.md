## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Shuah gammi Bildad in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places.

**TDB77:** “Pasian vanglian a, mi khempeuh in a zahtak ding ahi hi. Ama vantung kumpi gam, nuam takin a omsak zo hi.

**ZVS:** “Pasian vanglian a, mi khempeuh in a zahtak ding ahi hi. Ama vantung kumpi gam, nuamtakin a omsakzo hi.

### v3
**EN:** Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise?

**TDB77:** Ama na a sem a vantungmite a phazah kua in sim zo ding ahi hiam? Pasian’ khuavak in a bat lohna koi lai mun om ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Ama na a sem vantung mite bangzah pha cih kua in simzo ding ahi hiam? Pasian' khuavak in a batlohna koi lai mun om ahi hiam?

### v4
**EN:** How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman?

**TDB77:** Pasian’ muhna-ah kua dik zo ding a, kua siangtho zo ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian' muhna-ah kua dikzo ding a, siangthozo ding ahi hiam?

### v5
**EN:** Behold even to the moon, and it shineth not; yea, the stars are not pure in his sight.

**TDB77:** Ama mit-ah khapi nangawn taang sa zo lo a, aksite nangawn siang sa zo lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ama mitah khapi nangawn taang sa zolo lai a, aksite nangawn siang sa zolo lai hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v6
**EN:** How much less man, that is a worm? and the son of man, which is a worm?

**TDB77:** Tua ahih leh, lungno, ganhing no ahi lel mihing pen bangci ding hiam? Mihing in Pasian’ muhna-ah bang man lel ahi hiam?” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih leh, lungno, ganhing no ahi lel mihing pen bangci ding ahi hiam? Mihing in Pasian' muhna-ah bang man lel ahi hiam?” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying
