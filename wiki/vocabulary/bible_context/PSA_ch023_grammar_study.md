## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in tuu cin’ bang-a kei hong cingpa hi a, bangmah ka kisam kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } pen tuucin' bangin hong cing hi a, kisapna teng kicing ta hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** He maketh me to lie down in green pastures: he leadeth me beside the still waters.

**TDB77:** Lo nona-ah keimah hong lumsak a, tui kiangah kei hong paipih hi.

**ZVS:** Lo nona-ah hong lumsak a, tuidam kiangah hong paipih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** He restoreth my soul: he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake.

**TDB77:** Ka nuntakna tha hong dimsak a, ama min’ adingin thuman lampi-ah kei hong kha hi.

**ZVS:** Thahatna thak kei hong guan' hi. A hong ciamsa mah bangin-ah lampi manah kei hong kha hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil: for thou art with me; thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.

**TDB77:** Sihna lim guam sung nangawn ka nawk hangin, siatna ka kihta kei hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh kei tawh nang nong omkhawm hi. Na molhtum leh na ciangkhut in kei hong lungmuang sak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kei hong ompih na hih manin khuamial thukpi ka nawk hangin ka lau kei hi. Na molhtum leh na ciangkhut in kei hong hu hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies: thou anointest my head with oil; my cup runneth over.

**TDB77:** Ka galte omna mai-ah kei’ ading an nong lui hi; ka lu sathau nong nilh a, ka hai a dimlet hi.

**ZVS:** Keima galte' muhna munah an nong lui hi. Thupi takin nong pahtawi a, keima hai zong dimlet ta hi.

### v6
**EN:** Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life: and I will dwell in the house of the

**TDB77:** Hoihna leh hehpihna in ka khan’ tawntungin hong zui taktak ding a, Topa’ inn sungah ka om tawntung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na hoihna leh nong itna in ka khan'tawnin a hong zuih ding tel takin-ah ka thei ta a, a tawntungin na inn sungah ka om ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
