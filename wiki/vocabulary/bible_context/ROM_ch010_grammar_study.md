## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Brethren, my heart's desire and prayer to God for Israel is, that they might be saved.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, Israel mite in gupna a ngah nadingun ka lungsim in deihin, Pasian kiangah thu ka ngetsak hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, i mipihte in hotkhiatna a ngahna ding deihsakin lungsim takpi tawh Pasian kiangah thu ka ngetsak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** For I bear them record that they have a zeal of God, but not according to knowledge.

**TDB77:** Amaute in Pasian lamah lungsim lian a neih-te hangin, lungpilna man ahi kei hi ci-in amaute ading teci ka pang hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in Pasian a kin mahmah lamtak-te thei-in teci-in ka pang ngam hi. Ahi zongin amaute in a man lampi tawh kin khalo-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** For they being ignorant of God's righteousness, and going about to establish their own righteousness, have not submitted themselves unto the righteousness of God.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, amaute in Pasian’ dikna thei loin, amau dikna kipsak nading a zong-te ahih ciangin, Pasian’ dikna thu a mang kei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in Pasian tawh kipawl theihna ding lampi man theiloin amau theihtawmna tawh hanciam kha-te ahih manin amah tawh kipawl theihna dingin lampi a khun' zui khalo-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh upna a nei mi khempeuh Pasian tawh a kipawl theihna dingin Khrih in Thukham a beisak hi.

### v5
**EN:** For Moses describeth the righteousness which is of the law, That the man which doeth those things shall live by them.

**TDB77:** Moses in, “Thukham tawh a kipawl dikna thu a zuite in, tua dikna thu bangin a nungta ding hi,” ci-in a gelh hi.

**ZVS:** Moses in a laigelhna sungah, “Thukham deihna bangin a gamtazo mi khempeuh a nungta ding hi,” ci-in Thukham zuihna hangin Pasian tawh kipawl theihna ding thu a na gen hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

### v7

**TDB77:** A thuk sungah kua in a kumsuk ding hiam?” ci-in na lung sungah na ngaihsun kei un a ci hi. Tua bang a cihna in, Khazih hong paipihsuk dingin kua in a kahto ding hiam? Khazih sihna pan a thosak dingin a thuk sungah kua in a kumsuk ding hiam? a cihnopna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin upna hangin Pasian tawh kipawl theihna thu a kigenna-ah, “Vantungah kua va paito ding a,” ci kei un la, “Leinuai-ah kua va paisuk ding ahi hiam?” zong ci kei un, ci hi. Tua a cihna pen vantung panin Khrih a lasuk ding leh sihna panin a thosak ding cihna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v8

**TDB77:** Bang a ci hiam? Tua thu in nang tawh kinai-in na kam sungah, na lungsim sungah a om hi; ka hilh-te upna thu a cihnopna hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang a cih ciangin, “Pasian' thu pen nai mahmahin note kam sung leh na lungsim sung uhah om hi,” a cihnopna ahi hi. Tua Pasian' thu pen kote' hilhhilh, “Pasian um un,” cihna thu ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, “Zeisu in Topa ahi hi” ci-in na kam in mang a, Pasian in amah sihna pan a thosak kik zo hi ci-in na lungsim sungah na up leh, gupna na ngah ding hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh pen Topa hi, ci-in mite kiangah genin, Pasian in sihna panin amah hingkiksak zo hi, ci-in na lungsim sung uhah na up taktak-te leh hotkhiatna na ngah ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

**ZVS:** Tua ahih leh Israel mite in a thei-te hiam? Moses mahmah in Pasian' min tawh thu na gen a, “Keima mi pawl a hilote thupha pia-in, note' hazat dingin ka bawl ding a, tua mihaite' hangin note na hehna dingun ka hong bawl ding hi,” a cih mah bangin Israel mite in a thu a theilo-te hilo hi.
