### v1
**EN:** Sing aloud unto God our strength: make a joyful noise unto the God of Jacob.

**TDB77:** Eite’ thahatna ahi Pasian’ tungah nakpi-in la sa un la, Jakob Pasian’ tungah lungdamin awng un!

**ZVS:** Eite' thahatna Pasian' tungah nakpi takin la sa un la, Jakob Pasian' tungah lungdamin awng un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** Take a psalm, and bring hither the timbrel, the pleasant harp with the psaltery.

**TDB77:** La khat phuang-in, khuangno tum-in, a ngaih laiza khausai lasakna leh harp khausai lasakna tum un.

**ZVS:** La khat phuangin, khuangno tumin, a ngaih laia khausai lasakna leh harp khausai lasakna limtakin tum un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** Blow up the trumpet in the new moon, in the time appointed, on our solemn feast day.

**TDB77:** Kha-det, kha-liik, i pawi ni-in pengkul tum un.

**ZVS:** Khadet, khalik, i pawi ni-in peengkul tum un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** For this was a statute for Israel, and a law of the God of Jacob.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, tua in, Israelte’ adingin thukham hi a, Jakob Pasian’ biakna ngeina kip khat ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

### v7
**EN:** Thou calledst in trouble, and I delivered thee; I answered thee in the secret place of thunder: I proved thee at the waters of Meribah. Selah.

**TDB77:** Na cihmawh uh ciangin kei nong sam uh a, keimah in note kong honkhia hi; keimah in, a kilang lo vanging sung panin note kong dawng a, Meribah tui phualte-ah note kong ze-et hi.

**ZVS:** Na cihmawh uh ciangin kei na hong sam uh a, keimah in note ka hong honkhia hi. A kilanglo vanging sung panin note ka hong dawng a, Meribah tui phualte-ah note ka hong ze-et hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8

**TDB77:** Ka mite aw, note kong thuhilh laitakin hong ngai un! Israelte aw, ka thu hong ngai hi hoh le uh cin!

**ZVS:** Ka mite aw, note kong thuhilh laitakin limtakin hong ngai un. Israel-te aw, keima thu a hong ngai hi hoh le-uh cin!

### v9
