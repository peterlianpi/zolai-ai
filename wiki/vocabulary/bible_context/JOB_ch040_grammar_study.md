**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Shall he that contendeth with the Almighty instruct him? he that reproveth God, let him answer it.

**TDB77:** “Job aw, nang in Vanglian Pasian na dem hi; tu-in khawl ding na hi hiam, tua ahih kei leh ama thugen dawng ding na hi hiam?” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, nangmah in Vanglian Pasian nong dem hi. Tu-in khawl ding na hiam, ahih kei leh keima thugen nong dawng ding hiam?” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Job in Topa dawng a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Job in dawng a,

### v4
**EN:** Behold, I am vile; what shall I answer thee? I will lay mine hand upon my mouth.

**TDB77:** “Topa aw, ka haipau kha hi. Hong bangci dawn’ theih ding ka hi hiam? Bangmah ka pau nawn kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** “{ Topa } aw, ka haipau kha hi. Hong bangci dawn' theih ding ka hi hiam? Tu-a kipanin ka pau nawn kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka gen dinga kilawm zahval genkha ka hih manin, tu-a kipanin ka pau nawn het kei ding hi,” a ci hi.
