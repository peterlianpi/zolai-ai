## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise waiteth for thee, O God, in Zion: and unto thee shall the vow be performed.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, Zion-ah nangmah phat dingin kilawm hi; nangma tunga thuciamna sate hong kibawl ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, Zion-ah nangmah phat dingin kilawm a, nangma tunga thuciamsate hong kitungsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** O thou that hearest prayer, unto thee shall all flesh come.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in thungetna a za-pa na hi hi! Mawhna thu hangin nangma kiangah mi khempeuh hong pai ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Thungetna a zapa na hi a, mawhna hangin nangma kiangah mi khempeuh hong pai ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Iniquities prevail against me: as for our transgressions, thou shalt purge them away.

**TDB77:** Ka mawhnate un kote hong zawh ciangin, nangmah in tuate nong maisak hi.

**ZVS:** Ka mawhnate un kote hong zawh ciangin, nangmah in tuate na hong maisak hi.

### v4
**EN:** Blessed is the man whom thou choosest, and causest to approach unto thee, that he may dwell in thy courts: we shall be satisfied with the goodness of thy house, even of thy holy temple.

**TDB77:** Na inn-a om dinga na tel na lakte in mi nuamsa ahi hi! Nangma biakinn siangtho ahi na inn sunga hoihna tawh lungkim nung!

**ZVS:** Nangma inn-ah a om dingin na teel na lakte mi nuamsa ahi hi. Nangma biakinn siangtho, na innsunga hoihna tawh kote ka lungkim ding-te hi.

### v5
**EN:** By terrible things in righteousness wilt thou answer us, O God of our salvation; who art the confidence of all the ends of the earth, and of them that are afar off upon the sea:

**TDB77:** Tuipi gamla pente leh leimong dong khempeuhte’ lametna, komau’ kihotkhiatna Pasian,

**ZVS:** Tuipi gamla pente leh leimong khempeuhte' lamet, kote a hong honkhia Pasian,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Which by his strength setteth fast the mountains; being girded with power:

**TDB77:** nangma thahatna tawh mualte a bawl, vangliatna tawh a kidim,

**ZVS:** a thahatna tawh mualte a bawl, vangliatna tawh a kidim,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Which stilleth the noise of the seas, the noise of their waves, and the tumult of the people.

**TDB77:** tuipi gingte, tuihual gingte, mi gamlumte a daisakpa aw,

**ZVS:** tuipi gingte, tuihualte, mi gamlumte a daisakpa aw,

### v8
**EN:** They also that dwell in the uttermost parts are afraid at thy tokens: thou makest the outgoings of the morning and evening to rejoice.

**TDB77:** lei mong pek-a omte in na hihsa limte a kihtak nadingun kihtakhuai gamtatna zangin, hotkhiatna tawh kote’ ngetna nong pia hi; nisuahna leh nitumna kikal-a om mite khempeuh lungdam-a awng dingin na bawl hi.

**ZVS:** leimong peka omte in na hihna limte a kihtakna dingun a kihtakhuai gamtatna zangin, nong hotkhiatna tawh kote' ngetna nong pia hi. Nisuahna leh nitumna kikalah a om mi khempeuhte lungdamna tawh a awng dingin na bawl hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Thou visitest the earth, and waterest it: thou greatly enrichest it with the river of God, which is full of water: thou preparest them corn, when thou hast so provided for it.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in leitung en-in guah na zusak a, a hoihna lianpi-in na behlap hi; Pasian’ gunpi in tui tawh kidim hi; nangmah in amaute khaici na guan hi, tua ahih ciangin nangmah in na bawl khol hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in leitung enin, guah na hong zusak a, leitung a hoihna nakpi takin na behlap hi. Pasian' gunpi tui tawh kidim a, leitungah an na piangsak hi. Tua bangin nangmah in na khempeuh na bawlkhol hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Thou waterest the ridges thereof abundantly: thou settlest the furrows thereof: thou makest it soft with showers: thou blessest the springing thereof.

**TDB77:** Tu ma-kuakte tui tampi na buak a, leivumte zalh mai-in, guahtui tawh na nipsak a, a po kungte thupha na pia hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-ma-kuakte khempeuh tui tampi tak na buak a, leivumte zalhmai-in, guahtui tawh na nipsak a, a po kungte thupha na pia hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** Thou crownest the year with thy goodness; and thy paths drop fatness.

**TDB77:** Nangma hauhna tawh kum na phasak a, na leng paina tengah sathau taak hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma hauhna tawh kum na phasak a, na leeng paina khempeuhah sathau a taak hi.

### v12
**EN:** They drop upon the pastures of the wilderness: and the little hills rejoice on every side.

**TDB77:** Ganhing tatna, a noptuak munte-ah ganhing hon omin mualte lungdamna tawh kidim a,

**ZVS:** Ganhing tatna, a noptuak mahmah munte-ah ganhing honte omin, mualte khempeuh zong lungdamna tawh kidim a,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** The pastures are clothed with flocks; the valleys also are covered over with corn; they shout for joy, they also sing.

**TDB77:** lo no-na munte tuuhon tawh kizem-in, kuampite an tawh kidim a, lungdamin awngin amaute in la sakhawm-te hi.

**ZVS:** gamlak lonona munte tuuhon tawh kizemin, kuampite an tawh kidim a, amaute lungdamin awngin la a sa khawm-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
