## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I cried unto the

**TDB77:** Topa’ hong huh ding ngenin ka kiko a, Topa tungah ka thuum hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } hong huh ding ngenin ka kiko a, { Topa' } tungah ka thum hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** I poured out my complaint before him; I shewed before him my trouble.

**TDB77:** Ama mai-ah ka lungkim lohna ka sung a, ama mai-ah ka haksatna ka gen hi.

**ZVS:** Ama mai-ah ka lungkimlohna ka sung a, ama mai-ah ka haksatna teng ka gen hi.

### v3
**EN:** When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, then thou knewest my path. In the way wherein I walked have they privily laid a snare for me.

**TDB77:** Ka cih nading ka theih nawn loh ciangin, amah in ka mailam ding hong theihsak hi. Ka paina lampi-ah ka galte in tehlop khat tawh kei hong siahsim-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ka cihna ding ka theih nawnloh ciangin, amah in ka mailam ding hong theihsak hi. Ka paina lampi-ah ka galte in tehlop khat tawh kei hong siah-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** I looked on my right hand, and beheld, but there was no man that would know me: refuge failed me; no man cared for my soul.

**TDB77:** Ka kiim ka pam enin ka khuadak a, ahih hangin kei hong huh ding kuamah om lo hi; ka belh ding kuamah om lo a, kuamah in kei hong don lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ka kiim ka pam khuadakin ka en kawikawi a, ahih hangin kei hong huh ding kuamah omlo hi. Ka belh ding kuamah omlo a, kuamah in kei hong donlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** I cried unto thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nang tungah kong kiko hi; nang in keima belh na hi a, a hingte’ leitangah keima tanh na hi hi, kong ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nang tungah kong kiko hi. Nang pen keima belh na hi a, a hingte' leitangah keima tanh na hi hi, ka hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Attend unto my cry; for I am brought very low: deliver me from my persecutors; for they are stronger than I.

**TDB77:** Ka kikona hong ngai in; bang hang hiam cih leh kei hong kiniamkoih mahmah hi! Kei hong bawlsiate’ kiang panin hong honkhia in; bang hang hiam cih leh kei’ adingin amaute hatlua-te hi!

**ZVS:** Ka kikona hong ngai in. Bang hang hiam cih leh nakpi takin kei hong kiniamkoih suk hi. Kei hong bawlsiate' kiang pan hong honkhia in. Bang hang hiam cih leh kei-a' dingin amaute hatlua-te hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Bring my soul out of prison, that I may praise thy name: the righteous shall compass me about; for thou shalt deal bountifully with me.

**TDB77:** Thong sung panin kei hong lakhia in! Tua ciangin nangma mite tawh na min kong phat khawm ding-te hi; bang hang hiam cih leh nang in itna lianpi tawh kei tungah nong gamta hi.

**ZVS:** Thong sung panin kei hong lakhia in. Tua ciangin nangma mite tawh kong phatkhawm ding-te hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh itna lianpi tawh kei tungah nong gamta hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | plural: uh marker
