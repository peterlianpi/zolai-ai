## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na hehna tawh hong tai kei in la, lungso sa-in hong thuhilh kei in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, a hehsa-in kei hong taii kei in la, lungsosa-in kei hong thuhilh kei in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** For thine arrows stick fast in me, and thy hand presseth me sore.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh na thaltangte ka sungah hong tum khin a, ka tungah na khut hong tu khin hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nangma thaltangte ka sungah hong tum khin a, ka tungah na khuttum hong tu khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v3
**EN:** There is no soundness in my flesh because of thine anger; neither is there any rest in my bones because of my sin.

**TDB77:** Nangma hehna hangin ka ci na a, ka mawhna hangin ka pumtangin ka na hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma hehna hangin kei ka ci na a, ka mawhna hangin ka pumtangin ka na hi.

### v4
**EN:** For mine iniquities are gone over mine head: as an heavy burden they are too heavy for me.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ka mawhnate in ka lu tuum zo a, keima puak ding a giklua van-gik tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ka mawhnate in ka lu dong hong tuumzo a, keima puak dingin a giklua vangik tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** My wounds stink and are corrupt because of my foolishness.

**TDB77:** Ka haina hangin ka liamnate nai-in uih a,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** I am troubled; I am bowed down greatly; I go mourning all the day long.

**TDB77:** hong kikunsak, hong kibokcip sak hi; sunthapai-in kapin ka vak kawikawi hi.

**ZVS:** keimah hong kikunsak a, hong kibokcipsak hi. Sunthapai-in kapin ka vak kawikawi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** For my loins are filled with a loathsome disease: and there is no soundness in my flesh.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ka ci sa mahmah a, nakpi takin ka ci na hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v8
**EN:** I am feeble and sore broken: I have roared by reason of the disquietness of my heart.

**TDB77:** Ka tha bei khinin hong kinengcip hi; ka lungsim buaina hangin ka tau hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong kinengcip gawpin ka thahatna beimang khin a, ka lungsim gimna hangin thumin ka tautau hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Lord, all my desire is before thee; and my groaning is not hid from thee.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka deihna khempeuh nang na thei a, ka khuisatna nang kiang pan kiseel lo hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka deihna khempeuh na thei a, ka khuisatnate na za hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** My heart panteth, my strength faileth me: as for the light of mine eyes, it also is gone from me.

**TDB77:** Ka lungtang kisat a, ka tha in lel hi; ka mit in zong khua mu thei nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungtang kisat a, ka tha in lel hi. Ka mit in zong khua mu thei nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my sore; and my kinsmen stand afar off.

**TDB77:** Ka gim thuakna-ah ka lawmte leh ka kithuahpihte kidaang koih uh a, ka behte kei kiang pan gamla om uh hi.

**ZVS:** Ka gim thuakna-ah ka lawmte leh ka kithuahpihte kidang koih uh a, keima behte nangawn kei kiang pan gamla om uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** They also that seek after my life lay snares for me: and they that seek my hurt speak mischievous things, and imagine deceits all the day long.

**TDB77:** Kei hong that nuamte in a thang uh zakin, kei’ nat nading a zongte in siatna thu gen uh a, sunthapai-in khemna bek ngaihsun uh hi.

**ZVS:** Kei a hong that nuamte in a thang uh zak a, kei' natna ding a zongte in siatna ding thu bek gen uh a, sunthapai-in khemna bekbek ngaihsutin nei uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** But I, as a deaf man, heard not; and I was as a dumb man that openeth not his mouth.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin kei pen bilngong ka bang a, khua ka za kei hi, a kam ka lo, a pau theilo mi ka bang hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin kei pen bilngong ka bang a, khua ka za kei hi. A kam a ka theilo, a pau theilo mi ka bang hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v14
**EN:** Thus I was as a man that heareth not, and in whose mouth are no reproofs.

**TDB77:** Hi hi, bil khua za lo, taina kam a zang lo mi khat tawh kei ka kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Bil khua zalo, tawn'na kam a za theilo mi khat tawh kei ka kibang takpi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15

### v16
**EN:** For I said, Hear me, lest otherwise they should rejoice over me: when my foot slippeth, they magnify themselves against me.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, “Ka khe a taal ciangin kei tungah hong kihisakte, kei’ tungah lungdamsak kei un,” cih bek kong thum hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, “Ka khe a taal ciangin kei tunga hong kihisakte, keima gim thuakna-ah lungdam sak kei in,” cih bek ka hong thum hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** For I am ready to halt, and my sorrow is continually before me.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh puk dingin ka kithawi a, ka gimna kam tuan lo hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh puk dingin ka kithawi a, ka gimna kam tuanlo hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18

**ZVS:** Ka thumanlohna ka pulak a, ka mawhna hangin ka dah hi.
