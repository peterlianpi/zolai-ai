# Psalms Chapter 26 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 11 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 3 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 3 |
| negation: kei | 2 |
| future: will | 2 |
| causal: because | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Judge me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, a hoih-in gamta-in, lungka loin Topa muang ka hih manin, ka thutan’na hong lak in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, a hoihin gamta-in { Topa } hong muang ka hih manin, ka thutan'na hong lak in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because

### v2
**EN:** Examine me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, keimah hong ze-et in la hong sin in; ka lungsim leh ka ngaihsutna hong entel in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, hong ze-et in la hong sin in. Ka lungsim leh ka ngaihsutna hong entel in.

### v3
**EN:** For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh na itna kip ka mai-ah om a, na tungah citakin ka gamta hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh na itna kip in lam hong lak a, nangma tungah citakin ka gamta hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers.

**TDB77:** Mikhialte tawh tukhawm loin, thusim neite tawh ka kipawl kei hi;

**ZVS:** Mikhialte tawh tukhawm loin, thusim neite tawh ka kipawl kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** I have hated the congregation of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked.

**TDB77:** siatna bawl mihonte ka mudah a, migilote tawh ka tukhawm kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Siatna a bawl mite ka mudah a, migilote tawh ka tukhawm kei hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v6
**EN:** I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, mawh baan’lohna ka lahna-in ka khutte ka sil a, biakna pia-in na biakna tau kim-ah ka pai hi;

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, mawh baan'lohna ka lahna-in ka khutte ka sil a, biakna pia-in na biakna tau kiimah ka pai hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works.

**TDB77:** lungdam kohna la a gingin sa-in, nangma nasepna lamdangte ka gen kawm hi.

**ZVS:** Lungdam kohna la a gingin sa-in, nangma nasep a lamdangte ka gen kawm hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** In whose hands is mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes.

**TDB77:** Mimawhte, mi that nuamte, siatna a bawl mite, leh golhguk a la nuamte tawh kei hong susia khawm kei in.

### v11
**EN:** But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin kei ahih leh, ka hoih gamtat hi; hong tankhia in la kei hong hehpih in.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin kei ahih leh, ka hoih gamtat hi. Hong hehpih in la, kei hong honkhia in.

### v12
**EN:** My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I bless the

**TDB77:** Leizang mun-ah ka ding hi; mihon tampi sungah Topa ka phat ding hi.

**ZVS:** Leizang munah ka ding hi. Mihon tampi lakah { Topa } ka phat ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
