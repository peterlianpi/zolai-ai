### v1

**TDB77:** Hihte in, Noah’ tapate Shem, Ham leh Jafethte’ suan-le-khakte’ tangthu ahi hi; tuiciin khit ciangin amau' sung panin tapate hong piang hi.

### v9

**ZVS:** Amah pen { Topa' } mai-ah a minthang sabengpa zong ahi hi. Tua ahih ciangin, “{ Topa } in Nimrod bangin a minthang sabengpa hong suaksak ta hen,” kici hi.
