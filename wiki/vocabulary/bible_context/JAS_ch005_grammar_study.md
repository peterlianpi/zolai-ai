## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Go to now, ye rich men, weep and howl for your miseries that shall come upon you.

**TDB77:** Sum haute aw, tu-in hong pai un; note tungah hong tung ding gimna tawlna hangin kiko-in kap un.

**ZVS:** A haute aw, tu-in ka thugen hong ngai dih un. Note tungah a hong tungding lungkhamna hangin kapin thum un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Your riches are corrupted, and your garments are motheaten.

**TDB77:** Na sum na paaite-te muat gawpin a om hi. Na puante-te zong nget in a ne khin zo hi.

**ZVS:** Na neihsate-te muattumta a, na puante-te nget in netum khinta hi.

### v3
**EN:** Your gold and silver is cankered; and the rust of them shall be a witness against you, and shall eat your flesh as it were fire. Ye have heaped treasure together for the last days.

**TDB77:** Na kham na ngunte-te zong a keekseu khin hi. Tuate keekseuna in note lang khatah teci hi a, mei bangin note’ sa hong ne ding-te hi. Note in a nunung bel ni hi napi-in sum na khol-te hi.

**ZVS:** Na ngun leh na khamte-te kekseu khinta hi. Hih kekseuna pen note langah teci suak a, mei in a kattum bangin na sa-te hong netum ding hi. Hih a hun nunung ciangin tu-a bangin a piang dingin neihsa a khol na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | completed: already done | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Behold, the hire of the labourers who have reaped down your fields, which is of you kept back by fraud, crieth: and the cries of them which have reaped are entered into the ears of the Lord of sabaoth.

**TDB77:** Ngai un, note’ lo lai-ah an atte na kheem-te a, a thaman-te pia loin na om-te a hih ciangin tua thaman in kiko-in a om hi. Tua lo-ah a at mite a kikona aw un zong vantungmite’ Topa’ bil sungah a tum zo hi.

**ZVS:** Na lo-te a hong khawhsakte a thaman pialo na hih manun tu-in amaute' phunna za un. Anla dingin na sawl-te mite' phunna Vanglian Topa in za khinta hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Ye have lived in pleasure on the earth, and been wanton; ye have nourished your hearts, as in a day of slaughter.

**TDB77:** Note in leitungah lungdam nopsakna ngahin na om zo-te hi. Kithah ni adingin na lungsim-te na vaak thau zo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Hih leitung na nuntak sungun uang nekin na noptat mahmah-te hi. Note hong kigawh hun ding kigin'na-in a kithausak phitphet na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Ye have condemned and killed the just; and he doth not resist you.

**TDB77:** Midik tungah thu na khen-te a, na that zo-te hi. Amah in no hong thuk kei hi.

**ZVS:** A thuman mite mawhsakin na that-te a, amaute in na thu-te hong nial zolo-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Be patient therefore, brethren, unto the coming of the Lord. Behold, the husbandman waiteth for the precious fruit of the earth, and hath long patience for it, until he receive the early and latter rain.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ute naute aw, Topa hong pai matengin thuakin ngak un. Lokho mi in guahzu masa leh guahzu nunung a zuk matengin a manpha leitungah gahte thuum loin ngakin a lamen hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin sanggamte aw, Topa hong pai matengin lungduai takin om un. Lo khote in zong a lo panun an a ngahtheihna dingun lungduai takin khuapi guah leh tuk guah a ngakna thu thei un.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v8
**EN:** Be ye also patient; stablish your hearts: for the coming of the Lord draweth nigh.

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin note in zong thuakin ngak un. Na lungsim-te na kipsak un. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, Topa hong pai ding a nai zo hi.

**ZVS:** Note zong na lungduai un. Topa hong paiding hun naita ahih manin na lungsim-te na thahatsak un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Grudge not one against another, brethren, lest ye be condemned: behold, the judge standeth before the door.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, thukhenna tawh na kipelh nadingun khat leh na kiphunsan kei un. En un, kongkhak kiangah Thukhenpa ding-in a om hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, Pasian in note tungah thu hong khenkhak lohna dingin khatlekhat na kiphunsan kei un. Thukhen dingpa hong nai mahmah ta a, hong tung dektak ta hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Take, my brethren, the prophets, who have spoken in the name of the Lord, for an example of suffering affliction, and of patience.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, gimna tawlna-ah Topa’ min tawh thu a hilh kamsangte’ lim na ciamteh un.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, Topa' min tawh thu a gen kamsangte na phawk un. Gimna a thuak laitakun lungduai-in a thuaknate-te na etteh un.

### v11
**EN:** Behold, we count them happy which endure. Ye have heard of the patience of Job, and have seen the end of the Lord; that the Lord is very pitiful, and of tender mercy.

**TDB77:** En un, a thuak zo mite in thupha a ngah mite a hi-te ci-in i gen hi. Job’ thuakna thu na za zo-te hi. Pasian in ama thu a zawh khit ciangin a hehpih theih mahmahna thu zong na thei zo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bangin a thuak mite a hampha mahmah hi, i ci hi. Job in a thuakna-ah a lungduaina na thei-te a, Topa in dikna leh hehpihna nei ahih manin a tawpna-ah Job tungah a piak thuphate na thei-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** But above all things, my brethren, swear not, neither by heaven, neither by the earth, neither by any other oath: but let your yea be yea; and your nay, nay; lest ye fall into condemnation.

**TDB77:** Ka u ka naute aw, thu khempeuh tungah kiciamna pha takin na tang un; vantung tawh na kiciam kei un, leitung tawh na kiciam kei un. Bangmah dang tawh na kiciam kei un. Thukhenna tawh na kipelh theih nadingun ah, “Ahi hi,” na cih-te ahi mah hi ta hen; “Ahi kei hi,” na cih-te a hi lo mah hi ta hen.

**ZVS:** Hih thute khempeuh banah, sanggamte aw, thu khat peuhpeuh na gen-te ciangin kiciam se kei un. Vantung ahi a, na khat peuhpeuh tawh ahi zongin kiciam kei un. Pasian' thukhenna na thuakkhak lohna dingun a hite, “Hi,” ci lel un la, a hilote, “Hilo,” na ci lel un.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v13

**TDB77:** Note lakah mi khat peuhmah gimna thuak a om hiam? Amah in thungen ta hen. Khat peuhmah a lungdam leh, la sa ta hen.

**ZVS:** Lungkhamna a thuak na om-te leh thu na ngen un. A lungdam na om-te leh phatna la na sa un.

**ZVS:** A cina na om-te leh pawlpi upate sam un. Amaute in thu hong ngetsakin Topa min tawh sathau hong zut ding-te hi.

### v15

**TDB77:** Tua bang na bawl-te leh, upna thungetna in cinate a noptuamsak ding a, Topa in amah a thosak ding hi. Amah in mawhna ahih khak leh zong, tua mawhna a maisak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Upna tawh thu a kinget ciangin Topa in cinate noptuamsakin, damsak ding a, mawhna nei ahih leh zong a mawhna maisak ding hi.

### v16

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin na ci-te a damna dingin khatlekhat tungah na mawhna-te kipulakin thu na kingetsak ta un. Mihoihte' thungetna in vang nei a, na lianpi semzo hi.

**ZVS:** Elijah zong eite mah bangin mi mawkmawk hinapi-in, guah a zuklohna dingin lungsim takpi tawh thu a nget ciangin kum thum leh kum lang sung tua gamsungah guah zulo hi.
