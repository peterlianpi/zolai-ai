## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then was Jesus led up of the Spirit into the wilderness to be tempted of the devil.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin dawimangpa’ kheemna thuak nadingin Kha Siangtho in, Zeisu gampa lakah a sawl hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Dawimangpa' ze-etna a thuak dingin Kha Siangtho in gamlakah Jesuh a paipih hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to

**ZVS:** Jesuh in sun sawmli leh zan sawmli sung an neloin omkhin ahih manin a gil a kial hi.

### v3

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin a kheem dingpa in va nai a, “Nang in Pasian’ Tapa na hih takpi leh, hih suangte khomun suaksak in,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin ama kiangah Dawimangpa hong pai a, “Pasian' Tapa na hih takpi leh hih suangte anlum a suahna dingin thu pia in,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v4
**EN:** But he answered and said,

**TDB77:** Zeisu in, “Mi in khomun bek tawh nungta lo hi, Pasian’ kam sung pan hong pusuak thu khempeuh tawh a nungta hi,’ ci-in Lai Siangtho sungah kigelh hi,” a ci kik hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Jesuh in, “Lai Siangtho sungah, ‘Anlum bek tawh mite nungtalo a, Pasian genkhiat thute tawh nungta zaw hi,’ ci-in kigelh hilo ahi hiam?” ci-in a dawng hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

**ZVS:** “Pasian' Tapa taktak na hih leh Lai Siangtho sungah, ‘Pasian in nang a hong kem dingin a vantung mite hong sawl ding hi. Na khete leh suang a kitahkhak lohna dingin vantung mite in a khut uh tawh nang hong dom ding hi,’ ci-in kigelh ahih manin, hih lai panin tuaksuk in,” a ci hi.
