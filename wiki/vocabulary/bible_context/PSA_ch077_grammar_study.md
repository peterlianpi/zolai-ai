## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I cried unto God with my voice, even unto God with my voice; and he gave ear unto me.

**TDB77:** Pasian tungah a gingin ka kiko hi, amah in kei hong zak theih nadingin Pasian tungah a gingin ka kiko hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' tungah a gingin ka kiko khia a, a hong zaktheihna dingin a gingin ka kiko hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** In the day of my trouble I sought the Lord: my sore ran in the night, and ceased not: my soul refused to be comforted.

**TDB77:** Ka lungkham hunin keimah in Topa zongin; zanin thungenin gim het loin ka khut ka zan a, ahi zongin hehnepna ka ngah kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungkham hunin keimah in { Topa } ka zong hi. Zanin thu ngenin gim hetloin ka khut ka zan a, ahi zongin hehnepna ka ngah kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** I remembered God, and was troubled: I complained, and my spirit was overwhelmed. Selah.

**TDB77:** Keimah in Pasian ka ngaihsun a, ka tau hi; lung ka geelgeel a, ka gim mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah in Pasian ka ngaihsun a, ka tautau hi. Lung ka geelgeel a, ka gim mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v4
**EN:** Thou holdest mine eyes waking: I am so troubled that I cannot speak.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in ihmu lo dingin nong bawl a, lungkham-in ka pau thei kei hi.

**ZVS:** A ihmulo dingin kei na hong bawl a, lungkham lua-in ka pau thei kei hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | negation: kei
