## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un! Topa’ tungah la thak sa un, a thuman mite’ kikhopna sungah amah phatna sa un!

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. { Topa' } tungah la thak sa un. A thuman mite' kikhopna sungah amah phatna sa un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

**TDB77:** Amah a Bawlpa’ tungah Israel lungdam hen la, amau Kumpipa’ sungah Zion mite kipak ta hen!

**ZVS:** Amah a Bawlpa tungah Israel lungdam hen la, amau' Kumpipa' tungah Zion mite kipak ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp.

**TDB77:** Laamna tawh ama min phat un la, khuang leh laiza lasakna tawh ama tungah langaih sa un!

**ZVS:** Lamna tawh amah phat un la, khuang leh laia lasakna tawh ama tungah la ngaih sa un.

### v4
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ama mite tungah Topa lungdam a, miniamte gualzo sakin a pahtawi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ama mite tungah { Topa } lungdam a, miniamte gualzo sakin a pahtawi hi.

### v5
**EN:** Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds.

**TDB77:** A gualzawhna uh-ah thuman mite lungdamin diang-te hen la, ama mai-ah khukdin-in, nuamna la sa-te hen.

**ZVS:** A gualzawhna uhah thuman mite lungdamin diang-te hen la, ama mai-ah khukdin'in nuamna la sa-te hen.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v9
**EN:** To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Minam dangte tungah phu la-in gim a pia ding, a kumpite-te sikkhau tawh henin a miliante-te sikkol a bulh ding, a kigelhsa thukhenna bangin amaute tungah a semkhia dingin langnih a hiam namsau a tawi kawmun Pasian phatin awng ta-te hen. Hih pen Pasian' mite' gualzawhna ahi hi. { Topa } phat un.
