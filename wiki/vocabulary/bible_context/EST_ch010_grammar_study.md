## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And the king Ahasuerus laid a tribute upon the land, and upon the isles of the sea.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa Ahasu-eras in a ukna gam leitung leh tuikulh tunga teengte tung panin siah kai hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa Ahasueras in a ukna gam leitung leh tuikulh tunga tengte tung panin siah kai hi.

### v2
**EN:** And all the acts of his power and of his might, and the declaration of the greatness of Mordecai, whereunto the king advanced him, are they not written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Media and Persia?

**TDB77:** Ama vangliatna leh a thahatna tawh a sepnate khempeuh, leh Mordekai tungah a piak minphatnate, Media leh Persia Kumpite’ Khang Tangthu Laibu sungah kigelh hi.

**ZVS:** Ama vangliatna leh a sepnate khempeuh, leh Mordekai tungah a piak minphatnate, Media leh Persia Kumpite' Khang Tangthu Laibu sungah kigelh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For Mordecai the Jew was next unto king Ahasuerus, and great among the Jews, and accepted of the multitude of his brethren, seeking the wealth of his people, and speaking peace to all his seed.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Kumpipa Ahasu-eras’ zomah Mordekai in a za lianpen nihna ahi hi; Mordekai in ama mite’ phattuam nading leh a suan-le-khakte uh a khan’toh nading hanciam ahih manin Judah mite lakah milian khat hi a, a gam mihonpite in it kim uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Kumpipa Ahasueras' zomah Mordekai a lianpen ahi hi. Mordekai in ama mite' phattuamna ding leh a suanlekhakte uh a khan'tohna ding hanciam ahih manin Jew mite lakah milian khat hi a, a gam mihonpite in a it kim uh hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
