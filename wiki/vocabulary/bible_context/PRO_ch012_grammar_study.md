## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Whoso loveth instruction loveth knowledge: but he that hateth reproof is brutish.

**TDB77:** Pilna a deihte in kithuhilh ding ut a, kitai ding ut lohna in haina hi.

**ZVS:** Pilna a deihte, kithuhilh ding ut-te a, kitai ding utlohna pen haina ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** A good man obtaineth favour of the

**TDB77:** Mihoihte in Topa’ maipha ngah a, siatna a gamtate Topa in mawhsak hi.

**ZVS:** Mihoihte in { Topa' } maipha ngah a, siatna a gamtate amah in a mawhsak hi.

### v3
**EN:** A man shall not be established by wickedness: but the root of the righteous shall not be moved.

**TDB77:** Gitlohna in lungmuan’na piangsak lo a, thuman mite lungmuang takin om-te hi.

**ZVS:** Gitlohna in lungmuan'na piangsaklo a, thuman mite lungmuang takin om-te hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** A virtuous woman is a crown to her husband: but she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.

**TDB77:** Zi hoih in a pasal’ minthan’na leh lungdamna hi a, a pasal a maizumsak zi in a pasal adingin a dam thei lo guhnatna tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Zi hoih pen a pasal' minthan'na leh lungdamna hi a, a pasal a maizumsak zi pen a pasal-a' dingin a dam theilo guhnatna tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** The thoughts of the righteous are right: but the counsels of the wicked are deceit.

**TDB77:** Thuman mite in a kilawm a kituakin hong bawl a, migilote bek in hong khem nuam hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** The words of the wicked are to lie in wait for blood: but the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.

**TDB77:** Migilote’ thugente in mi thahnopna bekbek hi a, midikte’ thugente in a lauhuai sunga om mite honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote' thugente mi thahnopna bekbek hi a, midikte' thugente in lauhuaina sunga om mite a honkhia hi.

### v7
**EN:** The wicked are overthrown, and are not: but the house of the righteous shall stand.

**TDB77:** Migilote in sihna tuakin ci-mang a, thuman mite nungta-in khang kizom hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote in sihna tuakin cimang a, thuman mite nungta-in khang kizom hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** A man shall be commended according to his wisdom: but he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.

**TDB77:** Mipilte kiphatin kipahtawi a, mihaite kineu-et hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** He that is despised, and hath a servant, is better than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread.

**TDB77:** Nektawm ding nei lopi-in milianpi bang-a omna sangin, nuntak nading nasem-a mi mawkmawk hihna hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Nektawm ding neilopi-in milian bangin omna sangin nuntakna ding na semin mi mawkmawk hihna hoihzaw hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast: but the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.

**TDB77:** Midikte in a gannote hoihtak don a, migilote in a gannote bawlsia hi.

**ZVS:** Midikte in a gannote hoihtakin don a, migilote in a gannote bawlsia hi.

### v11
**EN:** He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread: but he that followeth vain persons is void of understanding.

**TDB77:** Nakpi tak-a lokhote in an hau a, man nei lopi-a hun beisakna in haina hi.

**ZVS:** Nakpi takin lo khote in an hau a, man neilopi-in hun beisakna in haina ahi hi.

### v12
**EN:** The wicked desireth the net of evil men: but the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.

**TDB77:** Migilote’ belh mun muanhuai in leikhal bangin kitamzan a, mihoihte’ zung kip tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote' belh munmuan'huai pen leikhal bangin kitamzan a, mihoihte' zung kip tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** The wicked is snared by the transgression of his lips: but the just shall come out of trouble.

**TDB77:** Migilote’ in a zuaugenna-te awkpih a, thuman mite, buaina tawh kipelh hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote in a zuaugenna-te awkpih a, thuman mite buaina tawh kipelh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth: and the recompence of a man's hands shall be rendered unto him.

**TDB77:** Mite in kamhoih pauna hangin thaman ngah a, a sepna bangun zong thaman ngah hi.

**ZVS:** Mite in kam hoih pauna hangin thaman ngah-te a, a sepna bangun zong thaman ngah-te hi.

### v15
**EN:** The way of a fool is right in his own eyes: but he that hearkeneth unto counsel is wise.

**TDB77:** Mihaite in amau mah thuman kisa tawntung a, mipilte in mi’ thugenna ngai nuam-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite pen amau mah thuman kisa tawntung a, mipilte in mi' thugenna ngai nuam-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v16
**EN:** A fool's wrath is presently known: but a prudent man covereth shame.

**TDB77:** Mihaite in a hehna-te langsak pah a, mipilte in a hehna-te iip zo hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite in a hehna-te langsakpah-te a, mipilte in a hehna-te ipzo hi.

### v17
**EN:** He that speaketh truth sheweth forth righteousness: but a false witness deceit.

**TDB77:** Thuman mite in thu taktak mah gen-te a, zuau tecite in khemna thu bek gen-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v18
**EN:** There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword: but the tongue of the wise is health.

**TDB77:** Ngaihsun lo-a kampauna in temhiam bangin mi liamsak thei a, mipilte’ kampauna in tua bang liamna damsak thei hi.

**ZVS:** Ngaihsun lo-a kampauna in tem hiam bangin mi liamsak thei a, mipilte' kampauna in tua bang liamna damsak thei hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v19
**EN:** The lip of truth shall be established for ever: but a lying tongue is but for a moment.

**TDB77:** Thuman genna kip tawntung a, thuzuau genna tomno sung bek kimang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20
**EN:** Deceit is in the heart of them that imagine evil: but to the counsellors of peace is joy.

**TDB77:** Thusia ngaihsunte lungkham a, thuhoih vaihawmte lungdam hi.

**ZVS:** Thusia ngaihsunte lungkham-te a, thuhoih vaihawmte lungdam-te hi.

### v21
**EN:** There shall no evil happen to the just: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief.

**TDB77:** Thumante tungah thusia tung lo a, migilote, lungkhamna tawh kidim hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mite tungah thusia tunglo a, migilote lungkhamna tawh kidim hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v22
**EN:** Lying lips are abomination to the

**TDB77:** Topa in zuau gen mite mudah a, mi citakte tungah lungdam hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in zuaugen mite mudah a, mi citakte tungah a lungdam hi.

### v23
**EN:** A prudent man concealeth knowledge: but the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.

**TDB77:** Mipilte in a theihna-te im khinkhian a, mihaite in a haina-te pholaklak hi.

### v24
**EN:** The hand of the diligent shall bear rule: but the slothful shall be under tribute.

**TDB77:** Mi thunuamte a vang lian a, a thadahte sila suak hi.

**ZVS:** Mi thanuamte vanglian a, mi thadahte sila suak hi.

### v25
**EN:** Heaviness in the heart of man maketh it stoop: but a good word maketh it glad.

**TDB77:** Lunghihmawhna in lungkhamna piangsak a, kampau hoih in mi lungdamsak hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v26
**EN:** The righteous is more excellent than his neighbour: but the way of the wicked seduceth them.

**TDB77:** Midikte in a gamtatna-te tel-et a, migilote, a gamtatna un lam pialsak hi.

**ZVS:** Midikte in a gamtatna-te tel et a, migilote a gamtatna un lam pialsak hi.

### v27
**EN:** The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting: but the substance of a diligent man is precious.

**TDB77:** Thadahte, a tup tung ngei lo a, thanuamte in a deih ngah hi.

**ZVS:** Thadahte a tup tung ngeilo a, a thanuamte in a deih ngah hi.

### v28
**EN:** In the way of righteousness is life; and in the pathway thereof there is no death.

**TDB77:** Mi thumante’ gamtatna in nuntakna piangsak a, mihaite’ gamtatna in sihna piangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Mi thumante' gamtatna in nuntakna piangsak a, mihaite' gamtatna in sihna piangsak hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix
