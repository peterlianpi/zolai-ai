## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And he said unto me, Son of man, stand upon thy feet, and I will speak unto thee.

**TDB77:** Tua awging in, “Mihing ahi Ezekiel aw, ding to in. Nang tungah thu kong gen nuam hi,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua awging in, “Mihing ahi Ezekiel aw, dingto in. Nang tungah thu kong gen nuam hi,” hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v2
**EN:** And the spirit entered into me when he spake unto me, and set me upon my feet, that I heard him that spake unto me.

**TDB77:** Tua bangin thu hong gen lai takin Pasian’ Kha ka sungah hong lutin ka khe-in hong ding to sak hi. Tua ciangin awging kizom lai-in kei kiangah,

**ZVS:** Tua bangin thu hong gen laitakin ka sungah Pasian' kha hong lutin ka khe-in hong dingtosak hi. Tua ciangin a awging kizom lai-in kei kiangah,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** And he said unto me, Son of man, I send thee to the children of Israel, to a rebellious nation that hath rebelled against me: they and their fathers have transgressed against me, even unto this very day.

**TDB77:** “Ezekiel aw, kei hong langpan’ in a hong lehdo Israel minamte kiangah nang a hong sawl ka hi hi. A pu a pate-te mah bangin amaute in tu dongin kei hong lehdo-te hi.

**ZVS:** “Mihing ahi Ezekiel aw, kei hong langpangin a hong lehdo Israel minamte kiangah nang a hong sawl ka hi hi. A pu a pate-te mah bangin amaute in tudongin kei hong lehdo lai-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** For they are impudent children and stiffhearted. I do send thee unto them; and thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the Lord

**TDB77:** Amaute a lungsim-te khauhin paupeng-te hi. Keimah Topa Pasian in amaute tungah ka gen ding thute a va gen dingin a hong sawl ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute a lungsim-te khauhin paupeng-te hi. Keimah Topa { Pasian } in amaute tungah ka gen ding thute a va gen dingin a hong sawl ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** And they, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear, (for they are a rebellious house,) yet shall know that there hath been a prophet among them.

**TDB77:** Amaute in nangma thu hong mang a, hong mang kei zongin, amaute lakah kamsang khat a om khit lam tak thei ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in nangma thu hong mang a, hong mang kei zongin, amaute lakah kamsang khat a omkhit lamtak thei ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** And thou, son of man, be not afraid of them, neither be afraid of their words, though briers and thorns be with thee, and thou dost dwell among scorpions: be not afraid of their words, nor be dismayed at their looks, though they be a rebellious house.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin Ezekiel aw, amaute ahi a, a thugente-te ahi zongin na kihta kei ding hi. Amaute in nang ling hong kaihnelh ding a, mom-ai laka om na bang ding hi. Tua bang hita se leh, hih a lehdo thei mite’ maipuakzia leh amaute’ kampaunate hangin lau tuan kei in.

**ZVS:** “Ahi zongin mihing ahi Ezekiel aw, amaute ahi a, a thugente-te ahi zongin na kihta kei ding hi. Amaute in nang ling hong kaihnelh ding-te a, mom-ai laka om na bang ding hi. Tua bang hita se leh, hih a lehdo thei mite' maipuak zia leh amaute' kampaunate hangin lau tuan kei in.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** And thou shalt speak my words unto them, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear: for they are most rebellious.

**TDB77:** Amaute in hong mang a, hong mang kei zongin, nang tungah kong gen khempeuh amaute tungah va gen in. Amaute pen a lehdo thei mi ahihna-te phawk in.

### v8
**EN:** But thou, son of man, hear what I say unto thee; Be not thou rebellious like that rebellious house: open thy mouth, and eat that I give thee.

**TDB77:** “Ezekiel aw, nang tungah kong gen thute ngai in. Amaute bangin hong langpang kei in. Na kam ka in la, tu-in ka hong piak ding na ne in,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** “Mihing ahi Ezekiel aw, nang tungah kong gen thute ngai in. Amaute bangin hong langpang kei in. Na kam ka in la, tu-in ka hong piak ding na ne in,” a hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v9
**EN:** And when I looked, behold, an hand was sent unto me; and, lo, a roll of a book was therein;

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin kei lamah khut khat hong kizan a, tua khut in laizial tawn khat a tawi ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** And he spread it before me; and it was written within and without: and there was written therein lamentations, and mourning, and woe.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin tua khut in tua lai kizial hong phel a, a lang nih tuakah lai kigelhin, dahna kahna thu leh thuumna tauna thute vive a kigelh ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin tua khut in tua lai kizial hong phel a, a lang nih tuakah lai kigelhin, dahlekah thu leh thumletau thute vive a kigelh ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix
