**EN:** Surely I have behaved and quieted myself, as a child that is weaned of his mother: my soul is even as a weaned child.

**TDB77:** A nu’ nawi tawh a kikhenta naupang bangin daii takin ka om dide zo ta hi; a daii hit-hiat-a a om naupang tawh ka kibang hi.
