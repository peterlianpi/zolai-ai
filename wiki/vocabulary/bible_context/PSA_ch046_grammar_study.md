## Verses

### v1
**EN:** God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.

**TDB77:** Pasian in eima belh leh i thahatna hi a, haksat hunin hong huh pahpahpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian, i belh leh i thahatna hi a, haksat hunin a hong huh pahpahpa ahi hi.

### v3
**EN:** Though the waters thereof roar and be troubled, though the mountains shake with the swelling thereof. Selah.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin, leitung kikhinin, mualte tuipi sung a kiden'na zongin, a tuite ging zuazua-in phuan zuazua a, hih gin'na hangin mual liante a ling zongin, i lau kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** There is a river, the streams whereof shall make glad the city of God, the holy place of the tabernacles of the most High.

**TDB77:** Sang Penpa’ ten’na khua siangtho ahi, Pasian’ khuapi, a lungdamsak gun khat a om hi.

**ZVS:** Sangpenpa' ten'na khua siangtho, Pasian' khuapi, a lungdamsak gun khat a om hi.

### v5
**EN:** God is in the midst of her; she shall not be moved: God shall help her, and that right early.

**TDB77:** Ama laizangah Pasian om a, amah a kiliing kei ding hi; Pasian in phalvak tungin amah huh ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ama laizangah Pasian om a, amah a kiling kei ding hi. Pasian in phalvak tungin amah huh ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** The heathen raged, the kingdoms were moved: he uttered his voice, the earth melted.

**TDB77:** Minamte heh mahmah uh a, kumpi gam kiliing hi; ama aw ging khiasak a, leitung kicimgawp hi.

**ZVS:** Minamte lungkham uh a, kumpi gamte kiling hi. Ama aw gingkhiasak a, leitung kicimgawp hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Mi honte’ Topa ei tawh hong omkhawm a, Jakob’ Pasian in eima belh ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Vanglian { Topa } eite tawh hong omkhawm a, Jakob Pasian eima belh ahi hi.

### v8
**EN:** Come, behold the works of the

**TDB77:** Hong pai un la, Topa’ sepna, leitung, gamkhing a suahsak khitna hong en un.

**ZVS:** Hong pai un la, { Topa' } sepna hong mu un. Leitung khempeuh gamkhing a suahsak khitna hong en un.

### v9
**EN:** He maketh wars to cease unto the end of the earth; he breaketh the bow, and cutteth the spear in sunder; he burneth the chariot in the fire.

**TDB77:** Amah in leimong dong ah gal beisak hi; amah in thalpi kuaitanin, teipi susia a, leengte mei tawh haltum hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in leimong dongah gal beisak hi. Amah in thalpi kuaitanin teipi susia a, leengte mei tawh a haltum hi.

### v10
**EN:** Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the heathen, I will be exalted in the earth.

**TDB77:** “Om hit-hiat un la, Pasian ka hihna thei un. Minamte lakah keimah hong kisangsak a, leitungah ka min hong kithangsak hi!”

**ZVS:** “Om hithiat un la, Pasian ka hihna thei un. Minamte lakah keimah hong kisangsak a, leitung khempeuhah ka min hong kithangsak hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Mi honte’ Topa in ei tawh omkhawm a, Jakob' Pasian in eima belh ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Vanglian { Topa, } eite tawh hong omkhawm a, Jakob' Pasian, eima belh ahi hi.
