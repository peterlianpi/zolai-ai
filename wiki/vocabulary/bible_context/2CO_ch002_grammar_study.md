**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, keimah in note ka hong dahsak leh, ka hong dahsakte longal, kei kua in hong lungdamsak ding hiam?

### v3
**EN:** And I wrote this same unto you, lest, when I came, I should have sorrow from them of whom I ought to rejoice; having confidence in you all, that my joy is the joy of you all.

**TDB77:** Ka lungdamna in note khempeuh lungdamna ahi hi ci-in ka lungmuang a, ka hong tun’ ciangin ka lung a hong damsak ding a kilawm mite pan dahna hong suah loh nadingin tu ma-in lai in ka hong khak hi.

**ZVS:** Kei lungdam leng note khempeuh zong a lungdam ding na hihna uh teltakin thei ka hih manin, kei a hong lungdamsak ding note in kei' dahna nong pian'sakkhak lohna ding uh ka deihna tawh kei hong pailoin tua lai a hong gelh ka hi hi.

### v4

**ZVS:** Tua lai ka gelh laitakin, note' dahna ding piangsakkha loin note ka hong it mahmahna na phawkna ding uh ka deihna tawh ka gelh hi a, lungkham sinkhamna leh khitui naptui kai-in a gelh ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: kei

### v5

**ZVS:** Mi khatpeuh in hehna ding a pian'sak ciangin kei bek a hong hehsak hiloin note khempeuh zong a hong hehsak ahi hi. Tua ahih manin tua bang mi tungah khauhtat lua nawn kei un.

### v10

**TDB77:** Midangte mawhna na maisak uh leh, tuate’ mawhna keimah in zong ka maisak ding hi. Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, keimah in mi khat mawhna ka maisak ngei leh, note hong ngaihsunin Khazih’ mai-ah ka maisak hi.
