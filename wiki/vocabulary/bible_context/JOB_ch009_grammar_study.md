**EN:** Then Job answered and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Job in dawng a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Job in Pasian dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

**TDB77:** “Kuamah in Pasian a zawhzawh lohna a tel takin ka thei ta hi.

**ZVS:** “Kuamah in Pasian a zawhzawh lohna a teltakin ka theita hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4

### v5
