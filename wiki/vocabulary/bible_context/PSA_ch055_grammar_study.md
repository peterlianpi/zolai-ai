## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Give ear to my prayer, O God; and hide not thyself from my supplication.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka thungetna hong ngai in la, kong thumna panin nangmah bu kei in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, ka thungetna hong ngai in la, ka hong thumna panin nangmah bu kei in.

### v2
**EN:** Attend unto me, and hear me: I mourn in my complaint, and make a noise;

**TDB77:** Kei lam hong nga in la, hong dawng in; ka gimna in kei hong zo hi.

**ZVS:** Keima lam hong nga in la, ka hong ngetna hong pia in. Ka lungsim gimna in kei hong zogawp ta hi.

### v3
**EN:** Because of the voice of the enemy, because of the oppression of the wicked: for they cast iniquity upon me, and in wrath they hate me.

**TDB77:** Migilote’ sukcipna, galte’ huging in kei hong gimsak hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute in kei tungah haksatna hong tun’ uh a, amaute in hehna tawh keimah hong galbawl ding uh nuamsa uh hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote' sukcipna, galte' huging in kei hong gimsak hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute in keima tungah haksatna hong tun' uh a, hehna tawh keimah hong galbawl ding amaute in nuamsa uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** My heart is sore pained within me: and the terrors of death are fallen upon me.

**TDB77:** Ka sungah ka lungsim na mahmah a, sih ding launate kei tung hong tung zo hi.

**ZVS:** Ka sungah ka lungsim na mahmah a, sih ding launate kei tungah hong tungzo hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v5
**EN:** Fearfulness and trembling are come upon me, and horror hath overwhelmed me.

**TDB77:** Launa leh liin’na ka tung hong tung a, launa in kei hong vukcip hi.

**ZVS:** Launa leh lin'na ka tungah hong tung a, launa in hong vukcip hi.

### v6
**EN:** And I said, Oh that I had wings like a dove! for then would I fly away, and be at rest.

**TDB77:** Hih bangin ka ci hi, “Vakhu bangin kha nei hi leng pawh! Ka leengkhia ding a, nuam takin ka om hithiat ding hi;

**ZVS:** Hih bangin ka ci hi: “Vakhu bangin kha nei hi leng! Ka leengkhia ding a, nuamtakin ka om hithiat ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will

### v7
**EN:** Lo, then would I wander far off, and remain in the wilderness. Selah.

**TDB77:** hi taktak hi, gamla pek-ah ka vakthap ding a, gam sungah ka giak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Gamla pekah ka vakthap ding a, gamsungah ka giak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** I would hasten my escape from the windy storm and tempest.

**TDB77:** Gensiatna huihpi leh huih husiate panin bukna ka zongpak ding hi,"

**ZVS:** Gensiatna huihpi leh huih husiate panin bukna ka zong pak ding hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** Destroy, O Lord, and divide their tongues: for I have seen violence and strife in the city.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, amau’ ngaihsutnate suksiatsak in la, a pau uh kilamdangsak in; bang hang hiam cih leh khua sungah ngongtatna leh kam kiselna ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, amau' geelnate suksiat sakin a pau uh kilamdangsak in. Bang hang hiam cih leh khua sungah ngongtatna leh kam kiselna ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Day and night they go about it upon the walls thereof: mischief also and sorrow are in the midst of it.

**TDB77:** Sun leh zanin amaute kulhpi tungah kot paipai uh a, a sungah siatna leh haksatnate om-in,

**ZVS:** Amaute sun leh zanin kulhpi tungah kot paipai uh a, khua sungah siatna leh ngongtatnate om a, mite lakah lungkhamna om hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v11

**TDB77:** a lakah siatna om hi; sumzuak sumleina mun panin kisukcipna leh khemna veng lo hi.

**ZVS:** Sumzuak sumleina mun panin kisukcipna leh kikhemnate venglo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** For it was not an enemy that reproached me; then I could have borne it: neither was it he that hated me that did magnify himself against me; then I would have hid myself from him:

**ZVS:** Kei hong phin galte hilo hi. Hi leh ka thuakzo ding hi. Kiphatsakna tawh kei hong bawlte, langpang mi hilo hi. Hi leh ama kiang panin ka bu thei ding hi.
