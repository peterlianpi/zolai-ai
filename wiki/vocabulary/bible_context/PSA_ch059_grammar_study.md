## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Deliver me from mine enemies, O my God: defend me from them that rise up against me.

**TDB77:** Ka Pasian aw, ka galte kiang panin keimah hong honkhia in, keimah langpanga hong dingte kiang panin kei hong dal in,

**ZVS:** Ka Pasian aw, ka galte' kiang panin keimah hong honkhia in. Kei a hong langpangte panin kei hong dal in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Deliver me from the workers of iniquity, and save me from bloody men.

**TDB77:** siatna a bawlte’ kiang panin hong khahkhia in la, mi that nuamte’ kiang panin keimah hong hon in.

**ZVS:** Siatna a bawlte' kiang panin hong suaktasak in la, mi that nuamte' kiang panin keimah hong hon in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For, lo, they lie in wait for my soul: the mighty are gathered against me; not for my transgression, nor for my sin, O

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, en in, amaute in kei hong pangsim-te hi; mi kihtak-huaite in keimah hong langpangin kikaikhawm-te hi. Topa aw, kei’ thu palsatna, kei’ mawhna hang hi lo,

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute in kei hong pangsim-te a, a kihtakhuai mite keimah hong langpangin kikaikhawm-te hi. { Topa } aw, keima thupalsatna hang hilo,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** They run and prepare themselves without my fault: awake to help me, and behold.

**TDB77:** kei’ khialhna hang hi lopi-in, amaute tai-in va kithawi-te hi. Nangmah in hong phawk tawm in, keimah huh dingin hong pai in la, en in!

**ZVS:** kei' khialhna hang hilopi-in, amaute va kithawi-te hi. Nangmah in hong phawk in. Keimah a hong huh dingin hong pai in la, kei hong en in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Thou therefore, O

**TDB77:** Nang, Vanglianpen Topa Pasian in, Israel’ Pasian na hi hi. Minam khempeuh daantat dingin tho in la, khemna tawh siat gamtat ding a sawmte kuamah siit kei in.

**ZVS:** Nang, Vanglian { Topa } Pasian, Israel' Pasian na hi hi. Minam khempeuh gim a pia dingin tho in la, khemna tawh siat gamtat ding a sawmte kuamah sit kei in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v6
**EN:** They return at evening: they make a noise like a dog, and go round about the city.

**TDB77:** Nitak simin amaute hong pai kikin, ui bangin ku-te a, khua sungah vak khuankhuan-te hi.

**ZVS:** Nitak simin amaute hong paikik zelzelin ui bangin ku-te a, khuasung tengah vak khuankhuan-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Behold, they belch out with their mouth: swords are in their lips: for who, say they, doth hear?

**TDB77:** Amaute tua lai-ah a kam-te tawh hawk a, a muk-te tawh zuau gen-te hi—bang hang hiam cih leh, “Kua’n hong za laizang ding ahi hiam?” ci-in amaute in ngaihsun-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute a kam-te tawh nakpipi-in hawk-te a, a muk-te tawh zuauthu gengen-te hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh, “Kua'n hong za ding hiam?” ci-in amaute in ngaihsun-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** But thou, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ahih hangin nangmah in amaute na nuihsan lel a, minam khempeuh na zahpih hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ahih hangin nang in amaute na nuihsan lel a, minam khempeuh na zahpih hi.

### v9
**EN:** Because of his strength will I wait upon thee: for God is my defence.

**TDB77:** Ka Thahatna aw, nangmah phatnate ka sa ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh, Pasian aw, nangmah in ka munkip muan’huai na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka thahatna aw, nangmah phatna ka sa ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh ka Pasian aw, ka munkip muan'huai na hi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v10
**EN:** The God of my mercy shall prevent me: God shall let me see my desire upon mine enemies.

**TDB77:** Ka Pasian in ama itna kip tawh kei hong dawn ding a, ka Pasian in gualzawhna tawh ka galte hong ensak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka Pasian in ama itna kip tawh keimah hong dawn ding a, ka Pasian in gualzawhna tawh ka galte hong en sak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Slay them not, lest my people forget: scatter them by thy power; and bring them down, O Lord our shield.

**TDB77:** Amaute that kei in, that lecin ka mite in mangngilh ding hi; na vangliatna tawh amaute kilok gawp sak in la, amaute niamkhiat in, Topa, komau’ lum aw!

**ZVS:** Amaute that kei in; that lecin ka mite in mangngilh ding hi. Na vangliatna tawh amaute kilokgawp sak in la, amaute niamkhiat in, Topa, kote hong hupa aw.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** For the sin of their mouth and the words of their lips let them even be taken in their pride: and for cursing and lying which they speak.

**TDB77:** Amau kam-a mawhna, amau muk tawh a pauna thu hangin, amau’ kiphatsakna-ah amau mah awksak in. Hamsiatna zangin zuauthu a genna hangun,

**ZVS:** Amau' kama a mawhna a muk-te tawh a pauna hangin, amau' kiphatsakna-ah amau mah awksak in. Amaute in hamsiatna zangin zuauthu a genna hangun,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Consume them in wrath, consume them, that they may not be: and let them know that God ruleth in Jacob unto the ends of the earth. Selah.

**TDB77:** lei mong dongin Jakob’ tungah Pasian maang hi, ci-in mite in a theih nadingin hehna tawh amaute beisak in la, amaute a bei khit mateng sisak in.

**ZVS:** hehna tawh amaute beisak in. Amaute khempeuh a beisiang dongin sumai in. Tua hi leh Pasian in Israel-te tungah leimong dongin a uk khitna thu mi khempeuh in thei ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v16
**EN:** But I will sing of thy power; yea, I will sing aloud of thy mercy in the morning: for thou hast been my defence and refuge in the day of my trouble.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin keimah in nangma vangliatna la ka sa ding a, zingsangin na itna kip phatna la nakpi-in ka sa ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in keima munkip muan’huai na hi a, ka gentheih hunin keima belh na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin keimah in na vangliatna la ka sa ding a, zingsangin na itna kip phatna la nakpi takin ka sa ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh nang, ka munkip muan'huai na hi a, ka gentheih hunin keima belh na hi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** Unto thee, O my strength, will I sing: for God is my defence, and the God of my mercy.

**TDB77:** Ka Thahatna aw, nangmah phatnate ka sa ding hi, bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian aw, nangmah in ka munkip muan’huai, kei tungah itna kip hong lak Pasian na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka thahatna aw, nangmah phatna ka sa ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian aw, nang pen ka munkip muan'huai, keima tungah itna kip a hong lak Pasian na hi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
