## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The burden of Damascus. Behold, Damascus is taken away from being a city, and it shall be a ruinous heap.

**TDB77:** Damaskas tawh kisai thu:— En in, Damaskas in khuapi hi nawn loin, ninbulomtang suak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Damaskas tawh kisai thu hih bangin gen hi: En un, Damaskas pen khuapi hi nawnloin ninbulomtang suak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** The cities of Aroer are forsaken: they shall be for flocks, which shall lie down, and none shall make them afraid.

**TDB77:** Ama khuapite ahi Aroer kinusiacip paisuak ding hi; tuuhonte’ omna ding suak ding a, amaute lum hithiatin bangmah in lausak lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ama khuapite kinusiacip paisuak ding hi. Tuuhonte' omna mun suak ding a, tua tuuhonte lum hithiatin bangmah in lausak lo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** The fortress also shall cease from Ephraim, and the kingdom from Damascus, and the remnant of Syria: they shall be as the glory of the children of Israel, saith the

**TDB77:** Efraim gam panin kulhpi bei-in, Damaskas khua panin kumpi gam mangthang ding hi; Siria mi a suakta laite pen Israel mite vangliatna mah bang ding uh hi, Vanglianpen Topa in ci hi.

**ZVS:** Efraim gam panin kulhpi bei-in, Damaskas khua panin kumpi gam mangthang ding hi. Syria mi a suakta laite pen Israel mite mah bangin mindai ding uh hi, Vanglian { Topa } in ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v4

**TDB77:** Tua ni ciangin Jakob’ liatna bei ding a, ama hauhna mangthang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ni ciangin Jakob' liatna bei ding a, ama hauhna khempeuh mangthang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v5

**TDB77:** An a la mi in ankung a ding laite kaikhawmin a khut in ante a lak khit ciangin leitang hawm guak ahi a, mi khat in Refaim Kuam sunga ansang a kaihkhop khit ciangin lo lai hawm guak tawh ahi zongin kibang ding hi.

**ZVS:** An a la mi in ankung ding laite kaikhawmin ante a kilak khit ciangin leitang a hawm guakin a om tawh kibang ding a, mi khat in Refaim Kuam sungah ansang a kaihkhop khit ciangin lolai a hawm guakin a om tawh zong kibang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

**TDB77:** Oliv kung a kisin’ ciangin a hiang sang pen dawnah pumnih pumthum, singkung hiang dawnah pumli pumnga a om lai tawh kibang ding hi, Israelte’ Topa Pasian in ci hi.

**ZVS:** Oliv kung a kisin' khit ciangin a hiang sangpen dawnah pum nih pum thum singkung hiang dawnah pum li pum nga a omlai tawh kibang lel ding hi, Israel-te' { Topa } Pasian in ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7

**ZVS:** Amaute in amau khut tawh a bawlsa uh biakna taute donlo ding uh a, amau' bawlsa Asherah milimte hita leh paknamtui halna biakna taute hita leh muan'in nei nawnlo ding uh hi.

### v9

**ZVS:** Tua ni ciangin amau' khuapi muan'huaite pen Hiv mite leh Amor mite in Israel mite' hangin a nusiat uh munte tawh kibang ding a, gamkhing a suak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nomau hong honkhia Pasian note in mangngilhin, nomau' belh Suangpi phawklo na hih manun note in biakna huan bawlin kung hoihte suan a, kung namdangte tawh pehin,

### v11
