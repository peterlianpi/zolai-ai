## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Whosoever believeth that Jesus is the Christ is born of God: and every one that loveth him that begat loveth him also that is begotten of him.

**TDB77:** Zeisu in Khazih ahi ci-a a um mi in Pasian’ pian’sak mi ahi hi. Pa a it mi in Pa’ pian’sak mi zong a it ding hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh, Khrih hi, ci-in a um mi khempeuh Pasian' tate hi a, pa a it mi peuhmah in a ta zong a it hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v2
**EN:** By this we know that we love the children of God, when we love God, and keep his commandments.

**TDB77:** Eite in Pasian itin a thupiaknate bangin i gamtat lai takin Pasian’ tate i it hi ci-in i thei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian itin ama thupiakte i zuihna tawh Pasian tate a it ihihlam i kithei ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** For this is the love of God, that we keep his commandments: and his commandments are not grievous.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, Pasian’ itna in ama thupiaknate zuihna ahi hi. Pasian’ thupiaknate zong a haksa lua ahi kei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian i it ciangin ama thupiakte a zui ihi hi. Eite-a' dingin ama thupiakte zuihding a haksa lua hilo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v4
**EN:** For whatsoever is born of God overcometh the world: and this is the victory that overcometh the world, even our faith.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, Pasian’ pian’sak peuhpeuh in leitung a zo thei hi. Eite upna in leitung zawhna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' ta ahite peuhmah in leitung nate zo uh a, i upna tawh leitung nate tungah a gualzo ihi hi.

### v5
**EN:** Who is he that overcometh the world, but he that believeth that Jesus is the Son of God?

**TDB77:** Zeisu in Pasian’ Tapa ahi ci-in a um mi sim loh, leitung a zo kua mi ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Kuate in leitung nate zo ding ahi hiam i cih leh, Jesuh pen Pasian' Tapa hi, ci-in a umte bek in zo ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** This is he that came by water and blood, even Jesus Christ; not by water only, but by water and blood. And it is the Spirit that beareth witness, because the Spirit is truth.

**TDB77:** Tui leh sisan tawh hong pai Topa in Zeisu Khazih ahi hi. Tui bek tawh hi loin, tui tawh ahi zongin, sisan tawh ahi zongin a hong pai hi.

**ZVS:** Hih Jesuh Khrih pen tui tawh kiphumna leh a sisan luangin sihna tawh a hong pai hi a, tui bek tawh a hong pai hiloin tui leh sisan tawh a hong pai ahi hi. Kha mahmah in hih thu a manna teci a pang hi. Kha pen thuman gen ahi hi.

### v7
**EN:** For there are three that bear record in heaven, the Father, the Word, and the Holy Ghost: and these three are one.

**TDB77:** Kha Siangtho in teci hong pang hi. Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, Kha Siangtho in thuman thutak ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thu-ah teci a pang thum om a,

### v8
**EN:** And there are three that bear witness in earth, the Spirit, and the water, and the blood: and these three agree in one.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, teci a pang thum ahi Kha Siangtho, tui leh sisan-te om a, tua thumte in pumkhat bek ahi uh hi.

**ZVS:** tuate pen Kha, tui, leh sisan ahi uh hi. Hih thumte' teci pan'na kibang kim hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** If we receive the witness of men, the witness of God is greater: for this is the witness of God which he hath testified of his Son.

**TDB77:** Mite teci pan’na eite in i man’ leh, Pasian’ teci pan’na a lianzaw hi. Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, Paisan’ teci pan’na in a Tapa ading teci a pan’ zawhna thu ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mihingte' teci pan'na sangthei ihih leh Pasian' teci pan'na i san'theih ding thupizaw hi. Pasian in ama Tapa' thu tu-a bangin teci hong pang hi.

### v10
**EN:** He that believeth on the Son of God hath the witness in himself: he that believeth not God hath made him a liar; because he believeth not the record that God gave of his Son.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ Tapa a um mi in ama sungah teci pan’na a ngah hi. Pasian a um lo mi in Pasian pen zuau gen mi hi cih a suaksak hi. Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, Pasian in ama Tapa ading a teci pan’na thu, tua mi in a um kei hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Pasian' Tapa a um mi peuhmah in Pasian' teci pan'na a sang ahi hi. Ahi zongin Pasian' thugen a umlo mi peuhmah in ama Tapa thu teci pangin a gennate a umlo uh ahih manin Pasian pen zuaugen a suaksak ahi uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v11
**EN:** And this is the record, that God hath given to us eternal life, and this life is in his Son.

**TDB77:** Tua teci pan’na in, Pasian in eite nuntak tawntungna hong pia a, tua nuntakna in Tapa sungah a om hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in Pasian' teci pan'na in: Pasian in ama Tapa tung tawnin eite tungah nuntak tawntungna a hong pia hi.

### v12
**EN:** He that hath the Son hath life; and he that hath not the Son of God hath not life.

**TDB77:** Tapa a nei mi in nuntakna a nei hi, Pasian’ Tapa a nei lo mi in nuntakna a nei kei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' Tapa tawh a kipawl peuhmah in nuntak tawntungna nei a, Pasian' Tapa tawh a kipawllote in nuntak tawntungna neilo uh hi, cihna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | negation: lo

### v13
**EN:** These things have I written unto you that believe on the name of the Son of God; that ye may know that ye have eternal life, and that ye may believe on the name of the Son of God.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ Tapa’ min a um note in nuntak tawntungna na ngahna thu uh na theih nadingun, hih thute kong gelh zo hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' Tapa a um mite in nuntak tawntungna a nei na hih lamtak uh na theihna dingun hih lai a hong gelh ka hi hi.

### v14
**EN:** And this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us:

**TDB77:** Ama mai-ah eite in i ngah lau loh nading thu in, ama deihna bangin na khempeuh thuumin i nget leh, a hong ngaih nading thu ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Eite in ama deihna tawh kituakin i nget nak leh i nget khempeuh Pasian in hong piading hi, ci-in kimuan'na lungsim i nei hi.

### v15
**EN:** And if we know that he hear us, whatsoever we ask, we know that we have the petitions that we desired of him.

**TDB77:** Thu i ngetna khempeuh a hong ngai hi ci-a i theih leh, ama kiangah i nget khempeuh i ngah ding i thei hi.

**ZVS:** Eite' ngetna khempeuh amah in na za hi, ci-in thei ihih manin ama kiang panin i nget khempeuh zong hong piading hi, ci-in i thei hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** If any man see his brother sin a sin which is not unto death, he shall ask, and he shall give him life for them that sin not unto death. There is a sin unto death: I do not say that he shall pray for it.

**TDB77:** Mi khatpeuh in a sanggampa in sihna thuak ding a kilawm lo mawhna a bawl a muh leh, thungen ta hen. Sihna a thuak ding a kilawm lo mawhna a bawl mite ading Pasian in nuntakna a pia ding hi. Sihna thuak ding a kilawm mawhna a om hi. Tua bang mawhna maisak nading thungen ding ka ci kei hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khatpeuh in a Khristian mipihte a sihna ding zah ciang a hilo mawhna a gamta a muh leh ama-a' dingin thu ngetsak ta hen. Ama thungetna bangin Pasian in a nuntakna suksaklo ding hi. Hih pen sihna zah ciang mawhna a hilote ka cihna ahi hi. Ahi zongin sihna ding zah ciang ahi mawhna om a, tuate-a' ding ngetsak un, a ci ka hi kei hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** All unrighteousness is sin: and there is a sin not unto death.

**TDB77:** A dik lo na khempeuh in mawhna ahi hi. Ahih hangin sihna thuak dinga kilawm lo mawhna a om hi.

**ZVS:** Gamtat khialhna khempeuh mawhna hi a, ahi zongin mawhna khempeuh in sihna ciangdong piangsak khinlo hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

**TDB77:** Pasian’ pian’sak mi khempeuh in mawhna ahih zom kei hi ci-in i thei hi. Pasian’ pian’sak mi in ama pumpi kem a, a gilo mi in tua mi a sukha thei kei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' tate in mawhna bawbawllo uh hi. Pasian' Tapa in amaute kem a, Dawimangpa in amaute susia zolo hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei

### v19

**TDB77:** Eite in Pasian’ tate i hi hi cih thu i thei hi, leitung khempeuh in a gilo mi’ khut sungah a om thu zong i thei hi.

**ZVS:** Leitung midang khempeuh Dawimangpa' khutsungah a om ahi zongin eite pen Pasian' mi ihihna thu i thei hi.

### v20
**EN:** And we know that the Son of God is come, and hath given us an understanding, that we may know him that is true, and we are in him that is true, even in his Son Jesus Christ. This is the true God, and eternal life.

**TDB77:** Tua banah Pasian’ Tapa hong pai a, thuman thutak Topa i theih nadingin pilna eite a hong piak thu zong i thei hi. Tua tham loin, a Tapa Zeisu Khazih ahi tua thuman Topa sungah i om hi ci-in i thei hi. Hih Topa in Pasian taktak ahi hi; nuntak tawntungna zong ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' Tapa hong pai-in eite thu hong theisak ahih manin Pasian taktak a thei ihihna thu i thei hi. A Tapa Jesuh Khrih tawh i kipawlna hangin Pasian taktak mah tawh a kipawlthei ihi hi. Tua Jesuh pen Pasian taktak mah hi a, nuntak tawntungna a piathei ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to
