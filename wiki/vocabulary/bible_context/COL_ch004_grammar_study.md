## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Masters, give unto your servants that which is just and equal; knowing that ye also have a Master in heaven.

**TDB77:** Tote aw, note in zong vantungah om Topa khat na neih-te na thei un la, thuman thutak leh a kilawm a kituakin na silate-te na bawl un.

**ZVS:** Tote aw, note in zong vantungah To khat na neihna-te phawk un la, na silate-te a kilawm a kituakin bawl un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Continue in prayer, and watch in the same with thanksgiving;

**TDB77:** Thungen tawntung un la, hong hehpihna phatin ngak un.

**ZVS:** Lungsim takpi tawh thu ngen tawntung un la, kidawm kawmin lungdamna tawh thu ngen un.

### v3
**EN:** Withal praying also for us, that God would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the mystery of Christ, for which I am also in bonds:

**TDB77:** Mite kiangah Khrih' thusim hilhtheihna dingin Pasian in kote tungah hunpha hong piaktheihna dingin thu hong ngetsak un. Ka thongkiatna pen hih thu hang mah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** That I may make it manifest, as I ought to speak.

**TDB77:** Mite' teltheih dingin a kitel takin ka gentheihna dingin thu hong ngetsak un.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.

**TDB77:** Pua lamah a om mite mai-ah pilna tawh gamta un la, a hun na teel un.

**ZVS:** A pualam mite tungah a kilawm a kituakin gamta un la, hunhoih na ngah khempeuh-te na valkhongsak ngei kei un.

### v6
**EN:** Let your speech be alway with grace, seasoned with salt, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man.

**TDB77:** Mi khempeuh tungah bang ci dawn’ kik ding cih na theih nadingun, note kam in ci al bangin a al limna tawh dimin kilawm tawntung ta hen.

**ZVS:** Kam na pau-te ciangin mi' zaknop, mi' ngaihnop dingin na pau un. Tua hi leh na kihopih-te mite khempeuh-a' dingin phattuamna a nei kam a pau na hi ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** All my state shall Tychicus declare unto you, who is a beloved brother, and a faithful minister and fellowservant in the Lord:

**TDB77:** Topa sungah dikna nei-in nasem khawm sila ka hihpih, a it huai mi ahi sanggampa Tikhikas in keima thu a om khempeuh note kiangah a hong gen ding hi.

**ZVS:** Topa' nasepna-ah citakin a manpha mahmah ka naseppih-te ka sanggam-te Tikhikas in kei' thu a kicing takin note kiangah hong gen ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that he might know your estate, and comfort your hearts;

**TDB77:** Hih lai-a kote' omzia hong gen ciangin note' lungsim a noptheihna dingin amah a hong paisak ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v9
**EN:** With Onesimus, a faithful and beloved brother, who is one of you. They shall make known unto you all things which are done here.

**TDB77:** Note' khuapih mi, a citak i sanggam itpa Onesimas zong ka hong zuisak hi. Amaute gel in hih lai-a thupiang khempeuh note kiangah hong gen ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** Aristarchus my fellowprisoner saluteth you, and Mark, sister's son to Barnabas, (touching whom ye received commandments: if he come unto you, receive him;)

**TDB77:** Kei tawh thong a kia khawm mi Aristarkhas akipan Barnabas’ nau Marka leh Justas a kici Zeisu te in note a hong hopih-te hi. Marka thu tawh kipawl note kiangah ka hong khak zo hi. Amah note kiangah a hong tun’ ciangin dawnin na tungsak un.

**ZVS:** Kei tawh thong a kiakhawm Aristarkhas in note hong hopih hi. Barnabas' sanggamnu' tapa Marka in zong hong hopih hi (amah hong pai ciangin hoihtakin na pawlbawl un, ci-a hong kigenpa ahi hi).

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** And Jesus, which is called Justus, who are of the circumcision. These only are my fellowworkers unto the kingdom of God, which have been a comfort unto me.

**TDB77:** Vunatna a thuak mite lakah hih mite bek Pasian’ Gam ading ka naseppihte ahi-te a, kei hong hehnem-te hi.

**ZVS:** Justas zong a kici zel Jesuh in zong note hong hopih hi. Vun-atna a zang Jew mi Khristian a hong suakte lak panin hih mi thumte bek, Pasian thu-ah ka naseppih lakah a hong kihel ahi hi. Ka nasepna-ah amaute in nakpi takin hong huh-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** Epaphras, who is one of you, a servant of Christ, saluteth you, always labouring fervently for you in prayers, that ye may stand perfect and complete in all the will of God.

**TDB77:** Note lakah a piang Khazih Zeisu’ sila Epafras in note hong hopih hi. Note kicingin Pasian’ deihna khempeuh ah lunghihmawhna om loin na lungkim nadingun, note ading hahkatin thu a ngen tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh Khrih nasem, note' makai Epafras in zong hong hopih hi. Pasian' deihna bangin note nungta-in, lungsim khanghamin kiptakin na omtheihna dingun amah in thu hong ngetsak tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** For I bear him record, that he hath a great zeal for you, and them that are in Laodicea, and them in Hierapolis.

**TDB77:** Note-a kipan Laodikia khua leh Hierapolis khua ah a om mite adingin nakpi-in a hahkatna thu keimah in amah ading teci ka pang hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in note-a' dinga kipan Laodisia khua leh Hierapolis khuamite-a' dingin nakpi takin na a sepna thu ka thei hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** Luke, the beloved physician, and Demas, greet you.

**TDB77:** Itna a ngah zato mangpa Luka leh Demas in note hong hopih-te hi.

**ZVS:** I it siavuanpa Luka leh Demas in zong hong hopih-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** Salute the brethren which are in Laodicea, and Nymphas, and the church which is in his house.

**TDB77:** Laodikia khua-ah a om ute naute a hi zongin, Nimfa akipan ama innah a om pawlpi ahi zongin na hopihsak un.

**ZVS:** Laodisia khua-a om i sanggamte leh Nymfas leh ama inn-a a kikhawm pawlpi mite na hopihsak un.

### v16
**EN:** And when this epistle is read among you, cause that it be read also in the church of the Laodiceans; and that ye likewise read the epistle from Laodicea.

**TDB77:** Hih lai na sim khit-te ciangin Laodikia khua-ah a om pawlpite zong na simsak un. Laodikia khua pan a kingah ding lai zong na sim un.

**ZVS:** Hih ka hong laikhak na sim khit-te ciangin Laodisia khua-a om pawlpite zong na simsak un la, Laodisia khua pawlpite kiang pan hong pai ding lai zong note in na sim hamtang un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v17
**EN:** And say to Archippus, Take heed to the ministry which thou hast received in the Lord, that thou fulfil it.

**TDB77:** Arkhippas kiangah zong, “Nangmah in Topa’ khut sungah na ngah nasepna na zawh khit nadingin ciamteh in,” ci-in na gen un.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Arkhippas kiangah, “Pasian-a' dingin nasepna nang tungah hong kipia pen hoihtakin na zo in,” ci-in na gen un.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** The salutation by the hand of me Paul. Remember my bonds. Grace be with you. Amen.

**TDB77:** Kei Paul in ka khut tawh note hopihna hih lai kong gelh hi. Kei’ thuak kikhihna na ngaihsun un. Note tungah hehpihna thupha om ta hen.

**ZVS:** Keimah Paul' khut mahmah tawh, Note ka hong hopih hi, cihna lai ka gelh ahi hi. Sikkhau khihna ka thuakna thu mangngilh kei un. Note khempeuh tungah Pasian' hehpihna thupha hong tung ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | reciprocal: ki- prefix
