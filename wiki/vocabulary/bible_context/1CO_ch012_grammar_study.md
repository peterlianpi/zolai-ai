## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Now concerning spiritual gifts, brethren, I would not have you ignorant.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, note in Kha Siangtho’ piak thupha thu na theih loh ding-te ka deih kei hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, Kha Siangtho' hong piak siamna a tuamtuamte tawh kisai thu na theihloh khak ding-te ka deih kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** Ye know that ye were Gentiles, carried away unto these dumb idols, even as ye were led.

**TDB77:** Note in Gentailte na hih lai takun, bang ci lam tawh na zuih-te ahi zongin, a pau thei lo milim omna lamah a zui mi na hih ngei-te na thei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Lawki na hih lai-un a pau theilo milimte' thu-in na gamta-te a, na lungsim-te tua milimte in hong zo mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Wherefore I give you to understand, that no man speaking by the Spirit of God calleth Jesus accursed: and that no man can say that Jesus is the Lord, but by the Holy Ghost.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin Pasian’ Kha Siangtho tawh, “Zeisu in hamsiatna a thuak hi,” ci-in kuamah in a gen theih loh mah bangin, “Zeisu in Topa hi,” ci-in kuamah in Kha Siangtho suang loin a pulak thei kei hi, ci-in kong theisak hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Kha Siangtho in lam a lah mite in, “Jesuh in samsiatna ngah ta hen,” a cihlohna, leh Kha Siangtho in lam lak kei leh kuamah in, “Jesuh pen Topa hi,” a cih theihlohna ding thu na theihding-te ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei

### v4
**EN:** Now there are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit.

**TDB77:** A hong piaksa hehpihna atuam atuam a om hangin a hong pia Kha Siangtho khat bek a om hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in siamna a nam tuamtuam om a, ahi zongin a hong pia Kha Siangtho a kibang ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** And there are differences of administrations, but the same Lord.

**TDB77:** Nasepna za atuam atuam a om hangin Topa khat bek a om hi.

**ZVS:** Nasepna a nam tuamtuam om a, ahi zongin a na i sepsak Topa a kibang ahi hi.

### v6
**EN:** And there are diversities of operations, but it is the same God which worketh all in all.

**TDB77:** Nasepna atuam atuam a om hangin mi khempeuh tungah na khempeuh a bawl Pasian khat bek a om hi.

**ZVS:** Nasepna ding siamna a nam tuamtuam om a, ahi zongin septheihna ding siamna a hong pia Pasian a kibang ahi hi.

### v7
**EN:** But the manifestation of the Spirit is given to every man to profit withal.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin a hoih a phattuam om nadingin Kha Siangtho’ kilatkhiat nading mikim tungah a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khempeuh-a' dingin phattuamna a omtheihna dingin Kha Siangtho' hong ompihna a nam tuamtuamin hong kilang hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** For to one is given by the Spirit the word of wisdom; to another the word of knowledge by the same Spirit;

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, tua Kha Siangtho tawh mi khat kiangah pilna thu a pia hi. Tua Kha Siangtho tawh mi khat kiangah siamna thu a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Kha Siangtho in mi khat tungah thuthuk pilna hong guan' a, tua Kha Siangtho mah in adang khat tungah thugen siamna hong guan' hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** To another faith by the same Spirit; to another the gifts of healing by the same Spirit;

**TDB77:** Tua Kha Siangtho tawh mi khat kiangah thu upna a pia hi. Tua Kha Siangtho tawh mi khat kiangah cina damsak theihna a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Adang khat tungah upna hong guan' a, adang khat tungah cina damsak theihna hong guan' hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** To another the working of miracles; to another prophecy; to another discerning of spirits; to another divers kinds of tongues; to another the interpretation of tongues:

**TDB77:** Mi khat kiangah nalamdang bawlna thu, mi khat kiangah kamsan’na thu, mi khat kiangah lungsim kha khentel theihna thu, mi khat kiangah paunam tampi gen theihna thu, mi khat kiangah tua paute a khiatna gen theihna thute a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Adang khat tungah na lamdang bawl theihna, adang khat tungah Pasian' thu hilh ding siamna, adang khat tungah Kha Siangtho piak siamna ahi leh a hilo khentheihna ding siamna, adang khat tungah pau lamdang pau theihna, adang khat tungah tua pau lamdang tuamtuam paunate a khiatna gentheihna hong pia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11

**TDB77:** Hih thu khempeuh Kha Siangtho khat bek in bawl a, ama deihna bangin mikim tungah a hawmsak hi.

**ZVS:** Hih siamna a tuamtuamte a hong pia pen Kha Siangtho a kibang khat bek mah hi a, mi a tuamtuamte tungah ama hoihsak bangin a hong piak ahi hi.

### v12
**EN:** For as the body is one, and hath many members, and all the members of that one body, being many, are one body: so also is Christ.

**TDB77:** Pumpi in khat bek hi-in na tampi nei a, tua pumpi khat tungah nate tampi a om napi-in pumpi khat bek a om mah bangin Khazih in ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pumpi pen khat bek hinapi-in na a tuamtuam a nei hi. Pumpi in na a tuamtuam tampi a neih hangin pumpi khat bek mah ahih veve mah bangin Khrih zong pumpi na a tuamtuam a nei pumpi khat ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v13

**ZVS:** Jew mi ihi a, Gentail mi ihi zongin, sila ihi a, sila a hilo ihi zongin, eite minam tuamtuam ihih hangin Kha Siangtho khat bek mah in Jesuh' pumpi a suak dingin ama sungah hong phum a, eite khempeuh tungah a hong kipia pen tua a kibang Kha Siangtho khat bek mah ahi hi.

**ZVS:** A vekpi-un sawltak hilo a, a vekpi-un kamsang zong hilo-te hi. A vekpi-un laihilh, thuhilh hilo-te a, a vekpi-un na lamdang zong bawllo-te hi.

**ZVS:** A vekpi-un cina damsakna ding siamna neilo-te a, a vekpi-un pau lamdang pau theilo-te a, a vekpi-un tua pau lamdangte a khiatna gen theilo-te hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix
