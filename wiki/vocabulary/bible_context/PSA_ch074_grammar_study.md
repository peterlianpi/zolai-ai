## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O God, why hast thou cast us off for ever? why doth thine anger smoke against the sheep of thy pasture?

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, bang hangin kote hong paikhia paisuak na hi hiam? Bang hangin nangma gam sunga na tuute tungah heh na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, bang hangin kote hong paiikhia paisuak na hi hiam? Bang hangin na gamsunga om nangma tuute tungah heh na hi hiam?

### v2
**EN:** Remember thy congregation, which thou hast purchased of old; the rod of thine inheritance, which thou hast redeemed; this mount Zion, wherein thou hast dwelt.

**TDB77:** Tanglai-a na ngahsa nangma mihonte, nangma luah minam dinga, na tatkhiatsate phawk in! Na ten’nasa Zion Mual phawk in.

**ZVS:** Tanglai-in na ngahsa na mihonte, nangma luah minam dingin na tatkhiatsate phawk in! Na ten'nasa Zion Mual hong phawk in.

### v3
**EN:** Lift up thy feet unto the perpetual desolations; even all that the enemy hath done wickedly in the sanctuary.

**TDB77:** A sia densa munah hong kalsuan in; biakinn sunga om nate khempeuh gal in susia khin ta!

**ZVS:** A sia densa munah hong pai in. Na biakinn sunga om khempeuh galte in susia khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v4
**EN:** Thine enemies roar in the midst of thy congregations; they set up their ensigns for signs.

**TDB77:** Na galte, nangma mun siangtho laizangah awng-te a, amau lumte lim dingin suang-te hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma galte mun siangtho laizangah awng-te a, amau' dialte gualzawhna lim dingin a khai-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** A man was famous according as he had lifted up axes upon the thick trees.

**TDB77:** Amaute pen, tuu-lak sing phatna sungah pai-in heipi tawh sing a phuk bang-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute pen tulak sing phatna-ah pai-in heipi tawh sing a phuk bang-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** But now they break down the carved work thereof at once with axes and hammers.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin biakinn na kizeemte khempeuh sek-hei leh sekpite tawh sat kham-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin biakinn na a kizemte khempeuh sek-hei leh sekpi tawh a satkham-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** They have cast fire into thy sanctuary, they have defiled by casting down the dwelling place of thy name to the ground.

**TDB77:** Amaute in nangma biakinn mei tawh hal-te a, nangmah hong kibiakna mun susia-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in mei tawh na biakinn hal-te a, nangmah hong kibiakna mun a susia-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** They said in their hearts, Let us destroy them together: they have burned up all the synagogues of God in the land.

**TDB77:** Amaute in, “A vekpi-in zomang khin ni,” kici-te a, gam sung teng-a om Pasian biakna inn khempeuh mei tawh hal-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in, “A vekpi-in zo mang khin ni,” kici-te a, gamsung tenga om Pasian biakna inn khempeuh mei tawh a hal-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** We see not our signs: there is no more any prophet: neither is there among us any that knoweth how long.

**TDB77:** Kote in ka mailam thu ding-te ka thei kei-te hi; kamsang khat zong om nawn lo a, bang tanvei hi ding ci-a a thei kuamah ka om kei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kote in ka mailam thu ding-te ka thei kei-te hi. Kamsang khat zong om nawnlo a, bang tanvei hi ding, ci-in a thei kuamah ka om kei-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** O God, how long shall the adversary reproach? shall the enemy blaspheme thy name for ever?

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, galte in bang tanvei hong thusim loin om ding ahi hiam? Langpangte in nangma min hong simmawh den ding ahi-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, ka galte in bang tanvei hong thusim loin om ding ahi hiam? Langpangte in nangma min hong simmawh den ding ahi-te hiam?

### v11
**EN:** Why withdrawest thou thy hand, even thy right hand? pluck it out of thy bosom.

**TDB77:** Bang hangin na khut dok kik a, na khut taklam na ang sungah koih na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang hangin na khut dokkik a, na khut taklam na ang-ah koihcip na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** For God is my King of old, working salvation in the midst of the earth.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin Pasian in tanglai pek-a kipan kumpi hi a, leitungah hotkhiatna a sem ka Kumpi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Pasian pen tanglai peka kipanin kumpi hi tawntung a, hih leitungah a hong honkhia zelzel ngei ka Kumpi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** Thou didst divide the sea by thy strength: thou brakest the heads of the dragons in the waters.

**TDB77:** Nangma vangliatna tawh tuipi na khenkham a, tuite tunga gulpite’ lutangte na sukham hi.

**ZVS:** Na vangliatna tawh tuipi na khenkham a, tuite tunga gulpite' lutangte na sukham hi.

### v14
**EN:** Thou brakest the heads of leviathan in pieces, and gavest him to be meat to the people inhabiting the wilderness.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in Awle’ lutangte na sukham a, amah pen, gamlak ganhingte’ nek dingin na pia hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in Awle' lutangte na sukham a, amah pen, gamlak ganhingte' nek dingin na pia hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v15
**EN:** Thou didst cleave the fountain and the flood: thou driedst up mighty rivers.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in ciktuite leh luite na phulsak a, guntui luangte na kangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Ciktuite leh luite na phulsak a, guntui luangte na kangsak hi.

### v16
**EN:** The day is thine, the night also is thine: thou hast prepared the light and the sun.

**TDB77:** Sun in nangma aa hi a, zan zong nangma aa ahi hi; nangmah in ni leh kha na bawl hi.

**ZVS:** Sun pen nangma-a' hi a, zan zong nangma-a' mah ahi hi. Nangmah in ni leh kha na bawl hi.

### v17
**EN:** Thou hast set all the borders of the earth: thou hast made summer and winter.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in leitung gamgi khempeuhte, phalbi hun leh khuakhal hunte na bawl khin hi.

**ZVS:** Leitung gamgi khempeuhte leh phalbi hun leh khuakhal hunte na bawl khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v18
**EN:** Remember this, that the enemy hath reproached, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, galte in hong thusimlohna leh, dawi bia mite in nangma min a simmawhna thu-te phawk in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, galte in nang hong thusimlohna leh, dawibia mite in nangma min a hong simmawhna hong phawk ta in.

### v19
**EN:** O deliver not the soul of thy turtledove unto the multitude of the wicked: forget not the congregation of thy poor for ever.

**TDB77:** A cimawh na mite, a gal gilote-te khut sungah nusia kei in la, bawlsiatna a thuak na mite mangngilh kei in.

**ZVS:** A cimawh mite a gal gilote-te khutsungah nusia kei in la, bawlsiatna a thuak mite mangngilh kei in.

### v20
**EN:** Have respect unto the covenant: for the dark places of the earth are full of the habitations of cruelty.

**TDB77:** Na thuciamna hong pingaihsut in; bang hang hiam cih leh leitung mun mialna khempeuh mi ngongtatte’ omna tawh kidim hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma thuciamna hong pi ngaihsut in; bang hang hiam cih leh leitung mun mialna khempeuh mi ngongtatte tawh kidim hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v21
**EN:** O let not the oppressed return ashamed: let the poor and needy praise thy name.

**TDB77:** A kisikcip mite zahhuaisak kei in la, mizawngte leh mi cimawhte nangma min kiphatsak in.

**ZVS:** A kisikcip mite zahhuaisak kei in la, mizawng leh mi cimawhte nangma min kiphatsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v22
**EN:** Arise, O God, plead thine own cause: remember how the foolish man reproacheth thee daily.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, tho in la, nangma thu-ah hong pang in; dawi bia mite in sun tawntungin nangmah hong thusim lohna phawk ta in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, tho in la, nangma thu-ah hong pang in. Dawibia mite in sun tawntungin nang a hong thusimlohna hong phawk ta in!

### v23
**EN:** Forget not the voice of thine enemies: the tumult of those that rise up against thee increaseth continually.

**TDB77:** Nangma galte’ kikona aw, nang hong langpangte’ gamlum semsemna mangngilh kei ta in!

**ZVS:** Nangma galte' kikona aw, nang a hong langpangte a gamlum semsemna mangngilh kei ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
