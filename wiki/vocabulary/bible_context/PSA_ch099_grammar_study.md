## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in uk hi; mihonte ling hen! Amah, Kherubte tungah tu hi; leitung liing ta hen!

**ZVS:** { Topa } in uk hi. Mihonte ling hen. Kherub-te tungah amah tu hi. Leitung ling ta hen.

### v2
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Zion khuapi-ah Topa lian hi; amah in leitung mi khempeuh uk hi.

**ZVS:** Zion khuapi-ah { Topa } lian a, amah in leitung mi khempeuh uk hi.

### v3
**EN:** Let them praise thy great and terrible name; for it is holy.

**TDB77:** A lian a kihtak-huai ama min, mi khempeuh in phat ta hen. Amah siangtho hi.

**ZVS:** A lian a kihtakhuai ama min mi khempeuh in phat ta uh hen. Amah siangtho hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** The king's strength also loveth judgment; thou dost establish equity, thou executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob.

**TDB77:** Thutang a deih, Vanglian Kumpipa aw, nangmah in thuman na phut khin a, Jakob’ sungah thutang leh thuman na semkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Thutang a deih, Vanglian Kumpipa aw, nangmah in thuman na phut khin a, Israel-te sungah thutang leh thuman na semkhia hi.

### v5
**EN:** Exalt ye the

**TDB77:** I Topa Pasian phat un; ama khe ngakna munah bia un! Amah siangtho hi!

**ZVS:** I { Topa } Pasian phat un la, ama khengakna munah bia un. Amah siangtho hi.

### v6
**EN:** Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among them that call upon his name; they called upon the

**TDB77:** Moses leh Aaronte in ama siampi hi uh a, amah a biate lakah Samuel zong kihel hi. Topa tungah amaute kiko uh a, amah in amaute a dawng hi.

**ZVS:** Moses leh Aaron-te ama siampi hi uh a, amah a biate lakah Samuel zong a kihel hi. { Topa' } tungah amaute kiko uh a, amah in amaute a dawng hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** He spake unto them in the cloudy pillar: they kept his testimonies, and the ordinance that he gave them.

**TDB77:** Amah in meii khuampi sung pan amaute tungah thu gen a, ama teci pan’nate leh amah in amau tunga a thukham piakte amaute in zui uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in meii khuampi sung pan amaute tungah thu gen a, ama teci pan'nate leh amah in amaute tungah a thukham piakte amaute in a zui uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** Thou answeredst them, O

**TDB77:** Kote’ Topa Pasian aw, nangmah in amaute na dawng hi; nangmah in amaute tungah mawhna a maisak Pasian na hi a, ahih hangin amau gamtat khialhnate a thukkik khat na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Kote' { Topa } Pasian aw, nang in amaute na dawng hi. Amau' mawhna hangin gim na piak tei hangin amau' mawhnate a maisak nuam Pasian na hihna amaute' tungah na kilangsak hi.

### v9
**EN:** Exalt the

**TDB77:** Topa Pasian phat un la, ama mual siangtho-ah bia un; bang hang hiam cih leh i Topa Pasian siangtho hi!

**ZVS:** I { Topa } Pasian phat un la, ama mual siangtho-ah bia un; bang hang hiam cih leh i { Topa } Pasian a siangtho hi.
