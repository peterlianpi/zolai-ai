## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ka to kumpipa tungah, “Na galte nangma khe ngakna-in ka bawl matengin ka taklam khutah tu in,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in ka to kumpipa tungah, “Na galte nangma khengakna-in ka bawl matengin ka taklamah tu in,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in Zion panin na kumpi ciangkhut hong puak hi. Na galte’ lakah uk in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } in Zion panin na kumpi ciangkhut hong puak hi. Na galte' tungah uk in.

### v3
**EN:** Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth.

**TDB77:** Na mihonte mual siangtho tungah na makaih ciangin, utna lungsim tawh na mite hong kipia ding uh hi. Daitui bangin zingsang ciangin na khangnona nangma tungah hong tung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma mihonte mual siangtho tungah na makaih ciangin, utna lungsim tawh na mite hong kipia ding uh hi. Daitui bangin zingsangin na khangnona nangma tungah hong tung ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in thuciam zo a, ama ngaihsutna kheel lo ding hi. “Melkhizedek bangin nang in a tawntungin siampi na hi hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in thu ciamzo a, ama ngaihsutna kheello ding hi. “Melkhizedek bangin a tawntungin siampi na hi hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will

### v5
**EN:** The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.

**TDB77:** Topa in nangma taklam-ah om hi; ama heh ni-in amah in kumpite zogawp ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma taklamah Topa hong om a, ama heh ni-in kumpite a zogawp ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v6
**EN:** He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa in minamte lakah thukhen ding a, tua munte misi luang tawh dimsak ding hi; amah in a zai mahmah leitungah ukpite susia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa in minamte lakah thu khen ding a, tua munte si luanghawm tawh a dimsak ding hi. Amah in a zai mahmah leitungah ukpite a susia ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will

### v7
**EN:** He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head.

**TDB77:** Amah in lampi gei-a luitui dawnin thahat ding a, amah a gualzo-in om ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in lampi gei-a om luitui dawn ding a, amah a tha hatin a gualzopa hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will
