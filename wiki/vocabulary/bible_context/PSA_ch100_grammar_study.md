## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Make a joyful noise unto the

**TDB77:** Gam khempeuh aw, Topa tungah lungdamna aw ging sak un!

**ZVS:** Gam khempeuh aw, { Topa' } tungah lungdamna aw gingsak un.

### v2
**EN:** Serve the

**TDB77:** Lungdamna tawh Topa’ na sem un! Ama omna-ah la sa kawmin hong pai un!

**ZVS:** Lungdamna tawh { Topa' } na sem un. Ama omna-ah la sa kawmin hong pai un.

### v3
**EN:** Know ye that the

**TDB77:** Topa pen Pasian a hihna thei un! Eite hong bawlpa in amah hi a, eite in ama aa i hi hi; eite in ama mi i hi a, ama gan khahna sunga a tuute i hi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa, } Pasian ahihna thei un. Eite hong bawlpa amah hi a, eite ama-a' ihi hi. Eite ama mi ihi a, ama gankhahna sunga om a tuute ihi hi.

### v4
**EN:** Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, and into his courts with praise: be thankful unto him, and bless his name.

**TDB77:** Lungdam kohna tawh ama kongpi-ah lut un la, phatna tawh ama huang sungah tum un; Ama tungah lungdam ko un la, ama min phat un!

**ZVS:** Lungdam kohna tawh ama kongpi-ah lut un la, amah phatna tawh ama huang sungah tum un. Ama tungah lungdam ko un la, ama min phat un.

### v5
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa hoih hi; ama itna kip-in kho tawntung a, ama thumanna a khang a khangin a kip hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } hoih hi. Ama itna kipin kho tawntung a, ama thumanna a khang khangin kip hi.

**Grammar:** adjective: hoih (good) | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix
