## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Wisdom hath builded her house, she hath hewn out her seven pillars:

**TDB77:** Pilna in a inn lam a, a inn ding khuampi sagih phut hi.

**ZVS:** Pilna in a inn lam a, a innkhuampi sagih a phut hi.

### v2
**EN:** She hath killed her beasts; she hath mingled her wine; she hath also furnished her table.

**TDB77:** Amah in pawi bawl dingin sa go-in leenggahzu khaihin, an nek nading sabuai bawl hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in pawi bawl dingin sa go-in leenggahzu khaih a, annekna ding sabuai a bawl hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** She hath sent forth her maidens: she crieth upon the highest places of the city,

**TDB77:** Khua sung mun sang khempeuh panin mi sam dingin, amah in a nasem numeite sawl a,

**ZVS:** Khuasung munsang khempeuh panin mi a sam dingin a nasem numeite sawl a,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** Whoso is simple, let him turn in hither: as for him that wanteth understanding, she saith to him,

**TDB77:** “Thu thei lote aw, hong pai un,” ci-in samsak a, mihaite kiangah,

**ZVS:** “Thu theilote aw, hong pai un,” ci-in samsak a, mihaite kiangah,

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Come, eat of my bread, and drink of the wine which I have mingled.

**TDB77:** “Hong pai un la, ka huansa ka an ne-in, ka bawlsa leenggahzu hong dawn un.

**ZVS:** “Hong pai un la, ka an huansa ne-in, ka bawlsa leenggahzu hong dawn un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** Forsake the foolish, and live; and go in the way of understanding.

**TDB77:** Thu thei lote taisanin khan’sau un la, pilna lampi zui un,” ci sak hi.

**ZVS:** Thu theilote taisanin khan'sau un la, pilna lampi zui un,” cisak hi.

### v7
**EN:** He that reproveth a scorner getteth to himself shame: and he that rebuketh a wicked man getteth himself a blot.

**TDB77:** Mihaite thuhilh lecin hong maizumsak ding a, migilote thuhilh lecin hong sugawp ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v8
**EN:** Reprove not a scorner, lest he hate thee: rebuke a wise man, and he will love thee.

**TDB77:** Mihaite thuhilh kei in, hong mudah ding hi. Mipilte thuhilh in, hong it ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite thuhilh kei in; hong mudah ding-te hi. Mipilte thuhilh in; hong it ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** Give instruction to a wise man, and he will be yet wiser: teach a just man, and he will increase in learning.

**TDB77:** Mipilte thuhilh lecin pil semsemna-in nei ding a, thuman mite thuhilh lecin a thu theihna khang semsem ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mipilte thuhilh lecin pil semsemna-in nei ding a, thuman mite thuhilh lecin a thutheihna-te khang semsem ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v10

**TDB77:** Topa zahtakna in pilna kipatna hi a, Siangtho Pa theihna in thumuhna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } zahtakna pen pilna kipatna hi a, Siangtho Pa theihna pen thumuhna ahi hi.

### v11
**EN:** For by me thy days shall be multiplied, and the years of thy life shall be increased.

**TDB77:** Pilna in na khang hong khan ding a, na nuntakna hong sausak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v12
**EN:** If thou be wise, thou shalt be wise for thyself: but if thou scornest, thou alone shalt bear it.

**TDB77:** Pil lecin nangma phattuam nading hi a, pilna don kei lecin nangma sup nading ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pil lecin nangma phattuamna ding hi a, pilna don kei lecin nangma supna ding ahi hi.

### v13
**EN:** A foolish woman is clamorous: she is simple, and knoweth nothing.

**TDB77:** Haina in a kamtam, zumna meel thei lo numei tawh kibang a,

**ZVS:** Haina pen a kamtam, zumna mel a theilo numei tawh kibang a,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14

**TDB77:** ama kong gei-ah ahi a, khuasung mun sangpen ah ahi zongin tu a,

**ZVS:** ama kong gei-ah ahi a, a sangpen munah ahi zongin amah tu a,

### v15

**TDB77:** ama kiang a nawkkha, amau’ nasep bek a phawk kimlai mite kiangah,

**ZVS:** ama kiang a nawkkha, amau' nasep bek a phawk kimlai mite kiangah,

### v16

### v18
**EN:** But he knoweth not that the dead are there; and that her guests are in the depths of hell.

**TDB77:** Tua lai-a a paite a sihna thu, tua lai-a lutsate sihna gamthuk sungah a om khitna thu, a khem zawh mite in phawk kha lo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua lai-ah a paite a sihna thu tua lai-ah a lutsate sihna gam thuk sungah a om khitna thu, a khemzawh mite in phawkkhalo-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | plural: uh marker
