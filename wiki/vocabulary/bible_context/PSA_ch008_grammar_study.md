## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa, kote’ Topa aw, leitung khempeuhah na min bangzahin lian! Vantungah na min a than’na

**ZVS:** { Topa } kote' Topa aw, leitung khempeuhah na min lian mahmah hi. Nangmah a hong kiphatna awgingte in vantung ban a,

### v2
**EN:** Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings hast thou ordained strength because of thine enemies, that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger.

**TDB77:** naungekte leh naupangte’ kam tawh kisa hi; gal na neih manin galte leh langpangte na daihsak nadingin panmun kip na bawl khin hi.

**ZVS:** naungekte leh naupangte' kam tawh nangmah phatna la kisa hi. Gal na neih manin galte leh langpangte na daihsakna dingin pan'munkip na bawl khin hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | completed: already done | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** When I consider thy heavens, the work of thy fingers, the moon and the stars, which thou hast ordained;

**TDB77:** Na khutmete’ nasepna, na bawlsa kha leh aksite, na vantungte ka et ciangin,

**ZVS:** Na nasepna, na bawlsa, kha leh aksite, leh na vantungte ka et ciangin,

### v4
**EN:** What is man, that thou art mindful of him? and the son of man, that thou visitest him?

**TDB77:** nangma ngaihsut tham dingin mi in bang hi a, na phawk tham dingin mihing’ ta bang hi lel ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** na ngaihsut tham dingin mihing bang hi lel a, na phawk tham dingin mihing' ta bang hi lel ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** For thou hast made him a little lower than the angels, and hast crowned him with glory and honour.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin, Pasian sangin a neuzaw deuhin amah na bawl a, minthan’na leh pahtawina na guan hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin, vantung mi sangin a neuzaw deuh bekin na bawl a, minthan'na leh pahtawina nangmah in na guan hi.

### v6
**EN:** Thou madest him to have dominion over the works of thy hands; thou hast put all things under his feet:

**TDB77:** Na khut tawh na bawlsate khempeuh tungah ukna amah na pia hi.

**ZVS:** Na khut tawh na bawlsate khempeuh tungah ukna thu ama tungah na pia a, na khempeuh na uksak hi.

### v7
**EN:** All sheep and oxen, yea, and the beasts of the field;

**TDB77:** tuu leh bawng khempeuh, gamsa khempeuh zong,

**ZVS:** Tuu leh bawng khempeuh, gamsa khempeuh zong,

### v8
**EN:** The fowl of the air, and the fish of the sea, and whatsoever passeth through the paths of the seas.

**TDB77:** huih lak-a leeng vasate, tui sunga om ngasate, tui’ lampi-a tawn khempeuhte, na khempeuh ama khe nuai-ah na koih hi.

**ZVS:** huih laka leng vasate, tui sunga om ngasate, tui, lampi-a a tawn khempeuhte, na khempeuh ama khenuai-ah na koih hi.

### v9
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa, kote’ Topa aw, leitung khempeuhah na min bangzahin lian!

**ZVS:** { Topa, } kote' Topa aw, leitung khempeuhah na min lian mahmah hi.
