## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Cast thy bread upon the waters: for thou shalt find it after many days.

**TDB77:** Tui tungah na anlum khia in, ni tampi khit ciangin na mu kik ding hi.

**ZVS:** Sumbawlna dingin na sumte zang in. Ni khat ni peuh teh a meet na ngah ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Give a portion to seven, and also to eight; for thou knowest not what evil shall be upon the earth.

**TDB77:** Na neihsate, mun sagih ahi a, mun giat-ah ahi zongin khen kawikawi in. Hih leitungah bang thusia hong piang ding cih na thei khol kei hi.

**ZVS:** Na sumbuk vante mun sagih-ah ahi a, mun giat-ah ahi zongin khen kawikawi in. Hih leitungah bang thusia hong piang ding cih na theikhol kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** If the clouds be full of rain, they empty themselves upon the earth: and if the tree fall toward the south, or toward the north, in the place where the tree falleth, there it shall be.

**TDB77:** Meii sungah tui a tamluat ciangin, leitungah guah-in hong zusuk a, singkung khatpeuh a puk ciangin, a pukna san san-ah om paisuak hi.

**ZVS:** Meii sungah tui a tamluat ciangin leitungah guah-in hong zusuk a, singkung khatpeuh a puk ciangin a pukna san sanah om paisuak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** He that observeth the wind shall not sow; and he that regardeth the clouds shall not reap.

**TDB77:** Huih leh guah ngakin, khuahun hoih dong a ngak mi in, khaici tuh ngei lo ding a, an zong la ngei lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Huih leh guah ngakin khuahun hoih dong a ngak mi in khaici tuh ngeilo ding a, an zong la ngeilo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v5
**EN:** As thou knowest not what is the way of the spirit, nor how the bones do grow in the womb of her that is with child: even so thou knowest not the works of God who maketh all.

**TDB77:** Nupi gai khat sungah nuntakna thak bangci kipat hiam, ci-in na theih zawh loh mah bangin, na khempeuh a bawl Pasian’ nasepna, na thei zo ngei kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nupi gai khat sungah nuntakna thak bangci kipat hiam, ci-in na theihzawhloh mah bangin na khempeuh a bawl Pasian' nasepna na theizo ngei kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** In the morning sow thy seed, and in the evening withhold not thine hand: for thou knowest not whether shall prosper, either this or that, or whether they both shall be alike good.

**TDB77:** Zingsang-a kipan khaici tuh in la, nitak dong mateng khawl kei in. A vek-a a po kim ding ahi a, khat sia-in, khat a po ding ahi zongin, bangmah na theih kholh om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Zingsanga kipan khaici tuh in la, nitak dong mateng khawl kei in. A vekpi-in a po kim ding ahi a, a khat sia-in, a khat a po ding ahi zongin na theihkholh bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Truly the light is sweet, and a pleasant thing it is for the eyes to behold the sun:

**TDB77:** Khuavak hoih mahmah a, i mit in nitaang a muh ciangin nuam mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** adjective: hoih (good) | intensifier: very/greatly

### v8
**EN:** But if a man live many years, and rejoice in them all; yet let him remember the days of darkness; for they shall be many. All that cometh is vanity.

**TDB77:** Mi khat sau veipi a nuntak leh a nuntak sung nuam takin om ta hen. Sau veipi tak a hing zongin, a sih khit hun a sau vei zawkna phawk ta hen. Mai lam-ah a lam-ethuai bangmah a om kei hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat sauveipi a nuntak leh a nuntak sung nuamtakin om ta hen. Sauveipi tak a hing zongin a sih khit hun a sauvei zawkna phawk ta hen. Mailamah a lamethuai bangmah a om kei hi.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | negation: kei

### v9
**EN:** Rejoice, O young man, in thy youth; and let thy heart cheer thee in the days of thy youth, and walk in the ways of thine heart, and in the sight of thine eyes: but know thou, that for all these things God will bring thee into judgment.

**TDB77:** Khangnote aw, na khangno uh nuam sa un la, na khangno lai-un lungdam un. Na hih nop peuhpeuh uh hih un la, na ut bang bangun gamta un. Ahi zongin na gamtatna uh tawh kizui-in, Pasian in thu hong khen ding lamtak phawk gige un.

**ZVS:** Khangnote aw, na khangnona uh nuamsa un la, na khangno lai-un lungdam un. Na hihnop peuhpeuh uh hih un la, na ut bangbangun gamta un. Ahi zongin na gamtatna uh tawh kizui-in Pasian in thu hong khen ding phawk gige un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Therefore remove sorrow from thy heart, and put away evil from thy flesh: for childhood and youth are vanity.

**TDB77:** Note kilunggimsak kei un la, na pumpi uh gentheisak kei un. Bang hang hiam cih leh na khangno hun sung uh sau vei lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Note kilunggimsak kei un la, na pumpi uh gentheisak kei un. Bang hang hiam cih leh na khangno hunsung uh sauveilo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
