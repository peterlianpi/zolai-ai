## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Behold, a king shall reign in righteousness, and princes shall rule in judgment.

**TDB77:** En un, ni khat ni ciangin thumanna tawh kumpi khat hong maang ding a, thutan’na tawh uliante in hong uk ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** En un, ni khat ni ciangin thuman takin kumpi khat in hong uk ding a, uliante in thutang tawh hong uk ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** For the vile person will speak villany, and his heart will work iniquity, to practise hypocrisy, and to utter error against the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi hai in haina thu gen a, a lungsim ah gitlohna om a, a hoihin gamtat loh ding, Topa’ thu a hilopi-in gen ding, a gilkialte’ an duhna a phat lohna ding leh dangtakte in tui dawn ding a neih theih loh nadingin, ama lungsim in mawhna bawl ding sawm hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mihai in haina thu gen a, a hoihin gamtat loh ding, { Topa}' thu a hoihlopi-in gen ding, a gilkialte' an duhna a phatlohna ding leh a dangtakte in tui dawn ding a neih theihlohna dingin, ama lungsim in mawhna bawl ding a sawm hi.

### v7

**ZVS:** Mi ginalo' ginatlohna siahuai mahmah hi. Zuauna kam tawh mizawngte a siasak ding leh mi cimawhte' thu a thuman ciang nangawnin a siasak dingin amah in gitloh nasepna a bawl hi.

### v9
