### v1

**TDB77:** Judah kumpipa Jehoiakim kumpi a suah zawh a kum thumna kumin, Babilon kumpipa Nebukhadnezzar in Jerusalem khuapi simin a umcih hi.

### v2

**TDB77:** Topa in, Jehoiakim kumpipa leh biakinnpi’ na manphate Nebukhadnezzar kumpipa a mansak hi. Amah in a mi matte Babilon gam ama pasian biakinnah paipih a, na manpha a matte sum koihna innah a koih hi.

**ZVS:** Topa in kumpi Jehoiakim leh biakinnpi na manphate kumpi Nebukhadnezzar a mansak hi. Amah in a mi matte Babylon gam ama pasian biakinn-ah paipih a, na manpha a matte sumkoihna inn-ah a koih hi.

### v3

### v4

**TDB77:** Tua mite in kumpi inn sunga nasem siamte a suah theih nadingun a mel hoih, pumpi tung poina neilo, a navak, hoih taka pilna kisinsak, na a thei baih lungsim navakte ngentang ding ahi hi. Ashpenaz in amaute Babilon lai leh pau a hilh ding ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua mite, kumpi innsunga nasem siamte a suahtheihna dingun a melhoih, pumpi tung poina a neilo, a navak, hoihtakin pilna a kisinsak, na a thei baih, a lungsim navak ngentang ding ahi hi. Ashpenaz in amaute Babylon lai leh a pau a hilh ding ahi hi.
