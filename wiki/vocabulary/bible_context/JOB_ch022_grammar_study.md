## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Teman khua mi Elifaz in dawng a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Teman khuami Elifaz in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** Can a man be profitable unto God, as he that is wise may be profitable unto himself?

**TDB77:** “Mihing a pil pente i cih nangawn, ama pumpi’ ading cih loh buang, Pasian’ ading a kiman’na om lo hi.

**ZVS:** “Mipil pente i cih nangawn, ama-a' ding cih lohbuang, Pasian-a' dingin a kiman'na omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Is it any pleasure to the Almighty, that thou art righteous? or is it gain to him, that thou makest thy ways perfect?

**TDB77:** Na gamtat hoihna in Pasian’ phattuam nading piangsak lo a, na hoihna in Pasian hoihsak tuan lo hi.

**ZVS:** Na gamtat hoihna in Pasian' phattuamna ding bangmah piangsaklo a, na hoihna in Pasian hoihsak tuanlo hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | negation: lo

### v4
**EN:** Will he reprove thee for fear of thee? will he enter with thee into judgment?

**TDB77:** Nangmah in Pasian na zahtakna hangin, amah in nang hong tai-in hong ze-et hilo hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in Pasian na zahtakna hangin amah in nang hong taii-in hong ze-et hilo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** Is not thy wickedness great? and thine iniquities infinite?

**TDB77:** Na mawhna a tamna hang, na gitlohna a liatna hang hizaw hi.

### v6
**EN:** For thou hast taken a pledge from thy brother for nought, and stripped the naked of their clothing.

**TDB77:** Na sanggampa’ hong sum bat taang dingin, a silh ding siitsak loin a puan teng na laksak hi.

**ZVS:** Na sanggampa' leiba tangin a silh ding sitsak loin a puan teng na laksak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v7
**EN:** Thou hast not given water to the weary to drink, and thou hast withholden bread from the hungry.

**TDB77:** A dangtakte tui na pia nuam kei a, a gilkialte an na pia nuam kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v8
**EN:** But as for the mighty man, he had the earth; and the honourable man dwelt in it.

**TDB77:** Na vangliatna leh na za liatna zangin, gam khempeuh na la khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v9
**EN:** Thou hast sent widows away empty, and the arms of the fatherless have been broken.

**TDB77:** Meigong numeite a huh nuam lo bek hi loin, mi tagahte bawlsia-in a neihsa uh na sut hi.

**ZVS:** Meigongte a huh nuamlo ciang bek hiloin, mi tagahte bawlsia-in a neihsa uh na sut hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10
**EN:** Therefore snares are round about thee, and sudden fear troubleth thee;

**TDB77:** Tua ahih manin na kimkotah thang hong siah a, thakhat thu-in launa in hong buaisak hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin na kimkotah thang hong kisiah a, thakhatthu-in launa in nang hong buaisak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Or darkness, that thou canst not see; and abundance of waters cover thee.

**TDB77:** Khuavak kimlai khuamial hong suak vatin khua na mu thei kei a, tuikhang in nang hong tuumcip hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Is not God in the height of heaven? and behold the height of the stars, how high they are!

**TDB77:** “Van a sang pen dawn-ah Pasian om a, a sang mahmah a om aksite hong suk et lai hi.

**ZVS:** “Van a sangpen dawnah Pasian a om hi a, a sang mahmaha om aksite hong suk et lai hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v13
**EN:** And thou sayest, How doth God know? can he judge through the dark cloud?

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin nangmah in, Pasian in bang thei ding ahi hiam? Meii in Pasian tuamcip hi—eite’ thu bang hong ci khen theih ding ahi hiam?’ na ci hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin nangmah in, ‘Pasian in bang thei ding ahi hiam? Meii in Pasian tuamcip hi. Eite' thu bangci-in khenthei ding ahi hiam?’ na ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v14
**EN:** Thick clouds are a covering to him, that he seeth not; and he walketh in the circuit of heaven.

**TDB77:** Van khumzangah Pasian a pai kawmin, meii pailetin Pasian in hong musuk thei lo ding hi, ci-in na ngaihsun hi.

**ZVS:** Van khumzangah Pasian a paikawmin meii pailetin Pasian in hong musuk theilo ding hi, ci-in na ngaihsun hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v15
**EN:** Hast thou marked the old way which wicked men have trodden?

**TDB77:** Migilote’ gamtat ngeite mah gamtat ding ngaihsun na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Migilote' gamtat ngeite mah gamtat ding ngaihsun na hi hiam?

### v16

**TDB77:** Amau’ hun tung lopi-in tuikhang in amaute taai hi.

**ZVS:** Amau' hun tunglopi-in tuikhang in amaute taii hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v17
**EN:** Which said unto God, Depart from us: and what can the Almighty do for them?

**TDB77:** Hih mite pen Pasian a nial, Pasian in amaute bangmah a cih thei lo dinga a ki-umte ahi uh hi.

**ZVS:** Hih mite pen Pasian a nial, Pasian in bangmah a cih theilo dingin a ki-um mite ahi uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v18

**ZVS:** Tua hita se leh amaute a daupaisak Pasian hi a, migilote' ngaihsutna pen keima ngaihsutna tawh kigamla mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** The righteous see it, and are glad: and the innocent laugh them to scorn.

**TDB77:** Migilote a kidaantatna a muh uh ciangin mi hoihte nuamin, pauban’na nei lo mite in nuihsan uh hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote tungah gim kipia a muh uh ciangin mihoihte nuam a, paubaan'na a neilo mite in a nuihsan uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v20

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh migilote' neih khempeuh kisuksiatsak gawp a, a omlai sunte nangawn mei in a kangtum hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v21
**EN:** Acquaint now thyself with him, and be at peace: thereby good shall come unto thee.

**TDB77:** “Job aw, tu-in Pasian tawh kilem in la, amah galbawl nawn kei in; tua hi leh amah in nang thupha hong pia ding hi.
