## Verses

### v1
**EN:** They that trust in the

**TDB77:** Topa a muang mite pen, ling loin a om tawntung Zion Mual tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } a muang mite pen ling hetloin a om tawntung Zion Mual tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** As the mountains are round about Jerusalem, so the

**TDB77:** Mualte in Jerusalem kimkotah a om mah bangin, Topa in ama mite’ kimkotah tu-a kipanin a tawntungin om ding hi.

**ZVS:** Jerusalem kimkotah mualte a om mah bangin ama mite' kimkotah tu-a kipan a tawntungin { Topa } om ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For the rod of the wicked shall not rest upon the lot of the righteous; lest the righteous put forth their hands unto iniquity.

**TDB77:** Thuman mite in khialhna a bawl khak loh nadingun, amaute tunga kipia leitang tungah migilote in amaute uk paisuak lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Thuman mite a khialhkhak lohna dingun amau a kipia leitang tungah migilote in amaute uk paisuaklo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Do good, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, a hoih mite tung leh na thupiakna a zui mite tungah hehpihna hong nei in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, a hoih mite tung leh na thupiakna a zui mite tungah hehpihna hong nei in.

### v5
**EN:** As for such as turn aside unto their crooked ways, the

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin amau’ gitlohna a zui mite pen siatna bawlte daan na piak ciangin, amaute tawh pia khawm in. Israelte tungah nopna om ta hen!

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin amau' gitlohna a zuite pen siatna a bawlte gim na piak ciangin amaute tawh piakhawm in. Israel tungah nopna om ta hen!

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be
