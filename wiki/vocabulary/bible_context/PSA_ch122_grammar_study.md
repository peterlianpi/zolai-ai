## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the

**TDB77:** Amaute in kei tungah, “Topa’ innah pai ni,” hong cih uh ciangin ka kipak hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in kei tungah, “{ Topa' } inn-ah pai ni,” hong cih uh ciangin kei ka kipak hi.

**Grammar:** hortative: let us | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Our feet shall stand within thy gates, O Jerusalem.

**TDB77:** Jerusalem aw, na kulh kongpi sungah kote hong tungin ka ding khin ta uh hi.

**ZVS:** Jerusalem aw, na kulhkongpi sungah kote hong tungin ka ding khinta uh hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Whither the tribes go up, the tribes of the

**TDB77:** Jerusalem khuapi pen Israel-te tungah thu a kipiaksa bangin { Topa' } tungah lungdamna a ko dingin { Topa' } minamte, Israel minamte, a hong paitohna, khuapi lian tawh a kibangin a kibawl, kiptakin a kilam bikbek khuapi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** For there are set thrones of judgment, the thrones of the house of David.

**TDB77:** David innkuante’ kumpi tokhom ahi, thukhen nading kumpi tokhom tua lai-ah kikoih hi.

**ZVS:** David' innkuante' kumpi tokhom, thukhenna ding kumpi tokhomte, tua lai-ah kikoih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: they shall prosper that love thee.

**TDB77:** Jerusalem khuapi’ nopsak nading thu ngen un la, “Nang hong itte a dau uh pai ta hen!

**ZVS:** Jerusalem khuapi, a nopsakna ding thu ngen un la, “Nang hong itte a dau pai ta hen.

### v7
**EN:** Peace be within thy walls, and prosperity within thy palaces.

**TDB77:** Na kulhte sungah lungnopna om hen la, na galvilna tausangte’ huam sungah lungmuan’na om ta hen” ci un.

**ZVS:** Na khuasungah lungnopna om hen la, na galvilna tausangte' huam sungah lungmuan'na om ta hen,” ci-in ngen un.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be

### v8
**EN:** For my brethren and companions' sakes, I will now say, Peace be within thee.

**TDB77:** Sanggamte leh kikholhpihte ka khualna-in, “Nang sungah nopna om ta hen!” ka ci ding hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte leh kikholhpihte ka khualna-in, “Jerusalem in nopna om ta hen,” ka ci ding hi.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Because of the house of the

**TDB77:** Topa i Pasian’ inn ka khualna-in nangma nopsakna ka zong ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } eite' Pasian' inn ka khualna-in nangma hoihna ding thu ka ngen ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will
