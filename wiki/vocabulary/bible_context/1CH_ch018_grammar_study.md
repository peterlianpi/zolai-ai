## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Now after this it came to pass, that David smote the Philistines, and subdued them, and took Gath and her towns out of the hand of the Philistines.

**TDB77:** Hih thu khit ciangin David in Filistiate zoin nuaisiah a, Filistiate’ khut sung panin Gath leh a khuaneute la hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thute khit ciangin David in Filistia mite zo-in nuaisiah a, Filistia mite' khutsung panin Gath leh a khuaneute a la hi.

### v2
**EN:** And he smote Moab; and the Moabites became David's servants, and brought gifts.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin amah in Moab mite zo a, Moab mite David’ nasem suakin siah hong puak uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin amah in Moab mite zo a, Moab mite David' nasem suakin siah hong puak uh hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** And David smote Hadarezer king of Zobah unto Hamath, as he went to stablish his dominion by the river Euphrates.

**TDB77:** Zobah leh Hamath gam kumpipa Hadadezer-in Eufrates gun kuam gamte ngah kik nuamin kuan ahih manin, David in amah doin zo hi.

**ZVS:** Zobah leh Hamath gam kumpipa Hadadezer in Eufratis gun kuam gamte ngahkik nuamin hong kuan ahih manin David in amah do-in a zo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin David in ama tung panin leeng galkap tul khat, sakol tung tuang galkap tul sagih, leh khe galkap tul sawmnih la hi. Tua ciangin David in leengkai sakol galkap khempeuh a zagui attatsak a, ahih hangin leeng za khat-a' dingin a kicing a sit hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix
