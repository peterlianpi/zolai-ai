## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And when he had called unto him his twelve disciples, he gave them power against unclean spirits, to cast them out, and to heal all manner of sickness and all manner of disease.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Zeisu in a nungzuite sawm leh nihte a sap khit ciangin, kihhuai dawite hawlkhiat theih nading ahi zongin, cinatna pawl khempeuh damsak theih nading ahi zongin thu a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh in a nungzui sawmlenihte sam a, dawite a hawlkhiat theihna ding-te leh natna nam khempeuh a damsak theihna dingun amaute tungah vangliatna a pia hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Now the names of the twelve apostles are these; The first, Simon, who is called Peter, and Andrew his brother; James the son of Zebedee, and John his brother;

**TDB77:** Sawltak sawm leh nihte a min un: a masa bel Piter kici, Simon leh a naupa Andru, Zebedi’ tapate Jakob leh Johan,

**ZVS:** Tua sawltak sawmlenihte' min in: A masa-in Simon (Peter zong a kicipa) leh a naupa Andru, Zebedi' tapa James leh a naupa Johan,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Philip, and Bartholomew; Thomas, and Matthew the publican; James the son of Alphaeus, and Lebbaeus, whose surname was Thaddaeus;

**TDB77:** Filip leh Bartholomai, Thomas leh siahdong Mattiu, Alfe-as’ tapa Jakob leh Thaddeas,

**ZVS:** Filip leh Bartholomew, Thomas leh siahdongpa Matthai, Alfeas' tapa James, Thaddeas,

### v4
**EN:** Simon the Canaanite, and Judas Iscariot, who also betrayed him.

**TDB77:** Kanaan mi Simon leh Zeisu a lehheekpa Judah Iskarot-te ahi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Simon (gam khualpa), leh Jesuh a lehhek Iskariot' tapa Judas ahi-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** These twelve Jesus sent forth, and commanded them, saying,

**TDB77:** Tua sawm leh nihte Zeisu in sawl a, “Gentailte omna-ah pai kei un. Samaria-te khua sungah lut kei un.

**ZVS:** Jesuh in hih mi sawmlenihte sawl a, “Gentail mite gamsung ahi a, Samaria mite khuasungah ahi zongin lut kei un.
