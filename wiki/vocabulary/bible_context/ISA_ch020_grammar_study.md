## Verses

### v1
**EN:** In the year that Tartan came unto Ashdod, (when Sargon the king of Assyria sent him,) and fought against Ashdod, and took it;

**TDB77:** Assiria kumpipa Sargon in a sawl galkap mang lian penpa in Filistia khuapi Ashdod-ah pai-in, tua khua va do hi.

**ZVS:** Assiria kumpipa Sargon in a sawl galkapmang lianpenpa, Filistia khuapi Ashdod-ah pai-in, tua khua va sim hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** At the same time spake the

**TDB77:** Tua hun ma kum thum lai takin Topa in Amoz’ tapa Isaiah tung tawnin thu genin, “Na puanhampi suah in la, na khe panin na khedapte suah in,” a ci hi. Amah zong tua mah bangin gamta-in, tangguak leh kheguak tawh lam pai hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hun ma kum thum laitakin { Topa } in Amoz' tapa Isaiah tung tawnin thu genin, “Na puanhampi suah in la, na khe panin na khedapte suah in,” a ci hi. Amah zong tua mah bangin gamta-in, tangguak leh kheguak tawh lam a pai hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** And the

**TDB77:** Ashdod khua a kilak khit ciangin Topa in, “Izipt leh Ethiopia’ lang khatah lim leh siahil dingin ka nasempa Isaiah tangguak leh kheguak tawh kum thum sung lam a pai mah bangin,

**ZVS:** Ashdod khua a kilak khit ciangin { Topa } in, “Egypt leh Ethiopia langkhatah lim leh siahil dingin ka nasempa Isaiah tangguak leh kheguak tawh kum thum sung lam a pai mah bangin,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** So shall the king of Assyria lead away the Egyptians prisoners, and the Ethiopians captives, young and old, naked and barefoot, even with their buttocks uncovered, to the shame of Egypt.

**TDB77:** Assiria kumpipa in, Izipt gam mite’ maizumna ahi, a moi a hamin, tangguak kheguakin, tawbo dawksa-in, Izipt mite gal-in manin, Ethiopia mite sal-in manin paikhiatpih ding hi.

**ZVS:** Assiria kumpipa in, Egypt gam mite' maizumna ahi, a moi a hamin, tangguak kheguakin, tawbo dawksa-in, Egypt mite galin manin, Ethiopia mite salin manin paikhiatpih ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | causal: because

### v5
**EN:** And they shall be afraid and ashamed of Ethiopia their expectation, and of Egypt their glory.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin amau’ muan Ethiopia leh amau’ kisialhpih Izipt’ hangin amaute lungkia-in a maizum ding-te thei lo ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin amau' lametna Ethiopia leh amau' kisialhpih Egypt' hangin amaute lungkia-in a cihna ding-te theilo ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** And the inhabitant of this isle shall say in that day, Behold, such is our expectation, whither we flee for help to be delivered from the king of Assyria: and how shall we escape?

**TDB77:** Tua ni ciangin hih tuipi gei-a teeng mite in, “En un, eite’ muan Assiria kumpipa’ kiang pan a, eite huhin honkhia dingin i belh mite’ tungah hih bang piang khin ta hi. Eite bangci suahtak ding i hi hiam?’ ci ding-te hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ni ciangin hih tuipi gei-a teng mite in, ‘En un, eite' lametna, Assiria kumpipa' kiang panin eite hong honkhia dingin i belh mite' tungah hih bang piang khinta hi. Eite bangci suahtak ding ihi hiam?’ ci ding-te hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | purpose: in order to | completed: already done | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
