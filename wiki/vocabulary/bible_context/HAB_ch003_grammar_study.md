## Verses

### v1
**EN:** A prayer of Habakkuk the prophet upon Shigionoth.

**TDB77:** Kamsang Habakkuk in hih bangin thu ngen hi:

### v2
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangma sepsa thute ka za a, lamdang ka sa mahmah hi. Na hih ngeisa na liante, ko’ hunin zong hong hih kik in. Na heh hun ciang nangawnin zong, hehpihna lungsim hong nei in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangma sepsa thute ka za a, lamdang ka sa mahmah hi. Na hih ngeisa na liante, kote' hunin zong hong hih kik in. Na heh hun ciang nangawnin zong hehpihna lungsim hong nei in.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** God came from Teman, and the Holy One from mount Paran. Selah. His glory covered the heavens, and the earth was full of his praise.

**TDB77:** Teman khua panin Pasian hong pai a, Paran Mual panin Siangtho Pa hong pai hi. Ama minthan’na in vanpi tuam a, leitung khempeuh, amah phatna tawh kidim hi.

**ZVS:** Teman khua panin Pasian hong pai a, Paran Mual panin Siangtho Pa hong pai hi. Ama minthan'na vang in vanpi tuam a, leitung khempeuh, amah phatna tawh kidim hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** And his brightness was as the light; he had horns coming out of his hand: and there was the hiding of his power.

**TDB77:** Khuaphialep taang bangin hong pai a, a khut sung panin khuavak taang khia hi; tua in a vangliatna a koihna mun ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Khuaphialep taang bangin amah hong pai a, a khutsung panin khuavak taangkhia a, tua pen a vangliatna a koihna mun ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** Before him went the pestilence, and burning coals went forth at his feet.

**TDB77:** Ama mai-ah cinatna tungsak a, tua nungah sihna zuisak pah hi.

**Grammar:** punctual: immediately/suddenly

### v6
**EN:** He stood, and measured the earth: he beheld, and drove asunder the nations; and the everlasting mountains were scattered, the perpetual hills did bow: his ways are everlasting.

**TDB77:** Amah a khawl ciangin leitung liing a, a et ciangin minam khempeuh liing uh hi. Taangtawng lai-a a totsa, taanglai mualliante kitamkham a, a om tawntung mualneute kimetcip hi.

**ZVS:** Amah a khawl ciangin leitung ling a, amah in a et ciangin minam khempeuh ling uh hi. Tangtawng lai-in a totsa, tanglai mual liante kitam kham a, a om tawntung mual neute kimetcip hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** I saw the tents of Cushan in affliction: and the curtains of the land of Midian did tremble.

**TDB77:** Kush gam mite a lau mahmahna, leh Midian gam mite a liin’na uh ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Kush gammite a lau mahmahna, Midian gammite a lin'na ka mu hi.

### v8
**EN:** Was the

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na sakolte tungah nang hong tuangin, na gualzawhna leengte tawh nong pai nadingin, gunpite tungah na hehsuak a, tuipi tungah na thangpai ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na sakolte tungah nang hong tuangin, na gualzawhna leengte tawh nong paina dingin, gunpite tungah na heh suak a, tuipi tungah thangpai na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v9
**EN:** Thy bow was made quite naked, according to the oaths of the tribes, even thy word. Selah. Thou didst cleave the earth with rivers.

**TDB77:** Na thal lawn dingin na kithawi a, na thallawng sungah thaltang a dimin om hi. Tuipi tawh leitung na phelkhap hi.

**ZVS:** Na thal a lawn dingin na kithawi a, na thallawng sungah na thaltang a dimin om a, na keek in leitung kapkham hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** The mountains saw thee, and they trembled: the overflowing of the water passed by: the deep uttered his voice, and lifted up his hands on high.

**TDB77:** Mualliante in nang hong muh ciangin liing uh a, van panin guah tampi hong zu hi. Lei nuai-a om tuite ging zuazua a, a tuihualte in sangpipi banto hi.

**ZVS:** Mual liante in nang hong muh ciangin ling uh a, van panin guah tampi hong zu hi. Leinuai-a om tuite ging zuazua a, tuihualte in a sangpipi banto hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** The sun and moon stood still in their habitation: at the light of thine arrows they went, and at the shining of thy glittering spear.

**TDB77:** Na thaltang leeng a muh ciang, na teipi tee zalzal a muh ciangin, ni leh kha a omna san-ah khawlcip uh hi.

**ZVS:** Na thaltang leeng a muh ciang, na teipi te zalzal a muh ciangin, ni leh kha a omna sanah a khawlcip uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** Thou didst march through the land in indignation, thou didst thresh the heathen in anger.

**TDB77:** A hehsa-in leitungah na pai a, a sinsosa-in minamte na tuancil hi.

**ZVS:** A hehsa-in leitungah na pai a, a sinso sa-in minamte na tuancil hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v13
**EN:** Thou wentest forth for the salvation of thy people, even for salvation with thine anointed; thou woundedst the head out of the house of the wicked, by discovering the foundation unto the neck. Selah.

**TDB77:** Na mihingte a honkhia dingin na paikhia a, na teel kumpipa, a honkhia dingin na kuankhia hi. Mi gilote a makaipa na sugawp a, a nungzui khempeuh tawh zong na sumai hi.

**ZVS:** Na mite a honkhia dingin na paikhia a, na teel kumpipa, a honkhia dingin na kuankhia hi. Migilote' makaipa na sugawp a, a nungzui khempeuh tawh zong na sumai hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v14
**EN:** Thou didst strike through with his staves the head of his villages: they came out as a whirlwind to scatter me: their rejoicing was as to devour the poor secretly.

**TDB77:** Mi cimawhte, mun simtham ah a sugawpte bangin, kote hong suthang dingin huihpi bangin hong pai laitakin, na thal tawh, a galkapmangpa na kaplet hi.

**ZVS:** Mi cimawhte, mun simthamah a sukgawp bangin, kote a hong suthang dingin huihpi bangin hong pai laitakin na thal tawh a galkapmangpa na kaplet hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v15
**EN:** Thou didst walk through the sea with thine horses, through the heap of great waters.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in na sakolte tawh tuipi na tuancil a, tui lianpite phuan zuazua hi.

### v16
**EN:** When I heard, my belly trembled; my lips quivered at the voice: rottenness entered into my bones, and I trembled in myself, that I might rest in the day of trouble: when he cometh up unto the people, he will invade them with his troops.

**TDB77:** Hih thute khempeuh ka zak ciangin ka liing gawp a, lau lua-in ka muk liing sapsap hi. Ka guh ka taang hong zen a, guh nei lo bangin ka pukcip suk hi. Ko hong simgawp mite Pasian in a daan piak ding hun, dai takin ka ngak khikhe ding hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thute khempeuh ka zak ciangin ka pumlin' a, lau lua-in ka muk ling sapsap hi. Ka guh ka taang hong zen a, guh neilo bangin ka pukcipsuk hi. Ko hong simgawp mite Pasian in gim a piak ding hun daitakin ka ngak khikhe ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: lo

### v17
**EN:** Although the fig tree shall not blossom, neither shall fruit be in the vines; the labour of the olive shall fail, and the fields shall yield no meat; the flock shall be cut off from the fold, and there shall be no herd in the stalls:

**TDB77:** Thei gahte hut khin a, leengguite a gah kei zongin, olive kungte gah lo a, lo-ah an a piang kei zongin, tuu khempeuh si khin a, ganbuk khempeuh a hawm khin zongin,

**ZVS:** Theigahte a hut khin a, leengguite a gah kei zongin, oliv kungte gahlo a, lo-ah an a piang kei zongin, tuu khempeuh a si khin a, ganbuk khempeuh a hawm khin zongin,

### v18
**EN:** Yet I will rejoice in the

**TDB77:** Topa Pasian in keimah hong honkhiapa ahih manin, lungdamin ka kipak lai veve ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } Pasian pen hong honkhiapa ahih manin lungdamin ka kipak lai veve ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v19
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa Pasian in keima thahatna ahi hi; sakhi khe bangin ka khe hong zaptelsak a, mun sangna ah, lungmuang takin hong paisak hi. (Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in khausai lasakna vante tawh tum theihte hong makaihpa ahi hi).

**ZVS:** { Topa } Pasian, keima thahatna ahi hi; sakhi khe bangin ka khe hong zaptelsak a, mun sangna-ah lungmuang takin kei hong paisak hi.
