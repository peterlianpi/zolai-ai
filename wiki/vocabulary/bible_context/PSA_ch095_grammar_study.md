## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O come, let us sing unto the

**TDB77:** Hong pai un, Topa phat ni; eite a hong hu, a hong honkhia pa tungah lungdamna la sa ni.

**ZVS:** Hong pai un, { Topa } phat ni. Eite a hong hu, a hong honkhiapa tungah lungdamna la sa ni.

### v2
**EN:** Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving, and make a joyful noise unto him with psalms.

**TDB77:** Ama omna-ah lungdamna ko-in pai ni; phatna late, ama tungah lungdam takin sa ni!

**ZVS:** Ama omna-ah lungdamna ko-in pai ni. Phatna late, ama tungah lungdam takin sa ni.

**Grammar:** hortative: let us | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** For the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa in a lian Pasian khat hi a, pasian khempeuh tungah Kumpi lian khat ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa, } a lian Pasian hi a, pasian khempeuh tungah Kumpi lianpa ahi hi.

### v4
**EN:** In his hand are the deep places of the earth: the strength of the hills is his also.

**TDB77:** Amah in leitung khempeuh uk a, guam thukpen panin mual sangpen dawn dongin amah in uk hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in leitung khempeuh uk a, guam thuk pen panin mual sang pen dawn dongin amah in a uk hi.

### v5
**EN:** The sea is his, and he made it: and his hands formed the dry land.

**TDB77:** Amah in a bawlsa tuipite uk a, ama bawlsa leitung zong uk hi.

**ZVS:** Ama bawlsa tuipite uk a, ama bawlsa leitung zong amah in a uk hi.

### v6
**EN:** O come, let us worship and bow down: let us kneel before the

**TDB77:** Hong pai un, bia-in kun ni, eite hong Bawl Topa mai-ah khukdin’ ni!

**ZVS:** Hong pai un, amah bia-in kun ni. Eite hong Bawl { Topa' } mai-ah khukdin' ni.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** For he is our God; and we are the people of his pasture, and the sheep of his hand. To day if ye will hear his voice,

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in eite’ Pasian hi a, eite in ama hong kep mite leh ama khut sunga om tuute i hi hi. Tu ni-in hih bangin ama hong thugen za un:

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah pen eite' Pasian hi a, eite pen ama hong kep mite leh ama khutsunga om a tuute ihi hi. Tuni-in hih bangin ama hong thugen za un:

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v9
**EN:** When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my work.

**TDB77:** Amaute in ka nasepna mu khin uh hinapi, Meribah leh Massah munah na pate un kei hong ze-etin, gamtatna tawh ka lah ding hong kalh hun lai uh bangin, note in na lungsim uh khauhsak kei un.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v10
**EN:** Forty years long was I grieved with this generation, and said, It is a people that do err in their heart, and they have not known my ways:

**TDB77:** Kum sawmli sung tua minam ka kih a, “Amaute in a lungsim sungah a khial mi hi a, amaute in ka thupiakte mang lo uh hi,” ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** Kum sawmli sung tua minam ka kih a, “Amaute pen a lungsim uhah a khial mi hi a, amaute in ka thupiakte manglo uh hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** Unto whom I sware in my wrath that they should not enter into my rest.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ka nopsakna mun-ah amaute a lut lo dingin sinso kawmin ka kiciam hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin keima nopsakna munah amaute a lutlo dingin sinso kawmin ka kiciam hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix
