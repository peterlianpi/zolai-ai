## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks, unto thee do we give thanks: for that thy name is near thy wondrous works declare.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangma tungah ka lungdam uh hi; nangma tungah ka lungdam uh hi; nangma liatna thu ka gen uh a, a lamdang na bawlnate ka sut kikkik uh hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nangma tungah ka lungdam mahmah uh a, nangma tungah ka nuam mahmah uh hi. Nangma liatna thu ka gen uh a, a lamdang na bawlnate ka sut kikkik uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** When I shall receive the congregation I will judge uprightly.

**TDB77:** A hun ka sehsa ni ciangin thu a maanin ka khen ding hi.

**ZVS:** A hun ka sehsa ni ciangin thu a manin ka khen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3
**EN:** The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. Selah.

**TDB77:** Leitung leh a tunga teengte khempeuh a kilin’ ciangin, a khuampite a kipsakpa in keimah ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Leitung leh a tunga tengte khempeuh a kilin' ciangin a khuampite a kipsakpa keimah ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly: and to the wicked, Lift not up the horn:

**TDB77:** A kiphasakte tungah, “Kiphasak kei un,” a gilote tungah, kihisak kei un;

**ZVS:** A kiphasakte tungah, “Kiphasak kei un,” a gilote tungah, “Kihisak kei un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Lift not up your horn on high: speak not with a stiff neck.

**TDB77:** kihisak kei un la, kiphatsakna tawh kam pau kei un,” ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** Kihisak kei un la, kiphatsakna tawh kampau kei un,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** For promotion cometh neither from the east, nor from the west, nor from the south.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh san’tohna in nisuahna lam ahi a, nitumna lam ahi a, gamlak panin ahi zongin hong pai hi lo hi;

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh vangliatna pen nisuahna lam ahi a, nitumna lam ahi a, gamlak pan ahi zongin a hong pai hilo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v7
**EN:** But God is the judge: he putteth down one, and setteth up another.

**TDB77:** ahih hangin mihing khat a sangsak, mihing khat a niamsak, thu a khenpa in Pasian ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mihing khat a sangsak, mihing khat a niamsak thukhenpa pen Pasian ahi hi.

### v8
**EN:** For in the hand of the

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa khut sungah leenggahzu a phuan, a kihelkim ngiingei hai khat om a, amah in tua sung panin hong bungkhia dinga, leitung migilote khempeuh in a tawdat dong takin liaktum ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa' } khutsungah leenggahzu a phuan, a kihelkim ngiingei hai khat om a, amah in tua sung panin hong bungkhia ding a, leitung migilo khempeuh in a tawdat dong takin a liaktum ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
