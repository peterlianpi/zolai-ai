### v2

### v3

**TDB77:** Pasian in kamsangpa Isaiah tungah a hilh ngei bangin: “Gampa lakah a kikopa’ aw in, ‘Topa paina lam na sial un, Ama lampi na tangsak un,’ a ci hi,” a cihna in tua mipa a cihnopna hi.

**ZVS:** Kamsang Isaiah in, “Gamsungah mi khat kiko a, ‘Topa' paina ding lampi bawl un la, a tot ding lampi tangsak un,’ ci hi,” ci-in a gen ciangin hih Johan' thu a gen ahi hi.

### v9

**ZVS:** Na kisikkik khitna uh a kilatna dingin tui sungah note ka hong phum a, ahi zongin kei nungah a hong paipa in Kha Siangtho leh mei sungah note hong phum ding hi. Tua a hong pai dingpa pen kei sangin nakpi takin lianzaw a, ama khedap a tawisak ding nangawnin ka kilawmzo nai kei hi.
