## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Gimna hunah Topa in nangmah hong en! Jakob Pasian min in nangmah hong dal!

**ZVS:** Gimna na thuak ciangin { Topa } in nang hong en hen la, Jakob' Pasian in nangmah hong dal ta hen!

### v2
**EN:** Send thee help from the sanctuary, and strengthen thee out of Zion;

**TDB77:** Siangtho mun panin amah in huhna nang hong puak hen la, Zion panin nangmah pan’pihna hong pia ta hen!

**ZVS:** Siangtho mun panin huhna nang hong puak hen la, Zion panin nang hong pan'pih ta hen!

### v3
**EN:** Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah.

**TDB77:** Na biakpiaknate khempeuh amah in hong phawk hen la, na meihal biakpiakna hong sang ta hen.

**ZVS:** Na biakpiaknate khempeuh amah in hong phawk hen la, na meihal biakpiakna hong sang ta hen!

### v4
**EN:** Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel.

**TDB77:** Na lungsim’ deihna amah in hong pia hen la, na geelna khempeuh hong tangtungsak ta hen!

**ZVS:** Na lungsim' deihna amah in hong pia hen la, na geelna khempeuh hong tangtungsak ta hen!

### v5
**EN:** We will rejoice in thy salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up our banners: the

**TDB77:** Na zawhna-ah eite lungdamin awng-in, i Pasian’ min tawh i lante khai ni! Na ngetna khempeuh Topa in hong tangtung sak ta hen!

**ZVS:** Na gualzawhna-ah eite lungdamin awngin, i Pasian' min tawh i lante khai ni!

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** Now know I that the

**TDB77:** Topa in a sathau nilhpa a huh ding tu in ka thei ta a, ama vantung siangtho panin ama taklam khut-a gualzawhna lianpite tawh amah a dawng ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in a sathau nilhpa a huh ding tu-in ka thei ta a, ama vantung siangtho panin amah dawng ding a, a vangliatna tawh amah gualzosak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v7
**EN:** Some trust in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the

**TDB77:** Pawlkhat in leengte kihihsak pih-a, pawlkhat in sakolte kihihsak pih hi; ahih hangin eite in Topa i Pasian’ min i kihihsak pih hi.

**ZVS:** Mi pawlkhat in leengte kihihsakpih a, mi pawlkhat in sakolte kihihsakpih hi. Ahih hangin eite in { Topa } i Pasian' min bek i kihihsakpih hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright.

**TDB77:** Amaute kinawmsawm-in puk ding uh a, ahih hangin eite tho-in i dingtang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute kinawmsawmin pukgawp ding uh a, ahih hangin eite tho-in i dingtang ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** Save,

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kumpipa tungah gualzawhna pia in la, kong sap ciangun hong dawng in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kumpipa tungah gualzawhna pia in la, kong sap ciangun hong dawng in.
