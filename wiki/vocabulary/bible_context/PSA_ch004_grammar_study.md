## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Hear me when I call, O God of my righteousness: thou hast enlarged me when I was in distress; have mercy upon me, and hear my prayer.

**TDB77:** Kong sap ciangin hong dawng in, a thuman ka Pasian! Gimna ka thuak ciangin nopsakna nong pia hi. Kei hong hehpih in la ka thungetna hong za in.

**ZVS:** A thuman ka Pasian aw, ka hong sap ciangin hong dawng in. Gimna ka thuak ciangin nopsakna nong pia hi. Kei hong hehpih in la, ka thungetna hong za in.

### v2
**EN:** O ye sons of men, how long will ye turn my glory into shame? how long will ye love vanity, and seek after leasing? Selah.

**TDB77:** Mite aw, ka minphatna in bang tanvei daina thuak ding ahi hiam? Bang tanvei thu mawkna deih-in, zuauthu zong ding na hi uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Mite aw, ka minphatna in bang tanvei daina thuak ding ahi hiam? Bang tanvei thumawkna deihin zuauthu zong ding na hi uh hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** But know that the

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin Topa in miphate ama adingin a koihtuam lam thei ta un; ke’n ka sap ciangin Topa in hong za hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin { Topa } in ama-a' dingin miphate a tuamkoihlam thei ta un. Ke'n ka sap ciangin { Topa } in hong za hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** Stand in awe, and sin not: commune with your own heart upon your bed, and be still. Selah.

**TDB77:** Lau-in liing un la, mawhna pel un; na lupna tung uh-ah lungngai phapha un la, om khikhe lel un.

**ZVS:** Lau-in ling un la, mawhna pel un. Na lupna tung uhah lungngai phapha un la, om khikhe un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** Offer the sacrifices of righteousness, and put your trust in the

**TDB77:** A maan biakna piaknate lui un la, Topa muang un.

**ZVS:** A man biakpiaknate lui un la, { Topa } muang un.

### v6
**EN:** There be many that say, Who will shew us any good?

**TDB77:** Mi tampi in, "Thupha ngah thei leeng ut si hang. Ko tungah na maitang vang hong taangsak in, Topa!" ci uh hi.

**ZVS:** Mi tampi in, “Thupha tampi hong pia in. Ko tungah na maitang vang hong taangsak in, { Topa}!” ci uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Thou hast put gladness in my heart, more than in the time that their corn and their wine increased.

**TDB77:** Amaute in an leh leenggahtui a hauh cianga a nuam uh sangin nuamna lianzaw ka lung sungah nong guan hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute, an leh leengtui a hauh cianga a nuam uh sangin nuamna lianzaw ka lung sungah nong guan hi.

### v8
**EN:** I will both lay me down in peace, and sleep: for thou,

**TDB77:** Nuam takin lumin ka ihmu ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh Topa aw, nangmah bek in lungmuan’na munah nong om sak hi.

**ZVS:** Nuamtakin lumin ka ihmu ta ding hi; bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } aw, nangmah bek in lungmuan'na munah na hong omsak hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
