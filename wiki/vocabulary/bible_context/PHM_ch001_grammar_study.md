## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Paul, a prisoner of Jesus Christ, and Timothy our brother, unto Philemon our dearly beloved, and fellowlabourer,

**TDB77:** Khazih Zeisu hanga thongkia kei, Paul leh i sanggampa Timoti in kote tawh nasem a, ka it uh Filemon,

**ZVS:** Khrih Jesuh hangin thong a kia keimah Paul leh i sanggampa Timoti in, Ka hong it uh, ka naseppih uh ahi Filemon,

### v2
**EN:** And to our beloved Apphia, and Archippus our fellowsoldier, and to the church in thy house:

**TDB77:** leh i sanggamnu Apfia leh ko tawh galkap ahi khawm Arkhippas akipan, nangma inn sungah a om pawlpite ka hong zasak uh hi.

**ZVS:** leh na inn-ah a hong kikhawm pawlpite leh i sanggamnu Apfia leh thu upna-ah galkap khawmpih Arkhippas-te tungah lai ka hong khak uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

**TDB77:** I Pa Pasian leh i Topa Zeisu Khazih kiangpan hehpihna thupha leh nopna note tungah om ta hen.

**ZVS:** I Pa Pasian leh Topa Jesuh Khrih in note tungah dikna leh nopsakna thupha hong tungsak ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Hearing of thy love and faith, which thou hast toward the Lord Jesus, and toward all saints;

**TDB77:** Pasian' mite khempeuh na itna thu leh Topa Jesuh na upna thu na zakhin ka hih manin Pasian kiangah thu ka nget simin nangmah hong phawkin Pasian tungah lungdamna thu ka ko tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** That the communication of thy faith may become effectual by the acknowledging of every good thing which is in you in Christ Jesus.

**TDB77:** Upna na zopna a vanglian-in, Khazih Zeisu sungah eite’ aa ahi, ahoih na a om khempeuhte midangte in a theih nadingun thu ka ngen hi.

**ZVS:** Thu umte ihihna hangin Khristian sungah eite i kikholh manin, Khrih hangin i nuntakna sungah i ngah thupha a hoih khempeuh na theihna a kicin' semsemna dingin thu ka ngen hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** For we have great joy and consolation in thy love, because the bowels of the saints are refreshed by thee, brother.

**TDB77:** Sanggam aw, nangma hangin misiangthote in lungsim thaksuahna a ngah uh ahih ciangin nangma itna ngaihsunin lungdam mahmahin ka nuam zo hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggam aw, nangma hangin Pasian' mite in lungnopna ngah uh ahih manin tua bang itna na neihna hangin ka lungdamin ka tha a nuam mahmah hi.

### v8
**EN:** Wherefore, though I might be much bold in Christ to enjoin thee that which is convenient,

**TDB77:** Tua ahih manin Khrih hangin unau khat ihihna tawh hih dingin a kilawm bangin na gamtatna dingin thu ka hong gen ngam hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Yet for love's sake I rather beseech thee, being such an one as Paul the aged, and now also a prisoner of Jesus Christ.

**TDB77:** Hih bangin ka hong genna pen itna i neihna hang ahi hi. Tu lai takin Khrih tangmi ahi keimah Paul zong, Khrih Jesuh hangin thongkia-in a om ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v10

**TDB77:** Kikhihna ka thuak lai takin ka tapa a hong suak,

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Khrih hangin ka ta ahi Onesimas-a' dingin ka hong thumsak hi. Thong sungah ka om sungun kha thu tawh kisai-ah ama pa ka suak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Which in time past was to thee unprofitable, but now profitable to thee and to me:

**TDB77:** nidangin nang adingin kiman’na om lo mi, tu-in ahih leh, kei leh nang adingin a kizang thei ding mi Onesimas ading ka hong thuum hi.

**ZVS:** Nidangin nang-a' dingin amah a kiman'na bangmah omlo a, ahi zongin tu-in eite gel-a' dingin mi manpha mahmah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12

### v13

**TDB77:** thupha na hong bawl ciangin, zawhthawh thu bang hi loin, na ut thu-a na bawl ahih nading ka deih a, sawlzawh thu tawh ka hong hih nuam kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin nangma nasep hoihna pen keima hong kalh hiloin nangma uttawmna tawh na sep a suahtheihna dingin nangma lungkimna om masa lopi-in bangmah ka hih nuam kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v15

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** Not now as a servant, but above a servant, a brother beloved, specially to me, but how much more unto thee, both in the flesh, and in the Lord?

**TDB77:** Tua bang ka cih ciangin sila bangin ngaihsun nawn loin, tham loin kei in ka it taktak ka nau hi a, nang adingin pumpi sungah ahi zongin, Pasian sungah ahi zongin bang zahin a it huai semsem naupa bangin na ngaihsut nading ka cihnopna hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in amah sal bek hi nawnloin sal sangin a thupizaw, Khrih hangin i sanggam hong hita hi. Kei-a' dingin amah a manphat mah bangin sal hihna leh Topa thu sungah sanggam hihna tawh nang-a' dingin zong hong manphazaw kan ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v17

### v18

**TDB77:** Hong lohkik ning ci-in keimah Paul in ka khut tawh ka hong gelh hi. Ahih hangin keima tungah na hong lohkik ding leiba om hi ka cihna a hi kei hi.

**ZVS:** Tua banah, Pasian in note khempeuh' thungetna hong pia-in, note kiangah kei hong paikik thei dingin kilamen ka hih manin ka omna ding inndei zong a baihsa-in na bawl un.
