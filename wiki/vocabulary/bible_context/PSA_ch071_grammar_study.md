## Verses

### v1
**EN:** In thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangmah kong bel hi; keimah hong zahhuaisak ngei kei in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nang kong bel hi. Kei hong zahhuaisak kei in.

### v2
**EN:** Deliver me in thy righteousness, and cause me to escape: incline thine ear unto me, and save me.

**TDB77:** Na thumanna tawh keimah hong gum in la hong honkhia in; kei lamah na bil hong ngat in la hong hon in!

**ZVS:** Na thumanna tawh kei hong huh in la, hong honkhia in. Keima lamah na bil hong ngat in la, hong hon in.

### v3
**EN:** Be thou my strong habitation, whereunto I may continually resort: thou hast given commandment to save me; for thou art my rock and my fortress.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in hong hon dingin ka belh suangpi, ka kulh muan’huai hong hi ta in, bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in keima suangpi leh ka kulhpi na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Hong hon dingin ka belh suangpi, ka kulh muan'huai, hong hi ta in; bang hang hiam cih leh keimah hong honpa leh ka belhpa na hi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** Deliver me, O my God, out of the hand of the wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man.

**TDB77:** Ka Pasian aw, migilote khutsung pan leh, thuman lote leh migilote’ matna panin keimah hong hon in.

**ZVS:** Ka Pasian aw, migilote' khutsung pan leh thumanlo mite leh migilote' matna panin keimah hong hon in.

### v5
**EN:** For thou art my hope, O Lord

**TDB77:** Topa aw, bang hang hiam cih leh ka neu lai-a kipanin nangmah in keima lametna, ka muan’na na hi hi, Topa aw.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa { Pasian } aw, ka neu lai-a kipanin ka hong lamet, ka hong muan' na hi hi, { Topa } aw.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

**TDB77:** Ka suah tunga kipanin nangmah kong bel hi, ka nu’ gil sung panin hong lakhia-pa na hi hi. Nangmah kong phat ngitnget hi.

**ZVS:** Ka suah tunga kipanin nangmah kong bel a, ka nu' gilsung panin hong lakhiapa na hi hi. Nangmah ka hong phat ngitnget hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7

**ZVS:** Mi tampite-a' dingin a siahil bang ka hih hangin nang pen a muan'huai, keima belh na hi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v8
