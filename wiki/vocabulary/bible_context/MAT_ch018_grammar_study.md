## Verses

### v1
**EN:** At the same time came the disciples unto Jesus, saying, Who is the greatest in the kingdom of heaven?

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin a nungzuite in Zeisu kiangah va nai uh a, “Vantung Gam sungah kua a lian bel ding hiam?” ci-in a dong uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua laitakin Jesuh kiangah nungzuite hong pai uh a, “Vantung ki-ukna sungah kua lianpen ding ahi hiam?” ci-in a dot uh ciangin,

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** And said,

**TDB77:** “Ke’n aman takpi kong cih-inah na kikheel uh a, hih naupangno bang na suah kei uh leh, Vantung Gam sungah na tum zo kei ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** “A man mahmahin note kiangah kong genin-ah: Note kikheelin naupang na bat kei uh leh vantung ki-ukna sungah na lut ngei kei ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v21
**EN:** Then came Peter to him, and said, Lord, how oft shall my brother sin against me, and I forgive him? till seven times?

**TDB77:** Tua khit ciangin Piter in va nai a, “Topa aw, ka u ka nau in kei tungah hong khial leh, ama mawhna bangzah vei ka maisak ding hiam? Sagih vei dongin ka maisak ding hiam?” ci-in a dot ciangin

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Peter in Jesuh va naih a, “Topa aw, ka sanggamte khatpeuh ka tungah hong khialh ciangin bangzah vei maisak ding ka hi hiam? Sagihvei dongtak maisak ding ka hi hiam?” ci-in a dot ciangin

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v22
**EN:** Jesus saith unto him,

**TDB77:** Zeisu in, “Sagih vei dong maisak in kong ci kei hi. Sawm sagih mun sagih vei dongin maisak in kong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Jesuh in, “Sagihvei ciangbek maisak in kong ci kei hi. Sawm sagih mun sagihvei maisak in kong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | negation: kei
