### v6

**ZVS:** Sarah zong tu-a bangmahin gamta a, a pasal Abraham' thu mangin, “Topa aw,” a ci hi. Note zong a hoihin gamta-in, bangmah launa neiloin na om nak-te leh tu-in Sarah' tanute na hita-te hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v7

**ZVS:** Tua mah bangin pasalte aw, note sangin na zite-te a thahatlo zawdeuh ahihna thu na phawk un. Note mah bangin Pasian' piak nuntakna a ngahte ahih-te manin na zite-te zahtak bawl un. Tua hi leh na thungetna-te bangmah in hong nawngkaisak lo ding hi.
