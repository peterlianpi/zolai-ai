## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And when he had opened the seventh seal, there was silence in heaven about the space of half an hour.

**TDB77:** Tuuno in sagihna ciamtehna a hon’ khit ciangin, vantungah nai lang sung bang ging loin a om hi.

**ZVS:** Tuuno in ciamtehna sagihna a hon' ciangin vantungah nai lang sung bang kidai dide hi.

### v2
**EN:** And I saw the seven angels which stood before God; and to them were given seven trumpets.

**TDB77:** Tua nung ciangin Pasian mai-ah ding-in a om vantungmi sagih ka mu hi. Tuate peengkul sagih a kipia hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Pasian mai-ah a ding vantung mi sagihte ka mu a, amaute tungah peengkul sagih a kipia hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** And another angel came and stood at the altar, having a golden censer; and there was given unto him much incense, that he should offer it with the prayers of all saints upon the golden altar which was before the throne.

**TDB77:** Kham kuang a tawi adang vantungmi khat in zong hong pai-in biakna tau kiangah ding a, tokhom mai-ah a om kham biakna tau tungah a om misiangthote khempeuh thungetna tungah sun’ sawn dingin paknamtui a pia hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin kham kibawl paknamtui kuang a tawi vantung mi adang khat hong pai-in biakna tau mai-ah a ding hi. Pasian' mite in thungetna-in a zat paknamtuite tawh hal beh ding leh tokhom mai-ah a om kham kibawl biakna tau tungah piak dingin amaute tungah paknamtui tampi tak a kipia hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** And the smoke of the incense, which came with the prayers of the saints, ascended up before God out of the angel's hand.

**TDB77:** Tua misiangthote thungetna tawh, paknamtui khu in zong tua vantungmi khut sung pan Pasian mai-ah tungto hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' mai-ah a ding vantung mipa' khutsung panin paknamtui khu leh Pasian' mite' thungetna van lam manawhin kizialto hi.

### v5
**EN:** And the angel took the censer, and filled it with fire of the altar, and cast it into the earth: and there were voices, and thunderings, and lightnings, and an earthquake.

**TDB77:** Tua nungciangin vantungmi in kuang la-in biakna tau pan mei tawh a dimsak khit ciangin leitungah a khiatsuk leh, vanging, aw-ging, khuaphelepte leh zinliingte a piang hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin tua vantung mi in paknamtui kuang la a, biakna tau panin mei-am la-in a sungah a koih khit ciangin leitungah lawn'suk a, nakpi takin van gingin zin ling a, khua phia-in kek a kia hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** And the seven angels which had the seven trumpets prepared themselves to sound.

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin peengkul sagih a nei vantungmi sagihte in peengkul mut dingin a kisa khol hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in peengkul sagih a tawi vantung mi sagihte a peengkul-te a tum dingin hong kithawi-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** The first angel sounded, and there followed hail and fire mingled with blood, and they were cast upon the earth: and the third part of trees was burnt up, and all green grass was burnt up.

**TDB77:** Khatna vantungmi in peengkul a mut leh, sisan tawh kihel gial leh mei leitungah kia-in, lei seh thum suah seh khat, sing seh thum suah seh khat leh lopa hing khempeuh a kangtum hi.

**ZVS:** A masa vantung mi in a peengkul hong tum a, gialpi leh mei, sisan tawh kihelin leitungah a kiasuk hi. Leitung sehthumsuah sehkhat, leh lopa hing sehthumsuah sehkhat a kangtum hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** And the second angel sounded, and as it were a great mountain burning with fire was cast into the sea: and the third part of the sea became blood;

**TDB77:** Nihna vantungmi in peengkul a tum ciangin, mei in a kat mualpi bang na khat in tuipi sungah kia a, tuipi seh thum suah seh khat sisan suakin,

**ZVS:** A nihna vantung mi in a peengkul hong tum a, mual lianpi tawh a kibang, mei in kangin a kuang zuazua khat, tuipi sungah kilawnsuk hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** And the third part of the creatures which were in the sea, and had life, died; and the third part of the ships were destroyed.

**TDB77:** tuipi sungah a om nuntakna a nei ganhing seh thum suah seh khat a si-te hi. Teembaw seh thum suah seh khat a kisia hi.

**ZVS:** Tuipi sehthumsuah sehkhat sisan suakin, tuipi sunga om ganhingte sehthumsuah sehkhat si a, teembaw sehthumsuah sehkhat a kisia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** And the third angel sounded, and there fell a great star from heaven, burning as it were a lamp, and it fell upon the third part of the rivers, and upon the fountains of waters;

**TDB77:** Thumna vantungmi in peengkul a tum ciangin meilah kuang bangin kuangin a om aksi lianpi khat in van panin gun seh thum suah seh khat leh ciktui seh thum suah seh khat tungah a kia hi.

**ZVS:** A thumna vantung mi in a peengkul hong tum a, vantung panin aksi lian khat meikuang bangin hong kiasukin gunte leh tuinakte sehthumsuah sehkhat tungah a tu hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** And the name of the star is called Wormwood: and the third part of the waters became wormwood; and many men died of the waters, because they were made bitter.

**TDB77:** Tua aksi a min in Gamsai kici hi. Tua lai takin tui seh thum suah seh khat in gamsai-tui suak a, tua tui kha ahih ciangin mi tampi a si-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua aksi a min Gamsai hi a, tui sehthumsuah sehkhat gamsai tui suak a, tua tui khasak ahih manin a dawn mi tampi tak a si-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** And the fourth angel sounded, and the third part of the sun was smitten, and the third part of the moon, and the third part of the stars; so as the third part of them was darkened, and the day shone not for a third part of it, and the night likewise.

**TDB77:** Lina vantungmi in peengkul a tum ciangah ni seh thum suah seh khat, kha seh thum suah seh khat, aksite seh thum suah seh khatte su kha a, seh thum suah seh khat a mial hi. Sun seh thum suah seh khat a tang kei hi, zan seh thum suah seh khat in khuavakna nei loin a piang hi.

**ZVS:** A lina vantung mi in a peengkul hong tum a, ni sehthumsuah sehkhat kisatkhia-in, kha zong sehthumsuah sehkhat mah kisatkhia a, aksi zong sehthumsuah sehkhat mah kisatkhia ahih manin a taan'na-te sehthumsuah sehkhat ciat a bei hi. Sunin hun sehthumsuah sehkhat tang khua vaklo a, zanin zong hun sehthumsuah sehkhat tang mah kha leh aksi taanglo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v13
**EN:** And I beheld, and heard an angel flying through the midst of heaven, saying with a loud voice, Woe, woe, woe, to the inhabiters of the earth by reason of the other voices of the trumpet of the three angels, which are yet to sound!

**TDB77:** Tua lai takin ka et ciangin muvanlai khat in van kim lai-ah leengin, “A om lai vantungmi thumte tum ding peengkulte’ awte hangin leitungah a om mite tungah gimna, gimna, gimna a om ding hi!” ci-in nakpi-a kiko ka za hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin ka khuadak leh vankim lai-ah muvanlai khat a leen'kawmin nakpi-in ham a, “Vantung mi adang thumte in a hong tum lai ding peengkulte hangin leitungah a teng mi tungah gimna, gimna, gimna lianpi hong tung ding hi,” a cihna ka za hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
