## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Be merciful unto me, O God: for man would swallow me up; he fighting daily oppresseth me.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, mite in kei hong tuancil ahih manin kei hong hehpih in; sunthapai in galte in kei hong sucip hi;

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, mite in kei hong tuancil ahih manin kei hong hehpih in. Sunthapai-in galte in kei hong sucip hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v2
**EN:** Mine enemies would daily swallow me up: for they be many that fight against me, O thou most High.

**TDB77:** sunthapai-in ka galte in kei hong tuancil a, kiphatsakna tawh mi tampi in kei hong do hi.

**ZVS:** Sunthapai-in ka galte in kei hong tuancil-te a, kiphatsakna tawh mi tampi in kei hong do hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.

**TDB77:** Kei ka lau ciangin nangmah ka hong muang hi.

### v4
**EN:** In God I will praise his word, in God I have put my trust; I will not fear what flesh can do unto me.

**TDB77:** A thu ka phat Pasian’ sungah, lunghihmawhna khat zong nei loin Pasian ka muang hi. Mihing ahi lelte in kei bang hong cih zo ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** A thu ka phat Pasian' sungah, lunghihmawhna khat zong nei hetloin Pasian ka muang hi. Mihing ahi lelte in kei, bang hong cihzo ding ahi hiam?

### v5
**EN:** Every day they wrest my words: all their thoughts are against me for evil.

**TDB77:** Amaute in ka vai siatsak ding sunthapai-in hanciam-te a, amau’ ngaihsutnate khempeuh-te keima siat nading ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in ka vai siatsak ding sunthapai-in hanciam-te a, amau' ngaihsutnate khempeuh keima siatna ding ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** They gather themselves together, they hide themselves, they mark my steps, when they wait for my soul.

**TDB77:** Amaute kikai khawmin hong buk-te hi, ka kalsuanna hong encik-te hi. Amaute in keimah hong thah ding a hanciam-te bangin,

**ZVS:** Amaute kikaikhawmin keimah hong buk-te a, ka gamtatna khempeuh hong encik-te hi. Amaute in keimah thah ding a hanciam-te bangin,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Shall they escape by iniquity? in thine anger cast down the people, O God.

**TDB77:** amau khialhna hangin amaute thuk in; Pasian aw, hehna tawh tua mite lawnkhia suk in!

**ZVS:** amau' khialhna hangin amaute thuk in. Pasian aw, hehna tawh tua mite lawnkhiasuk in.

### v8
**EN:** Thou tellest my wanderings: put thou my tears into thy bottle: are they not in thy book?

**TDB77:** Ka sin liin’nate nangmah in na ciamteh khin hi; ka khituite na thawl sungah thun in! Tuate na laibu sungah om lo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Ka sin lin'nate khempeuh nangmah in na ciamteh khin hi. Ka khituite khempeuh na thawl sungah thun in. Tuate na laibu sungah kiciamteh hilo ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** completed: already done | negation: lo

### v9
**EN:** When I cry unto thee, then shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for God is for me.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin, keimah in ka koh ni peuh ciangin ka galte nungkik ding hi. Pasian pen keima aa ahihna thu ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin, keimah in kong koh ni peuh ciangin ka galte nungkik ding hi. Pasian pen keima-a' ahihna ka thei hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will

### v10
**EN:** In God will I praise his word: in the

**TDB77:** A thu ka phat Pasian’ sungah, a thu ka phat Topa’ sungah,

**ZVS:** A thu ka phat Pasian' sungah, a thu ka phat { Topa' } sungah,

### v11
**EN:** In God have I put my trust: I will not be afraid what man can do unto me.

**TDB77:** lunghihmawhna khat zong nei loin Pasian ka muang hi. Mi in kei bang hong cih thei ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** lunghihmawhna khat zong nei hetloin Pasian ka muang hi. Mihing lellelte in kei, bang hong cihzo ding ahi-te hiam?

### v12
**EN:** Thy vows are upon me, O God: I will render praises unto thee.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nang tunga ka kiciamna ka zuih kul hi; nang’ tungah lungdamna kong thuk ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nang tungah ka kiciamna ka zuih kul hi. Nangma tungah lungdamna kong thuk hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** For thou hast delivered my soul from death: wilt not thou deliver my feet from falling, that I may walk before God in the light of the living?

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh sihna panin nong honkhia khin hi, hi taktak hi, Pasian’ mai, nuntakna khuavak sungah ka pai theih nadingin ka khe a taal nading panin nong honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh sihna panin nong honkhia khin hi. Pasian' mai, nuntakna khuavak sungah ka pai theihna dingin ka khe a taalna ding panin na hong honkhia hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | completed: already done
