## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And the third day there was a marriage in Cana of Galilee; and the mother of Jesus was there:

**TDB77:** Ni nih khit ciangin, Galile gam Kana khua-ah mo la lawmvakna om a, Zeisu’ nu zong tua lai-ah a om hi.

**ZVS:** Tua zawh ni nih khit ciangin Galilee gam Kana khua-ah mo pawi om a, Jesuh' nu zong tua pawi-ah a om hi.

### v2
