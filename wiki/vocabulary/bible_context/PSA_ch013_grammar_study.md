## Verses

### v1
**EN:** How long wilt thou forget me, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, bang tanvei hi ding ahi hiam? Kei hong mangngilh paisuak ding na hi hiam? Bang tanvei kei kiang pan na maitang seel ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, bang tanvei hi lai ding ahi hiam? Kei hong mangngilh paisuak ding na hi hiam? Bang tanvei kei kiang pan na maitang seel ding na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart daily? how long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?

**TDB77:** Bang tanvei gimna thuakin, sunthapai-in ka lungsim dah ding ahi hiam? Bang tanvei kei’ tungah ka galte gualzo ding a hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang tanvei gimna thuakin, sunthapai-in ka lungsim dah ding ahi hiam? Bang tanvei kei tungah ka galte gualzo ding ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** Consider and hear me, O

**TDB77:** Topa ka Pasian aw, ngaihsun in la hong dawng in, ihmu-in si kha ding ka hih manin ka mitte hong vak sak in;

**ZVS:** { Topa } ka Pasian aw, ngaihsun in la hong dawng in. Ihmulum khading ka hih manin ka mitte hong vaksak in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because

### v4
**EN:** Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; and those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved.

**TDB77:** ka galte in “Ama tungah gualzo khin ing” ci kha ding hi. Kei ka lin’ manin ka galte nuam kha ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka galte in, “Ama tungah gualzo khin ung,” ci khading hi. Kei ka lin' manin ka galte nuam khading hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v5
**EN:** But I have trusted in thy mercy; my heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin na itna kip ka muang a, nangma hotkhiatna sungah ka lungdam ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin na itna kip ka muang a, nong hotkhiatna hangin ka lungdam ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** I will sing unto the

**TDB77:** Kicing takin nong hehpih manin, Topa, la kong sak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nong hehpihna kicing ahih manin, { Topa, } la kong sak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix
