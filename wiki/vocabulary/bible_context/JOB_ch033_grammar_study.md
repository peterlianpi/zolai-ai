## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Wherefore, Job, I pray thee, hear my speeches, and hearken to all my words.

**TDB77:** “Job aw, tu-in ka thugen khempeuh lim takin ngai in.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, tu-in ka thugen khempeuh limtakin hong ngai in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** Behold, now I have opened my mouth, my tongue hath spoken in my mouth.

**TDB77:** Ka lungsim sunga om thute, a hong gen ding ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim sunga om thute a hong gen ding ka hi hi.

### v3
**EN:** My words shall be of the uprightness of my heart: and my lips shall utter knowledge clearly.

**TDB77:** Ka gen khempeuh lungsim takpi tawh ka gen hi a, aman thu a gen ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka gen khempeuh lungsim takpi tawh ka gen hi a, a man thu a gen ka hi hi.

### v4
**EN:** The Spirit of God hath made me, and the breath of the Almighty hath given me life.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian’ Kha in kei hong bawl a, Vanglianpa in kei tungah nuntakna hu hong pia hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian' kha in kei hong sawl a, Vanglianpa in kei tungah nuntakna hu hong pia hi.

### v5
**EN:** If thou canst answer me, set thy words in order before me, stand up.

**TDB77:** Nong dawn’ theih leh hong dawng in. Nong sel nading kiging in.

**ZVS:** “Na hong dawn' theih leh limtakin hong dawng in. Na hong seelna ding limtakin kiging in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Behold, I am according to thy wish in God's stead: I also am formed out of the clay.

**TDB77:** Job aw, nang leh kei, Pasian’ muhna-ah a kibang i hi a, ei tegel in leivui pan-a piang tuak i hi hi.

**ZVS:** Job aw, nang leh kei, Pasian' hong muhna-ah a kibang ihi a, i nih tuakin leivui pana piang ihi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Behold, my terror shall not make thee afraid, neither shall my hand be heavy upon thee.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih manin kei nong kihtak nading om lo a, nang kong zawhthawh nading om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin kei nong kihtakna ding bangmah thu omlo a, nang ka hong zawhthawh bawlgawpna ding zong bangmah thu om tuanlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Surely thou hast spoken in mine hearing, and I have heard the voice of thy words, saying,

**TDB77:** Keima zakin thu na gen khin a, na genna kamgingte ka za khin hi.

**ZVS:** “Keima zakin thu na gen khin a, na thugente ka za khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v9
**EN:** I am clean without transgression, I am innocent; neither is there iniquity in me.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in, ‘Kei ka mawh kei hi; khialhna bangmah ka gamta kei hi. Ka paubaang kei a, mawhna ka sukha kei hi.

**ZVS:** Nang in, ‘Kei ka mawh kei a, ka gamta khial kei hi. Ka paubaang kei a, mawhna ka sukha kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v10
**EN:** Behold, he findeth occasions against me, he counteth me for his enemy,

**TDB77:** Pasian in kei hong suk theih nading thu zong a, gal bangin kei hong bawl hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in kei hong sukna ding thu zongzong a, gal bangin kei hong bawl hi.

### v11
**EN:** He putteth my feet in the stocks, he marketh all my paths.

**TDB77:** Amah in ka khe kol hong bulh a, ka gamtatna khempeuh hong encik hi,’ na cihna ka za hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in ka khe kolbulh a, ka gamtatna khempeuh hong encik hi,’ na cihna ka za hi.

### v12
**EN:** Behold, in this thou art not just: I will answer thee, that God is greater than man.

**TDB77:** “Ahih hangin, Job aw, nangmah na khial hi. Pasian pen mihing sangin lianzaw hi.

**ZVS:** “Ahih hangin, Job aw, nangmah na khialzaw hi. Pasian pen mihing sangin lianzaw hi.

### v13
**EN:** Why dost thou strive against him? for he giveth not account of any of his matters.

**TDB77:** Mihingte’ phunna don ngei lo hi, ci-in bang hangin Pasian mawhsak na hi hiam?

### v14

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo

### v15

### v17

**ZVS:** Mite in khialhna a bawl nawnlohna ding, mi kiphasakte a suahkhak lohna ding, Pasian in a deihna hangin mite kiangah thu a gen hi.
