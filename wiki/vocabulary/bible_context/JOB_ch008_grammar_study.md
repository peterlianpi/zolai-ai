## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Shuah gammi Bildad in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** How long wilt thou speak these things? and how long shall the words of thy mouth be like a strong wind?

**TDB77:** “Tua bang thu bangtan vei gengen ding a, bangtan vei paupau ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Tua bang thu bang tanvei gengen ding a, bang tanvei paupau ding na hi hiam?

### v3
**EN:** Doth God pervert judgment? or doth the Almighty pervert justice?

**TDB77:** Pasian in thutang zang lo ahi hiam? Vanglianpa in thuman zang lo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian in thutang a zanglo sa na hi hiam? Vanglianpa in thuman a zanglo sa na hi hiam?

### v4
**EN:** If thy children have sinned against him, and he have cast them away for their transgression;

**TDB77:** Na tate ama tungah khial a, amau’ mawhna amaute a tuaksak hi.

**ZVS:** Na tate ama tungah khial a, amau' mawhnate amaute a tuaksak ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** If thou wouldest seek unto God betimes, and make thy supplication to the Almighty;

**TDB77:** A hun lai-in Pasian zong in la, Vanglianpa’ hehpih ding thuum lecin,

**ZVS:** A hun lai-in Pasian zong in la, Vanglianpa' hehpihna ding thuum lecin,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** If thou wert pure and upright; surely now he would awake for thee, and make the habitation of thy righteousness prosperous.

**TDB77:** nang dikin thutang kisa na hih leh pel loin amah in nang hong en ding a, na thumanna tawh kizui-in thaman hong pia na ding hi.

**ZVS:** nang dikin thutang kisa mahmah na hih leh pelloin amah in nang hong en ding a, na thumanna tawh kizui-in thaman hong pia ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Though thy beginning was small, yet thy latter end should greatly increase.

**TDB77:** Tua hi leh, na zawn’ mahmah khit hangin a tawpna-ah na hau kik ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi leh, na zawn' mahmah khit hangin a tawpna-ah na hau kik ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** For enquire, I pray thee, of the former age, and prepare thyself to the search of their fathers:

**TDB77:** “Khangluite thu kantel in la, pute pate’ tuahsa thute ngaihsun in.

**ZVS:** “Khangluite' thu kantel in la, pute pate' tuahsa thute ngaihsun pha in.

### v9
**EN:** (For we are but of yesterday, and know nothing, because our days upon earth are a shadow:)

**TDB77:** Eite in zan mah mi i hi a, bangmah i thei kei hi; leitunga i nuntaknate in niliim bang hi lel hi.

**ZVS:** Eite pen zan mah mi ihi a, bangmah i thei kei hi. Leitunga i nuntakna pen niliim bang lel ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v10
**EN:** Shall not they teach thee, and tell thee, and utter words out of their heart?

**TDB77:** Amaute in nang hong hilhin, a thu hong genin, amau’ phawksa thute hong gen ding hilo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Amaute in nang hong hilhin, a thu hong genin, amau' phawksa thute hong gen ding hilo ahi hiam?

### v11
**EN:** Can the rush grow up without mire? can the flag grow without water?

**TDB77:** “Ciknawng om lohna-ah laiphai kungte po thei ahi hiam? Tui om lohna-ah pumpeng kungte pung thei ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Ciknawng omlohna-ah laiphai kung po thei ahi hiam? Tui omlohna-ah pumpeeng pung thei ahi hiam?

### v12
**EN:** Whilst it is yet in his greenness, and not cut down, it withereth before any other herb.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin tuate a pak kimlai a phuk hun nai lopi-in lopa dang khempeuhte sangin keu masa zaw hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin tuate a pak kimlai a phuk hun nailopi-in kungno dang khempeuh sangin keu masa zaw hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13
**EN:** So are the paths of all that forget God; and the hypocrite's hope shall perish:

**TDB77:** Pasian a mangngilh mi khempeuh tua mah bang ahi hi; Pasian a don lo mite’ lametna a bei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian a mangngilhte tua bangmah ahi hi. Pasian a donlo mite' lametna a bei ta ahi hi.

### v14
**EN:** Whose hope shall be cut off, and whose trust shall be a spider's web.

**TDB77:** Ama kimuan’na maimom khau bang hi lel a, ama ki-upna maimom bu bang hi lel hi.

**ZVS:** Ama kimuan'na maimom khau bang hi lel a, ama ki-upna maimom bu bang hi lel hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** He shall lean upon his house, but it shall not stand: he shall hold it fast, but it shall not endure.

**TDB77:** A inn tungah kingam a, a inn hoi hi, a inn lencip a, ahi zongin kip zo lo hi.

**ZVS:** A inn tungah kingam a, a inn hoi hi. A inn lencip a, ahi zongin kip zolo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** He is green before the sun, and his branch shooteth forth in his garden.

**TDB77:** Amah in nitaang sungah a khang kungno, a huan sungah a dawn hilhhelh kungno ahi hi.

**ZVS:** “Amah pen nitaang sungah hoihtakin a khang kungno, a huan sungah a dawn hilhhelh kungno ahi hi.

### v17
**EN:** His roots are wrapped about the heap, and seeth the place of stones.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin suangtum lakah a zung a kivial, suangtawphah a phukha zung suak hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin suangtum lakah a zung a kivialgawp, suangtawphah a phukha zung a suak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** If he destroy him from his place, then it shall deny him, saying, I have not seen thee.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin mi khatpeuh in a phung pan zung bawh a, ‘Nang hong thei ngei zenzen kei ing,’ ci-in lawnkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin mi khatpeuh in a phung pan zungbawh a, ‘Cik mahin nang hong thei ngei peuhmah kei ing,’ ci-in a lawnkhia hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v19
**EN:** Behold, this is the joy of his way, and out of the earth shall others grow.

**TDB77:** Hih bang danin ama nuntakna bei a, lei sung panin kung dangte hong po khia hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bang danin ama nuntakna bei a, lei sung panin kung dangte hong pokhia hi.

### v20
**EN:** Behold, God will not cast away a perfect man, neither will he help the evil doers:

**TDB77:** “Thei tel in Pasian in mawhbaang lo mi nil khia lo ding a, gamtatsia mite huh lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** “Theitel in; Pasian in cikmah hunin mawhbaanglo mite nilkhialo ding a, gamtatsia mite huh ngeilo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v21
**EN:** Till he fill thy mouth with laughing, and thy lips with rejoicing.

**TDB77:** Na muk-ah nuihna hong omsak ding a, na kam-ah lungdam-a kikona hong omsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na mukah lungdam nuihna hong omsak ding a, na kam sungah lungdam-a kikona hong omsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v22
**EN:** They that hate thee shall be clothed with shame; and the dwelling place of the wicked shall come to nought.

**TDB77:** Nang hong mudahte in maizumna ngah ding uh a, migilote’ puanbuk maimang ding hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Nang hong mudahte in maizumna ngah ding uh a, migilote' puanbuk maimang ding hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | plural: uh marker
