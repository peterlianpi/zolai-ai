## Verses

### v1
**EN:** From whence come wars and fightings among you? come they not hence, even of your lusts that war in your members?

**TDB77:** Note lakah galdona leh kitotna koi pan hong piang ahi hiam? Note’ pumpi sung uhah a om nate lakah gal a bawl thei na deihgawhnate uh pan piang hilo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Note lakah a om kitotna leh kilaina koi panin hong piang ahi hiam? Deihluatna lungsim na neih manun note' pumsung a kitoktok niloh ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Ye lust, and have not: ye kill, and desire to have, and cannot obtain: ye fight and war, yet ye have not, because ye ask not.

**TDB77:** Note in na deihgawh uh hangin na ngah kei uh hi, na that uh a, na deih uh hangin na ngah kei uh hi, na kitawng uh a, na kido uh hi. Thu ngen lo na hih ciangun na ngah kei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Na na deih uh a, ahi zongin neilo na hih manun mi na thatnuam uh hi. Nate deihna lungsim na nei uh a, ahi zongin ngah zolo na hih manun kitawngin na kilailawh uh hi. Pasian kiangah na deih uh ngenlo na hih manun a ngahlo na hi uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Ye ask, and receive not, because ye ask amiss, that ye may consume it upon your lusts.

**TDB77:** Thu na nget uh hangin na deihgawh nopsakna hoih lote na ngah nadingun ngen khial na hih ciangun na ngah kei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian kiangah na nget sun uh ciangin zong note' nopsakna ding bek ngenin, na ngaihsutna uh manlo ahih manin a ngahlo na hi uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Ye adulterers and adulteresses, know ye not that the friendship of the world is enmity with God? whosoever therefore will be a friend of the world is the enemy of God.

**TDB77:** Apangtatte aw, leitung tawh kipawlna in Pasian tawh kilem lohna ahih lam thei lo na hi uh hiam? Tua ahih leh, leitung tawh a kipawl mi peuhmah in Pasian’ gal a suak hi.

**ZVS:** A citaklo mite aw, leitung tawh kipawlna pen Pasian tawh kigalneihna ahih lamtak theilo na hi uh hiam? Leitung tawh a kipawl mite pen Pasian tawh a kigalneih dingin a kibawlbawl ahi uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Do ye think that the scripture saith in vain, The spirit that dwelleth in us lusteth to envy?

**TDB77:** Lai Siangtho’ thu in: “Eite sunga teeng Kha Siangtho in eite hong aam mahmah hi,” a cihna in a mawkna ahi ci-in na um uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Lai Siangtho sungah, “Eite sungah a hong guat kha pen Pasian in ngai mahmah hi,” ci-in a kigelhna a mawkna-in a kigelh sa na hi uh hiam?

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** But he giveth more grace. Wherefore he saith, God resisteth the proud, but giveth grace unto the humble.

**TDB77:** Topa in thupha hehpihna a tamzaw hong pia hi. Tua ahih ciangin Lai Siangtho sungah a gen-inah, “A kisatheite Pasian in kham a, ahih hangin lung kiniamkhiat mite thupha hehpihna a pia hi,” ci-in a gen hi.

**ZVS:** Lai Siangtho in, “Pasian in a kihisakte nial a, a kiniamkhiatte a hehpih hi,” a cih mah bangin Pasian' hong hehpihna lianzaw hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin Pasian’ ukna na mang un. Dawimangpa na kham un; na kham uh leh, note kiang pan a taikhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Pasian' thu na mang un. Dawimangpa na nial un. Nial le uh cin no kiang panin taikhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you. Cleanse your hands, ye sinners; and purify your hearts, ye double minded.

**TDB77:** Pasian kiangah nai un. Tua bang na hih uh leh, nomau kiangah a hong nai ding hi. Mawhneite aw, na khut uh siangthosak un. Lungka mite aw, na lungsim uh na siangthosak un.

**ZVS:** Pasian kiang nai un. Amah in zong hong nai ding hi. Mawhneite in na khut uh silsiang un la, a kineihkhemte in na lungsim uh siangsak un.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Be afflicted, and mourn, and weep: let your laughter be turned to mourning, and your joy to heaviness.

**TDB77:** Lunggimna thuakin, thuumin, kapin, om un. Note’ nuihna pen dahna suahsak un. Note’ lungdamna uh zong dahna suaksak un.

**ZVS:** Dahin, thumin, kap un. Na nuihnate uh kahna suaksak zaw un la, na lungdamnate uh maigumna suaksak zaw un.

### v10
**EN:** Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and he shall lift you up.

**TDB77:** Topa mai-ah na kiniamkhiat un. Tua bang na hih uh leh, amah in note hong pahtawi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Topa mai-ah kiniamkhiat un. Tua hi leh amah in hong lian bawl ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Speak not evil one of another, brethren. He that speaketh evil of his brother, and judgeth his brother, speaketh evil of the law, and judgeth the law: but if thou judge the law, thou art not a doer of the law, but a judge.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, khat leh khat na kikosia kei un. A u a nau a kosia mi ahi zongin, a u a nau thukhen mi ahi zongin Pasian’ Thukham a kosia hi; Thukham tungah thu a khen hi. Pasian’ Thukham tungah thu na khen uh leh, Pasian’ thu zui na hi kei uh hi; Thu a khen na hi uh hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, khatlekhat kigensia kei un. A mipihte gensia-in thu a khente in Thukham gensia-in thu a khen ahi hi. Thukham na gensiat uh leh Thukham a zui hiloin thu a khen na hi uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** There is one lawgiver, who is able to save and to destroy: who art thou that judgest another?

**TDB77:** Thukham a pia mi leh thukhen mipa khat bek a om hi. Tua pa in gup ding ahi zongin, siatsak ding ahi zongin a hih thei hi. Na lawmte tungah thu a khen nang in kua mi na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Thukham a piapa leh thu a khenpa pen Pasian bek hi a, amah bek in hong honkhiazo a, hong susiazo hi. Tua ahih manin bang hangin na mipihte uh gensia-in thu a khenthei dingin kingaihsun zenzen na hi uh hiam?

### v13
**EN:** Go to now, ye that say, To day or to morrow we will go into such a city, and continue there a year, and buy and sell, and get gain:

**TDB77:** “Tu-ni ahi zongin, zingciang ahi zongin hih bang khuapi sungah pai-in kum khat tawntung omin sumbawlin, a meet ngah nading ka bawl ding uh hi,” a ci mite aw, ngai un.

**ZVS:** Tuni ahih kei leh zing ciangin tua khua tua khua-ah va pai-in, kum khat bang va omin, sumbawlin a meet tampi va ngah ni, a ci mite aw, ka thugen hong ngai dih un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v14

**ZVS:** Zing ciangin na nuntakna uh bangci ding cih nangawn na thei kei uh hi. Meiilum tawlkhat sung kaii-in a mangthang pak tawh a kibang lel na hi uh hi.
