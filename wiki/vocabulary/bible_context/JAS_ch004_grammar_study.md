## Verses

### v1
**EN:** From whence come wars and fightings among you? come they not hence, even of your lusts that war in your members?

**TDB77:** Note lakah galdona leh kitotna koi pan hong piang ahi hiam? Note’ pumpi sung uhah a om nate lakah gal a bawl thei na deihgawhnate-te pan piang hilo ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Note lakah a om kitotna leh kilaina koi panin hong piang ahi hiam? Deihluatna lungsim na neih manun note' pumsung a kitoktok niloh ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Ye lust, and have not: ye kill, and desire to have, and cannot obtain: ye fight and war, yet ye have not, because ye ask not.

**TDB77:** Note in na deihgawh-te hangin na ngah kei-te hi, na that-te a, na deih-te hangin na ngah kei-te hi, na kitawng-te a, na kido-te hi. Thu ngen lo na hih ciangun na ngah kei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Na na deih-te a, ahi zongin neilo na hih manun mi na thatnuam-te hi. Nate deihna lungsim na nei-te a, ahi zongin ngah zolo na hih manun kitawngin na kilailawh-te hi. Pasian kiangah na deih-te ngenlo na hih manun a ngahlo na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Ye ask, and receive not, because ye ask amiss, that ye may consume it upon your lusts.

**TDB77:** Thu na nget-te hangin na deihgawh nopsakna hoih lote na ngah nadingun ngen khial na hih ciangun na ngah kei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian kiangah na nget sun-te ciangin zong note' nopsakna ding bek ngenin, na ngaihsutna-te manlo ahih manin a ngahlo na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Ye adulterers and adulteresses, know ye not that the friendship of the world is enmity with God? whosoever therefore will be a friend of the world is the enemy of God.

**TDB77:** Apangtatte aw, leitung tawh kipawlna in Pasian tawh kilem lohna ahih lam thei lo na hi-te hiam? Tua ahih leh, leitung tawh a kipawl mi peuhmah in Pasian’ gal a suak hi.

**ZVS:** A citaklo mite aw, leitung tawh kipawlna pen Pasian tawh kigalneihna ahih lamtak theilo na hi-te hiam? Leitung tawh a kipawl mite pen Pasian tawh a kigalneih dingin a kibawlbawl ahi-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Do ye think that the scripture saith in vain, The spirit that dwelleth in us lusteth to envy?

**TDB77:** Lai Siangtho’ thu in: “Eite sunga teeng Kha Siangtho in eite hong aam mahmah hi,” a cihna in a mawkna ahi ci-in na um-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Lai Siangtho sungah, “Eite sungah a hong guat kha pen Pasian in ngai mahmah hi,” ci-in a kigelhna a mawkna-in a kigelh sa na hi-te hiam?

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v6
**EN:** But he giveth more grace. Wherefore he saith, God resisteth the proud, but giveth grace unto the humble.

**TDB77:** Topa in thupha hehpihna a tamzaw hong pia hi. Tua ahih ciangin Lai Siangtho sungah a gen-inah, “A kisatheite Pasian in kham a, ahih hangin lung kiniamkhiat mite thupha hehpihna a pia hi,” ci-in a gen hi.

**ZVS:** Lai Siangtho in, “Pasian in a kihisakte nial a, a kiniamkhiatte a hehpih hi,” a cih mah bangin Pasian' hong hehpihna lianzaw hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin Pasian’ ukna na mang un. Dawimangpa na kham un; na kham-te leh, note kiang pan a taikhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin Pasian' thu na mang un. Dawimangpa na nial un. Nial le-te cin no kiang panin taikhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you. Cleanse your hands, ye sinners; and purify your hearts, ye double minded.

**TDB77:** Pasian kiangah nai un. Tua bang na hih-te leh, nomau kiangah a hong nai ding hi. Mawhneite aw, na khut-te siangthosak un. Lungka mite aw, na lungsim-te na siangthosak un.

**ZVS:** Pasian kiang nai un. Amah in zong hong nai ding hi. Mawhneite in na khut-te silsiang un la, a kineihkhemte in na lungsim-te siangsak un.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Be afflicted, and mourn, and weep: let your laughter be turned to mourning, and your joy to heaviness.

**TDB77:** Lunggimna thuakin, thuumin, kapin, om un. Note’ nuihna pen dahna suahsak un. Note’ lungdamna-te zong dahna suaksak un.

**ZVS:** Dahin, thumin, kap un. Na nuihnate-te kahna suaksak zaw un la, na lungdamnate-te maigumna suaksak zaw un.

### v10
**EN:** Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and he shall lift you up.

**TDB77:** Topa mai-ah na kiniamkhiat un. Tua bang na hih-te leh, amah in note hong pahtawi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Topa mai-ah kiniamkhiat un. Tua hi leh amah in hong lian bawl ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Speak not evil one of another, brethren. He that speaketh evil of his brother, and judgeth his brother, speaketh evil of the law, and judgeth the law: but if thou judge the law, thou art not a doer of the law, but a judge.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, khat leh khat na kikosia kei un. A u a nau a kosia mi ahi zongin, a u a nau thukhen mi ahi zongin Pasian’ Thukham a kosia hi; Thukham tungah thu a khen hi. Pasian’ Thukham tungah thu na khen-te leh, Pasian’ thu zui na hi kei-te hi; Thu a khen na hi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, khatlekhat kigensia kei un. A mipihte gensia-in thu a khente in Thukham gensia-in thu a khen ahi hi. Thukham na gensiat-te leh Thukham a zui hiloin thu a khen na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** There is one lawgiver, who is able to save and to destroy: who art thou that judgest another?

**TDB77:** Thukham a pia mi leh thukhen mipa khat bek a om hi. Tua pa in gup ding ahi zongin, siatsak ding ahi zongin a hih thei hi. Na lawmte tungah thu a khen nang in kua mi na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Thukham a piapa leh thu a khenpa pen Pasian bek hi a, amah bek in hong honkhiazo a, hong susiazo hi. Tua ahih manin bang hangin na mipihte-te gensia-in thu a khenthei dingin kingaihsun zenzen na hi-te hiam?

### v13
**EN:** Go to now, ye that say, To day or to morrow we will go into such a city, and continue there a year, and buy and sell, and get gain:

**TDB77:** “Tu-ni ahi zongin, zingciang ahi zongin hih bang khuapi sungah pai-in kum khat tawntung omin sumbawlin, a meet ngah nading ka bawl ding-te hi,” a ci mite aw, ngai un.

**ZVS:** Tuni ahih kei leh zing ciangin tua khua tua khua-ah va pai-in, kum khat bang va omin, sumbawlin a meet tampi va ngah ni, a ci mite aw, ka thugen hong ngai dih un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** Whereas ye know not what shall be on the morrow. For what is your life? It is even a vapour, that appeareth for a little time, and then vanisheth away.

**TDB77:** Zingciangin bang thu a piang ding na thei kei-te hi. Note’ nuntakna un bang tawh a kibang hiam? Note in tawlkhat a kilat khit ciangin a mangthang thei tuikhu na hi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Zing ciangin na nuntakna-te bangci ding cih nangawn na thei kei-te hi. Meiilum tawlkhat sung kaii-in a mangthang pak tawh a kibang lel na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** For that ye ought to say, If the Lord will, we shall live, and do this, or that.

**TDB77:** “Pasian in hong awi leh, eite in nungta-in hih bang ahi zongin, tua bang ahi zongin i hih ding hi,” ci-in na gen dingun a kilawm hi.

**ZVS:** Note in, “Topa in hong thukimpih nak leh nungta-in tua bang tua bang i sem ding hi,” na cihding-te hi bek hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** But now ye rejoice in your boastings: all such rejoicing is evil.

**TDB77:** Tu-in ahih leh, note na kiphatsakna uhah na kisathei-te hi. Tua bang kisaktheihna ahoih kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin note kihisakin na kisial-te hi. Tua bangte hoih hetlo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v17
**EN:** Therefore to him that knoweth to do good, and doeth it not, to him it is sin.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin gamtatna hoih thei napi-in a gamta lo mi tungah mawhna a om hi.

**ZVS:** A hoih a manin gamtat ding thei napi-in a gamta lote a khial ahi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then)
