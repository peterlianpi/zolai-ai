## Verses

### v1
**EN:** He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.

**TDB77:** Sang Penpa’ liahna sungah a om mi, Vanglianpa’ liim sungah a om mi in,

**ZVS:** Sangpenpa' liahna sung, Vanglianpa' liim sungah a om mi khempeuh in

### v2
**EN:** I will say of the

**TDB77:** Topa tungah, “Ka bukna leh ka munkip muanhuai, ka muan’ ka Pasian na hi hi,” a ci ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } tungah, “Ka bukna leh ka munkip muan'huai, ka muan' ka Pasian na hi hi,” ci ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3
**EN:** Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in thangsiahpa’ thangzak leh a lauhuai pulnatna panin nangmah hong honkhia ding hi;

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in thangsiahpa' thangzak leh a lauhuai pulnatna panin nang hong honkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v4
**EN:** He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust: his truth shall be thy shield and buckler.

**TDB77:** amah in a khate tawh nang hong tuam ding a, ama khate nuai-ah buk nading na mu ding hi; ama thumanna in nang hong dalin hong hu ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in a khate tawh nang hong tuam ding a, ama khate' nuai-ah bukna ding na mu ding hi. Ama thumanna in nang hong dalin hong hu ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** Nor for the pestilence that walketh in darkness; nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in zana lauhuaina, sun-a thaltang leengte, khuamial lakah a vak khiankhian pulnatna, sun nitaang-a suksiatnate na kihta kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** A thousand shall fall at thy side, and ten thousand at thy right hand; but it shall not come nigh thee.

**TDB77:** Na kiangah mi tulkhat, na kimkotah mi tulsawm, hong tuk kha ding uh hi; ahih hangin tuate in na kiang nai hong neh lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na kiangah mi tul khat, na kimkotah mi tul sawm, hong tukin a sih hangin bangmah na ci kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold and see the reward of the wicked.

**TDB77:** Na mit tawh na en bek ding a, migilote a kithukkikna na mu ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Because thou hast made the

**TDB77:** Nangmah in Topa pen na buk nadingin bawlin, Sang Penpa, nang a hu dingin bawl khin na hih manin,

**ZVS:** Nangmah in { Topa } pen na bukna dingin bawlin, Sangpenpa, nang a hong hu dingin bawl khin na hih manin

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | causal: because

### v10
**EN:** There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling.

**TDB77:** na tungah siatna hong tung lo ding a, na innah gimna hong tung lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** nangma tungah siatna hong tunglo ding a, na inn sungah gimna hong tunglo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** For he shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh na lampi khempeuhah nangmah hong cing dingin amah in ama vantungmi nang hong kemsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah a hong cing dingin amah in ama vantung mi nang hong kemsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v12
**EN:** They shall bear thee up in their hands, lest thou dash thy foot against a stone.

**TDB77:** Na khete in suang a suihkhak loh nadingin, amaute in a khut uh tawh nang hong dom ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Na khete leh suang a kitahkhak lohna dingin amaute in a khut uh tawh nang hong dom ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder: the young lion and the dragon shalt thou trample under feet.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in humpinelkai leh gul gu nei tung na tawn ding a, na khe nuai-ah humpinelkai khang lai leh gulpi na tuancil ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in humpinelkai leh gul gunei tung na tawn ding a, na khenuai-ah humpinelkai khanglai leh gulpi na tuancil ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v14
**EN:** Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.

**TDB77:** Amah in itna tawh kei hong bel ahih manin amah ka honkhia ding a, amah in ka min thei ahih manin mun sangna-ah amah ka koih ding hi.

**ZVS:** Itna tawh hong bel ahih manin amah ka honkhia ding a, ka min hong thei ahih manin mun sangna-ah amah ka koih ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v15
**EN:** He shall call upon me, and I will answer him: I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him, and honour him.

**TDB77:** Amah in kei hong sap ciangin keimah in amah ka dawng ding hi; haksatna sungah amah ka ompih ding a, amah honkhia-in ka pahtawi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in hong sap ciangin amah ka dawng ding hi. Haksatna sungah amah ka ompih ding a, amah honkhia-in ka pahtawi ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v16
**EN:** With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation.

**TDB77:** Nuntak sau tawh amah ka lungkim sak ding a, amah ka honkhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nuntak sau tawh amah ka lungkimsak ding a, amah ka honkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
