## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And in that day seven women shall take hold of one man, saying, We will eat our own bread, and wear our own apparel: only let us be called by thy name, to take away our reproach.

**TDB77:** Tua ni ciangin, nupi sagih in papi khat nei khawm ding-te a, “Kote in komau’ an ciat mah ne-in, komau’ puanciat mah ka silh ding-te hi, kote pen, nangma zi cih min bek hong pua sak in; ka minsiatna teng-te hong hum in,” ci ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ni ciangin nupi sagih in papi khat neikhawm ding-te a, “Kote in komau' an ciat ne-in, komau' puan ciat mah ka silh ding-te hi. Kote pen, nangma zi cih min bek hong puasak in; ka minsiatna teng-te hong hum in,” ci ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v2
**EN:** In that day shall the branch of the

**TDB77:** Tua ni ciangin Topa’ bulsel pen mel hoihin minthang ding a, leitung pan-a a piang gahte pen, a suakta Israel mite’ kiphatsakna leh minthan’na hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ni ciangin { Topa}' bulsel pen melhoihin minthang ding a, leitung panin a piang gahte pen, a suakta lai Israel mite' kiphatsakna leh minthan'na hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** When the Lord shall have washed away the filth of the daughters of Zion, and shall have purged the blood of Jerusalem from the midst thereof by the spirit of judgment, and by the spirit of burning.

**TDB77:** Topa in thukhenna leh meihalna tawh Zion mite' mawhna teng siangsakin, Jerusalem khuasung panin mi thahna teng a beisak ciangin Zion-ah a omlai mi, Jerusalem-ah khang tawntungin a om dingin a kiciamtehsa mite khempeuh, a siangtho kici ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** And the

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Zion Mual tung khempeuh leh ama kikhopnate tungah, sun-in meii sungah, zan-in meikuang khu tawh Topa in hong bawl ding a, Topa’ minthan’na tung khempeuh ah a kizal khat om ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Zion Mual tung, tua lai-a kikhawm mi khempeuh tungah, sunin meii sungah, zanin meikuang khu tawh { Topa } hong kilang ding a, { Topa}' minthan'na vang in khuapi hu ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** And there shall be a tabernacle for a shadow in the daytime from the heat, and for a place of refuge, and for a covert from storm and from rain.

**TDB77:** Tua pen, sun ciangin nisa liah ding leh huihpi guahpi panin bukna leh belhna hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua minthan'na vang pen sun ciangin nisa a liah ding leh huihpi guahpi panin bukna leh belhna zong hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
