# Psalms Chapter 72 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 20 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 3 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 3 |
| optative: let there be / let it be | 3 |
| purpose: in order to | 2 |
| negation: lo | 1 |
| causal: because | 1 |

---

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Give the king thy judgments, O God, and thy righteousness unto the king's son.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangma thutang kumpipa hilh in la, nangma thuman kumpi tapa hilh in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nangma thutang, kumpipa hilh in la, nangma thuman, kumpi tapa hilh in.

### v2
**EN:** He shall judge thy people with righteousness, and thy poor with judgment.

**TDB77:** Amah in thumanna tawh na mite tungah thu khen hen la, na mizawngte’ tungah thutan’na tawh thu khen ta hen.

**ZVS:** Amah in thumanna tawh na mite tungah thu khen hen la, na mizawngte tungah thu a tangin khen ta hen.

### v3
**EN:** The mountains shall bring peace to the people, and the little hills, by righteousness.

**TDB77:** Mualliante leh mualneute in thumanna tawh na mite’ adingin nopna puak ta hen!

**ZVS:** Mual liante leh mual neute in thumanna tawh na mite-a' dingin nopna tampi piangsak ta hen.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v4
**EN:** He shall judge the poor of the people, he shall save the children of the needy, and shall break in pieces the oppressor.

**TDB77:** Amah in mizawngte’ ading thu khen hen la, mi cimawhte suahtakna pia-in, mi nengniamte sucip ta hen!

**ZVS:** Amah in mizawngte-a' ding thu a manin khen hen la, mi cimawhte tungah suahtakna pia-in, midang a nengniamte sucipgawp ta hen.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** They shall fear thee as long as the sun and moon endure, throughout all generations.

**TDB77:** Ni leh kha a om lai teng, khang tawntungin amah hing ta hen!

**ZVS:** Ni leh kha a omlaiteng, khang tawntungin amah hing ta hen.

### v6
**EN:** He shall come down like rain upon the mown grass: as showers that water the earth.

**TDB77:** Amah, pawn lak lopa tunga guah kia leh lei a kawtsak guahzu tawh kibang ta hen!

**ZVS:** Amah pen pawn lak lopa tungah a kia guah, lei a kawtsak guahzu tawh kibang ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** In his days shall the righteous flourish; and abundance of peace so long as the moon endureth.

**TDB77:** Ama hun sungin thumanna pung hen la, khapi a bei mateng nopna om ta hen!

**ZVS:** Ama hun sungin thuman pung hen la, khapi a bei mateng nopna om ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be

### v8
**EN:** He shall have dominion also from sea to sea, and from the river unto the ends of the earth.

**TDB77:** Ama ukna in tuipi khat pan tuipi khat, Gunpi panin leimong dongin om ta hen!

**ZVS:** Ama ukna, tuipi khat pan tuipi khat, Gunpi panin leimong dongin om tawntung ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be

### v9
**EN:** They that dwell in the wilderness shall bow before him; and his enemies shall lick the dust.

**TDB77:** Ama galte a mai-ah kunsuk uh hen la, amah langpangte in leivui liak ta uh hen!

**ZVS:** Ama galte khempeuh ama mai-ah kun uh hen la, amah a langpangte leilak kiden'na uh hen.

### v10
**EN:** The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall bring presents: the kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.

**TDB77:** Tarshis kumpite leh tuikulh neu-a kumpite in ama tungah siah pia uh hen la, Sheba leh Seba kumpite in letsong puak ta uh hen!

**ZVS:** Tarshish kumpite leh tuikulh neu-a kumpite in ama tungah siah pia uh hen la, Sheba leh Seba kumpite in letsong puak ta uh hen.

### v11
**EN:** Yea, all kings shall fall down before him: all nations shall serve him.

**TDB77:** Kumpi khempeuh ama mai-ah boksuk uh hen la, minam khempeuh in ama na sem ta uh hen!

**ZVS:** Kumpi khempeuh ama mai-ah bok uh hen la, minam khempeuh in ama na sem ta uh hen.

### v12
**EN:** For he shall deliver the needy when he crieth; the poor also, and him that hath no helper.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi cimawh mi in a koh ciangin honkhia a, a zawng mi leh huh ding nei lo mi honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi cimawhte in a koh ciangin amah in honkhia a, mizawng, a huh ding a omlote amah in a honkhia hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v13
**EN:** He shall spare the poor and needy, and shall save the souls of the needy.

**TDB77:** Amah in a thakiam mi leh a cimawh mite hehpih a, mi cimawhte’ nuntakna hon hi.

**ZVS:** A thakiam, a cimawh mite amah in hehpih a, mi cimawhte' nuntakna amah in a honkhia hi.

### v14
**EN:** He shall redeem their soul from deceit and violence: and precious shall their blood be in his sight.

**TDB77:** Amah in amaute pammaih sa ahih manin nen’niamna leh ngong hihna panin amaute tankhia hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in amaute pammaihsa ahih manin netniamna leh ngong hihna panin amaute a honkhia hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v15
**EN:** And he shall live, and to him shall be given of the gold of Sheba: prayer also shall be made for him continually; and daily shall he be praised.

**TDB77:** Amah khan’sau hen la, Sheba gam-a khamte ama tungah kipia ta hen! Ama adingin thu kingetsak tawntung hen la, sun thapai-in ama adingin thupha kingetsak ta hen!

**ZVS:** Amah a khan' sau hen la, Sheba gama khamte ama tungah kipia ta hen. Ama-a' dingin thu kingetsak tawntung hen la, sunthapai-in ama-a' dingin thupha kingetsak ta hen.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** There shall be an handful of corn in the earth upon the top of the mountains; the fruit thereof shall shake like Lebanon: and they of the city shall flourish like grass of the earth.

**TDB77:** Gam sungah an tampi piang hen la, mualliante dawn-ah dim mema-in Lebanon gam sung bang hen la, gam lak-a lopate bangin khuapite sung panin mite pung ta uh hen!

**ZVS:** Gamsung khempeuhah an tampi tak piang hen la, mual liante dawnah dim mema-in Lebanon gam hoih sung bang ta hen la, gamlaka lopate bangin khuapite sunga om mite pung ta uh hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v17
**EN:** His name shall endure for ever: his name shall be continued as long as the sun: and men shall be blessed in him: all nations shall call him blessed.

**TDB77:** Ama min kip tawntung hen la, ni a om lai teng a minthan’na kip suak hen! Ama hangin mite in thupha ngah hen la, minam khempeuh in amah pen thupha ngahpa, ci ta uh hen!

**ZVS:** Ama min kip tawntung hen la, a minthan'na kip paisuak hen. Ama hangin mite in thupha ngah hen la, minam khempeuh in amah pen thupha ngahpa, ci ta uh hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** Blessed be the

**TDB77:** Nalamdangte a bawl amah bek ahi, Israel Pasian, Topa in thupha ngah ta hen.

**ZVS:** Na lamdangte a bawl amah bek ahi Israel Pasian, { Topa } in thupha ngah ta hen.

### v19
**EN:** And blessed be his glorious name for ever: and let the whole earth be filled with his glory; Amen, and Amen.

**TDB77:** A minthang ama min in thupha ngah tawntung hen la, ama minthan’na in leitung khempeuh dim ta hen! Amen, Amen.

**ZVS:** A minthang ama min in thupha ngah tawntung hen la, ama minthan'na tawh leitung khempeuh kidim ta hen. Amen! Amen!

### v20
**EN:** The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended.

**TDB77:** Jessi’ tapa David’ thungetnate bei ta hi.

**ZVS:** Jessi' tapa David' thungetnate a beita ahi hi.
