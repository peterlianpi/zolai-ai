## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ka khuavak leh ka kihotkhiatna hi; kua zahtak ding ka hi hiam? Topa in ka nuntakna’ adingin mun muanhuai hi; bang laupih ding ka hi hiam?

**ZVS:** { Topa, } ka khuavak leh hong honpa ahi hi. Kua kihta ding ka hi hiam? { Topa, } kei-a' dingin munmuan'huai ahi hi. Bang laupih ding ka hi hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** When the wicked, even mine enemies and my foes, came upon me to eat up my flesh, they stumbled and fell.

**TDB77:** Ka langpangte leh ka galte ahi, siatna bawlte in hong do a, kei hong gensiat ciangun, amaute kisuikha-in puk ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Hong langpangte leh ka galte ahi, siatna bawlte in hong do a, kei nektum hong sawm ciangun, amaute kisukha-in puk ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** Though an host should encamp against me, my heart shall not fear: though war should rise against me, in this will I be confident.

**TDB77:** Mi honkhat in kei langpangin phual a sat hangin, ka lungsim lau lo ding hi; kei langpangin gal hong kuan hangin, tua nangawn-ah ka lungmuang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mi honkhat in keimah hong langpangin phual a sat uh hangin, ka lungsim laulo ding hi. Keimah hong langpangin gal hong kuan hangin tua nangawnah ka lungmuang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v4
**EN:** One thing have I desired of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ tungah na khat ka ngen khin a, tua ngen-a ka zon’ ding pen: Topa’ inn sungah ka khan’ tawntungin om nading, Topa’ hoihna et ding leh ama biakinn sungah thu dot dingte ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } tung panin na khat ka ngen khin a, tua ngenin ka zon' ding pen: { Topa' } inn sungah ka khan' tawntungin om ding, { Topa' } hoihna et ding leh ama biakinn sungah thu dot dingte ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** For in the time of trouble he shall hide me in his pavilion: in the secret of his tabernacle shall he hide me; he shall set me up upon a rock.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh haksat hun ciangin a buk sungah kei hong seel ding hi; a puanbuk khuhna nuai-ah kei hong seel ding-a, a san’na suang tungah hong koih ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh haksat hun ciangin amah in a buk sungah kei hong seel ding hi. A puanbuk khuhna nuai-ah kei hong seel ding a, a sangna suang tungah kei hong koih ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** And now shall mine head be lifted up above mine enemies round about me: therefore will I offer in his tabernacle sacrifices of joy; I will sing, yea, I will sing praises unto the

**TDB77:** Tu-in ka kimkot-a om ka galte tungah ka lu kilaamto-in, lungdam-a kikona tawh biak piaknate ama buk sungah ka pia ding a, la ka sa ding hi, Topa’ tungah la ngaih ka sa ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tu-in ka kimkotah a om ka galte tungah ka gualzo ding hi. Lungdamin kikona tawh ama buk sungah biakna ka pia ding a, la ka sa ding hi. { Topa } tungah la ngaih ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7

### v8

**TDB77:** “Ka mai hong zong in,” na ci khin zo hi. Ka lungsim in nang tungah, “Topa aw, nangma mai kong zong hi,” ci hi.

### v9
**EN:** Hide not thy face far from me; put not thy servant away in anger: thou hast been my help; leave me not, neither forsake me, O God of my salvation.

**TDB77:** Kei kiang panin na maitang seel kei in. Hehna tawh na nasempa hawlkhia kei in, kei hong huh ngeipa na hi hi. Hong paaikhia kei in la, hong nusia kei in, ka kihotkhiatna Pasian aw!

**ZVS:** Kei kiang panin na maitang seel kei in. Hehpihna tawh na nasempa hong hawlkhia kei in; kei hong neipa na hi hi. Hong paiikhia kei in la, hong nusia kei in, kei hong honkhia Pasian aw.
