### v1

**TDB77:** Topa in Moses kiangah,

**ZVS:** { Topa } in Moses kiangah,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, If a woman have conceived seed, and born a man child: then she shall be unclean seven days; according to the days of the separation for her infirmity shall she be unclean.

**TDB77:** “Israel mite tungah hih bangin gen in: Nupi khat gai-in ta pasal a neih leh ni sagih sung tua nu nin ding hi; a nin neih huna mah bangin amah a nin ding hi.

**ZVS:** “Israel mite tungah hih bangin gen in: Nupi khat gai-in ta pasal a neih leh ni sagih sung tuanu a nin hi ding a, a nin neih huna mah bangin amah a nin hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v3
**EN:** And in the eighth day the flesh of his foreskin shall be circumcised.

**TDB77:** A ni giat ni-in a naungek vun dawn ki-at ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v4
**EN:** And she shall then continue in the blood of her purifying three and thirty days; she shall touch no hallowed thing, nor come into the sanctuary, until the days of her purifying be fulfilled.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin nupi in ama sisan sian’sak ding a kipsakna hangin ni sawmthum leh nithum sung om lai ding hi; amah in a kisian’suahna ni a cin’ mateng a siangtho na khatpeuh peuh lawng loin, biakinn sungah zong tum lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin nupi in ama si luan'na hangin a sian'na dingin ni sawmthum leh ni thum sung omlai ding hi. Amah in kisian'suahna ni a cin' mateng a siangtho na khat peuhpeuh lawngloin, biakbuk sungah zong tumlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** But if she bear a maid child, then she shall be unclean two weeks, as in her separation: and she shall continue in the blood of her purifying threescore and six days.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin amah in ta numei a neih leh, ama nin neih huna mah bangin nipi kal nih sung a nin hi ding hi; ama sisan sian’sak ding a kipsakna hangin ni sawmguk leh ni guk sung om lai ding hi.

**ZVS:** “Ahih hangin amah in ta numei a neih leh, ama nin neih huna mah bangin nipikal nih sung a nin hi ding hi. Ama si luan'na hangin a sian'na dingin ni sawmguk leh ni guk sung omlai ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** And when the days of her purifying are fulfilled, for a son, or for a daughter, she shall bring a lamb of the first year for a burnt offering, and a young pigeon, or a turtledove, for a sin offering, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, unto the priest:

**TDB77:** “Amah a kisian’ suahna hun a cin’ ciangin, tapa’ ading hita leh tanu’ ading hita leh, tua nu in kikhopna buk konga siampipa kiangah meihal biak nadingin kum khat a pha tuuno khat, leh khialhna hanga biak nadingin kawl vakhuno khat ahi a, vakhu khat ahi zongin pia ding a,

**ZVS:** “Amah a kisian'suahna hun a cin' ciangin tapa-a' ding hita leh tanu-a' ding hita leh, amah in kikhopna bukkonga siampipa kiangah meihal biakna dingin kum khat a pha tuuno khat, leh khialhna hanga biakna dingin kawl vakhuno khat ahi a, vakhu khat ahi zongin pia ding a,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Who shall offer it before the

**TDB77:** siampipa in Topa mai-ah pia in, ama adingin kilemna bawlsak ding hi; tua ciangin ama si luan’na panin tua nu a siang hi ding hi. Hih in ta pasal hita leh ta numei hita leh a nei nu’ adingin ngeina ahi hi.

**ZVS:** siampipa in { Topa' } mai-ah pia-in, ama-a' ding kilemna bawlsak ding hi. Tua ciangin ama si luan'na panin tuanu a siang hi ding hi. Hih pen ta pasal hita leh ta numei hita leh a neinu-a' dingin ngeina ahi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** And if she be not able to bring a lamb, then she shall bring two turtles, or two young pigeons; the one for the burnt offering, and the other for a sin offering: and the priest shall make an atonement for her, and she shall be clean.

**TDB77:** Tua nu in tuuno a piak zawh kei leh vakhu nih ahi a, kawl vakhu no nih ahi zongin paipih ding a, khat in meihal biak nading hi a, adang khat pen khialhna hanga biak nading ahi hi; siampipa in ama adingin kilemna bawlsak ding a, tua nu siang ding hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** “Tuanu in tuuno a piak zawh kei leh vakhu nih ahi a, kawl vakhuno nih ahi zongin paipih ding a, khat pen meihal biakna ding, adang khat pen khialhna hanga biakna ding ahi hi. Siampipa in ama-a' dingin kilemna bawlsak ding a, tuanu a siang hi ding hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | purpose: in order to | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix
