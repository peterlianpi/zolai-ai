## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Blessed be the

**TDB77:** Ka khutte galdo nading, ka khutmete galkap nadinga a hilh, kei hong hu, Topa in thupha ngah ta hen;

**ZVS:** Ka khutte galdona ding ka khutmete galkapna dingin a hilh, keimah a hong hu, { Topa } in thupha ngah ta hen.

### v2
**EN:** My goodness, and my fortress; my high tower, and my deliverer; my shield, and he in whom I trust; who subdueth my people under me.

**TDB77:** amah in ka suangpi leh ka munkip muan’huai, ka kulhpi leh hong honkhiapa, ka lum leh ka belhpa, ka nuai-ah mite a zopa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Amah pen ka suangpi leh ka munkip muan'huai, ka kulhpi leh hong honkhiapa, ka lum leh ka belhpa, ka nuai-ah mite a zopa ahi hi.

### v4
**EN:** Man is like to vanity: his days are as a shadow that passeth away.

**TDB77:** Mi pen husan’ khat tawh kibang a, ama nite in a tai dandan nilim tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Mi pen husan' khat tawh kibang a, ama hunte pen a tai dandan nilim tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Bow thy heavens, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, na vanpite niamsak in la hong paisuk in! Mualte lawng in la meikhu suak sak in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na vanpite niamsak in la, hong paisuk in. Mualte lawng in la, meikhu suaksak in.

### v6
**EN:** Cast forth lightning, and scatter them: shoot out thine arrows, and destroy them.

**TDB77:** Khuaphialep phiasak in la, amaute kithehthang sak in, na thaltangte lawn in la amaute susia in!

**ZVS:** Khuaphialep phiasak in la, amaute kithehthang sak in. Na thaltangte lawn in la, amaute susia in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Send thine hand from above; rid me, and deliver me out of great waters, from the hand of strange children;

**TDB77:** A sangna mun panin na khut hong zan in la, tuilian lauhuaite, leh gamdang mite’ vangliatna panin hong honkhia in.

**ZVS:** A sangna mun panin na khut hong zan in la, tui lian lauhuaite leh gamdang mite' vangliatna panin hong honkhia in.

### v8
**EN:** Whose mouth speaketh vanity, and their right hand is a right hand of falsehood.

**TDB77:** A kam uh tawh zuauthu a gente leh kiciamna a bawl khit ciang nangawnin a zuau vevete’ kiang panin kei hong phelin hong honkhia in.

**ZVS:** A kam uh tawh zuauthu a gente leh limtakin kiciamna a bawl khit ciang nangawnin zuauthu mah a gen lai vevete' kiang pan kei hong phel in la, hong honkhia ta in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** I will sing a new song unto thee, O God: upon a psaltery and an instrument of ten strings will I sing praises unto thee.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, la thak khat kong sak ding hi; harp zang sawm khausai lasakna nang kong tum ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, la thak khat kong sak ding a, harp zangsawm khausai lasakna nang kong tum ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v10
**EN:** It is he that giveth salvation unto kings: who delivereth David his servant from the hurtful sword.

**TDB77:** Kumpite tungah gualzawhna a piapa, na nasempa David a honkhiapa na hi hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpite tungah gualzawhna a piapa, na nasem David a honkhiapa na hi hi.

### v11
**EN:** Rid me, and deliver me from the hand of strange children, whose mouth speaketh vanity, and their right hand is a right hand of falsehood:

**TDB77:** A gilo galte kiang panin kei hong hon in la, a kam uh tawh zuauthu a gen, kiciamna a bawl khit ciang nangawn-in a zuau veve gamdang mite’ khut sung panin kei hong honkhia in.

**ZVS:** A gilo galte kiang panin kei hong hon in la, a kam uh tawh zuauthu a gen limtakin kiciamna a bawl khit ciang nangawnin zuau a gen lai veve gamdang mite' khutsung pan kei hong honkhia in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** That our sons may be as plants grown up in their youth; that our daughters may be as corner stones, polished after the similitude of a palace:

**TDB77:** Ka tapate uh a tangval lai-in a khangcing kungte bang ta hen la, ka tanute uh kumpi inn lam nadinga kisui innkiu khuampite bang ta hen;

**ZVS:** Ka tapate uh a tangval lai-in khang cingin a khauh mahmah singkungte bang ta hen la, ka tanute uh a nungak lai-in kumpi inn lamna dinga a kisui innkiu khuampite bang ta hen.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** That our garners may be full, affording all manner of store: that our sheep may bring forth thousands and ten thousands in our streets:

**TDB77:** ka saalte uh dimin an nam khempeuh om ta hen la, ka tuute uh a tultul a thenthenin gamlakah suak ta hen;

**ZVS:** Ka saalte uh a dimin an nam khempeuh om ta hen la, ka tuute uh a tultul a thenthen-in gamlakah suak ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** That our oxen may be strong to labour; that there be no breaking in, nor going out; that there be no complaining in our streets.

**TDB77:** ka bawngte uh pung hen la a tolh om kei ta hen; ka kongzingte uh ah cimawh-a kikona om kei ta hen!

**ZVS:** Ka kongzingte uhah cimawhin kikona om kei ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** Happy is that people, that is in such a case: yea, happy is that people, whose God is the

**TDB77:** Tua bang thupha ngah mi in mi lungdam ahi hi! Topa pen amau’ Pasian ahi mite pen mi lungdam ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang thupha a ngah mi mi lungdam ahi hi. { Topa } pen amau' Pasian ahi mite pen mi lungdam ahi hi.
