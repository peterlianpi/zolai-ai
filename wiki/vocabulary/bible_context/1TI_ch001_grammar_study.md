### v2
**EN:** Unto Timothy, my own son in the faith: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.

**TDB77:** Upna sungah ka ta taktak ahi Timoti kiangah kong zasak hi. Pa Pasian leh Khazih Zeisu i Topa’ hehpihna thupha, hehpihna leh nopna nangma tungah om ta hen.

**ZVS:** Thu upna sungah ka tapa taktak ahi Timoti tungah lai ka hong khak hi. Pa Pasian leh eite' Topa Khrih Jesuh in dikna, hehpihna leh nopsakna thupha, nangma tungah hong tungsak ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** As I besought thee to abide still at Ephesus, when I went into Macedonia, that thou mightest charge some that they teach no other doctrine,

**TDB77:** Masedonia gam lam manawhin ka pai lai-in Efesa khua-ah na om ding ka hong sawl mah bangin tu-in zong tua khua mahah na omlai in la, thu upna manlo a hilh mite, a thuhilh a khawlna dingun thu na pia in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** Neither give heed to fables and endless genealogies, which minister questions, rather than godly edifying which is in faith: so do.

**TDB77:** Phuahtawm tangthute leh min tampipi kizom deudau-a khangsimnate in upna tawh i ngahtheih Pasian' vaihawmnate tangtungsak loin, khatlekhat kitotna piangsak lel ahih manin, tua bang thute a manlahpih lohna dingun na gen in.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5

**TDB77:** Hih thupiakna’ deihna bulpi in, lungsim siangtho, lungsim kisuan’na om lohna leh thuman upna tawh a kipawl itna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bangin thupiakna pen, upna taktak leh kisuan'na om hetloin lungsim siangtho taktak tawh ngaihsutna panin a hong piangkhia ki-itna a khan'theihna ding deihna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v7
**EN:** Desiring to be teachers of the law; understanding neither what they say, nor whereof they affirm.

**TDB77:** Tuate in Thukham hilhna a sep nop uh hangin amau kampau ahi zongin, bang ci thu lamah amaute in a kiptakin ahilh uh ahi zongin a thei kei uh hi.

**ZVS:** Thei kisa ngelin a gen kankan uh thute leh amau kampauna mahmah zong bang gen ka hi hiam cih a thei hetlo hinapi-in amaute pen Pasian' thukhamte a hilh dingin utzaw lai uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v8

**TDB77:** Thukham pen a kizatna ding a kilawm ciangah zangthei lehang a hoih thu khat hi, ci-in i thei hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin i phawk dingah, mi hoihte-a' dingin thukham a kibawl hilo a, thukham zuilote, thumang lote, Pasian' deih bangin a gamta lote leh mawhneite, biakna lungsim a neilote leh kam kilawmlo a paute, nute pate a thatte, midang a thatte,

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10

**ZVS:** Hihbangin na sepzawhna dingin thatang hatna a hong pia i Topa Khrih Jesuh tungah ka lungdam hi. Ama nasem dingin kei hong muangin hong seh ahih manin ka nuam hi.

**ZVS:** A beisa hunah amah gensia-in, simmawh bawlin, ama mite a bawlsiapa ka hih ngei tei hangin ama thu ka up nailoh manin theihlohna tawh a gamta ka hih manin Pasian in kei hong hehpih hi.

### v15

**TDB77:** Zeisu Khazih in mawhneite gum dingin leitungah hong paisuk hi, a cihna thu in thuman thutak hi a, a vekpi-in san’ ding a kilawm hi. Tua mawhnei mite lakah keimah in a mawhnei bel mi ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Khrih Jesuh, mawhneite a honkhia dingin leitungah hong pai hi, cihna thu a man hi a, a uphuai a san'huai thu ahi hi. Keimah zong tua mawhnei mite lakah a mawhpha bel ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** A mawhneipha bel ka hihna thu hang mahin, nidang ciangin amah umin nuntak tawntungna a ngahding mite' etteh theihdingin Khrih Jesuh in a lungduaina cingtakin kei tungah hong lahtheihna dingin Pasian in kei a hong hehpih ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ka ta Timoti aw, tanglai peka kipanin nang bang mite-a' dingin a kigensa thu tawh kizui-in nang tungah a thu ka hong ap hi. Hoihtakin gal na dozawhna dingin galhiamin tua thute zang in la,
