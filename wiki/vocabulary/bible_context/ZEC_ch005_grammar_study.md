## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then I turned, and lifted up mine eyes, and looked, and behold a flying roll.

**TDB77:** Ka khuadak kik leh huih lakah a leeng laizial khat ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Ka khuadak kik leh huihlakah a leeng laizial khat ka mu hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** And he said unto me, What seest thou? And I answered, I see a flying roll; the length thereof is twenty cubits, and the breadth thereof ten cubits.

**TDB77:** Vantungmi in, “Bang mu na hi hiam?” ci-in hong dong a, kei zong, “Laizial a leeng khat ka mu a, a dung pi sawmthum pha-in, a vai pi sawm leh pi nga pha hi,” ci-in ka dawng hi.

**ZVS:** Vantung mi in, “Bang na mu hiam?” ci-in hong dong a, kei zong, “Laizial a leeng khat ka mu a, a dung pi sawmthum pha-in, a vai pi sawm leh pi nga pha hi,” ci-in ka dawng hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** Then said he unto me, This is the curse that goeth forth over the face of the whole earth: for every one that stealeth shall be cut off as on this side according to it; and every one that sweareth shall be cut off as on that side according to it.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin amah kei kiangah, “leitung gam khempeuhah a pai ding hamsiatnate, hih lai tungah kigelh hi. Guta khempeuh, gam sung panin kisiak khia mang ding hi, ci-in laizial dal lang khatah kigelh a, a man lopi-in ka min tawh thuciam mi khempeuh zong kisiak khia mang ding hi, ci-in a lang khatah kigelh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin amah in kei kiangah, “Leitung gam khempeuhah a tung ding hamsiatnate, hih lai tungah kigelh hi. Guta khempeuh, gamsung panin kisiakmang ding hi, ci-in laizial dal lang khatah kigelh a, a manlopi-in ka min tawh thuciam mi khempeuh zong kisiakkhia mang ding hi, ci-in a lang khatah kigelh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

**TDB77:** Kei zong, “Bang ahi hiam?” ci-in ka dong hi. Amah in hong dawng a, “Tua in ciangpi hi a, gamsung khempeuh’ mawhna lim ahi hi,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Kei zong, “Bang ahi hiam?” ci-in ka dong hi. Amah in hong dawng a, “Tua pen ciangpi hi a, gamsung khempeuh' mawhna lim ahi hi,” hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7

**TDB77:** Tua ciangpi a sin in haknaw kibawl ahi hi. Tua banga ka et lai takin haknaw kibawl tua ciangpi sin pen kidomto a, tua ciangpi sungah numei khat tu hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin vantung mi in, “Hih numei pen gitlohna lim ahi hi,” hong ci hi. Tua ciangin vantung mi in ciangpi sungah tua numei hensuk kik a, a sin tawh tua ciangpi a sinkik hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix
