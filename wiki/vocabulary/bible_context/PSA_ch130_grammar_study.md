## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Out of the depths have I cried unto thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, a thukpi sung panin nang tungah kong kiko hi!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka lungkham mahmahna panin nang tungah kong kiko hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Lord, hear my voice: let thine ears be attentive to the voice of my supplications.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, ka aw hong za in! Ka thumna aw-gingte hong ngai in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, ka aw hong za in. Ka thumna awgingte hong ngai in.

### v3
**EN:** If thou,

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangmah in mawhnate ciamteh lecin, Topa aw, kua in thuak zo ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangmah in mawhneite ciamteh lecin, { Topa } aw, kua in thuakzo ding ahi hiam?

### v4
**EN:** But there is forgiveness with thee, that thou mayest be feared.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin nang hong kizahtak theih nadingin nangmah in mawhna na maisak hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin nangmah hong kizahtak theihna dingin nangmah in mawhna na maisak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** I wait for the

**TDB77:** Topa’ hong huh ding lawpna tawh ka ngak a, ama thugen ka muang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } hong huh ding lawpna tawh ka ngak a, ama thugen ka muang hi.

### v6
**EN:** My soul waiteth for the Lord more than they that watch for the morning: I say, more than they that watch for the morning.

**TDB77:** Galvilte in zingsang a ngaklah sangin, Topa ka ngaklah zaw hi.

**ZVS:** Galvilte in zingsang hun ding a ngaklah sangin galvilte in phalvak hun ding a ngaklah sangin, { Topa } ka ngaklah zaw hi.

### v7
**EN:** Let Israel hope in the

**TDB77:** Israel aw, Topa muang in! Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa itna kip tawntung a, amah in hong honkhia nuam tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** Israel aw, { Topa } muang in. Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa' } itna kip tawntungin, hong honkhia nuam tawntung hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** And he shall redeem Israel from all his iniquities.

**TDB77:** Amah in, Israelte a mawhna khempeuh panin tankhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mawhna a om khempeuh panin amah in Israel-te a honkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
