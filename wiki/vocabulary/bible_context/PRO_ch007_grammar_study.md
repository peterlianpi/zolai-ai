## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My son, keep my words, and lay up my commandments with thee.

**TDB77:** Ka ta aw, ka thugente ciamteh in la, kong sawl thute mangngilh ngei kei in.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, ka thugente ciamteh in la, kong sawl thute khempeuh mangngilh ngei kei in.

### v2
**EN:** Keep my commandments, and live; and my law as the apple of thine eye.

**TDB77:** Kong gen bangin gamta in; na nungta ding hi. Kong thuhilhte, na mitnauta bangin hoih takin kem in.

**ZVS:** Kong gen bangin gamta lecin na nungta ding hi. Kong thuhilhte, na mitnauta bangin hoihtakin kem in.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3

**TDB77:** Kong hilhnate kem tawntung in la, na lungtang tungah gelh in.

### v4
**EN:** Say unto wisdom, Thou art my sister; and call understanding thy kinswoman:

**TDB77:** Pilna, na sanggamnu dingin nei in la, thumuhna, na lawm itpen dingin nei in.

**ZVS:** Pilna na sanggamnu dingin nei in la, thumuhna na lawm it pen dingin nei in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** That they may keep thee from the strange woman, from the stranger which flattereth with her words.

**TDB77:** Tua hi leh, midangte’ zi tungah na khialh khak loh nading, kamkhum tawh hong zol numeite tawh na khialh khak loh nadingin hong hu ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi leh midangte' zi tungah na khialhkhak lohna ding, kam khum tawh a hong zol numei ginalote tawh na khialhkhak lohna dingin hong hu ding hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | future: will

### v6

**ZVS:** bangmah a phawk nailo tangval honkhat lakah, tangval thu theilo khat, ka lim muhkha hi.

**ZVS:** Lamdungah amah a vak laitakin, tua bang numei paktat a omna inn gei khatah va paikha a,

### v11

### v23
