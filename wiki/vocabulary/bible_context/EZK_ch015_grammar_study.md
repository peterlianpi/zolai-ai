### v2
**EN:** Son of man, What is the vine tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest?

**TDB77:** “Ezekiel aw, leenggui pen singkung tawh bangci kiteh theih ding ahi hiam? Tulak-a om singkungte tawh tehna-in leenggui hiangte banga kimang ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Mihing ahi Ezekiel aw, leenggui pen singkung tawh bangci kiteh ding ahi hiam? Tulaka om singkungte tawh tehna-in leenggui hiangte banga kimang ahi hiam?

### v3

**ZVS:** Leengguite na-in kizang thei ahi hiam? Vankhaina-a zat ding singzum khet dingin kimang thei ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet for any work?

**TDB77:** Mei tawh nading bekin kimang hi. A mong tuak a kattum khit ciangin a om lai a zul kat teng, bangin kimang nawn ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** A kat ma-in zong bangmahin kimanglo ahih manin, kangin a vom khit ciangin a kiman'na omlo semsem hi.

### v6

**ZVS:** “Tua ahih ciangin Topa { Pasian } in hih bangin ci hi: Tulaka om singte in kiman'na nei hi. Ahi zongin leengguite kila-in a kihaltum mah bangin Jerusalem-a teng mite lakhia-in
