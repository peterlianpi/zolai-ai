## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O God, thou hast cast us off, thou hast scattered us, thou hast been displeased; O turn thyself to us again.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, kote nong paai khin a, ka panmunte-te na sukham khin hi; kote tungah nangmah na heh khin hi; kote ka ngei-te hong bangsak kik in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, kote na hong nilkhia khin a, ka pan'munte-te na sukham mang khin hi. Kote tungah nangmah na heh khin hi. Kote ka ngei-te hong bangsakkik ta in.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Thou hast made the earth to tremble; thou hast broken it: heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in lei na liingsak khin a, lei na khaangkham sak khin hi; kip lo ahih ciangin a kitam mate mat kik in.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in lei na lingsak khin a, lei na khangkhamsak khin hi. Kiplo ahih ciangin a kitam mate matkik in.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Thou hast shewed thy people hard things: thou hast made us to drink the wine of astonishment.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in na mite haksatna na thuaksak khin hi; ka dawn dingun nangmah in ko hong hoisak leenggahzu nong piakhin hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in na mite a haksa na thuaksak khin hi. Ka dawn dingun ko a hong hoisak leenggahzu na hong pia khin hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v4
**EN:** Thou hast given a banner to them that fear thee, that it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.

**TDB77:** Thalte panin kihem-in a kikaihkhop dingun nangmah hong zahtakte’ adingin dial khat na koih khin hi.

**ZVS:** Thalte panin kihemin a kikaihkhopna dingun nang hong zahtakte-a' dingin dial khat na koih khin hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | completed: already done | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** That thy beloved may be delivered; save with thy right hand, and hear me.

**TDB77:** Nangma it mite a suahtak theih nadingin nangma khut taklam tawh gualzawhna pia in la, kote’ ngetna hong pia in!

**ZVS:** Nangma it mite a suahtak theihna dingin na khut taklam tawh gualzawhna pia in la, kote' ngetna hong pia in.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v6
**EN:** God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

**TDB77:** Ama biakinn sungah Pasian in thu gen khin hi: “Gualzo-a lungdamna tawh Sekhem gam khenkhia-in Sukkot gamkuam ka sehkhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** A biakinn sungah Pasian in thu gen a, “Gualzo-a lungdamna tawh Shekhem gam khenkhia-in Sukkoth gam kuam ka sehkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | completed: already done | future: will

### v7
**EN:** Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver;

**TDB77:** Gilead gam in keima’ aa hi; Manasseh gam in keima’ aa hi; Efraim gam in ka siklukhu hi; Judah gam in ka ciangkhut hi.

**ZVS:** Gilead gam keima-a' hi a, Manasseh gam zong keima-a' mah ahi hi. Efraim gam ka siklukhu hi a, Judah gam ka ciangkhut ahi hi.

### v8
**EN:** Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe: Philistia, triumph thou because of me.

**TDB77:** Moab gam in ka kisilna kuang hi; Edom gam tungah ka khedap ka lawn hi; Filistia gam tungah gualzawhna tawh ka awng hi,”

**ZVS:** Moab gam ka kisilna kuang hi a, ka neihsa hi cih lahna limin Edom gam tungah ka khedap ka lawn a, Filistia gam tungah gualzawhna tawh ka awng hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?

**TDB77:** Kua in khuapi muanhuai-ah kei hong paipih ding hiam? Kua in Edom gamah kei hong paipih ding hiam?

**ZVS:** Kua in khuapi muan'huai-ah kei hong paipih ding hiam? Kua in Edom gamah kei hong paipih ding hiam?

### v10
**EN:** Wilt not thou, O God, which hadst cast us off? and thou, O God, which didst not go out with our armies?

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangmah in kote hong don lo khin hi lo na hi hiam? Pasian aw, komau’ galkapte tawh na kuankhia kei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nangmah in kote a hong don nawnlo khinta hilo na hi hiam? Pasian aw, komau' galkapte tawh na hong kuankhia kei hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | negation: kei

### v11
**EN:** Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.

**TDB77:** Ka gal dona uh-ah huhna kote hong pia in, bang hang hiam cih leh mihing’ huhna a mawkna hi!

**ZVS:** Ka galdona uhah huhna hong pia in; bang hang hiam cih leh mihing' huhna a mawkna hi.

### v12
**EN:** Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.

**TDB77:** Pasian tawh khauh takin i hih ding hi; amah in i galte hong sikcipsak dingpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian tawh khauhtakin i hih ding hi. Amah pen i galte hong sikcipsak dingpa ahi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
