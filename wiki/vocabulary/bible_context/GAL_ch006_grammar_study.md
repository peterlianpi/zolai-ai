## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye which are spiritual, restore such an one in the spirit of meekness; considering thyself, lest thou also be tempted.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, mi khat kikeem khialin mawhna khatpeuh ahih khak leh, Kha Siangtho a nei note in mawhna hong zolna tawh na kipelh nadingun kikeemin, lungnemna tawh tua bang mi na kipsak un.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, mi khatpeuh a gamta khial a om ciangin, Kha Siangtho' deihna bangin a nungta note in tua a khialpa a manin a gamtatkik theihna dingin thunem zangin na huh un. Note in zong amah bangin ze-etna na tuahkhak lohna dingun na kikem un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ.

**TDB77:** Van puak ding a omte khat leh khat kihuhin na pua un la, Khazih’ thukham na zui un.

**ZVS:** Thu haksa na tuah-te ciangin khatlekhat na kihuh un. Tua hi leh Khrih' thukham a zui na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** For if a man think himself to be something, when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, mi khat in bangmah ahi lo napi-in na khat peuhpeuh ka hi ci-in a ki-up leh, tua mi in ama pumpi a kheem hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khatpeuh, bangmah hilo napi-in amah a kithupi ngaihsut mawkmawk leh amah leh amah a kikhem ahi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** But let every man prove his own work, and then shall he have rejoicing in himself alone, and not in another.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin mi khempeuh in ama bawlna thu kidong ciat-te hen. Tua bang ahih leh, midangte tawh kiteh loin ama tung bekah lung kiphat theihna a om ding hi.

**ZVS:** A kuamah peuh in ama gamtatna ciat entel hen la, kisialhna ding a om leh ama gamtatna siksanin kisial hen la, midangte tawh ki-ettehin kisial kei hen.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** For every man shall bear his own burden.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, mi khempeuh in ama van ciat a pua ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh a kuamah peuh in i gamtatna bang ciatin a ngah ding ihi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ Thu a sin mi in a hilhpa tungah a hoihna a om khempeuh hawmsawn ta hen.

**ZVS:** Pasian thu hilhna a za mipa in ama neih na hoihte khempeuh zong, amah a hilhpa tawh zangkhawm ta hen.

### v7
**EN:** Be not deceived; God is not mocked: for whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap.

**TDB77:** Note in na ngaihsun khial kei un. Pasian in thudon lohnate mang ahi kei hi. Mi khatpeuh in khaici a vawh bangin a gah a at kik ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian a kikhemzo dingin ngaihsunkha kei un. Mi khempeuh in ama vawh nam khaici mah ngahkik ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** For he that soweth to his flesh shall of the flesh reap corruption; but he that soweth to the Spirit shall of the Spirit reap life everlasting.

**TDB77:** Ama pumpi ading khaici a vawh mi in ama ci-le-sa hangah muatna a at ding hi. Kha Siangtho hangah khaici a vawh mi in Kha Saingtho hangah nuntak tawntungna a at ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khatpeuh in ama leitung pumpi deihna khaici a vawh leh sihna ngahding a, Kha Siangtho deihna khaici a vawh leh Kha Siangtho tung panin nuntak tawntungna ngahding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** And let us not be weary in well doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not.

**TDB77:** Eite in lungkia loin gamtat hoih gamta ni. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, lungkia loin i om leh, a hun a tun’ ciangin a gah i at ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin na hoih sepding cimtak kei ni. Lungkia loin i sep nak leh a hun ciangin thaman i ngah ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v10
**EN:** As we have therefore opportunity, let us do good unto all men, especially unto them who are of the household of faith.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin eite in a kilawm a kituak hun i neih bangin mi khempeuh tungah thupha bawlin, adiak diakin thu-um innkuanpihte tungah nakpi-in bawl ni.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin hunhoih i ngah simin midangte tungah a hoihin i gamta ding a, a diakdiakin thu upna sungah i innkuanpihte khempeuh tungah i hoih gamtatding i phawk ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | hortative: let us | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Ye see how large a letter I have written unto you with mine own hand.

**TDB77:** Bang zah a lian laimalte tawh ka khutin ka gelh, na en un.

**ZVS:** Tu-in keima khut mahmah tawh laimal golpipi-in lai ka hong gelh na mu-te hi.

### v12
**EN:** As many as desire to make a fair shew in the flesh, they constrain you to be circumcised; only lest they should suffer persecution for the cross of Christ.

**TDB77:** Amaute pumpi-te tawh kipawl minthan’na a deih mite in, Khazih’ singlamteh hangah thuakna leh bawlsiatna tawh kipelh nuam bekin, note vunat thuak nadingin zawhthawh thu tawh hong zol-te hi.

**ZVS:** Note in na vun-te na atna dingun a hong hahbawl mite in mihoih ahihna-te kilangsak nuamin, a kimuthei nate a kisialhpih mite ahi-te hi. Singlamteh tungah Khrih a sihna thu hangin amaute in bawlsiatna a ngahlohna dingun a tuaci gamtat ahi-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v13
**EN:** For neither they themselves who are circumcised keep the law; but desire to have you circumcised, that they may glory in your flesh.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, vunatna a thuak mite in Thukham zui loin note’ pumpi-te hangin kisathei nuamin note vunat nading a deih-te hi.

**ZVS:** Vun-atna a zang mite nangawn in Thukham zuinuam tuanlo-te hi. Hih a pualam gamtatna thu note in na zuihna hangun kisialnuam-te ahih manin note in vun-atna na zat ding-te a hong deih-te ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** But God forbid that I should glory, save in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom the world is crucified unto me, and I unto the world.

**TDB77:** Kei ka hih leh, eite Topa Zeisu Khazih’ singlamteh sim loh, bangmah dangah ka kisathei het kei ding hi. Tua singlamteh ah hih leitung in kei ading kikhenin a om hi. Keimah in zong leitung ading kikhenin ka om hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin keimah in ahih leh, singlamteh tungah i Topa Jesuh a sihna thu bek ka kisialhpih ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh singlamteh tungah ama sihna hangin kei-a' dingin leitung thute misi bang a, kei zong, leitung thu-ah misi mah ka bang hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision, but a new creature.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, vunatna in phattuamna a om kei hi, vunat lohna in zong phattuamna a om kei hi: mi thak suahna bek in phattuamna a om hi.

**ZVS:** Vun ki-atna leh ki-atlohna a thupi om tuanlo a, mi thak nam pawl hihding bek a thupi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v16
**EN:** And as many as walk according to this rule, peace be on them, and mercy, and upon the Israel of God.

**TDB77:** Hih ngeina a zui mi khempeuh tungah ahi zongin, Pasian’ Israel mi pawlte tungah ahi zongin nopna leh hehpihna om ta hen.

**ZVS:** Hih ka thugenna a zui mite-a kipanin Pasian' mite khempeuh tungah Pasian in nopsakna leh hehpihna thupha hong pia ta hen.

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be

### v17
**EN:** From henceforth let no man trouble me: for I bear in my body the marks of the Lord Jesus.

**TDB77:** Tu-a kipanin kuamah in kei hong nawngkaisak kei hen. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, ka pumpi tungah Zeisu’ ciamtehnate ka pua hi.

**ZVS:** A tawpna-ah ka gennop thu in: Ka pum tunga om meimapawn pen Khrih sila ka hihna lim ahih manin kuamah in kei hong nawngkaisak nawn kei ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** Brethren, the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen.

**TDB77:** Ute naute aw, i Topa Zeisu Khazih’ hehpihna thupha in note lungsim-te tawh omkhawm ta hen. Amen.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, note khempeuh tungah i Topa Jesuh Khrih hehpihna thupha hong tung ta hen. Amen.
