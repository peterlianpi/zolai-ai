## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Blessed is every one that feareth the

**TDB77:** Topa zahtak-in ama thu a zui mi khempeuh thupha ngah mi ahi hi!

**ZVS:** { Topa } zahtakin ama thu a zui mi khempeuh thupha ngah mi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.

**TDB77:** Na khut tawh na sepsa thaman na ne ding a, na lungdamin na ma vang ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na khut tawh na sep thaman na ne ding a, na lung damin na mavang ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v3
**EN:** Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine by the sides of thine house: thy children like olive plants round about thy table.

**TDB77:** Nangma inn sungah na zi in a gah pha mahmah leenggui tawh kibang ding a, na sabuai kim-ah na tate in oliv-no dawng hilhhelh tawh kibang ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma inn sungah na zi, a gah pha mahmah leenggui tawh kibang ding a, na sabuai kiimah a tu na tate, oliv no dawng hilhhelh tawh kibang ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that feareth the

**TDB77:** En un, Topa a zahtak mi tua bangin thupha kipia ding hi.

**ZVS:** En un, { Topa } a zahtak mi tua bangin thupha kipia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in Zion panin nang thupha hong pia hen la, na nuntak tawntung Jerusalem’ nopsakna mu ta in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } in Zion panin nang thupha hong pia hen la, na nuntak sung tawntung Jerusalem' nopsakna mu ta in.

### v6
**EN:** Yea, thou shalt see thy children's children, and peace upon Israel.

**TDB77:** Na tute a mu dingin hong siam hen la, Israelte tungah nopna om ta hen!

**ZVS:** Na tute a mu dingin hong siam ta hen. Israel tungah nopna om ta hen!

**Grammar:** optative: let there be / let it be | purpose: in order to
