## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangma min tawh kei hong hon in la, na vaang tawh ka dikna kilangsak in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, na thahatna tawh kei hong honkhia in la, na vangliatna tawh ka dikna hong kilangsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka thungetna hong za in la, ka kampaute hong ngai in.

### v3
**EN:** For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi kiphasakte in kei hong lang pang a, mi helhhawtte in thah dingin hong zong-te hi; amaute in Pasian thudon lo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mi kiphasakte in kei hong langpang-te a, mi helhhawtte in thah dingin hong zong-te hi. Amaute in Pasian thudonlo-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul.

**TDB77:** En in, Pasian in kei hong huhpa hi a, Topa in kei hong huhpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian, kei hong huhpa hi a, { Topa, } kei hong hupa ahi hi.

### v5
**EN:** He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth.

**TDB77:** Amah in ka galte siatna tawh thuk ding hi; na thumanna tawh amaute sumai in.

**ZVS:** Amah in ka galte siatna tawh thuk ding hi. Na thumanna tawh kizui-in amaute sumai in.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O

**TDB77:** Utna lungsim tawh na tungah biakpiakna kong pia ding a, nang hoih na hih manin, na tungah lungdamna kong ko ding hi, Topa aw.

**ZVS:** Utna lungsim tawh biakpiakna kong pia ding a, nang hoih na hih manin, nangma tungah lungdamna kong ko ding hi, { Topa } aw.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v7
**EN:** For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen his desire upon mine enemies.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh haksatna khempeuh panin kei nong honkhia khin a, ka galte a guallelhna ka mu khin hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh haksatna khempeuh panin kei nong honkhia khin a, ka galte a guallelhna keimah in ka mu khin ta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done
