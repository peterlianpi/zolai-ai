## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I will extol thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nangmah phatin ka diang ding hi, bang hang hiam cih leh kei nong kaito khin a, ka tungah ka galte na lungdamsak kei hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangmah hong phatin ka diang ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh kei nong laamto khin a, ka tungah ka galte na lungdamsak kei hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v2
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa ka Pasian aw, huhna ngenin na tung kong awng a, kei nong damsak khin hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } ka Pasian aw, huhna a hong ngenin nang tungah kong awng a, kei na hong damsak khin hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | completed: already done

### v3
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, Leinuai panin kei nong paikhiatpih to khin a, Leihawm sunga pai khinsate lak panin kei nong nungtakik sak hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, Leinuai panin kei nong paikhiatpihto khin a, Leihawm sungah a pai khinsate lak panin kei nong nungta kiksak hi.

### v4
**EN:** Sing unto the

**TDB77:** Ama misiangthote aw, Topa tungah phatna la sa un la, ama tungah lungdamna ko un.

**ZVS:** Ama mi siangthote aw, { Topa } tungah phatna la sa-in ama tungah lungdamna ko un.

### v5
**EN:** For his anger endureth but a moment; in his favour is life: weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning.

**TDB77:** Ama hehna tomno sung bek hi a, ama deihsakna in khang tawntung ading ahi hi. Zan sung teng kahna om kha mah ding a, ahih hangin zing khuavak tawh nuamna hong pai hi.

**ZVS:** Ama hehna tomno sung bek hi a, ama hong deihsakna pen khang tawntung-a ding ahi hi. Zan hun sungteng kahna omkha mah ding a, ahih hangin zing khuavak tawh nuamna hong tung hi.

### v6
**EN:** And in my prosperity I said, I shall never be moved.

**TDB77:** Keimah in ahih leh, ka daupai lai takin, “Ka kiling ngei kei ding hi,” ka ci kha hi.

**ZVS:** Keimah in ahih leh, ka daupai laitakin, “Ka kiling ngei kei ding hi,” ka ci kha hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** I cried to thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, nang tungah kong kiko hi; Topa tungah ka thum hi:

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, nangma tungah kong kiko a, { Topa } tungah kong thum hi:

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** What profit is there in my blood, when I go down to the pit? Shall the dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth?

**TDB77:** “Leihawm sungah paisukin ka sihna-ah bang metna om ahi hiam? Leivui in nang hong phat ding ahi hiam? Na thumanna gen ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Leihawm sungah paisukin ka sihna-ah bang meetna om ahi hiam? Leivui in nang hong phat ding ahi hiam? Na thumanna gen ding ahi hiam?

### v10
**EN:** Hear, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, hong za in la, kei hong hehpih in! Topa aw, nangmah in keimah huhpa hong hi in!”

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, hong za in la, kei hong hehpih in. { Topa } aw, keimah huhpa hong hi in,” ka ci hi.

### v12
**EN:** To the end that my glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent. O

**TDB77:** { Topa } ka Pasian aw, nangma tungah lungdamna ka hong koh tawntung theihna ding, ka lungsim in nang hong phat theihna ding, a dai om mawklohna dingin ka thumna leh ka taunate lamna tawh nong heisak a, dahna lim ahi ka puanhampi hong suahkhiat sakin nuamna na hong guan hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to
