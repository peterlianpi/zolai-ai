| Pattern | Count |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Lo, mine eye hath seen all this, mine ear hath heard and understood it.

**TDB77:** “Na gen thu khempeuh uh ka muhsa hi a, ka bil-in ka zaksa, ka theihsa thu ahi hi.

**ZVS:** “Na gen uh thu khempeuh ka muhsa vive hi a, ka bilin ka zakzaksa, ka theihtheihsa thu ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** What ye know, the same do I know also: I am not inferior unto you.

**TDB77:** Note’ theih khempeuh, kei zong ka thei hi. Note sangin ka neuzaw tuan kei hi.

**ZVS:** Note' theih khempeuh, kei zong ka thei hi. Note khempeuh sangin ka neuzaw tuan het kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** Surely I would speak to the Almighty, and I desire to reason with God.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin note tawh kam kinial hi loin Pasian tawh kinial ka hi hi. Pasian tawh hih thu ka kinial nuam hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin note tawh kam a kinial hiloin, Pasian tawh a kinial ka hi hi. Pasian tawh hih ka thu ka kinial nuam hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** But ye are forgers of lies, ye are all physicians of no value.

**TDB77:** Note in na haina uh zuauthu tawh na melh uh a, kuamah a damsak zolo zato siam-a kineite tawh na kibang uh hi.

**ZVS:** Note in na haina uh zuauthu tawh na melh uh a, kuamah a damsak zolo zato siam-a kinei mite tawh na kibang uh hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** O that ye would altogether hold your peace! and it should be your wisdom.

**TDB77:** Pau het loin om hi le-uhcin, mipilpi mah hong kisa zaw ding hi ve’n.

**ZVS:** Pau hetloin om hi le uh cin, mipil mah hong kisazaw ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Hear now my reasoning, and hearken to the pleadings of my lips.

**TDB77:** Tu-in ka thugen hong ngai un la, ka gen laitak limtak ngai un.

**ZVS:** “Tu-in ka thugen hong ngai un la, ka gen laitakin limtak ngai un.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v7
**EN:** Will ye speak wickedly for God? and talk deceitfully for him?

**TDB77:** Pasian’ min dingin a man lo genin, ama adingin zuauthu gensak ding na hi uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian' min dingin thumanlo genin, ama-a' dingin zuauthu gensak ding na hi uh hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v8
**EN:** Will ye accept his person? will ye contend for God?

**TDB77:** Pasian angvan ding a, ama ading gensak ding na hi uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian angvan ding a, ama-a' ding gensak ding na hi uh hiam?

### v9
**EN:** Is it good that he should search you out? or as one man mocketh another, do ye so mock him?

**TDB77:** Pasian in lim takin note hong entel leh note sungah hoihna mu ding ahi hiam? Mihingte na khem bangun Pasian khem thei dingin kingaihsun na hi uh hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian in limtakin note hong entel leh note' sungah hoihna mu ding ahi hiam? Mihingte na khem bangun Pasian a khem thei dingin kingaihsun na hi uh hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** He will surely reprove you, if ye do secretly accept persons.

**TDB77:** A simthamin angvanna na zat uh leh amah in note hong taai taktak ding hi.

**ZVS:** A simthamin angvanna na zat uh leh amah in note hong taii taktak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** Shall not his excellency make you afraid? and his dread fall upon you?

**TDB77:** Ama vangliatna in note hong lausak ding a, note tungah amah kihtakna hong tung ding hilo ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** Your remembrances are like unto ashes, your bodies to bodies of clay.

**TDB77:** Na thugennate uh vut khu bang hi lel a, na siksante uh leikhal bang hi lel hi.

**ZVS:** Na thugente uh vutkhu bang hi lel a, na siksante uh leivui bang hi lel hi.

### v13
**EN:** Hold your peace, let me alone, that I may speak, and let come on me what will.

**TDB77:** Pau kei un la, kei thu hong gensak dih un, a piang peuhpeuh a piang hi mai hen.

**ZVS:** Pau kei un la, kei, thu hong gensak dih un; a piang peuhpeuh a piang hi mai hen.

### v14
**EN:** Wherefore do I take my flesh in my teeth, and put my life in mine hand?

**TDB77:** Keimah in ka nuntakna pia ngamin, ka nuntakna ka ap ngam hi.

**ZVS:** “Ka nuntakna pia ngamin, ka nuntakna ka ap ngam hi.

### v15
**EN:** Though he slay me, yet will I trust in him: but I will maintain mine own ways before him.

**TDB77:** Pasian in kei hong that mah taleh zong, ke’n phamawh ka sa kei hi. Ahi zongin ama mai-ah ka thu ka gen veve ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in kei hong that mah taleh zong, phamawh ka sa kei hi. Ahi zongin ama mai-ah ka thu ka gen veve ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v16
**EN:** He also shall be my salvation: for an hypocrite shall not come before him.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ mai-ah migilote’ pai ngam lo ahih manin, ka han’na in ka gualzawhna hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' mai-ah migilote pai ngamlo uh ahih manin, ka han'na pen ka gualzawhna hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | causal: because

### v17
**EN:** Hear diligently my speech, and my declaration with your ears.

**TDB77:** Ka thugente limtak ngai un la, ka lahtelnate na za un.

### v18
**EN:** Behold now, I have ordered my cause; I know that I shall be justified.

**TDB77:** Ka thu a gen dingin a baihsa-in ka om a, thu ka zawh ding ka tel theih hi.

**ZVS:** Ka thu a gen dingin a baihsa-in ka om a, thu ka zawh ding lamtak teltakin ka thei hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v19
**EN:** Who is he that will plead with me? for now, if I hold my tongue, I shall give up the ghost.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, kei hong mawhsak ding na hi hiam? Nong mawhsak leh, pau loin a si dingin ka kiging hi.

**ZVS:** “Pasian aw, keimah hong mawhsak ding na hi hiam? Nong mawhsak leh pau hetloin a si dingin ka kiging hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20
**EN:** Only do not two things unto me: then will I not hide myself from thee.

**TDB77:** Thu nih kong ngen hi, hong pia in; tua ciangin nang tungah bangmah kong im kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Thu nih ka hong ngen hi, ka hong nget hong pia in. Tua ciangin nang tungah bangmah kong im kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | negation: kei

### v21

**TDB77:** Kei hong daantat nawn kei in la, launa hong thuaksak nawn kei in.

**ZVS:** Kei tungah gim hong pia nawn kei in la, hih bang launa hong thuaksak nawn kei in.

### v22

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, hong hopih masa in, kong dawng ding hi. Kei, thu hong gensak in la, nang’ hong dawng in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, hong hopih masa in, kong dawng ding hi. Kei, thu hong gensak in la, nangmah in hong dawng in.

**Grammar:** future: will

**ZVS:** Ka mawhna ka khialhna bangzah pha ahi hiam? Ka mawh ka khialhna teng hong theisak in.

**ZVS:** Bang hangin keimah lausak ding hanciam na hi hiam? Kei, singteh bang lel hilo ka hi hiam? Anvai themkhat galin do ding na hi hiam?
