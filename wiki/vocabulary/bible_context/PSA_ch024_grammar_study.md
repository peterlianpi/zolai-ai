## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The earth is the

**TDB77:** Leitang leh a tunga omte, leitung leh a tunga teengte in Topa aa hi;

**ZVS:** Leitung leh a tunga omte, leitung leh a tunga tengte { Topa}-a' ahi hi.

### v2
**EN:** For he hath founded it upon the seas, and established it upon the floods.

**TDB77:** bang hang hiam cih leh amah in tuipite tungah a bul phut a, gunte tungah kipsak hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh amah in tuipite sungah a bul phut a, tui tungah a kipsak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Who shall ascend into the hill of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ mual tungah kuate kah ding ahi hiam? Ama mun siangtho-ah kuate ding ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** { Topa' } mual tungah kuate kahto ding ahi hiam? Ama mun siangtho-ah kuate ding ding ahi hiam?

### v4
**EN:** He that hath clean hands, and a pure heart; who hath not lifted up his soul unto vanity, nor sworn deceitfully.

**TDB77:** Khut siangtho leh lung siangtho a nei, a man lo-ah a nuntakna pia loin, khemna tawh a kiciam lo mite ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Gamtat siangtho leh lungsim siangtho a nei, a manlo thu-ah a nuntakna pialoin, khemna tawh a kiciamlo mite ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** He shall receive the blessing from the

**TDB77:** Amah in Topa tung panin thupha ngah ding a, amah a honkhia Pasian kiang panin diksakna ngah ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang mite in { Topa' } tung panin thupha ngah ding-te a, amaute a honkhia Pasian' kiang panin diksakna ngah ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** This is the generation of them that seek him, that seek thy face, O Jacob. Selah.

**TDB77:** Tua bang mite pen, amah a zong, Jakob’ Pasian’ mai a zong minam ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang mite pen, amah a zong, Jakob' Pasian' mai a zong minam ahi hi.

### v7
**EN:** Lift up your heads, O ye gates; and be ye lift up, ye everlasting doors; and the King of glory shall come in.

**TDB77:** Minthang Kumpipa hong tum theih nadingin, khua kongpite aw, na lute-te sangsak un! Taanglai kong khakte aw, kilaamto un!

**ZVS:** Minthang Kumpipa hong tumtheihna dingin, kulh kongpite lian hon' un la, tanglai kongkhakte gol hon' un.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Who is this King of glory? The

**TDB77:** Minthang Kumpipa kua ahi hiam? Hat-a a vanglian Topa, galhat Topa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Minthang Kumpipa kua ahi hiam? Hatin a vanglian { Topa, } galhat { Topa } ahi hi.

### v9
**EN:** Lift up your heads, O ye gates; even lift them up, ye everlasting doors; and the King of glory shall come in.

**TDB77:** Mihing Kumpipa hong tum theih nadingin, khua kongpite aw, na lute-te sangsak un! Taanglai kong khakte aw, kilaamto un!

**ZVS:** Minthang Kumpipa hong tumtheihna dingin, kulh kongpite lian hon' un la, tanglai kongkhakte gol hon' un.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Who is this King of glory? The

**TDB77:** Hih minthang Kumpipa kua ahi hiam? Vanglianpen Topa, minthang Kumpipa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih a minthang Kumpipa kua ahi hiam? Vanglian{ Topa, } minthang Kumpipa ahi hi.
