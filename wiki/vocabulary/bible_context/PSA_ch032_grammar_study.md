## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Blessed is he whose transgression is forgiven, whose sin is covered.

**TDB77:** Ama khialhna kimaisak a, ama mawhna a kituamcipsak pa in thupha ngah mi ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ama khialhna kimaisak a, ama mawhna a kiselcipsak mi, thupha ngah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Blessed is the man unto whom the

**TDB77:** Topa in mawhna a khuksak loh mi, a lungsim-ah khemna a om lo mi in thupha ngah ahi hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in mawhna a khuksakloh mi, a lungsim sungah khemna a omlo mi, thupha ngah ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v3
**EN:** When I kept silence, my bones waxed old through my roaring all the day long.

**TDB77:** Ka mawhna ka pulak loh ciangin, sunthapai-a ka tautauna hangin ka pumpi a tha neem hi.

**ZVS:** Ka mawhna ka pulak loh ciangin, sunthapai ka tautauna hangin ka pumpi a thaneem hi.

### v4
**EN:** For day and night thy hand was heavy upon me: my moisture is turned into the drought of summer. Selah.

**TDB77:** Sun leh zan tawntung ka tungah na khut gik hi; khuakhal nisa bangin ka thahatna a keu hi.

**ZVS:** Sun leh zan tawntung gim nong piakna ka thuak a, khuakhal nisa' kat bangin ka thahatna a keucip hi.

### v5
**EN:** I acknowledged my sin unto thee, and mine iniquity have I not hid. I said, I will confess my transgressions unto the

**TDB77:** Ka khialhna na tungah kong pulak a, ka mawhna ka im kei hi; “Ka khialhnate Topa tungah ka pulak ding hi,” ka ci hi; tua ciangin ka mawhna nin nong maisak hi.

**ZVS:** Keima khialhnate nangma tungah kong pulak a, ka mawhna ka im kei hi. “Ka khialhnate khempeuh { Topa' } tungah ka pulak ding hi,” ka ci hi. Tua ciangin ka mawhna nin nong maisak hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | sequence: then/therefore | future: will | negation: kei

### v6
**EN:** For this shall every one that is godly pray unto thee in a time when thou mayest be found: surely in the floods of great waters they shall not come nigh unto him.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin nangmah hong kimuh theih lai-in mi hoih khempeuh, nangma tungah thu kingensak in; tua hi leh tuikhang in amaute ban lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin nang hong kimuh theih lai-in mihoih khempeuh, nang tungah thu kingensak in. Tua hi leh tuikhang in amaute banlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Thou art my hiding place; thou shalt preserve me from trouble; thou shalt compass me about with songs of deliverance. Selah.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in keima adingin bukna mun na hi a, haksatna panin kei hong kemin, hotkhiatna tawh nong tuam hi.

**ZVS:** Nang pen kei-a' dingin ka bukna mun na hi a, haksatna pan kei hong kemin hotkhiatna kicing nong pia hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v8
**EN:** I will instruct thee and teach thee in the way which thou shalt go: I will guide thee with mine eye.

**TDB77:** Na paina ding lampi lakin kong hilh ding a, ka mit tawh hong enin thu kong hilh ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na paina ding lampi lakin ka hong hilh ding a, ka mit tawh hong enin thu kong hilh ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v9
**EN:** Be ye not as the horse, or as the mule, which have no understanding: whose mouth must be held in with bit and bridle, lest they come near unto thee.

**TDB77:** Kamkaih tawh uk loh-a phamawh, thu theihna nei lo, sakol leh laa bang kei in.

**ZVS:** A kamkaih tawh uk lohin a phamawh, thutheihna a neilo, sakol leh la bang kei in.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v10
**EN:** Many sorrows shall be to the wicked: but he that trusteth in the

**TDB77:** Migilote in gimna tampi thuak uh a, Topa a muangte ahih leh itna kip in tuamcip hi.

**ZVS:** Migilote in gimna tampi thuak uh a, { Topa } a muangte ahih leh itna kip in a hu hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
