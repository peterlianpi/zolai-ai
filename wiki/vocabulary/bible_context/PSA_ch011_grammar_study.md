## Verses

### v1
**EN:** In the

**TDB77:** Topa ka bel hi; bangci-in ka tungah, “Vasa leeng bangin mual tungah tai-in,

**ZVS:** Ke'n { Topa } ka bel hi. Bang hangin ka tungah, “Vasa leeng bangin mualtungah tai in;

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** For, lo, the wicked bend their bow, they make ready their arrow upon the string, that they may privily shoot at the upright in heart.

**TDB77:** bang hang hiam cih leh, en in, migilote in, khuamial sung pan lungsim thutangte kap dingin a thalkhau uh-ah thaltang bulhin a thal-te keek hi;

**ZVS:** bang hang hiam cih leh, en in, migilote in khuamial sung panin lungsim thutangte a kap dingin a thalkhau uhah thaltang bulhin a thal-te a keek-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** If the foundations be destroyed, what can the righteous do?

**TDB77:** bulpite kisusia leh, midikte in bang cih thei nawn ding hiam?” nong ci thei diam?

**ZVS:** Bulpite kisusia khin leh midikte in bangcih thei nawn ding hiam?” nong ci thei diam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in ama biakinn siangtho sungah om a, Topa’ tokhom in vantungah om hi; a mit-in en a, a mit tawh mihing’ tate entel hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } a biakinn siangtho sungah om a, { Topa' } tokhom vantungah om hi. Amah in mihingte hong en a, mihingte' gamtatna khempeuh a theitel hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v5
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in midikte leh migilote en tel a, a ngongtat nuam mi a lungsim in mudah hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in midikte leh migilote entel a, a ngongtat nuam mite a lungsim in mudah hi.

### v6
**EN:** Upon the wicked he shall rain snares, fire and brimstone, and an horrible tempest: this shall be the portion of their cup.

**TDB77:** Migilote tungah amah in mei-am leh kat zusak ding a, amau ngah ding hai in huih sa mahmah hi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in migilote tungah mei-am leh kaat zusak ding a, amau' ngah ding gimna huihsa mahmah hi ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | future: will

### v7
**EN:** For the righteous

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa dik a, dik gamtatna deih hi; thutang mi in ama mai mu ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } dik mahmah a, dik gamtatna a deih hi. Thutang mi in ama mai a mu ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
