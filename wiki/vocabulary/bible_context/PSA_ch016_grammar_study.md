# Psalms Chapter 16 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 11 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| negation: kei | 4 |
| future: will | 2 |
| causal: because | 2 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 2 |
| quotative: said/saying | 1 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 1 |
| sequence: then/therefore | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Preserve me, O God: for in thee do I put my trust.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangmah hong bel ka hih ciangin keimah hong kem in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nang hong bel ka hih manin keimah hong kem in.

### v2
**EN:** O my soul, thou hast said unto the

**TDB77:** Topa tungah, “Nang in ka Topa na hi hi; nang tawh loin hoihna ka nei kei hi,” ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } tungah, “Nang, ka { Topa } na hi hi; nang tawh loin hoihna ka nei kei hi,” ka ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | negation: kei

### v3
**EN:** But to the saints that are in the earth, and to the excellent, in whom is all my delight.

**TDB77:** Leitung a, om mi siangthote ahih leh, amaute nampi hi a, amau sungah ka lungkimna teng om hi.

**ZVS:** Leitungah a om mi siangthote ahih leh, amaute nampi hi a, amaute sungah ka lungkimna teng om hi.

### v4
**EN:** Their sorrows shall be multiplied that hasten after another god: their drink offerings of blood will I not offer, nor take up their names into my lips.

**TDB77:** Pasian dang a teelte in amau dahna khangsak hi; amau’ si theh biaknate ke’n bawlpih loin, amau’ pasiante ka biakpih kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian dang a teelte in amau' dahna khangsak hi. Amau' si theh biaknate ke'n bawlpih loin, amau' pasian-te ka biakpih kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v5
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa pen keima luah dinga ka teelsa leh keima hai, na hi hi; keima ngah ding nangmah in na kem hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } pen keima luah dinga ka teelsa, keima muan'pi na hi a, keima ngah ding khempeuh na khutsungah om hi.

### v6
**EN:** The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places; yea, I have a goodly heritage.

**TDB77:** Kei-a’ pen mun nuam sung hong tu ahih manin gamluah hoih nei ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Kei-a' pen mun nuam mahmah sung hong tu ahih manin gamhluah hoih nei ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v7
**EN:** I will bless the

**TDB77:** Ngaihsutna hong lak Topa ka phat hi; zan-in zong ka lungsim-ah hong suak hi.

**ZVS:** Ngaihsutna a hong lak { Topa } ka phat a, zanin zong ka lungsimah hong suak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** I have set the

**TDB77:** Topa ka mai-ngat tawntung a, amah in ka taklam-ah om ahih manin ka kiling kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } ka maingat tawntung a, ka taklamah amah om ahih manin ka kiling kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Therefore my heart is glad, and my glory rejoiceth: my flesh also shall rest in hope.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin lungdamin, ka kipak a, lung muangin ka om hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin lungdamin ka kipak a, lungmuangin ka om hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh keimah Leinuai nong zuan sak kei a, na mi hoihpa Leihawmpi na mu sak kei hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Leinuai nong zuansak kei a, na mihoihpa Leihawmpi na musak kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v11
**EN:** Thou wilt shew me the path of life: in thy presence is fulness of joy; at thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore.

**TDB77:** Nangmah in nuntakna lampi keimah nong lak hi; nangma omna-ah lungdamna lian om a, na tak lam ah nop tawntungnate om hi.

**ZVS:** Nangmah in nuntakna lampi keimah nong lak hi. Nangma omna munah lungdamna lianpi om a, nangma taklamah nop tawntungnate a om hi.
