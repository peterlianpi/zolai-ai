## Verses

### v1
**EN:** My brethren, be not many masters, knowing that we shall receive the greater condemnation.

**TDB77:** Ka u ka naute aw, Sia a sem kote in a khauhzaw thukhenna thuak ding ka hih manun note a tampi Sia suah ding na sawm kei un. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh eite in thudotna i thuak zaw ding hi ci-in na thei ta un.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, mite tungah thu a hilh eite pen midangte sangin a khauhzaw-in thu hong kikhen ding ahih manin note tampi sia na suaknuam se kei un.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v2
**EN:** For in many things we offend all. If any man offend not in word, the same is a perfect man, and able also to bridle the whole body.

**TDB77:** Eite khempeuh in na tampi-ah i khial thei hi. Mi khat in kampau a khialh kei leh, tua mi in a kicing mi hi a, ama pumpi khempeuh a keem thei mi zong ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Eite khempeuh a khialthei zelzelte vive ihi hi. Kampauna-ah a khial ngeilo mi a om leh a cingtaak mahmah hi a, a pumpi zong a ukzo mi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Behold, we put bits in the horses' mouths, that they may obey us; and we turn about their whole body.

**TDB77:** Sakol uk nading kamkaih bulh leeng, a pumpi khempeuh i kiheisak thei hi.

**ZVS:** Sakol in i thu hong man'theihna dingin kamkaih i bulh a, a sakol pumpi buppi i ukzo hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Behold also the ships, which though they be so great, and are driven of fierce winds, yet are they turned about with a very small helm, whithersoever the governor listeth.

**TDB77:** Ngai un, teembawte a lian mahmah-te a, huihpi in nakpi-in a mut hangin heina lenpa in a deih bangin, a neu mahmah heina tawh a hei thei hi.

**ZVS:** Teembawte ngaihsun dih un. Teembaw lian mahmah a, huihpi in nakpi takin a mut hangin a hawlpa' deihna bangin a heina neu khat tawh ki-ukzo hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Even so the tongue is a little member, and boasteth great things. Behold, how great a matter a little fire kindleth!

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin leii in na neu ahih hangin alianpi nate a kisaktheihpih thei hi. Bang zah alian sing tampi a neu mahmah mei tawh a kikangsak thei hi cih no na en un.

**ZVS:** Tua mah bangin leii zong i pumpi na sung panin a neu mahmah khat hinapi-in thusia lianpipi piangsak zo hi. Gam khempeuh zong mei-ek neuno khat pan kipanin a kangtum thei hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** And the tongue is a fire, a world of iniquity: so is the tongue among our members, that it defileth the whole body, and setteth on fire the course of nature; and it is set on fire of hell.

**TDB77:** Leii in mei a hi hi. A gilo na, leitung na ahi eite’ pumpi sungah om a, pumpi khempeuh ninsak a, pumpi hih theihna khempeuh zong hal a, amahmah in hel khua ah halna a thuak hi.

**ZVS:** Leii pen mei tawh kibang hi. I pumpi na sung panin a neuno khat hinapi-in i pumpi buppi a sia suahthei a, hell khuk sung panin a hong piang mei tawh i nuntakna buppi hong kangtumsak thei hi.

### v7
**EN:** For every kind of beasts, and of birds, and of serpents, and of things in the sea, is tamed, and hath been tamed of mankind:

**TDB77:** Ganhing apawlpawl khempeuh, vasa apawl apawl khempeuh leh tuipi sungah a om apawl apawl khempeuh mite in a zualsak zo-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mihingte in gamsa, vasa, a bokvak ganhing leh tui sunga om ngasate-a kipan ganhing khempeuh zualsakin ukzo-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** But the tongue can no man tame; it is an unruly evil, full of deadly poison.

**TDB77:** Leii ahih leh, kuamah in a zozo kei hi. Leii in a ommawk thei lo, a gilo ahi, sih theihna a piangsak gu khal tawh a dim hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin siattheihna ding gukhal tawh a kidim, a omkhong theilo leii pen zualsakin a ukzo kuamah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | negation: kei

### v9
**EN:** Therewith bless we God, even the Father; and therewith curse we men, which are made after the similitude of God.

**TDB77:** Tua leii tawh eite in i Topa leh i Pa i phat thei hi, Pasian’ lim banga kibawl mite, leii tawh i samsia thei hi.

**ZVS:** I leii tawh eite' pa ahi Topa i phat thei a, Pasian tawh a kisunin a kibawl i mipihte zong tua i leii mah tawh i samsia thei hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Out of the same mouth proceedeth blessing and cursing. My brethren, these things ought not so to be.

**TDB77:** Kam khat bek panin phatna leh samsiatna a paikhia thei mawkmawk hi. Ka u ka naute aw, tua banga hih ding kilawm lo hi.

**ZVS:** Kam khat sung panin thupha leh thusia pusuak khawm hi. Sanggamte aw, hih bang ahi ding hi hetlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** Doth a fountain send forth at the same place sweet water and bitter?

**TDB77:** Ciktui in a vang khat bek sung pan tuilim leh tuikha phul khia khawm thei ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Tuinak khat panin tui lim leh tui limlo hong luangkhia khawm thei ahi hiam?

### v12
**EN:** Can the fig tree, my brethren, bear olive berries? either a vine, figs? so can no fountain both yield salt water and fresh.

**TDB77:** Ka u ka naute aw, theikung in oliv gah a gah thei hiam? Leengkung in theigah a gah thei hiam? Tua mah bangin tui al in tui lim a piangsak thei kei hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, theikung in oliv hong gahthei a, leenggui in theipum hong gahthei ahi hiam? Tui-al in tuilim piangsak ngeilo hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | negation: kei

### v13
**EN:** Who is a wise man and endued with knowledge among you? let him shew out of a good conversation his works with meekness of wisdom.

**TDB77:** Note lakah kua in pilna leh theihna a nei hiam? Tua mi in gamtatna hoihte hihin, pilna, lungnemna tawh ama gamtatnate lak ta hen.

**ZVS:** Note lakah pilin thuthei a kisa a om leh kiniamkhiatin pilin, nuntak hoih leh gamtat hoih tawh a lahkhiat ding ahi hi.

### v14
**EN:** But if ye have bitter envying and strife in your hearts, glory not, and lie not against the truth.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin note lungsim sung uhah hazatna leh kidemna siatna a om leh, thuman thutak tawh kilem loin kisathei kei un.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin na lungsim uhah en'na, gitlohna, leh huaihamna a om leh a zuaugen na hi-te a, pil ing, ci-in kisialhna ding bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** This wisdom descendeth not from above, but is earthly, sensual, devilish.

**TDB77:** Tua bang pilna in vantung pan hong tung pilna ahi kei hi; leitung pilna, pumpi pilna, dawi gilo pilna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua bang pilnate vantung panin a hong pai hilo a, leitung pilna, kha thu tawh a kisailo, a dawivai ahi hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v16
**EN:** For where envying and strife is, there is confusion and every evil work.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, hazatna leh kidem nopna a om leh, gamlumna leh a kihhuai gilo bawlna apawl apawl a piangsak-te hi.

**ZVS:** En'na leh huaihamna a om nak leh buaina leh siatna nam khempeuh tawh kizom pahpah hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v17
**EN:** But the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, and easy to be entreated, full of mercy and good fruits, without partiality, and without hypocrisy.

**TDB77:** Vantung pan hong tung pilna in a masa belin a siangtho hi. A nung ciangin kilemna ahi hi, lungnemna ahi hi, zol baihna ahi hi, hehpihna leh gah pha tawh a dim hi, maitang zahtakna leh simtatna a om kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin tunglam panin a kingah pilna a siangtho ahi hi. Mi tawh kilem thei-in dik a, mi tawh kikhawl thei a, hehpihna lungsim nei-in gamtat hoih a, mi lang-etlo a, kineihkhem cihte omlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** And the fruit of righteousness is sown in peace of them that make peace.

**TDB77:** Kilemna a piangsak thei mite ading dikna thu khaici kilemna tawh kivawhin a om hi.

**ZVS:** Kilemna a piangsak mite in lungnuam takin a tuh khaici in hoihna leh dikna a piangsak hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix
