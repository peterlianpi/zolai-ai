## Verses

### v1
**EN:** As the hart panteth after the water brooks, so panteth my soul after thee, O God.

**TDB77:** Sakhi in luitui luang sialsial a lunggulh bangin, nang kong lunggulh hi, Pasian aw.

**ZVS:** Sakhi in tuiluang sialsial a lunggulh bangin nang ka hong lunggulh hi, ka Pasian aw.

### v2
**EN:** My soul thirsteth for God, for the living God: when shall I come and appear before God?

**TDB77:** A nungta Pasian kong deih hi. Cik ciangin keimah hong pai-in Pasian’ maitang en ding ka hi hiam?

**ZVS:** A nungta Pasian kong deih hi. Cik ciangin kei hong pai-in Pasian' maitang hong mu thei ding ka hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v3
**EN:** My tears have been my meat day and night, while they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?

**TDB77:** Mite in keima tungah, “Na Pasian koi-ah om hiam?” hong cihcih sung teng, sun leh zanin ka khitui an-in ka nei hi.

**ZVS:** Mite in keima tungah, “Na Pasian koi-ah om ahi hiam?” hong cihcih sungteng, sun leh zanin ka khitui an-in ka ne hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v4
**EN:** When I remember these things, I pour out my soul in me: for I had gone with the multitude, I went with them to the house of God, with the voice of joy and praise, with a multitude that kept holyday.

**TDB77:** Ka lungsim sung ka suahkhia a, pawi bawl mi honkhat, kipak a otna leh nuamna la tawh Pasian’ innah ka makaihna, mihon tawh ka paina ka phawk hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim sung ka suahkhia a, pawi bawl mihon khat kipakin otna leh nuamna la tawh Pasian' inn-ah ka makaihna, mihonpi tawh ka paina ka phawkkik hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted in me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him for the help of his countenance.

**TDB77:** Bang hangin khakunkun ka hi hiam, bang hangin lunghimawh ka hi hiam? Pasian’ sungah lametna nei ning; bang hang hiam cih leh kei hong huhpa ka Pasian ka phat kik na ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hangin khakunkun a, lunghimawh niloh ka hi hiam? Pasian' sungah lametna nei ning; bang hang hiam cih leh kei a hong huh ka Pasian ka phatkik ngawn ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** O my God, my soul is cast down within me: therefore will I remember thee from the land of Jordan, and of the Hermonites, from the hill Mizar.

**TDB77:** Ka lungsim tamkham hi; tua ahih manin Jordan leh Hermon gam, Mizar mual tung panin nang kong phawk hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim tamkham ahih manin Jordan leh Hermon gam, Mizar mualtung panin nang ka hong phawk hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v7
**EN:** Deep calleth unto deep at the noise of thy waterspouts: all thy waves and thy billows are gone over me.

**TDB77:** Nangma tuizeu ging tawh tui thukte khat leh khat kidawng a, na tuihual neu leh na tuihual liante in kei hong tuum hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma tuizeu gingte khatlekhat kidawng uh a, dahna tuihualte in kei a hong tuum hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Yet the

**TDB77:** Topa in sunin a itna kip hong sawl hi; zan ciangin nuntakna hong pia Pasian tungah thungetna la ka sa hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in sun tawntungin a itna kip hong lak a, zan hun ciangin nuntakna a hong pia Pasian' tungah thungetna la ka sa hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** I will say unto God my rock, Why hast thou forgotten me? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

**TDB77:** “Bang hangin kei hong mangngilh khin na hi hiam? Galte’ sukcip manin bang hangin kapin ka kidik hiam?” ci-in ka Pasian, ka suangpi tungah ka gen hi.

**ZVS:** “Bang hangin kei hong mangngilh na hi hiam? Galte in hong sukcip manin bang hangin kapin kidikdik ka hi hiam?” ci-in ka Pasian, kei a hong huhpa' tungah ka gen hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** As with a sword in my bones, mine enemies reproach me; while they say daily unto me, Where is thy God?

**TDB77:** Ka langte in kei tungah, “Na Pasian koi-ah om ahi hiam?” hong cihcih sung teng, ka sungah meima nai tawm bangin hong tum ngangngang hi.

**ZVS:** Ka langte in kei tungah, “Na Pasian koi-ah om ahi hiam?” hong cihcih sungteng, ka sungah meima nai tawm bangin hong tum ngangngang hi.

### v11
**EN:** Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God.

**TDB77:** Bang hangin khakunkun ka hi hiam, bang hangin lunghimawh ka hi hiam? Pasian’ sungah lametna nei ning; bang hang hiam cih leh kei hong huhpa ka Pasian ka phatkik na ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hangin khakunkun a, lunghimawh niloh ka hi hiam? Pasian' sungah lametna nei ning; bang hang hiam cih leh kei a hong huh ka Pasian ka phatkik ngawn ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
