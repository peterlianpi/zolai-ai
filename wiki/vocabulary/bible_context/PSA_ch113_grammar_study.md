## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un! Topa’ nasemte aw, phat un, Topa’ min phat un!

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. { Topa' } nasemte aw, phat un. { Topa' } min phat un.

### v2
**EN:** Blessed be the name of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ min in tu-a kipanin a tawntungin thupha ngah ta hen!

**ZVS:** { Topa' } min in tu-a kipanin thupha ngah tawntung ta hen.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** From the rising of the sun unto the going down of the same the

**TDB77:** Nisuahna pan nitumna dongin Topa’ min phat ding ahi hi!

**ZVS:** Nisuahna pan nitumna dongin { Topa' } min phat ding ahi hi.

### v4
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in minam khempeuhte tungah uk a, ama minthan’na in vantung sangin sang zaw hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in minam khempeuh tungah uk a, ama minthan'na vantung sangin sangzaw hi.

### v6
**EN:** Who humbleth himself to behold the things that are in heaven, and in the earth!

**TDB77:** A sangna munah a tu, vantung leh leitung a hong ensuk { Topa, } eite' Pasian pen kua tawh kibang ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, and lifteth the needy out of the dunghill;

**TDB77:** Amah in leivui panin mizawngte lamto-in, ninphual panin mi cimawhte leh a lungkhamte domto a,

**ZVS:** Amah in leivui panin mizawngte laamto-in, ninphual panin mi cimawhte leh a lungkhamte domto a,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** That he may set him with princes, even with the princes of his people.

**TDB77:** uliante tawh kikhawlsak a, ama mite ulian-in a bawl hi.

**ZVS:** uliante tawh kikhawl sakin ama mite' ulianin a bawl hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** He maketh the barren woman to keep house, and to be a joyful mother of children. Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Amah in tasel numei inn guan a, ta a nei nu lungdamin a bawl hi. Topa phat un!

**ZVS:** Amah in tasel numei tungah ta guan' a, ta a nei nu a lungdamin a bawl hi. { Topa } phat un.
