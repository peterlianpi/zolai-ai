## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Awake, awake; put on thy strength, O Zion; put on thy beautiful garments, O Jerusalem, the holy city: for henceforth there shall no more come into thee the uncircumcised and the unclean.

**TDB77:** Zion aw, khanglo in, khanglo in la, na tha khauhsak in; khuapi siangtho Jerusalem aw, na puan hoihte silh in; bang hang hiam cih leh vun ki-at lo mite leh a nin mite nang sungah hong pai nawn lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Zion aw, khanglo in; khanglo in la na tha khauhsak in. Khuapi siangtho Jerusalem aw, na puan hoihte silh in. Bang hang hiam cih leh dawibia mite leh a nin mite nang sungah hong pai nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
