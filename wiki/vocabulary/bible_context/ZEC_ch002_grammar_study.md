## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I lifted up mine eyes again, and looked, and behold a man with a measuring line in his hand.

**TDB77:** Ka muhna dang khat leuleu-ah, a khut sungah tehna khau a tawi mi khat ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Ka muhna dang khat leuleu-ah, a khutsungah tehna khau a tawi mi khat ka mu hi.

### v2
**EN:** Then said I, Whither goest thou? And he said unto me, To measure Jerusalem, to see what is the breadth thereof, and what is the length thereof.

**TDB77:** “Koi-a pai ding na hi hiam?” ci-in ka dong a, amah in “Jerusalem khuapi a sauna leh a zaina ka theih nadingin a teh dingin a pai ding ka hi hi,” hong ci hi.

**ZVS:** Koi-a pai ding na hi hiam, ci-in ka dot ciangin amah in, “Jerusalem khuapi a sauna ka theihna dingin a teh dingin a pai ding ka hi hi,” hong ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** And, behold, the angel that talked with me went forth, and another angel went out to meet him,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin vantung mipa hong paikhia a, vantungmi dang khat in amah na dawntuah hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin vantung mipa hong paikhia a, vantung midang khat in amah na dawntuah hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v4
**EN:** And said unto him, Run, speak to this young man, saying, Jerusalem shall be inhabited as towns without walls for the multitude of men and cattle therein:

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin vantungmi a masapa in a dawntuahpa kiangah, “Va tai in la, tehna khau a tawi tangvalpa kiangah hih thu va gen in, Jerusalem khua sungah mihing leh ganhing tam mahmah ding ahih manin Jerusalem kulh huam sungah ta zo lo ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin vantung mi a masapa in a dawntuahpa kiangah, “Va tai in la, tehna khau a tawi tangvalpa kiangah hih thu va gen in. Jerusalem khuasungah mihing leh ganhing tam mahmah ding ahih manin Jerusalem kulhhuam sungah ta zolo ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | intensifier: very/greatly | future: will | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** For I, saith the

**TDB77:** Tua khuapi a hu dingin meikulh bangin Topa mahmah a om nading, leh tua khua sungah a minthan’na tawh a teen’ nading thu, Topa in kamciam zo hi, ci-in va gen in,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Tua khuapi a hu dingin meikulh bangin { Topa } mahmah a omna ding, leh tua khuasungah a minthan'na tawh a ten'na ding thu, { Topa } in kam ciamzo hi, ci-in va gen in,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v6

**TDB77:** Topa in ama mite kiangah, “Mun khempeuhah note kong thehthang khin hi. “Hong pai un! Hong pai un! Saklam gam panin taikhia un.”

**ZVS:** { Topa } in ama mite kiangah, “Mun khempeuhah note kong thehthang khin hi. Ahi zongin tu-in Babylon panin taikhia-in Jerusalem-ah ciahkik un.

### v8

**ZVS:** Hih bangin maang ka muh khit ciangin, ama mite' na a sutgawp minam dangte kiangah thu a gen dingin Vanglian { Topa } in kei hong sawl a, { Topa' } mite a su mi peuhpeuh in a mit nauta bangin a manphapen khat a su ahi hi.

### v9
**EN:** For, behold, I will shake mine hand upon them, and they shall be a spoil to their servants: and ye shall know that the

**TDB77:** Topa mahmah in note hong su ding a, ni danga note’ na hong sepsak mite in tu-in note’ nate hong lehsuh kik ding uh hi,” hong cisak hi. Hih bangin thu hong pian’ ciangin Vanglianpen Topa in kei hong sawl lam tak na thei ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } mahmah in note hong su ding a, nidanga note' na hong sepsak mite in tu-in note' nate hong lehsuh kik ding uh hi, hong cisak hi. Hih bangin thu hong pian' ciangin Vanglian { Topa } in kei hong sawl ahih lamtak na thei ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

**ZVS:** Tua hun ciangin { Topa } kiangah minam tampi hong pai ding uh a, ama mite a suak ding uh hi. { Topa } in note hong ompih ding a, tua ciangin note kiangah kei a hong sawl ahihna thu na thei ding uh hi.
