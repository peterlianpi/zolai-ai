## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Now the Philistines fought against Israel; and the men of Israel fled from before the Philistines, and fell down slain in mount Gilboa.

**TDB77:** Tu-in Filistiate in Israel mite sim-te hi; Filistiate mai-ah Israel mite tai-te a, Gilboa Mual tungah tuk zeizai-te hi.

**ZVS:** Filistia mite in Israel mite sim-te hi. Filistia mite mai-ah Israel mite tai-te a, Gilboa Mual tungah tuk zeizai-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v2

**TDB77:** Filistiate in Saul leh a tapate pha-te hi; tua ciangin Filistiate in Saul’ tapate Jonathan leh Abinadab leh Malkhishua that-te hi.

**ZVS:** Filistia mite in Saul leh a tapate pha-te hi. Filistia mite in Saul' tapate Jonathan leh Abinadab leh Malkhishua that-te hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | plural: uh marker

### v4

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Saul in a galvan puapa kiangah, “Na namsau dok in la tua tawh kei hong sun let in. Tua hi kei leh hih dawibia mite hong pai-in kei hong khelbawl khading hi,” a ci hi.
