## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation: O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka dikna hong langsak in la, migilote tawh ka thu-ah hong panpih in; mi kheemte leh thutang lote kiang panin kei hong honkhia in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, ka dikna hong langsak in la, migilote tawh ka thu-ah kei hong pan'pih in. Mi khemte leh thutanglote kiang panin kei hong honkhia in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** For thou art the God of my strength: why dost thou cast me off? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh nangmah in kong belh Pasian na hi hi; bang hangin kei hong nilkhia na hi hiam? Galte’ sukcip manin bang hangin kapkap-in ka kidik hiam?

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh ka hong belh Pasian na hi hi. Bang hangin kei hong nilkhia na hi hiam? Galte' sukcip manin bang hangin kapin kidikdik ka hi hiam?

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** O send out thy light and thy truth: let them lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles.

**TDB77:** Na khuavak leh na thuman hong sawl khia in la, na mual siangtho leh na omna-ah kei hong paipih sakin, hong khasak in!

**ZVS:** Na khuavak leh na thuman hong sawlkhia in la, na mual siangtho, na omna-ah kei hong paipih sakin, hong khasak in.

### v4
**EN:** Then will I go unto the altar of God, unto God my exceeding joy: yea, upon the harp will I praise thee, O God my God.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Pasian biakna tau ahi zongin, ka lungdamna Pasian kiangah ahi zongin ka pia dinga, laiza khausai lasakna tawh nang kong phat ding hi, aw Pasian, ka Pasian aw.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin na biakna tau-ah ahi a, ka lung a hong damsak Pasian kiangah ahi zongin ka pai ding a, laia khausai lasakna tawh nang ka hong phat ding hi, aw Pasian, ka Pasian aw.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God.

**TDB77:** Bang hangin khakunkun ka hi hiam, bang hangin lunghimawh ka hi hiam? Pasian sungah lametna nei ning; bang hang hiam cih leh kei hong huhpa ka Pasian ka phatkik na ding hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hangin khakunkun a, lunghimawh niloh ka hi hiam? Pasian sungah lametna nei ning; bang hang hiam cih leh kei a hong huh ka Pasian ka phatkik ngawn ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
