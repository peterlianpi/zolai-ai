## Verses

### v1
**EN:** And he said also unto his disciples,

**TDB77:** Khatvei Zeisu in a nungzuite kiangah, “Sumhaupa khat in sumkempa khat a nei hi. Tua sumkempa in a topa sum a thangbawl thu midangte in topa kiangah a ko uh hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Jesuh in a nungzuite kiangah, “Sum haupa khat om a, a neihsateng a uk dingin ama khutnuai-ah mi khat a koih hi. Tua a neihsateng a uk dingin a koih mipa in a topa' sumteng phengzat gawp hi, ci-in kikhia hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** And the Pharisees also, who were covetous, heard all these things: and they derided him.

**TDB77:** Dangka a deihgawh Farisaite in hih thu khempeuh a zak uh ciangin Zeisu a zahko uh hi.

**ZVS:** Sum a deih phadiak Farisi mite in hih thute a zak uh ciangin Jesuh a nuihsan uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** And he said unto them,

**TDB77:** Zeisu in, “Note in mite mai-ah phatna na lak nuam uh hi. Ahih hangin Pasian in note lungsim uh a thei hi. Bang hang hiam na cih uh leh, mite phatna in Pasian mai-ah kihhuaina ahi hi.”

**ZVS:** Jesuh in amaute kiangah, “Mite in note a man penin hong ngaihsut theihna dingin na om uh a, ahi zongin Pasian in na lungsimtawng uh a hong thei hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh mihingte in a manpha mahmahin a ngaihsutte pen Pasian in bangmahin simlo hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
