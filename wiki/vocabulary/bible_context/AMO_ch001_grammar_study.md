## Verses

### v1
**EN:** The words of Amos, who was among the herdmen of Tekoa, which he saw concerning Israel in the days of Uzziah king of Judah, and in the days of Jeroboam the son of Joash king of Israel, two years before the earthquake.

**TDB77:** Tekoa khua-a teeng tuucingte lakah Amos a kici mi khat om hi. Uzziah in Judah kumpi hi-in, Joash’ tapa Jeroboam in Israel kumpi a sep laitak, zinlin’ ma kumnih hun lai takin, Pasian in Amos tungah hih thute kilangsak hi.

**ZVS:** Tekoa khua-a a teng tuucingte lakah Amos a kici mi khat om hi. Uzziah pen Judah kumpi hi-in Joash' tapa Jeroboam in Israel kumpi a sep laitak, zinlin' ma kum nih hun laitakin Pasian in Amos tungah hih thute kilangsak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** And he said, The

**TDB77:** Amos in, “Zion Mual panin Topa hong hawksuk a, Jerusalem khua panin hong awngkek hi. Tua ahih manin, lonona gam teng keugawp a, Karmel mual dawn dum dide teng kausan ta hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Amos in, “Zion Mual panin { Topa } hong hawksuk a, Jerusalem khua panin hong awngkek hi. Tua ahih manin lonona gamteng keugawp a, Karmel mualdawn dum dide teng kausan ta hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | causal: because

### v3
**EN:** Thus saith the

**TDB77:** Topa in hih bangin ci hi: Damaskas khuamite a khialh kikkik nilohna hangun, pel loin amaute daan ka tat ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute in, angawina suang tawh gawizan bangin, Gilead gam mite gawi gawp uh hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in hih bangin ci hi: Damaskas khuamite thumvei a khialh khit, a liveina hangin pelloin amaute gim ka pia ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh amaute in an gawina suang tawh gawizan bangin, Gilead gammite gawigawp uh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
