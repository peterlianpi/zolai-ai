## Verses

### v2
**EN:** He that walketh uprightly, and worketh righteousness, and speaketh the truth in his heart.

**TDB77:** Paubang loin gamta-in, a manin na sem a, lungsim tak a gen mi,

**ZVS:** Paubaang loin gamta-in, a manin na sem a, lungsim tak a gen mi,

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | causal: because

### v3
**EN:** He that backbiteth not with his tongue, nor doeth evil to his neighbour, nor taketh up a reproach against his neighbour.

**TDB77:** a leii tawh gensia loin, a lawmte tungah siatna bawl lo a, a vengte a mawhsak lo mi,

**ZVS:** mi gensia loin, a lawmte tungah siatna bawllo a, a vengte a mawhsaklo mi,

### v4
**EN:** In whose eyes a vile person is contemned; but he honoureth them that fear the

**TDB77:** a mit in ginalote thusim lo a, ahi zongin Topa a zahtakte a pahtawi mi; ama nat nading nangawn-ah a ciampel lo mi,

**ZVS:** migilote thusimlo a, ahi zongin { Topa } a zahtakte a pahtawi mi, ama natna ding nangawnah a ciampello mi,

### v5
**EN:** He that putteth not out his money to usury, nor taketh reward against the innocent. He that doeth these things shall never be moved.

**TDB77:** a meet-in ama sum kha lo a, thutangte’ lang ah golhguk a la lo mi ahi hi. Hihte khempeuh a hih mi pen liing ngei lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** ama sum a meetin a khalo, thutang mite a langpan'lo, golhguk a lalo mite teng ding uh hi. Hih bangin a gamtate pen ling ngeilo ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will
