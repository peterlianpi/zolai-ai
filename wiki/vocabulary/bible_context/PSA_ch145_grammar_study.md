## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I will extol thee, my God, O king; and I will bless thy name for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** Ka Pasian leh ka Kumpi aw, nangmah hong pahtawi-in a tawntungin nangma min ka phatphat ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ka Pasian leh ka Kumpipa aw, nangmah hong pahtawi-in a tawntungin nangma min kong phatphat ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v2
**EN:** Every day will I bless thee; and I will praise thy name for ever and ever.

**TDB77:** Ni simin nangmah hong pahtawi-in, a tawntungin nangma min ka phatphat ding hi.

**ZVS:** Nisimin hong pahtawi-in a tawntungin nangma min kong phatphat ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v3
**EN:** Great is the

**TDB77:** Topa lian hi, nakpi tak-a phat ding hi a, ama liatna ngaihsut zawh ding hi lo hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } lian hi; nakpi takin phat ding hi a, ama liatna ngaihsutzawh ding hilo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: lo

### v4
**EN:** One generation shall praise thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts.

**TDB77:** A khang a khangin nangma nasepnate kiphatphat ding a, a vanglian na bawl nate kigengen ding hi.

**ZVS:** A khang a khangin nangma nasepnate kiphatphat ding a, a vanglian na bawlnate kigengen ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** I will speak of the glorious honour of thy majesty, and of thy wondrous works.

**TDB77:** Nangma liatna leh na minthan’na a lamdang nangma sepnate ka lungngaingai ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na liatna leh na minthan'na a lamdang nangma sepnate ka lungngaingai ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v6
**EN:** And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts: and I will declare thy greatness.

**TDB77:** Mite in a vanglian nangma bawlnate taangko ding a, keimah in nangma liatna ka pulak ding hi.

**ZVS:** A vanglian nangma bawlnate mite in taangko ding uh a, nangma vangliatna mah kei zong ka pulak ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v7
**EN:** They shall abundantly utter the memory of thy great goodness, and shall sing of thy righteousness.

**TDB77:** Amaute in nangma liatna na minthan’na genkhia-in, nangma thumanna nakpi-in sa ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in nangma liatna, na minthan'na genkhia-in nangma thumanna nakpi takin sa ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v8
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa dikin hehpihna nei a, hehhakin itna kip lianpi nei hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } dikin hehpihna nei a, heh hak mahmahin itna kip lianpi a nei hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9

**ZVS:** { Topa } pen mi khempeuh tungah hoih a, a bawlsa khempeuh tungah hehpihna a nei hi.

### v10
**EN:** All thy works shall praise thee, O

### v12

### v13

**TDB77:** Na Kumpigam pen a tawntunga om kumpigam hi a, na ukna in khang tawntungin a kip hi. Topa pen ama thute khempeuh ah thutak a, a gamtatna khempeuhah hehpihna nei hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma kumpi gam pen a kip tawntung kumpi gam hi a, nangma ukna khang tawntungin a kip hi. A thuciamna khempeuhah { Topa } thutak mahmah a, a gamtatna khempeuhah zong hehpihna a nei ahi hi.
