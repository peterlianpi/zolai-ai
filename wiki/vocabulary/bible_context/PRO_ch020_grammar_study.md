### v1
**EN:** Wine is a mocker, strong drink is raging: and whosoever is deceived thereby is not wise.

**TDB77:** Leenggahzu in kam tam sakin, khamtheih zu in kitawngsak a, zu khamna in haina ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Leenggahzu in mi kamtam sakin khamtheihzu in mi kitawngsak a, zukhamna pen haina ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2

**TDB77:** Kumpipa’ hehna in a lauhuai humpinelkai hawk bang hi a, kumpipa a hehsak khate in sih lawh uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker
