### v3
**EN:** In the mouth of the foolish is a rod of pride: but the lips of the wise shall preserve them.

**TDB77:** Mihaite a pau ciangin satna thuaklawh-te a, mipilte’ pauna in sat nading pan pengsak hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite a pau ciangin siatna thuaklawh-te a, mipilte' pauna in siatna ding pan pengsak hi.

### v4
**EN:** Where no oxen are, the crib is clean: but much increase is by the strength of the ox.

**TDB77:** Bawngtal om kei leh lo panin an tawm bek kila a, bawngtal’ tha tawh an kihau hi.

**ZVS:** Bawngtal om kei leh lo panin an tawm bek kila a, bawngtal tha tawh an a kihau ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** A faithful witness will not lie: but a false witness will utter lies.

**TDB77:** A citak teci in thuman bekbek gen tawntung a, a gina lo teci in zuauthu bekbek gen tawntung hi.

**ZVS:** A citak teci in thuman bekbek gen tawntung a, a ginalo teci in zuau bekbek gen tawntung hi.

### v6
**EN:** A scorner seeketh wisdom, and findeth it not: but knowledge is easy unto him that understandeth.

**TDB77:** Mi a simmawh theite in pilna a zon’zon hangin ngah zo lo a, thu ngaihsun theite in olno takin ngah thei-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mi a simmawh theite in pilna a zon' uh hangin ngah zolo-te a, thungaihsun theite in olno takin ngah thei-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v7

**TDB77:** Mihaite tawh kikhawl kei in, amau’ kiang pan pilna na ngah kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite tawh kikhawl kei in; amau' kiang pan pilna na ngah kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8

**ZVS:** Thungaihsun theite a pilna in lampi man musak a, mihaite a haina in lam khialsak hi.

### v9

**TDB77:** Mihaite in a khialh hangun phamawh sa lo-te a, mipilte a khialh ciangun mawh maisak ding deih-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite in a khialh hangun phamawh salo-te a, mipilte a khialh ciangun mawhmaisak ding deih-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v10

**TDB77:** Lungdamna leh lungkhamna eimah bek in i theih hi a, midang kuamah in hong phawkpih kha lo hi.

**ZVS:** Lungdamna leh lungkhamna eimah bek in i theih hi a, midang kuamah in hong phawkpih khalo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v11

**ZVS:** Migilote' inn kisusia ding a, thuman mite' inn kipin gina semsem ding hi.

### v12

**ZVS:** Mihingte in thuman lampi hi, ci-in a ngaihsutte pen sihna lampi hikha thei hi.

### v13

**TDB77:** Nuihna in cihmawh seelna zong hi thei a, kigualnop sakna a ven’ciangin dahna hong kilang kik hi.

**ZVS:** Nuihna pen cihmawh selna zong hithei a, kigualnop sakna a ven' ciangin dahna hong kilangkik hi.

### v14

**TDB77:** Misia in siatna thaman ngah a, mipha in phatna thaman ngah hi.

**ZVS:** Mihaite in a thuzak peuh um pahpah a, mipilte in a gamtatna ding ngaihsun cian hi.

### v16

**TDB77:** Mipilte in Topa zahtakin siatna panin kihem a, mihaite in thu ngaihsun masa loin gamta pahpah hi.

**ZVS:** Mipilte kidawmin siatna panin kihem a, mihaite in thu ngaihsun masa loin gamta pahpah hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

**ZVS:** Na a hoih a semte mite in muangin zahtak a, a sia gamtatna pen khialhna lianpi ahi hi.
