## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Truly my soul waiteth upon God: from him cometh my salvation.

**TDB77:** Dai dide-in Pasian bek ka ngak hi; keima kihotkhiatna ama tung pan hong pai hi.

**ZVS:** Dai dide-in Pasian bek ka ngak a, ama hong hotkhiat ding muangin ka om hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** He only is my rock and my salvation; he is my defence; I shall not be greatly moved.

**TDB77:** Ka suangpi, ka kihotkhiatna, ka munkip muan’huai in amah bek hi a, kei ka kinak lin' kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong huhpa leh hong honpa amah bek hi a, ka guallel ngei kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** How long will ye imagine mischief against a man? ye shall be slain all of you: as a bowing wall shall ye be, and as a tottering fence.

**TDB77:** Note khempeuh in, a hoi kawm, a kiliing gawl tawh a kibang mi khat, susia dingin bang tanvei hih huan ding na hi-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Note khempeuh in kawm a hoi tawh a kibang, gawl bangin a kiling mi khat a susia dingin bang tanvei hih huan nilohin, susia ding na hi-te hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** They only consult to cast him down from his excellency: they delight in lies: they bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah.

**TDB77:** Ama za-liatna panin sukniam ding bek amaute in sawm-te hi. Amaute in thuman lo-ah nuam sa-te hi. Amaute in a kam-te tawh phat-te a, ahih hangin a sungtawng uh-ah samsiat-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amah a zaliatna panin sukniam ding bekbek amaute in a sawm-te hi. Amaute in thuman lo-ah nuamsa mahmah-te hi. Amaute in a kampi-te tawh phat khem-te a, ahih hangin a sungtawng uhah samsiat-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** My soul, wait thou only upon God; for my expectation is from him.

**TDB77:** Ka lametna ama tung pan ahih manin, dai takin Pasian bek ka ngak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah bek ka muan' ahih manin, daitakin Pasian bek ka ngak hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v6
**EN:** He only is my rock and my salvation: he is my defence; I shall not be moved.

**TDB77:** Ka suangpi, ka kihotkhiatna, ka munkip muan’huai in amah bek hi a, kei ka kiling kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong hupa leh hong honpa amah bek mah hi a, ka guallel ngei kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** In God is my salvation and my glory: the rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.

**TDB77:** Keima suahtakna leh ka minphatna Pasian’ hang ahi hi; a muan’huai ka suangpi, ka bukna in Pasian ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Keima suahtakna leh ka minphatna Pasian' hang ahi hi. A muan'huai ka belhpa, ka bukna Pasian ahi hi.

### v8
**EN:** Trust in him at all times; ye people, pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. Selah.

**TDB77:** Mite aw, amah muang tawntung un; na lungkhamna teng-te ama tungah gen un; Pasian in eite’ bukna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Mite aw, amah muang tawntung un la, na lungkhamna teng-te ama tungah gen un. Pasian, eite' bukna ahi hi.

### v9
**EN:** Surely men of low degree are vanity, and men of high degree are a lie: to be laid in the balance, they are altogether lighter than vanity.

**TDB77:** Mineute ahih leh husan’ bang bek hi a, miliante pen a hawmpi ahi hi; tawikhaina-ah amaute in lek lo-te hi; amaute a kigawmin husan’ khat sangin zangzaw-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mihingte ahih leh husan' bang bek hi a, a lian a neu kibang lel hi. Tawi a kikhai ciangin amaute in leeklo-te a, amaute a kigawmin husan' sangin zangzaw hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** Trust not in oppression, and become not vain in robbery: if riches increase, set not your heart upon them.

**TDB77:** Zawhthawh thu tawh lakna muan’in, nei kei in la, suhgawpnate lamet-in nei kei in; na hauhna a khan’ leh, tuate muan’ in nei kei in.

**ZVS:** Zawhthawh thu tawh ngahna muan'in nei kei in la, suhgawpnate lametin nei kei in. Na hauhna a khan' leh, tuate muan'in nei kei in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** God hath spoken once; twice have I heard this; that power belongeth unto God.

**TDB77:** Pasian in khatvei thu gen khin hi, ke’n hih thu nihvei za khin ing; vangliatna in Pasian’ neihsa ahi hi;

**ZVS:** Vangliatna pen keima-a' hi, ci-in Pasian in a genna ka zak ngeingeisa ahi hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v12
**EN:** Also unto thee, O Lord, belongeth mercy: for thou renderest to every man according to his work.

**TDB77:** Topa aw, itna kip in nangma aa hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh mite ama sepna bangin na thukkik hi.

**ZVS:** Topa aw, itna kip nangma-a' ahi hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh a sepna bang ciatin mite na thuk hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
