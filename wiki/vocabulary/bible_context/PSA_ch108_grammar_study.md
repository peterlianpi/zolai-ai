# Psalms Chapter 108 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 13 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| future: will | 4 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 3 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |
| completed: already done | 2 |
| negation: kei | 1 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, ka lungsim a kigingsa-in om hi! Ka sa ding hi, phatna late ka sa ding hi! Kei ka tho ding hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, ka lungsim a kigingsa-in om hi. Ka sa ding hi; nangmah phatna late ka sa ding hi. Kei ka khanglo ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake early.

**TDB77:** Harp lasakna leh laiza lasakna aw, tho un! Zingsang khuavak hong phong ning!

**ZVS:** Harp lasakna leh laia lasakna aw, khanglo un. Zingsang khuavak hong phong ning.

### v3
**EN:** I will praise thee, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, mite lakah nang tung lungdamna kong pia ding a, minam dang lakah nangma tungah phatna ka sa ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, mite lakah nangma tungah lungdamna kong ko ding a, minam dangte lakah nangma tungah phatna ka sa ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v4
**EN:** For thy mercy is great above the heavens: and thy truth reacheth unto the clouds.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh na itna kip in vantung ban a, na thumanna in meiipite banto hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth;

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, vantungte’ tungah sangto lai in! Leitung khempeuhah na minthan’na om sak in!

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, vantungte' tungah sangto lai in. Leitung khempeuhah na minthan'na omsak in.

### v6
**EN:** That thy beloved may be delivered: save with thy right hand, and answer me.

**TDB77:** Nangma it mite, nangma hatna tawh kote hong hon in la, kong ngetna hong pia in!

**ZVS:** Nangma it mite, na hatna tawh hon in la, kong ngetna bangun hong pia in.

### v7
**EN:** God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

**TDB77:** Ama biakinn sungah Pasian in thuciam khin hi: “Gualzo-a lungdamna tawh Shekhem gam khenkhia-in Sukkot gamkuam ka sehkhia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ama biakinn sungah Pasian in thu ciam khin a, “Gualzo-a lungdamna tawh Shekhem gam khenkhia-in, Sukkoth gam kuam ka sehkhia ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | completed: already done | future: will

### v8
**EN:** Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver;

**TDB77:** Gilead gam in keima aa hi a, Manasseh gam in keima aa hi; Efraim gam in ka sik lukhu hi a, Judah gam in ka ciangkhut hi.

**ZVS:** Gilead gam keima-a' hi a, Manasseh gam zong keima-a' mah ahi hi. Efraim gam ka siklukhu hi a, Judah gam ka ciangkhut ahi hi.

### v9
**EN:** Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph.

**TDB77:** Moab gam in ka kisilna kuang hi a, Edom gam tungah ka khedap ka lawn hi; Filistia gam tungah gualzawhna tawh ka awng hi.

**ZVS:** Moab gam ka kisilna kuang hi a, keima neihsa hi, cih lahna limin Edom gam tungah ka khedap ka lawn hi. Filistia gam tungah gualzawhna tawh ka awng hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?

**TDB77:** Kua in khuapi muanhuai-ah kei hong paipih ding hiam? Kua in Edom gamah kei hong paipih ding hiam?

**ZVS:** Kua in khuapi muan'huai-ah kei hong paipih ding hiam? Kua in Edom gamah kei hong paipih ding hiam?

### v11
**EN:** Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, nangmah in kote hong don lo khin hi lo na hi hiam? Pasian aw, komau’ galkapte tawh na kuankhia kei hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, nangmah in kote hong donlo khinta hilo na hi hiam? Pasian aw, komau galkapte tawh na kuankhia kei hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | negation: kei

### v12
**EN:** Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.

**TDB77:** Ka galdona uh-ah huhna kote hong pia in, bang hang hiam cih leh mihing’ huhna a mawkna hi!

**ZVS:** Ka galdona uhah kote hong huh in. Bang hang hiam cih leh mihing' huhna a mawkna hi.

### v13
**EN:** Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.

**TDB77:** Pasian tawh khauh takin i hih ding hi; amah in i galte hong sikcipsak dingpa ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian tawh khauhtakin i hih ding hi. Amah pen i galte hong sikcipsak dingpa ahi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
