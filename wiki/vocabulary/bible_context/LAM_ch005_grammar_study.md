## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Remember, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kote tunga, thupiangte hong phawk in. Hong en in la, ka maizumna-te hong mu in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kote tunga thupiangte hong phawk in. Hong en in la, ka maizumna-te hong mu in.

### v2
**EN:** Our inheritance is turned to strangers, our houses to aliens.

**TDB77:** Ka neihsa teng-te gamdang mite’ khut sung tung khin a, ka innsung uhah gamdang mite a teeng-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ka neihsa teng-te gamdang mite' khutsungah tung mangkhin a, ka innsung uhah gamdang mite teng ta-te hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v3
**EN:** We are orphans and fatherless, our mothers are as widows.

**TDB77:** Ka pate-te gal in hong thahsak khin a, ka nute-te a meigong khin-te hi.

**ZVS:** Ka pate-te gal in hong thahsak khin a, ka nute-te meigong suak khin-te hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | completed: already done | plural: uh marker

### v4

**TDB77:** Ka tuidawn ding-te lei ding kul a, ka sing cih ding-te lei ding a kul hi.

**ZVS:** Ka tuidawn tawmding-te lei ding kul ta a, ka sing cihtawm ding-te lei ding kul ta hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** Our necks are under persecution: we labour, and have no rest.

**TDB77:** Kalaoh van pua leh la van pua bangin nakpi takin a hong kihawl gawpgawp hi. Gim mahmah hi napi, tawlngak na’ng hun a hong kipia kei hi.

**ZVS:** Kalaoh van pua leh la van pua bangin nakpi takin a hong kihawl gawpgawp hi. Gim mahmah hinapi tawlngakna ding hun a hong kipia kei hi.
