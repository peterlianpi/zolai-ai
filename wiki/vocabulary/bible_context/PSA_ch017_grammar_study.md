## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Hear the right, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, thuman kong ngetna hong za in la, ka otna aw hong ngai in! Zuau baang lo muk pana ka thugetna hong ngai in!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, thuman ka hong ngetna hong za in la, ka otna hong ngai in! Lungsim takpi tawh ka hong thungetna hong ngai in!

### v2
**EN:** Let my sentence come forth from thy presence; let thine eyes behold the things that are equal.

**TDB77:** Ka tan’na nangma kiang panin hong kilangsak ta in! Na mitte thuman musak ta in!

**ZVS:** Thuman mu na hih manin ka dikna hong kilangsak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Thou hast proved mine heart; thou hast visited me in the night; thou hast tried me, and shalt find nothing; I am purposed that my mouth shall not transgress.

**TDB77:** Keima lungsim hong sin in la, zan-in nangmah ka kiangah hong hawh in la, keimah hong ze-et lecin, ka sungah gitlohna na mu kei ding hi; ka kam in khial lo hi.

**ZVS:** Keima lungsim hong sin in la, zan hun sungin ka kiangah hong hawh in la, keimah hong ze-et lecin, ka sungah gitlohna na mu kei ding hi. Ka kampau-in zong bangmah ka khial kei hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Concerning the works of men, by the word of thy lips I have kept me from the paths of the destroyer.

**TDB77:** Mite’ gamtatna tawh kisai-in ci leng, nangma muk pan-a hong pai thu tawh mi ngongtatte’ lampi ka pelh kim hi.

**ZVS:** Gamtatna thu-ah ci leng, nangma thupiaknate ka zui a, mi ngongtatte' gamtat ziate ka pelh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** Hold up my goings in thy paths, that my footsteps slip not.

**TDB77:** Ka kalsuannate in nangma lampi-ah kip khin a, ka khe taal lo hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma lampi zui-in ka pai a, na lam ka pial ngei kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** I have called upon thee, for thou wilt hear me, O God: incline thine ear unto me, and hear my speech.

**TDB77:** Pasian aw, hong dawng ding na hih manin nang kong sam hi; na bil hong ngat in la ka thu hong za in.

**ZVS:** Pasian aw, hong dawng ding na hih manin nang kong sam hi. Na bil hong ngat in la, ka thu hong za in.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v7
**EN:** Shew thy marvellous lovingkindness, O thou that savest by thy right hand them which put their trust in thee from those that rise up against them.

**TDB77:** Amau’ langpangte’ kiang panin na taklam khut hong belte’ Honpa aw, na itna kip lamdang takin hong lak in.

**ZVS:** Amau' galte' kiang panin nang hong belte a honpa aw, na itna kip lamdang takin hong lak in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** From the wicked that oppress me, from my deadly enemies, who compass me about.

**TDB77:** Kei a hong sugawp migilo, kei a hong umcih a kihtakhuai mahmah ka galte kiang panin mitnauta bangin hong kem in la, na kha liimah hong seel in.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** They are inclosed in their own fat: with their mouth they speak proudly.

**TDB77:** Amaute in hehpihna lungsim nei lo uh a, a kam uh tawh amaute kiphatsak pau uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in hehpihna lungsim bangmah neilo uh a, a kam uh tawh amaute kiphatsak pau uh hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v11
**EN:** They have now compassed us in our steps: they have set their eyes bowing down to the earth;

**TDB77:** Amaute in kei hong sulzuih uh a, tu-in amaute in kei hong umcihin, keimah leilak den’ nadingin hong en uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in kei hong sulzuih uh a, tu-in kei hong umcihin, keimah leilak den'na dingin a hong en uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** Like as a lion that is greedy of his prey, and as it were a young lion lurking in secret places.

**TDB77:** Amaute in a balnen nuam humpinelkai tawh kibang uh a, humpinelkai khanglai sa-pang tawh kibang uh hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute pen a balnen nuam humpinelkai tawh kibang uh a, humpinelkai khanglai sabuk tawh kibang uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v14
**EN:** From men which are thy hand, O

**TDB77:** { Topa } aw, tho in! Amaute maiphut in la, thalpaih in! { Topa } aw, a deih khempeuh uh hih leitungah a nei migilote kiang panin na khut leh na namsau tawh kei hong honkhia in. Amau-a' dingin na koihcing gimnate thuaksak in la, a tate un zong a cingvalin thuak uh hen la, a naungekte uh-a' ding nangawn nusiat lai ta uh hen.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** As for me, I will behold thy face in righteousness: I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with thy likeness.

**TDB77:** Kei ka hih leh, thumanna tawh na maitang ka en ding a, ka khanlawh ciangin, nangma mel muhna tawh ka lungkim ding hi.

**ZVS:** Kei ka hih leh, thumanna tawh na maitang ka en ding a, ka khan'lawh ciangin nangma mel muhna tawh ka lungkim ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will
