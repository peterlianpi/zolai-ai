## Verses

### v1
**EN:** At this also my heart trembleth, and is moved out of his place.

**TDB77:** “Hih huihpi in ka dip hong kuasak a, ka dip van-a khai hong bangsak hi.

**ZVS:** “Hih huihpi in ka dip hong kuasak a, ka dip vana khai hong bangsak hi.

### v2
**EN:** Hear attentively the noise of his voice, and the sound that goeth out of his mouth.

**TDB77:** Ama kam panin hong gingkhia hih vanging, Pasian’ pauna awging ngai un.

**ZVS:** Ama kam panin a hong gingkhia hih vanging Pasian' pauna awging, limtakin ngai in.

### v3
**EN:** He directeth it under the whole heaven, and his lightning unto the ends of the earth.

**TDB77:** Amah in van-ah khua phiasak a, leimong khat pan mongkhat dong taan’ hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in vanah khua phiasak a, leimong khat pan mong khat dong a taan' hi.

### v4
**EN:** After it a voice roareth: he thundereth with the voice of his excellency; and he will not stay them when his voice is heard.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin a vanglian vanging ahi, ama awging hong kiza a, tua sung mahin khua phiaphia hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin a vanglian vanging, ama awging hong kiza a, tua hun sungteng mahin khua a phiaphia hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** God thundereth marvellously with his voice; great things doeth he, which we cannot comprehend.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ sawlna tawh na lamdang pipi piang a, eite’ theih zawh loh thute hong piang hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' sawlna tawh na lamdangpipi piang a, eite' theih zawhloh thute a hong piang hi.

### v6
**EN:** For he saith to the snow, Be thou on the earth; likewise to the small rain, and to the great rain of his strength.

**TDB77:** Amah in leitungah vuk a khia dingin sawl a, guah tampi tak a zu sak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in leitungah vuk a kia dingin sawl a, guah tampi tak a zusak hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v7
**EN:** He sealeth up the hand of every man; that all men may know his work.

**TDB77:** Amah in mihingte’ nasep khawlsak a, amah a gamtaang ahihna lak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in mihingte a nasep uh khawlsak a, amah a gamtang ahihna a kilangsak hi.

### v8

**TDB77:** Gamsate in a lupna ngei delh uh a, a kua sungah bucip uh hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v9

**TDB77:** Leitaw lam pan guahpi hong zu a, leilu lam pan huihpi hong nung hi.

**ZVS:** Leitaw lam panin guahpi hong zu a, leilu lam panin huihpi hong nung hi.

### v10

**TDB77:** Pasian’ husan’na in tui khalsak a, tui lianpite khalin sik bangin sak sak hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' husan'na in tuite khalsak a, tui lianpite khalin sik bangin a sak hi.

### v11
**EN:** Also by watering he wearieth the thick cloud: he scattereth his bright cloud:

**TDB77:** Amah in meiipi nomsak a, meiipi-in khua a phiasak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in meiipi nomsak a, meiipi in khua a phiasak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v12

**TDB77:** Pasian’ deihna zui-in kikhin kawikawi hi. Leitung mun khempeuhah pai- in, Pasian’ sawl teng sem uh hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' deihna zui-in a kikhin kawikawi hi. Leitung mun khempeuhah pai-in, Pasian' sawl teng a sem uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v13

**TDB77:** Pasian in mihingte daantah nading ahi a, a pahtakna a lah nading ahi zongin, leitungah tu-a bangin a piangsak hi.
