## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Help,

**TDB77:** Topa aw, hong huh in, bang hang hiam cih leh mihing’ tate lak panin mi thumante mangthang a, mipha khat beek om nawn lo hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, hong huh in; bang hang hiam cih leh mihingte lak panin mi thumante maimang ta a, mipha khat zong om nawnlo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo

### v2
**EN:** They speak vanity every one with his neighbour: with flattering lips and with a double heart do they speak.

**TDB77:** Mi kim in a vengte kiangah zuau thu gen ciat a, phat khemna kamzol leh lungsim nam nih tawh pau-te hi.

**ZVS:** Mi kim in a vengte kiangah zuauthu gen ciat a, phat khemna kam zol leh lungsim nam nih tawh thu a gen-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail; our lips are our own: who is lord over us?

**TDB77:** “I Leii tawh gualzo ding, i mukte eima neihsa; i to kua hiam?” a ci vial, kiphatsakna a piangsak leii, phat khemna muk khempeuh, { Topa } in atkhia ta hen.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** For the oppression of the poor, for the sighing of the needy, now will I arise, saith the

**TDB77:** Topa in, “Mizawng mi a neihsa kihawkkhia a, mi cimawh mi thuum ahih ciangin, tu’n ding-in, ama lunggulh lungmuan’na munah amah ka koih ding hi,” ci hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in, “Mizawngte a neihsa kihawkkhia a, mi cimawhte thumin tau-te ahih ciangin tu-in dingin, amau' lunggulh, lungmuan'na munah amaute ka koih ding hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** The words of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ thuciamnate in a siangtho thuciamna hi a, meikhuk sunga sagih vei a kisiangsak ngun tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } thuciamnate, a siangtho thuciamna hi a, meikhuk sungah sagihvei a kisiangsak ngun tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** Thou shalt keep them, O

**TDB77:** Topa aw, kote hong dal in la, tu khang mite kiang panin hong kem in.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, kote hong dal in la, tukhang mite kiang panin kote hong kem in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** The wicked walk on every side, when the vilest men are exalted.

**TDB77:** Mihing’ tate lakah siatna a kipahtawi ciangin mun khempeuhah migilote vak kawikawi hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh mihingte lakah siatna kipahtawi a, mun khempeuhah migilote kidimin vak kawikawi-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
