## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Thou therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin ka ta aw, nangmah in, Khazih Zeisu sungah a om hehpihna thupha tawh thahatin om in.

**ZVS:** Ka ta aw, nang na hih leh Khrih Jesuh tawh i kipawl manin i ngah hehpihna hangin thahatin om in.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also.

**TDB77:** Teci tampi mai-ah keima kiang pan na zaksa nate, dikna tawh dimin midangte a hilhsawn thei ding mite’ tungah na ap in.

**ZVS:** Mi tampite' theihin ka thuhilh, na zaksa thu khempeuh, midangte a hilhsawn thei dingin a citak mite tungah hoihtakin a zuih dingun na hilh in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3

**TDB77:** Nang in Khazih Zeisu’ galkap siampa bangin keimah tawh gimna na thuak khawm in.

**ZVS:** Khrih Jesuh' galkap hoih hihna tawh thuakdingin a kisam khempeuh na na thuak in.

### v4

**ZVS:** Kimawl kidemna-ah a kihel mi khatpeuh in kimawlna ngeina zui lopi-in gualzawh pahtawina ngah theilo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6

### v7

**ZVS:** Na mite thu kinialin a kitotlohna dingun Pasian' mai-ah thupi takin na hilhkhol in. Tua bang thu kinialna in a za mite phattuamna ngahsak loin a siasak bek ahi hi.
