**TDB77:** Obadiah gen kholhna: Edom mite gam tawh kisai-in Topa Pasian in hih bangin a gen hi. Minamte lakah kamtai khat kisawl zo a, “Kithawi un! Edom mite do dingin kuankhia ni” a cihna thu ka za zo hi.
