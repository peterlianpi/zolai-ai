## Verses

### v1
**EN:** I charge thee therefore before God, and the Lord Jesus Christ, who shall judge the quick and the dead at his appearing and his kingdom;

**TDB77:** Pasian mai-ah ahi zongin, a nungta mi leh mi si tungah thu a khen ding Khazih Zeisu mai-ah ahi zongin, Zeisu hong kilat nading leh ama kumpigam suangin thu ka hong piakna in:

**ZVS:** Pasian' mai, leh a hingte hita leh a site hita leh mi khempeuh' thu a khending Khrih Jesuh' mai-ah nakpi takin ka hong hanthawn hi. Amah Kumpi-in hong paikikin ukna hong kipan ding ahih manin,

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine.

**TDB77:** Pasian thu hilh in, a hun a om zongin a om kei zongin hahkatin mawhna lakin, taai in; lungnemna khempeuh tawh thu hilhin hansuah in.

**ZVS:** hunhoih hunsia cih khen tuanloin Pasian' thu hilh ding na kin ngaihsutna ding, lungduaina tawh mite' lungsim zo-in, mite taii-in, hanthawnin na hilhna dingin thu a hong pia ka hi hi.

### v3
**EN:** For the time will come when they will not endure sound doctrine; but after their own lusts shall they heap to themselves teachers, having itching ears;

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, mite in thu hilhna pha mang loin, a bil uh thakin, amau deihgawhna bang thuhilh a tampi, amau ading a koih ding uh a,

**ZVS:** Mite in thuman a ngaih noploh hun, amaute' deihna bek a zuihnop hun, amaute' zaknop thute bek a gen ding sia a cial thuahthuah hun hong tung ding hi.

**Grammar:** plural: uh marker

### v4
**EN:** And they shall turn away their ears from the truth, and shall be turned unto fables.

**TDB77:** thuman thutak ngai loin, tangthu ngai-in a paikhiat hun uh a tung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amaute in thuman thutak ngainuam nawnloin tangthu phuahtawm peuh ngaihsutpi-in nei ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** But watch thou in all things, endure afflictions, do the work of an evangelist, make full proof of thy ministry.

**TDB77:** Nangmah na hih leh, na khempeuh sungah na lungsim na kikeem in, gimna thuak in, thuhilh sia’ na mah na sem in; nang nasep ding nasepna a vekpi-in na sem in.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin nang na hih leh thu khempeuhah na lungsim ki-ukin haksatnate na thuak in la, Pasian' thugen nasep semin, na nasepna-ah ma na tun' in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** For I am now ready to be offered, and the time of my departure is at hand.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, keimah in tu lai takin biakna tau tungah kisungin ka om lai hi. Ka paina hun a hong tung zo hi.

**ZVS:** Kei ka hih leh Pasian biakna-in hong kipiak ding hun hong tungta hi. Hih leitung nuntakna ka nusiat ding hun hita hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the faith:

**TDB77:** Galdona a pha ka do zo hi, tai nading lam a mong dongin ka tai zo hi, upna thu ka keem zo hi.

**ZVS:** Kidemna-ah ka hat tawpin ka hih khin a, a mong dongin ka tai khin a, upna thu khotakin lenin ka zokhin hi.

### v8
**EN:** Henceforth there is laid up for me a crown of righteousness, which the Lord, the righteous judge, shall give me at that day: and not to me only, but unto all them also that love his appearing.

**TDB77:** Tu-a kipanin dikna lukhu, keimah ading a kikoih khol zo hi. Tua Ni ciangin thuman tawh a khenpa in tua lukhu kei hong pia ding hi. Keimah bek hong pia kei ding hi, ama kilat ding thu a lamente khempeuh zong a pia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Thu a manin a khen Topa in tua Ni ciangin kei a hong piakding thuman lukhu ahi gualzawhna thaman in tu-in kei hong ngak gige hi. Kei tung bekah a hong piading hilo a, ama hong paikik ding itna tawh a ngakte khempeuh tungah zong a piading ahi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Do thy diligence to come shortly unto me:

**TDB77:** Ka kiangah a manlangin na hong pai nading hahkat in.

**ZVS:** Kei kiangah na hong pai baih theihna dingin hong hanciam in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** For Demas hath forsaken me, having loved this present world, and is departed unto Thessalonica; Crescens to Galatia, Titus unto Dalmatia.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, Demas in tu lai nuntakna a it maninah kei hong taisanin, Thesalonika khua-ah a pai zo hi. Kresen in Galati gamah a pai zo hi. Titu in Dalmatia gamah a pai zo hi.

**ZVS:** Demas in hih leitung thu kinzaw ahih manin kei hong nusia-in Thesalonika khua-ah a pai hi. Kresen, Galatia gamah pai a, Titus, Dalmatia gamah pai hi.

### v11
**EN:** Only Luke is with me. Take Mark, and bring him with thee: for he is profitable to me for the ministry.

**TDB77:** Luka bek ka kiangah a om hi. Marka hong zawn in la, hong tonpih in. Bang hang hiam cih leh, ama nasepna ka phattuampih mahmah hi.

**ZVS:** Luka bek kei kiangah om hi. Marka in nasepna-ah nakpi takin hong huhthei ahih manin na hong pai ciangin amah hong tonpih in.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12

**TDB77:** Tikhikas ahih leh, Efesa khua-ah ka sawl zo hi.

**ZVS:** Tikhikas ahih leh Efesa khua-ah ka paisak khin hi.

### v13

**TDB77:** Troas khua-ah Karpas kiangah ka koih ka puanza leh laibute, leh a man takin savun lai dalte ahi zongin, na hong pai ciangin hong pua in.

**ZVS:** Na hong pai ciangin Troas khua-ah Karpas kianga ka nusiat ka puantualpi hong pua in la, laibute zong, a diakdiakin savun laidalte tawh zong hong pua in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14

**TDB77:** Sumsungpa Alekzander in keimah tungah a hoih lo tampi hong bawl hi. Amah in a hihna bangin ama tungah Topa in a thuk ding hi.

**ZVS:** Sikseekpa Alexander in kei tungah siatna lianpi hong bawl hi. Ama gamtatna tawh kizui-in Topa in thuk ding hi.

### v15

**TDB77:** Tua mi nangmah zong na kikeem in. Bang hang hiam cih leh, amah in eite thu nakpi-in a nial hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in i thuhilhna nakpi takin nial ahih manin amah tawh kisai-ah nangma pumpi kidawm mahmah in.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** At my first answer no man stood with me, but all men forsook me: I pray God that it may not be laid to their charge.

**TDB77:** Amasa-a ka kihutna thu ka gen lai takin keima lamah kuamah a om kei hi, mi khempeuh in kei hong taisan uh hi. Tua thu hangin amaute tungah mawhsakna om kei hen.

**ZVS:** Keimah kihutna ding thu ka gen masak ni-in kuamah in hong koppih loin hong taisanmang uh hi. Tua thu hangin mawhsakna thuak kei beek uh hen!

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v17
**EN:** Notwithstanding the Lord stood with me, and strengthened me; that by me the preaching might be fully known, and that all the Gentiles might hear: and I was delivered out of the mouth of the lion.

**TDB77:** Thuhilhna khempeuh ka hilh theih nading ahi zongin, Gentail mi khempeuh in a zak nadingun ahi zongin, Topa in kei lamah om a, keima tha hong dimsak a, humpinelkai’ kam sung pan hong honkhia hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin Topa in kei hong ompih a, Gentail mi khempeuh tungah Pasian' thu a kicingin ka gentheihna dingin thahatna a hong pia hi. Tua ahih manin sihna ding thukhenna pan hong suaktasak hi.

### v18

**ZVS:** Topa in siatna khempeuh panin kei hong honkhia ding a, ama vantung Gam-ah muan'huai takin hong tun'pih ding hi. Amah in a tawntungin minthan'na ngah ta hen. Amen.
