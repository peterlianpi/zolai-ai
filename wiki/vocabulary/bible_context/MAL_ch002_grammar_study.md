### v2
**EN:** If ye will not hear, and if ye will not lay it to heart, to give glory unto my name, saith the

**TDB77:** Na gamtatna-te tawh kei nong zahtak ding-te hi. Ka thugente ngai loin, thupi takin na lak kei-te leh note tungah samsiatna ka hong tungsak ding a, siampi hihna tawh na ngah-te thuphate tungah samsiatna ka tungsak ding hi. A taktakin ci lehang, note in ka thupiaknate thupilak lo na hih manun na thupha ngahte-te tungah samsiatna ka tungsak zo hi.

**ZVS:** Na gamtatna-te tawh kei nong zahtak ding-te hi. Ka thugente ngailoin, thupi takin na lak kei-te leh note tungah samsiatna ka hong tungsak ding a, siampi hihna tawh na ngah-te thuphate tungah samsiatna ka tungsak ding hi. A taktakin ci lehang, note in ka thupiaknate thupi laklo na hih manun na thupha ngahte-te tungah samsiatna ka tungsakzo hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

**ZVS:** Amaute in thuman taktakte mah hilhin gen-te a, amaute' kam panin zuauthu gingkhialo hi. Ka deihna bangin amaute gamta-te a, a khial ding mi tampite, thuman lamah a paisak-te hi.

### v7

**ZVS:** Siampite' nasep pen Pasian a manin theihna ding a hilh ding-te ahi hi. Amaute pen, Vanglian { Topa' } kamtaite ahih manun, mite in keima deihna a theihna dingun amaute' tungah thu a dot ding-te ahi hi.

**ZVS:** “Ahi zongin, no siampite na hih-te leh lampi man panin na pial khin-te hi. Na thuhilhnate un mi tampi tak khialsak hi. Note tawh ka bawl thuciamna na palsat khin-te hi.

### v9

### v10

**ZVS:** Eite khempeuh in, eite a hong piangsak Pasian ahi Pa khat bek nei hilo ihi hiam? Tua hinapi-in bang hangin eite khatlekhat tungah citakloin, i pu i pate tawh a bawl, Pasian' thuciamna palsat ihi hiam?
