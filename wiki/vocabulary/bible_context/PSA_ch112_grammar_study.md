## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Praise ye the

**TDB77:** Topa phat un. Topa zahtak-in ama thupiaknate nuam a sa mahmah mi in thupha ngahpa ahi hi!

**ZVS:** { Topa } phat un. { Topa } zahtakin a thupiaknate nuam a sa mahmah mi, thupha ngah mi ahi hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then)

### v2
**EN:** His seed shall be mighty upon earth: the generation of the upright shall be blessed.

**TDB77:** Ama suan-le-khakte in leitungah vanglian ding a, lungsim thutang a nei mipa’ suan-le-khakte’ thupha kipia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Ama suanlekhakte leitungah vanglian ding a, a lungsim thutang mite' suanlekhakte thupha kipia ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Wealth and riches shall be in his house: and his righteousness endureth for ever.

**TDB77:** Hauhna leh nopsaknate ama inn sungah dim ding a, ama thumanna kip tawntung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Hauhna leh nopsaknate a innkuan sungah dim ding a, ama thumanna kip tawntung ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness: he is gracious, and full of compassion, and righteous.

**TDB77:** Lungsim thutangte’ adingin khuamial sung panin khuavak hong taangkhia hi; Topa dik a, hehpihna nei-in, thuman hi.

**ZVS:** Lungsim thutangte-a' dingin khuamial sung panin khuavak hong taangkhia hi. { Topa } dik a, hehpihna nei-in, amah thuman hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** A good man sheweth favour, and lendeth: he will guide his affairs with discretion.

**TDB77:** Cingh gamtatin mi tungah leitawi pia a, thutangin a gamta mipa tungah thu hoih hi.

**ZVS:** Cingh gamtatin mite tungah leitawi pia a, thutangin a gamta mite tungah thu a hoih hi.

**Grammar:** adjective: hoih (good)

### v6
**EN:** Surely he shall not be moved for ever: the righteous shall be in everlasting remembrance.

**TDB77:** Amah kiliing lo taktak ding a, amah kiphawk tawntung ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah kilinglo taktak ding a, kiphawk tawntung ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: his heart is fixed, trusting in the

**TDB77:** Amah in thusia zak ding lau lo hi; Topa muang a, ama lungsim a kip hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in thusia zakding laulo hi. { Topa } muang a, ama lungsim a kip hi.

**Grammar:** negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** His heart is established, he shall not be afraid, until he see his desire upon his enemies.

**TDB77:** Amah lau lo, lunghimawh loin om hithiat a, amah a langpangte a guallelh ding lamtak theitel hi.

**ZVS:** Amah lauloin, lunghimawh loin om hithiat a, amah a langpangte a guallelh ding a theitel hi.

### v9
**EN:** He hath dispersed, he hath given to the poor; his righteousness endureth for ever; his horn shall be exalted with honour.

**TDB77:** Amah in hawmkhia zuahzuah a, mizawngte tungah pia khin hi; ama migitna kip tawntung a, amah vanglianin, kipahtawi ding hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in hawmkhia zuahzuah a, mizawngte tungah a piakhia hi. Ama migitna kip tawntung a, amah vanglianin kipahtawi ding hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** The wicked shall see it, and be grieved; he shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away: the desire of the wicked shall perish.

**TDB77:** Migilo in hih thu mu-in a hehsuak a, a ha gawigawi-a tuimang hi; migilopa’ lametna in a mawkna suak ta hi.

**ZVS:** Migilo in hih thu mu-in a heh suak mahmah a, a ha gawigawi-in a tuimang hi. Migilote' lametna a mawkna a suakta hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)
