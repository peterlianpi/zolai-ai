## Verses

### v3
**EN:** Upon an instrument of ten strings, and upon the psaltery; upon the harp with a solemn sound.

**TDB77:** Sangpenpa aw, { Topa } tungah lungdamna lah ding, nangma min phatna la sak dingin kilawm a, zangsawm khausai lasakna leh harp lasakna, laia lasaknate tumin, zingsangin nangma itna kip, zanin na thumanna genkhiat dingin a kilawm ahi hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** For thou,

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Topa aw, nangma nasepna tawh kei hong lungdamsak a, nangma nasepnate hangin lungdamin la ka sa hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh { Topa } aw, nangma sepna tawh kei nong lungdamsak a, nangma sepnate hangin lungdamin la ka sa hi.

### v5
**EN:** O

**TDB77:** Topa, na sepnate bangzah takin lian! Na ngaihsutnate thuk mahmah!

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na nasepnate lian mahmah a, na ngaihsutnate thuk mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v8
**EN:** But thou,

**TDB77:** Migilote, lopa bangin selkhia-in, siatna bawl khempeuh a pun' mahmah hangun, a tawntungin siatna a thuak ding uh ahi hi. Ahih hangin { Topa } aw, nang pen a sangna-ah a omden na hihna thu, lungsimmawl mi in thei theilo a, lungsim teilo mi in tel theilo hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v9
**EN:** For, lo, thine enemies, O

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam cih leh, Topa aw, na galte en in, bang hang hiam cih leh, en in, na galte beimang ding hi; siatna bawl khempeuhte kithehthang ding uh hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } aw, na galte si ta ding! Bang hang hiam cih leh na galte beimang ding a, siatna a bawl khempeuhte kithehthang ding uh hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v10
**EN:** But my horn shalt thou exalt like the horn of an unicorn: I shall be anointed with fresh oil.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin nangmah in zangsial bangin kei nong hatsak a, kei tungah thupha hong pia-in nong lungdam sak hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin nangmah in zangsial bangin kei na hong hatsak a, kei thupha hong pia-in nong lungdamsak hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** Mine eye also shall see my desire on mine enemies, and mine ears shall hear my desire of the wicked that rise up against me.

**TDB77:** Ka galte’ pukna ka mitte in mu khin a, keimah hong do a gilote’ siatna ka bilte in za khin ta hi.

**ZVS:** Ka galte' pukna ka mit in mu khinta a, keimah a hong do a gilote' siatna ka bil in za khinta hi.

**Grammar:** completed: already done

### v12
**EN:** The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree: he shall grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

**TDB77:** Thuman mi in nisuh kung bangin palh a, Lebanon mual tunga taamtaak kung bangin a khang hi.

**ZVS:** Nisuh kung bangin thuman mi palh a, Lebanon mualtunga om taamtak kung bangin a khang hi.

### v13
**EN:** Those that be planted in the house of the

**TDB77:** Topa’ inn sungah amaute kisuan a, i Pasian’ huang sungah amaute palh uh hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa' } inn sungah amaute kisuan a, i Pasian' huangsungah amaute palh uh hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v15
**EN:** To shew that the

**TDB77:** { Topa } thutang hi, ci-in a lahna dingun amaute teek napi-in gah lailai uh a, amaute tui kiuhkeuhin hing sitset uh hi. Amah pen kei hong hupa hi a, ama sungah thumanlohna omlo hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
