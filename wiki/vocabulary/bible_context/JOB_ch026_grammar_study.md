# Job Chapter 26 — Grammar Study

> Sources: TDB77 + Tedim2010 (ZVS) × KJV | 14 verses

---

## Grammar Patterns

| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 3 |
| purpose: in order to | 2 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 2 |
| plural: uh marker | 2 |
| sequence: then/therefore | 1 |
| negation: lo | 1 |
| quotative: said/saying | 1 |

---

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** But Job answered and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Job in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** How hast thou helped him that is without power? how savest thou the arm that hath no strength?

**TDB77:** “A hehpihhuai kei nong huhna, a tha zawngkhal kei nong hotna, hih bang ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** “A hehpihhuai kei nong huhna, a thazawngkhal kei nong hotna, hih bang ahi hiam?

### v3
**EN:** How hast thou counselled him that hath no wisdom? and how hast thou plentifully declared the thing as it is?

**TDB77:** Kei bang mihai tungah thumanpha nong gen a, pilna thu tampi nong pulak hi.

**ZVS:** Kei bang mihai tungah thu manpha nong gen a, pilna thu tampi nong pulak hi.

### v4
**EN:** To whom hast thou uttered words? and whose spirit came from thee?

**TDB77:** Tua bang thu gen dingin kua in hong sawl a, kua’ lungsim hong pulak khia na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Tua bang thu a gen dingin kua in hong sawl a, kua' lungsim hong pulakkhia na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v5
**EN:** Dead things are formed from under the waters, and the inhabitants thereof.

**TDB77:** Misite’ gam kiling a, a sunga teengte launa tawh ling-te hi.

**ZVS:** “Misite' gam kiling a, a sunga a teng mite launa tawh a ling hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v6
**EN:** Hell is naked before him, and destruction hath no covering.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ muhna-ah misite’ gam kilang sinsen a, a muh theih lohna dingin a liah bangmah om lo hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' muhna-ah misite' gam kilang sinsen a, a muh theihlohna dingin a liah bangmah omlo hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** He stretcheth out the north over the empty place, and hangeth the earth upon nothing.

**TDB77:** Pasian in mun awng tungah vanpi zal a, bangmah in len lopi-in leitung khai lingleng hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in mun awng tungah vanpi hong zalkhia a, bangmah in a lenlopi-in leitung a khai lingleng hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v8
**EN:** He bindeth up the waters in his thick clouds; and the cloud is not rent under them.

**TDB77:** Amah in meiilom lakah tuite khol a, tua tui gihna meii a pua zo sak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in meiilom lakah tuite khol a, tua a gik mahmah tui meii a puazosak hi.

### v9
**EN:** He holdeth back the face of his throne, and spreadeth his cloud upon it.

**TDB77:** Amah in kha a liah ding bawl a, meii tawh kha a khuh hi.

### v10
**EN:** He hath compassed the waters with bounds, until the day and night come to an end.

**TDB77:** Amah in muh tawpna-ah git dinden-in, khuavak leh khuamial khen hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in muh tawpna-ah git dinden a, khuavak leh khuamial a khen hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then)

### v11
**EN:** The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at his reproof.

**TDB77:** Amah in vanpi a pua khuampite a tai ciangin, tua khuampite lau-in ling-te hi.

**ZVS:** Amah in vanpi a pua khuampite a taii ciangin tua khuampite lau-in a ling-te hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** He divideth the sea with his power, and by his understanding he smiteth through the proud.

**TDB77:** Ama thahatna tawh tuipi zo a, ama siamna tawh tuipi ganhing golpi Rahab thatlum hi.

**ZVS:** Ama thahatna tawh tuipi zo a, ama siamna tawh tui ganhing golpi Rahab thatlum hi.

### v13
**EN:** By his spirit he hath garnished the heavens; his hand hath formed the crooked serpent.

**TDB77:** A hu a san’ ciangin vantung teng siang pah a, ama khut in a taikhia gulpi thatlum hi.

**ZVS:** A hu a san' ciangin vantung teng siang pah a, ama khut in a taikhia gulpi thatlum hi.

### v14
**EN:** Lo, these are parts of his ways: but how little a portion is heard of him? but the thunder of his power who can understand?

**TDB77:** Hihte in ama vangliatna themkhat bek hi pan a, i zak thute a heem lam bek hi pan hi. Pasian a liatna taktak kua in thei khin zo ding ahi hiam?” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Hihte pen ama vangliatna themkhat bek hi pan a, i zak thute pen a hemlam bek hi pan hi. Pasian a liatna taktak kua'n theizo ding ahi hiam?” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying
