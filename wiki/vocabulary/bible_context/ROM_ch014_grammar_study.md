## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Him that is weak in the faith receive ye, but not to doubtful disputations.

**TDB77:** Upna neu mi na mang un. Ahih hangin up khialh nading thute tua mi tawh na kikum kei un.

**ZVS:** Upna neu mite pawlbawl un la, na ngaihsutna-te a kituah kei leh zong mawhsak kei un.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v2
**EN:** For one believeth that he may eat all things: another, who is weak, eateth herbs.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in an pawl khempeuh neek theih nadingin upna a nei hi. Ahih hangin mi a thaneem mi in gah leh teh bek a ne hi.

**ZVS:** Mi pawlkhat in na khempeuh ka nektheih ding hi, ci-in ngaihsun-te hi; ahi zongin upna neute in anteh bek ne ngam-te hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to

### v3
**EN:** Let not him that eateth despise him that eateth not; and let not him which eateth not judge him that eateth: for God hath received him.

**TDB77:** An pawl khempeuh a ne mi in a ne lo mi simmawh kei hen. A ne lo mi in a ne mi dongtel kei hen. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, Pasian in tua pa a mang zo hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin a ne mite in a tang mite simmawhbawl kei hen la, a tang mite in zong a ne mite simmawhbawl kei hen. Bang hang hiam cih leh a ne mite zong Pasian in a san' mite mah ahi hi.

### v4
**EN:** Who art thou that judgest another man's servant? to his own master he standeth or falleth. Yea, he shall be holden up: for God is able to make him stand.

**TDB77:** Mi dang’ sawltak a dongtel nangmah in kua mi na hi hiam? Tua pa in a Topa thu dotna-ah a kip ahi zongin, a kip lo ahi zongin ahi ding hi. Ahih hangin a Topa in tua pa kipsak theihna a nei ahih ciangin a kipsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Mi khat' nasemte tungah kua in thu khen thei ding na hi-te hiam? A gina leh a ginalo a khen dingpa pen amau' Topa ahi hi. A Topa un hoih hi, a cih leh a kipsak zawhna ding vangliatna nei ahih manin a kip hi lel ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** One man esteemeth one day above another: another esteemeth every day alike. Let every man be fully persuaded in his own mind.

**TDB77:** Mi khat in ni khat sangin ni khat a manpha zaw hi ci-in a ciamteh hi. Mi khat in ni khempeuh a kibang hi ci-in a ciamteh thei hi. Mikim in ama lungsimah pha a sak bang hi ta hen.

**ZVS:** Mi pawlkhatte in ni khat peuh adang sangin thupi zaw hi, ci-in ngaihsun-te a, pawlkhatte in ni khempeuh kibangkim hi, ci-in ngaihsun-te hi. Tua ahih manin a kuamah peuh in amau' hoihsakna thu ciantakin thei ta-te hen.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** He that regardeth the day, regardeth it unto the Lord; and he that regardeth not the day, to the Lord he doth not regard it. He that eateth, eateth to the Lord, for he giveth God thanks; and he that eateth not, to the Lord he eateth not, and giveth God thanks.

**TDB77:** Ni a tang mi in Topa hangin a tang hi. An a ne mi in Topa hangin an a ne hi. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, amah in Pasian’ hehpihna a phat hi. An ne lo mi in Topa hangin ne lo a, Pasian’ hehpihna a phat hi.

**ZVS:** Ni khatpeuh thupi diak hi, ci-in a tang mite in Topa a pahtawina-in a tang ahi-te hi. An khempeuh a ne mi in zong Pasian' tungah lungdamna ko-in ne-te ahih manin Topa a pahtawina mahin a ne-te ahi hi. Nekdingin kilawmlo hi, ci-in a nelo mite in zong Pasian tungah lungdamna mah ko ahih manin Topa a pahtawina mahin a nelo-te ahi hi.

### v7
**EN:** For none of us liveth to himself, and no man dieth to himself.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, eite lakah kuamah in ama ading a nungta kei hi; kuamah in ama ading a si kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ama-a' ding bekin kuamah nungtalo a, ama-a' ding bekin kuamah silo hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v8
**EN:** For whether we live, we live unto the Lord; and whether we die, we die unto the Lord: whether we live therefore, or die, we are the Lord's.

**TDB77:** Eite i nuntak leh, Topa ading i nungta hi. Eite i sih leh, Topa ading i si hi. Tua ahih ciangin eite i nungta ahi zongin, i si ahi zongin, Topa’ aa i hi hi.

**ZVS:** I nuntak leh Topa-a' dingin a nungta ihi a, i sih leh zong Topa-a' ding mahin a si ihi hi. Tua ahih manin i si a, i hing zongin Topa-a' ihi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v9
**EN:** For to this end Christ both died, and rose, and revived, that he might be Lord both of the dead and living.

**TDB77:** Khazih in misite leh mi nungtate’ Topa a suah nadingin sihna thuak a, a sih khit ciangin a hing kik hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thu hangin Khrih pen a site leh a hingte' Topa ahih theihna dingin hong si-in hong nungtakik hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** But why dost thou judge thy brother? or why dost thou set at nought thy brother? for we shall all stand before the judgment seat of Christ.

**TDB77:** Nang in bang hangin na u na nau dongtelin thu na khen hiam? Na u na nau bang hangin na simmawh hiam? Eite khempeuh in Pasian’ thukhenna tokhom mai-ah i ding ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih leh bang hangin na Khristianpihte-te tungah thu khen a, bang hangin simmawhbawl thei na hi-te hiam? Thukhenna a thuak dingin Pasian' mai-ah eite khempeuh a ding ciat ding ihi hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** For it is written, As I live, saith the Lord, every knee shall bow to me, and every tongue shall confess to God.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, Lai Siangtho sungah: “Ka nuntak bangin ka mai-ah mi khempeuhte a khukdin’ ding-te a, Leii khempeuh in Pasian a phat ding-te hi, ci-in Pasian in thu a pia hi,” ci-in a kigelh hi.

**ZVS:** Lai Siangtho sungah, “Kei pen a nungta ka hih mah bangin ka mai-ah mi khempeuh hong kun ding a, Pasian ka hihna thu mi khempeuh in pulak taktak ding-te hi, Topa in ci hi,” ci-in a kigelh hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v12
**EN:** So then every one of us shall give account of himself to God.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin eite khempeuh in eima thu tekah Pasian’ thudotna i thuak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin a kuamah peuh in i gamtatna ciat Pasian' mai-ah a va kigentel ciat ding ihi hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will

### v13
**EN:** Let us not therefore judge one another any more: but judge this rather, that no man put a stumblingblock or an occasion to fall in his brother's way.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin eite in tu-a kipanin khat leh khat thu kisuah loin om ni. Unau khat puk nading ahi zongin, a khialh nading ahi zongin hih loh nadingin na ciamteh zaw un.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin khatlekhat kimawhsakin thu kikhen kei un la, i Khristianpihte i puksakkhak lohna ding, a dongtangsak ihihkhak lohna dingin ngaihsun ciat ni.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | hortative: let us | purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** I know, and am persuaded by the Lord Jesus, that there is nothing unclean of itself: but to him that esteemeth any thing to be unclean, to him it is unclean.

**TDB77:** Bangmah in a pian’pih tawh a kihhuai kei hi, keimah in Topa Zeisu muangin thei-in ka lungkim hi. Ahih hangin a kihhuai a sa mi khat a om leh, ama tungah a kihhuai hi.

**ZVS:** Topa Jesuh tawh kipawlin a lungsim ka theihna tawh, na khat peuhpeuh ama thu-in a dawizek om hetlo hi, ci-in teltakin ka thei hi. Ahi zongin mi khatpeuh in dawizek hi, ci-in a ngaihsut leh ama-a' dingin a dawizek hi pah hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** But if thy brother be grieved with thy meat, now walkest thou not charitably. Destroy not him with thy meat, for whom Christ died.

**TDB77:** Na u na nau in nang an nekna hangin a dah leh, nang in itna tawh na gamta kei hi. Amah tang ding Khazih a si khin mi khat na an nekna tawh na siasak kei in.

**ZVS:** An khatpeuh na nekna-te hangin na Khristianpihte' lungnopmawhna na pian'sak-te leh itna lungsim tawh a gamta na hi nawn kei-te hi. Na an nekna-te tawh, Khrih in a sihlawh mite' siatna ding piangsak kei un.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v16
**EN:** Let not then your good be evil spoken of:

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin na hih theihna-te ah gensiat nading omsak kei un.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin note in hoih na sak-te thute, midangte in a sia-in a genkhak loh theihna dingun na gamta un.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v17
**EN:** For the kingdom of God is not meat and drink; but righteousness, and peace, and joy in the Holy Ghost.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, Pasian’ Gam in an nekna, tui dawnna-ah a kip kei hi. Kha Siangtho hanga kingah dikna, kilemna leh lungdamna-ah a kip hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh Pasian' Ukna pen nek leh dawn tawh a kisai thu hilo a, Kha Siangtho hangin i ngah Pasian' deih bangin gamtatna, kilemna, lungdamna, cihte hizaw hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v18
**EN:** For he that in these things serveth Christ is acceptable to God, and approved of men.

**TDB77:** Tua bang gamta a, Khazih’ na a sem mi in Pasian lungdam sak a, mite mai-ah minphathuai ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Hih bang danin Khrih' na a sem mite in Pasian lungdamsak a, mite zong a lungkimsak-te hi.

### v19
**EN:** Let us therefore follow after the things which make for peace, and things wherewith one may edify another.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin kilem nading, khat leh khat kikipsak nading tawh kizom nate zui ni.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin mi tawh kilem theihna ding, leh kha thu-ah khatlekhat thahatna i kipiak theihna ding thute hanciam ciat ni.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v20
**EN:** For meat destroy not the work of God. All things indeed are pure; but it is evil for that man who eateth with offence.

**TDB77:** An nekna hangin Pasian bawlna na susia kei un. An khempeuh a siangtho hangin midangte a puk nading a ne mi in a khial hi.

**ZVS:** Annek huhau-in Pasian' sepsa thute susia kei ni. An khempeuh a kine thei ding ahi hi; ahi zongin tua nekna hangin mi khat peuhpeuh a pukding ahih leh a khial ihi hi.

### v21
**EN:** It is good neither to eat flesh, nor to drink wine, nor any thing whereby thy brother stumbleth, or is offended, or is made weak.

**TDB77:** Sa nekna, leenggahtui dawnna-a kipan na u na nau puk nading na khempeuh tan’huai hi.

**ZVS:** Midangte in a puklawh theih annekna, zunekna, leh na khat peuhpeuh i kikep ding a man pen ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v22
**EN:** Hast thou faith? have it to thyself before God. Happy is he that condemneth not himself in that thing which he alloweth.

**TDB77:** Na neihsa na upna, nangmah ading Pasian mai-ah na om sak in. A man’sa thu-ah amah le amah a kimawhsak lote in mi hampha ahi-te hi.

**ZVS:** Hih thu-ah no' upna leh no' ngaihsutnate, Pasian tawh note' kikal thu hisak un. Amau lungsim sungah man a sak bangun a gamta zote pen a lung-te nuam mahmah hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v23
**EN:** And he that doubteth is damned if he eat, because he eateth not of faith: for whatsoever is not of faith is sin.

**TDB77:** A um khial mi in a nek leh, upna lungsim nei loin ne ahih ciangin amah le amah a kimawhsak ahi hi. Upna lungsim tawh kituak lo gamtatna khempeuh in mawhna ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin lunghimawhpipi-in a ne a om leh, a upna-te tawh kizui-in a gamta hilo-te ahih manin Pasian in tua bang mite a mawhsak ding hi. I upna bangin gamtatlohna peuhpeuh mawhna ahi hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix
