## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Know ye not, brethren, (for I speak to them that know the law,) how that the law hath dominion over a man as long as he liveth?

**TDB77:** Thukham a thei ute naute aw, mi khat a nuntak lai takin thukham in tua mi a uk hi cih thei lo na hi-te hiam?

**ZVS:** Lawmte aw, note khempeuh in thukham thei na hih manun tu-a kong gen dingte na tel mahmah ding-te hi. Mi khat a nuntak sungteng bek thukham in uk thei hi.

### v2
**EN:** For the woman which hath an husband is bound by the law to her husband so long as he liveth; but if the husband be dead, she is loosed from the law of her husband.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, pasal a nei numei in a pasal a sih matengin thukham bangin a pasal tawh a kikhih khawm hi. A pasal a sih leh, numei in a pasal’ thukham tawh a kipelh hi.

**ZVS:** Gentehna-in, pasal nei numei khat pen a pasal a nuntak sungteng bek, tua thukham in a pasal tawh lencip hi. Ahi zongin a pasal a sih ciangin tua a pasal tawh a lencip thukham panin a suakta hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** So then if, while her husband liveth, she be married to another man, she shall be called an adulteress: but if her husband be dead, she is free from that law; so that she is no adulteress, though she be married to another man.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin a pasal a nuntak lai-in midang’ zi a suah leh, numei gilo a kici ding hi. Ahih hangin a pasal a sih leh, tua numei in thukham tawh a kipelhkhia a, midang tawh a kiteeng zongin numei gilo a kici kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin a pasal a nuntak hun sungin pasal dang tawh a lupkhawm leh mi tawh a mawh kici hi. Ahi zongin a pasal a si hi leh thukham tawh kisai-in a suakta hi a, pasal dang a ten'pih hangin mi tawh a mawh hilo hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Wherefore, my brethren, ye also are become dead to the law by the body of Christ; that ye should be married to another, even to him who is raised from the dead, that we should bring forth fruit unto God.

**TDB77:** Tua mah bangin ka ute ka naute aw, note in zong midang a kici sihna pan a thokik mi’ zi na hi-te a, Pasian’ deihna bangin gah na gah nadingun Khazih’ pumpi hangin na si-te a, Thukham tawh na kikhen-te hi.

**ZVS:** Sanggamte aw, note zong tua mah tawh a kibang na hi-te hi. Khrih tawh pumkhat na hihna-te hangin thukham tawh kisai thu-ah a sikhin na hi-te a, Pasian' nasepna-ah eite a man nei i suahtheihna dingin sihna panin a kithosak kik Khrih tawh nupa na hi-te hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** For when we were in the flesh, the motions of sins, which were by the law, did work in our members to bring forth fruit unto death.

**TDB77:** Eite in i pumpi ngeina bangin gamta-in i om lai takin, mawhna tawh akipawl i lungsim in Thukham hangin piang a, sihna suahna gah a gah nadingin i pumpi tung nate sungah nasem hi.

**ZVS:** I leitung lungsim tawh i nuntak laiteng, thukham hangin i hihnop semsem mawhnopnate i gamta a, tuate khempeuh sihna tawh a kizom ahi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v6
**EN:** But now we are delivered from the law, that being dead wherein we were held; that we should serve in newness of spirit, and not in the oldness of the letter.

**TDB77:** Tu-in ahih leh, thukham lui muang loin, Kha Siangtho’ nuntakna thak sungah na i sep nadingin hong ukpa lamah i sih khit ciangin thukham pan i suakta zo hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin nidangin a hong lencip thukham panin Pasian in eite hong suaktasak khin ahih manin tua thukham tawh kisai-ah eite a sikhin ihi hi. Lai sungah a kigelh thukham lui sungah nungta nawnloin, Kha Siangtho' hong piak nuntakna thakah a nungta ihi hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | purpose: in order to

### v7
**EN:** What shall we say then? Is the law sin? God forbid. Nay, I had not known sin, but by the law: for I had not known lust, except the law had said, Thou shalt not covet.

**TDB77:** Tua bang ahih leh, bang i ci gen ding hiam? Thukham in mawhna ahi i ci ding hiam? Tua bang hi zenzen lo hi. Ahi zongin thukham bek hangin mawhna lungsim ka thei hi. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, “Huaihamna lungsim nei kei in,” ci-in Thukham in na ci kei leh, huaihamna lungsim ke’n ka thei kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih leh Thukham mahmah pen a siahuai ahi hiam? A sia hilo hi. Mawhna bang hiam cih Thukham in hong theitel sak hi. Thukham in, “Hazatna lungsim nei kei in,” ci-in genlo hi leh, haza i cih bang ahi hiam, ci-in ka thei kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | negation: kei | negation: lo

### v8
**EN:** But sin, taking occasion by the commandment, wrought in me all manner of concupiscence. For without the law sin was dead.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin mawhna thu in thukham hangah thu ngah ahih ciangin ka lungsim sungah huaihamna apawlpawl a piangsak hi. Thukham om kei leh, mawhna a si ding hi.

**ZVS:** Thukham a omna hangin mawhna in nuamtak sa-in hazatna a tuamtuam ka neihtheihna ding hong piangsak hi. Thukham om kei leh mawhna in bangmah sem theilo hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | future: will

### v9
**EN:** For I was alive without the law once: but when the commandment came, sin revived, and I died.

**TDB77:** Nidang lai-inah thukham loin ka nungta hi. Ahih hangin thukham hong pian’ ciangin mawhna nungta kik a, kei ka si hi.

**ZVS:** Thukham ka theih ma-in mi nungta ka hi ngei a, thukham ka theih ciangin mawhnopna lungsim hong khangin kei ka si hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** And the commandment, which was ordained to life, I found to be unto death.

**TDB77:** Hin’na tawh kipawl thukham in sihna tawh kipawl ahih lam keimah in ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Nuntak ngahna dingin a kibawl thukham pen keima-a' dingin sihna hong hi zawsop hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v11
**EN:** For sin, taking occasion by the commandment, deceived me, and by it slew me.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, mawhna in thukham hangin thu ngah a, thukhamna tawh hong kheemin hong that hi.

**ZVS:** Bang hang hiam cih leh thukham a om manin mawhna in nuamtaksa a, kei hong khemin tua thukham tawh a hong that hi.

### v12
**EN:** Wherefore the law is holy, and the commandment holy, and just, and good.

**TDB77:** Tua hi a, Thukham in a siangtho hi. A thu in zong a siangtho, a dik, a pha hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin Thukham mahmah hita leh thupiaknate hita leh hoihin man a, siangtho hi.

### v13
**EN:** Was then that which is good made death unto me? God forbid. But sin, that it might appear sin, working death in me by that which is good; that sin by the commandment might become exceeding sinful.

**TDB77:** Tua bang ahih leh, a hoih a pha na in keima tungah sihna a suak ahi hiam? Tua bang hi zenzen lo hi. Mawhna in mawhna taktak ahihna a kilat nadingin a pha nate hangin keima sungah sihna thu a piangsak hi. Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, mawhna in thukham hangin mawhna taktak a pian’ theih nading ahi hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih leh a hoih na khat in kei' sihna ding hong piangsak ahi hiam? Hilo hi. Tua sihna pen mawhna' nasepna hi a, mawhna pianzia bangci bang taktak hiam, cih a kitheihna dingin a hoih na khat zangin kei tungah sihna a hong piangsak ahi hi. Tua ahih manin thukham a om manin mawhna a siahuai mahmah ahihna hong kilangzaw kan lai hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix | purpose: in order to | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** For we know that the law is spiritual: but I am carnal, sold under sin.

**TDB77:** Thukham in Kha Siangtho tawh kipawl ci-in i thei hi. Keimah in mawhna khut sungah a kizuak, a si thei leitungmi ka hi hi.

**ZVS:** Thukham pen i kha thu tawh kisai hi, ci-in i thei hi. Ahi zongin kei pen leitung pumpi tawh a nungta mi ka hi a, mawhna thu mangin a sal a suak ka hi hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v15
**EN:** For that which I do I allow not: for what I would, that do I not; but what I hate, that do I.

**TDB77:** Bang hang hiam na cih-te leh, ka gamtatna ka thei kei hi. Ka gamtat nop bangin ka gamta kei hi. Ka muhdah gamtatna ka hih hi.

**ZVS:** Ka hihnopteng hihkha loin ka hih noplohteng keek mah hih zawsop ka hih manin keima gamtatna mahmah zong ka tel thei kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v16
**EN:** If then I do that which I would not, I consent unto the law that it is good.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin ka gamtat nop lohna ka gamtat leh, Thukham in a pha hi ka ci hi.

**ZVS:** Ka hih noplohteng ka hih veve ciangin Thukham a deihhuai khat hi, ci-in ka ngaihsutna hong langsak hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying

### v17
**EN:** Now then it is no more I that do it, but sin that dwelleth in me.

**TDB77:** Tua ahih ciangin gamtatna a hoih lo keimah in ka hih kei hi, keima sungah a om khialhna in a hih hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih manin tua bangin a gamta taktak pen kei hiloin ka sunga om mawhna hizaw hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | negation: kei

### v18
**EN:** For I know that in me (that is, in my flesh,) dwelleth no good thing: for to will is present with me; but how to perform that which is good I find not.

**TDB77:** Ka sa ahi ka pum sungah a pha khat beek a om kei hi cih ka thei hi. Bang hang hiam na cih leh, a pha gamtat nopna lungsim ka neih hangin a pha gamtat theih nading ka mu kei hi.

**ZVS:** Ka leitung pumpi sungah a hoih na khat beek omlo hi, ci-in ka thei hi. Ka sungah a hoih gamtatnopna lungsim a om hangin ka hihzo kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v19
**EN:** For the good that I would I do not: but the evil which I would not, that I do.

**TDB77:** A pha ka gamtat nop ka hih thei kei hi. Ka gamtat nop loh gitlohna ka hih hi.

**ZVS:** A hoih ka gamtatnop teng gamta loin, ka hihnoploh siatnate ka gamta zawsop hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v20
**EN:** Now if I do that I would not, it is no more I that do it, but sin that dwelleth in me.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin gamtat nop loh na ka gamtat leh, keimah in ka gamta kei hi; ka sungah a om mawhna in a gamta hi.

**ZVS:** Ka gamtat noplohteng ka gamtat veve ciangin tua a gamta pen kei hi nawnloin ka sunga om mawhna a gamta ahihna a hong kilang hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v21
**EN:** I find then a law, that, when I would do good, evil is present with me.

**TDB77:** Tua hi a, a hoih a gamta nuam keima sungah mawhna thu a om cih ka thei hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ahih ciangin a hoih gamta nuam hinapi-in a sia bekbek a gamtakha ka hihna ka thei hi.

### v22
**EN:** For I delight in the law of God after the inward man:

**TDB77:** Ka sung mi hangin Pasian’ Thukham a pha ka sa hi.

**ZVS:** Ka lungsim tawng taktakah Pasian' thukham hoih ka sa hi.

### v23
**EN:** But I see another law in my members, warring against the law of my mind, and bringing me into captivity to the law of sin which is in my members.

**TDB77:** Ahi zongin ka pum tung nate lakah adang thukham khat ka lungsim tawh kido a, ka pum tung nate mawhna khut sungah manin a piak theih ka mu hi.

**ZVS:** Ahi zongin kei' deihloh thudang khat in ka lungsimah na hong sem hi. Tua ka sunga na hong sem pen in mawhna thu-ah kei hong mancip hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v24
**EN:** O wretched man that I am! who shall deliver me from the body of this death?

**TDB77:** Lunggim mi ka hi takpi hi! Hih a si ding pumpi panin kua in hong honkhia ding hiam?

**ZVS:** Kei pen a lungkham mi ka hita hi. Kei a hong sisak ding hih lungsim lui panin kua in kei hong honkhiazo ding ahi hiam?

### v25
**EN:** I thank God through Jesus Christ our Lord. So then with the mind I myself serve the law of God; but with the flesh the law of sin.

**TDB77:** Eite hong hon Topa Zeisu Khazih hangin Pasian’ hehpihna ka phat hi! Tua ahih ciangin ka lungsim hangin Pasian’ thukham deihna ka zuih hangin, ka pumpi hangin mawhna’ Thukham deihna ka zui hi.

**ZVS:** I Topa Jesuh Khrih in hong honkhiazo ding ahih manin Pasian tungah ka lung a dam hi. Kei' omzia taktak ahih leh: Ka lungsim tawh Pasian' thu ka zui a, ka pumpi in mawhna a piangsak thu a zui hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore
