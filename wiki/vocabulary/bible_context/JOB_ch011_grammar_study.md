| Pattern | Count |
|---|---|
| future: will | 7 |
| reciprocal: ki- prefix | 6 |
| negation: kei | 3 |
| sequence: then/therefore | 2 |
| serial verb: -in (and then) | 2 |
| plural: uh marker | 2 |

## Verses

### v1
**EN:** Then answered Zophar the Naamathite, and said,

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin Na-amah gammi Zofar in dawng a,

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin Naamah gammi Zofar in dawng a,

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore

### v2
**EN:** Should not the multitude of words be answered? and should a man full of talk be justified?

**TDB77:** “Kampau hihzahta hong kidawng lo ding sa a, kam tam pauna hangin thumanin hong kiseh ding sa na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Kampau hih zahta hong kidawnglo ding sa a, kam tam pauna hangin thumanin hong kiseh dingin ngaihsun na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v3
**EN:** Should thy lies make men hold their peace? and when thou mockest, shall no man make thee ashamed?

**TDB77:** Job aw, na thugente hong dawng thei lo ding hong sa na hi hiam? Na simmawh kampaute hong kidawng mawh ding sa na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Job aw, na thugente hong dawng theilo dingin hong ngaihsun na hi hiam? Na simmawh kampaute hong kidawng mawh dingin ngaihsun na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** For thou hast said, My doctrine is pure, and I am clean in thine eyes.

**TDB77:** Na thugente a man na sa kha a, Pasian’ muhna-ah a siangtho-in na kingaihsun kha hi.

**ZVS:** Nangma thugente a man na sa kha a, Pasian' muhna-ah a siangtho dingin na kingaihsun kha hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v5
**EN:** But oh that God would speak, and open his lips against thee;

**TDB77:** Pasian in na thugente hong dawngkik leh ut mahmah ing!

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly

### v6
**EN:** And that he would shew thee the secrets of wisdom, that they are double to that which is! Know therefore that God exacteth of thee less than thine iniquity deserveth.

**TDB77:** Pilna nam tampi a omna thu, mihingte’ theih zawh loh thuthuk a omna thu hong gen ding hi. Na mawhna zahin Pasian in hong daantah lohna thu thei in.

**ZVS:** Pilna nam tampi a omna thu, mihing' theih zawhloh thuthuk, a omna thu hong gen ding hi. Na mawhna zahin Pasian in gim hong piaklohna thei in.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v7
**EN:** Canst thou by searching find out God? canst thou find out the Almighty unto perfection?

**TDB77:** “Pasian’ thuthuk thei khin zo ding na hi hiam? Vanglianpa’ hih theihna thei khin zo ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** “Pasian' thuthuk thei khinzo ding na hi hiam? Vanglianpa' hih theihna thei khinzo ding na hi hiam?

### v8
**EN:** It is as high as heaven; what canst thou do? deeper than hell; what canst thou know?

**TDB77:** Pasian in vantung sangin sang zaw a, ahi zongin tua vantung nangawn na ban zo kei hi. Pasian in Leinuai thu thei a, ahi zongin nangmah in tua thu na thei kei hi.

**ZVS:** Vantung sangin Pasian sangzaw a, ahi zongin tua vantung nangawn na banzo kei hi. Pasian in leinuai thu thei a, ahi zongin nangmah in tua thu na thei kei hi.

**Grammar:** negation: kei

### v9
**EN:** The measure thereof is longer than the earth, and broader than the sea.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ liatna in leitung sangin golzaw a, tuipi sangin zaizaw hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' liatna pen leitung sangin golzaw a, tuipi sangin zaizaw hi.

### v10
**EN:** If he cut off, and shut up, or gather together, then who can hinder him?

**TDB77:** Pasian in nang manin thu hong sit leh amah a khaktan ding kua om ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian in nang hong manin thu hong sit leh amah a khaktan ding kua om ahi hiam?

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v11
**EN:** For he knoweth vain men: he seeth wickedness also; will he not then consider it?

**TDB77:** Pasian in mi hoihlote thei takpi hi; mawhna a muh ciangin thu don lo ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Pasian in mi hoihlote thei takpi hi. Mawhna a muh ciangin thudonlo ding ahi hiam?

### v12
**EN:** For vain man would be wise, though man be born like a wild ass's colt.

**TDB77:** Gam sung late mihing suak ngei ding a mihaite pil ngei ding ahi hiam?

**ZVS:** Gamlaka om late mihing suak ngei ding a, mihaite pil ngei ding ahi hiam?

### v13
**EN:** If thou prepare thine heart, and stretch out thine hands toward him;

**TDB77:** “Job aw, na lungsim a hoihin bawl in la, Pasian’ lam kihei in.

**ZVS:** “Job aw, na lungsim a hoihin bawl in la, Pasian' lamah kihei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14
**EN:** If iniquity be in thine hand, put it far away, and let not wickedness dwell in thy tabernacles.

**TDB77:** Khialhna na bawl khak leh paaikhia in la, na inn-ah gitlohna omsak kei in.

**ZVS:** Khialhna na bawl khak leh paiikhia in la, na inn sungah gitlohna omsak kei in.

### v15
**EN:** For then shalt thou lift up thy face without spot; yea, thou shalt be stedfast, and shalt not fear:

**TDB77:** Tua hi leh kisuan’na om loin na khuadak ngam ding a, na lungnuamin launa na nei kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi leh kisuan'na omloin na khuadak ngam ding a, na lung nuamin launa na nei kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v16
**EN:** Because thou shalt forget thy misery, and remember it as waters that pass away:

**TDB77:** Na lungkhamnate veng ding a, tuikhang pak bangin na phawk kha nawn kei ding hi.

**ZVS:** Na lungkhamnate veng ding a, tuikhang pak bangin na phawk nawn kei ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | negation: kei

### v17
**EN:** And thine age shall be clearer than the noonday; thou shalt shine forth, thou shalt be as the morning.

**TDB77:** Tua ciangin na nuntakna in sun sangin taangzaw ding a, nuntak lungzin pen hun in phalvak sangin taangzaw ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua ciangin na nuntakna, sun sangin taangzaw ding a, nuntak lungzin' pen hun, sun sangin taangzaw ding hi.

**Grammar:** sequence: then/therefore | future: will

### v18
**EN:** And thou shalt be secure, because there is hope; yea, thou shalt dig about thee, and thou shalt take thy rest in safety.

**TDB77:** Lungnuam tak leh lametna nei-in na om ding a, Pasian in nang hong hu-in tawldamna hong pia ding hi.

**ZVS:** Lungnuam tak leh lametna nei-in na om ding a, Pasian in nang hong hu-in, tawldamna hong pia ding hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v19
**EN:** Also thou shalt lie down, and none shall make thee afraid; yea, many shall make suit unto thee.

**TDB77:** Kuamah kihtak nei loin nuam takin na om ding a, mi tampi in nangma maipha hong zong ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kuamah kihtak neiloin nuamtakin na om ding a, mi tampi in nangma maipha hong zong ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker

### v20
**EN:** But the eyes of the wicked shall fail, and they shall not escape, and their hope shall be as the giving up of the ghost.

**TDB77:** Ahih hangin migilote ahih leh cimawhin khuadak ding-te a, suahtak nading lampi mu lo ding-te hi. Amau’ ngah ding ahih leh sihna bek ahi hi,” a ci hi.

**ZVS:** Ahih hangin migilote ahih leh cimawhin khuadak ding-te a, suahtakna ding lampi mulo ding-te hi. Amau' ngah ding ahih leh sihna bek ahi hi,” a ci hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | future: will | plural: uh marker
