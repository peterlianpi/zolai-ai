## Verses

### v1
**EN:** These are also proverbs of Solomon, which the men of Hezekiah king of Judah copied out.

**TDB77:** Judah kumpi Hezekiah’ lai-atte in a tei Solomon’ paunak dangte:

**ZVS:** Judah kumpi Hezekiah' lai-atte in a teikhiat-te Solomon' paunak dangte:

### v2
**EN:** It is the glory of God to conceal a thing: but the honour of kings is to search out a matter.

**TDB77:** Pasian in thu a ip manin minthang a, kumpite in thu a gen khiat manin minthang hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian in thu a ip manin a min thang a, kumpite in thu a genkhiat manin minthang-te hi.

**Grammar:** causal: because

### v3
**EN:** The heaven for height, and the earth for depth, and the heart of kings is unsearchable.

**TDB77:** Vantung sang lua a, lei sung a thuk luat mah bangin, kumpipa’ ngaihsutna thuk lua-in kithei zo lo hi.

**ZVS:** Vantung sang lua-in leisung a thuk luat mah bangin kumpipa' ngaihsutna thuk lua-in kithei zolo hi.

**Grammar:** serial verb: -in (and then) | negation: lo | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** Take away the dross from the silver, and there shall come forth a vessel for the finer.

**TDB77:** Ngun a ek teng paaikhia lehang, siksekpa in na hoihpi piangsak thei hi.

**ZVS:** Ngun a ek teng paikhia lehang sikseekpa in na hoihpi piangsak thei hi.

**Grammar:** causative: -sak suffix

### v5
**EN:** Take away the wicked from before the king, and his throne shall be established in righteousness.

**TDB77:** Kumpipa kiang pan migilote nilkhia lehang, thuman-a ama ukna kip thei bek hi.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa kiang pan migilote nilkhia lehang thumanin ama ukna kip thei bek hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** Put not forth thyself in the presence of the king, and stand not in the place of great men:

**TDB77:** Kumpipa mai-ah na om ciangin milian kineih kei in la, miliante’ tutna ding munah tu kha kei in.

**ZVS:** Kumpipa mai-ah na om ciangin milian kineih kei in la, miliante' tutna ding munah tukha kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v7
**EN:** For better it is that it be said unto thee, Come up hither; than that thou shouldest be put lower in the presence of the prince whom thine eyes have seen.

**TDB77:** Uliante a awn dingin hia lai-ah va tusuk in hong kicih sangin, hih lai-ah hong tuto in, hong kicih ding hoihzaw hi.

**ZVS:** Uliante a awn dingin hua lai-ah va tusuk in, hong kicih sangin hih lai-ah hong tuto in, hong kicih ding hoihzaw hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v8
**EN:** Go not forth hastily to strive, lest thou know not what to do in the end thereof, when thy neighbour hath put thee to shame.

**TDB77:** Thu khatpeuh na theih ciangin, thukhen zum tun’ pah kei in. Na vengpa in nang’ khialhna hong laktel kik leh, nang’ bang lawh nawn ding na hi hiam?

**ZVS:** Thu khatpeuh na theih ciangin thukhen zum tun' pah kei in. Na vengpa in nang' khialhna hong laktel kik leh nang bang lawh nawn ding na hi hiam?

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v9
**EN:** Debate thy cause with thy neighbour himself; and discover not a secret to another:

**TDB77:** Na thuneihpih na vengpa bek mah tawh thu kisel in la, mi khat’ thusim midang tungah genthaang kei in,

**ZVS:** Na thuneihpih na vengpa bek mah tawh thu kiseel in la, mi khat' thusim midang' tungah genthang kei in.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v10
**EN:** Lest he that heareth it put thee to shame, and thine infamy turn not away.

**TDB77:** Tua hi kei leh amah hehin nang hong maizumsak ding a, na minsiatna bei ngei lo ding hi.

**ZVS:** Tua hi kei leh amah hehin nang hong maizumsak ding a, na minsiatna bei ngei nawnlo ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will

### v11
**EN:** A word fitly spoken is like apples of gold in pictures of silver.

**TDB77:** A kilawm hun-a kammal khat paukhiatna in, ngunpeek tunga kham tang khat a kisuang tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** A kilawm hunin kammal khat paukhiatna pen ngun pek tunga kham pek khat a kisuang tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v12
**EN:** As an earring of gold, and an ornament of fine gold, so is a wise reprover upon an obedient ear.

**TDB77:** Hilhna a sangthei mipa’ adingin a pil thuhilhpa in, kizepna kham bilbah tawh kibang hi.

**ZVS:** Hilhna a sang thei mipa-a' dingin a pil thuhilhpa pen kizepna kham bilbah tawh kibang hi.

**Grammar:** purpose: in order to | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v13

**TDB77:** Amah a sawlte tungah a citak kamtaipa in, an lak hun-a tuikhal vot tawh kibang a, a tote’ lungsim thak suaksak hi.

**ZVS:** Amah a sawlte tungah a citak kamtaipa pen anlak huna tuivot tawh kibang a, a tote' lungsim thaksuak sak hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v14

### v15

**ZVS:** Lungduai-a zolzolna tawh gam ukte kizo thei a, kam nemin pauna tawh aana neite kizo thei hi.

### v16

**TDB77:** Khuaizu na nek ciangin a hoih khop zah bek ne in; tua hi kei leh kham lua-in na lua ding hi.

**ZVS:** Khuaizu na nek ciangin a hoihkhop bek zah ne in. Tua hi kei leh khamlua-in na lua ding hi.
