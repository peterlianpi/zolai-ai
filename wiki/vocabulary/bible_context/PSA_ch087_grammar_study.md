## Verses

### v1
**EN:** His foundation is in the holy mountains.

**TDB77:** Mual siangtho tungah ama phuhsa khuapi om hi.

**ZVS:** Mual Siangtho tungah ama phuhsa khuapi om hi.

### v2
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in Jakob’ teen’na mun khempeuhte sangin Zion kulh kongpi itzaw hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in Israel gam mundang khempeuhte sangin Zion kulhkongpi itzaw hi.

### v3
**EN:** Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah.

**TDB77:** Pasian’ khuapi aw, nangma minthan’na teng hong kigen hi.

**ZVS:** Pasian' khuapi aw, na minthan'na teng hong kigengen hi.

**Grammar:** reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v4
**EN:** I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this man was born there.

**TDB77:** Kei hong theite lakah Izipt leh Babilon ka hel hi; Filistia, Tai-ar leh Ethiopia-te zong—“Hihte pen hua lai mah pan-a piang ahi hi,” amaute in ci ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Kei hong theite lakah Egypt leh Babylon ka hel hi. Filistia, Taia leh Ethiopia-te zong kihel a, “Hihte pen hua lai mah panin a piang ahi hi,” kici ding hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | plural: uh marker

### v5
**EN:** And of Zion it shall be said, This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her.

**TDB77:** Zion thu tawh kisai-in ahih leh, “Minam khempeuh ama sung pan-a suak ahi hi,” kici ding hi; Bang hang hiam cih leh Sang Penpa mahmah in amah kipsak ding hi.

**ZVS:** Zion tawh kisai-in ahih leh, “Minam khempeuh ama sung pana suak ahi hi,” kici ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh Sangpenpa mahmah in amah kipsak ding hi.

**Grammar:** intensifier: very/greatly | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix

### v6
**EN:** The

**TDB77:** Topa in, “Hihte pen hua lai-a suak” ci-in minam khempeuh ciamtehin gelh ding hi.

**ZVS:** { Topa } in, “Hihte pen hua lai-a suak,” ci-in minam khempeuh ciamtehin a gelh ding hi.

**Grammar:** quotative: said/saying | serial verb: -in (and then) | future: will

### v7
**EN:** As well the singers as the players on instruments shall be there: all my springs are in thee.

**TDB77:** Tua sung a teeng mi khempeuh in a kibangin, “Ka lungdamna bulpite khempeuh-te nangma sungah om hi,” ci ding-te hi.

**ZVS:** Tua sunga teng mite in, “Kote' lungdamna khempeuh nangma sungah om hi,” ci ciat ding-te hi.

**Grammar:** future: will | reciprocal: ki- prefix | plural: uh marker
