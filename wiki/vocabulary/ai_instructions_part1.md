## Grammar Rules

- **OSV word order**: `Laibu ka sim hi` = I read a book
- **SOV word order with ergative marker**: `Ka pa in laibu sim hi` = My father reads a book
- **explicit subject marker 'in'**: `Kei in laibu ka sim hi` = I read a book
- **sentence-ending particles**: `Ka pai hi` = I go (present statement)

## Cultural Notes

- Zolai language has flexible word order and ergative-absolutive alignment
- Zolai language has a system of verb stem alternation
- Zolai language is a tonal language with three contrastive tones
- Zolai language has a distinction between short and long vowels
- Zolai language has different registers (formal, standard, colloquial) with varying levels of formality and grammatical complexity
