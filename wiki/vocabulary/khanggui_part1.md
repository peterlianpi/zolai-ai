## Grammar Rules

- **ZVS word order**: `Amaute sang ah pai uhhi` = They are going to school
- **use of 'uh' for third person singular**: `Amah sang ah pai hi` = He is going to school
- **use of 'i' and 'ei' for first person singular and plural**: `I pai hi` = I am going
- **use of 'uh' for third person singular in verb conjugation**: `A pai uhhi` = He/She is going
