
## Theological & Heart (Lung) Vocabulary

The Zolai language has a unique "Lung-Kha" (Heart-Spirit) architecture for expressing emotions, morality, and identity.

| Zolai | English | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Lung** | Heart / Mind | The center of emotion and thought (not the physical organ). |
| **Kha** | Spirit | The divine or eternal essence of a person. |
| **Lungsim** | Mind / Intellect | The conscious thought process. |
| **Lungdam** | Happy / Joy | Literal: "Heart-sweet/good". |
| **Lunghei** | Repent | Literal: "Heart-turn". |
| **Lungkia** | Discouraged | Literal: "Heart-fall". |
| **Lunglut** | Interested | Literal: "Heart-enter". |
| **Hehpihna** | Grace / Mercy | Foundational theological concept in Zolai. |
| **Laisiangtho** | Bible / Holy Book | Literal: "Book-Holy". |
| **Pasian** | God | The supreme being in Zolai cosmology. |
| **Hotkhiatna** | Salvation | Literal: "Rescue-out-ness". |
