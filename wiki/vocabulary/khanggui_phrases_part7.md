## Sentence Patterns

- `[Year] [Month] [Day] ni aa kipan, [Event] pan [Location] ah [Action]` = On [Date], [Event] in [Location] [Action]
  - e.g. `2013 July 24 ni aa kipan, Zomi National Congress – ZNC pan Myanmar kumpi hangin a min kilaih`
- `[Name] in [Topic] cih [Role]` = [Person] on [Topic] as [Role]
  - e.g. `Rev. Job Nang Khaw Thawng in hunzat ding cihte`
- `[Organization] ii [Action] tawh [Event] cih khawmpi bawl dingin pawi committee tetung kiza ahi hi` = [Organization] organized [Event] committee meeting
  - e.g. `Zomi Association for Christian Communication – ZACC ii hanciamna tawh “Zolai Revival” cih khawmpi bawl dingin pawi committee tetung kiza ahi hi`
