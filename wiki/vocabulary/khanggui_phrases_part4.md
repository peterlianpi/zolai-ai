## Sentence Patterns

- `Zopau [X] awsuah` = [X] Zopau language
  - e.g. `Zopau Norwegian awsuah`
- `Zomi [X] khuami` = [X] Zomi people
  - e.g. `Zomi Tedim khuami`
- `[X] leh [Y] pen kibang` = [X] and [Y] are different
  - e.g. `British English leh American English pen kibang`
- `[X] cihte pen [Y] ii malgawmzia` = [X] spelling is [Y] variant
  - e.g. `Colour, Centre cihte pen British English ii malgawmzia`
