## Sentence Patterns

- `o pen ou bang in awsuak hi` = `o` is pronounced like `ou`
  - e.g. `o pen ou bang in awsuak hi`
- `t le i kigawm ngeilo hi` = `t` and `i` do not combine
  - e.g. `t le i kigawm ngeilo hi`
- `C le a,e,o,aw te kigawm ngeilo hi` = `C` does not combine with `a, e, o, aw`
  - e.g. `C le a,e,o,aw te kigawm ngeilo hi`
- `Minpi (surname): capitalize all syllables` = Surname: capitalize all syllables
  - e.g. `Minpi (surname): capitalize all syllables`
- `Minno (given name): capitalize, write together` = Given name: capitalize, write together
  - e.g. `Minno (given name): capitalize, write together`
- `Zogam, Zomi, Zogam ei hong pia Pa Pasian thang hen` = Zogam, Zomi, Zogam — praise God who gave it to us
  - e.g. `Zogam, Zomi, Zogam ei hong pia Pa Pasian thang hen`
