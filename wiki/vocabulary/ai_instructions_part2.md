## Grammar Rules

- **Verb Stem Alternation**: `pai (Stem I) → pai/pain (Stem II)` = go → going
- **Ergative-Absolutive Alignment**: `Pasian in leitung a piangsak hi` = God created the earth
- **Word Order Patterns**: `Laibu ka sim hi` = I read a book
