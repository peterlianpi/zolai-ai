## Sentence Patterns

- `X in Y a khak khin cih a gen hi.` = X said that it had completely blocked Y.
  - e.g. `Amerika galkap in Iran tuikhuk lampi a khak khin cih a gen hi.`
- `X in Z a dawt hi.` = X threatened to do Z.
  - e.g. `Tehran in zong a mun tuamtuamah hong do kik ding ci-in a dawt hi.`
- `X in Y ah Z a kap hi.` = X fired Z at Y.
  - e.g. `Hezbollah in Israel vaang lam ah thaupi sawmthum a kap hi.`
- `Hih pen leitung adingin gimna lian mahmah a hi hi.` = This is a very serious danger for the world.
  - e.g. `Hih pen leitung adingin gimna lian mahmah a hi hi.`
