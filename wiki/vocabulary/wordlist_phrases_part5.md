## Sentence Patterns

- `zo phrase + tawm` = in order to + verb
  - e.g. `naupang amah a kikep tawm theihma tan khoito`
- `zo adjective + bangin` = adverb for + adjective
  - e.g. `sanggam bangin`
- `zo noun + na` = noun for the state of + noun
  - e.g. `hiam gamna`
