# Core Vocabulary (Zolai Standard Format 2018)

Based on the Zolai Standard Format (LT Tuang, 2018), common lexical entries are standardized to prevent ambiguity in translation and literature.

## Abstract Concepts
- `thuman` (truth, righteous)
- `thuman'na` (obedience)
- `thumanna` (truthfulness)
- `lungdam` (thank you, happy)
- `lungdamna` (happiness, joy)
- `kipsak` (confirm, strengthen)

## Honorifics and Personhood
- `Topa` (Lord, Master)
- `Pa` (Father)
- `Nu` (Mother)
- `Tapa` (Son)
- `Tanu` (Daughter)
- `Sanggam` (Sibling)
- `U` (Older sibling)
- `Nau` (Younger sibling)

## Temporal Markers
- `nidang` (formerly, in the past)
- `tuhun` (nowadays)
- `maihun` (future)
- `tawntung` (forever, eternally)

## Religious/Spiritual
- `Pasian` (God)
- `Kha` (Spirit)
- `Vantung` (Heaven, Sky)
- `Leitung` (Earth, World)
- `Siangtho` (Holy, Clean)
- `Mawh` (Sin, Wrong)
- `Hotkhiatna` (Salvation)
- `Thupha` (Blessing)
- `Hehpihna` (Grace, Mercy)
- `Thuciamna` (Covenant, Promise)
- `Lung` (The emotional and logical heart/center)

## Nature and Environment
- `Mual` (Mountain, hill)
- `Gun` (River)
- `Tui` (Water)
- `Vom` (Black, dark)
- `Kang` (White, light)
- `San` (Red)
- `Kham` (Gold, precious)

## Action and Direction
- `-toh` (Upward movement, exaltation)
- `-suk` (Downward movement, incarnation)
- `Paipih` (To go with, accompany)
- `Phat` (To praise, honor)
- `Zui` (To follow, obey)
