# General Knowledge and Vocabulary (English-Zolai Expansion)

This document maps general knowledge concepts, technical terms, and historical/scientific vocabulary from English to Zolai (Tedim).

## 1. History and Cultural Milestones
| English Concept | Zolai (Tedim) | Context / Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Enlightenment / Awareness | Khua muhna | Literally "vision of the village/world". |
| Civilization / Progress | Khantohna | To grow/rise up. |
| History | Tangthu | Traditional stories/records. |
| Research | Research / Sitcianna | Often kept as "Research" or using "investigate thoroughly". |
| Printing / Publication | Laikhenna / Hawmkhiatna | From "khen" (to strike/print) and "hawm" (to distribute). |
| Centennial | Kumza cinna | 100 years completion. |
| Association | Kipawlna | A joining together. |
| Human Rights | Mihingte’ dikna | Humans' rights/righteousness. |
