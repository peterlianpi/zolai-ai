## Sentence Patterns

- `Hih laibu pen Zomite’ lai neih zawh, kum 100 cin lopna leh Zolai tangthu ciaptehna hunthak mualsuang khat ahi hi.` = This book is a new milestone marking the completion of 100 years of Zomi literature and the establishment of Zolai history.
  - e.g. `Hih laibu pen Zomite’ lai neih zawh, kum 100 cin lopna leh Zolai tangthu ciaptehna hunthak mualsuang khat ahi hi.`
- `USA aa om, Zomi khanghamte sungpan, Pu X leh Pi Y te in, thupha piakna nei-te hi.` = Among the younger generation Zomis living in the USA, Pu X and Pi Y offered thanks.
  - e.g. `USA aa om, Zomi khanghamte sungpan, Pu Dal Za Khup leh Pi Cing Za Vai te in, thupha piakna nei uhhi.`
- `Hih laibu pen Zomi Khangnote ii nasepna lianpi khat ahi hi.` = This book is one great work of the Zomi Youth.
  - e.g. `Hih laibu pen Zomi Khangnote ii nasepna lianpi khat ahi hi.`
- `Hih laibu pan aa summeet ki-ngah khempeuh, Pasian, Zogam leh Zomi adingin kizeek kik gai ding ahi hi.` = All the money received from this book will be reinvested for God, Zogam, and the Zomi people.
  - e.g. `Hih laibu pan aa summeet ki-ngah khempeuh, Pasian, Zogam leh Zomi adingin kizeek kik gai ding ahi hi.`
- `A munmun, a gamgam, a khuakhua aa laibu zuak, a hawmsawn leh gensawt kawikawina tawh hong huh, ma a pang a kua mapeuh tungah zongh lungdam mahmah hi.` = We are also very thankful to everyone who helped by selling the book in every place, every region, every village, and by promoting and discussing it.
  - e.g. `A munmun, a gamgam, a khuakhua aa laibu zuak, a hawmsawn leh gensawt kawikawina tawh hong huh, ma a pang a kua mapeuh tungah zongh lungdam mahmah hi.`
- `Biak Pasian in mimal khatciat ii nasepnate khempeuh hong guaksuaksak kei tahen.` = May the living God bless all the works of every single individual.
  - e.g. `Biak Pasian in mimal khatciat ii nasepnate khempeuh hong guaksuaksak kei tahen.`
