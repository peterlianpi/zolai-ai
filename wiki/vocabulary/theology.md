## Syntactic Mapping

### Possessive Particles
- **Traditional:** `Pasian ii thu` (The word of God)
- **Modern Standard:** `Pasian’ thu` (Apostrophe for brevity and natural flow).

### Stem Shifts in Religious Contexts
- **Traditional:** `mu` (independent) vs `muh` (subordinate).
- **Zokam Standard:** Consistent use of `theihna` (knowledge), `muhna` (vision/seeing), `kahna` (crying) using Stem II.

## Comparative References
- **Traditional (TDM):** Bible translation from the early 20th century, often uses archaic "Toupa" and distinct spelling patterns.
- **Modern (Zokam):** Aligns with the 2018 LT Tuang standard, emphasizing phonetic clarity (e.g., `G` vs `Ng`) and standardized plural markers (`...te`).
