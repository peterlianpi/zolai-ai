## Sentence Patterns

- `OSV` = Object-Subject-Verb
  - e.g. `Laibu ka sim hi.`
- `SOV with in` = Subject-Object-Verb with ergative marker
  - e.g. `Ka pa in laibu sim hi.`
- `Subject + Verb` = Subject and Verb only
  - e.g. `Ka sim hi.`
