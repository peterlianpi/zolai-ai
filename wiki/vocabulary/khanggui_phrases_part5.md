## Sentence Patterns

- `X kumin Y khat Z hi` = In X year, Y text Z is published
  - e.g. `1965 kumin, 'Thu kituahna' lai kici aa`
- `X leh Y gam ah Z kiphuan aa` = In X and Y regions, Z is established
  - e.g. `Manipur gam leh Mizogam dong ah kikhawm ahi hi`
- `X in Y kici laibu khat Z lai tawh na bawlkhiat hi` = X's analysis of Y texts in Z language
  - e.g. `'Tiddim Chin a Descriptive Analysis of Two Texts' a kici laibu khat English (Mikang/Mang) lai tawh na bawlkhiat hi`
