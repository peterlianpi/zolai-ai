| Zolai | English | Note |
|---|---|---|
| gik | heavy | context: describing something as heavy |
| lua | too much | no direct context found |
| mahmah | very | used to emphasize a quality or quantity |
| tapa | father | used in several stories to refer to a father figure |
| tua | that | used as a pronoun to refer to something previously mentioned |
| pasian | God | used in several stories to refer to a higher power |
| gam | village | used to refer to a community or village |

## Grammar Rules

- **ZVS word order**: `A Deihna: Sum huah simin kaplo, lungnuam, nopsa cihna hi khin pah tuanlo aa` = God said, 'Let there be light, and there was light, and God saw that it was good'

## Cultural Notes

- Importance of community and family in Tedim Zolai culture
- Respect for elders and tradition in Tedim Zolai culture
