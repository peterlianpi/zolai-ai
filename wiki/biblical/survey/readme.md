# Zolai Biblical Curriculum Survey: Master Index

This directory contains the systematic linguistic analysis of the **66-book ZVS 2018 Zolai Bible**. This survey serves as the primary data source for the **Zolai AI Pedagogical Curriculum**, aligning biblical texts with the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR).

## Survey Batches

The Bible is divided into seven curriculum-focused batches:

### [Batch 1: Pentateuch (Genesis - Deuteronomy)](pentateuch.md)
*   **Difficulty:** A1 - B1
*   **Focus:** Core identity, foundational law, and origins.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** Covenant formulas, legal imperatives, and genealogy.

### [Batch 2: Historical Books (Joshua - Esther)](historical.md)
*   **Difficulty:** B1 - B2
*   **Focus:** Narrative leadership, institutional history, and public prose.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** First-person leadership rhetoric, administrative terms, and narrative transitions.

### [Batch 3: Poetic Books (Job - Song of Solomon)](poetic.md)
*   **Difficulty:** B2 - C1
*   **Focus:** Emotional expression, wisdom aphorisms, and sensory metaphors.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** Parallelism, antithetical logic, and sensory vocabulary.

### [Batch 4: Major Prophets (Isaiah - Daniel)](prophets.md)
*   **Difficulty:** B2 - C1
*   **Focus:** Grand grand rhetoric, visionary symbolism, and courtly prose.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** Prophet oracles, vision formulas, and royalty themes.

### [Batch 5: Minor Prophets (Hosea - Malachi)](minor_prophets.md)
*   **Difficulty:** B1 - B2
*   **Focus:** Concise ethical warnings and agricultural metaphors.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** Sharp moral imperatives, dialogic debate (Q&A), and natural signs.

### [Batch 6: Gospels & Acts (Matthew - Acts)](gospels_acts.md)
*   **Difficulty:** A2 - C1
*   **Focus:** Parabolic storytelling, direct teaching, and early mission history.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** Parable intros ("Like unto..."), I Am statements, and missionary travelogues.

### [Batch 7: Epistles & Revelation (Romans - Revelation)](epistles_revelation.md)
*   **Difficulty:** B2 - C1
*   **Focus:** Propositional logic, corrective ethics, and apocalyptic visuals.
*   **Linguistic Keys:** Logical conjunctions (Therefore/Because), ethical analogies, and celestial visuals.

## Pedagogical Integration

This survey is being used to:
1.  **Tag Datasets:** Proficiency levels are applied to the `zolai_parallel_master.jsonl` based on these findings.
2.  **Generate Lessons:** The AI Tutor selects texts based on the "Skill" identified in each book.
3.  **Linguistic Standards:** Refines the ZVS 2018 grammar rules for conditional negation and morphology.
