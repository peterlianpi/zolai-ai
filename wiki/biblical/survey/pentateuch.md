# Biblical Linguistic Survey: Batch 1 (Pentateuch)

This document contains a linguistic analysis of the first five books of the Bible (Torah), mapping their unique grammatical patterns, domain-specific vocabulary, and CEFR-aligned proficiency levels.

## 1. Genesis (Piancil)
**Genre:** Narrative / Pre-History / Biography
**Proficiency Level:** A2 (Narrative) to B1 (Theology)

### Core Patterns
- **Genealogy Formula (A2):** `[Name]’ tapa [Son's Name]` (The son of [Name] is [Son's Name]).
- **Narrative Sequencing (B1):** `Tua khit ciangin` (After that), `Tua ahih ciangin` (Therefore / And it came to pass).
- **Creation Verbs (B1):** `Pianciilsak` (Created), `Bawl` (Made), `Piansak` (Brought forth).

### Verse Evidence
- **Gen 1:1:** `A kipatcil-in Pasian in vantung leh leitung piansak hi.` (In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth.)
- **Gen 5:3:** `Adam a nuntak sawmkhai leh sawmthum a phak ciangin, amah tawh a kibang tapa khat nei a...` (And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness...)

## 2. Exodus (Pusuahna)
**Genre:** Legal / Narrative / Liberation
**Proficiency Level:** B1 (Action Narrative) to B2 (Legalism)

### Core Patterns
- **Prohibitive Commands (B1):** `Na hih kei ding uh hi` (You shall not do).
- **Standard Interrogatives (B1):** `Bang ci-a ... ding i hi hiam?` (How shall we ...?).
- **Passive-Voice Prefixes (B2):** `Ki-` (e.g., `Kideiduhna` - favoritism, `Kithahna` - murder).

### Verse Evidence
- **Exo 20:3:** `Keimah longal pasian dang na nei kei ding hi.` (Thou shalt have no other gods before me.)
- **Exo 14:13:** `Lau kei un; dingkip un la, tu ni-a Topa in note hong gumpuina en un;` (Fear ye not, stand still, and see the salvation of the LORD, which he will shew to you to day:)

---

## 3. Leviticus (Siampite Nasep)
**Genre:** Legal / Ritualistic / Instructional
**Proficiency Level:** B2 (Formal Instruction)

### Core Patterns
- **Ritualistic Protocols:** `Topa mai-ah ... go ding hi` (Shall slaughter before the Lord).
- **Conditional Rituals:** `Mi khatpeuh in ... paipih leh` (If any person brings ...).
- **Formal Address:** `Topa in Moses kiangah, “Hih bangin gen in,” ci-in hopih hi.` (The Lord spoke to Moses, saying, "Speak thus...")

### Verse Evidence
- **Lev 1:2:** `Israel mite kiangah hih bangin gen in: No sung pan mi khatpeuh in Topa tungah biakna a paipih leh...` (Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, If any man of you bring an offering unto the LORD...)

---

## 4. Numbers (Gamlak Vakna)
**Genre:** Statistical / Sequential / Narrative
**Proficiency Level:** B1 (Logic) to B2 (Organization)

### Core Patterns
- **Census Statistics:** `[Group] minam tul [N] leh [N] pha hi.` (The [Group] tribe was [N] thousand and [N].)
- **Sequential Lists:** `... sung panin ...' tapa ...` (Of the [Group], [Name] the son of [Name]...)
- **Organizational Hierarchies:** `Lutang` (Head/Leader), `Makaite` (Princes), `Hon` (Company/Host).

### Verse Evidence
- **Num 1:3:** `... galdo dingin a kuan thei ding mi khempeuh a hon a honin na sim ding uh hi.` (... all that are able to go forth to war in Israel: thou and Aaron shall number them by their armies.)

---

## 5. Deuteronomy (Thu Hilhkikna)
**Genre:** Hortatory / Sermonic / Law Recap
**Proficiency Level:** B2 (Advanced Logic & Ethics)

### Core Patterns
- **Hortatory Admonition:** `Israel-te aw, za un...` (Hear, O Israel...).
- **Conditional Blessings/Curses (B2):** `Na zuih uh leh ... na zuih kei uh leh ...` (If you follow ... if you do not follow ...).
- **Logical Deductions:** `Tua ahih ciangin` (Therefore), `Bang hang hiam cih leh` (Because).

### Verse Evidence
- **Deut 6:4-5:** `Israelte aw, za un; Topa i Pasian in Topa khat bek ahi hi; Topa note’ Pasian, na lungsim khempeuh... na it ding uh hi.` (Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD: And thou shalt love the LORD thy God with all thine heart...)
- **Deut 28:1:** `Tu ni-a kong piak ama thupiak khempeuh lim takin na zui-in...` (And it shall come to pass, if thou shalt hearken diligently unto the voice of the LORD thy God, to observe and to do all his commandments...)
