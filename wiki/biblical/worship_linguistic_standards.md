# Zolai Worship & Formal Address Patterns (ZVS 2018)

## 1. Addressing the Divine (Vocative)
Always use the vocative particle `aw` for direct address.
- ✅ `Topa aw, ...`
- ✅ `Pasian aw, ...`

## 2. Inclusive Plural Agreement (The "i" Rule)
The first-person inclusive plural marker `i` (we) must NEVER be combined with the plural marker `uhhi`.
- ✅ `Na min siangtho i phat hi.` (We praise Your holy name.)
- ❌ `Na min siangtho i phat uhhi.` (Forbidden)

## 3. Praise Patterns (Divine Attributes)
Use the pattern: `[Possessive Marker] + [Attribute/Name] + i + [Verb] + hi`.
- **For Second Person (Your):** `Na min siangtho i phat hi.`
- **For Third Person (His/God's):** `A min siangtho i phat hi.`

*Note: The choice of 'Na' or 'A' depends on whether you are directly addressing the Divine (Vocative) or describing the Divine.*

## 4. Gathering/Worship Patterns
Use the pattern: `[Sanggamte] + tawh + i + [Verb] + hi`.
- ✅ `Sanggamte tawh biakinn ah i kikhawm hi.`
- ✅ `Pasian biak dingin i pai hi.`

## 5. Standard Orthography for Ritual Terms
- **Holy:** `siangtho` (joined: `min siangtho`, `Pasian siangtho`)
- **Glorious/Name:** `minthang` (joined compound)
- **Church:** `biakinn` (joined compound)
- **Worship:** `biakna` (Stem II of `bia`)
