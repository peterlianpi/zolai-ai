## Detailed Usage

### `tawh` — with / by means of / instrument
- `Topa tawh kikhawl hi` = walked with God
- `Namsau tawh thah hi` = killed with a sword
- `Kampau tawh ahi zongin` = not only with words
- `Hehpihna tawh` = with grace/kindness

### `ah` — in / at / into (locative)
- `Jerusalem ah teeng hi` = dwelt in Jerusalem
- `Inn sungah lut hi` = entered into the house
- `Vantungah` = in heaven
- `Leitungah` = on earth

### `tungah` — to / unto (dative, things/places)
- `Amah tungah pia hi` = gave to him
- `Topa tungah thunget hi` = prayed to the Lord
- `Note tungah gen hi` = said to you (all)

### `kiangah` — to / unto (personal, direct address)
- `David kiangah gen hi` = said to David
- `Amaute kiangah` = to them
- `Ka kiangah` = to me

### `hangin` — because of / for the sake of / through
- `Ama' hangin` = because of him / for his sake
- `Pasian' hangin` = for God's sake
- `Khazih hangin` = through Christ

### `bangin` — like / as / according to
- `Topa' thu bangin` = according to the Lord's word
- `A sih mah bangin` = just as he died
- `Ka gamtat mah bangin` = as I do

### `panin` — from / out of
- `Gam sung panin` = out of the land
- `Ama' tung panin` = from him
- `Vantung panin` = from heaven

### `sungah` — inside / within / in the midst of
- `Inn sungah` = inside the house
- `Biakinn sungah` = inside the temple
- `Huan laizangah` = in the midst of the garden (laizangah = in the middle)

### `nadingin` — in order to / for the purpose of
- `Pai nadingin` = in order to go
- `Hotkhiat nadingin` = in order to save
- `Theih nadingin` = in order to know

### `manin` — because / since (causal suffix)
- `A om lo manin` = because it does not exist
- `Ahih manin` = because it is so (most common: 258 occurrences)
- `Ka it manin` = because I love

### `ading` — for / for the sake of
- `Eite ading` = for us
- `Amah ading` = for him
- `Pasian ading` = for God

### `mateng` — until
- `Ka hong pai mateng` = until I come (78 occurrences)
- `A dam mateng` = until he recovers
- `Tua hun mateng` = until that time

## Possession Marker: Apostrophe `'`

Possession shown by `'` (apostrophe/pawfi) after the possessor:
- `Pasian' inn` = God's house
- `David' tapa` = David's son
- `ama' zi` = his wife
- `ka' lungsim` = my heart (often written `ka lungsim`)
- `amau' omna` = their dwelling

**Rule:** Apostrophe marks possession. Different from contraction (`na'ng` = you + going).
