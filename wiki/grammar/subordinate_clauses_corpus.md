# Zolai Subordinate Clauses & Connectors — Corpus Verified

> Source: TDB77 Bible (28,756 verses)
> Last updated: 2026-04-17

## 1. Temporal Clauses (Time)

### When: `ciangin` (1,740 occurrences)
- `A tun ciangin...` = When (he) arrived...
- `A nek khit ciangin...` = After eating...
- `Zingsangin David a thawh ma-in...` = Before David rose in the morning...

### While: `lai takin` / `laitakin`
- `Kumpipa tawh kiho-in a om lai takin Nathan hong tum hi.` = While she was talking with the king, Nathan came in.
- `Ka om lai takin...` = While I was there...

### After: `khit ciangin`
- `A nek khit ciangin...` = After eating...
- `Ni tampi khit ciangin...` = After many days...
- `Tua khit ciangin...` = After that...

### Before (time): `ma-in`
- `Ama hun ma-in` = before his time
- `Ka sih ma-in` = before I die
- `Zingsangin David a thawh ma-in` = before David rose in the morning
- `Tu ma-in` = previously / before now

### Before (space/presence): `mai-ah`
- `Topa mai-ah` = before the Lord / in the Lord's presence
- `Kumpipa mai-ah` = before the king
- `Topa' biakna tau mai-ah` = before the altar of the Lord

**Key distinction:**
- `ma-in` = before in **time** (temporal)
- `mai-ah` = before in **space/presence** (locative)

### Until: `mateng` (78 occurrences)
- `Ka hong pai mateng` = until I come
- `A dam mateng` = until he recovers
- `A bei mateng` = until it subsides

### Up to a point: `ciangdong` / `dongin`
- `Ciangdong` = up to that point / as far as
- `Tua hun ciangdong` = up to that time

## 2. Manner Clauses (How)

### Just as / Even as: `mah bangin`
- `Adam sungah mi khempeuh a sih mah bangin Khazih sungah mi khempeuh a nungtasak ding hi.` = Just as in Adam all die, even so in Christ all shall be made alive.
- `A siangtho mah bangin na siangtho uh hen.` = Be holy as he is holy.
- `Ka gamtat mah bangin note zong gamtat un.` = Do as I do.

### According to: `bangin`
- `Topa' thu bangin` = according to the Lord's word
- `A kisam bangin` = as appointed / as arranged

---

## 3. Result Clauses

### So that / With the result: `ahih manin` / `manin`
- `Amah in muan'huaina mun-ah amaute paipih ahih manin amaute lau lo uh hi.` = He led them safely so that they did not fear.
- `Tua ahih manin...` = With the result that... / Therefore...

---

## 4. Exception & Exclusion

### Only: `bek`
- `Moses bek in hong nai ding hi.` = Only Moses shall come near.
- `Note bek tawh a bawl hi lo.` = Not with you only.
- `Khat bek` = only one
- `Keimah bek` = only me / I alone

### Except / Save: `longal`
- `Kha Siangtho longal kuamah in a thei kei hi.` = No one knows except the Holy Spirit.
- `Zeisu Khazih longal` = except Jesus Christ

### Nothing / No one: `kuamah` + `kei`
- `Kuamah in a thei kei hi.` = No one knows.
- `Bangmah nei het lo hi.` = Has nothing at all.

---

## 5. Addition & Contrast

### Not only...but also: `tua tham loin...zong`
- `Tua tham loin, sumpi keempa in lungsim dik a nei ding hi.` = Moreover, a steward must be found faithful.
- `Tua tham loin...zong` = Not only that...but also

### Furthermore / Moreover: `tua tham loin`
- `Tua tham loin amah in Topa' thuciamna singkuang mai-ah na semin...` = Moreover he appointed Levites to minister before the ark.

### Also / Too: `zong`
- `Kei zong ka pai ding hi.` = I also will go.
- `Amaute zong...` = They also...
- `Tua tham loin...zong` = Not only...but also

### But / However: `ahih hangin` (912 occurrences)
- `Ahih hangin ka pai kei hi.` = But I did not go.
- `Ahih hangin mual tunga biaknate kila khia lo hi.` = But the high places were not removed.

### Although / Even though: `napi` / `napi-in`
- `Ta taktak ahi napi-in gimna tawlna a thuakna tawh thuman'na a sin hi.` = Although truly a Son, he learned obedience through suffering.
- `Tu-in na tampi a om napi-in pumpi khat bek a om hi.` = Now there are many members, yet only one body.

---

## 6. Degree

### Very / Greatly: `mahmah`
- `Lian mahmah hi.` = Very great.
- `Heh mahmah hi.` = Very angry.

### So...that: `[adj] mahmah...manin`
- `Tangvalte' khialhna lian mahmah hi.` = The sin of the young men was very great.

### How much more: `a lian zaw`
- `Eite in vantungmite thu i khen ding thei lo na hi uh hiam? Tua ahih ciangin leitung thu...` = Do you not know we will judge angels? How much more things of this life!
