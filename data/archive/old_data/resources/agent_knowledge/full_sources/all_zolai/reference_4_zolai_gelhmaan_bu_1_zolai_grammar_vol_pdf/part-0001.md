Gentehna2in, ka laigelhna pawlkhat2ah “ih” (“ih gam ih lei”) ci2
in ka zangh2a, pawlkhat2ah “i” (“i gam i lei”) ci2in ka zangh hi.
[Word processing progam2ah, “i” (small letter) i khet ciangin,
“I” (capital letter) hong suak hi. Tua ahih manin, “ih” ka zangh
hi. Tu2in, “i” (small) ka khet ciangin, “I” (capital letter) a suak
lo dingin, bawl thei ta ka hih manin, “i” ka zangh kik hi.] Tuate
pen, ka gelh laitak2a bang lian2in, hih laibu sung2ah kitei kik hi.

5.2 Pawlkhat2ah, ka gelh masa2te, a hi bang lian2in la leng, ka
gelh nunung2te tawh kikhentel thei lo thei2in lungsim buaina a
piang kha thei ding om hi. Tuate pen, a nunung lam2a ka gelh
dan2in ka puah hi.

Gentehna2in, 197021980 lai2in vowel pen “lai2aw” ka ci2a, tu
(200622010) ciangin, “laimung” ka ci hi. Nidang2a “lai2aw” ka
cihna mun teng2ah “laimung” ci2in ka kheel hi. Tua bangmah2
in, tua lai2in, phoneme pen “awmal” ka ci hi. Tu2in, syllable pen
“awmal” ci ka hih manin, phoneme pen “awmal” ci lai leng,
buai huai ahih manin, phoneme pen “awbul” ka ci hi. Tua ahih
manin, tua hun lai2a phoneme pen “awmal” ci2a ka gelh pen, hih
laibu sung2ah “awbul” ci2in, ka laih hi.

Thupatna: Zolai Telkan Un!
   Mahatma Gandhi in, “Lai a nei lo minam a om leh
mangthang ding hi,” ci hi. Tua ahih leh ei Zomite i mangthang
ding hiam? Kimangthang lo ding hi. Bang hang hiam cih leh,
ei’n lai nei hi hang. Tua ahih leh, tua i lai neih a kicing hiam?
Kicing nai lo hi. A kicin’ na’ng2a tavuan nei kuate hiam?
Minam dangte hi lo2in, Zomite mah hi pah hi.

   Tu hun laitak2in, leitung bup2a a kizang lai in Manglai ahi hi.
Leitung mun tuamtuam2ah Manglai a kisinna pen, Manglai2ah,
kammal leh zatna a kicin’ man ahi hi. Tua bang2a a kicin’
na’ngin Mangkangte in na hanciam uh hi.

      Kammal i hauh theih na’ngin lampi tuamtuam om hi. Tuate
in:

      (1) A omsa i lai, kepcingna (preservation)
      (2) A kimangngilh khinsate phot (phong) kikna (revival)
      (3) A om nai lo kammalte, phuat thakna (invention or

    Manglai bek2ah hi lo2in, Zolai2ah zong, Kamzepna
(Punctuation) i nei hi. Tua Kamzepnate (Punctuations) in, lai i
atna2ah, i cihnopna hong kicingsak hi; hong kilangsak hi; hong
