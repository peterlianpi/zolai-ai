By Taang Zomi
Hih bangin, Zomi Nam Ni muakna laimat (sticker) a
kihawmkhiat pen thupi sa ing.

Gelhmaan (grammar) pen ka uukna khat ahih manin, “I’m
Proud of Zomi” cih laigelh tawh kisai2in, ka ngaihsutna hong
gelh nuam ing.

I am Proud of Zomi
I am Proud of a Zomi
I am Proud of Being a Zomi

Hih a tung2a teng pen a khial om lo hi. Ahi zongin a khiatna
(semantics) kibang lo hi.

“I am proud of Zomi” (“I am proud of a Zomi” hi lo) ci2a i gelh
ciangin, Zomi a kici mi, thu, na, silsiam pen a huampi2a i genna
ahi hi.

“I am proud of a Zomi” (“I am proud of Zomi” hi lo) ci2a i gelh
ciangin Zomi a kici mi, thu, na, silsiam sung pan2a khat, i genna
ahi hi.
