                     THUMAPUI
        Labupi leh Lathabu sut ding ZCMC Executive
Committee thuphasah banga suhdih dinga Office Bearer te
ngansie ahi dungzui in et zou leh sut zou a, a um tah ziehin i
Pa Pasian tungah lungdam thu i tut masa uhi.
        Zou Presbyterian Church Synod, Manipur Evangelical
Lutheran Church leh Zo-Christian Bible Church kigawm a
Zou Khristian Labu (Labupi leh Lathabu) gawmkhawm ding a
phasah ahi dungzui in gawmkhawm a sut ahi.
        Hamlepau lam ah “Zou Holy Bible” leh “Zou Literature
Society” pansan a hing kibawl ahia, theitawp kisuo hinanleh
mihing hina dawl ah suhkhiel um thei ahina ah a dihloute
kigensie mailou in kithuzah a, i sut kia lam chiengua a dih sem
ding a pan la thei dingin i kingen uhi.
        Laphuotute tup leh dawi sumillou a bawl ahiziehin,
labu lui a toh a kikhiehna zong um thei ding ahia, etsahna
dingin Latha No. 77na ah “A tam zawn ke’n sang vang” chipen
a phuotute chi dan in “Ale li in sang vang” chia hen ahi bang a
kiheng zong um ding hi.
        Tam Labu sutna ding neilou a ZCMC a um lai in Zou
Presbyterian Church Synod in hing sutsah in a bei zousie ahing
tuohna tunguah a kiphalnate ziehun kipah thu i tut ua, Pasian
in guolzawlna tam zaw sem a vua ding i thumna uh ahi.
        Mi koipou in tam laa a sah a, Pasian guolzawlna donga
Mangpa Jesu pahtawi a um ding khu ZCMC tup-le-ngim ahi.
        Tam Khristian Labu sa a, zangkha zousie tahsa leh hagau
a i khantouna ding un PASIAN in hing guolzawlta hen.
        Mangpa’n thupina tangta hen.
