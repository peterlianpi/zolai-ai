                                           Thu Masa
Hih laibu, “Zeisu Christ Pianna Khanggui” laibu sungah Laisiangtho siksanna bekbek tampi
kimu thei hi. Leitung ah Topa Zeisu Christ hong suakna khanggui thu limtakin etcianna kinei
hi. Hih khanggui sung aa mimal a tamzaw ii tangthute, etphatna le lungngailhna tawh
thalaaknate kimu thei hi. Hih laibu ah deihkaihna omlo aa, a khanggui zui-in amau tangthu ciat,
a baanbaan aa kigualsuk hi.

                                          Laibu
A masa in khanggui kizom khempeuh “A Huampi” aa kigelhsuk gai hi. Tua pan a huampiteng
“Tom Laakna” kineisuk beh hi. Hih laibu sungah Zeisu hong suakna khanggui hoihlohnateng
pan "Tha Laakna" le "Phawk Khakhuai Thute" kimu thei hi. Azom ah, hih thuteng ii
Laisiangtho munte kigelh hi. A kithei diak, ahihkei leh thu a tuam suah diakzawdeuh
Laisiangtho munte uum (box/table) in kigelh hi.




                                                                                    5 (LTTuang)

                                          A Huampi
Pasian, Adam, Seth
Pasian ii ta Adam ci-in Luke lungdamna laibu sung aa Topa Zeisu Christ ii pianna khanggui
ciaptehna ah kigelh hi. Adam le Eve in Cain le Abel nei aa, Cain in a nau Abel a that khitciang,
Adam in Seth kici tapa khat neikik hi. Seth ii khanggui pan Zeisu Christ hong suak hi.

Pasian Biak Kipan, A Min Lawh Kipan, Vantung Ah Kilato
Seth in Enosh kici tapa khat nei hi. Tua hun aa kipan mite in Topa bia-in a Min lawh kipan hi.
Enosh khit khang lina ah Enok (Enoch) suak hi. Enok pen Pasian tawh kikhawlin Pasian in
vantung ah lato aa kimu nawnlo hi. A sihna, a sih hun cih bang ciaptehna omlo hi.

A Tupa Samsiat
Enok (Enoch) khit khang thumna ah Noah suak hi. Noah hun pen leitung pian zawh aa kipan
leitung bei dong a om ngeilo ding tuicinpi a tun hun ahi hi. Noah in lenggahzu kham aa a
guaktangin a om laitak, a tapa Ham in a zahmawh sel saklo aa a sanggam nihte tung ah gen
ahih manin Noah in Ham ii tapa Canaan samsiat hi. Ham ii khialhna pen a tapa Canaan in
thuaklawh hi. Noah ii zukhamna le Ham in a pa a maizumsakna cihte panin a suan a khak sung
vuah samsiatna hong piang hi. Shem le Jafeth in a pa uh (Noah) ii hoihlohnate selsakin a
pholaakloh manun a pa thupha piakna ngah hi. Hih unau 3 lak ah Shem ii khanggui pan Zeisu
Christ hong suak hi.

A Kilemlo Khanggui Nih Piang
Shem khit khang sawmna ah Abraham suak hi. Abraham in Pasian kamciam a tangtun theih
nadingin mihing ngaihsut tawm, a zi geelna mang khial kha hi. Tua hangin Hagar tawh a ngah
suanlekhakte le Sarah tawh a ngah suanlekhakte ii kilemlohna pan leitung ah buaina tampi
piang hi. Abraham in Pasian tungah Isaac tawh melhal biakpiakna a neih ding ciangin a zi,
Isaac ii nu, Sarah a kuppihna thu, mihing ngaihsutna geelnate Laisiangtho sung ah omlo hi.
Abraham in amah a kithat ding alau manin a zi, Sarah pen a sanggamnu hi ci-in Kumpi Pharoah
khem aa Kumpi Abimelech zongh khem leuleu hi. Abraham in Keturah tawh ta 6 nei hi. Hagar
tawh tapa khat nei-in amin Ishmael ahi hi. Sarah tawh tapa khat nei-in amin Isaac ahi hi. Tua
teng lak ah Isaac ii khanggui pan Zeisu Christ hong suak hi.

A Zi Pen A Sanggamnu Ci-in Zuaukhem
Isaac in a pa, Abraham mah bangin amah a kithat ding alau manin a zi, Rebecca pen a
sanggamnu hi ci-in Kumpi Abimelech khem hi. Isaac in Rebecca tawh tapapeeng Esau le Jacob
nei hi. Issac in Esau itzaw aa Rebecca in Jacob itzaw hi. Jacob in a nu sawl bangin a pa, Isaac
khem aa thupha kipia sak hi. Isaac in zongh a zi le a tapa kipawl in a khemna thuak aa Jacob
thupha pia kha hi. Isaac in Jacob thupha a piak khitciang Esau ading thupha om nawnlo hi. Tua
aa kipan Esau in anau Jacob thahsawm hi. Tua panin Jacob zongh a nu sawl bangin a pute (a
nu' sanggam, Laban) omna ah galtai hi.

A Pa Khem
Jacob in Laban ading kum sagih sung na a sep leh a tanu Rachel tawh kiteng sak ding ci aa, ahi
zongin khem aa Rachel ii a u, Leah tawh kiteng sak hi. Laban in ko gam ah a uzaw kaanin a
nauzaw piak ding ngeina omlo hi cihsan hi. Jacob in a pa khem aa, aman zongh a pu Laban ii
khemna thuakkik kha veve hi. Tua panin Laban tanu a uzaw, Leah tawh kitengin ta 6 nei hi. A
pu ading nasem suak aa Rachel thalawh in a ngah khitteh Rachel tawh ta 2 (Joseph le Benjamin)
nei hi. A pu Laban ii piak nasem numei Bilhah le Zilpah tawh zongh ta nei hi. Jacob ii ngaih
Rachel tawh a ngah suanlekhak sung pan Zeisu Christ hong suakloin a deihloh Leah tawh a
neih tapa 6 lak ah Judah ii khanggui pan hong suak hi.

A Monu Tawh Ta Nei
Judah in a tapa upen Er adingin Tamar kici numei khat laaksak hi. Er pen Topa' muhna ah
hoihlo ahih manin Topa' thahna thuak hi. Tua ciangin Tamar pen meigong suak hi. Tua panin
Tamar pen numei kizuak kineihin a teekpa Judah tawh lumkhawm aa tapeeng ngah uhhi. A
tapeeng pan a khut a dawkkhia masazaw pen a uzaw (Zerah) ci hi. Ahi zongin a khut a
dawkkhia masalo pen (Perez) suakkhia masazaw hi. Judah in zipi nei, ta zongh nei napi tuate
ii suanlekhak sung pan Zeisu Christ hong suaklo aa, Judah in numei kizuak a sak, a monu tawh
a ngah Perez ii khanggui pan hong suak hi.

Numei Kizuak Tawh Kiteng
Perez khit khang ngana ah Salmon suak hi. Salmon pen Rahab tawh kiteng hi. Rahab pen
Joshua te in Jericho khua a do lai un, Israel galkap thukansimte a sel, a honkhia numei kizuaknu
ahi hi. Salmon in numei kizuak Rahab tawh a ngah tapa, Boaz ii khanggui pan Zeisu Christ
hong suak hi.

Meigong Tawh Kiteng
Boaz pen Ruth ii pasal kik ahi hi. Ruth pen Moab minam hi aa Naomi ii mo ahi hi. Naomi ii
tapa, Ruth ii pasal a sih khitciangin Boaz tawh a kiteng ahi hi. Amau suanlekhak sung pan
Zeisu Christ hong suak hi.

Mi’ Zi Tawh Mawhin, A Pasal Thatsak
Boaz khit khang thumna ah David suak hi. Kumpi David in zi le ta tampi nei napi, a galkap
Uriah ii zi Bathsheba mawhpih in ta nei hi. Kumpi David in a mawhnate a sel nadingin
Bathsheba ii pasal, a ma galkap tektek ii sih nading vaihawm hi. A zi dangte tawh a neih
suanlekhak sung pan Zeisu Christ hong suaklo hi. A that galkappa' zi, Bathsheba tawh a ngah
a tapa Solomon ii suan pan tonu Mary ii pasal, Joseph suak hi. A that galkappa' zi, Bathsheba
tawh a ngah a tapa Nathan ii suan pan tonu Mary suak hi.

Milim Bia Zi Nei
Solomon in zi tampi nei aa minam dang, biakna upna dang tampi zongh kihel hi. A zite in
milim a biak ding uh zongh Solomon in phal hi. Solomon in a zi tampitak lak pan Naamah
tawh Rehoboam kici tapa khat ngah hi. Rehoboam ii khanggui pan Zeisu Christ hong suak hi.

Kumpi Gilo
Rehoboam pen kumpi gilo, kumpi hoihloin kiciamteh hi. Ama hun ciang Israel gam phel nih
kisuah hi. Tua bangin Israel gam a tapkhap ding pen Pasian in Solomon hunlai pekin a geel
kholhsa ahi hi. Hih pen Solomon in Pasian awiloh aa a gamtat man ahi hi. Beh 12 lak pan a
neupen le a hatlopen Benjamin beh le a behpih Judah behte bekin Rehoboam panpih hi. Levi
beh pen Siampi nam ahih manin guptuam paihtuam panpih tuam neiloin a gam phel nih tuak
ah om uhhi. A kawm kawikawituak zongh om hi. Tualo beh dang khempeuh in Rehoboam
langpan uhhi.

            Rehoboam khit khang sawmnih le lina ah Tonu Mary ii lawmpa, Joseph suak hi. A
            khang sawmnih le ngana ah Zeisu Christ hong suak hi.

Kumpi Hoih
Kumpi Gilo Rehoboam ii a tusawn, Jehoshaphat pen apa Asa ii khekhap zui-in gamtat hoih in
gamta hi. A pa zongh kumpi hoih khat in kiciamteh aa, a pa hun aa Pasian deihlohnate le

paikhiatpih lai in Pasian in Israel te a do sakloh, Moab minamte le Ammon mite in amah do
dingin kithawi uhhi. Jehoshaphat in a gambup in Pasian mai zon ding thupia hi. Pasian in do

Joseph (Pasal) lam Khanggui
Solomon pen Kumpi David in mi’ zi a laak sak, Bethsheba tawh a ngah tapa ahi hi. Nathan
tawh nu kibang, pa kibang unau sitset ahi uhhi. Solomon khit khang sawmnih le khang ngana
ah Joseph suak hi. Joseph pen Zeisu Christ ii hong suakna tonu ii pasal ahi hi. Matthew laibu
sung ah pasal lam khanggui kigen hi.

Mary (Numei) lam khanggui
Nathan pen Kumpi David in mi’ zi a laak sak, Bethsheba tawh a ngah tapa ahi hi. Solomon
tawh nu kibang pa kibang unau sitset ahi uhhi. Nathan pen Kamsang Nathan tawh min kibangin
a nu, Bathsheba ii minphuah ahi hi. Nathan khit khang sawmlina ah Mary suak hi. Mary pen
Zeisu Christ ii hong suakna tonu ahi hi. Luke laibu sung ah numei lam khanggui kigen hi.

                                           Tha Laakna
Khanggui hoihlo tampi a kizom suksuk sung pan Zeisu Christ hong suak ciang, tua khanggui
pen a manpha khanggui hoih, a hampha khanggui thupi hong suak hi. Mihing pen kuakua ahi
zong kicing cih bang om hetlo cih kilangh hi. I nuntakna ah hoihlohna vive tawh a kidim kha
phial zongin i sung ah Zeisu hong suak leh i nuntakna in manpha in hoih hi. Mite bangin
muanlesuan ding ulenau sansggam thupi i neikei phial zongin i sung pan Zeisu hong suakkhiat
aa leh a hampha, a thupi ihi ta hi. Samsiatna, maizumna, mindaina le mawhna khempeuh thupha
in hong heisak pen Pasian Hehpihna a liatluat hang bekbek mah ahi hi. I sung ah Zeisu paai in
i nuntakna ah i suakkhiat sak ding kisam hi.

Bethlehem khua pen khuaneu khat hi. Ahi zongin tua khua ah Topa Zeisu hong suah ding
ciangin Bethlehem khua pen “Khuaneu khua ginalo na hikei hi” ci-in na kigen hi. Bangmah i
hihloh hangin i sung pan Zeisu Christ hong suak naak aa leh a ginalo i hi nawnkei ding hi.

Nazareth khua pan Topa Zeisu khangkhia hi. Hih Nazareth khua pen misia mi hoihlo tampi a
omna khua khat ahi hi. Banghang hiam cih leh Nazareth khua ii nisuahna lam ah sehnel gam
omin, nitumna lam ah tuipi om hi. Tua ahih manin leilu leitaw kizopna lampi in Nazareth khua
nawk hi. Nisuah nitumna gamte pan Egypt (Africa) gam lam ahi zong, Egypt (Africa) gam pan
nisuah, nitumna lam ahi zongin kizopna khempeuh in hih Nazareth khua a nawk kul hi. Hih
gam tampi, khua tampi kizopna lampi hangin Nazareth khua ah khualzin mi tamin, minam kim
aa ngeina kim hi. Sum zongh a tamna khua khat hi aa misia mi hoihlo zongh tampi om pah hi.

Hih bangin khua hoihlo sese pan Zeisu hong khangkhia hi. I nuntakna, i pilna, i sumbawlna a
hoihlo sung tektek mah pan Zeisu in na lamdang hong semkhia nuam hi.

                                Phawk Khakhuai Thu Pawlkhat
Cain in Abel haza, ama tung hehin thagum hatna tawh a suamlup hang, ama khanggui zangh
in Zeisu Christ hong suak tuanlo hi. Ama khanggui khempeuh si mangin leitung maitang pan
bei siang khin zo lai hi. Ei thagum hatna, ei sinphatna, ei ngaihsut tawm thu tawh a kigual zo,
nuam a kisa cih bang ahihloh lam kilangh hi.

Noah in a zukhamna pan in a tupa nangawn samsiat lawh kha liang hi. Zu na khamlo zenzen
hileh hi bang thu hoihlote pianglo ding hi. Zu pen hoih hi, hoihlo hi cih ahih pahloh hang zu
hangin siatna piang thei cih bel kimu hi.

Abraham in Pasian ii hopihna za khin napi, leitung taksa tawh khuak zangh in a gamtat khialhna
pan leitung a nawngkaisak zo a kilemlo khanggui hong piang hi. Pasian ii zia ngak zolo aa
mihing ngaihsut tawm tawh kikhial thei hi.

Abraham in a zi pen a sanggamnu ci aa zuau a khem mah bangin a tapa Isaac in zongh a pa
mah suun aa, a zi pen a sanggamnu mah ci-in zuau na phuak leuleu hi. Sakhi a pi san, a no san
cih bang dan ahi mai diam.

Jacob in a pa a khem mah bangin a pu Laban ii khemna zongh a man thuakkik kha veve hi.
Tua bek thamloin a tate khemna zongh na thuak kha beh lai hi. Joseph pen si ci-in a sanggamte
in a pa Jacob khem uhhi. A vawh bangin atkik kha cih bang dan ahi mai diam.

Judah in a monu tungah a kamciam peel ahih manin a monu in amah leh khemkik aa, a monu
tawh ta neih lawh hi.

Jericho khua aa numei kizuak nu in a galte (Pasian mite) matna thahna a thuak nading panin na
honkhia hi. Hih bangin hotkhiatna pen a honkhia ziau hilo aa luangkham ahi hi. A zenzenin a
selsim lam kithei hileh thatna thuak ding hi. Hih bangin thupha a bawlna pan Salmon tawh
kiteng kha in a suan a khak sung pan Topa Zeisu hong suak hi.

Naomi in a tate a sih mang khitciang a mote a nungsang vuah a ciahkik dingin sawl hi. Ruth lo
buang a mo dangte ciahkik uhhi. Ruth ahih leh Moab minam hi aa a teknu tawh zongh minam
kibanglo uhhi. Ahi zong “na paina-ah ka pai ding aa, na giahna-ah ka giak ding hi. Na mite
keima mite hi ding a, na Pasian keima Pasian hi ding hi” ci-in a teknu nusialo hi. A teknu sawl
bangin lo ah an tawmin a omna pan lo neipa Boaz in meigong khat ahih hang tenpih hi. Boaz
tawh a ngah suanlekhak sung pan Topa Zeisu hong suak hi. Noami pen Boaz tawh tanau ahih
hangin innkuan khat hilo hi. Ahi zong Ruth hangin Boaz tawh innkuan khat suak kha hi.

Kumpi David in mawhna khialhna tampi a neih hangin a kisikkik pahpah khat hi. Tua kisikna
in Pasian lungtang tuak hi.

Solomon in Pasian awiloh tampi a sep khakna hangin Pasian in a tapa hun ciang a minam pih,
beh dangte ii langpanna thuaksak hi. Solomon in Pasian maipha a muhloh manin Israel gam
phelnih suah dingin Pasian in geel hi. A pa hoihlohna hangin a ta in thuaklawh hi. Makai khat
hoihlohna hangin gambup in thuaklawh hi.

Rehoboam in phiangsiah tam ngenlua aa, a mipite lungkimlo uhhi. Phiangsiah khiap ding a
nget uhhang khiapsak nuamlo ahih manin mipite in langpan uh aa a gam phel nih kisuah hi.
