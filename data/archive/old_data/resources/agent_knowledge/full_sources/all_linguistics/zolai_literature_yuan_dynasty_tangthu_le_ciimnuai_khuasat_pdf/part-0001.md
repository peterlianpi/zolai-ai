Yuan Dynasty hong buaikhit ciangin, Mogols te kithehthangna Bagan khua kiim (tai 300

humna sung ah), Kawlte ahilo Yaw minam kici tampi teng uhhi. Saw khua cih zongh om

hi. Tuni dong Kawlte in Yaw te khua ci-in a ciapteh uh khua tampi om hi. Asho kici

minam zongh om hi. Zo kici minam zongh om hi.

Sente ii Zhou Dynasty le Qhin Dynasty tangthu tawh Mongols te ii Yuan Dynasty tangthu

i etkhop ciang, Zomite mun le mual ahi zong khuasat hunte pen Mogols te ii Yuan

Dyansty tangthu tawh kinaizaw cih theih hi.




Yuan panin, Yaw, Zo, Asho cih bang dan minte a kikhel/a ka toto ahi kha diam cih nop

huai hi. Kithehtang aa kizopna a omloh zawh a sawt ciang, paulehamte zongh

damdamin hong kidang cih theih thu hi. Zuatna tuamtek zui-in munlemual tungtawn

pan ahi zong, pauleham tungtawn pan ahi zong minlepuamte zongh hong kibanglo

tektek cih theih hi.

Gtn. Ciimnuai hun bek pan zongh kikhenkhiatna a omkhit ciang, beh tuamtuam a

thakthak piang thuahthuah hi. Pagan hun pek pan hi leh bel, min tampi a piang

thuahthuah thei thu mah hi.
