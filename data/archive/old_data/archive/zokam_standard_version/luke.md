# Cleaned Bible Texts: Zolai-English Parallel Corpus - Luke

This file contains cleaned parallel texts from the Zokam Standard Version (Zolai) and English translations (KJV/WEB) for the book of Luke.

## Format
Each entry shows: **Reference** | **Zolai Text** | **English Text**

---

## LUKE

**LUK 1:1**
- **Zolai:** Eite lakah thu a piangte a ciamteh dingin mi tampi in pan a la zo uh hi.
- **English:** Forasmuch as many have taken in hand to set forth in order a declaration of those things which are most surely believed among us,

**LUK 1:2**
- **Zolai:** A kipatcil panin a mute leh Thugen nasemte in a hong pahtawite uh bangin,
- **English:** Even as they delivered them unto us, which from the beginning were eyewitnesses, and ministers of the word;

**LUK 1:3**
- **Zolai:** Keimah zongin a kipatcil panin a banbanin a ciamteh dingin ka lungsim ka koih hi, Theofilus mangpa aw,
- **English:** It seemed good to me also, having had perfect understanding of all things from the very first, to write unto thee in order, most excellent Theophilus,

**LUK 1:4**
- **Zolai:** Nangmah in na theihsa thute a man takpi ahihlam na theih nadingin.
- **English:** That thou mightest know the certainty of those things, wherein thou hast been instructed.
