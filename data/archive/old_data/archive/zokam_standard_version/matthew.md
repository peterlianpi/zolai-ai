# Cleaned Bible Texts: Zolai-English Parallel Corpus

This file contains cleaned parallel texts from the Zokam Standard Version (Zolai) and English translations (KJV/WEB) for linguistic study.

## Format
Each entry shows: **Reference** | **Zolai Text** | **English Text**

---

## MATTHEW

**MAT 1:1**
- **Zolai:** Abraham’ suan, David’ suan, Zeisu Khazih’ khang ciamtehna lai inah:
- **English:** The book of the generation of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.

**MAT 1:2**
- **Zolai:** Abraham’ tapa Isaak, Isaak’ tapa Jakob, Jakob’ tapa Judah, leh a unaute teng hi.
- **English:** Abraham begat Isaac; and Isaac begat Jacob; and Jacob begat Judah and his brethren;

**MAT 1:3**
- **Zolai:** Judah in a zi Tamar tawh a neih a tapa Perez leh Zerah, Perez’ tapa Hezron, Hezron’ tapa Aram,
- **English:** And Judah begat Phares and Zara of Thamar; and Phares begat Esrom; and Esrom begat Aram;

**MAT 1:4**
- **Zolai:** Aram’ tapa Amminadab, Amminadab’ tapa Nashon, Nashon’ tapa Salmon,
- **English:** And Aram begat Aminadab; and Aminadab begat Naasson; and Naasson begat Salmon;

**MAT 1:5**
- **Zolai:** Salmon in a zi Rahab tawh a neih a tapa Boaz, Boaz in a zi Ruth tawh a neih a tapa Obed, Obed’ tapa Jessi,
- **English:** And Salmon begat Booz of Rahab; and Booz begat Obed of Ruth; and Obed begat Jesse;

**MAT 1:6**
- **Zolai:** Jessi’ tapa David, David’ tapa Solomon, Solomon’ tapa Roboam, Roboam’ tapa Abia,
- **English:** And Jesse begat David the king; and David begat Solomon; and Solomon begat Roboam; and Roboam begat Abia;

**MAT 1:7**
- **Zolai:** Abia’ tapa Asa, Asa’ tapa Josaphat, Josaphat’ tapa Joram, Joram’ tapa Ozias,
- **English:** And Abia begat Asa; and Asa begat Josaphat; and Josaphat begat Joram; and Joram begat Ozias;

**MAT 1:8**
- **Zolai:** Ozias’ tapa Joatham, Joatham’ tapa Achaz, Achaz’ tapa Ezekias, Ezekias’ tapa Manases,
- **English:** And Ozias begat Joatham; and Joatham begat Achaz; and Achaz begat Ezekias; and Ezekias begat Manases;

**MAT 1:9**
- **Zolai:** Manases’ tapa Amos, Amos’ tapa Josias, Josias’ tapa Jekonias, leh Jekonias’ le a unaute teng hi,
- **English:** And Manases begat Amos; and Amos begat Josias; and Josias begat Jekonias; and Jekonias begat his brethren;

**MAT 1:10**
- **Zolai:** Jekonias’ tapa Salathiel, Salathiel’ tapa Zorobabel, Zorobabel’ tapa Abiud, Abiud’ tapa Eliakim,
- **English:** And Jekonias begat Salathiel; and Salathiel begat Zorobabel; and Zorobabel begat Abiud; and Abiud begat Eliakim;

**MAT 1:11**
- **Zolai:** Eliakim’ tapa Azor, Azor’ tapa Zadok, Zadok’ tapa Achim, Achim’ tapa Eliud,
- **English:** And Eliakim begat Azor; and Azor begat Zadok; and Zadok begat Achim; and Achim begat Eliud;

**MAT 1:12**
- **Zolai:** Eliud’ tapa Eleazar, Eleazar’ tapa Matthan, Matthan’ tapa Jacob,
- **English:** And Eliud begat Eleazar; and Eleazar begat Matthan; and Matthan begat Jacob;

**MAT 1:13**
- **Zolai:** Jacob’ tapa Joseph, Joseph’ lei Mary’ lei, Mary lei Zeisu Khazih’ lei hi.
- **English:** And Jacob begat Joseph the husband of Mary, of whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.

**MAT 1:14**
- **Zolai:** Zeisu Khazih’ lei hi, leh i suan lei hi,
- **English:** So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations; and from David until the carrying away into Babylon are fourteen generations; and from the carrying away into Babylon unto Christ are fourteen generations.

**MAT 1:15**
- **Zolai:** Hi khat ah, David khang ciangin Abraham khang panin vekin khang sawm le khang li,
- **English:** Now the birth of Jesus Christ was on this wise: When as his mother Mary was espoused to Joseph, before they came together, she was found with child of the Holy Ghost.

**MAT 1:16**
- **Zolai:** David khang panin Babylon gama saltang dinga a kimat hun ciang khang sawm le khang li,
- **English:** Then Joseph her husband, being a just man, and not willing to make her a publick example, was minded to put her away privily.

**MAT 1:17**
- **Zolai:** Babylon gama saltan hun pan Khazih khang ciang khang sawm le khang li mah ahi hi.
- **English:** But while he thought on these things, behold, the angel of the Lord appeared unto him in a dream, saying, Joseph, thou son of David, fear not to take unto thee Mary thy wife: for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy Ghost.

**MAT 1:18**
- **Zolai:** Zeisu Khazih a suahzia hih bang ahi hi.
- **English:** And she shall bring forth a son, and thou shalt call his name JESUS: for he shall save his people from their sins.

**MAT 1:19**
- **Zolai:** A nu Mary pen Joseph zi dingin zuthawl kipiasa hi a, a kiteenma un Kha Siangtho hangin gai hi ci in kithei hi.
- **English:** Now all this was done, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken of the Lord by the prophet, saying,

**MAT 1:20**
- **Zolai:** A lawmpa Joseph in mi lungsim hoih hi a, a lawmnu Mary min daisak nuamlo ahih manin maksim dingin a ngaihsun hi.
- **English:** Behold, a virgin shall be with child, and shall bring forth a son, and they shall call his name Emmanuel, which being interpreted is, God with us.
