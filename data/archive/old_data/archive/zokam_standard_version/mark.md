# Cleaned Bible Texts: Zolai-English Parallel Corpus - Mark

This file contains cleaned parallel texts from the Zokam Standard Version (Zolai) and English translations (KJV/WEB) for the book of Mark.

## Format
Each entry shows: **Reference** | **Zolai Text** | **English Text**

---

## MARK

**MRK 1:1**
- **Zolai:** Pasian’ Tapa Zeisu Khazih’ Lungdamna Thu a kipatna.
- **English:** The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God;

**MRK 1:2**
- **Zolai:** Kamsangpa Isaiah’ laibu sungah, “En in, ka vantungmi na mai-ah ka sawl hi; Amah in na paina lam a sial ding hi.”
- **English:** As it is written in the prophets, Behold, I send my messenger before thy face, which shall prepare thy way before thee.

**MRK 1:3**
- **Zolai:** “Gampa lakah a kikopa’ aw in, ‘Topa’ lampi sial un, a lampite tang un,’ a ci hi,” ci-in a kigelh hi.
- **English:** The voice of one crying in the wilderness, Prepare ye the way of the Lord, make his paths straight.
