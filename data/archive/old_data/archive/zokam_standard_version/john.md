# Cleaned Bible Texts: Zolai-English Parallel Corpus - John

This file contains cleaned parallel texts from the Zokam Standard Version (Zolai) and English translations (KJV/WEB) for the book of John.

## Format
Each entry shows: **Reference** | **Zolai Text** | **English Text**

---

## JOHN

**JHN 1:1**
- **Zolai:** A kipatcil-in Thu a om hi. Tua Thu pen Pasian tawh omkhawm a, tua Thu pen Pasian ahi hi.
- **English:** In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.

**JHN 1:2**
- **Zolai:** Amah pen a kipatcil-in Pasian tawh a omkhawm ahi hi.
- **English:** The same was in the beginning with God.

**JHN 1:3**
- **Jolai:** Vun khempeuh ama tungtawnin a piang ahi hi. A piang khempeuh lakah amah tello-in bangmah a piang kei hi.
- **English:** All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made.

**JHN 1:4**
- **Zolai:** Ama sungah nuntakna a om a, tua nuntakna pen mihingte ading khuavak ahi hi.
- **English:** In him was life; and the life was the light of men.

**JHN 1:5**
- **Zolai:** Tua khuavak pen khuamial sungah a taang a, khuamial in tua khuavak a nusia kei hi.
- **English:** And the light shineth in darkness; and the darkness comprehended it not.

**JHN 1:14**
- **Zolai:** Tua Thu pen mihing suakin eite lakah a teeng hi. Pa’ kiang pan a suak Tapa khat bek’ minthan’na bangin ama minthan’na ka mu uh hi. Amah pen hehpihna leh thumanin a dim ahi hi.
- **English:** And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us, (and we beheld his glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father,) full of grace and truth.
